UniPool: A Globally Shared Expert Pool for Mixture-of-Experts UniPool：用于混合专家模型的全局共享专家池

🧑‍🔬 Minbin Huang, Han Shi, Chuanyang Zheng, Yimeng Wu et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 全局共享专家池替代逐层专家集，参数更省、性能更好
A globally shared expert pool replaces per-layer expert sets in MoE, cutting expert parameters while improving quality.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 传统的混合专家模型（MoE）为每一层单独分配一套专家，导致专家参数量随模型深度线性增长。研究发现，深层的路由机制存在大量冗余——用随机路由替换深层的学习路由，模型性能几乎不变。那么，每层真的需要独立的专家集吗？ Standard Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) transformer architectures assign a dedicated, isolated set of experts to each layer. This means expert parameters grow linearly with model depth, even when deeper layers may not need unique expert capacity. The question is: do we really need every layer to own its own separate experts?
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **全局专家池**：用单一共享池替代每层独立专家集，各层通过独立路由器访问同一池，深度扩展与专家参数量解耦。 - **池级辅助损失**：提出跨整个专家池的负载均衡损失，而非仅在层内平衡，保证训练稳定且专家被均匀利用。 - **NormRouter**：引入稀疏且尺度稳定的路由机制，专为共享池设计，有效避免训练不稳定问题。 - **亚线性专家扩展**：证明专家参数无需随深度线性增长，压缩版UniPool仅用41.6%–66.7%的标准专家参数预算即可持平或超越原始MoE。 - **Global Expert Pool**: Replaces per-layer expert ownership with a single shared pool accessed by independent per-layer routers, decoupling depth from expert-parameter count. - **Pool-Level Auxiliary Loss**: Introduces a novel balancing loss across the entire pool (not just per-layer), ensuring stable and uniform expert utilization during training. - **NormRouter**: Adopts a sparse, scale-stable routing mechanism specifically designed to reliably address a shared pool, preventing training instability. - **Sublinear Expert Scaling**: Demonstrates that expert parameters can grow sublinearly with depth — reduced-pool variants use only 41.6–66.7% of standard expert budgets while matching or beating vanilla MoE.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在五个规模的LLaMA架构模型（参数量分别为182M、469M、650M、830M、978M）上进行，所有模型均在Pile数据集的300亿token上训练。基线为参数量匹配的逐层MoE模型。评估指标包括验证损失和困惑度。另外进行了消融实验，探索使用41.6%–66.7%专家参数预算的压缩池变体，以及UniPool与更细粒度专家分解的可组合性。还设计了路由探针实验，用均匀随机路由替换学习路由来量化各层路由冗余。 Experiments were conducted on five LLaMA-architecture model scales (182M, 469M, 650M, 830M, and 978M parameters), all trained on 30 billion tokens from the Pile dataset. The baselines are vanilla per-layer MoE models matched in total parameter count. Evaluation metrics include validation loss and perplexity. Additional ablations explore reduced-pool variants (using 41.6%–66.7% of the standard expert-parameter budget) and the composability of UniPool with finer-grained expert decomposition. A routing probe experiment replaces learned routers with uniform random routing to quantify per-layer routing redundancy.
📊 实验
结果		 在全部五个模型规模上，UniPool均稳定优于参数匹配的原始MoE基线，最高将验证损失降低0.0386。压缩版UniPool仅使用标准专家参数预算的41.6%–66.7%，即可持平甚至超越完整逐层MoE。路由探针实验显示，在多个生产级MoE模型中，用随机路由替换深层的学习路由仅导致下游准确率下降1.0–1.6个点，充分验证了共享池设计的动机。 Across all five model scales, UniPool consistently improves validation loss and perplexity over matched vanilla MoE baselines. The best improvement reaches a validation loss reduction of up to 0.0386 compared to vanilla MoE. Critically, reduced-pool UniPool variants using only 41.6%–66.7% of the standard expert-parameter budget match or outperform the full layer-wise MoE at the tested scales. The routing probe further shows that replacing learned routing with random routing in deeper layers drops accuracy by only 1.0–1.6 points across multiple production MoE models, confirming the motivation for the shared-pool design.
🏁 结论 UniPool证明了MoE架构中的专家容量无需按层分配——单一全局共享专家池配合独立路由器，在多个规模上均能提升验证性能，并实现专家参数随深度亚线性增长。本文将池大小确立为一个有意义的深度缩放超参数，挑战了"每层需要独立专家容量"的传统假设。 UniPool demonstrates that expert capacity in MoE architectures need not be allocated per-layer; a single globally shared expert pool accessed by independent routers consistently improves validation loss and enables sublinear expert-parameter scaling with depth. This work establishes pool size as a meaningful depth-scaling hyperparameter and challenges the prevailing assumption that every transformer layer requires isolated expert capacity.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

UniPool将每层独立拥有专家集的传统设计，改为所有层共用一个全局专家池。每层保留各自独立的路由器来决定激活哪些专家，但所有路由器都指向同一个共享池。为了确保训练稳定，作者引入了池级辅助损失，在整个专家池范围内强制均衡专家利用率，而不是仅在单层内平衡。同时采用NormRouter，这是一种稀疏且尺度稳定的路由机制，能有效防止访问共享池时的训练不稳定性。该设计使池大小成为一个显式的深度缩放超参数，让用户可以灵活权衡专家容量与参数效率。 UniPool replaces the conventional design where each transformer layer owns its own expert set with a single globally shared pool of experts. All layers access this same pool, but each layer retains its own independent router to select which experts to activate. To ensure balanced and stable training across this shared pool, the authors introduce a pool-level auxiliary loss that enforces uniform utilization of experts across all layers simultaneously, rather than balancing only within each layer. They also adopt NormRouter, a routing mechanism that produces sparse, scale-stable gating scores, which is critical to prevent instability when routing into a shared pool. This design explicitly makes pool size a hyperparameter for depth scaling, allowing practitioners to trade off expert capacity versus parameter efficiency.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{pool-bal}} = N_{\text{experts}} \cdot \sum_{i=1}^{N_{\text{experts}}} f_i \cdot P_i\]
💡 池级均衡辅助损失：对整个共享专家池中所有专家的实际使用频率（f_i）与路由概率（P_i）之积求和，鼓励专家被均匀使用。 Pool-level balancing auxiliary loss: sums the product of each expert's actual load fraction (f_i) and its routing probability (P_i) across the entire shared pool, encouraging uniform expert utilization.
\[g_i = \frac{\exp(s_i / \|s\|)}{\sum_j \exp(s_j / \|s\|)}\]
💡 NormRouter路由打分：在计算softmax之前先用分数向量的范数归一化，使路由打分对输入尺度不敏感，从而提供稳定的稀疏路由。 NormRouter gating score: normalizes raw router scores by their vector norm before softmax, making routing scale-invariant and producing stable sparse gates into the shared pool.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着大语言模型规模持续扩大，降低MoE模型中专家参数的增长成本对训练和推理效率至关重要。UniPool提供了一种原则性的架构级解决方案，在不牺牲（甚至提升）模型质量的前提下，实现更高效的MoE参数利用。 As LLMs scale to hundreds of billions of parameters, reducing the cost of expert-parameter growth in MoE models has direct implications for training and serving efficiency. UniPool offers a principled, architecture-level path to more parameter-efficient MoE designs that do not sacrifice — and may even improve — model quality.

Optimizer-Model Consistency: Full Finetuning with the Same Optimizer as Pretraining Forgets Less 优化器-模型一致性：使用与预训练相同的优化器进行全量微调可减少遗忘

🧑‍🔬 Yuxing Liu, Jianyu Wang, Tong Zhang

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 预训练与微调使用同一优化器可显著减少灾难性遗忘
Using the same optimizer for both pretraining and finetuning reduces catastrophic forgetting in LLMs.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型在微调时常常面临"灾难性遗忘"问题——模型学会新任务的同时，会丢失预训练阶段积累的知识。实际操作中，研究者往往不考虑预训练时使用的优化器，随意为微调阶段选择不同的优化器，却不清楚这种"不一致"是否会加剧遗忘。此外，LoRA等参数高效微调方法也被广泛使用，但它们与全量微调在遗忘行为上的比较尚不清晰。 When fine-tuning large language models (LLMs), a common problem is "catastrophic forgetting" — the model learns new tasks but loses previously acquired knowledge. Practitioners often choose optimizers for fine-tuning independently of what was used in pretraining, without knowing whether this mismatch causes more forgetting. Additionally, parameter-efficient methods like LoRA are widely used, but their forgetting behavior relative to full fine-tuning is not well understood.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **优化器-模型一致性**：首次系统性发现，预训练与微调阶段使用相同优化器，可在不牺牲新任务性能的前提下显著减少知识遗忘，效果甚至优于LoRA。 • **机制解释**：优化器对模型激活值具有隐式正则化效应，塑造了预训练检查点附近的损失地貌；使用相同优化器能使权重更新自然对齐该地貌，从而降低遗忘。 • **Muon的死记硬背弱点**：首次揭示Muon优化器倾向于死记硬背，在数据量有限的SFT场景中会损害模式泛化能力，通过合成语言建模实验加以验证。 • **Optimizer-Model Consistency**: A novel observation that matching the optimizer between pretraining and SFT leads to less catastrophic forgetting while maintaining or improving task performance — even outperforming LoRA. • **Mechanistic Explanation**: Optimizers act as implicit regularizers on model activations, shaping the loss landscape around pretrained checkpoints; using the same optimizer naturally keeps weight updates aligned with that landscape. • **Muon's Memorization Weakness**: A new finding that Muon optimizer has a strong tendency toward rote memorization, which hurts pattern generalization during SFT with limited data — demonstrated via a synthetic language modeling experiment.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在LLM预训练和监督微调（SFT）两个阶段展开，主要使用AdamW和Muon两种优化器。评估指标涵盖通用知识基准（衡量预训练知识遗忘程度）和推理/特定任务基准（衡量新任务学习效果）。LoRA被用作关键基线进行对比。此外，还专门设计了合成语言建模任务，用于孤立研究Muon的死记硬背倾向与模式习得行为。实验在受控设置下比较了多种优化器组合（预训练与微调阶段相同 vs 不同）。 Experiments were conducted on LLM pretraining and supervised fine-tuning (SFT) stages using AdamW and Muon as the primary optimizers. Evaluations covered general knowledge benchmarks (to measure forgetting of pretrained knowledge) and reasoning/task-specific benchmarks (to measure new task learning). LoRA was used as a key baseline for comparison. A synthetic language modeling task was additionally designed to isolate Muon's memorization vs. pattern learning behavior. Multiple optimizer combinations (same vs. mismatched across stages) were compared in a controlled setup.
📊 实验
结果		 使用与预训练相同优化器进行全量微调，在学习-遗忘权衡上优于不一致优化器配置和LoRA。具体而言，优化器一致的全量微调在新任务性能持平或更优的同时，遗忘预训练知识更少。当预训练与微调阶段优化器不匹配时（如预训练用Muon、微调用AdamW），遗忘程度可观测地增加。Muon预训练模型在推理任务SFT阶段用Muon微调，效果差于AdamW一致配置；合成实验定量表明，Muon的死记硬背倾向在小数据SFT场景中显著损害模式习得能力。 Full fine-tuning with the same optimizer as pretraining achieves a better learning-forgetting tradeoff than mismatched optimizer configurations and LoRA. Specifically, optimizer-model consistent full fine-tuning forgets less pretrained knowledge while achieving the same or better performance on new tasks compared to LoRA. When Muon is used for pretraining but AdamW for SFT (or vice versa), forgetting increases measurably. Muon-pretrained models fine-tuned with Muon on reasoning tasks perform worse than AdamW-consistent counterparts, with the synthetic experiment showing Muon's rote memorization tendency quantitatively hurts few-data pattern acquisition during SFT.
🏁 结论 本文证明，"优化器-模型一致性"——在预训练和微调阶段使用相同优化器——是一个具有理论依据和实践价值的重要原则，能有效减少LLM的灾难性遗忘。同时揭示了Muon优化器的记忆偏好使其不适合推理类SFT任务，为LLM训练流程提供了可操作的指导。 This paper proves that optimizer-model consistency — using the same optimizer in both pretraining and fine-tuning — is a practically important and theoretically grounded principle that reduces catastrophic forgetting in LLMs. It also reveals that Muon's memorization bias makes it less suitable for reasoning-focused SFT, offering actionable guidance for LLM training pipelines.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者首先通过受控实验，系统比较不同优化器组合（预训练阶段 vs 微调阶段）在新任务学习与知识遗忘之间的权衡表现。随后进行理论分析，揭示优化器对模型激活值施加隐式正则化，从而塑造预训练检查点附近的损失地貌。他们论证，SFT阶段的权重更新应遵循与该地貌相适应的特定结构，而使用相同优化器恰好能自然满足这一条件。最后，设计合成语言建模实验，专门隔离并研究Muon优化器的死记硬背倾向与模式习得能力之间的差异。 The authors first conduct controlled experiments comparing different optimizer combinations across pretraining and SFT stages, measuring the trade-off between new task learning and forgetting of pretrained knowledge. They then perform theoretical analysis showing that optimizers impose implicit regularization on model activations, which shapes the loss landscape near the pretrained checkpoint. They argue that weight updates during SFT should follow structures compatible with this landscape, and that using the same optimizer naturally satisfies this constraint. Finally, a synthetic language modeling experiment is designed to isolate and study Muon's rote memorization tendency versus pattern acquisition ability.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{SFT}}(\theta) + \lambda \cdot \Omega(\theta; \theta_0)\]
💡 隐式正则化目标：SFT损失加上以预训练参数θ₀为中心的正则化项Ω，优化器通过塑造Ω的结构来影响遗忘程度 Implicit regularized objective: SFT loss plus a regularization term Ω anchored at pretrained weights θ₀; the optimizer shapes the structure of Ω, which determines how much forgetting occurs
\[\Delta W = W_{\text{SFT}} - W_0 \in \mathcal{S}_{\text{optimizer}}\]
💡 权重更新ΔW应属于由预训练优化器决定的特定结构集合S，使用相同优化器可自然满足此约束，从而减少遗忘 The weight update ΔW during SFT should belong to a structured set S determined by the pretraining optimizer; using the same optimizer naturally satisfies this constraint, reducing forgetting

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着Muon等新型优化器在预训练中被广泛采用，微调阶段的优化器选择已成为影响知识保留的隐性关键超参数。本研究提供了理论框架和实践建议，无需任何架构改动即可直接改善LLM微调流程，对工业界和学术界均具有即时参考价值。 As the community increasingly adopts diverse optimizers like Muon for pretraining, the choice of fine-tuning optimizer has become a hidden but critical hyperparameter that directly impacts knowledge retention. This work provides both a theoretical framework and practical recommendations that can immediately improve LLM fine-tuning pipelines without any architectural changes.

When and Why SignSGD Outperforms SGD: A Theoretical Study Based on $\ell_1$-norm Lower Bounds SignSGD 何时以及为何优于 SGD：基于 ℓ₁ 范数下界的理论研究

🧑‍🔬 Hongyi Tao, Dingzhi Yu, Lijun Zhang

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 用ℓ₁范数几何理论证明SignSGD在稀疏噪声下比SGD快d倍
Proves theoretically that SignSGD beats SGD by a factor of d under sparse noise using ℓ₁-norm geometry.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 SignSGD、Muon 等基于符号的优化器在训练大型基础模型时表现出色，但理论上一直无法解释它们为何优于普通的 SGD。核心障碍在于：在标准平滑度和有限方差条件下，SGD 已经是极小极大最优的，理论上不给符号类方法留任何超越空间。 Sign-based optimizers like SignSGD and Muon work surprisingly well for training large language models, but nobody has been able to explain theoretically *when* or *why* they outperform vanilla SGD. The fundamental obstacle is that under standard assumptions, SGD is already provably optimal (minimax), leaving no room for sign-based methods to show a theoretical advantage.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 全新问题几何框架：采用 ℓ₁ 范数平稳性 + ℓ∞ 光滑性 + 可分离噪声模型，精准捕捉符号更新的逐坐标特性，绕开了 SGD 最优的 ℓ₂ 框架。 • 匹配的上下界：推导出 SignSGD 的紧收敛复杂度界，并明确刻画了 SignSGD 可证明优于 SGD 的问题类别。 • 维度倍数加速：在稀疏噪声条件下，SignSGD 相比 SGD 将复杂度降低了整整 d 倍（d 为问题维度）。 • 矩阵域推广：将框架推广至矩阵域，证明 Muon 优化器的矩阵符号算子同样保持最优的维度缩放，为矩阵符号更新提供首个理论依据。 • Novel problem geometry: uses ℓ₁-norm stationarity + ℓ∞-smoothness + separable noise model to capture the coordinate-wise nature of sign updates, breaking out of the ℓ₂ framework where SGD is optimal. • Matched upper & lower bounds: derives tight complexity bounds for SignSGD and explicitly characterizes the problem class where it provably dominates SGD. • Dimension-factor speedup: shows SignSGD reduces complexity by a factor of d (problem dimension) over SGD under sparse noise conditions. • Matrix extension: lifts the framework to the matrix domain, proving Muon optimizer preserves optimal dimensional scaling — providing the first theoretical justification for matrix-sign-based updates.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在 1.24 亿参数的 GPT-2 模型上进行预训练实验，以验证理论预测。实验在符合稀疏噪声条件的设定下对比了 SignSGD 与 SGD 基线的收敛行为。同时，他们还在训练过程中监测 ℓ₁ 与 ℓ₂ 平稳性度量，以确认理论框架能准确预测实际收敛表现。 The authors conducted GPT-2 pretraining experiments on a 124M parameter model to validate their theoretical predictions. They compared SignSGD against SGD baselines under conditions designed to reflect the sparse noise regime identified by their theory. They also analyzed the ℓ₁ vs. ℓ₂ stationarity measures during training to confirm that the theoretical framework accurately predicts practical convergence behavior.
📊 实验
结果		 在稀疏噪声条件下，SignSGD 相比 SGD 的优化复杂度可证明降低 d 倍（d 为问题维度），实现维度级别的提升。在 1.24 亿参数 GPT-2 预训练中，SignSGD 收敛速度更快，与理论预测一致。匹配的上下界表明：在 ℓ₁/ℓ∞ 几何下 SignSGD 的复杂度为 Θ，而稀疏噪声场景下相比 SGD 的 ℓ₂ 下界恰好实现完整 d 倍加速。 SignSGD provably reduces optimization complexity by a factor of d (problem dimension) compared to SGD under sparse noise — a dimension-level improvement. In GPT-2 (124M) pretraining, SignSGD achieves faster convergence in practice, consistent with the theoretical prediction of a d-factor speedup. The matched upper and lower bounds confirm that SignSGD's complexity under ℓ₁/ℓ∞ geometry is Θ(d·σ²/(ε²n)) vs. SGD's lower bound of Ω(d·σ²/(ε²n)) in the ℓ₂ setting, with the sparse noise regime delivering the full d-factor advantage.
🏁 结论 本文首次从理论上严格解释了 SignSGD 何时以及为何优于 SGD，通过匹配的 ℓ₁/ℓ∞ 范数上下界证明了在稀疏噪声下存在 d 倍复杂度优势，并将结论推广至矩阵域，为 Muon 优化器提供了理论支撑。 This paper provides the first rigorous theoretical explanation for when and why SignSGD outperforms SGD, proving a dimension-factor d complexity advantage under sparse noise via matched ℓ₁/ℓ∞-norm bounds, and extends this result to validate the Muon optimizer in the matrix domain.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者将优化问题重新建模为 ℓ₁ 范数平稳性（而非标准 ℓ₂ 梯度范数）+ ℓ∞ 光滑性 + 可分离坐标噪声模型的框架。在此几何下，他们分别推导 SignSGD 的上界（收敛保证）和 SGD 的下界（算法难度下限），再对比二者得出可证明的性能差距。随后，他们将符号算子替换为矩阵符号算子，将框架推广至矩阵域，证明 Muon 优化器同样保持最优的维度缩放性质。最后，通过在符合稀疏噪声条件的 GPT-2 预训练实验中验证理论预测。 The authors reframe the optimization problem using ℓ₁-norm stationarity (instead of the standard ℓ₂ gradient norm), ℓ∞-smoothness, and a separable coordinate-wise noise model. Under this geometry, they derive both upper bounds (convergence guarantees for SignSGD) and lower bounds (fundamental hardness for SGD), then directly compare the upper bound of SignSGD against the lower bound of SGD to establish a provable gap. They extend this framework to the matrix setting by replacing the sign operator with the matrix sign operator (related to the Muon optimizer), proving the same optimal scaling holds. Finally, they connect theory to practice via GPT-2 pretraining experiments designed to match the sparse-noise regime.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

SignSGD 和 Muon 等符号优化器正被广泛用于训练前沿大语言模型，但其超越 SGD 的优势此前仅停留在经验观察层面——本文以严格的理论保证弥合了理论与实践之间的鸿沟。通过精确刻画符号方法可证明占优的条件（稀疏噪声、ℓ∞ 光滑性），本文为从业者提供了选择优化器的理论依据，也为后续理论驱动的优化器设计提供了新方向。 Sign-based optimizers like SignSGD and Muon are increasingly used to train frontier LLMs, yet their advantage over SGD has been purely empirical — this paper closes the theory-practice gap with rigorous guarantees. By identifying the precise conditions (sparse noise, ℓ∞-smoothness) under which sign methods provably win, it gives practitioners a principled basis for choosing optimizers and inspires further theoretically-grounded optimizer design.
