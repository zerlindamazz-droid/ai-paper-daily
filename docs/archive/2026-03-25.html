End-to-End Training for Unified Tokenization and Latent Denoising 统一分词与潜在去噪的端到端训练

🧑‍🔬 Shivam Duggal, Xingjian Bai, Zongze Wu, Richard Zhang et al.

🎯 单阶段联合训练图像分词器与潜在扩散模型
UNITE jointly trains image tokenization and latent diffusion in a single stage via shared encoder weights.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练潜在扩散模型（如Stable Diffusion）需要两个独立阶段：先训练一个图像压缩器（分词器），冻结后再训练扩散模型。这种流程复杂、耗时，且两个模块无法相互协同优化。理论上分词和生成没有必要分开训练，但如何将它们统一一直是个难题。 Training latent diffusion models (LDMs) like Stable Diffusion requires an awkward two-stage pipeline: first train a tokenizer (autoencoder) to compress images into latent codes, then freeze it and train a separate diffusion model on top. This staging adds complexity, slows iteration, and prevents the two components from co-adapting to each other. There is no principled reason why tokenization and generation must be separate — yet until now, combining them has been elusive.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **统一架构（UNITE）**：单一"生成编码器"通过参数共享同时承担分词器和潜在去噪器两个角色，无需独立编码器。 • **优雅的统一视角**：将分词和生成重新定义为相同的潜在推断问题——仅条件不同（完整图像 vs. 噪声潜变量+文本/类别标签），从而可用单一目标函数联合优化。 • **单阶段训练**：每次迭代通过共享编码器做两次前向传播，联合优化两个任务，无需对抗损失或预训练编码器（如DINO），从头训练即可形成"公共潜在语言"。 • **Unified architecture (UNITE):** A single "Generative Encoder" acts as both tokenizer and latent denoiser through weight sharing — no separate encoder needed. • **Elegant reframing:** Tokenization and generation are cast as the same latent inference problem under different conditioning (observed image vs. noisy latent + text/class), enabling a single training objective. • **Single-stage training:** Two forward passes through the shared encoder per iteration jointly optimize both tasks, letting gradients sculpt a shared "common latent language" from scratch — no adversarial losses or pretrained encoders (e.g., DINO) required.
🧪 实验
内容		 主要实验在ImageNet 256×256上进行类别条件图像生成，评估Base和Large两个规模的UNITE模型。对比基线包括LDM、DiT、MAR及使用预训练DINO编码器的方法等主流模型。主要评估指标为FID（Fréchet Inception Distance，值越低越好）。此外还在分子生成任务上验证了跨模态泛化能力，并通过消融实验分析了生成编码器的表征对齐性和压缩特性。 Experiments were conducted on ImageNet 256×256 for class-conditional image generation, serving as the primary benchmark. The authors evaluated Base and Large variants of UNITE and compared against leading LDMs and tokenizer-based generative models including LDM, DiT, MAR, and methods using pretrained DINO encoders. Evaluation used FID (Fréchet Inception Distance) as the primary metric for generation quality. Additional experiments covered molecule generation to demonstrate cross-modality generality, and ablation studies examined representation alignment and compression properties of the Generative Encoder.
📊 实验
结果		 UNITE在ImageNet 256×256上，Base模型FID达2.12，Large模型FID达1.73，接近当前最优水平。这些成绩是在不使用对抗损失、不依赖DINO等预训练编码器的情况下取得的，与依赖复杂两阶段流程的顶级方法相当。分子生成实验进一步证明了该单阶段联合训练范式可推广到图像以外的模态。 UNITE achieves FID 2.12 on ImageNet 256×256 with the Base model and FID 1.73 with the Large model — near state-of-the-art performance without adversarial losses or pretrained encoders like DINO. These results are competitive with top-performing two-stage methods that rely on significantly more complex training pipelines. The molecule generation experiments further confirm that the single-stage joint training paradigm generalizes beyond image data.
🏁 结论 UNITE证明了图像分词与潜在扩散生成可以统一为单一架构并从头进行单阶段端到端训练，在ImageNet上取得接近最优的FID成绩，且无需对抗损失或预训练编码器。这表明长期以来被视为必要的分阶段LDM训练流程并非不可替代——联合优化完全可行且高效。 UNITE demonstrates that image tokenization and latent diffusion generation can be unified into a single architecture and trained end-to-end in one stage from scratch, achieving near state-of-the-art FID scores on ImageNet without adversarial losses or pretrained encoders. This proves that the long-assumed necessity of staged LDM training pipelines is not fundamental — joint optimization is both feasible and effective.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

UNITE的核心是一个共享参数的"生成编码器"，它同时扮演两个角色：（1）图像分词器——将完整图像压缩为潜在编码；（2）去噪网络——在文本或类别条件下对含噪潜变量进行迭代去噪生成。训练时，每个批次通过同一编码器进行两次前向传播，分别对应两个任务，合并后的梯度同时更新共享参数。这迫使潜在空间既易于解码（重建质量好），又易于去噪（生成质量好），形成互利的"公共潜在语言"。整个过程无需对抗训练或冻结的预训练特征提取器。 UNITE introduces a Generative Encoder whose weights are shared between two roles: (1) as a standard image encoder that compresses a full image into latent codes (tokenization), and (2) as a denoising network that iteratively refines noisy latents conditioned on text or class labels (generation). During training, each batch runs two forward passes through this same encoder — one for each task — and the combined loss gradients update the shared parameters simultaneously. This forces the latent space to simultaneously be reconstructable (good for decoding) and learnable (good for denoising), creating a mutually beneficial "common latent language". A standard decoder reconstructs images from the latents, and no adversarial training or frozen pretrained feature extractors are needed.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[x \sim q(x|z, c_{\text{image}}), \quad z \sim q(z|x_t, c_{\text{gen}})\]
💡 统一视角：分词是从完整图像条件推断潜变量z；生成是从噪声潜变量和文本/类别条件推断z——两者是同一推断问题的不同条件版本。 The unifying insight: tokenization infers latent z conditioned on a fully observed image, while generation infers z from noisy latents and text/class conditioning — both are the same latent inference problem under different conditions.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{UNITE}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{tokenize}}(\theta) + \mathcal{L}_{\text{denoise}}(\theta)\]
💡 联合训练目标：对同一组共享参数θ，同时优化分词重建损失和扩散去噪损失，两路梯度共同塑造潜在空间。 The joint training objective: the same shared parameters θ are optimized simultaneously with both the tokenization reconstruction loss and the diffusion denoising loss, with gradients from both tasks shaping the latent space together.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

消除潜在扩散模型的两阶段训练瓶颈，大幅降低了工程复杂度，为真正端到端优化的生成系统铺平了道路。这一范式转变有望加速研究迭代速度，并启发图像以外更多生成模态的统一架构设计。 Eliminating the two-stage training bottleneck of latent diffusion models significantly reduces engineering complexity and opens the door to truly end-to-end optimized generative systems. This paradigm shift could accelerate research iteration and inspire unified architectures across other generative modalities beyond images.

CanViT: Toward Active-Vision Foundation Models CanViT：迈向主动视觉基础模型

🧑‍🔬 Yohaï-Eliel Berreby, Sabrina Du, Audrey Durand, B. Suresh Krishna

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 首个任务无关的主动视觉基础模型，通过序列局部扫视感知场景
CanViT is the first general-purpose active vision foundation model that perceives scenes via sequential glimpses.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 主动视觉让模型像人眼一样逐步"扫视"场景局部，而非一次性看完整张图，理论上更高效也更仿生。但此前没有可以通用预训练的主动视觉基础模型——现有模型都是针对特定任务设计的，性能远落后于传统的被动视觉模型。整个领域缺乏一个可以"训练一次、广泛使用"的基础架构。 Active computer vision — where a model sequentially looks at small parts of a scene rather than the full image at once — is more biologically plausible and computationally efficient, but no general-purpose pretrained architecture has existed for it. Previous active vision models were task-specific, couldn't scale, and fell far behind passive (standard) vision models in performance. The field lacked a foundation model that could be pretrained once and applied broadly.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 场景相对位置编码（Scene-relative RoPE）将局部视网膜式ViT骨干与全局空间"画布（canvas）"隐空间绑定，赋予模型持久的空间记忆。 • Canvas Attention是一种新颖的非对称交叉注意力机制，让骨干网络高效读写高容量画布，省去画布侧的自注意力和全连接层，实现低延迟序列推理。 • 提出无标签预训练方案——被动到主动的密集隐空间蒸馏，训练模型从随机扫视序列重建DINOv3的全场景嵌入，无需任何标注，且与具体策略完全解耦。 • Scene-relative RoPE binds a retinotopic (glimpse-local) ViT backbone with a spatiotopic (scene-global) "canvas" latent workspace, giving the model a persistent spatial memory of where it has looked. • Canvas Attention is a novel asymmetric cross-attention mechanism that lets the backbone efficiently write into and read from the high-capacity canvas without expensive self-attention on the canvas side, enabling low-latency sequential inference. • A label-free pretraining scheme — passive-to-active dense latent distillation — trains the model to reconstruct scene-wide DINOv3 embeddings from random glimpse sequences, requiring no annotations and decoupling pretraining from any specific policy.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在单张H100 GPU上，用1.32亿张ImageNet-21k图像（10亿随机扫视序列）从零预训练CanViT-B，耗时166小时。下游评估包括：ADE20K语义分割（冻结骨干探针，无微调）和ImageNet-1k图像分类（冻结教师探针）。基线包括当前最佳主动视觉模型和FLOP匹配/输入匹配条件下的DINOv3教师模型。还测试了泛化能力：更长滚动序列、更大场景、以及预训练中未见过的新扫视策略。 The authors pretrained CanViT-B from scratch on 13.2 million ImageNet-21k images using 1 billion random glimpse sequences on a single H100 GPU over 166 hours. They evaluated on ADE20K semantic segmentation (using frozen backbone probes, no fine-tuning) and ImageNet-1k image classification (frozen teacher probes). Baselines included the best prior active vision model and the DINOv3 teacher itself under FLOP- or input-matched conditions. The model was also tested for generalization to longer rollouts, larger scenes, and novel glimpse policies unseen during pretraining.
📊 实验
结果		 在ADE20K语义分割上，冻结的CanViT-B仅用单次低分辨率扫视即达到38.5% mIoU，比此前最佳主动视觉模型的27.6%提升10.9个百分点，同时推理FLOPs减少19.5倍，且无需微调。给定多次扫视后，CanViT-B可达到45.9% mIoU。在ImageNet-1k分类上，冻结探针下达到81.2% top-1准确率。CanViT在分割任务上还超越了FLOP匹配和输入匹配条件下的DINOv3教师模型。 On ADE20K segmentation, frozen CanViT-B achieves 38.5% mIoU with a single low-resolution glimpse — outperforming the previous best active model's 27.6% mIoU (+10.9 pp) while using 19.5× fewer inference FLOPs, and with no fine-tuning. With additional glimpses, CanViT-B reaches 45.9% mIoU on ADE20K. On ImageNet-1k classification, CanViT-B achieves 81.2% top-1 accuracy with frozen teacher probes. CanViT also outperforms its FLOP-matched and input-matched DINOv3 teacher on segmentation.
🏁 结论 CanViT建立了首个可扩展、任务与策略无关的主动视觉基础模型，大幅缩小了主动视觉与被动视觉在语义分割上的长期性能差距。研究证明，面向序列扫视感知的通用预训练既可行又高度有效。 CanViT establishes the first scalable, task- and policy-agnostic Active Vision Foundation Model, closing the long-standing performance gap between active and passive vision on semantic segmentation. It demonstrates that general-purpose pretraining for sequential, glimpse-based perception is both feasible and highly effective.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

CanViT由两部分组成：处理局部低分辨率扫视的ViT骨干，以及跨扫视积累信息的大型"画布"空间隐变量。骨干每次处理一个扫视后，通过Canvas Attention（单向交叉注意力）将信息写入画布，避免画布侧的重复计算。场景相对RoPE编码每个扫视在全图中的空间位置，使模型无论扫视顺序或缩放级别如何都能构建连贯的空间地图。预训练无需标签：模型接收随机扫视序列，目标是重建预训练DINOv3被动模型对完整场景产生的密集块级嵌入，从而将被动视觉知识蒸馏进主动架构。 CanViT pairs a standard Vision Transformer backbone (operating on low-resolution glimpses) with a large "canvas" — a spatially structured latent workspace that accumulates information across glimpses. The backbone processes each glimpse and writes into the canvas via Canvas Attention, a one-sided cross-attention that avoids redundant computation on the canvas side. Scene-relative RoPE encodes the spatial position of each glimpse relative to the full scene, allowing the model to build a coherent spatial map regardless of glimpse order or zoom level. For pretraining, the model is trained without labels: it takes sequences of random glimpses and must reconstruct the dense patch-level embeddings that a pretrained DINOv3 passive model would produce for the full scene — effectively distilling passive vision knowledge into an active architecture.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Canvas Attention}(Q_c, K_b, V_b) = \text{softmax}\left(\frac{Q_c K_b^\top}{\sqrt{d}}\right) V_b\]
💡 Canvas Attention是非对称交叉注意力：画布（canvas）作为查询（Q），骨干网络的扫视特征作为键（K）和值（V），画布侧无自注意力，降低了计算量。 Canvas Attention is asymmetric cross-attention where the canvas queries the backbone's glimpse features; by avoiding self-attention on the canvas side, it reduces computation while still letting the canvas accumulate spatial information across glimpses.
\[\mathcal{L} = \left\| f_{\text{CanViT}}(g_{1:T}) - f_{\text{DINOv3}}(I) \right\|_2^2\]
💡 预训练损失：CanViT从T个随机扫视序列重建DINOv3对完整图像I产生的密集特征嵌入，用L2距离衡量重建误差，无需任何人工标注。 The pretraining objective: CanViT must reconstruct the dense DINOv3 embeddings of the full scene from a sequence of T random glimpses, supervised purely by a frozen passive teacher with no human labels.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

主动视觉是机器人、具身AI和边缘计算高效感知的核心能力，但长期缺乏可复用的基础模型。CanViT填补了这一空白，为需要自适应、低成本视觉探索的机器人和强化学习研究提供了可直接使用的预训练骨干网络。 Active vision — perceiving the world through directed, sequential attention rather than full-frame snapshots — is fundamental to robotics, embodied AI, and efficient edge inference, yet has lacked a foundation model to build upon. CanViT fills this gap, providing a pretrained backbone that robotics and RL researchers can directly adopt for tasks requiring adaptive, low-cost visual exploration.

On the Role of Batch Size in Stochastic Conditional Gradient Methods 批量大小在随机条件梯度方法中的作用

🧑‍🔬 Rustem Islamov, Roman Machacek, Aurelien Lucchi, Antonio Silveti-Falls et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 理论揭示随机条件梯度训练的最优批量大小与步长选择规律
Theory reveals optimal batch size and stepsize scaling rules for stochastic conditional gradient training.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练大型模型（如GPT）时，批量大小和学习率的选择至关重要，但两者如何相互影响缺乏理论指导。实践中往往依赖经验或昂贵的超参数搜索。本文研究：在随机条件梯度方法中，能否有原则地同时确定最优批量大小和步长？ Training large models like GPT requires choosing batch size and learning rate carefully, but there is little theoretical guidance on how these hyperparameters interact. Practitioners often rely on intuition or expensive grid searches. This paper asks: is there a principled way to set batch size and stepsize jointly for stochastic conditional gradient methods?
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次揭示"区间依赖"现象：批量大小增加初期提升优化精度，超过临界阈值后收益饱和甚至在固定token预算下反而降低性能。• 在μ-KL条件下，首个显式刻画步长、批量大小与随机噪声三者交互关系的随机条件梯度理论框架（适用于Scion等算法）。• 提出自适应训练策略：训练过程中逐步增大批量大小和序列长度，同时保证收敛性，与现代LLM训练实践高度吻合。 • Novel regime-dependent analysis: shows that increasing batch size helps up to a critical threshold, beyond which benefits saturate or even reverse under a fixed token budget. • First theoretical framework for batch size scaling in stochastic conditional gradient methods (e.g., Scion) under the μ-KL condition, explicitly capturing stepsize–batch size–noise interactions. • Proposes a principled adaptive training schedule that progressively increases batch size and sequence length while maintaining convergence guarantees, aligning theory with modern LLM training practice.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在NanoGPT语言模型训练任务上进行，验证理论预测的三种批量大小区间（欠饱和、饱和、过饱和）。对比不同批量大小和步长组合下的训练损失，并测试自适应批量增大调度策略与固定批量策略的效果。以训练损失为主要评估指标，token预算（总训练token数）作为计算成本约束。 Experiments are conducted on NanoGPT language model training to empirically validate the three theoretically predicted batch size regimes (under-saturated, saturated, over-saturated). Different combinations of batch size and stepsize are evaluated under a fixed token budget, and the proposed adaptive schedule (progressively increasing batch size and sequence length) is compared against fixed-batch baselines. Training loss is the primary evaluation metric.
📊 实验
结果		 NanoGPT实验一致验证了理论预测的三种批量区间现象。在相同token预算下，自适应批量调度策略相比固定批量基线取得更低的最终训练损失。理论推导的最优步长量级与实验中表现最佳的步长吻合，有效弥合了理论与实践之间的鸿沟。具体损失数值在理论预测的临界批量大小处呈现饱和与性能下降效应，验证了闭式临界批量公式的准确性。 NanoGPT experiments consistently confirm all three predicted scaling regimes. The adaptive batch size schedule achieves lower final training loss than fixed-batch baselines under the same token budget. The theoretically derived optimal stepsize magnitude matches the empirically best-performing stepsize, bridging the theory-practice gap. Specific numerical loss values show the saturation and degradation effects at predicted batch size thresholds, validating the closed-form critical batch size formula.
🏁 结论 本文建立了首个理论框架，揭示批量大小、步长与随机噪声如何共同决定随机条件梯度方法的收敛性，发现在固定token预算下存在临界批量阈值，超过该阈值后继续增大批量反而有害。理论指导原则与自适应调度策略在NanoGPT上得到实证验证，为大规模语言模型训练提供了有原则的超参数选择工具。 This paper establishes the first theoretical framework explaining how batch size, stepsize, and stochastic noise jointly govern convergence in stochastic conditional gradient methods, revealing a critical batch size threshold beyond which scaling hurts under a fixed token budget. The derived guidelines and adaptive schedule are practically validated on NanoGPT, offering principled tools for large-scale LLM training.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者在μ-Kurdyka-Łojasiewicz（μ-KL）条件下分析基于动量的随机条件梯度算法（以Scion为代表），该条件是PL条件的推广，适用于非凸优化。推导出新的收敛界，显式刻画步长、批量大小和随机梯度噪声三者共同决定优化精度的方式。从界中识别出临界批量大小阈值，并推导出闭式最优步长公式。进而设计了一种在训练中逐步增大批量大小和序列长度的自适应调度策略，并提供理论收敛保证。 The authors analyze momentum-based stochastic conditional gradient algorithms (specifically Scion) under the μ-Kurdyka-Łojasiewicz (μ-KL) condition, a generalization of the Polyak-Łojasiewicz condition that covers non-convex optimization. They derive new convergence bounds that explicitly track how stepsize, batch size, and stochastic gradient noise jointly determine optimization accuracy. From these bounds, they identify a critical batch size threshold and derive closed-form optimal stepsize formulas. They then design an adaptive schedule that ramps up batch size and sequence length during training, backed by theoretical convergence guarantees.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\min_{b} \; \mathcal{E}(b) \approx \frac{\sigma^2}{b} + \delta^2\]
💡 优化误差由两部分组成：随机噪声项（随批量b增大而减小）和不可消除的噪声下界δ²，两者共同决定最优批量大小的临界点。 The optimization error has two parts: a stochastic noise term that shrinks with batch size b, and an irreducible noise floor δ². Their interplay defines the critical batch size beyond which increasing b no longer helps.
\[b^* \asymp \frac{\sigma^2}{\delta^2}\]
💡 临界最优批量大小b*正比于随机梯度噪声方差σ²与不可消除噪声δ²之比，超过此值后增大批量无益甚至有害。 The critical optimal batch size b* scales as the ratio of stochastic gradient noise variance σ² to irreducible noise δ²; beyond this threshold, larger batches yield no further benefit.

随着LLM训练成本急剧攀升，如何最优地设置批量大小和学习率对提升训练效率至关重要，本文为现代条件梯度优化器（如Scion）的超参数选择提供了罕见的理论依据。预测的批量区间规律和自适应调度策略可直接指导实践，有望减少大规模训练中的算力浪费。 As LLM training costs soar, understanding how to optimally set batch size and learning rate is critical for efficiency — this paper provides rare theoretical backing for these choices in the context of modern conditional gradient optimizers like Scion. The predicted scaling regimes and adaptive schedule offer actionable guidance that could reduce wasted compute in large-scale training runs.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

daVinci-MagiHuman将文本、视频和音频token拼接为一条长序列，用单一Transformer通过标准自注意力统一处理，无需独立编码器、交叉注意力桥接或多流流水线，训练和优化更为简便。为实现快速推理，作者采用步骤蒸馏减少扩散步数，用潜空间超分辨率模块高效提升分辨率，并引入Turbo VAE解码器加速潜变量解码。整个模型端到端训练，能从文本提示生成具有表达力、音画同步的人物视频。 daVinci-MagiHuman uses a single Transformer that concatenates text, video, and audio tokens into one long sequence and processes them jointly with standard self-attention. This eliminates the need for separate encoders, cross-attention bridges, or multi-stream pipelines, making the architecture straightforward to train and optimize. To achieve fast inference, the authors apply step distillation to reduce the number of diffusion steps, use a latent-space super-resolution module to upscale low-resolution outputs efficiently, and introduce a Turbo VAE decoder that decodes latents faster than standard VAEs. The model is trained end-to-end to produce expressive, synchronized human-centric video and audio from text prompts.
