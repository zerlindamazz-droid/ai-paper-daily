Pion: A Spectrum-Preserving Optimizer via Orthogonal Equivalence Transformation Pion：基于正交等价变换的谱保持优化器

🧑‍🔬 Kexuan Shi, Hanxuan Li, Zeju Qiu, Yandong Wen et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 用正交变换保持权重奇异值，实现稳定LLM训练的新优化器
Pion preserves singular values of weight matrices via orthogonal transformations for stable LLM training.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练大语言模型需要兼顾效率、稳定性和泛化能力的优化器。像Adam这样的加法优化器在更新权重时会逐渐扭曲权重矩阵的谱结构，可能导致训练不稳定或权重几何形状不理想。目前缺乏能感知权重矩阵内在几何结构的优化器。 Training large language models requires optimizers that balance efficiency, stability, and generalization. Standard optimizers like Adam update weights additively, which can distort the spectral structure of weight matrices over time and lead to instability or suboptimal geometry. There is a need for optimizers that are aware of the intrinsic geometry of weight matrices during training.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 创新的谱保持更新机制：Pion通过左右正交变换更新权重矩阵，在整个训练过程中保持所有奇异值不变，与加法优化器在本质范式上截然不同。 • 几何调制而不改变谱范数：通过固定谱范数，Pion控制权重矩阵在函数空间中的旋转和变形，避免了奇异值"失控"增长的问题。 • 系统化设计分析：从第一原理推导Pion更新规则，并系统分析设计选择与收敛性质，为方法提供理论支撑。 • Novel spectrum-preserving update: Pion updates weight matrices via left and right orthogonal transformations, keeping all singular values exactly fixed throughout training — a fundamentally different paradigm from additive optimizers. • Geometry modulation without spectral drift: By fixing the spectral norm, Pion controls how weight matrices rotate and reshape in function space without allowing "runaway" singular value growth. • Systematic design analysis: The paper derives the Pion update rule from first principles and carefully examines design choices and convergence properties, providing theoretical grounding for the approach.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验涵盖大语言模型的预训练和微调两个场景。预训练阶段在多种规模的语言模型上进行，基线对比包括Adam、AdamW和Muon优化器。微调阶段在指令跟随和下游NLP任务上评估Pion。评估指标包括困惑度（perplexity）、下游任务准确率以及训练损失曲线，同时考察不同模型规模下的性能与训练稳定性。 Experiments cover both LLM pretraining and finetuning. For pretraining, models are trained on standard language modeling benchmarks at various scales, comparing Pion against Adam, AdamW, and Muon as baselines. For finetuning, Pion is evaluated on instruction-following and downstream NLP tasks. Evaluation metrics include perplexity, downstream task accuracy, and training loss curves. The experimental setup examines both performance and training stability across different model sizes.
📊 实验
结果		 在LLM预训练基准上，Pion的困惑度与Adam和Muon相当或更优，训练损失曲线在多种模型规模下均保持稳定。微调任务中，Pion在标准NLP基准上与AdamW持平或略优。实验证明谱保持不会损失模型表达能力——Pion达到相近的最终损失值，同时在训练过程中谱范数行为比加法优化器更为稳定。 Pion achieves competitive or superior perplexity compared to Adam and Muon on LLM pretraining benchmarks, with empirical results showing stable training loss curves across model scales. On finetuning tasks, Pion matches or outperforms AdamW baselines on standard NLP benchmarks. The paper demonstrates that spectrum preservation does not hinder expressiveness — Pion reaches similar final loss values while exhibiting more stable spectral norm behavior throughout training compared to additive optimizers.
🏁 结论 Pion证明了通过正交等价变换保持权重矩阵奇异值谱是一种可行且有竞争力的LLM训练优化策略。它为Adam类加法优化器提供了一种有理论支撑的几何替代方案，在预训练和微调任务中实现了稳定训练且性能相当或更优。 Pion demonstrates that preserving the singular value spectrum of weight matrices via orthogonal equivalence transformations is a viable and competitive optimization strategy for LLM training. It offers a principled geometric alternative to Adam-style additive optimizers, achieving stable training with comparable or better performance on pretraining and finetuning tasks.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Pion将每次权重矩阵的更新视为一次正交等价变换：每步通过在左右两侧乘以正交矩阵来更新权重矩阵W，即 W ← U W Vᵀ，其中U和V是由梯度推导出的正交矩阵。这种方式在保持W奇异值谱完全不变的同时，通过改变奇异向量（几何结构）来实现模型学习。正交矩阵通过梯度信号的斜对称部分经Cayley映射或类似收缩方法计算得到。论文系统研究了如何最优地构建正交更新，并分析了收敛性保证。 Pion treats each weight matrix update as an orthogonal equivalence transformation: at each step, the weight matrix W is updated by multiplying it on the left and right by orthogonal matrices, i.e., W ← U W Vᵀ, where U and V are orthogonal matrices derived from the gradient. This preserves the singular value spectrum of W exactly while still allowing the model to learn by changing the singular vectors (the geometry). The orthogonal matrices are computed via a Cayley-map or similar retraction from the skew-symmetric part of the gradient signal. The paper systematically studies how to best construct these orthogonal updates and analyzes convergence guarantees for the resulting optimizer.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[W \leftarrow U W V^\top\]
💡 Pion的核心更新规则：权重矩阵W通过左乘正交矩阵U、右乘正交矩阵V的转置进行更新，保持W的所有奇异值不变，仅改变其几何方向。 Pion's core update rule: the weight matrix W is updated by multiplying on the left by orthogonal matrix U and on the right by orthogonal matrix Vᵀ, preserving all singular values of W while rotating its geometric orientation.
\[U = \exp(S_U), \quad V = \exp(S_V), \quad S = -S^\top\]
💡 正交矩阵U和V通过对梯度信号的斜对称矩阵S（满足S = -Sᵀ）取矩阵指数得到，确保变换的正交性。 The orthogonal matrices U and V are computed as matrix exponentials of skew-symmetric matrices S (where S = -Sᵀ) derived from the gradient, guaranteeing the transformations remain orthogonal.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着大语言模型规模不断增大，训练稳定性和权重矩阵几何结构变得越来越重要，Pion提供了一种将优化视为几何变换而非加法扰动的全新视角。这项工作为尊重神经网络权重内在结构的优化器开辟了新的设计空间，有望影响未来超越LLM领域的优化器发展。 As LLMs grow larger, training stability and the geometry of learned weight matrices become increasingly critical concerns — Pion offers a novel lens through which to think about optimization as geometric transformation rather than additive perturbation. This work opens a new design space for optimizers that respect the intrinsic structure of neural network weights, potentially influencing future optimizer development beyond LLMs.

Trust the Batch, On- or Off-Policy: Adaptive Policy Optimization for RL Post-Training 信任批量数据，无论同策略还是异策略：用于RL后训练的自适应策略优化

🧑‍🔬 Rasool Fakoor, Murdock Aubry, Nicholas Stranges, Alexander J. Smola

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 用归一化有效样本量替代固定裁剪，实现无需调参的自适应策略优化
A hyperparameter-free adaptive RL objective using normalized effective sample size replaces fixed clipping in policy optimization.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大模型强化学习后训练（如RLHF）非常脆弱，因为策略的更新会不断改变学习所用的数据分布。现有方法（如PPO）使用固定的裁剪超参数来控制信任域和异策略问题，但每当任务、模型规模或数据分布发生变化时，都需要重新手动调参。在实际训练系统中，训练端和推理端的数值精度和采样细节不同，进一步加剧了这种脆弱性。 Reinforcement learning for large model training (e.g., LLM post-training) is fragile because the policy constantly shifts the data distribution it learns from. Existing methods like PPO use fixed clipping hyperparameters to manage trust-region and off-policy concerns, but these require tedious retuning whenever the task, model scale, or data distribution changes. This fragility is compounded in real systems where training and rollout differ in numerical precision and sampling details.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 用策略比率的归一化有效样本量（ESS）替代固定的PPO裁剪，每批次自动自适应调整，无需手动设置。 • 将信任域约束和异策略可靠性约束统一到同一个原则性统计量下，不引入任何新超参数，并移除了多个现有超参数。 • 与硬裁剪不同，对高比率（过期/异策略）的token保留非零学习信号，不丢弃梯度信息。 • 数据过期或分布不匹配时自动收紧正则化，接近同策略时自动放松，全程无需人工干预。 • Replaces fixed PPO-style clipping with a dynamic, data-driven statistic — the normalized effective sample size (ESS) of policy ratios — that adapts automatically each batch. • Unifies the trust-region concern and the off-policy reliability concern under a single, principled statistic with no new hyperparameters. • Retains a nonzero learning signal even on high-ratio (stale/off-policy) tokens, unlike hard clipping which discards gradient information entirely. • Automatically tightens regularization when data is stale or mismatched, and relaxes when data is nearly on-policy — all without manual intervention.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验覆盖了多种场景，包括数学推理任务（使用Qwen、DeepSeek等模型）、代码生成基准测试，以及通用大模型后训练场景。对比基线包括PPO（调优裁剪参数）、GRPO、DAPO、Dr. GRPO等最新RL后训练方法。评估指标包括数学基准（如MATH、AMC、AIME）上的准确率、代码任务的pass@k，以及训练稳定性指标。实验同时覆盖同策略和刻意构造的异策略条件（模拟推理端与训练端的分布不匹配）。 Experiments were conducted across a wide range of settings including math reasoning tasks (using models like Qwen and DeepSeek), code generation benchmarks, and general LLM post-training scenarios. Baselines compared include PPO (with tuned clipping), GRPO, DAPO, Dr. GRPO, and other recent RL post-training methods. Evaluation metrics include task accuracy on math benchmarks (e.g., MATH, AMC, AIME), pass@k on code tasks, and training stability measures. The setup spans both on-policy and deliberately off-policy conditions (simulating distribution mismatch between rollout and training).
📊 实验
结果		 FeynRL在所有基准测试中与调优基线持平或超越，且不引入任何新超参数。在数学推理任务（MATH500、AMC、AIME）上，FeynRL的准确率与最优调参PPO和GRPO变体相当或更优。在代码生成基准上同样与调优竞争方法持平。关键在于，在异策略条件（分布不匹配）下，FeynRL性能下降幅度显著小于固定裁剪方法——在PPO类方法崩溃或需要重新调参的场景中保持稳定学习。该方法移除了现有方法所需的3个以上超参数（ε裁剪、KL系数、比率阈值）。训练稳定性显著提升，梯度尖峰和策略崩溃事件明显减少。 FeynRL matches or exceeds all tuned baselines across benchmarks without any new hyperparameters. On math reasoning (MATH500, AMC, AIME), FeynRL achieves accuracy comparable to or better than best-tuned PPO and GRPO variants. On code generation benchmarks, FeynRL similarly matches tuned competitors. Critically, under off-policy conditions (distribution mismatch), FeynRL degrades significantly less than fixed-clipping methods — maintaining stable learning where PPO-style methods collapse or require retuning. The method also removes 3+ hyperparameters (ε clipping, KL coefficient, ratio threshold) that existing methods require. Training stability is notably improved, with fewer gradient spikes and policy collapse events observed.
🏁 结论 本文证明了策略比率的归一化有效样本量是一个充分且原则性的统计量，可同时处理RL后训练中的信任域约束和异策略问题，从而消除手动调整裁剪超参数的需要。FeynRL为大模型后训练建立了一个新的鲁棒基线，无需重新调参即可泛化到不同任务、模型规模和同/异策略数据场景。 This paper proves that the normalized effective sample size of policy ratios is a sufficient, principled statistic for jointly handling trust-region and off-policy concerns in RL post-training, eliminating the need for manually tuned clipping hyperparameters. FeynRL establishes a new, robust baseline for LLM post-training that generalizes across tasks, model scales, and on/off-policy data regimes without retuning.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

核心思想是用当前批次策略比率的归一化有效样本量（nESS）替代PPO中固定的裁剪超参数。nESS定义为重要性权重的平方和之比，衡量当前批次"有多接近同策略"：所有比率均匀时等于1（完全同策略），比率高度集中时趋近于0（严重异策略）。这一标量同时控制：得分函数权重的上限（即策略梯度的缩放）、异策略KL正则化的强度，以及在批次数据新鲜时保持接近标准同策略梯度更新。最终得到的批次自适应目标函数FeynRL不引入任何新超参数，并自然地移除了若干现有超参数（裁剪阈值、KL系数等）。 The core idea is to replace PPO's fixed clipping hyperparameter with the normalized effective sample size (nESS) computed from the current batch's policy ratios. The nESS — defined as the ratio of the squared sum to the sum of squares of importance weights — measures how "on-policy" the current batch is: it equals 1 when all ratios are uniform (perfectly on-policy) and approaches 0 when ratios are highly concentrated (very off-policy). This single scalar simultaneously caps the score-function weight (scaling the policy gradient), sets the strength of an off-policy KL regularizer, and keeps the update close to a standard on-policy gradient step when the batch is fresh. The result is a batch-adaptive objective called FeynRL that requires no new hyperparameters and naturally removes several existing ones (clipping threshold, KL coefficient, etc.).

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{nESS}(r_{1:N}) = \frac{\left(\sum_{i=1}^{N} r_i\right)^2}{N \cdot \sum_{i=1}^{N} r_i^2}\]
💡 归一化有效样本量（nESS）：重要性权重（策略比率）的平方和之比，衡量当前批次数据有多接近同策略分布。值为1表示完全同策略，趋近0表示严重异策略。 Normalized Effective Sample Size (nESS): ratio of the squared sum to N times the sum of squares of policy ratios. Equals 1 when all ratios are uniform (on-policy), approaches 0 when ratios are highly concentrated (off-policy). This single value drives all adaptive behavior in FeynRL.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{FeynRL}}(\theta) = \text{nESS} \cdot \mathbb{E}\left[r_i(\theta)\, A_i\right] - (1 - \text{nESS}) \cdot D_{\text{KL}}(\pi_\theta \| \pi_{\text{ref}})\]
💡 FeynRL目标函数：nESS同时作为策略梯度项的权重（接近同策略时放大）和KL正则化项的权重（异策略时增强），实现自动平衡。 FeynRL objective: nESS simultaneously weights the policy gradient term (amplified when on-policy) and the KL regularization term (strengthened when off-policy), automatically balancing learning signal vs. stability without any fixed hyperparameters.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

超参数敏感性是大规模部署RL进行LLM后训练最棘手的实际障碍之一，一个无需重新调参即可自适应的方法大幅降低了这一门槛。通过将信任域约束和异策略问题统一到单一可解释统计量下，本文既提供了实用工具，也为理解策略优化稳定性提供了更清晰的理论视角。 Hyperparameter sensitivity is one of the most painful practical barriers to deploying RL for LLM post-training at scale; a method that self-adapts without retuning dramatically lowers this barrier. By unifying trust-region and off-policy concerns under a single interpretable statistic, this work provides both a practical tool and a cleaner theoretical lens for understanding policy optimization stability.

AlphaGRPO: Unlocking Self-Reflective Multimodal Generation in UMMs via Decompositional Verifiable Reward AlphaGRPO：通过分解式可验证奖励解锁统一多模态模型的自反思多模态生成

🎯 用强化学习让多模态模型自我反思并生成更高质量的图像
AlphaGRPO applies GRPO reinforcement learning to unified multimodal models via a decomposed verifiable reward for better image generation and self-correction.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 统一多模态模型（UMMs）在处理复杂用户指令时表现不佳，也无法自动检测和纠正自身输出中的错误。将强化学习（RL）应用于这类模型非常困难，因为多模态生成任务缺乏稳定、细粒度的奖励信号。现有的整体标量奖励方法过于粗糙，难以为真实场景的生成任务提供可靠的监督。 Unified Multimodal Models (UMMs) that handle both text and image generation struggle with complex user instructions and cannot autonomously detect or fix errors in their own outputs. Applying reinforcement learning (RL) to these models is difficult because multimodal generation lacks stable, fine-grained reward signals. Existing holistic scalar reward approaches are too coarse to provide reliable supervision for real-world generation tasks.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **无冷启动直接施加GRPO**：直接将组相对策略优化应用于AR-扩散统一多模态模型，无需预热式监督微调阶段。 • **分解式可验证奖励（DVReward）**：用LLM将复杂提示分解为原子化、可二值验证的语义与质量子问题，再由通用MLLM逐项打分，奖励信号可解释且稳定可靠。 • **涌现的自反思能力**：训练后的模型能够自主诊断生成图像中的语义偏差并迭代修正，这一能力并非显式训练所得，属于涌现行为。 • **GRPO on AR-Diffusion UMMs without cold-start**: Directly applies Group Relative Policy Optimization to autoregressive-diffusion unified models, eliminating the need for a warm-up supervised fine-tuning stage. • **Decompositional Verifiable Reward (DVReward)**: An LLM decomposes complex prompts into atomic, binary-verifiable semantic and quality sub-questions, then a general MLLM scores each — yielding interpretable and reliable reward signals. • **Emergent self-reflective capabilities**: The trained model autonomously learns to diagnose misalignments in its own generated images and iteratively refine them, a capability not explicitly trained.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在四个文生图基准上评估AlphaGRPO：GenEval、TIIF-Bench、DPG-Bench和WISE，以及图像编辑基准GEdit（注意：未在编辑任务上做任何针对性训练）。基线包括当前最优的统一多模态模型和专用生成模型。框架基于现有AR-扩散UMM构建，并与基础版本及其他RL调优或监督微调变体对比，以验证GRPO和DVReward各自的贡献。 Experiments evaluate AlphaGRPO on four text-to-image generation benchmarks — GenEval, TIIF-Bench, DPG-Bench, and WISE — along with the GEdit benchmark for image editing tasks (notably without any editing-specific training). Baselines include state-of-the-art UMMs and specialized generation models. The framework is built on top of an existing AR-Diffusion UMM and compared against its base version and other RL-tuned or supervised fine-tuned variants to isolate the contribution of GRPO and DVReward.
📊 实验
结果		 AlphaGRPO在所有四个生成基准上均超越基础UMM：在GenEval上组合生成准确率显著提升；在TIIF-Bench和DPG-Bench上指令遵循保真度得分改善；在WISE上世界知识驱动的生成指标提升。在GEdit图像编辑基准上，模型在未训练任何编辑数据的情况下取得显著增益，证明自反思能力具有强泛化性。各基准上均呈现多个百分点的持续提升，验证DVReward比整体标量奖励基线提供更有效的RL监督信号。 AlphaGRPO achieves robust improvements across all four generation benchmarks over the base UMM: on GenEval it yields notable gains in compositional generation accuracy; on TIIF-Bench and DPG-Bench it improves instruction-following fidelity scores; on WISE it advances world-knowledge-grounded generation metrics. On GEdit (image editing), the model achieves significant gains despite zero training on editing data, demonstrating strong generalization of the self-reflective capability. Specific reported figures from the paper show consistent multi-point percentage improvements across benchmarks, validating that DVReward provides more effective RL supervision than holistic scalar reward baselines.
🏁 结论 AlphaGRPO证明了将GRPO与分解式可验证奖励结合应用于统一多模态模型，能够有效激发自反思推理与高保真生成能力，且无需冷启动。DVReward的设计为复杂多模态生成任务中稳定、可解释的RL监督提供了实用范式。 AlphaGRPO demonstrates that applying GRPO with a decomposed, verifiable reward to unified multimodal models effectively unlocks self-reflective reasoning and high-fidelity generation without cold-start overhead. The DVReward design establishes a practical blueprint for stable, interpretable RL supervision in complex multimodal generation tasks.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AlphaGRPO将GRPO强化学习算法应用于AR-扩散统一多模态模型。GRPO对同一提示采样多组输出，通过组内相对排名计算策略梯度，无需额外的价值网络。为提供稳定的RL监督信号，作者设计了DVReward：给定复杂提示，LLM（如GPT-4）将其分解为一系列原子化是/否问题，覆盖语义正确性和视觉质量；再由强力MLLM对生成图像逐题作答，汇总得分作为奖励。两项任务联合训练：推理式文生图（从模糊提示推断用户真实意图）和自反思细化（检测并纠正生成图像中的错误），均由DVReward监督，无需冷启动初始化。 AlphaGRPO wraps an AR-Diffusion Unified Multimodal Model with the GRPO RL algorithm, which groups multiple sampled outputs and computes relative policy gradients — avoiding the need for a separate value network. To provide stable RL supervision, the authors design DVReward: given a complex user prompt, an LLM (e.g., GPT-4) decomposes it into a list of atomic yes/no questions covering semantic correctness and visual quality. A powerful MLLM then answers each question for the generated image, and the aggregate score becomes the reward.
