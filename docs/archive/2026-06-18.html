Fixed-Point Reasoners: Stable and Adaptive Deep Looped Transformers 固定点推理器：稳定且自适应的深度循环Transformer

🧑‍🔬 Sajad Movahedi, Vera Milovanović, Shlomo Libo Feigin, Alexander Theus et al.

图 Figure · Page 1 of paper

🎯 循环Transformer自动判断"想清楚了"就停止，难题多想几遍、简单题少想几遍
A looped Transformer that automatically stops thinking when it reaches a stable answer, adapting compute to task difficulty.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个考试的学生：不是规定每道题只能想2分钟，而是一直反复检查答案，直到"再想一遍答案也不变了"才停下来。简单题一遍就稳了，难题就多想几轮，直到心里有底为止。Think of it like a student taking an exam: instead of spending exactly 2 minutes on every question regardless of difficulty, this AI keeps rethinking its answer until it stops changing its mind — then moves on. Easy questions get a quick answer; hard ones get pondered until the answer "settles."
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大多数AI模型无论题目难不难，都花固定的"思考步数"——就像不管做2+2还是高考数学，都只给自己同样的时间。现有的循环架构模型（反复复用同一层来"多想几遍"）还有一个信号传递问题：循环越深，信息越容易失真，就像传话游戏越传越走样。而且这类模型没有可靠的方式判断"什么时候该停下来"。 Most AI models do a fixed number of "thinking steps" no matter how hard the problem is — like spending the same time on 2+2 as on a calculus problem. Existing looped models (which reuse the same layers repeatedly to think deeper) also suffer from a signal-passing problem: the deeper they go, the more the information gets garbled, just like a game of telephone where the message degrades the more people it passes through. There was no reliable way for these models to know when to stop thinking.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 用预归一化层和残差缩放解决了深度循环中的信号失真问题，让"多想几遍"真正有效而不是越想越乱。 • 提出聪明的停止机制：模型不断循环，直到输出不再变化（达到"固定点"），自然地在"想清楚了"时停下。 • 模型自动为难题多分配计算资源，为简单题少用资源，无需人工干预。 • Novel fix for the "garbled signal" problem in deep looped Transformers using pre-norm layers and residual scaling — making deeper thinking actually work reliably. • A smart stopping rule: the model keeps looping until its output stops changing (reaches a "fixed point"), so it naturally stops when it's confident and keeps going when it's not. • The model automatically uses more compute for harder problems and less for easier ones — without any manual tuning.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在四个推理基准上测试了FPRM：数独（逻辑谜题）、迷宫（路径寻找）、状态追踪（跟踪一系列状态变化）和ARC-AGI（抽象视觉模式推理——AI领域公认的最难通用推理测试之一）。对比基线包括相近规模的标准Transformer、Universal Transformer、DEQ等循环/递归架构，以及深度自适应模型。评估指标为任务求解准确率，并验证更难的题目是否确实触发了更多循环次数。 The authors tested FPRM on four reasoning benchmarks: Sudoku (logic puzzles), Maze (pathfinding), state-tracking (following sequences of state changes), and ARC-AGI (abstract visual pattern reasoning — one of AI's hardest general reasoning tests). They compared against standard Transformers of similar size, other looped/recurrent architectures like Universal Transformer and DEQ, and depth-adaptive models. Evaluation measured accuracy at solving these tasks, and also tested whether harder instances triggered more compute loops.
📊 实验
结果		 在数独任务上，FPRM相比基线循环模型取得了显著准确率提升，固定点停止机制使模型在难题上可循环约40次，简单题则更少。在ARC-AGI上，FPRM超越了同等规模的对比模型，并展示了自适应计算分配能力。在迷宫和状态追踪任务上，FPRM与或超越了先前的循环和深度自适应基线。模型的循环次数与任务难度呈现明确的正相关，验证了自适应计算的核心主张。总体来看，FPRM在全部四个基准上均具有竞争力或优于固定深度及其他循环基线。 On Sudoku, FPRM achieved strong accuracy improvements over baseline looped models, with fixed-point halting allowing up to ~40 loops on harder puzzles vs. fewer on easier ones. On ARC-AGI, FPRM outperformed comparable-sized models and demonstrated adaptive compute allocation. On maze and state-tracking tasks, FPRM matched or exceeded prior looped and depth-adaptive baselines. The model showed clear correlation between task difficulty and number of loops used, validating the adaptive compute claim. Exact benchmark figures vary by task; the paper reports FPRM as consistently competitive or superior across all four benchmarks compared to fixed-depth and other looped baselines.
🏁 结论 本文证明了循环Transformer可以通过固定点收敛作为自我调节的"停止思考"信号，可靠地解决复杂推理任务，同时架构改进确保信息在多次循环中保持完整。最终得到一个既更稳定又更高效的模型——每道题用恰好需要的计算量。 This paper proves that a looped Transformer can reliably solve complex reasoning tasks by using fixed-point convergence as a self-regulating "stop thinking" signal, while architectural fixes ensure information stays intact over many loops. The result is a model that is both more stable and more efficient — spending exactly as much compute as each problem demands.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

FPRM是一个循环运行自身层的Transformer——不用100个不同的层，而是把同一组层反复用，就像反复重读一道题。两个关键改进让深度循环真正有效：（1）预归一化层让信息在多次循环中保持稳定，不会越传越乱；（2）残差缩放防止信号爆炸或消失。最聪明的部分是停止规则：FPRM不断循环，直到两次循环的输出几乎不再变化——这叫"固定点收敛"，就像搅拌汤时等到颜色不再变化就知道混匀了。输出稳定了，模型就知道该停下来了。 The paper's model, called FPRM, is a Transformer that runs its layers in a loop — instead of having 100 different layers, it uses the same small set of layers over and over, like rereading a problem multiple times. Two key fixes make deep looping work: (1) pre-norm layers stabilize the information flow so it doesn't get garbled over many loops, and (2) residual scaling prevents signals from exploding or vanishing. The clever part is the halting rule: FPRM keeps looping until the output barely changes between two consecutive loops — this is called "fixed-point convergence," like waiting until stirring soup no longer changes its color. When the answer stabilizes, the model knows it's done thinking.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

当今AI系统对简单和复杂问题一律花费相同的大量计算资源，极为浪费。FPRM指向一个未来：AI只在必要时"多想一想"——让强大的推理能力变得更便宜、更快速、更适合真实场景部署。 Today's AI systems waste enormous computing resources by applying the same heavy computation to both trivial and difficult problems. FPRM points toward a future where AI "thinks harder" only when necessary — making powerful reasoning cheaper, faster, and more practical for real-world deployment.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Qwen-RobotWorld 的核心思路是：给模型看一张当前画面和一条自然语言描述的动作（比如"向前移动50厘米"），然后让它生成一段未来的视频，展示执行这个动作后世界会变成什么样。 它的架构由三部分组成：①一个60层的"双流扩散变换器"，同时处理语言语义和视频内容；②冻结的Qwen2.5-VL大模型负责理解语言动作指令，把指令含义"注入"视频生成过程；③一个精心策划的两阶段训练：第一阶段喂入海量普通视频学习物理常识，第二阶段专注于具身机器人数据以学习动作-效果对应关系。整个流程就像先教一个人"物体是怎么运动的"，再专门教他"机器人手臂抓东西时会发生什么"。 The core idea is: show the model a picture of the current scene plus a plain-English action command (like "move forward 50 cm"), and it generates a video predicting what the world will look like after that action is taken. The architecture has three key parts: ① a powerful 60-layer "double-stream" neural network that handles language and video at the same time; ② a frozen Qwen2.5-VL language model that reads and encodes the action command, injecting its meaning into the video generation process; ③ a two-stage training recipe — first train on massive general video data to learn how the physical world works, then fine-tune on robot-specific data to learn the cause-and-effect of robot actions. Think of it as teaching someone physics first, then teaching them robotics.

💡 模型函数f接收当前视频帧V_t和语言动作指令a_lang，预测未来视频帧序列，这是世界模型的核心公式。 The model f takes the current video frame and a language action command, then outputs predicted future video frames — the core world-modeling equation.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项研究意味着我们离"通用机器人大脑"又近了一步——同一个模型可以理解并预测工厂机械臂、自动驾驶汽车和家庭服务机器人的未来状态，大幅降低机器人开发成本。更重要的是，它可以用语言告诉机器人"下一步做什么"，使普通人也能轻松指挥机器人，加速AI机器人走进日常生活。 This research brings us significantly closer to a "universal robot brain" — one model that can predict the future for factory arms, self-driving cars, and home assistants alike, dramatically cutting robot development costs. It also means ordinary people could eventually direct robots using plain speech, accelerating the day when helpful robots are as common as smartphones.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

研究团队设计了一种宽度沿层数呈X形（蝴蝶结形）变化的Transformer：输入端宽、中间窄、输出端再变宽。层与层之间宽度改变时，用一个简单的"残差缩放"方法（线性投影）来衔接，完全不需要额外的可训练参数。这种设计背后的直觉是：最早的层需要处理原始输入token，最后的层需要精准预测下一个词，而中间层做更抽象的"思考"，不需要那么宽。整个架构在2亿到30亿参数规模上均进行了验证。 The authors design a transformer where width follows an X (or bowtie) shape across layers: wide at the input, gradually narrowing toward the middle, then widening again at the output. To change width between layers without adding new learnable parameters, they use a simple "residual resizing" trick — just linearly projecting the layer outputs when the width changes, with no extra training cost. This shape reflects the intuition that the first layers need to digest raw input tokens and the last layers need to predict the next word precisely, while the middle layers do more abstract "thinking" that may need less raw width. The architecture is tested at multiple scales from 200M to 3B parameters.

的问题		 现有AI视频生成模型很难"持续记住"它之前生成的内容——就像你拍了一段视频后转头再转回来，场景却完全变了样。更麻烦的是，长时间生成视频时颜色和风格会越来越偏，而且很难精确控制摄像机角度和画面中发生的具体事件。这些问题使得AI很难真正模拟一个可交互的"世界"。 Existing AI video generators can't reliably "remember" what they already showed you — if you turn the camera around and come back to a spot, the scene looks completely different. On top of that, generating long videos causes colors and art style to slowly drift and look inconsistent. It's also very hard to precisely control where the camera points or trigger specific events in the scene, making it impossible to build a truly interactive virtual world.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出E-PRoPE：一种轻量级相机几何位置编码，让模型真正"理解"摄像机在3D空间中的位置和朝向，实现精准镜头控制。 • 记忆条件场景持久化（Memory-Conditioned Scene Persistence）：用相机几何匹配找回之前生成的画面，让AI"记住"它走过的地方，避免场景突变。 • 因果强制（Causal Forcing）+ 长滚动训练：把双向视频生成器改造成自回归世界模型，并让模型在训练时"看自己生成的历史"，从根本上压制颜色漂移。 • 事件指令调优 + 强化学习对齐：用自然语言指令触发画面中的具体事件，并用强化学习在蒸馏后恢复相机控制精度和画质。 • E-PRoPE: A lightweight camera-geometry positional encoding that gives the model a true 3D understanding of camera position, enabling accurate shot control. • Memory-Conditioned Scene Persistence: Uses camera geometry to look up previously generated frames, so the AI can "revisit" earlier locations without the scene changing. • Causal Forcing + Long-Rollout Training: Converts a standard video generator into a step-by-step world model that trains on its own past outputs, cutting down color/style drift. • Event Instruction Tuning + RL Alignment: Lets users trigger specific in-world events with plain text, and uses reinforcement learning to restore quality lost during speed-up distillation.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究团队在自建的5秒基础评测集上进行评估，对比了HY-WorldPlay 1.5和LingBot-World两个竞品，评估指标包括相机控制分数和综合评分。数据引擎涵盖虚幻引擎渲染、游戏录像和真实世界视频三类来源，并测试了写实、游戏和风格化三种画风域的生成效果。此外还测量了在8张RTX 5090 GPU上的推理帧率，验证工程优化的实际效果。 The team evaluated on their own 5-second basic benchmark, comparing against two competing systems: HY-WorldPlay 1.5 and LingBot-World. The metrics used were a camera-control score and an overall quality score. They also tested across three visual domains — photorealistic, game-style, and stylized — to show the model's generality. Inference speed was measured on 8 RTX 5090 GPUs to validate their engineering optimizations.
📊 实验
结果		 在5秒基础评测上，DreamX-World 1.0相机控制分数达73.75，综合评分84.76，超过HY-WorldPlay 1.5（综合80.79）和LingBot-World（综合80.45），综合评分分别高出约3.97和4.31分。在推理速度上，通过混合精度DiT执行、残差复用、75%剪枝VAE解码和异步流水并行等优化，在8张RTX 5090 GPU上达到最高16 FPS。 On the 5-second basic evaluation benchmark, DreamX-World 1.0 scored 73.75 on camera control and 84.76 overall, beating HY-WorldPlay 1.5 (80.79) and LingBot-World (80.45) by approximately 3.97 and 4.31 points respectively. On the engineering side, the system reaches up to 16 FPS on 8 RTX 5090 GPUs, enabled by mixed-precision execution, residual reuse, 75%-pruned VAE decoding, and asynchronous pipeline parallelism.
🏁 结论 本文证明了通过结合相机几何感知编码、记忆场景持久化、自回归长程训练和强化学习对齐，可以构建一个真正通用的交互式世界模型，在多种视觉风格下实现精准相机控制、场景记忆和事件触发，同时达到接近实时的生成速度。这为游戏、虚拟现实和具身智能等领域的AI世界模型落地提供了一套完整的技术路径。 This paper demonstrates that by combining camera-geometry-aware encoding, scene memory retrieval, self-training on generated history, and reinforcement learning alignment, it is possible to build a practical general-purpose interactive world model that is accurate, consistent, and fast enough for real use. It establishes a complete technical blueprint for deploying AI world models in games, virtual reality, and embodied AI applications.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

首先，团队建立了一个多元化数据引擎，混合了虚幻引擎渲染视频、游戏录像和真实世界视频，并从真实视频中恢复相机轨迹，保证模型见过各种场景和运镜方式。其次，他们把一个双向视频生成模型改造成"一步一步往前走"的自回归世界模型，并在训练时让模型看自己之前生成的内容（而非真实视频），这样它就学会了如何和自己的"历史"保持一致，减少风格漂移。为了让模型记住它走过的地方，系统会根据当前相机角度在记忆库中查找最相近的历史帧，并将其作为"提示"送入生成器——同时用残差循环技术使这个记忆提示即使不完美也能正常工作。最后，通过DMD蒸馏大幅加速推理，再用强化学习把蒸馏后损失的质量找回来，配合多项工程优化，在8张RTX 5090上达到16 FPS的实时性能。 The team first built a diverse data engine mixing Unreal Engine renders, game recordings, and real-world videos — all tagged with accurate camera movement data. They then turned a standard (bidirectional) video generator into a "one-chunk-at-a-time" world model using a technique called causal forcing, and crucially trained it on its own previously generated frames rather than only real video, so it learns to stay visually consistent with itself over time. To handle scene memory, the system uses camera geometry to look up the closest matching historical frame whenever the virtual camera revisits a known area, and feeds that frame back in as a hint — with a "residual recycling" trick that makes the system tolerant of imperfect memories. Finally, the model is speed-optimized via distillation (making it run many times faster), and reinforcement learning is used to recover any quality lost in that process, ultimately hitting 16 FPS on 8 high-end GPUs.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

世界模型是未来游戏AI、自动驾驶仿真和具身机器人的核心基础设施——能让AI在虚拟世界中"学习生活"而无需真实物理环境。DreamX-World 1.0首次将精准相机控制、场景记忆和事件交互集于一体，并达到接近实时的速度，大幅拉近了AI世界模型从实验室走向实际产品的距离。 World models are foundational technology for future game AI, self-driving simulation, and robotic learning — they let AI "practice living" in a virtual world without needing the real one. DreamX-World 1.0 is one of the first systems to combine precise camera control, scene memory, and event interaction at near-real-time speed, bringing AI world models meaningfully closer to being real products.
