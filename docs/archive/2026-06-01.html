LongTraceRL: Learning Long-Context Reasoning from Search Agent Trajectories with Rubric Rewards LongTraceRL：从搜索智能体轨迹中学习长文本推理并结合评分奖励

🧑‍🔬 Nianyi Lin, Jiajie Zhang, Lei Hou, Juanzi Li

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 用"评分奖励"训练AI在超长文本中精准多跳推理
Teaching LLMs to reason over long, distracting documents using search traces and fine-grained rubric rewards.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像培训一个侦探：不仅要让他在满屋子的假线索（而不是几张纸）中找到真正的证据，还要像老师一样检查他的每一步推理是否有理有据——而不是只看最后他猜没猜对凶手。Think of it like training a detective: instead of giving them a tiny folder with obvious clues, you bury the real evidence inside a massive pile of realistic red herrings — and then grade not just whether they named the right suspect, but whether each step of their deduction was backed by actual evidence from the file.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 AI语言模型在阅读超长文档时，经常"找不到重点"——文章越长、干扰信息越多，它越容易迷失。现有训练方法给的"练习题"太简单，干扰文段不够真实，而且只告诉模型最终答案对不对，无法指导它一步步推理的过程。就像让学生做卷子只给对错不给部分分，聪明的学生也很难真正学到推理方法。 Large language models often get lost when reading very long documents full of distracting information — they struggle to find the right clues and connect them step by step. Existing training methods use distractors that are too easy to ignore, and only reward the model for getting the final answer right, with no guidance on whether the reasoning steps in between were any good. This is like grading a math test by only checking the final answer and ignoring all the working-out.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - 🧩 **分层干扰文段**：利用搜索智能体的真实浏览轨迹，构造"高混淆度"（看过但没引用）和"低混淆度"（搜到但没打开）两类干扰文档，比随机抽样的干扰更真实、更难。 - 🏆 **评分奖励（Rubric Reward）**：不只看最终答案，沿着推理链逐个检查关键实体是否被正确引用，像老师批改作文一样给过程打分。 - ✅ **仅对正确答案施加过程奖励**：只对最终答案正确的回答做精细评分，避免模型"钻空子"（奖励黑客），同时区分同样正确答案中推理质量的高低。 - 🧩 **Tiered Distractors**: Uses real search-agent browsing histories to create two levels of tricky fake documents — ones the agent read but ignored, and ones it never even opened — making training far harder and more realistic than random sampling. - 🏆 **Rubric Reward**: Instead of just checking the final answer, it scores each reasoning step by checking whether the model correctly identified the key entities along the reasoning chain — like a teacher grading the working-out, not just the answer. - ✅ **Positive-Only Strategy**: Fine-grained process rewards are only applied when the final answer is already correct, preventing the model from gaming the scoring system while still distinguishing good reasoning from lucky guesses.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究者在三个不同规模的推理大模型（4B到30B参数）上进行实验，评估五个长文本推理基准测试，包括MuSiQue、HotpotQA、2WikiMultihopQA、LongBench等。基线方法涵盖标准监督微调（SFT）、仅有结果奖励的RLVR，以及其他近期长文本推理方法。评估指标主要为F1分和精确匹配（EM）。 Experiments were run on three reasoning LLMs ranging from 4B to 30B parameters, evaluated across five long-context benchmarks including MuSiQue, HotpotQA, 2WikiMultihopQA, and LongBench. Baselines included standard supervised fine-tuning (SFT), outcome-only RLVR, and other recent long-context reasoning approaches. The main metrics were F1 score and exact match (EM).
📊 实验
结果		 LongTraceRL在五个长文本基准上持续超越所有强基线。例如，在MuSiQue上相比仅有结果奖励的RLVR提升约4-6个F1点；在HotpotQA和2WikiMultihopQA上也取得同等或更优的成绩。在7B模型上，LongTraceRL的综合表现超过了参数量更大的30B基线模型，显示出更强的训练效率。所有三种规模的模型均受益于分层干扰和评分奖励的组合，证明了方法的规模普适性。 LongTraceRL consistently outperforms all strong baselines across five long-context benchmarks. For example, on MuSiQue it improves F1 by approximately 4–6 points over outcome-only RLVR; it achieves comparable or better results on HotpotQA and 2WikiMultihopQA. Notably, the 7B model trained with LongTraceRL surpasses 30B baseline models in aggregate performance, demonstrating strong training efficiency. All three model sizes benefit from the combination of tiered distractors and rubric rewards, confirming the method scales well.
🏁 结论 LongTraceRL证明了：通过构造更真实的干扰文档和细粒度的过程奖励，可以显著提升大语言模型在超长文本中的多跳推理能力，且在不同模型规模上均有效。 LongTraceRL demonstrates that combining realistic, search-trajectory-derived distractors with step-level rubric rewards significantly improves LLM multi-hop reasoning over long documents — and this improvement holds consistently across model sizes from 4B to 30B.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

LongTraceRL分两步走：第一步造"高质量难题"。研究者在知识图谱上随机游走，自动生成需要多步推理的问题（比如"A的老师的出生地在哪？"）。然后让一个搜索智能体去网上找答案，把它真正读过却没引用的文章作为"高混淆干扰"，把它搜到但没打开的文章作为"低混淆干扰"，组合成超长、超难的上下文。第二步设计"评分奖励"。训练时，模型每生成一个推理过程，系统就对照标准答案中的关键实体逐一检查：每找到一个关键节点就加分，最终答案错了则不启用过程奖励。这样模型既要答对，推理过程也要言之有据。 LongTraceRL works in two stages. First, it builds super-hard training problems: it walks through a knowledge graph to create multi-hop questions (like "Where was the teacher of person A born?"), then sends a search agent to find the answers online. Documents the agent actually read but chose not to cite become "high-confusability" distractors, and documents it saw in search results but never opened become "low-confusability" distractors — together forming brutally challenging long documents. Second, it trains the model with a rubric reward: for each response with a correct final answer, it checks step-by-step whether the model mentioned the right key entities along the reasoning chain, awarding partial credit like a teacher marking an exam, and only applying this detailed scoring when the final answer is already correct to stop the model from cheating.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[R(y, y^*) = \mathbb{1}[\text{EM}(y, y^*)] \cdot \frac{1}{|E|} \sum_{e \in E} \mathbb{1}[e \in y]\]
💡 评分奖励公式：只有当最终答案完全正确时（EM=1），才对推理过程中每个关键实体e是否出现在回答y中进行计分，所有关键实体的命中率平均后作为最终奖励。 The rubric reward formula: only if the final answer is exactly correct (EM=1) does the system check what fraction of the key reasoning entities (E) the model mentioned in its response — the reward is the average hit rate over all required entities.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

现实世界的AI助手（如法律文书分析、医疗文献检索）需要在海量文本中精确定位并整合多个关键信息点，LongTraceRL让这类任务更加可靠。更重要的是，它提供了一种低成本、自动化的方式来生成高质量训练数据和奖励信号，降低了构建强大长文本推理模型的门槛。 Real-world AI applications — like reading legal contracts, scanning medical literature, or analyzing lengthy reports — demand exactly this kind of precise, multi-step reasoning over long texts, and LongTraceRL makes AI significantly more reliable at it. It also offers a scalable, automated pipeline for generating high-quality training data, which could accelerate progress across many document-heavy AI use cases.

🧑‍🔬 Ruihang Lai, Hao Kang, Haozhan Tang, Akaash R. Parthasarathy et al.

🎯 为AI编程智能体设计的轻量级MoE训练框架，大幅降低智能体操作成本
A compact MoE training framework designed for AI coding agents, cutting agent effort by up to 64%.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个新员工入职时，有些公司给的是一本厚厚的、东拼西凑的老手册（现有框架），有些公司给的是一张简洁清晰的流程图（PithTrain）——聪明的AI助手在后者面前能更快上手、少犯错、省时省力。Imagine giving a new intern either a messy, 1000-page operations manual full of jargon (existing frameworks) or a clean, one-page flowchart (PithTrain). The AI coding "intern" using the clean guide finishes the job in far fewer tries and wastes way less time — same result, much less confusion.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练大型AI模型（尤其是"混合专家"架构）需要复杂的工程框架，这些框架经过多年积累，代码庞大难懂。如今AI编程智能体可以帮助开发和扩展这些框架，但现有框架对智能体极不友好——智能体需要读懂大量复杂代码、反复试错，消耗大量时间和算力。这种"智能体使用成本"此前从未被系统衡量过。 Training large AI models with "Mixture-of-Experts" (MoE) architectures requires complex engineering frameworks built up over years. These frameworks are huge and hard to navigate. Now that AI coding agents could help build and improve these systems, there's a hidden problem: these existing frameworks are so complicated that even AI agents struggle — they waste many back-and-forth attempts and burn through expensive GPU time just to understand and modify the code. Nobody had measured this "agent usability" cost before.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次提出"智能体任务效率"（ATE）这一新指标，衡量AI编程智能体使用训练框架的成本，填补了业界评估盲区。 • 设计并开源了ATE-Bench基准测试，涵盖真实世界训练框架任务，让ATE可量化、可比较。 • 基于四大"原生智能体"设计原则构建PithTrain框架：代码简洁、模块清晰、易于理解和扩展，同时不牺牲训练性能。 • Introduces a brand-new metric called "Agent-Task Efficiency (ATE)" — measuring how hard it is for an AI coding agent to work with a training framework, something no one tracked before. • Releases ATE-Bench, a real-world benchmark of training-framework tasks, making this new metric measurable and comparable. • Builds PithTrain from scratch using four "agent-native" design principles — keeping code compact, modular, and readable — without sacrificing training speed.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究团队在ATE-Bench上测试了PithTrain与主流生产级训练框架（如Megatron-LM等）的对比，使用AI编程智能体执行真实的训练框架任务（如添加新特性、修复bug、扩展架构）。评估指标包括"智能体回合数"（Agent Turns，智能体与代码交互的次数）和"活跃GPU时间"（Active GPU Time，执行任务消耗的GPU计算时间）。同时也测试了吞吐量（tokens/秒），验证PithTrain在保持训练效率的同时是否真的对智能体更友好。 The team tested PithTrain against production-grade training frameworks (like Megatron-LM) on their new ATE-Bench benchmark. AI coding agents were given real training-framework tasks — like adding new features, fixing bugs, or extending model architectures. They measured how many "Agent Turns" (back-and-forth interactions with the code) and how much "Active GPU Time" (GPU compute burned during the task) each framework required. They also measured raw training throughput (tokens per second) to confirm PithTrain doesn't sacrifice speed for simplicity.
📊 实验
结果		 在ATE-Bench上，PithTrain相比生产框架减少了最多62%的智能体回合数（Agent Turns），以及最多64%的活跃GPU时间（Active GPU Time）——意味着智能体完成同样任务所需的交互次数和算力大幅下降。与此同时，PithTrain的训练吞吐量与主流生产框架相当，没有因为追求简洁而损失训练速度。 On ATE-Bench, PithTrain reduced Agent Turns by up to 62% and Active GPU Time by up to 64% compared to production frameworks — meaning AI agents needed far fewer attempts and burned far less compute to complete the same tasks. At the same time, PithTrain's raw training throughput matched that of leading production frameworks, proving you don't have to sacrifice speed to gain agent-friendliness.
🏁 结论 本文证明了在不牺牲训练性能的前提下，可以通过精心设计让训练框架对AI编程智能体更加友好，并且首次提出了衡量这种友好程度的系统性指标和基准。PithTrain和ATE-Bench为未来"智能体辅助AI系统开发"提供了重要的工具和方向。 This paper proves that a training framework can be both high-performance and easy for AI coding agents to work with — you don't have to choose one or the other. By introducing ATE as a metric and ATE-Bench as a benchmark, it gives the community the tools to measure and improve agent-friendliness in AI infrastructure going forward.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

PithTrain从零开始设计，遵循四个"原生智能体"原则：代码尽量简短精炼、模块边界清晰、逻辑透明易追溯、扩展点明确。就像把一本厚重的说明书精简成一张清晰的流程图——智能体不需要翻遍几千行代码就能找到要改的地方。ATE-Bench则收集了真实的训练框架开发任务，用"智能体回合数"和"活跃GPU时间"来量化智能体完成任务的难易程度，从而客观比较不同框架对智能体的友好程度。 PithTrain is built from the ground up following four "agent-native" design principles: keep the codebase compact (fewer lines), make modules clearly separated, keep logic easy to trace, and mark extension points explicitly. Think of it like rewriting a tangled instruction manual into a clean, one-page checklist — an AI coding agent can quickly find what to change without wading through thousands of confusing lines. ATE-Bench gathers real tasks from training-framework development and measures how many back-and-forth agent attempts ("Agent Turns") and how much GPU compute ("Active GPU Time") are needed to finish each task, giving an objective score for agent-friendliness.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{ATE} = f(\text{Agent Turns},\ \text{Active GPU Time})\]
💡 智能体任务效率（ATE）由两个核心维度衡量：智能体与代码交互的回合数，以及完成任务消耗的活跃GPU时间——两者越少，框架对智能体越友好。 Agent-Task Efficiency (ATE) is measured by two dimensions: how many back-and-forth turns an agent needs with the code, and how much GPU time is consumed — lower is better, meaning the framework is easier for agents to use.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着AI编程智能体越来越多地参与AI系统的开发，训练框架是否"智能体友好"将直接影响AI研发的速度和成本。PithTrain和ATE-Bench为行业提供了新的设计思路和评估标准，有助于加速未来AI模型的迭代。 As AI coding agents are increasingly used to help build and improve AI systems, how "agent-friendly" a framework is will directly affect how fast and cheaply AI can be developed. PithTrain and ATE-Bench give the industry a new lens and toolset to design better infrastructure for the age of AI-assisted development.

Lumos-Nexus: Efficient Frequency Bridging with Homogeneous Latent Space for Video Unified Models Lumos-Nexus：面向视频统一模型的高效频率桥接与同质潜空间方法

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 训练轻量、推理高质的推理驱动视频统一生成框架
A smart two-stage framework that trains cheaply but generates high-quality, reasoning-driven videos at inference time.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像请一位实习生先画草图、确定构图和故事意图（便宜又高效），等草图完成后再交给顶级插画师润色成精美成品——既省了顶级插画师的全程工时费，又保证了最终作品的高水准。Think of it like hiring an intern to sketch out a storyboard (cheap and fast), then handing it to a master artist to paint the final masterpiece — you get the creative vision from the intern's sketch AND the stunning visuals from the master, without paying the master artist for all the planning work.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的"既能理解指令又能生成视频"的AI模型，训练时需要把一个超大型视频生成器塞进来一起训练，计算代价极高。结果要么视频画质很差，要么对复杂推理指令的理解不到位。就像要同时又画画又写小说，哪件事都做不好。 Existing AI models that can both "understand" a prompt and generate a video need a very large, expensive generator baked into training — which is slow and costly. 的亮点		 • 两阶段设计：训练时只用小型轻量生成器（省钱省时），推理时偷偷换成强大的预训练大模型，两全其美。 • 统一渐进频率桥接（UPFB）：像接力赛一样，先由推理模块控制大方向（低频语义），再逐步交棒给大模型补充细节（高频画质）。 • 新基准VR-Bench：填补了"推理驱动视频生成"评测的空白，专门测量AI能否把复杂推断意图转化为语义一致的视频内容。 • Two-stage design: train with a small lightweight generator (cheap!), then at inference time silently swap in a powerful pretrained generator for beautiful visuals — best of both worlds. • Unified Progressive Frequency Bridging (UPFB): a clever technique that gradually hands off from coarse reasoning control to fine-grained visual detail, like a relay race between two runners. • New benchmark VR-Bench: the authors also created a brand-new test specifically for measuring whether AI can translate complex inferred intent into matching video content — a gap that previously had no proper benchmark.

They compared against several existing connector-based unified video generation models. The experiments tested both the quality of generated videos and how well the model could follow complex, inference-requiring instructions.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Lumos-Nexus分两个阶段工作。训练阶段，只让一个小型轻量生成器与语言推理模块对接并一起训练，因为生成器很小，所以训练成本很低。关键在于：小型训练生成器和大型高质量预训练生成器共享同一个"潜在空间"（即表示视频帧的数学维度是一样的）。 推理阶段，模型先完成语义推理，再通过UPFB技术逐步把控制权从小生成器交给大生成器。就像先画一幅简单草图（推理模块给出大方向），然后让专业画家在草图上精细上色（大模型补充高质量细节）——整个过程既不失原始意图，又保证了视觉精美。 Lumos-Nexus works in two stages. During training, a small, lightweight video generator is connected to a language reasoning block and trained together — this is affordable because the generator is tiny. The key insight is that both the small training generator and a large high-quality pretrained generator share the same "latent space" (the same mathematical dimension for representing video frames). At inference time, the model runs its reasoning to get semantic guidance, then uses a technique called Unified Progressive Frequency Bridging (UPFB) to gradually hand off control from the small generator to the big one. Think of it as starting with a rough sketch (low-frequency structure from reasoning) and then having a professional artist refine it into a polished painting (high-frequency details from the big generator) — without ever losing the original intent of the sketch.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\hat{z}_t = \text{UPFB}(z_t^{\text{small}}, z_t^{\text{large}}, t)\]
💡 UPFB在每个去噪时间步t，将小生成器的潜变量和大生成器的潜变量按频率逐步混合，生成最终的潜变量表示。 At each denoising step t, UPFB progressively blends the latent codes from the small (reasoning-aligned) generator and the large (high-fidelity) generator across frequency bands to produce the final latent representation.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作让"能推理又能生成高质量视频"的AI助手变得更加实用和经济，不再需要超级计算机来训练。这将加速创意内容生成、影视辅助制作和交互式AI叙事等现实应用的落地。 This work makes it far more practical to build AI video assistants that can reason through complex instructions ("show a person slipping on ice because the floor looks wet") and generate high-quality video — without needing a supercomputer for training. It could accelerate real-world applications in creative content generation, film production assistance, and interactive AI storytelling.
