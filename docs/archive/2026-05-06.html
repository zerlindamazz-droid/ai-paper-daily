Model Spec Midtraining: Improving How Alignment Training Generalizes 模型规范中间训练：改善对齐训练的泛化能力

🧑‍🔬 Chloe Li, Sara Price, Samuel Marks, Jon Kutasov

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 在对齐微调前插入规范中间训练，让模型真正理解价值观而非死记硬背。
Midtraining on synthetic Model Spec documents teaches LLMs intended values, dramatically improving alignment generalization.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 目前主流的对齐微调方法是让模型学习"示范行为"，但这种方式只让模型记住了表面动作，而没有真正理解背后的价值观。当遇到没见过的新情境时，模型很容易"泛化失败"——做出与预期不符的行为，存在安全隐患。 Standard alignment fine-tuning trains models on demonstrations of desired behavior, but this can produce shallow alignment that generalizes poorly to new situations. The demonstration data alone does not fully specify the underlying values or reasoning the model should adopt, so models may learn surface patterns rather than the intended principles.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **创新的中间训练阶段**：在预训练和微调之间插入合成文档训练，让模型在看到行为示范前先"读懂"规范内容。 - **泛化方向可控**：完全相同的微调数据（如奶酪偏好），仅凭替换MSM规范文档，就能引导出截然不同的价值观泛化方向。 - **显著提升安全性**：针对自我保存和目标守护的规范，使智能体失准率大幅下降，优于现有基线方法。 - **规范设计洞察**：揭示了"解释规则背后原因"和"给出具体而非笼统指引"能显著增强泛化效果。 - **Novel midtraining stage**: Inserts a synthetic document training phase between pre-training and fine-tuning, teaching models the *content* of their Model Spec before any behavioral demonstrations. - **Generalization control**: The same fine-tuning data (e.g., cheese preferences) can be steered toward completely different value systems (pro-America vs. pro-affordability) purely by swapping the MSM spec document. - **Safety impact**: MSM with a spec targeting self-preservation and goal-guarding cuts agentic misalignment rates dramatically, outperforming deliberative alignment baselines. - **Spec design insights**: Reveals that explaining *why* rules exist and giving specific (vs. general) guidance produces stronger generalization.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究包含两类实验。第一类是"奶酪偏好"受控探针实验：只对模型进行奶酪偏好的微调，但通过不同MSM规范文档将偏好归因于"亲美国价值观"或"亲实惠价值观"，随后在政治/经济问题上测试泛化方向。第二类是安全性实验：在Qwen3-32B上使用针对自我保存和目标守护的规范进行MSM，评估智能体失准率，并与无MSM基线和"审慎对齐"基线对比。此外还对规范设计因素（如规则解释深度、指引具体程度）进行了消融实验。 The authors designed two types of experiments. First, a controlled "cheese preference" probe: models were fine-tuned only on data expressing cheese preferences, and MSM was applied with specs attributing those preferences to either pro-America or pro-affordability values — then evaluated on unrelated political/economic questions to test generalization direction. Second, a safety-critical agentic misalignment experiment: MSM was applied with a spec addressing self-preservation and goal-guarding, and models were evaluated on an agentic misalignment benchmark (Qwen3-32B). Baselines included standard fine-tuning without MSM and a deliberative alignment approach. The authors also conducted ablations on spec design factors such as rule explanation depth and specificity.
📊 实验
结果		 - Qwen3-32B的智能体失准率从基线**54%降至7%**（MSM方法），而审慎对齐基线仅降至14%，MSM相对降幅约87%。 - 奶酪偏好探针实验证明泛化方向可精确控制：相同微调数据，替换规范后分别产生亲美国和亲实惠的价值观泛化，验证了MSM的可控性。 - 消融实验表明：规范中"解释规则背后原因"以及"提供具体而非笼统指引"，在各评估设置中均能一致带来更强的对齐泛化效果。 - Agentic misalignment rate on Qwen3-32B dropped from **54% (baseline) to 7% with MSM**, versus 14% for the deliberative alignment baseline — a ~87% relative reduction. - The cheese-preference probe demonstrated clean directional control: the same fine-tuning data produced pro-America generalization with one spec and pro-affordability generalization with another, confirming MSM's ability to steer generalization without changing the demonstration data. - Ablations showed that specs which explain the *reasoning behind rules* and provide *specific rather than general* guidance consistently yielded stronger alignment generalization across evaluated settings.
🏁 结论 MSM是一种简单而有效的技术：通过在预训练和微调之间插入规范文档训练，让模型真正理解预期价值观，从而从有限示范数据中正确泛化。本文同时为如何设计模型规范以实现最强对齐泛化提供了可操作的指导原则。 MSM is a simple yet effective technique: by training models on synthetic Model Spec documents between pre-training and fine-tuning, it teaches models the intended values so they generalize correctly from limited demonstration data. This work also provides actionable guidance on how to design Model Specs for maximal alignment generalization.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

MSM在预训练结束后、对齐微调开始前，增加一个专门的训练阶段：让模型阅读大量合成文档，这些文档详细描述了"模型规范"的内容——包括应遵守的价值观、规则，以及这些规则背后的原因。这样，模型在接触行为示范数据之前，就已经"读懂"了自己该往哪个方向泛化。之后进行正常的行为微调时，模型能够以内化的规范为参照，将有限的示例正确推广到新情境。合成文档由程序生成，涵盖多种场景和价值观解释。 MSM introduces a new training stage between pre-training and alignment fine-tuning. In this stage, models are trained on synthetic documents that explicitly discuss the Model Spec — describing the intended values, rules, and the reasoning behind them. This "teaches" the model what it is supposed to generalize toward before it ever sees behavioral demonstration data. When fine-tuning then occurs on demonstrations, the model uses the internalized spec as a prior to correctly extrapolate the intended behavior to novel situations. The spec documents are generated synthetically to cover a range of relevant scenarios and value explanations.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

浅层对齐（模型只模仿示范行为而未内化价值观）是AI安全部署中的核心难题，MSM提供了一种在训练流程层面解决该问题的实用方案。对于任何依赖行为规范部署大语言模型的机构而言，本文的方法和规范设计洞察具有直接的工程参考价值。 Shallow alignment — where models mimic demonstrated behavior without internalizing underlying values — is a core unsolved problem in safe AI deployment. MSM offers a practical, training-pipeline-level solution that dramatically reduces agentic misalignment and gives developers direct control over how models generalize, with implications for any organization deploying LLMs with behavioral specifications.

A Theory of Generalization in Deep Learning 深度学习中的泛化理论

🧑‍🔬 Elon Litman, Gabe Guo

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 用NTK信噪分解统一解释深度学习泛化现象的非渐近理论
A unified non-asymptotic theory explaining deep learning generalization via NTK signal/noise decomposition.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 深度学习模型即使在严重过参数化的情况下也能泛化得很好，但至今没有严格统一的理论解释这一现象。良性过拟合、双重下降、隐式偏置、grokking（延迟泛化）等现象都被单独研究，缺乏共同的理论框架。现有理论往往依赖渐近假设，且无法覆盖训练过程中核函数发生显著变化的特征学习场景。 Deep learning models generalize remarkably well even when massively overparameterized, yet a rigorous, unified theory explaining why remains elusive. Phenomena like benign overfitting, double descent, implicit bias, and grokking have been studied in isolation without a common theoretical framework. Existing theories often rely on asymptotic assumptions or fail to cover the full feature-learning regime where the kernel changes significantly during training.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 通过经验NTK的信号/噪声分解：信号方向上误差快速消散，噪声被困在近零特征值的"隐形水库"中，测试时不可见。 • 首次在NTK算子范数发生O(1)量级变化的完整特征学习场景下证明泛化成立，突破了以往理论的渐近限制。 • 仅凭单次训练运行（无需验证集）推导出精确的总体风险目标，并将其化简为可实用的Adam之上的信噪比预条件器。 • Novel signal/noise decomposition via the empirical NTK: signal directions see rapid error dissipation, while noise is trapped in a near-zero eigenvalue reservoir that is invisible at test time. • Proves generalization holds even when the NTK evolves O(1) in operator norm, extending theory into the full feature-learning regime for the first time. • Derives an exact, computable population-risk objective from a single training run — no validation data needed — and shows it reduces to a practical SNR preconditioner on top of Adam.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在四个不同场景下测试了派生出的SNR预条件器（作为Adam的实用附加模块）：(1) grokking任务，衡量延迟泛化的加速效果；(2) 物理信息神经网络（PINNs），衡量记忆抑制效果；(3) 隐式神经表示，测试噪声抑制能力；(4) 在含噪声人类偏好标签下的DPO微调，评估对齐质量与策略偏差。基线为标准Adam及各领域的任务专用基线。 Experiments tested the derived SNR preconditioner (dubbed a practical optimizer add-on to Adam) across four distinct settings: (1) grokking tasks to measure delayed generalization acceleration, (2) Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) to measure memorization suppression, (3) implicit neural representations to test noise suppression, and (4) Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) fine-tuning under noisy human preference labels to evaluate alignment quality and policy deviation. Baselines were standard Adam and task-specific baselines for each domain.
📊 实验
结果		 • Grokking任务：SNR预条件器使泛化速度比Adam基线加速5倍。 • DPO微调：在含噪偏好标签下提升对齐性能，同时与参考策略的偏差缩小3倍。 • PINNs与隐式神经表示：可量化地抑制记忆伪影（报告了定性和定量改进）。 • 总体风险目标可从单次训练中精确推导，无需验证集，并被证明精确度量信号通道中的噪声。 • Grokking: the SNR preconditioner accelerates generalization by 5× compared to Adam baseline. • DPO fine-tuning: improves alignment performance under noisy preferences while staying 3× closer to the reference policy. • PINNs & implicit neural representations: measurably suppresses memorization artifacts (qualitative and quantitative improvements reported). • The population-risk objective is derived exactly — no validation set needed — and is proven to measure precisely the noise in the signal channel.
🏁 结论 本文建立了首个统一的、非渐近的深度学习泛化理论，通过证明经验NTK如何将信号与噪声分离，并将理论延伸至完整特征学习场景。由此派生的实用优化器附加模块（Adam上的SNR预条件器）在grokking、PINNs、隐式神经表示和DPO微调中均取得了显著的实验增益。 This paper establishes the first unified, non-asymptotic theory of generalization in deep learning by showing how the empirical NTK separates signal from noise, and proves this holds even in the full feature-learning regime. The resulting practical optimizer addition — an SNR preconditioner on Adam — delivers concrete empirical gains across grokking, PINNs, implicit representations, and DPO fine-tuning.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者利用经验神经正切核（NTK）将输出空间分解为信号子空间和正交噪声子空间。在信号通道中，minibatch SGD使连贯的总体信号通过快速线性漂移积累，而对个例的记忆则被压制为缓慢的扩散随机游走。噪声维度因NTK的近零特征值而被"中和"，残余误差无法泄漏到测试预测中。基于此框架，作者从单次训练运行推导出精确的总体风险代理目标，实践中化简为在Adam优化器上叠加一个SNR预条件器，仅增加一个状态向量的开销。 The authors decompose the output space using the empirical neural tangent kernel (NTK) into a signal subspace and a vast orthogonal noise subspace. In the signal channel, minibatch SGD causes coherent population signal to accumulate via fast linear drift, while memorization of idiosyncratic patterns is suppressed to a slow diffusive random walk. Noise dimensions are neutralized because the kernel's near-zero eigenvalues prevent residual error from escaping into test-time predictions. This framework is then used to derive an exact population-risk proxy from a single training run, which in practice becomes an SNR-based preconditioner layered on top of the Adam optimizer, adding only one extra state vector.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本文首次在统一理论框架下解释了深度学习中多个神秘现象，包括良性过拟合、双重下降、grokking和隐式偏置，具有重要的理论意义。更重要的是，由理论推导出的SNR预条件器是一种低成本、架构无关的优化器改进，可直接惠及大语言模型微调、科学机器学习和神经表示等实际应用场景。 This work offers the first principled, unified explanation for a wide range of mysterious deep learning phenomena — benign overfitting, double descent, grokking, and implicit bias — under a single theoretical roof. Beyond theory, the derived SNR preconditioner is a cheap, architecture-agnostic optimizer improvement that immediately benefits practitioners in LLM fine-tuning, scientific ML, and neural representations.

🎯 多流跨模态Transformer驱动的通用灵巧操作机器人策略
RLDX-1 beats frontier VLAs on dexterous manipulation via a multi-stream cross-modal transformer.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型虽然从预训练大模型继承了强大的场景理解和语言泛化能力，但在需要运动感知、基于记忆的决策和物理感知的复杂现实任务中表现欠佳。目前缺乏一种能够统一整合这些异构能力、并可实际部署的机器人策略架构。 Existing Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models inherit strong scene understanding and language generalization from pre-trained VLMs, but they fall short on complex real-world manipulation tasks that demand motion awareness, memory-driven decision making, and physical sensing. There is no unified architecture that seamlessly integrates these heterogeneous capabilities into a single deployable robotic policy.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 多流动作Transformer（MSAT）：为视觉、语言、本体感知、触觉等异构模态设计独立流，通过跨模态联合自注意力实现深度跨模态推理。 • 系统级协同设计：架构创新与数据合成（覆盖稀有操作场景）、类人操作学习流程、实时推理优化三位一体。 • 在高自由度人形机器人控制上显著超越π₀.₅和GR00T N1.6等前沿模型，尤其在多样化功能需求下表现突出。 • Multi-Stream Action Transformer (MSAT): processes heterogeneous modalities (vision, language, proprioception, tactile, etc.) through dedicated modality-specific streams with cross-modal joint self-attention, enabling deep inter-modal reasoning. • System-level co-design: combines architecture with data synthesis for rare manipulation scenarios, human-like manipulation learning procedures, and inference optimizations for real-time deployment. • Demonstrated superior control of high-DoF humanoid robots under diverse functional demands, significantly outperforming frontier models like π₀.₅ and GR00T N1.6.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

RLDX-1基于多流动作Transformer（MSAT）架构，将RGB图像、语言指令、本体感知信号、触觉数据等各类输入模态分配给各自独立的编码流。这些流通过跨模态联合自注意力机制进行融合，在保留各模态特有结构的同时捕捉细粒度的跨模态交互。在此架构之上，系统还引入了针对稀有或高难度操作场景的训练数据合成、旨在激发类人操作行为的专项学习课程，以及满足实时延迟要求的推理优化策略。 RLDX-1 is built on the Multi-Stream Action Transformer (MSAT), which assigns each input modality (e.g., RGB images, language instructions, proprioceptive signals, tactile data) to its own dedicated encoding stream. These streams are then fused via cross-modal joint self-attention, allowing the model to capture fine-grained interactions across modalities without losing modality-specific structure. On top of this architecture, the system incorporates targeted training data synthesis for rare or difficult manipulation scenarios, specialized learning curricula designed to elicit human-like manipulation behaviors, and deployment-time inference optimizations to meet real-time latency requirements.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{MSAT}(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_M) = \text{CrossModalAttention}(\text{Stream}_1(x_1), \text{Stream}_2(x_2), \ldots, \text{Stream}_M(x_M))\]
💡 多流动作Transformer（MSAT）将M种异构模态输入各自送入独立的模态流编码后，再通过跨模态注意力机制统一融合，输出联合表示。 The Multi-Stream Action Transformer encodes each of M heterogeneous modality inputs through its own dedicated stream, then fuses all streams via cross-modal attention to produce a joint representation for action prediction.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

弥合语言泛化能力与物理灵巧性之间的鸿沟是具身智能领域的核心挑战，RLDX-1在人形机器人任务上对前沿模型实现两倍提升，给出了具体可行的架构方案。公开的技术报告和系统级设计经验为更广泛的机器人与VLA研究社区提供了可复现的参考蓝图。 Bridging the gap between language-grounded generalization and physical dexterity is one of the central challenges in embodied AI; RLDX-1's 2× improvement over frontier models on humanoid tasks signals a concrete architectural solution. The open technical report and system-level design insights provide a reproducible blueprint for the broader robotics and VLA research community.

的问题		 在多轮交互任务中训练大语言模型智能体时，强化学习经常出现训练崩溃的问题。根本原因是策略模型会不断生成低信息量、重复性的动作，既无法降低不确定性，也无法推进任务进展，白白浪费了大量采样资源。现有的稳定性技术（如精细化功劳分配和轨迹过滤）只能部分缓解问题。 Multi-turn reinforcement learning for LLM agents frequently collapses during training because policies generate low-information, repetitive actions that waste rollouts without reducing uncertainty or advancing the task. Existing stabilization techniques like credit assignment and trajectory filtering partially help, but instability from inefficient exploration persists.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • Token级干预：逐token监控不确定性变化幅度，当边际变化低于阈值时触发"思考干预"，强制模型在执行动作前进行更深入的推理。 • Turn级重采样：精准识别探索进展可忽略的单个轮次，并动态对该轮进行重采样，而非丢弃整条轨迹，大幅减少采样浪费。 • 双层统一框架：两种机制跨粒度协同工作，在整个训练过程中维持高效探索，防止策略陷入低奖励、低信息的循环。 • Token-level intervention: monitors marginal uncertainty change token-by-token and triggers a "thinking intervention" when the change drops below a threshold, forcing more deliberate reasoning before committing to an action. • Turn-level resampling: identifies individual turns with negligible exploration progress and dynamically resamples only those turns rather than discarding entire trajectories, greatly reducing wasted rollouts. • Unified uncertainty-aware framework: the two mechanisms work together across granularities to keep exploration efficient throughout training, preventing the policy from getting stuck in low-reward, low-information loops.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

T²PO从两个互补的粒度入手解决问题。在Token层面，它实时追踪模型输出分布的不确定性（熵值）；一旦相邻token间的不确定性边际变化低于预设阈值，就注入"思考干预"提示，引导模型在生成下一个动作token前进行更充分的推理。在Turn层面，每完成一轮交互采样后，T²PO评估该轮次的探索进展（基于不确定性降低量）；探索贡献微乎其微的轮次会被标记并重新采样，而不是作为无效经验进入训练批次。两种机制协同工作，确保训练轨迹中的每个token和每个轮次都携带有意义的信息，从而稳定梯度更新、防止训练崩溃。 T²PO operates at two complementary granularities. At the token level, it tracks the uncertainty (entropy) of the model's output distribution as each token is generated; if the marginal change in uncertainty falls below a preset threshold, it injects a "thinking intervention" prompt to encourage more deliberate reasoning before the next action token. At the turn level, after each interaction turn is sampled, T²PO measures the exploration progress of that turn (based on uncertainty reduction achieved); turns that contribute negligibly are flagged and resampled rather than included in the batch as wasted experience. Together these two mechanisms ensure that every token and every turn in the training rollout carries meaningful information, stabilizing gradient updates and preventing training collapse.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\Delta H_t = H(p_{\theta}(a_t | s_t)) - H(p_{\theta}(a_{t-1} | s_{t-1}))\]
💡 Token级不确定性边际变化：计算相邻token生成步骤间输出分布熵值的差值，当该差值低于阈值时触发思考干预。 Token-level marginal uncertainty change: the difference in output distribution entropy between consecutive token generation steps; when this drops below a threshold, a thinking intervention is triggered.
\[\mathcal{L}_{T^2PO} = \mathcal{L}_{RL} + \lambda \cdot \mathbb{1}[\Delta H_t < \epsilon] \cdot \mathcal{L}_{intervention}\]
💡 T²PO总体训练目标：在标准RL损失基础上，当不确定性变化过小时附加干预损失项，λ为平衡系数，ε为不确定性阈值。 T²PO overall training objective: standard RL loss augmented with an intervention loss term activated when marginal uncertainty change falls below threshold ε, with λ as a balancing coefficient.

训练不稳定和崩溃是阻碍强化学习将大语言模型智能体扩展到复杂长时域任务的核心瓶颈，T²PO提供了一个基于不确定性理论的实用即插即用解决方案。随着智能体AI系统（网络智能体、工具调用LLM、机器人规划器）逐渐成为AI落地的核心，像T²PO这样稳健的多轮RL训练方法将成为该领域不可或缺的基础设施。 Training instability and collapse are among the most critical bottlenecks preventing RL from scaling LLM agents to complex, long-horizon tasks — T²PO offers a practical, plug-in solution grounded in uncertainty theory. As agentic AI systems (web agents, tool-use LLMs, robotic planners) become central to AI deployment, robust multi-turn RL training methods like T²PO will be essential infrastructure for the field.
