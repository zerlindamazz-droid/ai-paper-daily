JoyAI-LLM Flash: Advancing Mid-Scale LLMs with Token Efficiency JoyAI-LLM Flash：以Token效率推进中等规模大语言模型

🧑‍🔬 Aichen Cai, Anmeng Zhang, Anyu Li, Bo Zhang et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 48B参数仅激活2.7B的高效MoE大模型，兼顾推理质量与Token效率
A sparse MoE LLM with 48B params but only 2.7B active, achieving top-tier performance via novel RL and token-efficient reasoning.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练既强大又高效的大语言模型一直是业界难题。大多数前沿模型要么以性能换效率，要么需要极高计算成本。在500亿参数以下的中等规模范围内，如何在不浪费大量推理Token的前提下保持强大性能，尤其困难。 Training large language models that are both highly capable and computationally efficient is a major challenge. Most frontier models either sacrifice performance for efficiency or require massive compute budgets that limit accessibility. In the sub-50B parameter range, achieving strong benchmark results without burning excessive tokens during inference is especially difficult.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **FiberPO算法**：受纤维化理论启发的新型强化学习算法，将信任域约束分解为全局与局部两个层次，实现多尺度稳定的策略优化。 • **极致稀疏架构**：总参数48B，每次前向传播仅激活2.7B，稀疏比例远超同量级主流模型，大幅降低推理成本。 • **思考/非思考模式动态平衡**：在链式推理与直接回答之间智能切换，减少无效Token消耗，提升Token利用效率。 • **训练-推理协同设计**：联合采用密集多Token预测（MTP）与量化感知训练（QAT），在不损失质量的前提下显著提升实际推理吞吐量。 • **FiberPO**: A novel RL algorithm inspired by fibration theory that decomposes trust-region constraints into global and local components, enabling multi-scale stable policy optimization during post-training. • **Extreme Sparsity**: 48B total parameters with only 2.7B activated per forward pass — a sparsity ratio far exceeding comparable industry models, drastically cutting inference cost. • **Thinking/Non-Thinking Mode Balancing**: A deliberate cognitive mode strategy that adaptively switches between chain-of-thought reasoning and direct answering to minimize wasted tokens. • **Training-Inference Co-Design**: Joint use of dense Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) and Quantization-Aware Training (QAT) to boost real-world inference throughput without sacrificing quality.
🧪 实验
内容		 模型在多个标准基准上进行了全面评估，涵盖数学推理（MATH-500、AIME 2024/2025、AMC）、代码生成（HumanEval、LiveCodeBench）、语言理解（MMLU、MMLU-Pro、C-Eval）及通用推理（BBH、ARC-Challenge）。基线模型包括DeepSeek-V3、Qwen3-30B-A3B和Mistral等同量级主流开源与闭源模型。同时对思考模式与非思考模式分别评估，以衡量不同任务下的Token效率表现。 The model was evaluated on a comprehensive suite of standard benchmarks covering math reasoning (MATH-500, AIME 2024/2025, AMC), coding (HumanEval, LiveCodeBench), language understanding (MMLU, MMLU-Pro, C-Eval), and general reasoning (BBH, ARC-Challenge). Baselines include prominent open-source and proprietary models of comparable scale such as DeepSeek-V3, Qwen3-30B-A3B, and Mistral models. Both thinking and non-thinking inference modes were evaluated to assess token efficiency under different task types.
📊 实验
结果		 JoyAI-LLM Flash在多个基准上达到或超越参数量更大模型的表现。在MATH-500上媲美DeepSeek-V3与Qwen3-30B-A3B；在AIME 2024上位列50B以下模型第一梯队；在MMLU上得分超过85%，可与更大稠密模型抗衡。在简单任务上，非思考模式相比链式推理基线平均减少约40–60%的Token消耗。MTP+QAT协同设计在实际部署中相比标准自回归解码实现了显著的吞吐量提升。 JoyAI-LLM Flash achieves competitive or superior results across benchmarks compared to models with significantly more active parameters. On MATH-500, it achieves scores rivaling DeepSeek-V3 and Qwen3-30B-A3B. On AIME 2024, it reaches top-tier performance in the sub-50B class. On MMLU, the model scores above 85%, competitive with much larger dense models. The non-thinking mode produces correct answers using substantially fewer tokens than chain-of-thought baselines — reported reductions of up to 40–60% in average token usage on simpler tasks. The MTP+QAT co-design yields measurable throughput gains in real deployment settings compared to standard autoregressive decoding.
🏁 结论 JoyAI-LLM Flash证明了极致稀疏架构结合多阶段强化学习后训练（尤其是FiberPO）与自适应推理模式，能够在50B以下参数规模上实现媲美更大模型的性能，同时大幅降低推理成本。其开源发布为高效MoE中等规模模型树立了新的基准。 JoyAI-LLM Flash demonstrates that extreme architectural sparsity combined with multi-stage RL post-training (especially FiberPO) and adaptive reasoning modes can yield a mid-scale LLM that rivals much larger models while dramatically reducing inference cost. Its open release sets a strong new baseline for the sub-50B efficient MoE model class.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

JoyAI-LLM Flash基于混合专家（MoE）架构，总参数48B，通过稀疏专家路由每次前向传播仅激活2.7B参数。模型在20万亿Token上完成预训练，随后经历多阶段后训练流程：监督微调（SFT）、直接偏好优化（DPO），以及使用新提出的FiberPO算法进行大规模强化学习。FiberPO将信任域约束分解为全局正则化项和局部逐Token修正项，借鉴纤维化理论数学思想，实现更稳定的策略更新。模型还通过训练动态选择"思考模式"（逐步推理）和"非思考模式"（直接回答），按任务复杂度切换，减少Token浪费。最后联合应用多Token预测（MTP）和量化感知训练（QAT）提升实际推理吞吐。 JoyAI-LLM Flash is built on a Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture with 48B total parameters, activating only 2.7B per forward pass through sparse expert routing. The model is pretrained on 20 trillion tokens and then put through a multi-stage post-training pipeline: supervised fine-tuning (SFT), Direct Preference Optimization (DPO), and large-scale reinforcement learning using the newly proposed FiberPO algorithm. FiberPO stabilizes policy updates by decomposing the trust-region constraint into a global regularization term and local per-token corrections, drawing on mathematical concepts from fibration theory. Token efficiency is further improved by training the model to dynamically select between a "thinking" mode (step-by-step reasoning) and a "non-thinking" mode (direct answers) based on task complexity. Finally, Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) and Quantization-Aware Training (QAT) are jointly applied to improve real-world inference speed.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{FiberPO}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{global}}(\pi_\theta) + \lambda \sum_{t} \mathcal{L}_{\text{local}}(\pi_\theta, t)\]
💡 FiberPO的总损失由全局信任域约束项与所有Token上的局部约束项加权求和组成，λ控制两者平衡，实现多尺度稳定的策略优化。 FiberPO's total loss combines a global trust-region regularization term and a sum of local per-token constraint terms weighted by λ, enabling stable policy updates at both macro and micro scales.
\[r_{\text{sparsity}} = \frac{N_{\text{active}}}{N_{\text{total}}} = \frac{2.7\text{B}}{48\text{B}} \approx 5.6\%\]
💡 稀疏比约为5.6%，即每次前向传播只激活约5.6%的参数，远低于同规模竞品，是实现高效推理的核心设计。 The activation sparsity ratio is ~5.6%, meaning only about 1 in 18 parameters is used per forward pass — far sparser than comparable industry models, making inference dramatically cheaper.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着业界越来越关注在资源受限场景中部署高性能大模型，本文展示了一条通过稀疏架构与Token高效推理实现低成本高性能的可行路径，且完全开源。新提出的FiberPO算法在理论上比现有PPO/GRPO更稳定，具有超越本模型的广泛应用价值。 As the field moves toward deploying capable LLMs in resource-constrained environments, this work shows a practical path to high performance at low inference cost through sparsity and token-efficient reasoning — without sacrificing open accessibility. The novel FiberPO algorithm also offers a theoretically grounded, more stable alternative to existing RL fine-tuning methods like PPO and GRPO, with broad applicability beyond this specific model.

Self-Distilled RLVR 自蒸馏RLVR

🧑‍🔬 Chenxu Yang, Chuanyu Qin, Qingyi Si, Minghui Chen et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 将自蒸馏与RLVR融合，同时获得细粒度更新幅度与可靠更新方向
RLSD combines self-distillation for fine-grained update magnitudes with RLVR for reliable update directions.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 用可验证奖励强化学习（RLVR）训练大模型时，只能得到稀疏的对错信号，粒度太粗。在线自蒸馏（OPSD）虽然能提供逐token的细粒度信号，但依赖"特权信息"（如参考答案），会导致信息泄露和训练不稳定。两种方法单独使用都有明显缺陷，无法同时保证训练稳定性和性能上限。 Training large language models with reinforcement learning from verifiable rewards (RLVR) only gives sparse, coarse signals — right or wrong per response. On-policy self-distillation (OPSD) adds fine-grained token-level signals by using the model itself as a teacher with privileged information (e.g., reference answers), but this causes information leakage and unstable long-term training. Neither approach alone is sufficient for robust, high-ceiling learning.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 精准诊断：首次指出纯OPSD会造成信息泄露与长期训练不稳定，为混合设计提供理论动机。 • 巧妙分工：自蒸馏仅用于计算逐token的更新"幅度"，RLVR负责提供可靠的更新"方向"，分工明确、原则清晰。 • 优势互补：RLSD同时继承RLVR的训练稳定性和自蒸馏的细粒度信号，将收敛上限推向更高。 • Novel diagnosis: formally identifies that pure OPSD causes information leakage and long-term instability, motivating a hybrid design. • Smart role split: self-distillation is used only to compute token-level update *magnitudes* (how much to update), while RLVR provides update *directions* (which way to go) — a clean and principled decomposition. • Best-of-both-worlds: RLSD simultaneously achieves the training stability of RLVR and the fine-grained signal richness of self-distillation, raising the convergence ceiling.
🧪 实验
内容		 在AIME、AMC、MATH500、Minerva Math等多个数学推理基准上评估RLSD，基线包括标准RLVR（如GRPO）、纯OPSD以及多种混合变体。实验涵盖不同规模的模型，并通过长周期训练测试稳定性与收敛上限。同时进行消融实验，分别验证自蒸馏提供"幅度"与RLVR提供"方向"各自的贡献。 The authors evaluated RLSD on multiple mathematical reasoning benchmarks including AIME, AMC, MATH500, and Minerva Math, comparing against strong baselines: standard RLVR (e.g., GRPO), pure OPSD, and hybrid variants. They used models of varying scales and tested training stability over extended training runs to assess both convergence ceiling and long-term robustness. Ablation studies were conducted to isolate the contribution of each component (magnitude from self-distillation vs. direction from RLVR).
📊 实验
结果		 在所有测试基准上，RLSD均优于纯RLVR和纯OPSD。在AIME上，相比基于GRPO的RLVR，pass@1准确率提升约3–5%；在MATH500上，相比基线RLVR提升约2–4个百分点。与纯OPSD相比，RLSD训练曲线更稳定，因信息泄露导致的长期性能退化问题得到有效抑制，最终收敛上限显著更高。 RLSD consistently outperforms both pure RLVR and OPSD baselines across all tested benchmarks. On AIME, RLSD achieves notable gains over GRPO-based RLVR, with improvements of approximately 3–5% in pass@1 accuracy. On MATH500, RLSD reaches accuracies surpassing baseline RLVR by around 2–4 percentage points. Across all benchmarks, RLSD shows significantly more stable training curves with a higher final convergence ceiling compared to pure OPSD, which degrades over long training horizons due to information leakage.
🏁 结论 RLSD证明了自蒸馏与RLVR并非相互竞争，而应作为互补机制协同工作——前者提供细粒度更新幅度，后者提供可靠更新方向。这一混合设计为高性能、高稳定性的大模型推理训练树立了新标杆。 RLSD proves that self-distillation and RLVR are best used as complementary mechanisms — one for fine-grained update magnitudes, the other for reliable update directions — rather than as competing paradigms. This hybrid design sets a new standard for stable, high-performance LLM reasoning training without sacrificing signal quality.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

RLSD让同一个模型以两种模式运行：普通模式（学生）和拥有参考答案的特权模式（教师）。比较两种模式下逐token的对数概率分布差异，得到细粒度的"更新幅度"信号。与此同时，RLVR根据环境反馈（如最终答案是否正确）决定"更新方向"。两类信号分工协作，既避免了对特权信息的过度依赖，又充分利用了逐token的细粒度监督。 RLSD runs the same model in two modes: a standard mode (student) and a privileged mode with access to reference answers (teacher). The teacher's token-level log-probability distribution is compared with the student's to compute fine-grained policy differences, which determine how large each parameter update should be. Simultaneously, RLVR uses environmental feedback — such as whether the model's final answer is correct — to determine the direction of each update. By combining these two signals, RLSD avoids over-relying on potentially leaked privileged information while still benefiting from dense, token-level supervision.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着RLVR和自蒸馏在大模型后训练中的广泛应用，深入理解二者的缺陷与协同效应对构建更强推理模型至关重要。RLSD提供了一套有原则、可落地的训练方案，可直接推广到各类RLVR框架，对下一代推理大模型的训练范式产生深远影响。 As the LLM community increasingly adopts RLVR and self-distillation for post-training, understanding their failure modes and synergies is critical for building more capable reasoning models. RLSD offers a principled, practical recipe that can be applied to any RLVR framework, directly impacting how next-generation reasoning LLMs are trained.

Agentic-MME: What Agentic Capability Really Brings to Multimodal Intelligence? Agentic-MME：智能体能力究竟为多模态智能带来了什么？

🧑‍🔬 Qianshan Wei, Yishan Yang, Siyi Wang, Jinglin Chen et al.

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 过程验证基准揭示多模态大模型智能体能力的真实短板
A process-verified benchmark exposing true agentic capability gaps in multimodal LLMs.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 多模态大模型正在从"被动观察者"进化为能调用视觉工具、搜索网络的"主动智能体"，但现有评测基准只看最终答案，无法判断模型是否真正调用了工具、用对了工具，或用得高不高效。当前基准与真实世界多步骤、多工具协同任务之间存在巨大鸿沟。 Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) are increasingly being used as autonomous agents that can invoke visual tools and search the web, yet existing benchmarks only check final answers and test visual and search tools in isolation. This means we have no reliable way to tell whether a model actually used its tools correctly, efficiently, or at all. There is a critical gap between what current benchmarks measure and the real-world complexity of agentic, multi-step multimodal problem solving.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **过程级验证**：不止看最终答案，通过2000+人工标注的分步检查点审核每一个中间状态，每道题平均耗费10+人时标注。 - **双轴评估框架（S轴与V轴）**：S轴衡量推理步骤正确性，V轴衡量视觉工具调用的准确性，全面捕捉模型的推理质量与工具使用能力。 - **过度思考指标**：创新性地引入"overthinking"效率指标，量化模型轨迹与人类参考路径的偏差，惩罚冗余步骤。 - **Process-level verification**: Instead of just checking final answers, Agentic-MME audits fine-grained intermediate steps via 2,000+ manually annotated stepwise checkpoints, each costing 10+ person-hours per task. - **Dual-axis evaluation (S-axis & V-axis)**: Tasks are evaluated along two axes — solution correctness steps (S-axis) and visual tool usage correctness (V-axis) — capturing both reasoning quality and tool-use fidelity. - **Overthinking metric**: A novel efficiency measure that quantifies how much a model's trajectory deviates from the concise human reference path, penalizing unnecessary steps.
🧪 实验
内容		 使用Agentic-MME基准对Gemini 2.5 Pro、GPT-4o、Claude 3.5 Sonnet、Qwen-VL等多个主流多模态大模型进行评测。任务覆盖6个领域、3个难度级别（L1-L3），共418道题。评估指标包括S轴/V轴分步得分、最终答案准确率，以及基于人类参考轨迹的"过度思考"效率指标，全面考察模型的过程表现与效率。 The benchmark was used to evaluate a range of state-of-the-art MLLMs including Gemini 2.5 Pro, GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Qwen-VL, and others under the unified agentic framework. Models were tested on 418 tasks spanning 6 domains and 3 difficulty levels (Level-1 to Level-3). Evaluation used both the S-axis and V-axis checkpoints for process-level scoring, the final answer accuracy, and the overthinking efficiency metric, all benchmarked against human reference trajectories.
📊 实验
结果		 最强模型Gemini 2.5 Pro整体准确率仅为56.3%，在最难的Level-3任务上急剧下滑至23.0%。这一巨大的难度落差表明，现有多模态大模型在需要视觉工具与网络搜索协同配合的复杂多步智能体任务上仍有严重不足。所有测试模型在过程正确性和工具使用效率上均与人类参考轨迹存在显著差距。 The best-performing model, Gemini 2.5 Pro, achieves only 56.3% overall accuracy, which drops sharply to 23.0% on the hardest Level-3 tasks. This significant performance gap between easy and hard tasks reveals that current MLLMs struggle substantially with complex, multi-step agentic reasoning that requires coordinating visual tools and web search together. All tested models show pronounced weaknesses in process correctness and tool-use efficiency compared to human reference trajectories.
🏁 结论 Agentic-MME证明，即使是当前最强的多模态大模型，在真实世界智能体任务上仍远未达标——最难任务得分仅23%。该基准建立了严格的过程级评估标准，揭示了仅看最终答案的评测体系所系统性忽视的工具调用、多步推理和效率三大核心短板。 Agentic-MME demonstrates that existing MLLMs, even the best available, are far from mastering real-world agentic multimodal problem solving, with top models scoring only 23% on hard tasks. The benchmark establishes a rigorous, process-verified evaluation standard that exposes critical gaps in tool use, multi-step reasoning, and efficiency that final-answer-only evaluations systematically miss.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Agentic-MME构建了涵盖6个领域（如科学、日常生活、编程等）、3个难度等级的418道真实任务，每道题配备人类参考轨迹。任务运行在统一沙盒环境中，模型可自由调用代码、视觉工具和网络搜索API。人工标注员沿双轴标注分步检查点：S轴追踪每步推理/行动是否正确，V轴追踪视觉工具是否被正确调用。此外，"过度思考"指标通过对比模型与人类轨迹的长度和绕路率来衡量效率，从而不仅评估"答对了没有"，还能评估"怎么答的"。 Agentic-MME constructs a benchmark of 418 real-world tasks across 6 domains (e.g., science, daily life, coding) and 3 difficulty levels, each paired with a human reference trajectory. Each task supports a unified sandboxed execution environment where both code and external APIs (visual tools and web search) can be freely invoked. Human annotators label stepwise checkpoints along two axes: the S-axis tracks whether each reasoning/action step is correct, and the V-axis tracks whether visual tools were invoked and applied properly. An overthinking metric compares model trajectory length and detour rate against the human reference to measure efficiency. This design allows the benchmark to verify not just what answer was reached, but how it was reached.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着多模态大模型在现实中被越来越多地部署为自主智能体，了解其是否真正正确、高效地使用工具——而非碰运气答对——对安全性和可靠性至关重要。Agentic-MME提供了迄今最严格的过程级智能体评估框架，将为构建真正具备能力的多模态智能体指明方向。 As MLLMs are increasingly deployed as autonomous agents in real-world applications, knowing whether they truly use tools correctly and efficiently — not just whether they luck into a right answer — becomes critical for safety and reliability. Agentic-MME provides the field's most rigorous process-level agentic evaluation framework to date, setting a new standard that will guide the development of genuinely capable multimodal agents.

🎯 开源600K多样数据的RL方案超越专有视觉推理模型
An open RL recipe with 600K diverse data beats proprietary VLMs on visual reasoning.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 目前最强的视觉语言模型依赖私有强化学习流程和非公开数据，外界难以复现或研究。究竟需要哪些关键要素——数据规模、多样性、奖励设计——才能让模型在图表、科学、空间理解等多种视觉推理任务上全面表现出色，目前仍不清楚。 State-of-the-art vision-language models (VLMs) with strong visual reasoning capabilities are built on proprietary RL pipelines and non-public data, making them hard to study or reproduce. It remains unclear what specific ingredients — data scale, diversity, and reward design — are actually needed to achieve broad visual reasoning across charts, science, spatial tasks, and open-ended questions.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • Vero-600K：整合59个数据集、覆盖6大视觉任务类别的60万样本开源RL训练集，数据广度在开源研究中极为罕见。 • 任务路由奖励机制：针对多选、数值、开放式等异质答案格式，自动分派对应验证器，统一处理多样化任务。 • 系统消融实验揭示：不同任务类别激发出质地各异的推理模式，彼此迁移效果差——证明广泛数据覆盖（而非单纯扩大规模）才是RL性能提升的核心驱动力。 • Vero-600K: A large-scale, fully open RL training dataset aggregating 600K samples from 59 datasets across 6 broad visual task categories, enabling broad coverage rarely seen in open research. • Task-routed rewards: A reward routing mechanism that handles heterogeneous answer formats (multiple choice, numeric, open-ended, etc.) by dispatching the appropriate verifier per task type. • Systematic ablation study revealing that different task categories elicit qualitatively distinct reasoning patterns that transfer poorly to one another — proving broad data diversity, not just scale, is the key driver of RL performance.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在VeroEval上进行评测，该套件包含30个涵盖图表、科学、空间、数学、文档及开放式类别的视觉推理基准。模型在四个不同基础模型（包括Qwen2.5-VL-7B和Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct等变体）上训练，与主流开源权重VLM及专有思考模型对比。消融实验通过分别在单一任务类别与完整混合数据集上训练，分析数据覆盖对性能的影响。 Vero is evaluated on VeroEval, a newly introduced suite of 30 challenging visual reasoning benchmarks spanning charts, science, spatial, math, document, and open-ended categories. The model is trained on top of four different base models (including Qwen2.5-VL-7B and Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct variants) and compared against leading open-weight VLMs and proprietary thinking models. Ablation studies are conducted by training on individual task categories in isolation versus the full combined dataset to understand data coverage effects.
📊 实验
结果		 Vero在VeroEval 30个基准上对四个基础模型的平均提升幅度为3.7–5.5分。以Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct为起点，Vero在30个基准中的23个上超过Qwen3-VL-8B-Thinking，且未使用任何专有思考数据。在相同基础模型下，Vero-600K在所有任务类别上均优于现有开源RL数据集。 Vero improves over four base models by 3.7–5.5 points on average across the 30 VeroEval benchmarks. Starting from Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct, Vero outperforms Qwen3-VL-8B-Thinking on 23 out of 30 benchmarks — without using any additional proprietary thinking data. When trained from the same base model, Vero-600K consistently outperforms existing open RL datasets across all task categories.
🏁 结论 Vero证明，跨多样视觉任务类别的广泛数据覆盖（而非单纯的数据规模）是基于RL的视觉推理性能提升的首要驱动力，且完全开源的方案可以媲美甚至超越专有VLM。通过公开全部数据、代码和模型，Vero为社区提供了可复现的视觉推理研究基础。 Vero demonstrates that broad data coverage across diverse visual task categories — rather than sheer data scale — is the primary driver of strong RL-based visual reasoning, and that a fully open recipe can match or surpass proprietary VLMs. By releasing all data, code, and models, Vero provides the community with a reproducible foundation for future visual reasoning research.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Vero采用强化学习（类GRPO训练）对视觉语言模型进行训练，使用自建的Vero-600K数据集。该数据集整合了59个已有数据集，覆盖图表、科学、数学、空间推理、文档理解和开放式视觉问答六大类别。任务路由奖励系统根据任务类型和答案格式自动分派对应的验证器（如精确匹配、数值容差、LLM评判），使单一RL训练循环能同时处理所有任务的多样化答案格式，最终训练出通用视觉推理模型。 Vero trains VLMs with reinforcement learning (specifically GRPO-style training) using the newly constructed Vero-600K dataset. The dataset is built by curating and unifying 59 existing datasets across six task categories: charts, science, math, spatial reasoning, document understanding, and open-ended visual QA. A task-routing reward system assigns the appropriate answer verifier (e.g., exact match, numeric tolerance, LLM-as-judge) based on the task type and answer format. This allows a single RL training loop to handle the diverse answer formats found across all task categories simultaneously, producing a generalist visual reasoner.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r(y, y^*) = \text{TaskRouter}(\text{format}(y),\, y^*)\]
💡 任务路由奖励函数：根据答案格式自动选择对应的验证器计算奖励，统一处理异质任务类型。 Task-routed reward function: automatically selects the appropriate verifier based on the answer format to compute the reward, unifying reward computation across heterogeneous task types.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

专有RL流程一直是开源社区复现前沿VLM推理能力的主要障碍，Vero以完全开源可复现的方案正面突破这一壁垒。"任务多样性（而非规模）驱动RL增益"这一发现，将深刻影响未来研究者在训练通用推理模型时对数据策划策略的思考方式。 Proprietary RL pipelines have been the main barrier preventing the open research community from replicating frontier VLM reasoning capabilities — Vero directly dismantles this barrier with a fully open, reproducible recipe. The finding that task diversity (not just scale) drives RL gains reshapes how future researchers should think about data curation for training general-purpose reasoning models.
