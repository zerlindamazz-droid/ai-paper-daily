When Flat Minima Fail: Characterizing INT4 Quantization Collapse After FP32 Convergence 平坦最小值的失效：FP32收敛后INT4量化崩溃的系统性刻画

🧑‍🔬 Marcus Armstrong

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 FP32收敛恰恰触发而非避免INT4量化的灾难性崩溃
FP32 convergence triggers—not prevents—catastrophic INT4 quantization collapse in LLMs.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练后量化（PTQ）默认认为，FP32充分收敛的模型就是"量化友好"的模型。但本文发现，INT4量化质量恰恰在FP32收敛*之后*出现灾难性崩溃。目前学界对这种崩溃发生的时机与机制缺乏系统性认知。 Post-training quantization (PTQ) assumes that once a model is fully trained and converged in FP32, it is ready for quantization. This paper challenges that assumption, showing that INT4 quantization quality actually degrades catastrophically *after* FP32 convergence, not before. The community currently lacks a mechanistic understanding of when and why this collapse occurs.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次对Pythia-160m全部154个训练检查点进行系统量化鲁棒性审计，揭示了FP32指标完全无法感知的三阶段分歧结构。 • 精确定位触发时机：INT4崩溃始于FP32困惑度收敛时，而非学习率开始衰减时，提供了更细粒度的预测信号。 • 通过峰度测量排除了权重异常值累积的机制假说，并证明INT8全程免疫，将根因锁定在INT4仅16级量化网格的粗粒度特性上。 • First systematic audit of quantization robustness across all 154 public training checkpoints of Pythia-160m, revealing a three-phase divergence structure invisible to FP32 metrics alone. • Identifies the precise onset trigger: INT4 collapse begins when FP32 perplexity converges, not when the learning rate starts decaying — a finer-grained predictor than previously assumed. • Rules out weight outlier accumulation (via kurtosis) as the mechanism, and shows INT8 is fully immune, pinpointing the 16-level grid coarseness of INT4 as the key vulnerability.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验以Pythia-160m全部154个公开训练检查点为主要对象，对每个检查点施加无校准分组INT4探针，以INT4相对FP32的困惑度差距为核心评估指标，INT8作为对照组。分叉实验从预崩溃检查点出发，对余弦延续、SGDR热重启和所提出的OLI调度各运行9次独立实验，通过配对胜率和平均INT4差距降低量进行评估。 The study uses all 154 publicly released training checkpoints of Pythia-160m as the primary experimental substrate. A calibration-free per-group INT4 probe is applied to each checkpoint as the evaluation tool, with INT4 perplexity gap (relative to FP32) as the core metric. INT8 is used as a control condition. A fork experiment at the pre-divergence checkpoint compares cosine continuation, SGDR warm restarts, and the proposed OLI schedule across 9 independent runs per condition, evaluated by pairwise win rates and mean INT4 gap reduction.
📊 实验
结果		 在爆炸性分歧阶段，INT4差距从11%飙升至517%，而FP32困惑度几乎没有变化。INT8在全部三个阶段均完全免疫。分叉实验中，SGDR对余弦调度的配对胜率为0/9（全面加速崩溃）；而所提出的OLI调度平均将INT4差距降低2.2个百分点，统计显著性极强（t = -5.46，p < 0.0001）。 The INT4 gap compounds from 11% to 517% during the explosive divergence phase, while FP32 perplexity barely moves. INT8 remains fully immune across all three phases. In the fork experiment, SGDR achieves 0/9 pairwise wins against cosine (uniformly accelerating divergence), while the proposed OLI schedule reduces the INT4 gap by 2.2 percentage points on average, with strong statistical significance (t = -5.46, p < 0.0001).
🏁 结论 本文证明，FP32收敛并不等同于量化就绪——收敛后的权重更新才是INT4崩溃的直接原因，这是一种标准训练指标完全无法感知的结构性失效模式。所提出的OLI学习率调度提供了经统计验证的实用缓解方案，完整的检查点审计为领域提供了可复用的诊断框架。 This paper proves that FP32 convergence is not a proxy for quantization readiness — post-convergence weight updates are the proximate cause of INT4 collapse, a structured failure mode invisible to standard training metrics. The proposed OLI learning rate schedule offers a practical, statistically validated mitigation, and the full checkpoint audit provides a reusable diagnostic framework for the field.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者对Pythia-160m全部154个公开训练检查点应用无校准的分组INT4探针，追踪整个训练过程中INT4与FP32困惑度差距的变化。通过测量权重峰度来检验异常值假说，并对比INT4与INT8的表现以隔离崩溃机制。最后，从预崩溃检查点出发，设计受控分叉实验，对比三种学习率调度方案（余弦延续、SGDR热重启和新提出的振荡锁定OLI），每种方案运行9次独立实验，评估调度设计是否能缓解量化崩溃。 The authors apply a calibration-free per-group INT4 probe to all 154 publicly available Pythia-160m training checkpoints and track the INT4 perplexity gap relative to FP32 perplexity across training. They measure weight kurtosis to test the outlier hypothesis and compare INT4 vs. INT8 behavior to isolate the mechanism. Finally, they run a controlled fork experiment starting from a pre-divergence checkpoint, comparing three learning rate schedules — cosine continuation, SGDR warm restarts, and a newly proposed Oscillatory Lock-In (OLI) — across nine independent runs each to assess whether schedule design can mitigate collapse.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着INT4量化成为大语言模型部署的主流格式，本文揭示了一个关键盲区：标准FP32训练指标无法检测模型何时变得"不可量化"。检查点审计方法和OLI调度为从业者提供了具体工具，无需从头重训即可诊断并部分预防INT4崩溃。 As 4-bit quantization becomes the dominant deployment format for large language models, this work reveals a critical blind spot: standard FP32 training metrics cannot detect when a model is becoming un-quantizable. The checkpoint audit methodology and OLI schedule give practitioners concrete tools to diagnose and partially prevent INT4 collapse without retraining from scratch.

Stability and Generalization in Looped Transformers 循环Transformer的稳定性与泛化性

🧑‍🔬 Asher Labovich

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 不动点框架揭示循环Transformer何时泛化、何时记忆
A fixed-point framework reveals which looped transformer designs generalize vs. memorize at test time.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 循环Transformer通过反复使用同一组权重来处理问题，理论上可以在测试时为更难的问题分配更多计算。但目前不清楚哪些架构设计能让它们真正泛化到新难度的问题，而不是死记硬背训练集的答案。这一缺口限制了循环架构的可靠设计。 Looped transformers reuse the same weights across multiple iterations, promising smarter use of test-time compute by spending more steps on harder problems. However, it's been unclear which architectural choices allow them to genuinely generalize to unseen difficulty rather than just memorizing solutions from training. This gap limits our ability to design reliable, scalable looped systems.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出三轴不动点稳定性框架（可达性、输入依赖性、几何性），系统分析循环Transformer的行为。 • 理论证明：无recall机制的循环网络不动点数量有限（可数），且在任何谱范围内都无法实现强输入依赖性。 • 提出"内部recall"新变体：结合外层归一化后，在推理任务上媲美甚至显著优于标准recall放置方式。 • Introduces a three-axis fixed-point stability framework (reachability, input-dependence, geometry) to systematically analyze looped transformer behavior. • Theoretically proves that looped networks without recall have only countably many fixed points and cannot achieve strong input-dependence regardless of spectral regime. • Proposes "internal recall" — a novel placement of the recall mechanism — which, combined with outer normalization, matches or surpasses standard recall on reasoning benchmarks.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在三个推理任务上训练单层循环Transformer：国际象棋落子预测、数独求解、前缀和计算。对比的架构变体包括无recall、标准recall（外部）和新提出的内部recall，每种变体分别测试有无外层归一化的情况。用任务准确率衡量模型是泛化还是记忆，并将结果与框架的理论预测对照。 The authors train single-layer looped transformers on three reasoning tasks: chess move prediction, sudoku puzzle solving, and prefix-sums computation. They compare architectural variants including no-recall, standard recall (external), and the newly proposed internal recall, each tested with and without outer normalization. Evaluation is based on task accuracy as a proxy for whether the model generalizes or memorizes, and results are compared against the framework's theoretical predictions across all configurations.
📊 实验
结果		 在数独任务上，内部recall+外层归一化的表现显著优于标准recall放置，是所有任务中差距最明显的。在国际象棋和前缀和任务上，内部recall+外层归一化与标准recall相当。三项任务的下游性能均与理论框架预测高度吻合：被预测为具有稳定、输入依赖不动点的架构表现更好；无recall或无归一化的配置则如预期般下降。摘要未直接给出集中的精确准确率数字，但跨架构配置的趋势强力支持该框架。 On sudoku, internal recall with outer normalization substantially outperforms standard recall placement, showing the clearest empirical gap across all tasks. On chess and prefix-sums, internal recall with outer normalization becomes competitive with standard recall. Across all three tasks, downstream performance consistently tracks the framework's theoretical predictions: configurations predicted to have stable, input-dependent fixed points perform better, while those without recall or normalization degrade as expected. Specific accuracy numbers are not centrally reported in the abstract, but qualitative and quantitative trends across architectural configurations strongly support the framework.
🏁 结论 本文通过理论与实验证明，recall机制结合外层归一化是循环Transformer实现泛化（而非记忆）的关键架构配方。所提出的不动点稳定性框架为设计能随测试时计算可靠扩展的循环架构提供了可操作的设计原则。 This paper establishes that recall combined with outer normalization is the key architectural recipe for looped transformers to generalize rather than memorize, validated by both theory and experiments on chess, sudoku, and prefix-sums. The introduced fixed-point stability framework provides actionable design principles for building looped architectures that reliably scale with test-time compute.

链接		 arXiv: 2604.15259v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

论文构建了一个不动点分析框架，从三个稳定性维度评估循环架构：可达性（迭代是否收敛到不动点）、输入依赖性（不动点是否随输入有意义地变化）、几何性（不动点是否局部平滑且支持稳定反向传播）。基于此框架，作者从理论上分析recall连接与外层归一化如何影响这三个维度，并通过在不同架构配置下训练单层循环Transformer来实验验证预测结论。 The paper develops a fixed-point analysis framework that evaluates looped architectures along three stability axes: reachability (does iteration converge to a fixed point?), input-dependence (does the fixed point meaningfully vary with the input?), and geometry (is the fixed point locally smooth and compatible with stable backpropagation?). Using this framework, the authors theoretically characterize how architectural components — specifically recall connections and outer normalization — affect each axis. They then empirically validate predictions by training single-layer looped transformers with various configurations and measuring downstream task performance.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

测试时计算扩展已成为提升AI推理能力的核心策略，理解哪种架构设计能让循环模型真正泛化对整个领域至关重要。本文同时提供了理论基础和实用设计指导，将直接影响未来循环Transformer与隐式深度网络的设计方向。 As test-time compute scaling becomes a central strategy for improving AI reasoning, understanding which architectural choices enable genuine generalization in looped models is critical for the field. This work offers both a theoretical foundation and practical design guidance, directly informing future looped transformer and implicit-depth network design.

Vision-Language Models · 视觉语言模型Token Pruning · Token剪枝Efficiency · 计算效率Neural Architecture Search · 神经架构搜索
VisPCO: Visual Token Pruning Configuration Optimization via Budget-Aware Pareto-Frontier Learning for Vision-Language Models VisPCO：面向视觉语言模型的预算感知帕累托前沿学习视觉Token剪枝配置优化

🧑‍🔬 Huawei Ji, Yuanhao Sun, Yuan Jin, Cheng Deng et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 自动搜索视觉语言模型最优Token剪枝配置的帕累托学习框架
Automatically finds optimal visual token pruning configs for VLMs via Pareto-frontier learning.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 视觉语言模型在处理高分辨率图像或视频时，会产生大量视觉Token，导致计算量呈二次方增长。现有的Token剪枝方法虽能降低计算开销，但其剪枝配置（如在哪些层剪、剪多少）都是人工预设的，无法保证在精度与效率之间达到最优平衡。目前缺乏一种在给定计算预算下自动找到最佳剪枝策略的原则性方法。 Vision-language models (VLMs) process high-resolution images and videos, generating large numbers of visual tokens that cause quadratic growth in computation costs. Existing token pruning methods reduce this cost but rely on manually predefined pruning configurations — such as how many tokens to prune and at which layers — without any guarantee that these configurations are actually optimal in the accuracy-efficiency trade-off. There is no principled, automatic way to find the best pruning strategy given a computational budget.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **帕累托配置优化**：将Token剪枝配置问题重新定义为多目标优化，自动识别精度-效率帕累托前沿上的最优配置，无需人工调参。 • **可微分梯度搜索**：通过连续松弛和直通估计器（Straight-Through Estimator）使离散剪枝决策可微，并借助增广拉格朗日法高效求解。 • **可学习逐层剪枝模式**：引入可学习核函数，发现多步渐进式剪枝能更好地捕捉VLM的层级压缩结构，精度-效率权衡优于单层剪枝方案。 • **Pareto Formulation**: Reframes token pruning configuration as a multi-objective optimization problem, automatically identifying configurations on the accuracy-efficiency Pareto frontier rather than relying on hand-tuned settings. • **Gradient-Based Search**: Uses continuous relaxation and straight-through estimators to make the discrete pruning decisions differentiable, enabling efficient gradient-based optimization via the Augmented Lagrangian method. • **Learnable Layer-wise Patterns**: Introduces learnable kernel functions to discover that multi-step progressive pruning across layers better captures the hierarchical compression structure of VLMs, outperforming single-layer pruning.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在8个视觉基准测试上展开，涵盖图像理解和视频理解任务。通过将搜索到的配置与穷举网格搜索得到的经验帕累托前沿进行对比（作为黄金标准）来评估方法效果。实验还测试了多种剪枝方法（如FastV、SparseVLM等）和多种VLM架构，验证框架的泛化能力。基线包括使用预设剪枝配置的原方法及其他高效VLM方案，评估指标同时涵盖任务精度和计算开销（FLOPs或Token保留比例）。 Experiments were conducted on 8 visual benchmarks covering image understanding and video understanding tasks. The framework was evaluated by comparing discovered configurations against an empirical Pareto frontier obtained via exhaustive grid search (the gold standard). Multiple pruning methods (e.g., FastV, SparseVLM, etc.) and multiple VLM architectures were tested to assess generalizability. Baselines included default/predefined pruning configurations and other efficiency-oriented VLM approaches, with evaluation metrics covering both task accuracy and computational cost (FLOPs or token retention ratio).
📊 实验
结果		 VisPCO在全部8个视觉基准测试上均能有效逼近穷举网格搜索得到的经验帕累托前沿，且搜索成本大幅降低。在相同计算预算下，VisPCO优化的配置相比预设剪枝配置在各基准上均有精度提升。框架发现的多步渐进式剪枝模式在精度-效率权衡上优于单层剪枝基线。该方法在不同剪枝方法和VLM架构上均表现出良好泛化性，在图像和视频理解任务上均取得一致性增益（论文未公布单一最优数字，以帕累托曲线逼近质量为主要量化指标）。 VisPCO closely approximates the empirical Pareto frontier obtained through exhaustive grid search across all 8 benchmarks, achieving this with a fraction of the search cost. Under equivalent computational budgets, VisPCO-optimized configurations outperform default predefined pruning settings, with accuracy improvements reported across benchmarks. Multi-step progressive pruning patterns discovered by VisPCO achieve superior accuracy-efficiency trade-offs compared to single-layer pruning baselines. The framework generalizes across different pruning methods and VLM architectures without architecture-specific tuning, demonstrating consistent gains on both image and video understanding tasks.
🏁 结论 VisPCO证明了视觉Token剪枝配置可以被形式化为帕累托优化问题，并通过梯度搜索自动找到接近最优的精度-效率平衡点，无需人工调参。其核心贡献在于提供了一个原则性、可泛化的框架，取代了临时性的剪枝配置选择，并揭示了多层渐进式剪枝是VLM压缩的本质上更优的策略。 VisPCO proves that visual token pruning configuration can be formulated and solved as a Pareto optimization problem using gradient-based search, automatically finding near-optimal accuracy-efficiency trade-offs without manual tuning. Its key contribution is a principled, generalizable framework that replaces ad-hoc pruning configurations and reveals progressive multi-layer pruning as a fundamentally better compression strategy for VLMs.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

VisPCO将剪枝配置（如每层保留多少Token）视为连续可学习变量，而非固定超参数。通过连续松弛将离散的保留/剪除决策转化为软性可微操作，并用直通估计器在反向传播中传递梯度。整体优化被建模为约束问题——在满足计算预算约束的前提下最大化模型精度——并通过增广拉格朗日法以惩罚项形式处理预算约束。此外，利用可学习核函数对逐层剪枝模式进行建模和发现，揭示了将剪枝分散到多个层（渐进式剪枝）系统性地优于在单层一次性剪除大量Token的方案。 VisPCO treats the pruning configuration (e.g., how many tokens to retain at each layer) as continuous learnable variables instead of fixed hyperparameters. A continuous relaxation turns the discrete keep/prune decisions into soft, differentiable choices, and straight-through estimators allow gradients to flow through during backpropagation. The optimization is framed as a constrained problem — maximize accuracy subject to a computational budget — and solved using the Augmented Lagrangian method, which handles the budget constraint via penalty terms. Additionally, learnable kernel functions are used to model and discover layer-wise pruning patterns, revealing that spreading pruning across multiple layers (progressive pruning) is systematically better than pruning all tokens at once in a single layer.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\min_{\mathbf{c}} \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{c}) \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \text{FLOPs}(\mathbf{c}) \leq B\]
💡 核心优化目标：在满足计算预算B（如FLOPs上限）的约束下，最小化模型在下游任务上的损失，配置向量c决定每层保留多少Token。 Core optimization objective: minimize task loss with respect to pruning configuration c (tokens kept per layer), subject to a computational budget constraint B (e.g., FLOPs limit).
\[\hat{z} = z + \text{sg}(\mathbb{1}[z > 0.5] - z)\]
💡 直通估计器（STE）：前向传播时使用硬阈值二值化决策（保留或剪除），反向传播时梯度直接通过，使离散决策可微。 Straight-Through Estimator (STE): in the forward pass, a hard threshold binarizes the keep/prune decision; in the backward pass, gradients flow through unchanged, making discrete choices differentiable.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着VLM被广泛应用于高分辨率图像和视频场景，计算效率已成为关键瓶颈，本文用自动化的原则性优化取代了繁琐的人工调参过程。VisPCO作为即插即用的配置优化器，可直接提升各类现有VLM压缩技术的效率，对整个视觉语言模型高效部署领域具有广泛的实用价值。 As VLMs are increasingly deployed on high-resolution and video inputs, computational efficiency is a critical bottleneck — this work removes the guesswork from tuning token pruning strategies, replacing it with an automatic and principled optimizer. By generalizing across architectures and pruning methods, VisPCO acts as a plug-in optimizer that can immediately improve the efficiency of a wide class of existing VLM compression techniques.

IG-Search introduces a step-level reward signal based on information gain for training large language models in search-augmented reasoning via reinforcement learning. Existing trajectory-level reward methods fail to differentiate between precise and vague search queries and suffer from near-zero gradients in certain training scenarios. By rewarding each individual search step according to how much useful information it retrieves, IG-Search encourages more targeted and effective querying.

FedIDM is a new Byzantine-robust federated learning method that uses iterative distribution matching to distinguish between honest and malicious client updates.
