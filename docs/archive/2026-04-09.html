Video-MME-v2: Towards the Next Stage in Benchmarks for Comprehensive Video Understanding Video-MME-v2：迈向综合视频理解基准测试的下一阶段

🧑‍🔬 Chaoyou Fu, Haozhi Yuan, Yuhao Dong, Yi-Fan Zhang et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 分层任务+组合评分，精准暴露视频多模态大模型真实短板
A rigorous new video benchmark with hierarchical tasks and group-based scoring to expose real MLLM limitations.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有视频理解基准已趋于饱和，模型在排行榜上得分很高，但实际能力并不匹配。这种"刷榜"现象掩盖了模型真实的视频理解水平。领域亟需一个更严格、更难的评测基准来真实区分模型能力。 Existing video understanding benchmarks are becoming saturated — top models score very high on leaderboards, but this doesn't reflect real-world capability. There is a growing gap between benchmark scores and what models can actually do. The field needs a harder, more rigorous testbed that can meaningfully differentiate strong models from weak ones.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 渐进式三级层级体系：任务从多点视觉信息聚合 → 时序动态建模 → 复杂多模态推理逐层递进，迫使模型展示真实的分层理解能力。\n• 基于组的非线性评估策略：相关问题作为一组整体打分，而非逐题独立计分，碎片化或猜测性正确答案会被惩罚，只有有效推理链支撑的答案才能得分。\n• 严格的人工标注流程：耗时3300人时，12名标注员、50名独立审核员、最多5轮质量审查，确保数据质量权威可信。 • Progressive tri-level hierarchy: tasks scale from basic visual aggregation → temporal dynamics modeling → complex multimodal reasoning, forcing models to demonstrate layered understanding.\n• Group-based non-linear evaluation: instead of scoring each question independently, related questions are scored as a group — partial or guess-based correct answers are penalized, rewarding only coherent reasoning chains.\n• Rigorous human annotation: 3,300 human-hours, 12 annotators, 50 independent reviewers, and up to 5 rounds of quality assurance ensure benchmark integrity.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在Video-MME-v2上评测了一系列当前最先进的视频多模态大模型，包括Gemini-2.5-Pro等闭源模型和多个开源模型。实验设置了有字幕和无字幕两种条件，以检验模型对文本线索的依赖程度。所有模型在三个层级上均有详细测评，并以人类专家表现作为上界参照。 The authors evaluated a wide range of state-of-the-art video multimodal large language models (MLLMs) on Video-MME-v2, including proprietary models like Gemini-2.5-Pro and open-source alternatives. They tested models with and without subtitle inputs to examine reliance on textual cues versus pure visual reasoning. Performance was measured across all three levels of the hierarchy, allowing analysis of where in the reasoning chain models fail. Human expert performance was also measured as an upper-bound reference.
📊 实验
结果		 最优模型Gemini-2.5-Pro的得分仍显著低于人类专家水平，显示出前沿模型与真实能力之间的巨大差距。实验揭示了清晰的层级瓶颈效应：第一层（视觉聚合）和第二层（时序建模）的错误会向上传播，严重拖累第三层（复杂推理）的表现。具备思维链推理能力的模型在有字幕时表现更好，但在纯视觉场景中有时反而下降，说明其对文本线索存在过度依赖。整体来看，所有模型均未接近满分，成功规避了先前基准的饱和问题。 The best-performing model, Gemini-2.5-Pro, still falls substantially short of human expert performance, revealing a large capability gap even for frontier models. A clear hierarchical bottleneck is observed: errors in Level 1 (visual aggregation) and Level 2 (temporal modeling) propagate and compound to severely limit Level 3 (complex reasoning) performance. Thinking-based reasoning models improve with subtitle inputs but sometimes degrade in purely visual settings, indicating over-reliance on textual cues. Specific numerical scores place the best model well below human experts, with the benchmark effectively avoiding the saturation problem seen in prior video benchmarks — no model achieved near-perfect scores.
🏁 结论 Video-MME-v2通过层级任务设计和组合评估机制，严格揭示了当前视频多模态大模型的真实能力缺口，证明现有最优模型距离人类水平的视频理解仍有显著差距。该基准为推动下一代视频AI系统的发展提供了权威、高要求的评测平台。 Video-MME-v2 establishes a rigorous, high-quality benchmark that exposes genuine gaps in current video MLLMs through hierarchical task design and group-based evaluation, proving that today's best models are far from human-level video understanding. It provides the field with a demanding and trustworthy testbed to drive the development of next-generation video AI systems.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Video-MME-v2构建了三层递进评测体系：第一层考察多点视觉信息聚合能力（识别并整合关键视觉线索），第二层考察时序动态建模能力（理解事物随时间的变化），第三层考察复杂多模态推理能力（综合视觉、音频、文本与逻辑）。评分机制上，用"基于组的非线性评估"替代传统的逐题准确率，将相关问题作为组整体评分，防止模型靠猜测拿到碎片化分数。整个数据集经过多轮严格人工审核流程构建，保证每道题的质量与无歧义性。 Video-MME-v2 introduces a three-tiered evaluation hierarchy: the first level tests multi-point visual information aggregation (recognizing and combining key visual cues), the second level tests temporal dynamics modeling (understanding how things change over time), and the third level tests complex multimodal reasoning (integrating visuals, audio, text, and logic). To make scoring more meaningful, the benchmark replaces per-question accuracy with a group-based non-linear evaluation that scores clusters of related questions together, penalizing models that get isolated answers right by guessing while failing the broader reasoning context. The entire dataset was built through a controlled annotation pipeline with multiple rounds of review to ensure every question is high-quality and unambiguous.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[S_{group} = \prod_{i=1}^{N} s_i\]
💡 组合评分策略：一组相关问题的得分为各题得分的乘积，任何一题猜错都会拖累整组得分，防止碎片化猜测。 Group-based non-linear score: the score for a cluster of related questions is the product of individual question scores, so a single wrong answer penalizes the whole group, discouraging guessing.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

基准饱和是AI研究中的严重问题——当所有模型得分趋近满分时，排行榜就失去了衡量真实进步的意义。Video-MME-v2通过更难的任务、更智能的评分机制和严格的数据质量控制，为多模态AI社区提供了长期可用的权威评测资源。其揭示的具体失败模式（视觉聚合错误、文本线索过度依赖）也为研究者提供了明确的模型改进方向。 Benchmark saturation is a serious problem in AI research — when all models score near 100%, leaderboards stop being useful signals for real progress. Video-MME-v2 directly combats this by raising the bar with harder tasks, smarter scoring, and rigorous data quality, making it a valuable long-term resource for the multimodal AI community. Its findings also reveal actionable failure modes (visual aggregation errors, textual over-reliance) that give researchers concrete targets for model improvement.

MegaTrain: Full Precision Training of 100B+ Parameter Large Language Models on a Single GPU MegaTrain：在单张GPU上以全精度训练1000亿参数以上的大型语言模型

🧑‍🔬 Zhengqing Yuan, Hanchi Sun, Lichao Sun, Yanfang Ye

图 Figure · Page 1 of paper

🎯 单张GPU全精度训练千亿参数大模型的高效内存系统
MegaTrain trains 100B+ parameter LLMs at full precision on a single GPU using CPU memory offloading and pipelined execution.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练1000亿参数以上的大语言模型通常需要昂贵的多GPU集群，绝大多数研究者根本负担不起。即使使用DeepSpeed ZeRO-3这类CPU卸载方案，CPU到GPU的带宽瓶颈也会严重拖慢训练速度。在单张GPU上以全精度训练这一规模的模型，此前被认为几乎不可行。 Training large language models with 100B+ parameters typically requires expensive multi-GPU clusters, making it inaccessible to most researchers. Even with CPU offloading strategies like DeepSpeed ZeRO-3, the CPU-GPU bandwidth bottleneck severely limits training throughput. Full-precision training at this scale on a single GPU has been considered practically infeasible.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **以内存为中心的架构**：颠覆传统范式——将CPU主机内存（1.5TB）作为参数和优化器状态的主存储，GPU仅作为临时计算引擎，不再承担持久内存的角色。 • **流水线双缓冲执行引擎**：通过多个CUDA流，同时重叠参数预取、前向/反向计算和梯度卸载三个阶段，GPU始终保持满负荷运行，无需等待数据传输。 • **无状态层模板**：用轻量可复用的层模板替代持久化的自动微分计算图，权重在流入时动态绑定，消除了图元数据开销，同时提升调度灵活性。 • **Memory-centric architecture**: Flips the traditional paradigm — CPU host memory (1.5TB) becomes the primary storage for parameters and optimizer states, while the GPU is treated as a transient compute engine rather than a persistent memory holder. • **Pipelined double-buffered execution**: Overlaps parameter prefetching, forward/backward computation, and gradient offloading across multiple CUDA streams simultaneously, so the GPU never idles waiting for data. • **Stateless layer templates**: Replaces persistent autograd computation graphs with lightweight, reusable layer templates that bind weights dynamically as they stream in — eliminating graph metadata overhead and enabling flexible scheduling.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在配备1.5TB主机内存的单张NVIDIA H200 GPU（80GB显存）及GH200系统上进行了基准测试。主要基线为DeepSpeed ZeRO-3（启用CPU卸载），测试了7B至120B不同规模模型的训练吞吐量（tokens/秒）、最大可训练模型规模，以及长上下文训练能力（最长512k token）。超长上下文实验在GH200上进行，用于验证极端序列长度下的可扩展性。 The authors benchmarked MegaTrain on a single NVIDIA H200 GPU (80GB VRAM) with 1.5TB host memory and a GH200 system. They compared training throughput against DeepSpeed ZeRO-3 with CPU offloading as the primary baseline across model sizes from 7B to 120B parameters. Experiments measured tokens-per-second throughput, maximum trainable model size, and long-context training capability (up to 512k tokens). The 7B long-context experiment was run on a GH200 to test extreme sequence length scalability.
📊 实验
结果		 • MegaTrain在单张H200 GPU（1.5TB主机内存）上成功训练高达**1200亿参数**的模型，此前该规模需要多GPU集群。 • 训练140亿参数模型时，训练吞吐量达到DeepSpeed ZeRO-3（CPU卸载）的**1.84倍**。 • 在单张GH200上实现**70亿参数模型、512k token超长上下文**的训练，展示了极端序列长度的可扩展性。 • 全程保持**全精度（BF16/FP32）**训练，无需量化，不牺牲模型精度。 • MegaTrain successfully trains models up to **120B parameters** on a single H200 GPU with 1.5TB host memory — a scale previously requiring multi-GPU clusters. • Achieves **1.84× the training throughput** of DeepSpeed ZeRO-3 with CPU offloading when training 14B parameter models. • Enables **7B model training with 512k token context** on a single GH200, demonstrating extreme long-context scalability. • Maintains **full precision (BF16/FP32)** training throughout, avoiding quantization-induced accuracy degradation.
🏁 结论 MegaTrain通过重新定义内存层次结构——以CPU内存为主存储、GPU为临时计算引擎——证明了在单张消费级GPU上以全精度训练1000亿参数以上大模型的可行性。其流水线双缓冲与无状态执行模型在规模和吞吐量上均超越了DeepSpeed ZeRO-3等现有CPU卸载方案。 MegaTrain proves that full-precision training of 100B+ parameter LLMs is feasible on a single commodity GPU by rethinking memory hierarchy — treating CPU memory as the primary store and the GPU as a transient compute engine. Its pipelined double-buffering and stateless execution model deliver both scale and throughput improvements over prior CPU-offloading systems like DeepSpeed ZeRO-3.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

MegaTrain将GPU视为无状态的计算加速器：所有模型参数和优化器状态都存储在CPU主机内存中，训练时逐层流式传输到GPU。双缓冲流水线确保在GPU计算当前层时，下一层参数已经在预取，上一层的梯度也在同步卸载回CPU——三者通过独立的CUDA流并行执行。MegaTrain不构建持久化的自动微分计算图，而是使用无状态层模板，在权重流入时动态绑定，大幅减少设备上的持久内存占用。这一设计使单张H200 GPU（80GB显存）搭配1.5TB主机内存，即可以全精度训练最高1200亿参数的模型。 MegaTrain treats the GPU as a stateless compute accelerator: all model parameters and optimizer states live in CPU host memory and are streamed to the GPU layer by layer during training. A double-buffered pipeline ensures that while one layer is being computed on the GPU, the next layer's parameters are already being prefetched and the previous layer's gradients are being offloaded back to CPU — all in parallel via separate CUDA streams. Instead of building a full autograd graph that persists in GPU memory, MegaTrain uses stateless layer templates that are instantiated on-the-fly with dynamically bound weights, drastically reducing persistent device memory usage. This design allows a single H200 GPU (80GB VRAM) paired with 1.5TB of host memory to train models up to 120B parameters in full precision (FP32/BF16).

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Throughput}_{\text{MegaTrain}} = 1.84 \times \text{Throughput}_{\text{ZeRO-3}}\]
💡 MegaTrain在14B模型训练上的吞吐量是DeepSpeed ZeRO-3（CPU卸载模式）的1.84倍，体现了流水线优化的实际收益。 MegaTrain achieves 1.84× the training throughput of DeepSpeed ZeRO-3 with CPU offloading on 14B model training, reflecting the gains from the pipelined double-buffered execution engine.
\[M_{\text{GPU}} = M_{\text{layer}} \times 2 \quad (\text{double buffer})\]
💡 双缓冲策略在GPU上同时保留两层的参数缓冲区，使预取与计算可以重叠进行，GPU显存占用仅需两层参数量而非整个模型。 The double-buffer strategy keeps only two layers' worth of parameters on the GPU at any time — one being computed, one being prefetched — so GPU memory usage is proportional to a single layer, not the full model.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作大幅降低了训练前沿规模大语言模型的硬件门槛，让拥有单张高端GPU的学术实验室和个人研究者也能训练1000亿参数以上的模型。通过系统级创新（而非模型压缩）解决CPU-GPU带宽瓶颈，MegaTrain保全了完整的训练精度，对研究可复现性和模型质量至关重要。 This work dramatically lowers the hardware barrier to training frontier-scale LLMs, putting 100B+ model training within reach of academic labs and individual researchers who own a single high-end GPU. By solving the CPU-GPU bandwidth bottleneck with systems-level innovations rather than model compression, MegaTrain preserves full training fidelity — a critical advantage for research reproducibility and model quality.

StarVLA: A Lego-like Codebase for Vision-Language-Action Model Developing StarVLA：一个乐高式的视觉-语言-动作模型开发代码库

🧑‍🔬 StarVLA Community

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 乐高式开源框架，统一VLA模型开发、训练与评测
A modular, open-source codebase unifying VLA model development, training, and benchmarking for robotics.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 构建能感知、理解语言并执行动作的通用机器人智能体是具身AI的核心难题。目前VLA领域的研究代码库、架构和评测协议各自为营、互不兼容，导致方法之间难以公平比较，复现结果也极为困难。市面上缺乏一个能同时支持基于VLM和基于世界模型的VLA方法的统一开发平台。 Building generalist robot agents that integrate vision, language, and action is a core challenge in embodied AI. However, VLA research is currently fragmented across incompatible codebases, architectures, and evaluation protocols, making it very hard to fairly compare methods or reproduce results. There is no shared platform that supports both VLM-based and world-model-based VLA approaches under a common interface.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 乐高式模块化设计：骨干网络（VLM或世界模型）与动作头可独立替换，支持快速原型开发新型VLA组合。• 统一训练策略：跨机器人形态学习和多模态联合训练在所有支持范式下一致可用，无需针对每种方法单独调参。• 全面基准集成：统一评测接口覆盖LIBERO、SimplerEnv、RoboTwin 2.0、RoboCasa-GR1和BEHAVIOR-1K，打通仿真与真实机器人部署。 • Lego-like modularity: backbone (VLM or world model) and action head can each be swapped independently, enabling rapid prototyping of novel VLA combinations. • Unified training strategies: cross-embodiment learning and multimodal co-training work consistently across all supported paradigms without per-method tuning. • Comprehensive benchmarking: a single evaluation interface covers LIBERO, SimplerEnv, RoboTwin 2.0, RoboCasa-GR1, and BEHAVIOR-1K, bridging simulation and real-robot deployment.
🧪 实验
内容		 StarVLA在五大机器人基准上进行评测：LIBERO（桌面操作）、SimplerEnv（真实机器人泛化）、RoboTwin 2.0（双臂任务）、RoboCasa-GR1（人形机器人家务任务）、BEHAVIOR-1K（多样化家务活动）。基线方法包括OpenVLA、π0、RoboVLMs等已有VLA方法。分别测试了基于VLM（Qwen-VL）和基于世界模型（Cosmos）的骨干网络。评测主要指标为各仿真环境下的任务成功率，并附有真实机器人实验结果。 StarVLA was evaluated across five major robotics benchmarks: LIBERO (tabletop manipulation), SimplerEnv (real-robot generalization), RoboTwin 2.0 (dual-arm tasks), RoboCasa-GR1 (humanoid household tasks), and BEHAVIOR-1K (diverse household activities). Baselines included prior VLA methods such as OpenVLA, π0, and RoboVLMs, among others. Both VLM backbones (Qwen-VL) and world-model backbones (Cosmos) were tested. Evaluation used task success rate as the primary metric across simulation environments, with additional real-robot trials reported.
📊 实验
结果		 在最小数据工程下，StarVLA的单基准训练配方在多个基准上以VLM和世界模型骨干均达到或超越了已有方法。在LIBERO上与OpenVLA、π0等强基线相当或更优；在SimplerEnv和RoboTwin 2.0上同样达到领先或持平水平。摘要未披露全部具体数字，但论文报告在所有五个基准上均达到最优或接近最优表现，基于世界模型（Cosmos）的骨干在需要物理场景理解的任务中表现尤为突出。 Despite using minimal data engineering, StarVLA's single-benchmark training recipes match or surpass prior methods on multiple benchmarks with both VLM and world-model backbones. On LIBERO, StarVLA achieves competitive success rates against strong baselines such as OpenVLA and π0. On SimplerEnv and RoboTwin 2.0, the framework's recipes outperform or match existing methods. Specific numbers are not fully disclosed in the abstract, but the paper reports state-of-the-art or near-state-of-the-art results across all five benchmarks, with the world-model backbone (Cosmos) providing strong performance in tasks requiring physical scene understanding.
🏁 结论 StarVLA证明了一个设计良好的模块化代码库能够统一碎片化的VLA研究生态，在几乎不需要繁琐数据工程的情况下，即可实现公平比较、可复现性，并达到或超越已有最优方法的性能。它是目前最全面的开源VLA框架之一，大幅降低了复现已有工作和原型化新方法的门槛。 StarVLA demonstrates that a well-designed modular codebase can unify fragmented VLA research, enabling fair comparison and reproducibility while matching or surpassing prior state-of-the-art methods with minimal engineering effort. It stands as one of the most comprehensive open-source VLA frameworks, lowering barriers for both reproducing existing work and prototyping new approaches.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

StarVLA采用共享的"骨干网络-动作头"抽象设计，任何支持的骨干（如VLM的Qwen-VL、世界模型的Cosmos）均可与任意动作解码头（如扩散、自回归或流匹配头）自由组合，只需模块替换即可。跨机器人形态学习和多模态联合训练等可复用训练策略在框架层面实现，适用于所有组合，无需重复代码。统一评测接口封装了五大主流仿真基准，并支持真实机器人部署，单一训练配方即可在多个测试平台验证。代码库附带最小数据工程的单基准训练配方，开箱即用便可达到竞争性乃至领先的性能。 StarVLA adopts a shared backbone–action-head abstraction where any supported backbone (e.g., Qwen-VL for VLMs, Cosmos for world models) can be paired with any action-decoding head (e.g., diffusion, autoregressive, or flow-matching heads) by simply swapping modules. Reusable training strategies such as cross-embodiment learning and multimodal co-training are implemented at the framework level and apply to all combinations without code duplication. A unified evaluation interface wraps five major simulation benchmarks and supports real-robot deployment, so a single training recipe can be validated across multiple test beds. The codebase ships with minimal-data-engineering single-benchmark recipes that already achieve competitive or state-of-the-art performance out of the box.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

VLA研究的碎片化一直是具身AI领域科学进步的重大瓶颈，StarVLA这样的共享标准化平台能让领域研究积累叠加，而非每篇新论文都从头造轮子。通过在同一框架下支持VLM和世界模型两种范式，并覆盖真实机器人部署，StarVLA大幅加速了从研究到实用通用机器人的落地进程。 The fragmentation of VLA research has been a major bottleneck slowing scientific progress in embodied AI; a shared, principled platform like StarVLA enables the field to build cumulatively rather than reinventing the wheel with each new paper. By supporting both VLM and world-model paradigms under one roof and covering real-robot deployment, StarVLA accelerates the path from research to practical generalist robots.

PaperOrchestra: A Multi-Agent Framework for Automated AI Research Paper Writing PaperOrchestra：面向自动化AI研究论文写作的多智能体框架

🧑‍🔬 Yiwen Song, Yale Song, Tomas Pfister, Jinsung Yoon

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 多智能体框架自动将原始材料转化为可投稿的完整AI论文。
A multi-agent system that turns raw research materials into submission-ready AI papers with literature reviews and visuals.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 将零散的研究材料（如实验记录、笔记、参考文献）整合成完整的学术论文是科研中极耗时的环节。现有AI写作工具与特定实验流程深度绑定、文献综述质量浅薄，难以适配真实科研需求。此外，目前缺乏标准化的评测基准来客观衡量自动化论文写作系统的好坏。 Writing a research paper requires synthesizing scattered, unstructured materials — experiment logs, notes, references — into a coherent, publication-ready manuscript. Existing AI writing tools are tightly coupled to specific pipelines and produce shallow literature reviews, making them impractical for real-world research workflows. There is also no standardized benchmark to objectively evaluate automated paper writing systems.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **灵活输入**：接受无约束、异构的预写作材料（笔记、图表、实验结果），无需绑定特定实验流程。 - **端到端论文生成**：输出包含完整文献综述、图表和示意图的可投稿LaTeX稿件，而非仅生成文本草稿。 - **新基准PaperWritingBench**：首个从200篇顶级AI会议论文逆向工程构建的标准化评测基准，支持可复现的自动化评估。 - **Flexible input handling**: PaperOrchestra accepts unconstrained, heterogeneous pre-writing materials (notes, figures, results) without requiring a fixed pipeline. - **End-to-end manuscript generation**: Produces submission-ready LaTeX manuscripts including comprehensive literature reviews, plots, and conceptual diagrams — not just text drafts. - **New benchmark PaperWritingBench**: First standardized benchmark reverse-engineered from 200 top-tier AI conference papers, enabling reproducible, automated evaluation of paper writing quality.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者构建了PaperWritingBench基准，通过逆向工程从200篇顶级AI会议论文（如NeurIPS、ICML、ICLR）中提取原始材料作为输入，将生成的稿件与原始论文进行对比。基线模型为现有的自主论文写作系统。评估采用全套自动化指标与人工侧边对比评估（side-by-side），分别衡量文献综述质量和整体稿件质量。 The authors built PaperWritingBench, a new benchmark constructed by reverse-engineering raw materials from 200 top-tier AI conference papers (e.g., from NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR). These raw materials — stripped of the final paper — serve as input, and the generated manuscripts are compared against the original papers. Baselines include existing autonomous paper-writing systems. Evaluation uses a comprehensive suite of automated metrics and side-by-side human evaluations, assessing both literature review quality and overall manuscript quality.
📊 实验
结果		 在人工侧边对比评估中，PaperOrchestra在文献综述质量上相对自主基线的绝对胜率差达 **50%–68%**，在整体稿件质量上达 **14%–38%**。以上结果基于PaperWritingBench的200篇顶级AI会议论文测试集。 In side-by-side human evaluations, PaperOrchestra achieves an absolute win rate margin of **50%–68%** over autonomous baselines in literature review quality, and **14%–38%** in overall manuscript quality. These results are on PaperWritingBench across 200 top-tier AI conference papers.
🏁 结论 PaperOrchestra证明了多智能体框架能够将异构研究材料灵活转化为高质量可投稿论文，在文献综述深度上大幅超越现有自主写作基线。结合PaperWritingBench，本文为AI驱动的科学写作研究奠定了可复现的评测基础。 PaperOrchestra demonstrates that a multi-agent framework can flexibly transform heterogeneous research materials into high-quality, submission-ready manuscripts, substantially outperforming existing autonomous writing baselines — especially in literature review depth. Alongside PaperWritingBench, this work establishes a reproducible foundation for evaluating and advancing AI-driven scientific writing.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

PaperOrchestra采用多智能体架构，不同专属Agent分别负责文献检索与深度综述、图表生成以及LaTeX稿件结构化组装。各Agent之间通过结构化输出相互协作——例如，专属文献Agent对论文进行深度语义检索与综合，而非浅层摘要。系统接受任意形式的预写作材料作为输入，由调度层协调各Agent，最终输出完整的可投稿格式稿件。 PaperOrchestra uses a multi-agent architecture where specialized agents handle distinct subtasks: literature retrieval and deep synthesis, figure/diagram generation, and structured manuscript assembly in LaTeX. Agents collaborate by passing structured outputs between them, allowing each to focus on what it does best — for example, a dedicated literature agent performs deep semantic search and synthesis across papers rather than superficial summarization. The system takes any form of pre-writing material as input and orchestrates these agents to produce a complete, formatted manuscript ready for submission.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着AI系统越来越深度地参与科学发现流程，从原始研究素材自动生成高质量论文是亟待解决的关键缺口。本文同时提供了实用系统与首个严格基准，推动了全自主AI科研智能体的发展进程。 As AI systems increasingly participate in the scientific discovery pipeline, automating high-quality paper writing from raw research artifacts is a critical missing piece. This work provides both a capable system and the first rigorous benchmark, accelerating progress toward fully autonomous AI researchers.
