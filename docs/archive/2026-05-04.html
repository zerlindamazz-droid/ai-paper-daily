Robot Learning from Human Videos: A Survey 机器人从人类视频中学习：综述

🧑‍🔬 Junyi Ma, Erhang Zhang, Haoran Yang, Ditao Li et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 全面综述机器人从人类视频学习操作技能的方法与进展
A comprehensive survey on teaching robots manipulation skills by learning from human activity videos.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 机器人训练数据难以大规模获取，是具身AI发展的核心瓶颈。人类活动视频在互联网上大量存在，包含丰富的操作技能，但机器人与人类存在外形和动作的巨大差异（embodiment gap），如何让机器人有效地从这些视频中学习是一大难题。 Scaling robot training data is a critical bottleneck for embodied AI: collecting robot demonstrations is expensive, slow, and hard to generalize. Human activity videos, however, exist in massive quantities on the internet and capture a rich diversity of manipulation skills. The challenge is bridging the gap between human and robot embodiment so robots can actually learn from these videos.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - 提出层次化分类体系，将人类视频到机器人技能的迁移分为任务导向、观察导向、动作导向三大路径，逻辑清晰。 - 进行跨类别分析，系统梳理每种迁移路径与不同数据配置（如配对/非配对数据）及学习范式（RL、IL、生成模型）的耦合关系。 - 不仅覆盖技能迁移方法，还深入调研数据基础（数据集和视频生成），并提供数据集发展的大规模统计趋势。 - Introduces a hierarchical taxonomy organizing human-video-to-robot-skill transfer into three pathways: task-oriented, observation-oriented, and action-oriented. - Provides a cross-family analysis linking each transfer pathway to different data configurations (e.g., paired vs. unpaired data) and learning paradigms (e.g., RL, IL, generative models). - Covers both the skill-transfer techniques and the data foundations (datasets + video generation), offering large-scale statistical trends in dataset development over time.
🧪 实验
内容		 本文是综述论文，没有新的实验。作者进行了大规模文献梳理和统计分析，汇总了现有人类视频数据集的发展趋势（数据规模、标注类型、任务覆盖范围），整理了各类方法所用的基准测试，并按仿真/真实环境、任务类型（如抓取、灵巧操作）对方法进行分类统计。 As a survey paper, there are no new experiments conducted. Instead, the authors perform a large-scale literature review and statistical analysis of existing datasets and methods. They compile and analyze trends in human video dataset development and utilization (dataset sizes, annotation types, task coverage), catalog the benchmarks used across the surveyed works, and organize methods by their evaluated environments (simulated vs. real-world) and task types (e.g., pick-and-place, dexterous manipulation).
📊 实验
结果		 作为综述，本文无单一基准数字，但统计了如下趋势：可用人类视频数据集已达数十个（如EPIC-Kitchens、Something-Something、HOI4D、Assembly101），相关论文数量逐年大幅增长。综述覆盖100余种方法，涉及多个大规模数据集，涵盖数百万视频片段和数千小时人类活动视频。 As a survey, no single benchmark result is reported by the authors themselves. However, the paper catalogues statistical trends: the field has seen rapid growth with dozens of human video datasets now available (e.g., EPIC-Kitchens, Something-Something, HOI4D, Assembly101), and the number of papers leveraging human videos for robot learning has grown substantially year-over-year. The survey covers 100+ methods and multiple large-scale datasets spanning millions of video clips and thousands of hours of human activity footage.
🏁 结论 本综述构建了机器人从人类视频学习领域首个系统性、层次化的综合综述，将分散的研究纳入任务导向、观察导向和动作导向三大迁移路径的统一框架。它厘清了该领域的结构体系，点明了具身差异、数据对齐等核心挑战，并为未来可扩展的通用机器人系统研究指明了方向。 This survey establishes the first comprehensive, hierarchically-organized review of robot learning from human videos, unifying scattered literature under a coherent taxonomy of task-, observation-, and action-oriented transfer pathways. It serves as a foundational reference that clarifies the field's structure, highlights key challenges such as the embodiment gap and data alignment, and charts concrete directions for future research toward scalable generalist robot systems.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本综述通过结构化的层次分类体系来组织现有工作。顶层将研究分为"人-机器人技能迁移"和"数据基础"两大板块。在技能迁移部分，区分三条路径：任务导向（用视频定义目标/奖励）、观察导向（对齐人类与机器人视角的视觉表征）和动作导向（直接从人类视频中提取或转换动作）。随后对每条路径与配对/非配对数据及RL、IL、生成模型等学习范式的耦合关系进行交叉分析。数据基础部分独立梳理了基准数据集和视频生成技术。 The survey organizes existing work through a structured, hierarchical taxonomy. At the top level, it separates "human-robot skill transfer" from "data foundations." Within skill transfer, it distinguishes three pathways: task-oriented (using videos to define goals/rewards), observation-oriented (aligning visual representations between human and robot views), and action-oriented (extracting or translating actions directly from human video). The survey then cross-analyzes how each pathway couples with paired or unpaired data and with learning paradigms such as reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and generative-model-based approaches. Data foundations are covered separately, reviewing benchmark datasets and video generation techniques used to augment robot training data.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

机器人演示数据的稀缺是构建通用机器人的最大障碍之一，本综述系统梳理了利用互联网海量人类视频来突破这一瓶颈的全部解决方案空间。统一的分类体系和交叉分析为研究者提供了清晰的路线图，有助于减少这一快速增长领域中的重复工作。 The scarcity of large-scale robot demonstration data is one of the most pressing obstacles to building general-purpose robots; this survey maps out the entire solution space of using the internet's vast human video resources to overcome that bottleneck. By providing a unified taxonomy and cross-analysis, it gives researchers a clear roadmap and reduces duplicated effort across the fast-growing community.

维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型语义理解能力强，但对物理世界动态的细粒度建模较弱。将视频预测、动作生成和世界建模统一到一个可扩展框架中非常困难。此外，推理速度慢和跨机体迁移数据不足，也严重制约了真实世界的部署。 Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models are good at semantic understanding but poor at modeling fine-grained physical world dynamics. Existing robot learning models struggle to unify video prediction, action generation, and world modeling in a single scalable framework. Real-world deployment is further hindered by slow inference speeds and the difficulty of adapting to new robot bodies with limited data.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 统一世界动作模型：单一模型在UniDiffuser框架下同时支持策略学习、世界建模、视频生成、逆动力学和视频-动作联合预测。 • 三流混合变换器（MoT）架构：视觉、动作、文本分别独立建流，实现更强的语言-动作耦合与高效多模态扩展。 • 推理速度提升50倍以上：结合步数缩减、FP8量化、DiT缓存和V2A风格纯动作推理，实现最高11 Hz的实时闭环控制。 • Unified World Action Model: a single model handles policy learning, world modeling, video generation, inverse dynamics, and joint video-action prediction under a UniDiffuser formulation. • Three-stream Mixture-of-Transformers (MoT): separate streams for vision, action, and text enable stronger language-action coupling and efficient multimodal scaling. • 50x+ inference speedup: combines step reduction, FP8 quantization, DiT caching, and V2A-style action-only inference to achieve up to 11 Hz real-time closed-loop control.
🧪 实验
内容		 MotuBrain在RoboTwin 2.0机器人操作基准（干净和随机化两种设置）和WorldArena世界建模评估套件（EWMScore指标）上进行了评估。对比基线包括已有VLA模型及前代模型Motus。团队还测试了仅使用50–100条演示轨迹适配新人形机器人机体的能力，并在多种优化配置下对推理速度进行了对比测试。 MotuBrain was evaluated on RoboTwin 2.0 (a robot manipulation benchmark with clean and randomized settings), and on WorldArena (a world modeling evaluation suite using EWMScore). Baselines include prior VLA models and the predecessor Motus model. The team also tested real-world adaptation to a new humanoid robot embodiment using only 50–100 demonstration trajectories. Inference speed was benchmarked against a naive baseline across multiple optimization configurations.
📊 实验

Achieves the strongest reported EWMScore on WorldArena among compared methods. Adapts to a new humanoid embodiment with only 50–100 trajectories. Inference achieves over 50x speedup vs. naive baseline, reaching up to 11 Hz real-time closed-loop control.
🏁 结论 MotuBrain证明了将世界建模与动作生成统一于单一扩散框架中，可显著提升机器人控制性能、跨机体泛化能力和真实世界可部署性，为可扩展统一世界动作模型树立了新标杆。 MotuBrain demonstrates that unifying world modeling and action generation in a single diffusion-based framework significantly improves robot control performance, generalization across embodiments, and real-world deployability. It establishes a new standard for scalable, unified world action models in robotics.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

MotuBrain基于UniDiffuser框架，通过共享扩散模型对视频帧和动作序列进行联合去噪。架构采用三流混合变换器（MoT），分别为视觉、动作和新增的独立文本流，各模态在共享跨模态注意力的同时高效处理各自信息。统一的跨机体动作表示使模型能在纯视频、任务无关及跨机器人等异构数据上联合训练。在部署端，通过步数缩减、模型编译、FP8量化、DiT缓存和分块闭环执行等激进推理优化，在保证控制质量的同时大幅降低延迟。 MotuBrain builds on a UniDiffuser formulation where video frames and action sequences are jointly denoised by a shared diffusion model. The architecture uses a three-stream Mixture-of-Transformers (MoT) with dedicated streams for vision tokens, action tokens, and a new independent text stream, allowing each modality to be processed efficiently while sharing cross-modal attention. A shared cross-embodiment action representation allows the model to train on heterogeneous data including video-only, task-agnostic, and cross-robot datasets. For deployment, an aggressive inference optimization stack — including step reduction, model compilation, FP8 quantization, DiT caching, and chunked closed-loop execution — reduces latency dramatically while maintaining control quality.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[p(\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{a}) = \int p_\theta(\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{a} | \mathbf{z}) p(\mathbf{z}) d\mathbf{z}\]
💡 UniDiffuser联合建模公式：视频观测x与动作序列a通过共享潜变量z在统一扩散模型下联合建模，实现视频与动作的耦合生成。 The UniDiffuser joint modeling objective: video observations x and action sequences a are jointly modeled via a shared latent variable z, enabling coupled video-and-action generation in a single diffusion model.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本工作弥合了世界模型研究与实际机器人部署之间的鸿沟，证明单一统一模型可同时实现强预测精度与超过11 Hz的实时控制。仅需50–100条轨迹即可跨机体迁移，对面向多样化硬件平台的机器人学界具有重要实践意义。 This work closes the gap between world model research and practical robot deployment by showing that a single unified model can achieve both strong predictive accuracy and real-time control at over 11 Hz. The cross-embodiment generalization with minimal data (50–100 trajectories) is especially impactful for the broader robotics community working with diverse hardware platforms.

Contextual Agentic Memory is a Memo, Not True Memory 上下文智能体记忆是备忘录，而非真正的记忆

🧑‍🔬 Binyan Xu, Xilin Dai, Kehuan Zhang

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 智能体"记忆"本质是查找，无法实现真正的泛化学习
Current agentic memory systems are just lookup tables, not true memory — and this distinction provably limits agent capability.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当前AI智能体使用向量数据库、RAG、草稿本和上下文窗口作为"记忆"，但这些系统本质上只是根据相似度检索已存储的内容，并不能归纳抽象规则或泛化到真正新颖的情境。把"查找"误当"记忆"会带来可证明的能力上限和安全漏洞。 Modern AI agents use vector stores, RAG, scratchpads, and context windows as their "memory." But these systems only retrieve stored examples by similarity — they don't learn abstract rules or generalize to genuinely novel situations. This conflation of lookup with memory creates fundamental, provable limitations in agent capability.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 正式区分"查找"（基于检索）与"真正记忆"（基于权重的泛化），论证两者混淆是可证明后果的范畴错误。• 引入神经科学互补学习系统（CLS）理论，指出生物大脑将海马体快速存储与新皮层慢速权重整合配对，而当前AI智能体仅实现了前半部分。• 识别出可证明的泛化天花板：无论上下文窗口多大或检索质量多高，都无法克服组合新颖性，且检索式记忆在结构上易受持久性记忆投毒攻击。 • Formally distinguishes "lookup" (retrieval-based) from "true memory" (weight-based generalization), arguing their conflation is a category error with provable consequences. • Draws on Complementary Learning Systems (CLS) neuroscience theory to show biological brains pair fast hippocampal storage with slow neocortical consolidation — current AI agents only implement the first half. • Identifies a provable generalization ceiling: no increase in context size or retrieval quality can overcome compositional novelty, and retrieval-based memory is structurally vulnerable to persistent memory poisoning attacks.
🧪 实验
内容		 本文是立场/理论论文，未进行数据集或基线模型的实证实验。作者通过形式化证明、逻辑推导和理论案例分析，论证检索式记忆的泛化天花板。同时对记忆投毒攻击的结构性漏洞进行理论安全分析，并对四种反驳观点进行了结构化回应。 This is a position/theory paper; no empirical experiments with datasets or baselines were conducted. The authors instead present formal proofs, logical derivations, and theoretical case studies to demonstrate the generalization ceiling of retrieval-based memory. They also analyze the structural vulnerability to memory poisoning attacks as a theoretical security argument, and engage with four counterarguments or alternative views in a structured rebuttal.
📊 实验
结果		 作为理论立场论文，本文不报告基准数字或百分比提升。核心形式化结论是可证明的泛化天花板：对于任何组合新颖性任务（需要组合存储样例中从未共现过的规则），无论上下文大小或检索质量如何，检索增强智能体的性能都存在有界上限。安全性结论表明，注入向量存储的投毒条目会在所有未来会话中传播且无自然衰减——这是结构性属性，而非实测概率。 As a theoretical position paper, no benchmark numbers or percentage improvements are reported. The key formal result is a provable generalization ceiling: for any compositionally novel task (one requiring combination of rules never co-occurring in stored examples), retrieval-augmented agents have bounded performance regardless of context size or retrieval quality. The security result shows that poisoned entries injected into a vector store propagate across all future sessions with no natural decay — a structural property, not a measured rate.
🏁 结论 本文证明了基于检索的智能体记忆系统在范畴上并非真正的记忆，在泛化、长期学习和安全性方面存在可证明的硬性上限。核心贡献是以神经科学CLS理论为基础的严格理论框架，呼吁AI智能体将快速检索与慢速权重整合配对，从而模仿生物智能。 This paper proves that retrieval-based agentic memory systems are categorically not memory and face hard, provable limits in generalization, long-term learning, and security. The key contribution is a rigorous theoretical framework — grounded in neuroscience CLS theory — calling for AI agents to pair fast retrieval with slow weight consolidation, mirroring biological intelligence.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文采用理论论证而非实验系统。作者正式定义了基于样例的查找与基于权重的记忆整合之间的区别，并推导出纯检索智能体在泛化能力上的可证明上限。借鉴神经科学的互补学习系统（CLS）框架——描述海马体快速存储情节而新皮层慢速整合抽象知识——并将其映射到AI智能体架构。最终提出一个检索机制与权重整合机制共存的框架，并向系统构建者、基准设计者和记忆研究社区发出具体行动号召。 The authors construct a theoretical argument rather than an empirical system. They formally define the distinction between exemplar-based lookup and weight-based memory consolidation, then derive provable limits on what retrieval-only agents can generalize to. They borrow the Complementary Learning Systems (CLS) framework from neuroscience — which describes how the hippocampus handles fast episode storage while the neocortex slowly consolidates abstract knowledge — and map it onto AI agent architectures. They then propose a co-existence framework where both retrieval-based and weight-consolidation mechanisms operate together, and issue concrete calls to action for system builders, benchmark designers, and the memory research community.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[P(\text{correct} \mid \text{novel composition}) \leq \max_{e \in \mathcal{M}} \text{sim}(q, e)\]
💡 对于组合新颖性任务，检索式智能体的正确率上界等于查询与记忆库中最相似条目的相似度——相似度不足时，无论检索质量多高都无法突破此天花板。 For compositionally novel tasks, a retrieval-based agent's accuracy is bounded above by the maximum similarity between the query and any stored memory entry — if no close match exists, performance is provably limited regardless of retrieval quality.
\[\forall t' > t_{\text{inject}}: \; c_{\text{poison}} \in \text{Retrieve}(q, \mathcal{M}_{t'})\]
💡 一旦投毒内容在时间t被注入记忆库，它将在所有未来时刻t'的检索结果中持续出现，不会自然衰减。 Once a poisoned entry is injected into the memory store at time t, it persists in all future retrieval results without natural decay — formalizing the structural security vulnerability of retrieval-based memory.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着智能体AI被部署于长期任务和安全关键应用，将检索等同于记忆的错误假设可能导致能力停滞且易受隐蔽投毒攻击的不可靠智能体。本文提供了理论基础，引导领域转向真正支持终身学习和鲁棒泛化的架构设计。 As agentic AI systems are deployed in long-horizon tasks and safety-critical applications, the false assumption that retrieval equals memory could lead to fundamentally unreliable agents that plateau in capability and are silently vulnerable to poisoning. This paper provides the theoretical grounding to redirect the field toward architectures that actually support lifelong learning and robust generalization.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Using this dataset, they train Themis-RM—a suite of reward models from 600M to 32B parameters—and run detailed ablations to study the effect of data diversity, multi-criteria supervision, and model scale on reward quality.
