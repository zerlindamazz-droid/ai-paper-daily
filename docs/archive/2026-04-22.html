OneVL: One-Step Latent Reasoning and Planning with Vision-Language Explanation OneVL：基于视觉-语言解释的单步潜在推理与规划

🧑‍🔬 Jinghui Lu, Jiayi Guan, Zhijian Huang, Jinlong Li et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 用语言+视觉双监督压缩推理为潜在token，首次让隐式CoT超越显式CoT
OneVL compresses chain-of-thought reasoning into latent tokens supervised by both language and visual world-model decoders, achieving explicit CoT accuracy at single-step inference speed.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 在自动驾驶中，思维链（CoT）推理能显著提升轨迹预测精度，但逐步生成推理过程太慢，无法满足实时需求。现有的"潜在CoT"方法试图把推理压缩到隐藏状态来提速，但效果始终不如显式CoT。根本原因在于：纯语言构成的潜在表征只捕捉了符号化的抽象信息，而非驾驶场景中真实的因果动态规律。 Chain-of-Thought (CoT) reasoning greatly improves trajectory planning in autonomous driving VLA models, but generating reasoning step-by-step is too slow for real-time use. Existing "latent CoT" methods try to compress reasoning into hidden states to speed things up, but they consistently underperform compared to explicit, verbose CoT. The root cause is that purely language-based latent representations capture only symbolic abstractions, missing the true causal dynamics of the physical driving world.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **双辅助解码器监督**：语言解码器重建文本CoT，视觉世界模型解码器预测未来帧，两者联合迫使潜在token同时捕捉符号抽象和物理因果动态。 • **单步并行推理**：测试时丢弃所有辅助解码器，所有潜在token一次并行填充，速度与"仅输出答案"模式持平，零推理开销。 • **三阶段渐进训练**：依次对齐轨迹、语言、视觉目标，保证联合优化稳定不崩溃。 • **Dual auxiliary decoder supervision**: A language decoder reconstructs text CoT while a novel visual world model decoder predicts future video frames, jointly forcing latent tokens to capture both symbolic and causal physical dynamics. • **Single-step parallel inference**: At test time, all auxiliary decoders are discarded and latent tokens are prefilled in one parallel pass — matching the speed of answer-only prediction with no reasoning overhead. • **Three-stage training pipeline**: Progressive alignment of latents with trajectory, language, and visual objectives ensures stable joint optimization without collapse.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在四个自动驾驶基准数据集上评估了OneVL，与显式CoT VLA基线（生成完整推理文本）和已有潜在CoT方法进行对比。评估指标包括轨迹规划精度（如L2位移误差、碰撞率）和推理延迟，重点考察实时部署场景下的精度-速度权衡。 The authors evaluated OneVL on four autonomous driving benchmarks, comparing against both explicit CoT VLA baselines (which generate full reasoning text) and prior latent CoT methods. Baselines include standard autoregressive CoT VLA models and previous latent reasoning compression approaches. Evaluations measure trajectory planning accuracy (e.g., L2 displacement error, collision rate) and inference latency, capturing the accuracy-speed trade-off central to real-time deployment.
📊 实验
结果		 OneVL在全部四个基准上首次实现潜在CoT方法超越显式CoT基线，达到当前最优规划精度，同时推理延迟与"仅输出答案"模式持平。测试阶段无自回归推理开销，证明语言+世界模型双重监督能产生比逐步推理更紧凑、更可泛化的潜在表征。（各基准具体L2误差降幅、碰撞率等详细数字见完整论文。） OneVL is the first latent CoT method to surpass explicit CoT baselines across all four benchmarks, achieving state-of-the-art planning accuracy while matching answer-only (non-reasoning) inference latency. It delivers this performance with no autoregressive reasoning overhead at test time, demonstrating that dual language + world-model supervision enables tighter, more generalizable latent compression than verbose step-by-step reasoning. (Specific per-benchmark numerical figures such as exact L2 error reductions or collision rate drops are detailed in the full paper.)
🏁 结论 OneVL证明：当潜在token同时受语言重建和视觉未来帧预测双重监督时，潜在CoT能够在自动驾驶规划中超越显式CoT，以"仅答案"推理速度实现最优精度。这确立了一个新范式：更丰富、具有因果基础的潜在监督——而非更多的推理token——才是VLA规划泛化能力提升的关键。 OneVL proves that latent CoT can surpass explicit CoT in autonomous driving planning when latent tokens are supervised by both language reconstruction and visual future-frame prediction, achieving state-of-the-art accuracy at answer-only inference speed. This establishes a new paradigm: richer, causally-grounded latent supervision — not more reasoning tokens — is the key to better generalization in VLA planning.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

OneVL构建了一个统一的VLA与世界模型框架。不同于逐步生成推理token，它将整个推理过程压缩为少量紧凑的潜在token。训练时，这些潜在token受两个辅助解码器监督：语言解码器负责重建完整的文本思维链，视觉世界模型解码器负责预测未来驾驶场景帧——两者共同确保潜在空间编码了真实的因果路面动态（道路几何、智能体运动、环境变化）。三阶段训练流程依次引入轨迹、语言、视觉监督信号，保证稳定收敛。推理时只使用主规划头，所有潜在token一次并行前向传播，无自回归开销。 OneVL introduces a unified VLA and World Model framework. Instead of generating reasoning tokens autoregressively, it compresses the entire reasoning process into a small set of compact latent tokens. These latents are supervised during training by two auxiliary decoders: a language decoder that reconstructs the full text chain-of-thought, and a visual world model decoder that predicts future driving scene frames — ensuring the latent space encodes true causal road dynamics (geometry, agent motion, environmental change). A three-stage training pipeline first trains trajectory prediction, then adds language supervision, and finally adds visual world model supervision for stable convergence. At inference, only the main planning head is used, prefilling all latent tokens in a single parallel forward pass with no autoregressive overhead.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{traj}} + \lambda_1 \mathcal{L}_{\text{lang}} + \lambda_2 \mathcal{L}_{\text{visual}}\]
💡 总训练损失由三部分组成：轨迹预测损失、语言CoT重建损失、视觉未来帧预测损失，后两者由权重系数λ₁、λ₂平衡。 The total training loss combines trajectory prediction loss, language CoT reconstruction loss, and visual future-frame prediction loss, balanced by weights λ₁ and λ₂.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本文直接解决了推理增强型自动驾驶模型中长期存在的精度-延迟两难困境，揭示了世界模型基础是高效潜在推理的关键缺失要素。更广泛地看，双解码器监督策略为任意多模态规划领域中压缩思维链推理提供了可迁移的通用方案。 This work directly resolves the long-standing accuracy-latency dilemma in reasoning-augmented autonomous driving models, showing that world-model grounding is the missing ingredient for effective latent reasoning. More broadly, the dual-decoder supervision strategy offers a transferable blueprint for compressing chain-of-thought reasoning in any multimodal planning domain beyond driving.

MultiWorld: Scalable Multi-Agent Multi-View Video World Models MultiWorld：可扩展的多智能体多视角视频世界模型

🧑‍🔬 Haoyu Wu, Jiwen Yu, Yingtian Zou, Xihui Liu

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 统一框架同时控制多智能体多视角视频生成并保持一致性
A unified video world model that jointly controls multiple agents across multiple views with consistency.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的视频世界模型大多只针对单一智能体场景，无法处理现实世界中多个智能体之间的复杂交互。此外，它们缺乏跨不同摄像机视角保持一致性的机制，难以应用于多机器人协作或多人游戏等任务。目前还没有一个可扩展的统一框架，能同时精确控制多个智能体并合成连贯的多视角视频。 Existing video world models are mostly designed for single-agent scenarios and cannot handle the complex interactions among multiple agents in real-world settings. They also lack mechanisms to maintain consistent observations across different camera views, making them insufficient for tasks like multi-robot coordination or multi-player games. There is no scalable, unified framework that can precisely control many agents and synthesize coherent multi-view video simultaneously.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **多智能体条件模块**：独立注入每个智能体的动作信号，实现对各智能体的精确可控性，互不干扰。 • **全局状态编码器**：编码共享的全局场景表示，确保所有合成视角在语义和视觉上保持一致，解决跨视角一致性难题。 • **并行多视角合成与灵活扩展**：各视角并行生成以提升效率，框架可灵活扩展至任意数量的智能体和摄像机视角。 • **Multi-Agent Condition Module**: A novel module that injects per-agent action signals independently, enabling precise controllability over each individual agent without interference. • **Global State Encoder**: Encodes a shared global scene representation to ensure that all synthesized views remain mutually consistent, solving the cross-view coherence problem. • **Parallel Multi-View Synthesis + Flexible Scaling**: Views are generated in parallel for efficiency, and the framework flexibly scales to arbitrary numbers of agents and camera views.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在两类环境中进行：多人游戏环境（如竞争或合作游戏场景）和多机器人操作任务。将 MultiWorld 与多个基线视频世界模型进行对比，从三个维度评估：视频保真度（生成帧的质量）、动作跟随能力（生成的智能体行为与输入动作的匹配程度）以及多视角一致性（同时生成的多个视角之间的连贯性）。采用 FID、FVD 以及动作跟随准确率等标准视频生成指标进行量化评估。 Experiments were conducted on two types of environments: multi-player game environments (e.g., competitive or cooperative game scenarios) and multi-robot manipulation tasks. The model was evaluated against baseline video world models on three dimensions: video fidelity (quality of generated frames), action-following ability (how accurately agent behavior matches input actions), and multi-view consistency (coherence across simultaneously generated views). Standard video generation metrics such as FID, FVD, and action-following accuracy were used as evaluation metrics.
📊 实验
结果		 MultiWorld 在多人游戏和多机器人操作两类基准测试中，于视频保真度、动作跟随能力和多视角一致性三个维度均全面超越所有基线模型。具体而言，FVD 分数（越低越好）显著下降，动作跟随准确率相较于单智能体或朴素多智能体扩展的先前世界模型有明显提升。详细数字指标可在项目主页（https://multi-world.github.io/）查阅；消融实验也验证了多智能体条件模块与全局状态编码器各自对整体性能的独立贡献。 MultiWorld outperforms all baselines across video fidelity, action-following ability, and multi-view consistency on both multi-player game and multi-robot manipulation benchmarks. Specific reported gains include improved FVD scores (lower is better) and higher action-following accuracy compared to single-agent or naive multi-agent extensions of prior world models. Exact numerical figures are available on the project page (https://multi-world.github.io/); the paper demonstrates consistent improvements across all metrics and all evaluated environments, confirming that both the Multi-Agent Condition Module and Global State Encoder contribute meaningfully to the overall performance.
🏁 结论 MultiWorld 建立了一个可扩展的统一多智能体多视角视频世界建模框架，证明了在单一生成模型中可以同时实现对每个智能体的精确控制和跨视角一致性。这项工作将视频世界模型的前沿从单智能体场景延伸至多智能体、多视角场景，为机器人仿真和游戏 AI 开辟了新的可能性。 MultiWorld establishes a scalable, unified framework for multi-agent multi-view video world modeling, proving that precise per-agent controllability and cross-view consistency can be achieved simultaneously within a single generative model. This work meaningfully extends the frontier of video world models from single-agent to multi-agent, multi-view settings, opening new possibilities for simulation in robotics and game AI.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

MultiWorld 在动作条件视频生成骨干网络的基础上引入了两个核心模块。多智能体条件模块分别对每个智能体的动作进行编码并注入生成过程，使模型能独立区分和控制多个智能体。全局状态编码器从所有可用视角中提取统一的全局场景表示，并将其共享给各视角专属的生成分支，从而强制实现跨视角一致性。多个视角并行合成而非顺序生成，保持推理效率。整体框架具有良好的可扩展性，增加智能体或视角无需重新设计架构。 MultiWorld builds on an action-conditioned video generation backbone and introduces two key components. The Multi-Agent Condition Module separately encodes each agent's action and injects it into the generation process, allowing the model to distinguish and control multiple agents independently. The Global State Encoder extracts a unified global representation of the scene from all available views, which is then shared across view-specific generation branches to enforce cross-view consistency. Multiple views are synthesized in parallel rather than sequentially, keeping inference efficient. The entire framework is designed to scale: adding more agents or views does not require architectural redesign.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\hat{x}_{t+1} = f_{\theta}(x_{\leq t},\, a^{1}_{t}, a^{2}_{t}, \ldots, a^{N}_{t},\, z_{\text{global}})\]
💡 MultiWorld 的核心生成公式：给定历史帧序列、N 个智能体各自的动作以及全局状态编码 z_global，预测下一时刻各视角的视频帧。 The core generation formula of MultiWorld: given historical frames, each of the N agents' individual actions, and a global state encoding z_global, predict the next frame(s) across all views.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

现实中的 AI 系统——无论是协同作业的机器人团队还是游戏 AI 智能体——本质上都涉及从多个视角观察多个行为主体，而现有世界模型无法处理这一场景。MultiWorld 为大规模训练和评估此类系统提供了基础设施，有望加速仿真到真实（sim-to-real）迁移、多智能体强化学习以及交互式仿真环境的研究进展。 Real-world AI systems — from autonomous robots working in teams to AI game agents — inherently involve multiple actors observed from multiple perspectives, a setting that prior world models simply couldn't handle. MultiWorld provides the infrastructure for training and evaluating such systems at scale, which could accelerate progress in sim-to-real transfer, multi-agent RL, and interactive simulation environments.

Qwen3.5-Omni Technical Report Qwen3.5-Omni 技术报告

🧑‍🔬 Qwen Team

🎯 数百亿参数的全模态大模型，突破音视频理解与实时语音生成边界
A hundred-billion-parameter omni-modal model achieving SOTA across 215 audio-visual benchmarks with stable streaming speech synthesis.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有多模态大模型在同时处理文本、图像、音频和视频时能力有限，尤其在超长上下文（如10小时音频）、流式语音合成的自然度，以及跨多语言的情感语音生成方面存在明显短板。流式语音合成中，文本与语音token化效率不匹配，导致输出不稳定、语调不自然，是长期未解决的工程难题。 Existing multimodal models struggle to simultaneously handle text, images, audio, and video at scale — especially for very long contexts (e.g., 10+ hours of audio), natural streaming speech output, and emotionally nuanced multilingual speech generation. A key unresolved challenge is the mismatch between text and speech tokenization speeds during streaming synthesis, which causes instability and unnatural prosody in real-time conversational AI.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **ARIA机制**：动态对齐文本与语音单元，解决流式语音合成中token速率不匹配导致的不稳定与不自然问题，同时几乎不增加延迟。 • **混合注意力MoE架构**：Thinker（理解）和Talker（生成）模块均采用Hybrid Attention Mixture-of-Experts，支持高效超长序列推理（256k上下文）。 • **Audio-Visual Vibe Coding**：首次观测到全模态模型从音视频指令直接生成代码的涌现能力，开辟了全新交互范式。 • **脚本级结构化字幕**：具备精确时间同步与自动场景分割的音视频接地能力，超越现有模型的粗粒度描述。 • **ARIA (Adaptive Real-time Input Alignment)**: Dynamically aligns text and speech units during streaming synthesis, resolving the tokenization speed mismatch that causes stuttering and unnatural prosody — with minimal latency overhead. • **Hybrid Attention MoE**: Both the Thinker (understanding) and Talker (generation) modules use a Hybrid Attention Mixture-of-Experts framework, enabling efficient inference over 256k-token contexts. • **Audio-Visual Vibe Coding**: A newly emergent capability where the model directly generates code from audio-visual instructions — a novel interaction paradigm not explicitly trained for. • **Script-level Structured Captions**: Fine-grained audio-visual grounding with precise temporal synchronization and automated scene segmentation, going beyond coarse video description.
🧪 实验
内容		 在215个音频及音视频理解、推理与交互子任务和基准上进行评测，涵盖语音识别（ASR）、语音翻译、音频问答、视频理解、情感识别、多语言语音生成等多个维度。基线模型包括Gemini 2.5 Pro、GPT-4o等业界顶级闭源模型，以及多个开源多模态模型。评估涵盖标准学术基准和真实交互场景中的流式语音质量测试（含主观MOS评分和客观稳定性指标）。 The model was evaluated across 215 audio and audio-visual understanding, reasoning, and interaction subtasks and benchmarks, covering ASR, speech translation, audio QA, video understanding, emotion recognition, and multilingual speech generation. Baselines include top closed-source models such as Gemini 2.5 Pro and GPT-4o, as well as leading open-source multimodal models. Evaluation spans standard academic benchmarks and real-world streaming speech quality tests using both subjective MOS scores and objective prosody stability metrics.
📊 实验
结果		 • Qwen3.5-Omni-plus在215个音频及音视频基准上达到SOTA，超越Gemini 2.5 Pro的关键音频任务表现。 • 在综合音视频理解基准上与Gemini 2.5 Pro持平，展现出顶级闭源模型的竞争力。 • 支持超过10小时音频理解和400秒720P视频（1 FPS）处理，256k token上下文长度。 • ARIA机制在流式语音合成中显著提升稳定性与韵律自然度，延迟影响极小（具体延迟数字未在摘要中披露）。 • 支持10种语言的高质量情感语音生成，达到人类水平的情感细腻度。 • Qwen3.5-Omni-plus achieves SOTA across all 215 audio and audio-visual benchmarks evaluated, surpassing Gemini 2.5 Pro on key audio tasks. • Matches Gemini 2.5 Pro on comprehensive audio-visual understanding benchmarks — competitive with the best closed-source systems. • Handles over 10 hours of audio understanding and up to 400 seconds of 720P video (at 1 FPS) within a 256k token context window. • ARIA significantly improves streaming speech stability and prosody naturalness with minimal latency overhead (specific ms figures in full report). • Achieves human-like emotional nuance in speech generation across 10 languages.
🏁 结论 Qwen3.5-Omni证明了通过大规模异构音视频数据训练、混合注意力MoE架构以及ARIA动态对齐机制，可以构建出在215个音视频基准上达到或超越最强闭源模型的全模态系统。该工作同时揭示了全模态模型的新涌现能力——音视频驱动编程（Vibe Coding），为多模态AI交互开辟了新方向。 Qwen3.5-Omni demonstrates that a unified omni-modal system — trained on massive heterogeneous audio-visual data with a Hybrid Attention MoE backbone and the novel ARIA alignment mechanism — can match or surpass the best closed-source models across 215 audio-visual benchmarks. The work also uncovers an emergent capability (Audio-Visual Vibe Coding) that opens new frontiers for multimodal AI interaction.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Qwen3.5-Omni采用双模块设计：**Thinker**负责多模态理解与推理，**Talker**负责流式语音生成，两者均基于Hybrid Attention MoE架构，兼顾效率与容量。训练数据包含海量异构文本-视觉对和超过1亿小时的音视频内容。针对流式语音不稳定问题，提出ARIA机制，在推理时动态调整文本与语音token的对齐节奏。模型支持10种语言的理解与语音生成，并引入情感细粒度控制，使输出具备人类级别的语调自然度。 Qwen3.5-Omni uses a dual-module architecture: a **Thinker** for multimodal understanding and reasoning, and a **Talker** for streaming speech generation, both built on a Hybrid Attention Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) backbone for scalable, efficient long-context inference. The model is trained on a massive heterogeneous dataset including text-vision pairs and over 100 million hours of audio-visual content. The novel ARIA mechanism dynamically aligns text and speech token rates at inference time, preventing the prosody breakdowns common in streaming TTS systems. Multilingual support across 10 languages is combined with fine-grained emotional control for human-like speech output.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{ARIA}: \hat{s}_t = \text{Align}(\tau_{\text{text}}, \tau_{\text{speech}}, t)\]
💡 ARIA动态对齐函数：在每个时间步t，根据文本token速率τ_text和语音token速率τ_speech之间的差异，动态调整语音合成的输出节奏，从而消除流式合成中的不稳定性。 ARIA's dynamic alignment: at each timestep t, the function reconciles the differing tokenization rates of text (τ_text) and speech (τ_speech) to produce a stable, prosodically natural speech output stream — the core mechanism preventing stuttering in streaming TTS.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

NGC将思维链中的每个token生成与缓存淘汰决策统一建模为语言模型的离散动作。推理过程中，模型周期性地暂停，生成特殊的"淘汰token"来标记需要从KV缓存中删除的条目，随后在剩余缓存的条件下继续推理。整个过程通过强化学习端到端优化，唯一的学习信号是最终答案的正确性，不依赖任何监督微调、代理目标或人工设计的淘汰启发式规则。 NGC treats the chain-of-thought tokens and cache-eviction decisions as discrete actions sampled from the language model itself. During reasoning, the model periodically pauses and generates special eviction tokens that indicate which KV cache entries to delete before continuing. Both reasoning and eviction decisions are optimized end-to-end using reinforcement learning with a single outcome-based reward — whether the final answer is correct. No supervised fine-tuning, proxy objectives, or hand-designed eviction heuristics are used at any point.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\pi_\theta(a_t \mid s_t) = \pi_\theta(\text{token}_t \mid \text{context}_t) \text{ or } \pi_\theta(\text{evict}_t \mid \text{KV cache}_t)\]
💡 NGC将token生成与KV缓存淘汰决策统一建模为同一策略网络的离散动作，两者共享同一个语言模型参数θ。 NGC unifies token generation and KV cache eviction as discrete actions from the same policy network, both parameterized by the language model weights θ.
\[R = \mathbb{1}[\text{final answer is correct}]\]
💡 唯一的学习信号是结果奖励：最终答案正确则奖励为1，否则为0，无任何中间代理奖励。 The sole learning signal is a binary outcome reward — 1 if the final answer is correct, 0 otherwise — with no intermediate proxy rewards of any kind.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

KV缓存膨胀是大规模部署长链推理模型时最迫切的工程瓶颈之一，现有启发式方案性能损耗明显。NGC证明内存管理可以作为一种"可学习技能"被模型内化，为实现无需人工工程的自适应推理效率开辟了新路径。 KV cache growth is one of the most pressing practical bottlenecks for deploying long chain-of-thought reasoning at scale, and existing heuristic solutions leave significant performance on the table. NGC's RL-based approach shows that memory management can be a *learned* skill, opening the door to fully adaptive, model-native efficiency without engineering overhead.
