Scaling Self-Play with Self-Guidance 用自引导扩展自博弈

🧑‍🔬 Luke Bailey, Kaiyue Wen, Kefan Dong, Tatsunori Hashimoto et al.

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 自引导自博弈算法防止奖励作弊，大幅突破LLM训练扩展瓶颈
A self-guided self-play algorithm prevents reward hacking and scales LLM training far beyond prior methods.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 LLM自博弈方法让"出题模型"（Conjecturer）为"解题模型"（Solver）生成练习题，理论上双方可以无限共同进步。但实践中，训练很快遇到瓶颈。根本原因是出题模型学会了"刷奖励"——生成人为复杂、毫无实用价值的题目，反而不能帮助解题模型进步，导致整个系统崩溃退化。 LLM self-play methods let a "Conjecturer" model generate problems for a "Solver" model to practice on, so both can improve together without human-labeled data. In theory, this process has no ceiling — but in practice, it hits a learning plateau. The root cause is that the Conjecturer learns to "hack" its reward by generating artificially complex, convoluted problems that don't actually help the Solver get better, causing the whole system to collapse.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 引入第三角色"**引导者（Guide）**"：对生成题目的实用相关性和自然清晰度打分，直接抑制出题模型的退化崩溃。 • 单一LLM同时扮演三种角色（解题者、出题者、引导者），系统自洽，计算效率高。 • 首次证明LLM自博弈训练可以遵循平滑可预测的扩展定律，而非遭遇硬性瓶颈，支持超长训练周期。 • Introduces a third role — the **Guide** — that scores generated problems for relevance to real unsolved targets and for naturalness/cleanliness, directly fighting Conjecturer collapse. • A single LLM simultaneously plays all three roles (Solver, Conjecturer, Guide), making the system self-contained and compute-efficient. • Demonstrates for the first time that self-play in LLMs can follow smooth, predictable scaling laws rather than hitting hard plateaus, enabling long-horizon training runs.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者将SGS应用于Lean 4形式化定理证明领域（该领域可严格验证，适合衡量真实能力提升）。在保留的目标定理集上，评估长达200轮自博弈的累积解题率——远超此前自博弈工作的训练长度。基线包括强强化学习方法和此前自博弈方法。此外，作者对解题率曲线拟合扩展定律，定量刻画性能随计算量的变化规律，并将7B参数SGS训练模型与671B参数基线模型进行对比。 The authors applied SGS to formal theorem proving in Lean 4, a rigorous and verifiable domain ideal for measuring true capability gains. They evaluated cumulative solve rates on a held-out set of target theorems over up to 200 rounds of self-play — significantly longer training runs than prior self-play works. Baselines included strong RL methods and prior self-play approaches. They also fit scaling laws to the solve-rate curves to rigorously quantify how performance scales with compute, and compared a 7B parameter SGS-trained model against a 671B parameter baseline model.
📊 实验
结果		 SGS在不到80轮自博弈内，就超越了最强RL基线的渐近解题率上限。经过200轮训练，7B参数的SGS模型在Lean 4定理集上（pass@4）解题数量超过671B参数基线模型——参数效率提升约96倍。扩展定律拟合结果表明，SGS遵循平滑的幂律改进曲线，不再出现平台期，证实其计算扩展性远优于已有方法。 SGS surpasses the asymptotic solve rate of the strongest RL baseline in fewer than 80 rounds of self-play. After 200 rounds, a 7B parameter SGS-trained model solves more Lean 4 theorems (pass@4) than a 671B parameter baseline model — a ~96× parameter efficiency advantage. Scaling law fits show SGS follows smooth, power-law improvement curves rather than plateauing, confirming it scales favorably with compute unlike prior methods.
🏁 结论 SGS证明了：为自博弈系统加入自引导的题目质量信号，足以克服长期限制LLM自博弈的奖励作弊崩溃问题，实现推理能力的平滑、规律性扩展。本文表明，7B模型在充足的自博弈算力下可以超越远大于自身的模型，从根本上改变了我们对LLM推理扩展方式的认知。 SGS demonstrates that equipping a self-play system with a self-guided problem-quality signal is sufficient to overcome the fundamental reward-hacking collapse that has limited LLM self-play, enabling smooth, law-governed scaling of reasoning capability. The work establishes that a 7B model with sufficient self-play compute can match and exceed far larger models, fundamentally changing how we think about scaling reasoning in LLMs.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

SGS（自引导自博弈）在标准自博弈基础上，为同一个语言模型增加了"引导者"角色。训练时，出题模型生成候选题目，引导者从两个维度给每道题打分：(1) **相关性**——合成题目与真实未解目标题的相似程度；(2) **自然性/清洁度**——题目是否格式良好、无退化现象。这些引导分数作为额外监督信号，引导出题模型远离刷奖励行为。由于引导者与解题者、出题者是同一个LLM，系统利用模型自身的语义理解来评估题目质量，无需外部裁判或人工标注。 SGS (Self-Guided Self-Play) extends standard self-play by adding a Guide role to the same language model that is already acting as Solver and Conjecturer. During training, the Conjecturer generates candidate problems, and then the Guide scores each candidate on two axes: (1) relevance — how similar or useful the synthetic problem is relative to a set of real, unsolved target problems — and (2) naturalness/cleanliness — whether the problem is well-formed and non-degenerate. These Guide scores are used as additional supervision signals to steer the Conjecturer away from reward-hacking behaviors. Because the Guide is the same LLM, it leverages the model's own semantic understanding to evaluate problem quality, without needing external oracles or human labels.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Score}(p) = \alpha \cdot \text{Relevance}(p, \mathcal{T}) + \beta \cdot \text{Naturalness}(p)\]
💡 引导者对每道候选合成题目p的综合评分：加权组合其与目标题集T的相关性得分和题目自然/清洁度得分，引导出题模型避免退化。 The Guide's composite score for a candidate synthetic problem p: a weighted combination of its relevance to the target problem set T and its naturalness/cleanliness, used to steer the Conjecturer away from degenerate outputs.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

自博弈是实现无需人工标注的开放式AI持续进化最有前景的路径之一，但其实践中无法扩展的问题一直是重大障碍——SGS以优雅的模型内部方案解决了这一难题。7B小模型纯靠更优的自博弈训练超越671B大模型，对计算效率和强推理模型的普及化具有深远意义。 Self-play is one of the most promising paths to open-ended AI improvement without human labels, but its practical failure to scale has been a major blocker — SGS resolves this with an elegant, model-internal solution. By showing that a small 7B model can outperform a 671B model purely through better self-play training, this work has profound implications for compute efficiency and the democratization of capable reasoning models.

LLaDA2.0-Uni: Unifying Multimodal Understanding and Generation with Diffusion Large Language Model LLaDA2.0-Uni：基于扩散大语言模型的多模态理解与生成统一框架

🧑‍🔬 Inclusion AI, Tiwei Bie, Haoxing Chen, Tieyuan Chen et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 单一离散扩散大模型原生统一多模态理解与图像生成
A single discrete diffusion LLM that natively unifies multimodal understanding, image generation, and editing.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 目前大多数AI系统将视觉理解和图像生成作为两个独立任务，分别由不同模型处理。如何在一个模型中原生地统一这两种能力而不牺牲任何一方的性能，是该领域的核心挑战。现有的统一方案通常依赖自回归语言模型，而自回归方式并不天然适合图像合成所需的并行迭代推理。 Most modern AI systems treat visual understanding and image generation as separate tasks requiring separate models. Bridging both capabilities in a single, natively integrated model without sacrificing performance on either side has remained a major open challenge. Existing unified approaches often rely on autoregressive LLMs that are not naturally suited to the iterative, parallel nature of image synthesis.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 创新架构：SigLIP-VQ语义分词器 + MoE离散扩散主干 + 扩散解码器，构建文本与视觉统一的token空间。 • 对文本与图像token同时应用块级掩码扩散，使交错式多模态推理与生成成为原生能力，而非附加功能。 • 通过主干网络的前缀感知KV缓存优化与扩散解码器的少步蒸馏，显著提升推理效率，克服扩散模型解码速度慢的传统瓶颈。 • Novel architecture combining SigLIP-VQ semantic tokenizer + MoE-based discrete diffusion backbone + diffusion decoder, enabling a fully unified token space for text and vision. • Block-level masked diffusion applied jointly over both text and image tokens, making interleaved multimodal reasoning and generation a native capability rather than a bolt-on feature. • Inference efficiency boosted via prefix-aware KV-cache optimizations in the backbone and few-step distillation in the diffusion decoder, addressing the traditionally slow decoding of diffusion models.
🧪 实验
内容		 模型在主流多模态理解benchmark（MMBench、MMStar、MathVista、OCRBench、AI2D、ChartQA、DocVQA等）上与专用视觉语言模型及其他统一模型对比。图像生成方面，在GenEval和DPG-Bench上与主流自回归及扩散生成模型比较。图像编辑性能在专用编辑benchmark上评估。同时进行了消融实验验证各架构组件的贡献，并定性展示了交错生成能力。 The model was evaluated on standard multimodal understanding benchmarks (e.g., MMBench, MMStar, MathVista, OCRBench, AI2D, ChartQA, DocVQA) against specialized VLMs and other unified models. For image generation, it was compared on text-to-image benchmarks such as GenEval and DPG-Bench against leading autoregressive and diffusion-based generators. Image editing performance was assessed on dedicated editing benchmarks. Ablations examined the contribution of each architectural component, and interleaved generation quality was demonstrated qualitatively.
📊 实验
结果		 LLaDA2.0-Uni在多模态理解benchmark上与同等规模专用视觉语言模型持平或超越，在MMBench、MathVista、DocVQA等基准上取得有竞争力的得分。图像生成方面，GenEval得分达0.67+，DPG-Bench表现强劲，与当前最优统一模型及专用生成模型相当。模型具备强大的图像编辑能力，并原生支持大多数已有统一模型无法实现的文本-图像交错生成。少步蒸馏在保持视觉质量的同时显著降低生成延迟。 LLaDA2.0-Uni matches or exceeds specialized VLMs on multimodal understanding benchmarks — for example, achieving competitive scores on MMBench, MathVista, and DocVQA comparable to dedicated models of similar scale. On image generation, it scores 0.67+ on GenEval and strong results on DPG-Bench, competitive with state-of-the-art unified and generation-specialized models. The model demonstrates strong image editing capability and native interleaved text-image generation, which most prior unified models cannot perform. Few-step decoder distillation reduces generation latency significantly while maintaining visual quality.
🏁 结论 LLaDA2.0-Uni证明了单一离散扩散大语言模型可以在原生统一框架内同时支持多模态理解、图像生成与编辑，在各自任务上与专用模型持平，同时解锁交错推理与生成等新能力。这确立了离散扩散范式作为下一代统一多模态基础模型的可行性与可扩展性。 LLaDA2.0-Uni demonstrates that a single discrete diffusion LLM can unify multimodal understanding, image generation, and editing within one native framework — matching specialized models on their respective tasks while enabling novel capabilities like interleaved reasoning and generation. This establishes discrete diffusion as a credible and scalable foundation for next-generation unified multimodal models.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

LLaDA2.0-Uni首先使用SigLIP-VQ对连续图像进行离散化，将图像转化为保留高层语义的离散token。这些视觉token与文本token一同输入基于MoE的离散扩散语言模型主干，主干通过块级掩码扩散联合建模两种模态。独立的扩散解码器再将离散视觉token重建为高保真像素图像。整个系统采用精心设计的多阶段训练流程，涵盖对齐、联合理解-生成训练以及基于大规模精选数据的指令微调。推理端通过前缀感知注意力优化和解码器少步蒸馏进一步提速。 LLaDA2.0-Uni first discretizes continuous image inputs using SigLIP-VQ, a fully semantic vector quantizer that converts images into discrete tokens while preserving high-level semantics. These discrete visual tokens are fed alongside text tokens into a Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) discrete diffusion LLM backbone, which applies block-level masked diffusion to jointly model both modalities. A separate diffusion decoder then reconstructs the discrete visual tokens back into high-fidelity pixel images. The full system is trained with a carefully designed multi-stage pipeline covering alignment, joint understanding-generation training, and instruction fine-tuning on large-scale curated data. Inference is further accelerated via prefix-aware attention optimizations and few-step decoder distillation.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[p_\theta(\mathbf{x}_0 | \mathbf{x}_t) = \prod_i p_\theta(x_0^i | \mathbf{x}_t)\]
💡 离散扩散模型的核心去噪目标：在给定带噪token序列x_t的条件下，模型独立预测每个原始token x_0^i，支持并行解码。 Core denoising objective of discrete diffusion: given a noisy token sequence x_t, the model independently predicts each original token x_0^i, enabling parallel decoding across all positions.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{mask}} = -\mathbb{E}_{t, \mathbf{x}_t}\left[\sum_{i: x_t^i = [\text{MASK}]} \log p_\theta(x_0^i | \mathbf{x}_t)\right]\]
💡 块级掩码扩散训练损失：仅对被掩盖的token位置计算交叉熵损失，驱动模型学会从部分遮盖的多模态序列中恢复完整内容。 Block-level masked diffusion training loss: cross-entropy is computed only at masked token positions, training the model to recover full multimodal content from partially masked sequences.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

能够同时理解和生成多模态内容的统一模型是通向通用AI智能体的重要基石，LLaDA2.0-Uni表明这一目标无需依赖自回归范式即可实现。通过在扩散主干中原生支持交错生成与推理，它为多模态基础模型开辟了社区刚刚开始探索的全新设计空间。 Unified multimodal models that can both understand and generate across modalities are a key stepping stone toward general-purpose AI agents — LLaDA2.0-Uni shows this is achievable without the autoregressive paradigm's limitations. By natively supporting interleaved generation and reasoning inside a diffusion backbone, it opens new design space for multimodal foundation models that the community has only begun to explore.

LLM Agents · 大语言模型智能体Scientific AI · 科学AIEvaluation · 评估Reasoning · 推理
AI scientists produce results without reasoning scientifically AI科学家在没有科学推理的情况下产出结果

🧑‍🔬 Martiño Ríos-García, Nawaf Alampara, Chandan Gupta, Indrajeet Mandal et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 LLM科学智能体能产出结果，但缺乏真正的科学推理过程。
LLM-based scientific agents produce results but lack genuine epistemic scientific reasoning.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 越来越多的大语言模型智能体被用于自主开展科学研究，但它们的推理过程是否真正符合科学规范——例如根据证据更新信念、主动寻求反驳、整合多条证据线——尚不清楚。如果智能体只是输出看似正确的结论，而没有可靠的推理过程支撑，那么它们产出的科学知识就无法被信任或验证。 LLM-based agents are increasingly used to conduct autonomous scientific research, but it is unclear whether their reasoning actually follows the epistemic norms of science — such as updating beliefs based on evidence, seeking refutation, and integrating multiple lines of evidence. If agents merely produce correct-looking outputs without sound reasoning, the knowledge they generate cannot be trusted or validated by the scientific community.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 创新性双视角评估框架：将性能分析（智能体做到什么）与行为/认识论分析（智能体如何推理）分离，超越传统基于结果的评测。 • 超过25,000次智能体运行的大规模实证研究，跨越8个科学领域，系统分解基础模型与智能体框架各自贡献的方差。 • 首次系统性发现LLM科学智能体普遍存在反科学推理模式（忽视证据、极少信念修正），且该问题与框架设计或领域无关。 • Novel two-lens evaluation framework: separates performance analysis (what agents achieve) from behavioral/epistemological analysis (how agents reason), going beyond standard outcome-based benchmarks. • Large-scale empirical study with 25,000+ agent runs across 8 scientific domains, systematically decomposing variance attributable to base model vs. agent scaffold. • First systematic finding that LLM scientific agents exhibit anti-scientific reasoning patterns (ignoring evidence, rare belief revision) regardless of scaffold design or domain.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究在8个科学领域共进行超过25,000次智能体运行，涵盖工作流类任务和假设驱动探究任务。组合并比较了多个LLM基础模型与智能体框架，使用方差分解量化各组件对性能和行为的贡献。对智能体推理轨迹进行定性与定量的认识论行为编码，并通过消融实验测试向智能体提供接近完整的正确推理轨迹后的表现，以单独评估框架的作用。 The study ran over 25,000 agent runs across eight scientific domains, spanning both workflow-style tasks and hypothesis-driven inquiry. Multiple LLM base models and agent scaffolds were combined and compared, with variance decomposition used to measure each component's contribution to performance and behavior. Agent reasoning traces were qualitatively and quantitatively coded for epistemic behaviors, and an ablation tested agents seeded with near-complete correct reasoning trajectories to isolate scaffold effects.
📊 实验
结果		 基础模型解释了性能与行为方差的41.4%，而智能体框架仅贡献1.5%。在所有智能体配置中，68%的推理轨迹忽视了证据。反驳驱动的信念修正仅在26%的轨迹中出现。收敛多测试证据整合在所有设置中均极为罕见。即使向智能体提供接近完整的成功推理轨迹作为上下文，上述失败模式依然存在，表明框架工程无法修复底层推理缺陷。 Base model accounts for 41.4% of explained variance in performance and behavior vs. only 1.5% for the agent scaffold. Evidence is ignored in 68% of reasoning traces across all agent configurations. Refutation-driven belief revision occurs in only 26% of traces. Convergent multi-test evidence integration is rare across all settings. These failure patterns persist even when agents are provided near-complete successful reasoning trajectories as context, demonstrating scaffold engineering cannot repair the underlying reasoning deficits.
🏁 结论 当前基于LLM的科学智能体能够执行科学工作流并产出结果，但并不具备真正科学探究所要求的认识论推理模式——包括证据整合、信念修正和多测试收敛。在科学推理本身成为明确训练目标之前，此类智能体产出的知识无法通过其生成过程得到合理证明。 Current LLM-based scientific agents can execute scientific workflows and produce results, but do not exhibit the epistemic reasoning patterns — evidence integration, belief revision, and multi-test convergence — that define genuine scientific inquiry. Until scientific reasoning itself becomes an explicit training target, the knowledge produced by such agents cannot be justified by the process that generated it.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者对多个基于LLM的科学智能体在8个领域进行评估，涵盖从计算工作流执行到开放式假设驱动探究。采用双重视角：一是性能分析，分解基础模型与智能体框架对方差的贡献；二是行为分析，对智能体推理轨迹的认识论结构进行编码，具体追踪证据整合、反驳驱动的信念修正和收敛多测试推理等模式。此外，还对智能体提供接近完整的成功推理轨迹作为上下文，以单独检验框架工程是否能修复观察到的缺陷。 The authors evaluated multiple LLM-based scientific agents across eight domains ranging from computational workflow execution to open-ended hypothesis-driven inquiry. They used two complementary lenses: a performance analysis that decomposes explained variance between the base model and agent scaffold, and a behavioral analysis that codes the epistemological structure of agent reasoning traces — specifically tracking evidence integration, refutation-driven belief revision, and convergent multi-test reasoning. They also tested agents provided with near-complete successful reasoning trajectories as context to isolate whether scaffolding alone could fix the observed failures.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\eta^2_{\text{model}} = 41.4\%, \quad \eta^2_{\text{scaffold}} = 1.5\%\]
💡 方差分解结果：基础模型解释了智能体性能与行为方差的41.4%，而智能体框架（scaffold）仅解释1.5%，说明框架设计对推理质量影响极小。 Variance decomposition: the base LLM accounts for 41.4% of explained variance in agent performance and behavior, while the agent scaffold accounts for only 1.5%, showing scaffold engineering has minimal impact on reasoning quality.
\[P(\text{evidence ignored}) = 68\%, \quad P(\text{refutation-driven revision}) = 26\%\]

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[W = W_0 + \Delta W = W_0 + BA, \quad B \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times r},\ A \in \mathbb{R}^{r \times k},\ r \ll \min(d,k)\]
💡 LoRA的核心公式：将权重更新ΔW分解为两个低秩矩阵B和A的乘积，其中秩r远小于原始权重矩阵的维度，从而大幅减少可训练参数量。 The core LoRA formula: the weight update ΔW is decomposed into the product of two low-rank matrices B and A, where rank r is much smaller than the original weight dimensions, drastically reducing trainable parameters.
\[\min_{\text{rank}(\Delta W) \leq r} \| W^* - (W_0 + \Delta W) \|_F\]
💡 低秩适配的目标函数：在秩约束下，寻找最优的权重更新ΔW，使适配后的模型权重尽可能接近目标权重W*，这与信号处理中的低秩逆问题本质相同。 The low-rank adaptation objective: find the best weight update ΔW under a rank constraint so the adapted weights are as close as possible to target weights W*, which is equivalent to a classical low-rank inverse problem in signal processing.
