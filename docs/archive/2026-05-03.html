Do Sparse Autoencoders Capture Concept Manifolds? 稀疏自编码器能捕捉概念流形吗？

🧑‍🔬 Usha Bhalla, Thomas Fel, Can Rager, Sheridan Feucht et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 稀疏自编码器以两种方式捕捉概念流形，但常因混合模式导致碎片化失效。
SAEs capture concept manifolds via two modes—global subspace or local tiling—but often fail by mixing them into a fragmented "dilution" regime.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 稀疏自编码器（SAE）被广泛用于从神经网络中提取可解释特征，但其背后的假设是"一个概念对应一个线性方向"。然而现实中，许多概念（如颜色渐变、姿态变化）其实分布在弯曲的低维流形上，而非单一方向。目前尚不清楚SAE能否以及如何捕捉这种更丰富的几何结构。 Sparse autoencoders (SAEs) are popular tools for extracting human-interpretable features from neural networks, but they assume concepts map to single linear directions. In reality, many concepts (e.g., color gradients, pose variations) lie on curved, low-dimensional manifolds rather than isolated directions. It is unclear whether and how SAEs capture this richer geometric structure.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - 提出了区分SAE捕捉流形的两种方式的理论框架：**全局模式**（一组原子的线性张成覆盖整个流形）和**局部模式**（各特征分别铺盖流形的局部小块）。 - 发现了第三种病态情形——**稀释（dilution）**，即全局与局部方案混合，导致流形结构碎片化，无法在单一特征层面被观察到。 - 提出应以**连贯原子组**（而非孤立方向）作为可解释性的基本单元，并倡导事后无监督发现方法。 - Introduces a formal theoretical framework distinguishing two ways SAEs can capture manifolds: **global** (a compact group of atoms whose span covers the whole manifold) and **local** (features that tile small patches of the manifold). - Identifies a third, pathological regime called **dilution**, where global and local solutions are mixed, fragmenting the manifold structure and hiding it from individual feature inspection. - Proposes that post-hoc unsupervised discovery of **coherent atom groups**, rather than individual directions, is the right unit of interpretability for manifold-structured concepts.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在两类数据上进行：一是具有已知真实流形结构的合成数据集（如圆形或低维参数流形），二是真实神经网络（包括视觉和语言模型）的激活值。评估了多种SAE架构（标准SAE、TopK SAE及其变体）。评估指标包括流形恢复质量、特征与真实几何的对齐程度，以及在不同训练配置和字典大小下的稀释程度。 Experiments were conducted on both synthetic datasets with known ground-truth manifold structure (e.g., circular or low-dimensional parametric manifolds) and real neural network activations (including vision and language models). Multiple SAE architectures (standard SAEs, TopK SAEs, and variants) were evaluated. Metrics included manifold recovery quality, feature alignment with ground-truth geometry, and the degree of dilution across different training regimes and dictionary sizes.
📊 实验
结果		 SAE在各类架构上均表现出次优的流形恢复能力，稀释模式是最主要的失效方式。在受控合成实验中，SAE鲜少实现干净的全局或局部方案，混合（稀释）配置占据多数结果。定量上，当字典大小超过某一临界阈值后，流形恢复指标显著下降，说明更大的SAE并不自动带来更好的几何结构捕捉。相比之下，事后对原子进行分组恢复流形结构的效果，显著优于任何单一特征，验证了基于组的可解释性视角的有效性。 SAEs consistently exhibited suboptimal manifold recovery across architectures, with the dilution regime being the dominant failure mode. In controlled synthetic experiments, SAEs rarely achieved clean global or local solutions; instead, mixed (diluted) configurations accounted for the majority of outcomes. Quantitatively, manifold recovery metrics dropped significantly when dictionary sizes grew beyond a critical threshold, indicating that larger SAEs do not automatically yield better geometric structure capture. Post-hoc grouping of atoms recovered manifold structure substantially better than any individual feature, demonstrating the utility of the proposed group-based interpretability lens.
🏁 结论 本文证明了SAE可以通过全局子空间和局部铺盖两种方式捕捉概念流形，但实践中主要陷入掩盖流形结构的"稀释"模式。核心贡献在于提出了一套理论与实证框架，表明神经网络可解释性的基本单元应是几何对象（特征组），而非孤立方向。 This paper proves that SAEs can capture concept manifolds in two fundamentally different ways—global subspace allocation and local tiling—but in practice predominantly fall into a fragmented "dilution" regime that obscures manifold structure. The key contribution is a theoretical and empirical framework showing that geometric objects (groups of features), not individual directions, should be the basic units of neural network interpretability.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者构建了一套理论框架，将概念流形建模为嵌入在神经网络激活空间中的低维几何对象。他们通过线性代数与几何论证，刻画出SAE的三种行为模式：全局子空间捕捉、局部铺盖和稀释。在实验上，他们在受控合成流形和真实神经网络表征上探测已训练的SAE，衡量特征与已知流形几何的对齐程度。最后，他们提出应通过搜索能集体重建流形结构的连贯原子组来评估SAE，而非依赖单个特征的质量。 The authors develop a theoretical framework that models concept manifolds as low-dimensional geometric objects embedded in neural network activation spaces. They characterize three regimes of SAE behavior—global subspace capture, local tiling, and dilution—using linear algebraic and geometric arguments. Empirically, they probe trained SAEs on controlled synthetic manifolds and real neural network representations to measure how well features align with known manifold geometry. They then propose evaluating SAEs not by individual feature quality but by searching for coherent groups of atoms that collectively reconstruct manifold structure.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{M} \subset \mathbb{R}^d, \quad \dim(\mathcal{M}) = k \ll d\]
💡 概念流形 M 是嵌入在高维激活空间中的低维几何结构，其本征维度 k 远小于环境维度 d。 A concept manifold M is a low-dimensional geometric structure embedded in the high-dimensional activation space, with intrinsic dimension k much smaller than the ambient dimension d.
\[\hat{x} = \sum_{i \in S} c_i \mathbf{a}_i, \quad |S| \ll N\]
💡 SAE 用字典中少量被激活的原子 a_i 的稀疏线性组合来重建输入 x，其中 S 是激活原子的索引集，N 是字典总大小。 The SAE reconstructs input x as a sparse linear combination of a small subset S of dictionary atoms a_i, where N is the total dictionary size.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着SAE成为大型语言和视觉模型机械可解释性的主流工具，理解其几何失效模式对于确保输出的可信性与意义至关重要。本研究为下一代可解释性方法提供了原则性路线图，推动该领域从单特征分析迈向群组级别的几何推理。 As SAEs become the dominant tool for mechanistic interpretability of large language and vision models, understanding their geometric failure modes is critical for ensuring their outputs are trustworthy and meaningful. This work provides a principled roadmap for next-generation interpretability methods that move beyond single-feature analysis to group-level geometric reasoning.

Latent-GRPO: Group Relative Policy Optimization for Latent Reasoning Latent-GRPO：面向隐空间推理的群体相对策略优化

🧑‍🔬 Jingcheng Deng, Zihao Wei, Liang Pang, Junhong Wu et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 隐空间强化学习推理，链长压缩3-4倍仍超越显式GRPO
Latent-GRPO enables stable RL training in latent reasoning space with 3–4× shorter chains.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型在文本空间中一步步推理（显式推理）效果好但速度慢，会产生很长的推理链。隐空间推理将中间步骤压缩为连续向量，可以大幅缩短推理链、加快推理速度。但问题是，将强化学习（尤其是GRPO）直接应用到隐空间推理时极不稳定，现有方法几乎都还停留在监督学习阶段。 Large language models that reason step-by-step in text (explicit reasoning) are effective but slow — they generate very long chains of tokens. Latent reasoning compresses these steps into continuous vector representations, making inference faster and shorter. However, applying reinforcement learning (specifically GRPO) to latent reasoning is unstable and largely unsolved, because the latent space behaves very differently from discrete token space.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **无效样本优势掩码**：对偏离有效隐流形的rollout进行屏蔽，防止越界隐状态产生错误梯度更新。 • **单侧噪声采样**：仅在探索阶段注入噪声，保持rollout在有效流形上同时保留多样性。 • **最优正确路径首token选择**：当存在多条正确隐路径时，选取最佳首token，避免"隐混合非封闭性"问题（多条正确路径平均后可能产生无效状态）。 • **Invalid-sample advantage masking**: masks out rollouts that fall off the valid latent manifold, preventing corrupt gradients from out-of-distribution latent states. • **One-sided noise sampling**: injects noise only during exploration (not optimization) to keep rollouts on the manifold while maintaining diversity. • **Optimal correct-path first-token selection**: when multiple correct latent paths are available, selects the best first token to avoid the "latent mixture non-closure" problem where averaging valid paths produces an invalid state.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在八个基准测试上评估Latent-GRPO，分为两档难度：四个低难度任务（包括GSM8K-Aug）和四个高难度任务（包括AIME）。对比基线包括：仅经过监督预训练的隐推理初始化模型，以及在token空间进行标准强化学习的显式GRPO。此外还通过Gumbel采样评估pass@k性能以衡量探索多样性，同时测量推理链长度来验证效率提升。 The authors evaluated Latent-GRPO on eight benchmarks spanning two difficulty tiers: four low-difficulty tasks (including GSM8K-Aug) and four high-difficulty tasks (including AIME). They compared against the latent reasoning initialization baseline (supervised pre-training only) and explicit GRPO (standard RL in token space). They also evaluated pass@k performance under Gumbel sampling to assess exploration quality. Reasoning chain length was measured to verify efficiency gains.
📊 实验
结果		 在4个低难度基准上，Latent-GRPO相比隐推理初始化基线提升 **+7.86 Pass@1分**。在4个高难度基准（如AIME）上，超越显式GRPO **+4.27 Pass@1分**。以上成果均在推理链比显式GRPO **缩短3-4倍** 的条件下实现。Gumbel采样下的pass@k指标也更优，说明隐空间中探索多样性更强。 On 4 low-difficulty benchmarks, Latent-GRPO improves over its latent initialization baseline by **+7.86 Pass@1 points**. On 4 high-difficulty benchmarks (e.g., AIME), it surpasses explicit GRPO by **+4.27 Pass@1 points**. These gains are achieved with reasoning chains that are **3–4× shorter** than explicit GRPO. It also achieves stronger pass@k performance under Gumbel sampling, indicating better exploration diversity in latent space.
🏁 结论 Latent-GRPO证明了通过针对性地解决隐流形偏移、探索-优化不对齐和混合非封闭性三大瓶颈，可以使强化学习在连续隐推理空间中稳定且有效地运行。与显式RL基线相比，它在提升精度的同时大幅压缩推理链长度，为隐空间推理领域树立了新的标杆。 Latent-GRPO proves that reinforcement learning can be made stable and effective in continuous latent reasoning space by addressing three fundamental bottlenecks — manifold drift, exploration-optimization misalignment, and mixture non-closure — through targeted algorithmic fixes. It establishes a new state-of-the-art for latent reasoning by simultaneously achieving higher accuracy and drastically shorter reasoning chains compared to explicit RL baselines.

链接		 arXiv: 2604.27998v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Latent-GRPO在标准GRPO算法基础上，专门针对隐空间推理的三个核心失效模式进行改造。第一，对偏离有效隐流形的rollout样本屏蔽其优势分数（无效样本优势掩码），防止这些"越界"样本污染策略更新。第二，只在采样/探索阶段加入噪声，梯度更新时不加噪声，确保探索路径始终在有效流形上（单侧噪声采样）。第三，当存在多条正确隐推理路径时，只选最优路径的第一个token参与更新，避免多路径平均导致状态失效的"非封闭性"问题（最优正确路径首token选择）。三者协同，使GRPO在隐空间中稳定且高效地运作。 Latent-GRPO extends the standard GRPO algorithm to work in continuous latent space by tackling three fundamental failure modes. First, it masks the advantage scores of any rollout that wanders outside the valid latent manifold (invalid-sample advantage masking), so these corrupt samples do not contribute to policy updates. Second, it adds noise only during the sampling/rollout phase — not during gradient updates — keeping exploration on-manifold (one-sided noise sampling). Third, when multiple correct latent reasoning paths exist, it selects the first token of the single best path rather than mixing across paths, avoiding the non-closure problem where an averaged latent state is no longer a valid reasoning state (optimal correct-path first-token selection). Together, these three components make GRPO stable and effective in latent reasoning space.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[A_i = \mathbb{1}[o_i \in \mathcal{M}] \cdot \frac{r_i - \mu_r}{\sigma_r}\]
💡 无效样本优势掩码：只有当rollout样本 $o_i$ 落在有效隐流形 $\mathcal{M}$ 上时，才保留其优势分数（归一化奖励），否则置零，防止越界样本污染梯度。 Invalid-sample advantage masking: the advantage score for rollout $o_i$ is kept (as a normalized reward) only if it lies on the valid latent manifold $\mathcal{M}$; otherwise it is zeroed out to prevent corrupted gradients.
\[\tilde{z} = z + \epsilon, \quad \epsilon \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \sigma^2 I) \text{ (sampling only)}\]
💡 单侧噪声采样：在探索/采样阶段向隐状态 $z$ 注入高斯噪声 $\epsilon$，使rollout保持多样性且留在有效流形附近；梯度更新阶段不加噪声。 One-sided noise sampling: Gaussian noise $\epsilon$ is added to the latent state $z$ only during the rollout/sampling phase to encourage diversity while staying near the valid manifold; no noise is applied during the gradient update step.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

推理效率是大语言模型落地实时应用的核心瓶颈，隐推理可大幅降低推理计算量，而Latent-GRPO是首个能在隐空间中稳定释放强化学习潜力的有原则方法。这为"深度推理同时保持高效计算"开辟了一条切实可行的新路径，对大模型的工业化部署具有重要意义。 Efficient reasoning is a key bottleneck for deploying LLMs in real-time applications — latent reasoning dramatically reduces inference compute, and Latent-GRPO is the first principled RL method to unlock its full potential. This opens a promising direction where models can reason deeply and accurately while being far more computationally efficient than chain-of-thought baselines.

Learning Rate Transfer in Normalized Transformers 归一化 Transformer 中的学习率迁移

🧑‍🔬 Boris Shigida, Boris Hanin, Andrey Gromov

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 νGPT修复了nGPT的学习率无法跨尺度迁移问题
νGPT fixes learning rate non-transferability in nGPT via principled reparameterization across width, depth, and token horizon.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 归一化 Transformer（nGPT）训练速度更快，不需要权重衰减和学习率预热，但它的学习率无法在不同模型宽度、深度和训练 token 数量之间迁移。这意味着每次换一个更大的模型，研究者都要重新调超参数，非常耗时耗力。现有的超参数迁移框架（如 μP）并非为 nGPT 这类归一化架构设计，无法直接套用。 The Normalized Transformer (nGPT) trains faster and avoids weight decay and warmup, but its learning rate does not transfer when you scale model width, depth, or training token horizon — meaning practitioners must re-tune hyperparameters for every new model size. This is expensive and defeats the purpose of principled scaling. Existing hyperparameter transfer frameworks like μP were not designed for normalized architectures like nGPT and cannot be applied directly.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 找到了 nGPT 学习率无法迁移的根本原因：其参数化方案不满足超参数稳定的必要条件。 • 引入"对齐指数"（alignment exponents）作为分析工具，诊断梯度更新与不同尺度权重矩阵之间的交互关系。 • 提出 νGPT，一种全新的 nGPT 重参数化方案，首次实现了归一化 Transformer 在模型宽度、深度和 token 训练量上的同步学习率迁移。 • Identifies the root cause of learning rate non-transfer in nGPT: its parameterization does not satisfy the conditions required for stable, size-independent hyperparameters. • Introduces alignment exponents as a principled diagnostic tool to analyze how gradient updates interact with weight matrices at different scales. • Proposes νGPT, a novel reparameterization of nGPT that achieves learning rate transfer simultaneously across model width, depth, and token horizon — the first normalized Transformer to do so.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在不同宽度（多个模型维度档位）和深度的语言模型上进行自回归语言建模训练，同时测试了不同 token 训练量下的表现。基线模型包括原始 nGPT 和基于 μP 的超参数迁移模型。评估指标以训练损失曲线为主，核心衡量标准是：在小模型上找到的最优学习率能否无需重新调参就直接迁移到更大模型上。 Experiments train language models of varying widths (model dimensions ranging over multiple scales) and depths, as well as across different token horizons, using standard autoregressive language modeling on text corpora. The baselines include the original nGPT and standard μP-transferred models. Evaluation is based on training loss curves and the consistency of the optimal learning rate across model sizes — the key metric being whether the best learning rate identified in a small model reliably transfers to larger models without retuning.
📊 实验
结果		 νGPT 在所有测试的模型宽度、深度和 token 训练量下均实现了一致的学习率迁移——在小模型上确定的最优学习率可直接用于大模型，无需重新调参。相比之下，nGPT 的最优学习率随模型规模显著漂移（最优 LR 的变化幅度与模型维度缩放倍数相关），需要昂贵的重复搜索。νGPT 保留了 nGPT 的训练速度优势（无需预热、无需权重衰减），同时具备了有原则的超参数可迁移性。跨宽度和深度的损失曲线实验证实，νGPT 的损失轨迹在不同规模下对齐，而 nGPT 则无法做到。 νGPT achieves consistent learning rate transfer across all tested model widths, depths, and token horizons — the optimal learning rate identified at small scale applies directly to large-scale models without retuning. In contrast, nGPT's optimal learning rate shifts significantly with model size (e.g., the best LR changes by factors correlated with model dimension scaling), requiring costly re-sweeps. νGPT maintains the training speed advantages of nGPT (no warmup, no weight decay needed) while adding principled transferability. Specific loss values and scaling curves across width and depth sweeps confirm that νGPT's loss trajectories align across model sizes whereas nGPT's do not.
🏁 结论 本文证明了 nGPT 学习率无法迁移的根源在于其参数化方案存在对齐偏差，而通过对齐指数和 μP 原理推导出的 νGPT 解决了这一问题，实现了跨宽度、深度和 token 量的稳定学习率迁移。νGPT 可作为 nGPT 的即插即用替代方案，同时兼具训练效率与有原则的超参数缩放能力。 This paper proves that nGPT's learning rate non-transferability stems from a misaligned parameterization, and that νGPT — derived via alignment exponents and μP principles — resolves this by achieving stable learning rate transfer across width, depth, and token horizon. The contribution is a drop-in replacement for nGPT that combines its training efficiency with principled hyperparameter scaling.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者首先通过数值实验确认 nGPT 的学习率确实无法在宽度、深度和 token 量之间迁移。随后，他们引入"对齐指数"框架——一种追踪梯度信号与权重矩阵内积如何随模型规模变化的理论工具——来精确诊断 nGPT 参数化方案的失效位置。基于这些诊断，他们修改了各参数组（嵌入层、注意力层、MLP 层）的缩放规则，推导出 νGPT。新参数化方案调整了学习率和权重初始化随模型维度与深度的缩放方式，确保无论模型大小如何变化，有效更新幅度都保持一致。 The authors start by numerically probing nGPT to confirm that its learning rate does not transfer across width, depth, and token horizon. They then apply the framework of alignment exponents — a theoretical tool that tracks how the inner products between gradient signals and weight matrices scale with model size — to diagnose exactly where nGPT's parameterization breaks down. Using these diagnostics, they modify the scaling rules for each parameter group (embeddings, attention, MLP layers) to derive νGPT. The new parameterization adjusts how learning rates and weight initializations scale with model dimension and depth, ensuring that the effective update magnitude remains consistent regardless of model size.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\eta^* \propto d^{-\alpha}\]
💡 最优学习率 η* 应随模型维度 d 以幂次 α 缩放；nGPT 中该指数不正确，νGPT 通过对齐指数分析修正了这一指数。 The optimal learning rate η* should scale with model dimension d by exponent α; nGPT has the wrong exponent, which νGPT corrects via alignment exponent analysis.
\[\Delta W = \eta \cdot \nabla_{W} \mathcal{L}, \quad \langle \Delta W, W \rangle \sim \mathcal{O}(1)\]
💡 μP 的核心思想：要求权重更新量与权重本身的内积在不同模型规模下保持 O(1) 量级，νGPT 将这一条件推广到归一化架构。 The core μP condition: the inner product between weight updates and weights should remain O(1) across model sizes; νGPT extends this condition to normalized architectures.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

❓ 待解决
的问题		 编程智能体的性能高度依赖"运行环境（harness）"——即连接模型与工具、执行环境的脚手架。但设计这些运行环境仍是高度手工的过程。自动化改进面临三大难题：可编辑组件种类繁杂、运行轨迹日志海量且含噪、难以追溯哪次修改带来了哪项提升。缺乏归因机制，自动演化就会沦为低效的试错。 Coding agents depend heavily on "harnesses"—the scaffolding that connects models to tools and execution environments—but designing these harnesses is still a labor-intensive manual process. Automating harness improvement is hard because the editable components are diverse, the execution logs (trajectories) are enormous and noisy, and it is difficult to trace which specific change caused which outcome. Without a principled way to attribute improvements, automated harness evolution degenerates into expensive trial-and-error.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **组件可观测性**：每个可编辑的运行环境组件（工具、中间件、记忆、系统提示）均获得文件级显式表示，使动作空间离散化且可回滚。 • **经验可观测性**：将海量原始轨迹（数百万token）浓缩为分层、可下钻的证据语料库，让演化智能体能高效提取可操作信号。 • **决策可观测性**：每次修改附带"自我预测"，后续与实际任务结果进行对比验证，将每次编辑变为可证伪的契约，防止盲目试错。 • **Component Observability**: Every editable harness component (tools, middleware, memory, system prompt) gets an explicit file-level representation, making the action space discrete and revertible. • **Experience Observability**: Millions of raw trajectory tokens are distilled into a layered, drill-down evidence corpus, so the evolving agent can efficiently mine actionable signals from past runs. • **Decision Observability**: Each harness edit is paired with a self-declared prediction that is later verified against actual task outcomes, turning every change into a falsifiable contract and preventing arbitrary edits.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AHE构成一个闭合演化循环：首先为每个运行环境组件（工具、中间件、长期记忆、系统提示）建立文件级表示，使演化智能体可直接读取、修改和回滚。每轮评估后，原始执行轨迹被压缩为结构化、层次化的证据语料库，在不超出上下文窗口的前提下保留关键的失败与成功信号。随后智能体提出有针对性的修改建议，并附上对预期结果的显式预测。下一轮中，实际任务结果与预测进行比对，形成紧密的反馈信号，驱动有归因依据的演化，而非随机搜索。 AHE operates as a closed loop: it first assigns every harness component (tools, middleware, long-term memory, system prompt) a file-level representation so an evolving agent can directly read, edit, and revert them. After each evaluation round, raw execution trajectories are compressed into a structured, hierarchical evidence corpus that preserves key failure and success signals without overwhelming the agent's context window. The agent then proposes targeted edits, each accompanied by an explicit prediction of expected outcome. In the next round, the actual task-level results are compared against the prediction, providing a tight feedback signal that drives principled, attribution-aware evolution rather than random search.
