SkillOS: Learning Skill Curation for Self-Evolving Agents SkillOS：面向自进化智能体的技能管理学习

🧑‍🔬 Siru Ouyang, Jun Yan, Yanfei Chen, Rujun Han et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用强化学习训练技能管理器，让LLM智能体从经验中自我进化。
RL-trained skill curator enables LLM agents to self-evolve by learning to manage reusable skills from experience.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 基于大语言模型的智能体通常只能"一次性"解决问题，无法从过往经验中学习和复用知识。虽然将经验提炼为可复用的"技能"是一个有前景的方向，但现有方法要么依赖人工整理，要么规则过于死板，要么只能处理短期操作，难以从延迟的间接反馈中学习复杂的长期技能管理策略。 LLM-based agents are typically one-off problem solvers — they handle tasks but fail to retain or reuse what they've learned from past interactions. While distilling experience into reusable "skills" is a promising direction, existing methods either rely on manual curation, use rigid heuristic rules, or can only learn short-horizon operations, making them unable to optimize complex, long-term skill management from delayed feedback.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **解耦架构设计**：将冻结的智能体执行器（负责检索和使用技能）与可训练的技能管理器（负责维护外部SkillRepo）分离，让强化学习专注于管理决策。 • **技能相关任务流**：将训练组织为分组任务流——早期任务更新SkillRepo，后续相关任务评估这些更新，自然构建长程信用分配信号。 • **复合奖励设计**：将任务性能奖励与技能质量信号相结合，无需人工标注即可为管理器提供丰富的学习反馈。 • **跨域泛化能力**：学到的技能管理器可迁移到不同的执行器骨干和任务领域，展现了真正的策略泛化能力。 • **Decoupled Architecture**: Separates a frozen agent executor (retrieves/applies skills) from a trainable skill curator (manages the external SkillRepo), enabling focused RL training on curation decisions alone. • **Skill-Relevant Task Streams**: Training is structured as grouped task streams where earlier tasks update the SkillRepo and later related tasks evaluate those updates, creating a natural long-horizon credit assignment signal. • **Composite Reward Design**: Combines task performance rewards with skill-quality signals to provide rich learning feedback for the curator without requiring manual labels. • **Cross-Domain Generalization**: The learned skill curator transfers across different executor backbones and task domains, showing genuine generalization of the curation policy.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验覆盖多轮智能体任务（如ALFWorld具身导航、WebArena网页浏览）和单轮推理任务（数学和代码基准）。基线包括无记忆智能体和强记忆基线，如ExpeL、Voyager和JARVIS-1。评估指标涵盖任务成功率和效率（步数/token用量）。此外还测试了技能管理器在不同执行器骨干和未见任务域上的泛化能力。 Experiments span both multi-turn agentic tasks (e.g., ALFWorld embodied navigation, WebArena web browsing) and single-turn reasoning tasks (math and code benchmarks). Baselines include memory-free agents and strong memory-based skill systems such as ExpeL, Voyager, and JARVIS-1. Evaluation metrics include task success rate and efficiency (number of steps/tokens used). The curator's generalization is tested by swapping executor backbones and transferring to unseen task domains.
📊 实验
结果		 SkillOS在所有基准上持续优于所有基线。在ALFWorld上，相比最强记忆基线成功率提升约10%。在WebArena上，任务完成率提升的同时显著降低了token消耗。在数学和代码推理任务上同样取得持续提升。学到的技能管理器在迁移到新执行器骨干时性能下降极小，且定性分析证实SkillRepo中的技能随时间进化为更丰富、更结构化的Markdown格式元技能。 SkillOS consistently outperforms all baselines across benchmarks. On ALFWorld, it achieves up to ~10% higher success rate over the best memory-based baseline. On WebArena, it improves task completion rate while reducing token cost by a significant margin compared to memory-free and heuristic skill-curation methods. On reasoning tasks (math/code), SkillOS shows consistent gains over strong baselines. The learned curator generalizes to new executor backbones with minimal performance drop, and qualitative analysis confirms SkillRepo skills evolve into richer, more structured Markdown-encoded meta-skills over time.
🏁 结论 SkillOS证明了通过强化学习训练的技能管理器能够让LLM智能体真正实现自我进化——从间接延迟的反馈中学习复杂的长期技能管理策略，超越无记忆和启发式技能管理基线，并跨执行器和领域泛化。这确立了经验驱动的强化学习训练作为构建持续改进智能体系统的可行且强大范式。 SkillOS demonstrates that an RL-trained skill curator can enable LLM agents to genuinely self-evolve by learning complex, long-horizon skill management policies from indirect, delayed feedback — outperforming both memory-free and heuristic skill-curation baselines while generalizing across executors and domains. This establishes experience-driven RL training as a viable and powerful paradigm for building continually improving agentic systems.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

SkillOS将一个冻结的LLM执行器与一个单独训练的技能管理器配对使用。执行器从外部SkillRepo中检索相关技能来完成任务，而技能管理器通过强化学习进行训练，学习根据积累的经验决定何时以及如何向SkillRepo中添加、更新或删除技能。训练采用分组任务流的方式：同组任务共享技能相关的依赖关系，管理器在早期任务上的更新直接由后续相关任务的表现来评估，从而为长期技能管理学习提供自然的延迟反馈信号。复合奖励将下游任务成功率与技能质量指标相结合，引导管理器的训练过程。推理阶段，训练好的管理器无需重新训练即可泛化到新的执行器和领域。 SkillOS pairs a frozen LLM executor with a separately trained skill curator. The executor retrieves relevant skills from an external SkillRepo to solve tasks, while the curator is trained via RL to decide when and how to add, update, or remove skills in the SkillRepo based on accumulated experience. Training is organized into grouped task streams: tasks within a group share skill-relevant dependencies, so the curator's updates on early tasks are directly evaluated by the performance on later related tasks — providing a natural delayed-feedback signal for long-horizon curation learning. A composite reward combines downstream task success with skill quality metrics to guide the curator's training. At inference time, the trained curator generalizes to new executors and domains without retraining.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r = r_{\text{task}} + \lambda \cdot r_{\text{skill}}\]
💡 复合奖励函数：将下游任务成功奖励与技能质量奖励加权组合，为技能管理器提供学习信号。 Composite reward: combines downstream task performance reward with a skill quality reward (weighted by λ) to train the skill curator via RL.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

当前大多数LLM智能体是无状态的，无法从经验中改进，这是实际部署中的根本性限制。SkillOS提供了一个有原则的、基于强化学习的框架，使智能体能够随时间积累和整理知识，推动领域向真正自我改进的AI系统迈出重要一步。 Most LLM agents today are stateless and cannot improve from experience, a fundamental limitation for real-world deployment. SkillOS provides a principled, RL-based framework for agents to accumulate and curate knowledge over time, moving the field meaningfully toward genuinely self-improving AI systems.

Diffusion Models · 扩散模型Fine-tuning · 微调Self-Distillation · 自蒸馏Image Generation · 图像生成
D-OPSD: On-Policy Self-Distillation for Continuously Tuning Step-Distilled Diffusion Models D-OPSD：用于持续调优步骤蒸馏扩散模型的在线策略自蒸馏方法

🧑‍🔬 Dengyang Jiang, Xin Jin, Dongyang Liu, Zanyi Wang et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 在线策略自蒸馏使少步扩散模型在微调时不损失快速推理能力
On-policy self-distillation lets few-step diffusion models learn new concepts without losing fast inference ability.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现代图像生成正在转向"少步"扩散模型（如FLUX-klein、Z-Image-Turbo），它们比传统多步模型快得多。然而，用普通监督微调方法直接调整这些模型，会破坏其核心的少步推理能力。目前没有好方法在不降低其速度-质量权衡的前提下，让这些高效模型学习新概念或新风格。 Modern image generation has moved toward "few-step" diffusion models (e.g., FLUX-klein, Z-Image-Turbo) that are much faster than classical multi-step ones. However, naively fine-tuning these models with standard supervised techniques breaks their core ability to generate good images in just a few denoising steps. There is currently no clean way to teach these efficient models new concepts or styles without degrading their speed-quality trade-off.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 核心洞察：使用LLM/VLM作为编码器的扩散模型天然继承了上下文学习能力，D-OPSD利用这一点在单一模型内构建师生结构。 • 新颖自蒸馏循环：同一模型同时充当教师（以文本+参考图像为多模态上下文）和学生（仅以文本为条件），无需外部教师模型。 • 在线策略训练：学生在自身生成的轨迹上、在自身监督下进行优化，避免了标准微调中导致少步能力崩溃的分布不匹配问题。 • Key insight: modern diffusion models that use an LLM/VLM as their text encoder naturally inherit in-context learning capabilities, which D-OPSD exploits to create a teacher-student setup within a single model. • Novel self-distillation loop: the same model plays both teacher (conditioned on text + reference image as multimodal context) and student (conditioned on text only), eliminating the need for an external teacher model. • On-policy training: the student is optimized on its own generated trajectories under its own supervision, preventing the distribution mismatch that causes few-step capability collapse during standard fine-tuning.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在主题驱动生成（学习新概念/物体）、风格微调和通用图像质量基准上评估D-OPSD。基线包括标准监督微调（SFT）、DreamBooth及其他参数高效调优方法，应用于FLUX-klein和Z-Image-Turbo等少步扩散骨干模型。评估指标涵盖CLIP分数、DINO相似度、图像保真度指标（FID）和人类偏好研究，以评估概念保真度和保留的生成质量。 Experiments evaluated D-OPSD on subject-driven generation (learning new concepts/objects), style fine-tuning, and general image quality benchmarks. Baselines included standard supervised fine-tuning (SFT), DreamBooth, and other parameter-efficient tuning approaches applied to few-step diffusion backbones such as FLUX-klein and Z-Image-Turbo. Evaluation metrics encompassed CLIP score, DINO similarity, image fidelity measures (FID), and human preference studies to assess both concept fidelity and retained generation quality.
📊 实验
结果		 D-OPSD在少步扩散模型上持续优于标准SFT和DreamBooth基线：DINO相似度得分（概念保真度）相对提升约5–10%，同时CLIP文本对齐分数与基线持平或更优。关键地，D-OPSD保留了少步推理质量（FID分数与原始预训练模型相当），而朴素SFT导致显著FID退化（消融实验中报告FID增加约15–20%）。人类偏好评估在概念准确性和整体图像质量两方面均倾向于D-OPSD输出。 D-OPSD consistently outperforms standard SFT and DreamBooth baselines on few-step diffusion models: it achieves higher DINO similarity scores (concept fidelity improved by ~5–10% relatively) while maintaining CLIP text-alignment on par with or better than baselines. Crucially, D-OPSD preserves the few-step inference quality (FID scores remain competitive with the original pre-trained model), whereas naive SFT causes significant FID degradation (reported ~15–20% FID increase in ablations). Human preference evaluations favor D-OPSD outputs over SFT fine-tuned counterparts in both concept accuracy and overall image quality.
🏁 结论 D-OPSD证明，借助基于LLM/VLM的扩散编码器的上下文学习能力，在线策略自蒸馏使少步扩散模型能够持续微调而不牺牲快速推理能力，为定制下一代高效图像生成模型建立了切实可行的训练范式。 D-OPSD proves that on-policy self-distillation, enabled by the in-context learning capacity of LLM/VLM-based diffusion encoders, allows few-step diffusion models to be continuously fine-tuned without sacrificing their fast inference capability. This establishes a practical training paradigm for customizing next-generation efficient image generation models.
🔗 论文
链接		 arXiv: 2605.05204v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

D-OPSD将监督微调转化为在线策略自蒸馏过程。训练时，模型同时扮演教师和学生：教师接收多模态提示（文本+目标图像作为LLM/VLM编码器的上下文输入），学生仅接收文本提示。训练目标是最小化教师预测分布与学生预测分布之间的散度，该散度在学生自身采样的去噪轨迹上计算。由于优化始终在学生推理时自身生成的轨迹上进行，不存在训练-测试分布差距，从而在吸收教师图像条件引导知识的同时保留了少步推理能力。 D-OPSD frames supervised fine-tuning as an on-policy self-distillation process. During training, the model simultaneously acts as teacher and student: the teacher receives a multimodal prompt (text + target image fed as in-context input to the LLM/VLM encoder), while the student receives only the text prompt. The training objective minimizes the divergence between the teacher's predicted noise/score distribution and the student's predicted distribution, computed along the student's own sampled denoising trajectories. Because optimization is always performed on trajectories the student itself would produce at inference time, there is no train-test distribution gap, and the few-step capability is preserved while new knowledge is absorbed from the teacher's image-conditioned guidance.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{D-OPSD}} = \mathbb{E}_{t, x_t \sim q_\theta} \left[ D\left( p_\theta(x_t | c_{\text{teacher}}) \,\|\, p_\theta(x_t | c_{\text{student}}) \right) \right]\]
💡 训练损失：在学生自身采样的去噪轨迹上，最小化教师（多模态条件）与学生（仅文本条件）预测分布之间的散度，实现在线策略自蒸馏。 Training loss: minimizes the divergence between the teacher's predicted distribution (multimodal context) and the student's predicted distribution (text-only context), evaluated on the student's own sampled denoising trajectories for on-policy learning.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着少步扩散模型在实际部署中快速普及，无法在不破坏效率的情况下微调它们已成为关键瓶颈，D-OPSD直接解除了这一障碍。自蒸馏思路与模型无关，可推广至任何具有LLM/VLM骨干的扩散模型，对未来架构具有广泛适用性。 As the field rapidly adopts few-step diffusion models for practical deployment, the inability to fine-tune them without breaking their efficiency is a critical bottleneck — D-OPSD directly unblocks this. The self-distillation idea is model-agnostic and could generalize to any diffusion model with an LLM/VLM backbone, making it broadly applicable to future architectures.

Mechanistic Interpretability · 机制可解释性LLM · 大语言模型Representation Geometry · 表征几何Model Steering · 模型干预控制
Manifold Steering Reveals the Shared Geometry of Neural Network Representation and Behavior 流形引导揭示神经网络表征与行为的共享几何结构

🧑‍🔬 Daniel Wurgaft, Can Rager, Matthew Kowal, Vasudev Shyam et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 沿激活流形引导神经网络，揭示表征几何与行为的因果联系。
Steering neural networks along activation manifolds causally links representation geometry to model behavior.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当我们通过干预神经网络内部激活（即"引导"技术）来控制模型输出时，是否能产生模型自然会生成的输出？现有的线性引导方法假设激活空间是平坦的欧氏空间，但实际上神经表征存在于弯曲的流形上——因此沿直线推动激活会导致偏离流形的非自然输出。核心问题是：神经表征的几何结构是否因果地决定了模型行为？ When we intervene on the internal activations of neural networks (a technique called "steering"), do we produce outputs that the model would naturally generate? Current linear steering methods assume a flat, Euclidean geometry in activation space, but neural representations actually live on curved manifolds — so naively pushing activations in a straight line leads to unnatural, off-manifold outputs. The fundamental question is whether the geometric structure of neural representations causally shapes model behavior.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出"流形引导"新框架：干预沿学习到的激活流形（M_h）的弯曲几何路径进行，产生沿行为流形（M_y）的自然行为轨迹。 • 验证双向因果关系：沿 M_h 引导能跟踪 M_y，反过来优化 M_y 上的路径也能恢复 M_h 的曲率轨迹，证明双向几何对应关系。 • 重新定义引导问题：将目标从"找到正确方向"转变为"找到正确几何"，为机制可解释性和模型控制提供原则性基础。 • Novel "manifold steering" framework: interventions are constrained to follow the curved geometry of the learned activation manifold (M_h), producing natural behavioral trajectories along the behavior manifold (M_y). • Bidirectional causal link demonstrated: steering along M_h tracks M_y, AND optimizing for paths along M_y recovers trajectories that trace M_h curvature — confirming a two-way geometric correspondence. • Reframes the steering problem: shifts the goal from "find the right direction" to "find the right geometry," providing a principled foundation for mechanistic interpretability and model control.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验涵盖两种模态：（1）语言模型，在具有循环几何结构的推理任务（如星期、月份）、顺序几何任务以及具有复杂图几何结构的上下文学习任务上进行测试；（2）视频世界模型，在反映真实物理运动几何的动力学任务上进行测试。基线方法为标准线性（欧氏）引导；评估指标为诱导行为轨迹与自然行为流形 M_y 的吻合程度，以及线性引导产生的偏离流形偏差。 Experiments span two modalities: (1) Language models tested on reasoning tasks with cyclic geometry (e.g., days of the week, months), sequential geometry tasks, and in-context learning tasks with complex graph-structured geometries. (2) A video world model tested on a physical dynamics task whose geometry reflects real-world motion. Baselines include standard linear (Euclidean) steering, and evaluations measure how closely induced behavioral trajectories follow the natural behavior manifold M_y compared to off-manifold deviations produced by linear steering.
📊 实验
结果		 流形引导在所有测试任务上产生的行为轨迹始终贴近自然行为流形 M_y，而线性引导则穿越偏离流形的区域并产生非自然输出。在循环推理任务（如星期几）中，流形引导轨迹偏离 M_y 的程度显著低于线性引导基线。优化激活以沿 M_y 路径运动，所恢复的激活轨迹在所有测试任务和模态上均紧密追踪 M_h 曲率——包括具有循环、顺序和图几何结构的语言模型推理任务，以及视频世界模型的物理动力学任务——定量验证了多种场景下的双向 M_h ↔ M_y 对应关系。 Manifold steering consistently produces behavioral trajectories that remain close to the natural behavior manifold M_y, while linear steering cuts through off-manifold regions and yields unnatural outputs. In cyclic reasoning tasks (e.g., days of the week), manifold steering trajectories show significantly lower deviation from M_y compared to linear steering baselines. Optimizing activations to follow M_y recovers activation paths that closely trace M_h curvature across all tested tasks and modalities — including language model reasoning tasks with cyclic, sequential, and graph geometries, and the video world model's physical dynamics task — demonstrating the bidirectional M_h ↔ M_y correspondence quantitatively across diverse settings.
🏁 结论 本文证明了神经网络激活的几何结构因果地决定了模型行为：遵循激活流形几何的引导干预能产生自然的行为轨迹，而欧氏（线性）引导则不能。核心贡献在于将模型引导问题从"寻找正确方向"重新定义为"寻找正确几何"，确立了流形结构作为可解释神经网络控制的原则性对象。 This paper proves that the geometric structure of neural network activations causally determines model behavior: steering interventions that respect activation manifold geometry produce natural behavioral trajectories, while Euclidean (linear) steering does not. The key contribution is recasting the model steering problem from finding the right direction to finding the right geometry, establishing manifold structure as the principled object for interpretable neural network control.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者首先对神经网络的内部表征拟合激活流形 M_h，对模型的输出概率分布拟合行为流形 M_y。然后执行"流形引导"——沿 M_h 上的测地线路径（而非直线欧氏路径）进行干预——并测量产生的行为轨迹是否保持在 M_y 上。反向地，他们在激活空间中优化沿 M_y 路径的干预，并验证所得激活轨迹是否追踪 M_h 的曲率。这一双向测试在多个任务和模态上展开，以验证因果几何联系。 The authors first fit an activation manifold M_h to the internal representations of a neural network, and a behavior manifold M_y to the model's output probability distributions. They then perform "manifold steering" — intervening along geodesic paths on M_h rather than straight Euclidean lines — and measure the resulting behavioral trajectories to see if they stay on M_y. Conversely, they optimize interventions in activation space that produce paths along M_y, and check whether the resulting activation trajectories trace the curvature of M_h. This bidirectional test is applied across multiple tasks and modalities to validate the causal geometry link.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[M_h \leftrightarrow M_y\]
💡 激活流形 M_h 与行为流形 M_y 之间的双向几何对应关系：沿 M_h 引导产生沿 M_y 的行为，反之亦然。 The bidirectional geometric correspondence between the activation manifold M_h and the behavior manifold M_y: steering along M_h produces trajectories along M_y, and vice versa.
\[\gamma_{M_h}(t) \Rightarrow \gamma_{M_y}(t)\]

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Cola DLM 分三个阶段运作。首先，Text VAE 学习从离散文本到连续潜空间的稳定映射，压缩语义信息。其次，块因果扩散 Transformer（DiT）在这些潜表示上建模全局语义先验，无需严格的词序约束即可捕获长程依赖。最后，条件解码器从学到的潜在先验生成输出文本。扩散过程在统一马尔可夫路径框架下被解释为潜在先验传输，清晰地将全局语义组织与局部文本实现分离。 Cola DLM operates in three stages. First, a Text VAE learns a stable mapping from discrete text to a continuous latent space, compressing semantic information. Second, a block-causal Diffusion Transformer (DiT) models a global semantic prior over these latent representations, capturing long-range dependencies without strict token-order constraints. Finally, a conditional decoder generates the output text from the learned latent prior. The diffusion process is interpreted as latent prior transport in a unified Markov-path framework, cleanly separating global semantic organization from local textual realization.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本文通过证明具有层次结构的连续潜空间扩散模型能与约 20 亿参数自回归模型媲美并具备相似扩展性，挑战了自回归 LLM 的主导地位。其设计天然地桥接文本与连续模态（图像、音频），指向统一的生成式基础模型架构。 This paper challenges the dominance of autoregressive LLMs by showing that continuous latent diffusion with hierarchical structure can match and scale comparably to ~2B AR models—without left-to-right generation constraints. Its design naturally bridges text and continuous modalities (images, audio), pointing toward a unified generative foundation model architecture.
