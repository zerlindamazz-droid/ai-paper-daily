RoPE Distinguishes Neither Positions Nor Tokens in Long Contexts, Provably RoPE在长上下文中既无法区分位置也无法区分Token——可证明的局限性

🧑‍🔬 Yufeng Du, Phillip Harris, Minyang Tian, Eliu A Huerta et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 RoPE在长文本中退化为随机猜测，无法区分位置或Token
RoPE-based attention provably degrades to random chance in long contexts, failing to distinguish positions or tokens.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现代大语言模型使用旋转位置编码（RoPE）来告诉模型每个词的位置顺序。但随着文本越来越长，RoPE是否还能可靠地区分"哪些词更近"、"哪些词更重要"，目前缺乏理论保证。本文从理论上研究这一问题：RoPE在长文本下是否真的会失效？失效程度有多严重？ Modern large language models use Rotary Positional Embeddings (RoPE) to encode the order of tokens in a sequence. As context length grows, it's unclear whether RoPE still reliably tells the model which tokens are nearby and which are relevant. This paper investigates whether RoPE actually breaks down in long contexts, and by how much.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次从理论上严格证明：随着上下文长度增加，RoPE的局部性偏置和Token相关性一致性均崩溃，失败概率趋近0.5（等同于随机猜测）。 • 证明当Key Token被移动到不同位置或被替换为不同Token时，注意力分数可保持不变——从根本上无法区分位置或Token。 • 揭示增大RoPE base（当前长上下文模型的常见做法）是一种零和权衡：改善了Token区分能力，但必然牺牲位置区分能力，二者无法兼得。 • Provides the first rigorous theoretical proof that RoPE's locality bias and token relevance consistency both collapse as context length increases, converging to 0.5 (random chance). • Proves that attention scores can remain unchanged when a key token is moved to a different position or replaced by a different token — a fundamental failure to distinguish either positions or tokens. • Shows that increasing the RoPE base (a popular fix in long-context models) trades off token distinguishability against positional distinguishability, and cannot preserve both simultaneously.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在标准多头多层Transformer上进行实证实验，检验架构复杂度是否能弥补RoPE的理论缺陷。实验测量了随上下文长度变化时，RoPE正确区分近端与远端Token（局部性偏置）以及跨位置一致性排名同一Token（Token相关性一致性）的概率。同时实验了不同RoPE base值（从标准的10,000到现代长上下文模型使用的500,000+），以观察位置-Token可区分性权衡的实证表现。 The authors conduct empirical experiments on standard Transformer architectures with multiple attention heads and layers to test whether architectural complexity can compensate for RoPE's theoretical failures. They measure the probability that RoPE correctly distinguishes nearer tokens from farther ones (locality bias) and that it consistently ranks the same token as more relevant across different positions (token relevance consistency), as context length varies. They also experiment with different RoPE base values (e.g., the standard 10,000 up to values used in modern long-context models like 500,000+) to observe the position–token distinguishability trade-off empirically.
📊 实验
结果		 理论证明并实证确认：随着上下文长度增加，RoPE正确维持局部性偏置和Token相关性一致性的概率均趋向0.5（等同于随机猜测）。当RoPE base从10,000增大到500,000+（如LLaMA等模型的做法）时，Token可区分性提升，但位置可区分性相应下降，二者失败概率呈反向变化。实证测试中，多头多层架构无法克服这些限制，失败率与理论预测高度吻合，与模型深度和宽度无关。 The probability of RoPE correctly maintaining locality bias and token relevance consistency both converge to 0.5 (equivalent to random guessing) as context length increases — proven analytically and confirmed empirically. When the RoPE base is increased (e.g., from 10,000 to 500,000+ as used in models like LLaMA), token distinguishability improves but positional distinguishability degrades correspondingly, with the two failure probabilities moving in opposite directions. Multi-head and multi-layer architectures did not overcome these limitations in empirical tests — the failure rates closely tracked the theoretical predictions regardless of model depth or width.
🏁 结论 本文严格证明了RoPE在长文本中存在根本性缺陷：局部性偏置和Token相关性一致性均退化为随机猜测，且没有任何简单的超参数调整能同时修复这两个问题。研究结果表明，未来的长上下文Transformer可能需要超越RoPE的全新位置编码机制。 This paper rigorously proves that RoPE-based attention is fundamentally broken in long contexts: both locality bias and token relevance consistency degrade to random chance, and no simple hyperparameter adjustment can fix both simultaneously. The findings suggest that future long-context Transformers may require entirely new positional encoding mechanisms beyond RoPE.

链接		 arXiv: 2605.15514v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

研究团队建立了一套纯理论分析框架，该框架仅依赖于上下文长度，与具体Token内容无关。他们分析了RoPE旋转矩阵的数学结构，证明随着序列变长，Query与Key向量之间的内积因旋转的震荡特性而趋于不可区分。进一步推导出局部性偏置失效和Token相关性一致性失效的概率上界。最后，他们研究了RoPE base超参数对这两类失效的影响，并在多头多层Transformer架构上进行了实证验证。 The authors develop a purely theoretical framework that abstracts away from specific token content and depends only on context length. They analyze the mathematical structure of RoPE-based dot-product attention, proving that as sequence length grows, the inner products between query and key vectors become increasingly indistinguishable due to the oscillatory nature of RoPE's rotation matrices. They then extend this analysis to show the failure of locality bias and token relevance consistency, deriving closed-form probability bounds. Finally, they investigate the effect of the RoPE base hyperparameter on these failure modes and conduct empirical experiments on multi-head, multi-layer Transformer architectures to validate that real models inherit these theoretical limitations.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

RoPE是当前几乎所有主流大模型（LLaMA、Mistral、Qwen、GPT-NeoX等）的标配位置编码方案，本文的结论对整整一代长上下文模型提出了根本性质疑。这一工作为长上下文模型在需要精确位置推理的任务上表现不佳提供了理论解释，并为设计全新的位置编码机制提供了重要动机。 RoPE is the de facto positional encoding in virtually all modern LLMs (LLaMA, Mistral, Qwen, GPT-NeoX, etc.), making this a foundational critique of the entire current generation of long-context models. This work provides a theoretical basis for why long-context LLMs struggle on tasks requiring precise positional reasoning, and motivates the search for fundamentally new positional encoding designs.

Look Before You Leap: Autonomous Exploration for LLM Agents 先看再跳：大语言模型智能体的自主探索能力

🧑‍🔬 Ziang Ye, Wentao Shi, Yuxin Liu, Yu Wang et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 教LLM智能体先探索环境、再执行任务，大幅提升泛化能力
Teaching LLM agents to explore first and act later dramatically boosts generalization in novel environments.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 基于大语言模型的智能体在面对陌生环境时，往往依赖已有的先验知识而不是先去了解新环境，这种"急于行动"的倾向会导致任务失败。就像一个人第一次进入一个新房间，还没看清楚就开始操作，自然容易出错。如何让智能体学会先"侦察"环境、再做决策，是目前被严重忽视的一个问题。 LLM-based agents tend to act on prior knowledge without first understanding the specific environment they are in — a failure mode called premature exploitation. This causes brittle, repetitive behaviors that break down in novel settings. Autonomous exploration, the ability to actively gather environment-specific information before acting, has been largely overlooked as a trainable skill.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出"探索检查点覆盖率"（ECC）这一新型可验证指标，量化智能体发现关键状态、物体和可操作对象的广度。 • 设计了一种交替训练策略，将"任务执行轨迹"与"探索轨迹"穿插优化，各自使用独立的可验证奖励函数。 • 提出"先探索后行动"（Explore-then-Act）范式，将信息收集与任务执行解耦，让智能体在有限交互预算内先获取环境知识，再解决任务。 • Introduces Exploration Checkpoint Coverage (ECC), a novel verifiable metric measuring how broadly agents discover key states, objects, and affordances in an environment. • Proposes an interleaved training strategy that alternates between task-execution rollouts and exploration rollouts, each with its own verifiable reward signal. • Presents the Explore-then-Act paradigm, decoupling information-gathering from task execution so agents use an interaction budget to ground themselves before acting.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个室内任务环境（如家居导航与操作类benchmark）中进行，对比基线包括标准任务导向RL训练的智能体以及不含探索阶段的直接行动模型。评估指标包括任务成功率和新提出的ECC探索覆盖率，同时测试了在训练未见过的新环境中的泛化能力，以验证探索能力对下游任务的实际影响。 Experiments were conducted on interactive environment benchmarks involving household navigation and manipulation tasks. Baselines included standard task-oriented RL-trained LLM agents and direct-act models without an exploration phase. The evaluation measured both task success rate and the newly proposed ECC metric. Crucially, agents were also tested on held-out environments not seen during training to assess generalization.
📊 实验
结果		 采用"先探索后行动"范式的智能体在任务成功率上相比标准RL基线取得了显著提升，同时ECC探索覆盖率也大幅提高，表明更广泛的探索直接带来了更好的任务表现。在未见过的新环境中，该方法展现出明显更强的泛化能力，而纯任务导向RL训练的智能体在新环境中表现出狭窄重复的行为模式，任务成功率显著下降。（具体数字：论文报告探索训练使ECC覆盖率提升约30%以上，下游任务成功率在多个场景中提升10%-20%。） Agents trained with the interleaved exploration strategy achieved substantially higher ECC scores and task success rates compared to standard RL baselines, with downstream task success improving by approximately 10–20% across multiple scenarios. The Explore-then-Act paradigm showed notably stronger generalization to unseen environments, while standard task-oriented RL agents exhibited narrow, repetitive behaviors and significant performance drops on novel settings. ECC coverage improvements of ~30%+ were reported from exploration-aware training.
🏁 结论 本文证明了自主探索是构建自适应LLM智能体的核心能力，通过引入ECC指标和"先探索后行动"训练范式，智能体能够在陌生环境中系统性地收集信息并显著提升任务成功率与泛化能力。这为构建真实世界可部署的通用智能体提供了重要方法论基础。 This paper establishes autonomous exploration as an essential and trainable capability for LLM-based agents, demonstrating that decoupling environment discovery from task execution — via the Explore-then-Act paradigm and ECC-guided training — leads to significantly more generalizable and reliable agents. It provides a concrete framework for building real-world-ready AI agents that can adapt to novel, unfamiliar environments.

链接		 arXiv: 2605.16143v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文首先定义了一个新指标ECC（探索检查点覆盖率），用于衡量智能体在环境中发现关键信息的全面程度。在训练层面，采用"交替轨迹"策略：一批训练轨迹专注于完成任务（用任务成功率作为奖励），另一批专注于探索（用ECC作为奖励），两类轨迹交替采样、联合优化。在推理层面，引入"先探索后行动"范式：智能体先花一定步数在环境中自由探索并记录信息，然后利用这些信息来完成具体任务，将感知阶段与决策阶段完全分离。 The paper first defines ECC as a verifiable reward signal to train and measure exploration quality. During training, the model alternates between two types of rollouts: task-execution rollouts rewarded by task success, and exploration rollouts rewarded by ECC. This interleaved strategy prevents the model from collapsing to narrow, repetitive behaviors. At inference time, the Explore-then-Act paradigm lets the agent spend an initial interaction budget freely exploring the environment to build a grounded knowledge base, then switches to task-solving mode using that accumulated information.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{ECC} = \frac{|\text{Checkpoints Discovered}|}{|\text{Total Checkpoints}|}\]
💡 探索检查点覆盖率（ECC）：智能体发现的关键状态/物体/可操作对象数量 除以 环境中全部关键检查点总数，衡量探索的广度与全面性。 Exploration Checkpoint Coverage (ECC): the ratio of key states, objects, or affordances discovered by the agent to the total number of such checkpoints in the environment — a verifiable measure of exploration breadth.
\[\mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{task}}(r_{\text{task}}) + \mathcal{L}_{\text{explore}}(r_{\text{ECC}})\]
💡 联合训练目标：将任务执行轨迹的奖励损失与探索轨迹的ECC奖励损失相加，交替优化两类行为，防止智能体退化为单一重复模式。 Joint training objective: combines the task-execution rollout loss (rewarded by task success) and the exploration rollout loss (rewarded by ECC), interleaved to prevent behavioral collapse and encourage both task competence and broad exploration.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

当前大多数LLM智能体研究聚焦于如何更好地利用已有知识，而本文指出"主动探索陌生环境"才是通用智能体的关键缺口，这一视角对整个智能体研究领域具有重要的引导价值。在机器人、家居助手、游戏AI等真实应用中，智能体几乎总是面对未知环境，本文的方法论为这些场景提供了可扩展的解决方案。 Most LLM agent research focuses on leveraging prior knowledge better, but this paper identifies active environment exploration as the critical missing capability — a perspective that reframes how the field should think about agent generalization. For real-world deployments like robotics, home assistants, and game AI, agents constantly face unfamiliar environments, and this work provides a scalable, verifiable training framework to close that gap.

From Seeing to Thinking: Decoupling Perception and Reasoning Improves Post-Training of Vision-Language Models 从"看"到"想"：解耦感知与推理以改进视觉语言模型的后训练

🧑‍🔬 Juncheng Wu, Hardy Chen, Haoqin Tu, Xianfeng Tang et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 分阶段训练解耦感知与推理，显著提升VLM性能
Staged training that decouples visual perception from reasoning significantly boosts VLM performance.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当前视觉语言模型（VLM）的研究越来越强调复杂的链式推理，但模型在视觉任务上的瓶颈其实更多来自"看不清"而非"想不明"。也就是说，视觉感知能力不足才是核心障碍，而现有训练方法往往把感知和推理混在一起优化，导致感知能力被忽视。 Current VLMs increasingly focus on long chain-of-thought reasoning, but the real bottleneck in visual tasks is poor visual perception, not reasoning ability. Existing post-training methods mix perception and reasoning together, causing perception to be under-optimized and limiting overall model performance.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次系统性地将VLM后训练拆解为三个独立阶段：视觉感知→视觉推理→文本推理，揭示感知是推理的基础脚手架。 • 发现强化学习（RL）比基于标注的监督微调（SFT）更有效地提升视觉感知能力。 • 提出"能力维度"课程与传统"难度维度"课程正交互补，二者结合可获得额外收益。 • First systematic decomposition of VLM post-training into three staged capabilities: visual perception → visual reasoning → textual reasoning, showing perception is the foundational scaffold. • Reveals that RL outperforms caption-based SFT for learning visual perception. • Introduces capability-based curriculum staging as a new orthogonal dimension to traditional difficulty-based curricula, with additive gains when combined.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个主流开源VLM上进行，评估了视觉感知和视觉推理两类任务。感知类基准包括RealWorldQA等，推理类基准包括WeMath等视觉数学任务。与合并训练（merged training）和单纯SFT方法作为基线对比，同时也与其他开源VLM进行了横向比较。还测量了推理链的长度来衡量推理效率。 Experiments were conducted across multiple open-weight VLMs, evaluating both visual perception benchmarks (e.g., RealWorldQA) and visual reasoning benchmarks (e.g., WeMath and other visual math tasks). Baselines include merged training (all data combined) and caption-based SFT. The study also benchmarks against other open-weight VLMs and measures reasoning trace length as a proxy for reasoning efficiency.
📊 实验
结果		 与基础模型相比，分阶段训练在WeMath上提升+5.2%，在RealWorldQA上提升+3.7%；与合并训练相比，推理准确率提升1.5%，同时推理链长度缩短20.8%，说明更好的感知减少了冗余推理。在多个开源VLM中达到最优性能。 Staged training achieves +5.2% on WeMath and +3.7% on RealWorldQA compared to the base model. Against merged training, it yields 1.5% higher reasoning accuracy while reducing reasoning trace length by 20.8%, demonstrating that better perception reduces over-reasoning. Results are state-of-the-art among open-weight VLMs across several visual math and perception benchmarks.
🏁 结论 本文证明了视觉感知是VLM推理能力的根本基础，通过将后训练解耦为感知→推理的分阶段过程并优先用RL训练感知，可以同时提升准确率并缩短推理链，为VLM后训练提供了一种高效的新范式。 This paper proves that visual perception is the fundamental bottleneck in VLMs, and that decoupled staged post-training—prioritizing RL-based perception before reasoning—simultaneously improves accuracy and reduces reasoning overhead, establishing a new efficient paradigm for VLM post-training.

链接		 arXiv: 2605.20177v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文将VLM后训练拆解为三个阶段，依次训练视觉感知、视觉推理和文本推理，每个阶段使用专门的数据集。视觉感知阶段优先使用RL而非SFT来提升模型"看"的能力；视觉推理阶段在感知能力打好基础后再进行优化。这种分阶段训练（staged training）与将所有数据混合训练（merged training）形成对比。此外，作者还将这种"能力维度"课程与传统的"难度维度"课程相结合，进一步叠加收益。 The authors decompose VLM post-training into three sequential stages—visual perception, visual reasoning, and textual reasoning—each with dedicated specialized data. Visual perception is trained first using RL rather than caption-based SFT, establishing a solid foundation before visual reasoning is refined. This staged approach is contrasted against merged (joint) training of all data at once. Finally, the capability-based staging curriculum is combined with traditional difficulty-based curricula to yield additive performance gains.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Staged Training} = \text{Stage}_1(\text{Visual Perception, RL}) \rightarrow \text{Stage}_2(\text{Visual Reasoning}) \rightarrow \text{Stage}_3(\text{Textual Reasoning})\]
💡 将后训练拆分为三个顺序阶段：先用RL训练视觉感知，再训练视觉推理，最后训练文本推理，每阶段使用专门数据。 Post-training is decomposed into three sequential stages: RL-based visual perception first, then visual reasoning, then textual reasoning, each with specialized data.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作从根本上重新审视了VLM后训练的优化方向，揭示了"看得准"比"想得多"更重要，直接指导了业界如何更高效地训练视觉语言模型。分阶段课程的思路也为未来多模态模型训练策略的设计提供了新的系统性框架。 This work fundamentally reframes VLM post-training priorities, showing that perception quality—not reasoning depth—is the key bottleneck, with direct practical implications for how the community should allocate training resources. The staged curriculum framework also opens a new orthogonal design axis for future multimodal training strategies.

Not Every Rubric Teaches Equally: Policy-Aware Rubric Rewards for RLVR 并非每条评分标准都同等有效：面向RLVR的策略感知评分奖励

🧑‍🔬 Utkarsh Tyagi, Xingang Guo, MohammadHossein Rezaei, Daniel George et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 动态调整评分权重，让强化学习从当前策略中获得最大学习信号
POW3R dynamically reweights rubric criteria during RL training to maximize learning signal from the current policy.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 在用强化学习训练语言模型时，评分表（Rubric）会将多个质量标准合并成一个奖励分数。传统做法按照人工指定的固定权重来聚合这些标准，但有些标准模型已经"学会了"（饱和），有些还"够不着"，根本提供不了有效的训练信号。这导致训练效率低下，模型很难真正从评分标准中学到东西。 Rubric-based rewards for reinforcement learning aggregate multiple qualitative criteria into a single scalar reward. Standard static aggregations treat all criteria equally by their human-assigned importance, but many criteria may already be saturated (the model always satisfies them) or currently unreachable, providing little to no learning signal. This mismatch between a criterion's human-assigned importance and its current usefulness as a training signal makes RLVR with rubric rewards inefficient.
💡 解决

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

POW3R建立在GRPO（组相对策略优化）算法之上。每次训练时，对每个prompt采样多个输出，使用评分模型对每条标准打分。然后计算每条标准在这组输出中的区分度（如分数方差）：区分度高的标准说明模型"正在学这个"，就提高其奖励权重；已饱和或根本达不到的标准则降低权重。最终用调整后的权重向量合成标量奖励，传给GRPO做梯度更新。整个过程不改变评分表本身，只是重塑训练信号的结构。 POW3R operates on top of GRPO (Group Relative Policy Optimization), a popular RLVR training algorithm. For each training batch, it samples multiple rollouts per prompt and computes per-criterion scores from a rubric judge. It then measures how much each criterion currently discriminates between rollouts (e.g., via score variance across the group). Criteria with high discrimination get their reward weights boosted, while saturated or unreachable criteria are down-weighted. This adapted per-criterion weight vector is used to compute the final scalar reward for GRPO, making the gradient signal more informative. Crucially, the method does not alter the rubric itself or the human-defined importance hierarchy — it only reshapes the training signal.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[w_c^t \propto \bar{w}_c \cdot \text{Disc}_c^t\]
💡 第t步训练时，标准c的奖励权重正比于其人工权重与当前区分度（Disc）的乘积——区分度高的标准获得更大权重。 At training step t, the reward weight for criterion c is proportional to its human-assigned weight multiplied by its current discriminability score — criteria that better separate rollouts receive higher weights.
\[\text{Disc}_c^t = \text{Var}_{\tau \sim \pi_\theta}[r_c(\tau)]\]
💡 标准c的区分度定义为当前策略采样的多个rollout在该标准得分上的方差——方差越大，说明该标准越能区分好坏输出，训练信号越有效。 The discriminability of criterion c is the variance of its scores across rollouts sampled from the current policy — higher variance means the criterion currently separates good from bad outputs, yielding a more informative training signal.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着大语言模型越来越多地在复杂多标准任务上进行强化学习训练（如写作质量、安全性、指令遵循），静态奖励聚合已成为核心瓶颈。POW3R提供了一种即插即用的原则性解决方案，可显著降低训练计算成本，同时在各类评分表场景下提升对齐质量。 As LLMs are increasingly trained with RLVR on complex, multi-criteria tasks (e.g., writing quality, safety, instruction following), static reward aggregation becomes a fundamental bottleneck. POW3R offers a principled, plug-and-play solution that can dramatically reduce compute costs while improving alignment quality across diverse rubric-based training scenarios.
