Nemotron-Cascade 2: Post-Training LLMs with Cascade RL and Multi-Domain On-Policy Distillation Nemotron-Cascade 2：基于级联强化学习与多领域在线策略蒸馏的大语言模型后训练

🧑‍🔬 Zhuolin Yang, Zihan Liu, Yang Chen, Wenliang Dai et al.

图 Figure · Page 1 of paper

🎯 30B MoE模型以极低参数量达到IMO/IOI金牌级数学推理水平
A 30B MoE model achieves Olympic gold-medal math and coding performance with 20x fewer parameters than frontier models.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

训练一个既小巧又具备顶尖推理能力的语言模型极具挑战性。现有的大型前沿模型（如671B参数模型）虽然性能卓越，但部署成本极高。如何让一个只激活3B参数的小模型也能解决奥林匹克竞赛级别的数学和编程难题？ Training a compact language model that rivals much larger frontier models in complex reasoning is extremely difficult. Existing top-performing models require hundreds of billions of parameters, making deployment costly. The challenge is enabling a model with only 3B activated parameters to solve Olympic-level mathematics and programming competition problems.

⚙️ Method · 方法

本文提出了Nemotron-Cascade 2，首先在精心筛选的数据集上进行监督微调（SFT），然后大幅扩展了"级联强化学习（Cascade RL）"训练框架，覆盖更广泛的推理和智能体（Agentic）任务领域。核心创新在于引入了"多领域在线策略蒸馏"技术：在级联RL训练过程中，针对每个具体领域，动态选用该领域最强的中间教师模型进行蒸馏，从而及时修复训练中出现的性能退步问题，并持续推动各项能力的提升。模型和训练数据均已开源发布。 Nemotron-Cascade 2 first applies supervised fine-tuning (SFT) on a carefully curated dataset, then substantially expands the Cascade RL training framework to cover a much broader range of reasoning and agentic domains. The key innovation is multi-domain on-policy distillation: throughout the Cascade RL process, the strongest intermediate teacher model for each specific domain is used to distill knowledge on-policy, efficiently recovering benchmark regressions that arise during training and sustaining continuous performance gains. Both model checkpoints and training data are publicly released.

📊 Results · 结果

Nemotron-Cascade 2（30B MoE，仅激活3B参数）成为继DeepSeekV3.2-Special-671B-A37B之后，全球第二个在2025年国际数学奥林匹克（IMO）、国际信息学奥林匹克（IOI）以及ICPC世界总决赛中同时达到金牌级别表现的开源大语言模型。与671B参数的对比模型相比，Nemotron-Cascade 2实现了约20倍的参数效率提升，在数学和编程推理性能上接近当前最强的开源前沿模型水平，同时还具备强大的智能体（Agentic）能力。 Nemotron-Cascade 2 (30B MoE, 3B activated parameters) becomes only the second open-weight LLM — after DeepSeekV3.2-Special-671B-A37B — to achieve Gold Medal-level performance across all three of: the 2025 International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO), the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI), and the ICPC World Finals. It achieves this with approximately 20x fewer parameters than the 671B comparison model, with mathematical and coding reasoning performance approaching that of frontier open models, while also demonstrating strong agentic task capabilities.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作证明了"智能密度"可以通过精细的后训练策略（级联RL + 多领域在线蒸馏）大幅提升，打破了"顶尖推理能力必须依赖超大模型"的固有认知。模型和数据的完全开源，将为整个社区提供宝贵的研究资源，推动高效推理模型的进一步发展。 This work demonstrates that exceptional reasoning intelligence can be packed into a highly efficient MoE model through advanced post-training techniques, fundamentally challenging the assumption that frontier-level reasoning requires hundreds of billions of parameters. By fully open-sourcing both model checkpoints and training data, it provides the broader research community with valuable resources to advance efficient, high-capability AI systems.

Spectrally-Guided Diffusion Noise Schedules 频谱引导的扩散噪声调度

🧑‍🔬 Carlos Esteves, Ameesh Makadia

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 基于图像频谱特性，为扩散模型设计最优噪声调度
Principled spectral-guided noise schedules eliminate redundant diffusion steps and boost image generation quality.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

扩散模型的性能高度依赖噪声调度（即训练和采样过程中噪声水平的安排方式），但现有的噪声调度通常是手工设计的，需要针对不同分辨率反复手动调整，缺乏理论依据，效率低下。 Diffusion models rely heavily on noise schedules — the sequence of noise levels applied during training and sampling — but these schedules are typically handcrafted and require tedious manual tuning for different image resolutions. There is no principled framework to determine optimal minimum and maximum noise levels, leading to wasted computation on redundant diffusion steps.

⚙️ Method · 方法

本文利用图像的频谱特性（即图像在频率域上的能量分布）为每张图像量身定制噪声调度。通过推导噪声最小值和最大值有效性的理论上下界，作者设计出"紧凑型"噪声调度，去除冗余步骤。在推理阶段，模型根据输入图像的频谱信息有条件地采样噪声调度，从而实现逐实例自适应的生成过程。 The authors derive theoretical bounds on the efficacy of minimum and maximum noise levels using each image's spectral properties — essentially its energy distribution across spatial frequencies. These bounds allow them to construct "tight" per-instance noise schedules that cut out redundant steps at both ends of the diffusion chain. During inference, the model conditionally samples these spectral-guided schedules based on each input image's frequency characteristics, enabling adaptive, instance-specific generation.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[q(x_t | x_0) = \mathcal{N}(x_t; \sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_t} x_0, (1 - \bar{\alpha}_t) \mathbf{I})\]
💡 扩散前向过程：在时间步t，图像x₀被逐渐加入高斯噪声，信号保留比例为√ᾱₜ，噪声方差为(1-ᾱₜ)。噪声调度本质上是对ᾱₜ序列的设计。 The forward diffusion process: at timestep t, the original image x₀ is corrupted with Gaussian noise. The schedule controls ᾱₜ, which determines how much signal is retained versus noise added at each step.
\[\text{SNR}(t) = \frac{\bar{\alpha}_t}{1 - \bar{\alpha}_t}\]
💡 信噪比（SNR）：衡量在时间步t图像信号相对于噪声的强度。频谱引导的噪声调度通过图像的频率能量分布来确定每张图像的有效SNR范围边界，从而裁剪冗余步骤。 Signal-to-noise ratio at timestep t: the ratio of retained signal power to noise power. The spectral approach uses per-image frequency energy to set tight bounds on the useful SNR range, cutting steps where SNR is too high or too low to contribute meaningfully.

📊 Results · 结果

实验表明，所提出的噪声调度在单阶段像素扩散模型上提升了生成质量，尤其在低步数采样（low-step regime）场景下效果显著。该方法有效减少了冗余扩散步骤，在保持甚至提升图像质量的同时提高了采样效率。具体数值提升体现在FID等生成质量指标上，低步数下的改善幅度尤为突出。 The proposed spectral noise schedules improve generative quality on single-stage pixel diffusion models, with the most pronounced gains in the low-step sampling regime — the practically important setting where fewer denoising steps are used for faster inference. The method demonstrably eliminates redundant steps at both ends of the diffusion process, yielding better FID scores compared to standard handcrafted schedules, particularly when the number of sampling steps is small.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

噪声调度是扩散模型的核心组件，本文首次将图像频谱分析与噪声调度设计相结合，提供了一套有理论支撑的自动化设计方案，减少了对繁琐人工调参的依赖。这对提升扩散模型在实际部署中的效率和质量具有重要意义，尤其对低步数快速生成场景价值突出。 This work shifts noise schedule design from art to science by grounding it in spectral theory, potentially replacing tedious manual tuning across resolutions with a principled, automated approach. Improvements in the low-step regime are especially impactful for real-world deployment, where fast inference is critical for practical image and video generation applications.

multilingual embeddings · 多语言嵌入LLM fine-tuning · 大语言模型微调multilinguality · 多语言性representation learning · 表示学习
F2LLM-v2: Inclusive, Performant, and Efficient Embeddings for a Multilingual World F2LLM-v2：面向多语言世界的包容性、高性能与高效嵌入模型

🧑‍🔬 Ziyin Zhang, Zihan Liao, Hang Yu, Peng Di et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 覆盖200+语言的多尺寸多语言嵌入模型家族，兼顾性能与效率
A family of 8 multilingual embedding models spanning 80M–14B parameters, covering 200+ languages with state-of-the-art efficiency.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

现有的大语言模型嵌入模型往往只关注英语或少数高资源语言，对中低资源语言支持不足，同时模型体积庞大、计算开销高，难以在资源受限的场景中部署。如何在保持高性能的同时，兼顾多语言覆盖广度与模型效率，是当前嵌入模型研究的核心挑战。 Existing LLM-based embedding models tend to focus on English and a handful of high-resource languages, leaving mid- and low-resource languages underserved. At the same time, these models are often large and computationally expensive, making them impractical for resource-constrained deployments. The challenge is to achieve strong multilingual coverage without sacrificing performance or efficiency.

⚙️ Method · 方法

F2LLM-v2 采用两阶段基于LLM的嵌入训练流程，首先在6000万条高质量公开数据样本上进行大规模多语言预训练，重点覆盖中低资源语言。随后引入"俄罗斯套娃学习"（Matryoshka Learning）技术，使同一模型可输出不同维度的嵌入向量，灵活适配不同场景。最后结合模型剪枝与知识蒸馏技术，将大模型的能力迁移至更小的模型中，在8个不同规模（80M至14B）之间实现性能与效率的平衡。 F2LLM-v2 uses a two-stage LLM-based embedding training pipeline, first pre-training on a curated dataset of 60 million high-quality multilingual samples with emphasis on underserved languages. Matryoshka learning is then applied so that a single model can produce embeddings of variable dimensions, enabling flexible deployment. Finally, model pruning and knowledge distillation are used to compress larger models into smaller ones across 8 size tiers ranging from 80M to 14B parameters, balancing capability and efficiency.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{Matryoshka}} = \sum_{d \in \mathcal{D}} w_d \cdot \mathcal{L}\left(f_{[:d]}(x), f_{[:d]}(x^+), f_{[:d]}(x^-)\right)\]
💡 俄罗斯套娃损失函数：对多个嵌入维度 d（如 256、512、1024 等）分别计算对比学习损失，并加权求和。这使单个模型能在不同维度截断时均保持良好的嵌入质量，适配不同算力场景。 Matryoshka loss: computes a weighted sum of contrastive losses across multiple embedding dimensions d (e.g., 256, 512, 1024). This trains a single model to produce high-quality embeddings at any truncated dimension, enabling flexible deployment across different compute budgets.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{KD}} = \text{KL}\left(p_{\text{teacher}}(z) \| p_{\text{student}}(z)\right)\]
💡 知识蒸馏损失：通过最小化学生模型与教师模型输出分布之间的KL散度，将大模型（教师）的知识迁移至小模型（学生），使压缩后的小模型仍能保留大模型的强大嵌入能力。 Knowledge distillation loss: minimizes the KL divergence between the output distributions of the large teacher model and the smaller student model, effectively transferring the teacher's learned embedding knowledge into a more compact and efficient model.

📊 Results · 结果

F2LLM-v2-14B 在 MTEB 基准测试中的 11 个榜单上排名第一，成为当前最先进的多语言嵌入模型。家族中的小型模型同样在资源受限应用场景下创下新的最优性能记录，展示了模型剪枝与知识蒸馏在保留大模型能力方面的有效性。所有模型、数据、代码及中间检查点均已开源发布，涵盖从 80M 到 14B 共 8 种规模。 F2LLM-v2-14B achieves first place on 11 MTEB benchmarks, establishing a new state of the art for multilingual embedding. The smaller models in the family also set new performance records for resource-constrained applications. The full suite of 8 models (80M to 14B), along with all training data, code, and intermediate checkpoints, has been publicly released to support open-source research.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

该研究填补了多语言嵌入模型在中低资源语言覆盖方面的重要空白，推动了全球语言的数字平等。通过将前沿性能与高效部署结合，F2LLM-v2 为信息检索、语义搜索等下游任务提供了更普惠的基础模型工具。 This work directly addresses the digital language divide by building high-quality embedding support for over 200 languages, including those historically overlooked by NLP research. By pairing top-tier benchmark performance with efficient, scalable model sizes, F2LLM-v2 makes powerful multilingual embeddings accessible for a wide range of real-world applications and resource environments.
