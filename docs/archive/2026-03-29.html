GlowQ: Group-Shared LOw-Rank Approximation for Quantized LLMs GlowQ：面向量化大语言模型的组共享低秩近似方法

🧑‍🔬 Selim An, Il hong Suh, Yeseong Kim

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 组共享低秩修正显著降低量化LLM的误差与推理延迟
GlowQ shares a single low-rank correction factor across layer groups to fix quantization errors with far less overhead.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 将大语言模型量化为4位等低比特格式能节省内存、加快部署，但会明显损害模型精度。现有的低秩纠错方法（如LQER、QERA、ASER）虽然能弥补精度损失，但需要在每一层都插入误差修正模块，导致内存开销大、推理延迟增加。 Quantizing large language models to low-bit formats (e.g., 4-bit) saves memory and speeds up deployment, but significantly hurts model accuracy. Existing low-rank correction methods (like LQER, QERA, ASER) fix this accuracy loss but insert error-correction modules into every single layer, which adds substantial memory overhead and slows down inference latency.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **组共享缓存机制**：不再为每一层单独存储低秩修正矩阵，而是对"输入共享组"（共享同一输入激活值的层组）缓存一个共享的右因子矩阵，大幅减少参数量。 • **选择性应用（GlowQ-S）**：选择性变体仅对精度收益最大的层或组应用共享修正模块，跳过收益不明显的层，进一步降低开销。 • **一次计算、多次复用**：每组仅计算一次高精度投影并在组内所有模块间复用，在不增加逐层代价的前提下保留了层级表达能力。 • **Group-Shared Caching**: Instead of storing a separate low-rank correction for every layer, GlowQ caches a single shared right-factor matrix per "input-sharing group" (layers that share the same input activations), dramatically cutting parameter count. • **Selective Application (GlowQ-S)**: A selective variant only applies the shared correction module to the layers or groups where it yields the greatest accuracy benefit, skipping layers where the overhead isn't justified. • **Compute-Once, Reuse-Many**: The high-precision projection is computed once per group and reused across all modules in that group, preserving per-layer expressivity without per-layer cost.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在WikiText-2上评估困惑度（perplexity），并在标准NLP下游任务上评估准确率，基线模型包括BitsAndBytes、AWQ、GPTQ、LQER、QERA和ASER等主流方法。推理效率通过首字节时延（TTFB）和吞吐量衡量。实验在主流开源LLM上以4位量化设置进行，贴近真实部署场景。 Experiments evaluated perplexity on WikiText-2 and downstream task accuracy across standard NLP benchmarks, comparing GlowQ and GlowQ-S against strong baselines including BitsAndBytes, AWQ, GPTQ, LQER, QERA, and ASER. Inference efficiency was measured via Time-To-First-Byte (TTFB) and throughput. Tests were conducted on popular open-source LLMs under 4-bit quantization settings to reflect realistic deployment scenarios.
📊 实验
结果		 与强基线相比：GlowQ平均降低TTFB（首字节时延）5.6%、提升吞吐量9.6%，同时WikiText-2困惑度降低0.17%，下游任务准确率提升0.42个百分点。选择性变体GlowQ-S效率提升更为显著——TTFB降低23.4%、吞吐量提升37.4%，且平均精度损失控制在0.2个百分点以内。 Compared to strong baselines: GlowQ reduces TTFB by 5.6% and increases throughput by 9.6% on average, while also reducing WikiText-2 perplexity by 0.17% and improving downstream accuracy by +0.42 percentage points. The selective variant GlowQ-S achieves much larger efficiency gains — cutting TTFB by 23.4% and boosting throughput by 37.4% — while maintaining accuracy within 0.2 percentage points of the full correction baseline.
🏁 结论 GlowQ证明了在输入共享层组间复用单一低秩修正因子，足以恢复量化精度并显著降低推理延迟与内存开销。GlowQ-S进一步表明，仅对收益最大的层选择性施加修正，可在几乎不损失精度的前提下实现大幅效率提升。 GlowQ demonstrates that sharing a single low-rank correction factor across input-sharing layer groups is sufficient to recover quantization accuracy while meaningfully reducing inference latency and memory overhead. GlowQ-S further shows that selectively applying corrections only where they matter most enables dramatic efficiency gains with negligible accuracy trade-off.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

GlowQ识别每个Transformer解码器块中共享相同输入激活的层组（例如Q、K、V投影层都接收相同的隐状态）。对每个这样的组，仅计算并缓存一个共享的低秩右因子矩阵，再叠加层特定的左因子矩阵，实现误差修正而无需冗余计算。选择性变体GlowQ-S按每单位精度提升对所有层和组进行排序，只为贡献最大的层激活共享模块，其余层保持标准量化状态。这种设计以极小的精度牺牲换取首字节时延（TTFB）和吞吐量的大幅提升。 GlowQ identifies groups of layers within each transformer decoder block that share the same input activations (e.g., Q, K, V projections all receive the same hidden state). It computes a single shared low-rank right-factor matrix for each such group once, caches it, and applies layer-specific left factors on top — achieving error correction without redundant computation. The selective variant GlowQ-S ranks all groups and layers by their per-unit accuracy improvement and only activates the shared module for the top-contributing ones, leaving the rest as standard quantized layers. This design trades a small accuracy concession for large gains in time-to-first-byte (TTFB) and throughput.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[W_{corrected} = W_q + A_i B_{shared}\]
💡 量化权重修正公式：W_q 为量化后的权重矩阵，B_shared 为组内共享的低秩右因子矩阵，A_i 为该层特有的左因子矩阵，两者之积作为误差修正项叠加到量化权重上。 The corrected weight is the quantized weight W_q plus a low-rank correction: A_i is a layer-specific left factor and B_shared is the group-shared right factor cached once for all layers in the group.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着大语言模型规模不断增大，在不损失精度的前提下实现低比特高效部署是实际应用的关键瓶颈。GlowQ为现有量化流程提供了实用的即插即用改进方案，帮助从业者以更低延迟、更高吞吐量部署高质量量化模型。 As LLMs grow larger, efficient low-bit deployment without accuracy degradation is a critical bottleneck for real-world applications. GlowQ offers a practical, plug-in improvement over existing quantization pipelines, making it easier for practitioners to deploy high-quality quantized models with better latency and throughput.

LanteRn: Latent Visual Structured Reasoning LanteRn：潜在视觉结构化推理

🧑‍🔬 André G. Viveiros, Nuno Gonçalves, Matthias Lindemann, André Martins

图 Figure · Page 1 of paper

🎯 让多模态模型在潜空间中进行视觉推理，无需像素或文字中转
LanteRn lets multimodal models reason visually in compact latent space instead of pixels or text.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当前的大型多模态模型在需要精细空间理解的视觉推理任务上表现欠佳。大多数模型通过将视觉内容转化为文字描述来绕过这一问题，但这会丢失关键的空间细节。直接在像素空间推理或依赖外部工具的方法，要么计算成本过高，要么系统架构不够稳健。 Current large multimodal models (LMMs) struggle with visual reasoning tasks that require fine-grained spatial understanding. Most models work around this by converting visual content into text descriptions, which loses critical spatial details. Approaches that reason directly in pixel space or rely on external tools are either computationally expensive or architecturally fragile.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 创新的"视觉思维嵌入"：模型在推理过程中直接生成并关注紧凑的连续潜在表示，无需将视觉内容转为文字或重新生成像素图像。 • 两阶段训练流程：先用监督微调将视觉特征锚定到潜在状态，再用强化学习将潜在推理与任务级奖励对齐，设计清晰且有原则。 • 完全内部化架构：无需外部工具或模块，视觉潜在思维原生嵌入Transformer的生成过程中。 • Novel "visual thought embeddings": the model generates and attends to compact continuous latent representations mid-reasoning, rather than converting vision to text or regenerating pixels. • Two-stage training pipeline: supervised fine-tuning first grounds visual features into latent states, then RL aligns latent reasoning with task-level reward signals — a clean, principled design. • Fully internal architecture: no external tools or modules needed; latent visual thoughts are interleaved natively within the transformer's generation process.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在三个以感知为核心的视觉推理基准上评估LanteRn：VisCoT（视觉思维链定位）、V*（细粒度视觉搜索与问答）和Blink（多图视觉感知任务）。基线方法包括将视觉内容转化为文字的标准多模态模型，以及通过生成中间图像或调用外部工具进行推理的近期方法。评估指标涵盖视觉定位准确率和各基准上的细粒度推理质量。 The authors evaluate LanteRn on three perception-centric visual reasoning benchmarks: VisCoT (visual chain-of-thought grounding), V* (fine-grained visual search and QA), and Blink (multi-image visual perception tasks). Baselines include standard LMMs that verbalize visual content into text and recent methods that reason via intermediate image generation or tool invocation. The evaluation setup measures visual grounding accuracy and fine-grained reasoning quality across these diverse benchmarks.
📊 实验
结果		 LanteRn在三个基准测试上相对基线多模态模型均取得了一致性提升。在VisCoT上，潜在推理显著改善了视觉定位效果；在V*细粒度视觉问答任务上，视觉思维嵌入在空间理解方面取得了可观的提升；在Blink上，模型超越了纯文本推理基线。强化学习阶段相比仅有监督微调的模型带来了额外增益，验证了两阶段训练设计的有效性。（摘要未完整披露各基准的具体数字，论文整体报告了三项任务上的"一致性改进"。） LanteRn achieves consistent improvements across all three benchmarks over baseline LMMs. On VisCoT, the framework demonstrates improved visual grounding with latent reasoning. On V* (fine-grained visual QA), latent visual thought embeddings yield measurable gains in spatial understanding tasks. On Blink, the model outperforms text-only reasoning baselines. The paper reports consistent gains across perception-centric tasks, with the RL stage providing additional uplift over the SFT-only model, validating the two-stage training design. (Exact per-benchmark numbers are not fully disclosed in the abstract; the paper reports "consistent improvements" across all three.)
🏁 结论 LanteRn证明了将紧凑的潜在视觉表示与语言token交织使用，比文字描述或像素空间推理更高效、更准确。监督定位与强化学习对齐的组合训练方案，确立了"潜在视觉思维"作为多模态AI推理新范式的可行性与前景。 LanteRn demonstrates that interleaving compact latent visual representations with language tokens enables more efficient and accurate multimodal reasoning than text-verbalization or pixel-space approaches. The combination of supervised grounding and RL-based alignment establishes latent visual thought as a viable and promising paradigm for multimodal AI.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

LanteRn对标准视觉语言Transformer进行扩展，使其能够在推理链中生成"视觉思维嵌入"——紧凑的连续潜在向量，并与语言token交替关注。训练分为两个阶段：第一阶段通过监督微调，让模型学会将图像编码器提取的视觉特征锚定到这些潜在状态；第二阶段通过强化学习，以任务级正确性作为奖励信号，引导潜在推理过程朝着真正有用的方向优化。推理时，模型自然地将语言与视觉潜在思维交替生成，无需调用任何外部工具，也无需重建像素级图像。 LanteRn augments a standard vision-language transformer so that it can generate "visual thought embeddings" — compact continuous latent vectors — and attend to them during the reasoning chain, interleaved with language tokens. Training proceeds in two stages: first, supervised fine-tuning teaches the model to ground visual features from the image encoder into these latent states. Second, reinforcement learning (RL) is applied to reward the model based on task-level correctness, nudging the latent reasoning process toward outcomes that are actually useful. At inference time, the model naturally interleaves language and latent visual thoughts without invoking any external tool or regenerating pixel-level images.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{SFT}} + \mathcal{L}_{\text{RL}}\]
💡 两阶段训练目标：先用监督微调损失将视觉特征锚定到潜在状态，再用强化学习损失以任务级奖励对齐潜在推理。 Two-stage training objective: supervised fine-tuning loss grounds visual features into latent states, followed by an RL loss that aligns latent reasoning with task-level reward signals.
\[h_t = \text{Transformer}([x_{\text{img}}, z_1, w_1, z_2, w_2, \ldots, z_t])\]
💡 模型在推理时将图像特征、潜在视觉思维嵌入z和语言token w交替输入Transformer，实现语言与视觉的深度融合。 At inference, the model interleaves image features, latent visual thought embeddings z, and language tokens w as inputs to the Transformer, enabling deep fusion of visual and linguistic reasoning.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

大多数多模态模型的根本瓶颈在于将丰富的视觉信息强行压缩成语言token，LanteRn提供了一条有原则的突破路径——将视觉推理保留在潜空间中。这为高效、可扩展的多模态推理系统开辟了新的设计维度，对具身智能、机器人感知以及所有需要精确空间理解的任务都具有重要意义。 Most multimodal models are fundamentally bottlenecked by forcing rich visual information through the narrow channel of language tokens — LanteRn offers a principled escape route by keeping visual reasoning in latent space. This opens a new design axis for efficient, scalable multimodal reasoning systems that could benefit embodied AI, robotics perception, and any task requiring precise spatial understanding.

Autonomous Driving · 自动驾驶VLA Models · 视觉-语言-动作模型Personalization · 个性化Preference Alignment · 偏好对齐
Drive My Way: Preference Alignment of Vision-Language-Action Model for Personalized Driving Drive My Way：面向个性化驾驶的视觉-语言-动作模型偏好对齐

🧑‍🔬 Zehao Wang, Huaide Jiang, Shuaiwu Dong, Yuping Wang et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 让自动驾驶系统学会模仿每个人独特的驾驶风格
A VLA driving framework that personalizes autonomous driving to individual driver habits and real-time language instructions.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的端到端自动驾驶系统只会按照"通用目标"来规划行驶，无法适应不同用户的个人习惯。每个人的驾驶风格千差万别——有人刹车早、有人并线果断——这些习惯是长期积累的。目前的系统既不能复现某个人的专属驾驶风格，也无法结合自然语言指令做实时调整。 Current end-to-end autonomous driving systems optimize for a single generic driving objective or offer only a few fixed driving modes, making them unable to adapt to individual user preferences. People drive very differently — some brake earlier, some merge more aggressively — and these personal styles are shaped by both long-term habits and short-term intentions. No existing system can capture and reproduce a specific person's driving behavior while also responding to natural language commands.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 用户嵌入模块：从每位驾驶员的历史轨迹中学习紧凑的个人风格向量，驱动策略模型模仿该用户的独特行为。 • 双层个性化机制：将长期习惯建模（用户嵌入）与短期自然语言指令相结合，实现实时意图适配。 • 构建了全新的个性化驾驶数据集，涵盖多名真实驾驶员在多种条件下的驾驶数据，为风格学习提供数据基础。 • Novel user embedding module: learns a compact personal style vector from each driver's historical trajectories, conditioning the VLA policy to mimic that driver's unique behavior. • Dual-level personalization: combines long-term habit modeling (user embedding) with short-term natural language instructions for real-time intent adaptation. • New personalized driving dataset collected from multiple real human drivers across diverse conditions, enabling data-driven style learning.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在Bench2Drive闭环基准测试上评估DMW，这是一个标准的端到端自动驾驶仿真评估套件。与通用驾驶策略和固定模式系统进行对比，测试风格指令适配能力和整体驾驶表现。此外，还进行了用户研究，让人类参与者判断系统生成的驾驶行为是否能被识别为某位特定驾驶员的风格。 The authors evaluate DMW on the Bench2Drive closed-loop benchmark, a standard simulator-based evaluation suite for end-to-end autonomous driving. They compare against baselines including generic driving policies and fixed-mode systems, testing style instruction adaptation and overall driving performance. Additionally, user studies are conducted where human participants judge whether the system's generated driving behaviors are recognizable as belonging to a specific real driver.
📊 实验
结果		 DMW在Bench2Drive基准上相比通用策略和固定模式基线，在风格指令适配指标上取得了显著提升。用户研究中，参与者能够可靠地从DMW生成的驾驶行为中识别出目标驾驶员的风格，验证了个性化质量。DMW在驾驶风格匹配指标上优于所有基线系统，体现了可量化的个性化行为还原能力。（注：摘要未披露完整精确数值，结果基于闭环基准和用户研究综合判断。） DMW achieves improved style instruction adaptation on the Bench2Drive benchmark compared to generic and fixed-mode baselines. User studies confirm that participants could reliably identify the target driver's style in DMW's generated behaviors, validating the personalization quality. While the paper focuses on qualitative and user-study validation, DMW outperforms baseline systems on driving style matching metrics on Bench2Drive, demonstrating measurable gains in personalized behavior fidelity. (Note: precise numerical scores are not fully detailed in the abstract; results reflect closed-loop benchmark and user study outcomes.)
🏁 结论 本文证明了通过用户嵌入与自然语言指令的双重条件机制，VLA模型可以有效学习并复现个体驾驶员的长期风格习惯，同时响应实时指令，为以人为中心的自动驾驶提供了可行的个性化对齐框架。 DMW demonstrates that a VLA-based autonomous driving policy can be effectively aligned with individual driver preferences by conditioning on learned user embeddings and natural language instructions, establishing personalization as a practical and measurable capability for human-centered autonomous driving.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

DMW基于视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型，输入摄像头图像和语言指令，输出驾驶动作。核心创新是从每位驾驶员的真实轨迹数据中学习一个"用户嵌入"向量，将其作为额外条件信号输入策略网络，相当于告诉模型"要像这个人一样开车"。推理时，短期自然语言指令（如"开得保守一点"）在个人风格基础上叠加实时调整。用户嵌入与语言指令的双重条件，使模型能同时兼顾长期习惯和当下意图。 DMW builds on a Vision-Language-Action (VLA) model that takes camera observations and language instructions as input to produce driving actions. The key innovation is a user embedding learned from each driver's real-world trajectory data, which is fed into the policy network as an additional conditioning signal — essentially telling the model "drive like this person." During inference, short-term natural language instructions (e.g., "drive more conservatively") provide an extra layer of real-time adjustment on top of the learned personal style. The combined conditioning of user embedding and language instruction allows the model to simultaneously honor long-term habits and respond to immediate user intent.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\pi(a \mid o, e_u, l)\]
💡 驾驶策略 π 以观测 o、用户嵌入 e_u 和语言指令 l 为条件，输出动作 a，同时融合长期风格和短期意图。 The driving policy π generates action a conditioned on observation o, user embedding e_u, and language instruction l — jointly encoding long-term personal style and short-term intent.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着自动驾驶走向实际部署，"千人一面"的驾驶行为将成为用户接受度的重大障碍——人们希望车子像自己一样开。DMW为具身AI中的偏好对齐提供了具体可行的框架，其思路不仅适用于驾驶，也可推广到任何需要适配个体用户行为的VLA系统。 As autonomous vehicles move toward real-world deployment, one-size-fits-all driving behavior will be a major barrier to user acceptance — people want a car that drives like *them*. DMW provides a concrete framework for preference alignment in embodied AI, with implications extending beyond driving to any VLA system that must adapt to individual user behavior.
