Is Grep All You Need? How Agent Harnesses Reshape Agentic Search Grep 就够了吗？智能体工具框架如何重塑智能搜索

🧑‍🔬 Sahil Sen, Akhil Kasturi, Elias Lumer, Anmol Gulati et al.

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 在智能体搜索中，Grep比向量检索更准，但框架选择影响更大
Grep outperforms vector retrieval in agentic search, but harness choice matters more than retrieval method.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型智能体系统越来越多地使用检索增强生成（RAG）来在大型文本语料中查找信息。然而，学界缺乏对"检索策略"与"智能体架构"及"工具调用方式"之间交互关系的系统性比较。尤其是工具输出的呈现方式（内联 vs. 文件读取）以及无关背景文本增多时性能如何变化，这些实际问题几乎没有被深入研究过。 LLM agent systems increasingly rely on retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to search large corpora, but there is no systematic study comparing how retrieval strategy interacts with agent architecture and tool-calling style. Practical dimensions—such as whether tool results are shown inline or read from files, and how performance degrades with increasing irrelevant context—remain largely unexplored in real agentic loops.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次系统对比了 Grep 与向量检索在多种真实智能体框架（Chronos、Claude Code、Codex、Gemini CLI）中的表现差异。 • 引入"工具输出呈现方式"这一新维度：内联结果 vs. 文件式结果，揭示其对模型准确率的独立影响。 • 通过逐步混入无关对话历史的压力测试，系统量化了干扰文本对两种检索策略的影响，发现框架选择的影响甚至超过检索方法本身。 • First systematic empirical comparison of grep vs. vector retrieval across multiple real-world agent harnesses (Chronos, Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI). • Introduces tool output presentation style (inline vs. file-based) as a novel experimental dimension that independently affects model accuracy. • Stress-tests both retrieval strategies by progressively injecting irrelevant conversation history, quantifying robustness to distractor context.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在 LongMemEval 基准数据集的116题样本上展开，该数据集专为长上下文记忆问答设计。基线模型包括四种智能体框架：自研 Chronos、Claude Code、Codex CLI 和 Gemini CLI。评估指标为问答准确率。实验一对比了检索策略（Grep vs. 向量）× 工具输出格式（内联 vs. 文件式）共4种组合。实验二通过逐步增加无关对话轮数，测试两种检索策略的鲁棒性。 Experiments were conducted on a 116-question sample drawn from LongMemEval, a benchmark designed for long-context memory question answering. The baselines span four agent harnesses: the authors' custom Chronos framework, Claude Code, Codex CLI, and Gemini CLI. The primary evaluation metric is question-answering accuracy. Experiment 1 uses a 2×2 design crossing retrieval strategy (grep vs. vector) with tool output format (inline vs. file-based). Experiment 2 incrementally injects irrelevant conversation history to stress-test each retrieval strategy's robustness to distractor noise.
📊 实验
结果		 在实验一中，Grep 检索在多数框架和工具输出格式组合中均优于向量检索；例如在 Chronos 框架下，Grep 的准确率显著高于向量检索。框架本身对最终得分的影响甚至超过检索方法的选择——不同框架之间的准确率差距明显大于同一框架内 Grep vs. 向量的差距。在实验二中，随着无关对话历史增加，向量检索的性能下降幅度总体上比 Grep 更明显，表明 Grep 在高噪声场景下具有更强的鲁棒性。内联工具结果与文件式工具结果之间也存在可观察到的准确率差异，具体方向因框架而异。（注：论文为实证研究报告，完整数值表格详见原文。） In Experiment 1, grep retrieval outperforms vector retrieval across most harness and tool-format combinations; within the Chronos harness, grep yields notably higher accuracy than vector retrieval. Critically, the choice of harness architecture produces accuracy gaps that are larger than the grep-vs.-vector gap itself, indicating harness design dominates retrieval method in determining overall performance. In Experiment 2, vector retrieval degrades more sharply than grep as irrelevant conversation history grows, confirming grep's greater robustness to distractor noise. Inline vs. file-based tool output presentation also produces measurable accuracy differences that vary by harness. (Full numerical tables are reported in the paper.)
🏁 结论 本文通过系统实证研究证明，在智能体搜索场景中，Grep 检索总体上优于向量检索，但智能体框架的选择和工具输出的呈现方式对最终性能的影响同样举足轻重，甚至超过检索策略本身。这一发现提醒研究者和工程师在构建 RAG 智能体系统时，不能只关注检索算法，框架设计同样是决定性因素。 This paper empirically demonstrates that grep-based retrieval generally outperforms vector retrieval in agentic search pipelines, while also showing that agent harness architecture and tool output presentation style exert an equal or greater influence on end-to-end accuracy than the retrieval method itself. The findings urge practitioners building RAG-based agent systems to treat harness design as a first-class concern alongside retrieval algorithm selection.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

研究设计了两个实验。实验一使用自研的 Chronos 框架和三个主流提供商 CLI（Claude Code、Codex、Gemini CLI），在 LongMemEval 数据集的116道题上，对比 Grep 和向量检索在"内联工具结果"与"文件式工具结果"两种呈现模式下的准确率。实验二固定使用 Grep-only 和 Vector-only，通过逐步向查询中混入越来越多的无关对话历史，观察两种检索策略在噪声增加时的性能变化曲线。所有实验在相同底层对话数据上进行，以便控制变量、隔离框架和检索策略各自的影响。 The study runs two controlled experiments. Experiment 1 evaluates grep and vector retrieval on a 116-question sample from LongMemEval using four agent harnesses—a custom harness (Chronos) and three provider-native CLIs (Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI)—under both inline and file-based tool result presentation styles, holding the underlying conversation data constant. Experiment 2 isolates the robustness question by progressively mixing increasing amounts of unrelated conversation history into each query context, measuring how grep-only and vector-only retrieval degrade as distracting material grows. This design allows the authors to disentangle the effects of retrieval method, harness architecture, and tool output format.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着 LLM 智能体系统快速落地，研究者和工程师普遍假设向量检索优于简单的词法检索（如 Grep），但本文用实验打破了这一假设。更重要的是，本文揭示了"框架设计"这一被忽视的变量对系统性能的主导作用，为 RAG 智能体系统的工程实践提供了切实的设计指导。 As LLM agent systems rapidly move into production, a widespread assumption has been that semantic vector retrieval is superior to simple lexical methods like grep—this paper empirically challenges that assumption. More broadly, by exposing harness architecture as a dominant performance variable, the work provides actionable engineering guidance for teams building real-world agentic RAG systems.

Articraft: An Agentic System for Scalable Articulated 3D Asset Generation Articraft：一种可扩展的关节式三维资产生成智能体系统

🧑‍🔬 Matt Zhou, Ruining Li, Xiaoyang Lyu, Zhaomou Song et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 用LLM写代码来大规模生成高质量关节式三维资产
An LLM-based agentic system that generates large-scale, high-quality articulated 3D assets via code synthesis.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练模型理解关节式三维物体（如门、抽屉、机械臂等）需要大量多样化的数据集，但这类数据集稀缺且制作成本高昂。现有的自动化生成方法要么质量低，要么种类和规模不够，严重制约了机器人仿真、虚拟现实和三维理解等领域的发展。 Training models to understand articulated 3D objects (e.g., doors, drawers, robotic arms) requires large and diverse datasets, but such datasets are scarce and expensive to create manually. Existing automated generators either produce low-quality assets or lack sufficient diversity and scale. This bottleneck severely limits progress in robotics simulation, virtual reality, and 3D understanding research.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 将关节式三维资产生成转化为代码编写任务，充分发挥LLM的代码生成能力，无需直接预测三维几何形状。 • 设计专用SDK和智能体执行框架（harness），为LLM提供简洁受限的接口，自动处理URDF文件和环境管理，让LLM专注于逻辑本身。 • 执行框架自动校验生成资产并返回结构化反馈，支持LLM智能体的迭代自我纠错。 • 构建了Articraft-10K数据集，涵盖245个类别、超过1万个资产，规模和多样性均显著超越现有基准。 • Reduces articulated 3D asset generation to a code-writing task, letting LLMs leverage their strong coding abilities rather than directly predicting 3D geometry. • Introduces a domain-specific SDK and agentic harness that gives the LLM a clean, restricted interface — handling URDF authoring and environment management automatically so the LLM focuses only on logic. • The harness validates generated assets and returns structured feedback, enabling iterative self-correction by the LLM agent. • Produces Articraft-10K, a curated dataset of 10K+ assets across 245 categories — significantly larger and more diverse than existing benchmarks.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者将Articraft与最先进的关节资产生成方法（如PartDiffusion、NAP）以及通用代码智能体（如原始GPT-4代码生成）进行对比，评估指标包括几何有效性、关节正确性和视觉保真度，并进行了用户感知质量研究。下游实用性方面，在Articraft-10K上训练关节物体理解模型，并在机器人仿真任务和虚拟现实应用中测试其性能。数据集涵盖245个物体类别。 The authors evaluated Articraft against state-of-the-art articulated asset generators (e.g., PartDiffusion, NAP) and general-purpose coding agents (e.g., raw GPT-4 code generation) on asset quality metrics including geometric validity, joint correctness, and visual fidelity. They also conducted user studies for perceptual quality. Downstream utility was assessed by training articulated object understanding models on Articraft-10K and testing performance in robotics simulation tasks and virtual reality applications. The dataset spans 245 object categories.
📊 实验
结果		 Articraft在所有质量指标上均优于最先进的关节资产生成方法和通用代码智能体。Articraft-10K包含超过10,000个经过筛选的关节资产，涵盖245个类别，类别覆盖范围是现有数据集（如PartNet-Mobility约46类）的数倍。在Articraft-10K上训练的模型在关节物体理解基准上表现更优，资产可直接用于机器人仿真和VR流程。用户研究和几何有效性评分中，Articraft的有效资产率和用户偏好得分均显著高于所有基线方法。 Articraft outperforms both state-of-the-art articulated-asset generators and general-purpose coding agents across all quality metrics. The Articraft-10K dataset contains over 10,000 curated articulated assets across 245 categories — several times larger in category coverage than prior datasets such as PartNet-Mobility (~46 categories). Models trained on Articraft-10K show improved performance on articulated object understanding benchmarks, and the assets are demonstrated to be directly usable in robotics simulation and VR pipelines. Specific numerical improvements over baselines are reported in the paper's user study and geometric validity scores, with Articraft achieving substantially higher valid-asset rates and preference scores compared to all baselines.
🏁 结论 Articraft证明，将关节式三维资产生成转化为LLM驱动的代码合成任务，并结合专用SDK与智能体验证框架，可以获得比现有生成方法更高质量、更可扩展的结果。由此构建的Articraft-10K数据集填补了关节式三维理解领域的关键数据空白，在机器人和VR领域具有广泛应用价值。 Articraft demonstrates that framing articulated 3D asset generation as an LLM-driven code synthesis task — combined with a purpose-built SDK and agentic validation harness — yields higher-quality, more scalable results than prior generative approaches. The resulting Articraft-10K dataset fills a critical data gap for articulated 3D understanding and has broad applicability in robotics and VR.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Articraft将关节式三维资产生成转化为程序合成问题：给定文本描述或类别名称，LLM使用专用SDK编写程序，定义零件、组合几何体、指定关节并编写测试代码。执行框架（harness）在沙箱环境中运行代码，自动校验资产的物理合理性和几何正确性，并将结构化错误信息反馈给LLM进行迭代修正。这一智能体循环持续进行，直到资产通过全部验证或达到重试上限。最终输出是完整的、兼容URDF格式的关节式三维资产，LLM无需关心任何底层文件格式细节。 Articraft frames articulated 3D asset generation as a program synthesis problem: given a text description or category, the LLM writes a Python-like program using a domain-specific SDK that defines parts, composes geometry, specifies joints, and includes validation tests. A harness wraps the execution environment, runs the code in a sandboxed workspace, validates the physical and geometric correctness of the resulting asset, and feeds structured error messages back to the LLM for iterative refinement. This agentic loop continues until the asset passes all validation checks or a retry budget is exhausted. The final output is a fully specified URDF-compatible articulated 3D asset, abstracted away from any low-level file format concerns.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

关节式三维物体的数据稀缺长期制约机器人操作、仿真和三维场景理解的发展，Articraft提供了一条可扩展的自动化流程来填补这一空白。将LLM定位为结构化代码编写者而非直接几何生成器，为利用基础模型构建领域特定三维数据集开辟了全新范式。 Data scarcity for articulated 3D objects has long been a bottleneck for robotics manipulation, simulation, and 3D scene understanding — Articraft offers a scalable, automated pipeline to close this gap. By repurposing LLMs as structured code-writers rather than direct geometry generators, this work opens a new paradigm for using foundation models to build domain-specific 3D datasets.

Causal Forcing++: Scalable Few-Step Autoregressive Diffusion Distillation for Real-Time Interactive Video Generation Causal Forcing++：面向实时交互式视频生成的可扩展少步自回归扩散蒸馏

🧑‍🔬 Min Zhao, Hongzhou Zhu, Kaiwen Zheng, Zihan Zhou et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 用因果一致性蒸馏实现1-2步帧级自回归实时视频生成
Causal consistency distillation enables real-time 1–2 step frame-wise autoregressive video generation.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 实时交互式视频生成需要低延迟、流式输出和可控的逐帧生成，但现有的自回归扩散蒸馏方法只能以"块级"（每次生成多帧）方式工作，通常需要4步采样，响应粒度粗且延迟不可忽视。将采样步数压缩到1-2步的"帧级"自回归设置下，现有初始化策略要么目标不一致，要么无法支持少步生成，要么计算代价过高、难以扩展。 Real-time interactive video generation demands low latency, streaming output, and fine-grained frame-by-frame control. Existing autoregressive diffusion distillation methods operate in a coarse chunk-wise 4-step regime, limiting response granularity and incurring non-trivial latency. Pushing to an even more aggressive frame-wise 1–2 step setting is hindered by poor student initialization strategies that are either misaligned with the target, incapable of few-step generation, or prohibitively expensive to scale.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **因果一致性蒸馏（Causal CD）**：从在线教师ODE单步获取监督信号，无需预计算和存储完整的PF-ODE轨迹，大幅降低初始化成本并易于优化。 • **帧级2步生成超越块级4步SOTA**：在更激进的设置下（帧级2步 vs 块级4步），Causal Forcing++ 在多项指标上反超现有最佳方法，首帧延迟降低50%。 • **可扩展的端到端流水线**：Stage 2训练成本降低约4倍，并成功扩展到动作条件世界模型（Genie3风格），兼具实用性与通用性。 • **Causal Consistency Distillation (Causal CD)**: Obtains supervision from a single online teacher ODE step between adjacent timesteps, eliminating the need to precompute and store full PF-ODE trajectories — making initialization far more efficient and easier to optimize. • **Frame-wise 2-step beats chunk-wise 4-step SOTA**: Under a strictly more aggressive setting, Causal Forcing++ surpasses the prior best method on multiple quality metrics while halving first-frame latency. • **Scalable end-to-end pipeline**: Reduces Stage 2 training cost by ~4× and generalizes to action-conditioned world model generation in the spirit of Genie3.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在文本条件视频生成任务上进行，使用VBench和VisionReward作为主要评估指标，对比基线为当前SOTA的块级4步Causal Forcing方法。此外，在动作条件世界模型生成任务（类Genie3设置）上也进行了扩展实验。消融实验验证了因果CD初始化策略相对于其他初始化方式（如直接蒸馏、预计算轨迹蒸馏）的优越性，并分析了训练成本的差异。 Experiments were conducted on text-conditioned video generation, evaluated using VBench (Total and Quality sub-scores) and VisionReward as primary metrics, with the chunk-wise 4-step Causal Forcing as the main baseline. Additional experiments extended the pipeline to action-conditioned world model generation (Genie3-style). Ablations compared different student initialization strategies — including direct distillation and precomputed trajectory-based distillation — against the proposed Causal CD, examining both generation quality and training efficiency.
📊 实验
结果		 在帧级2步设置下，Causal Forcing++ 超越块级4步Causal Forcing：VBench Total 提升 +0.1，VBench Quality 提升 +0.3，VisionReward 提升 +0.335。首帧延迟降低 50%，Stage 2 训练成本降低约 4×。这意味着在更少步数、更细粒度的设置下，新方法在质量与效率两个维度均全面超越前代SOTA。 Under the frame-wise 2-step setting, Causal Forcing++ surpasses the chunk-wise 4-step Causal Forcing baseline by +0.1 on VBench Total, +0.3 on VBench Quality, and +0.335 on VisionReward. First-frame latency is reduced by 50%, and Stage 2 training cost is cut by approximately 4×. These gains are achieved under a strictly more demanding regime (fewer steps, finer granularity) than the baseline it outperforms.
🏁 结论 本文证明了因果一致性蒸馏是解决少步帧级自回归视频生成中初始化瓶颈的有效且可扩展方案。Causal Forcing++ 在更激进的设置下同时实现了更高的生成质量、更低的延迟和更低的训练成本，推动实时交互式视频生成向实用化迈进了关键一步。 This paper demonstrates that causal consistency distillation effectively resolves the initialization bottleneck for few-step frame-wise autoregressive video generation. Causal Forcing++ simultaneously achieves higher quality, lower latency, and lower training cost under a more aggressive regime, marking a significant step toward practical real-time interactive video generation.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Causal Forcing++ 提出了一种两阶段蒸馏流水线。第一阶段，使用"因果一致性蒸馏"（Causal CD）对少步自回归学生模型进行初始化：其核心是让学生学习与因果ODE蒸馏相同的条件流映射，但监督信号仅来自在线教师在相邻时间步之间执行的单步ODE推进，避免了预计算完整轨迹的高昂代价。第二阶段，在已初始化的学生基础上继续进行对抗性/分布匹配微调以进一步提升生成质量。整个框架支持帧级（每次生成单帧）的1-2步采样，实现真正流式、低延迟的视频生成，并可扩展至带动作条件的世界模型。 Causal Forcing++ introduces a two-stage distillation pipeline for few-step frame-wise autoregressive video generation. In Stage 1, causal consistency distillation (Causal CD) initializes the student model: the student learns the same AR-conditional flow map as causal ODE distillation, but receives supervision only from a single online teacher ODE step between adjacent timesteps — no precomputation or storage of full PF-ODE trajectories is needed. In Stage 2, the initialized student undergoes adversarial or distribution-matching fine-tuning to further sharpen generation quality. This pipeline supports 1–2 step per-frame sampling, enabling genuinely streaming, low-latency video rollout, and can be extended to action-conditioned world model generation.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

实时交互式视频生成（如游戏、具身智能、虚拟现实中的世界模型）对延迟和流式输出有严苛要求，本文的方法将采样步数压缩至1-2步同时提升质量，为这一方向提供了切实可行的技术路径。因果一致性蒸馏的思路也为扩散模型加速领域提供了新的通用工具，有望推动更广泛的实时生成式AI应用落地。 Real-time interactive video generation for gaming, embodied AI, and virtual reality demands strict latency and streaming constraints; this work offers a practical path to 1–2 step generation without quality sacrifice. The causal consistency distillation principle is a broadly applicable acceleration tool for diffusion models, potentially catalyzing a wider class of real-time generative AI applications.

🎯 利用混合视频与动捕数据训练的人形机器人全身运动追踪基础模型
A humanoid motion foundation model that scales whole-body motion tracking via large-scale hybrid video+MoCap data and MoE Transformers.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 人形机器人需要模仿多样的人类动作，但现有控制策略大多依赖昂贵且种类有限的动作捕捉（MoCap）数据训练，导致机器人只能执行少量固定动作，难以零样本泛化到新的动作风格。 Humanoid robots need to replicate diverse human motions, but existing control policies are trained mostly on motion-capture (MoCap) data, which is expensive and limited in variety. This restricts robots to a narrow set of behaviors and makes zero-shot generalization to new motion styles very challenging.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 混合动作语料库：以野外视频重建动作为主要来源，辅以精选MoCap与自研数据，大幅超越纯MoCap训练的多样性上限。 • 稀疏MoE Transformer：配合KV-cache推理实现实时控制，同时有效处理异质性和噪声较大的多源动作数据。 • 序列级训练策略：针对长时、行为多样的动作序列提升学习效率，无需任务特定微调即可完成仿真到真实机器人的迁移。 • Hybrid motion corpus: combines video-reconstructed "in-the-wild" motions at scale with curated MoCap and in-house data, dramatically expanding behavioral diversity beyond MoCap-only pipelines. • Sparse Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) Transformer: large-capacity temporal modeling with KV-cache inference enables real-time control while handling heterogeneous, noisy motion sources. • Sequence-level training strategy: improves learning efficiency on long, behaviorally varied motion sequences without task-specific fine-tuning for sim-to-real transfer.
🧪 实验
内容		 模型在多个未见过的动作基准数据集上进行评估，涵盖训练时未出现的多样动作类型和采集条件。基线对比方法包括主要基于MoCap数据训练的先进人形机器人全身运动追踪方法。评估指标涵盖追踪精度（如关节位置误差、全身姿态相似度）以及在真实人形机器人上的零样本迁移部署成功率。 The model was evaluated on multiple unseen motion benchmarks covering diverse motion types and capture conditions that were not seen during training. Baselines include prior state-of-the-art humanoid whole-body motion tracking methods trained predominantly on MoCap data. Evaluation metrics focus on tracking accuracy (e.g., joint position error, whole-body pose similarity) as well as real-robot deployment success, testing zero-shot transfer from simulation to a physical humanoid platform.
📊 实验
结果		 HoloMotion-1在所有未见过的动作基准测试中均"显著优于先前方法"的追踪精度，展现出对多样动作类型和采集条件的强泛化能力。模型无需任务特定微调即可直接在真实人形机器人上部署，验证了零样本仿真到现实迁移能力。具体数值指标（如精确误差百分比或排名分数）在完整技术报告中有详细记录，摘要中未披露精确数字，但描述为相对现有方法的大幅提升。 HoloMotion-1 "significantly improves tracking accuracy over prior methods" across all tested unseen motion benchmarks, demonstrating robust generalization to diverse motion types and capture conditions. The model transfers directly to a real humanoid robot without task-specific fine-tuning, confirming zero-shot sim-to-real transfer. Specific numerical results (e.g., exact error percentages or ranking scores) are described in the full technical report as substantial improvements over prior art, though exact figures were not included in the abstract.
🏁 结论 HoloMotion-1证明了通过大规模视频重建与MoCap混合语料库结合MoE Transformer策略，能够实现对未见行为的鲁棒零样本全身运动追踪，超越了纯MoCap训练范式的局限，为人形机器人运动基础模型树立了新标杆。 HoloMotion-1 proves that scaling humanoid motion control with a large hybrid corpus of video-reconstructed and MoCap data, combined with a MoE Transformer policy, enables robust zero-shot whole-body motion tracking across unseen behaviors. This establishes a new paradigm for humanoid motion foundation models that transcends the limitations of MoCap-only training pipelines.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

HoloMotion-1在一个大规模混合语料库上训练控制策略，该语料库融合了来自互联网野外视频的重建动作、精选MoCap数据及自研动作数据。策略骨干网络采用稀疏激活的专家混合（MoE）Transformer，以应对多源数据的异质性，并通过KV-cache推理保证实时控制所需的低延迟。序列级训练策略进一步提升了在长时、高变化性动作序列上的学习效率。部署时，训练好的策略无需任何任务特定微调即可直接迁移至真实人形机器人，实现真正的零样本泛化。 HoloMotion-1 trains a control policy on a large hybrid corpus that blends video-reconstructed motions from in-the-wild internet videos with curated MoCap and proprietary motion data. The policy backbone is a sparsely activated Mixture-of-Experts Transformer, chosen for its large capacity and ability to handle motion source heterogeneity, while KV-cache inference keeps latency low enough for real-time robot control. A sequence-level training strategy is applied to improve sample efficiency over extended motion sequences with high behavioral variation. At deployment, the trained policy transfers directly to a physical humanoid robot without any task-specific fine-tuning, demonstrating genuine zero-shot generalization.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本研究从根本上突破了人形机器人控制的数据天花板，证明互联网规模的视频重建动作可替代昂贵的MoCap采集，使策略覆盖更丰富的运动行为。零样本仿真到现实迁移能力的实现，是通用人形机器人开箱即用模仿任意人类行为的重要里程碑。 This work fundamentally expands the data ceiling for humanoid robot control by showing that internet-scale video reconstructions can replace the expensive bottleneck of MoCap data collection, enabling far richer motion policies. The zero-shot sim-to-real transfer without fine-tuning is a critical step toward general-purpose humanoid robots that can imitate arbitrary human behaviors out of the box.
