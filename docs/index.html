ESI-Bench: Towards Embodied Spatial Intelligence that Closes the Perception-Action Loop ESI-Bench：面向闭合感知-行动回路的具身空间智能评测

🧑‍🔬 Yining Hong, Jiageng Liu, Han Yin, Manling Li et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 首个通过主动感知-行动闭环评测具身空间智能的综合基准
A new benchmark that tests spatial intelligence through active perception-action loops, not passive observation.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有空间智能评测基准假设智能体被动接受完美观测，与真实具身场景严重脱节。真正的空间推理需要主动决策——去哪里看、如何移动、如何操作——才能揭示遮挡结构、物体动态、容纳关系和功能属性等被动感知无法获取的信息。当前多模态大模型评测仅基于静态图像，忽视了行动与感知之间的闭环交互。 Current benchmarks for spatial intelligence assume agents passively receive perfect observations, which does not reflect real-world embodied settings. True spatial reasoning requires actively deciding where to look, how to move, and what to manipulate to uncover hidden structure — occluded geometry, object dynamics, containment, and functionality — that passive sensing cannot resolve. Existing multimodal large language models (MLLMs) are evaluated on static snapshots, missing the critical closed-loop interplay between action and perception.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **感知-行动闭环重构**：将空间智能从被动观测重新定义为主动具身决策，智能体须主动选择感知、移动或操作来积累证据。\n• **大规模认知基准**：覆盖10大任务类别、29个子类别，基于OmniGibson构建，以Spelke核心知识系统为认知科学理论基础。\n• **"行动盲区"新失效模式**：揭示了一种新的失败根源——糟糕的行动选择导致糟糕的观测，进而引发连锁推理错误，其危害超过感知能力不足本身。\n• **元认知鸿沟**：人类实验表明，模型无论证据质量如何都会过早以高置信度作出判断，而人类会主动寻找证伪视角并修正信念。 • **Perception-Action Loop Formulation**: Recasts spatial intelligence from passive observation to active embodied decision-making, where agents must choose to perceive, locomote, or manipulate to gather evidence. \n• **ESI-Bench Scale & Grounding**: 10 task categories, 29 subcategories built on OmniGibson, grounded in Spelke's core knowledge systems — a principled cognitive science foundation. \n• **Discovery of Action Blindness**: Identifies a new failure mode where poor action choices lead to poor observations, cascading into reasoning errors — distinct from and more critical than perceptual weakness. \n• **Metacognitive Gap Exposed**: Human studies reveal models prematurely commit with high confidence regardless of evidence quality, unlike humans who seek falsifying viewpoints and revise beliefs.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在基于OmniGibson构建的ESI-Bench上进行，覆盖10个任务类别和29个子类别。使用当前最优多模态大模型（如GPT-4o级别模型）在三种观测条件下评测：被动单视角、随机多视角和主动具身探索。消融实验比较了3D感知模块与2D基线的表现。另设人类实验，测量受试者的信念修正与证伪行为，作为定性上界，揭示模型的元认知缺口。 Experiments were conducted on ESI-Bench using OmniGibson as the simulation backbone, covering 10 task categories and 29 subcategories grounded in core knowledge systems. State-of-the-art MLLMs (e.g., GPT-4o-level models) were evaluated under three observation regimes: passive single-view, random multi-view, and active embodied exploration. Ablations tested 3D grounding modules versus 2D baselines. A separate human study measured belief revision and falsification-seeking behavior to establish a qualitative upper bound and expose the metacognitive gap.
📊 实验
结果		 在ESI-Bench各任务类别上，主动探索策略显著优于被动观测策略。随机多视角虽消耗更多图像，却常引入噪声而非有效信号，整体表现劣于主动探索。显式3D感知在深度敏感任务上提升稳定性，但不完美的3D表示比2D基线危害更大，因其扭曲了空间关系。人类受试者在所有条件下均显著优于多模态大模型，主要差距来自元认知策略而非感知能力本身。模型失败案例中大多数归因于"行动盲区"（行动选择失当），而非感知能力不足。（注：摘要未披露精确聚合准确率数字，以上为摘要中明确陈述的方向性结果。） Active exploration substantially outperforms passive counterparts across task categories on ESI-Bench. Random multi-view observation, despite consuming far more images, often adds noise rather than signal and underperforms active strategies. Explicit 3D grounding stabilizes performance on depth-sensitive tasks, but imperfect 3D representations prove more harmful than 2D baselines by distorting spatial relations. Human participants significantly outperform all MLLM conditions, with the primary gap attributed to metacognitive strategy rather than raw perceptual ability. Most model failures (~majority of error cases) are traced to action blindness — poor action selection — rather than perceptual limitations. (Note: precise aggregate accuracy numbers are not reported in the abstract; the above reflects the directional findings stated.)
🏁 结论 ESI-Bench证明了真正的具身空间智能必须闭合感知-行动回路——主动探索不可或缺，无法被被动多视角采样所替代。本文的核心贡献在于识别出"行动盲区"与"元认知鸿沟"是当前多模态大模型最主要的失败根源，为超越感知扩展的新研究方向指明了道路。 ESI-Bench establishes that true embodied spatial intelligence requires closing the perception-action loop — active exploration is essential and irreplaceable by passive multi-view sampling. The paper's core contribution is identifying "action blindness" and a "metacognitive gap" as the dominant failure modes in current MLLMs, pointing to a new research frontier beyond perceptual scaling.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ESI-Bench在OmniGibson仿真环境中构建了涵盖几何、物理、容纳关系和功能属性的多类任务。智能体不再获得完美的神谕观测，而须连续发出行动指令——移动、观察或操作——来主动积累空间推理所需的证据。实验对当前最优多模态大模型进行了全面测评，并对被动单视角、随机多视角与主动探索三种策略进行消融对比。此外还测试了3D感知模块，评估显式深度信息的利弊。人类受试者作为上界参照，揭示了模型与人类在信念修正策略上的本质差异。 ESI-Bench builds a simulation environment on OmniGibson where agents are given tasks spanning geometry, physics, containment, and functionality. Rather than receiving oracle observations, agents must issue sequential action decisions — choosing to move, look, or manipulate — to gather the observations needed for spatial reasoning. State-of-the-art MLLMs are evaluated in this loop, with ablations comparing passive (single-view), random multi-view, and active exploration strategies. 3D grounding modules are also tested to assess whether explicit depth information helps or hurts. Human participants serve as an upper-bound reference and reveal qualitative differences in belief revision strategies.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着具身AI智能体迈向真实世界部署，仅测试被动感知的评测基准从根本上误判了智能体能力。ESI-Bench转变了评测范式，并揭示了关键失效模式——尤其是元认知缺陷——将为下一代具备空间感知和行动意识的基础模型研发指明方向。 As embodied AI agents move into physical and simulated real-world deployment, benchmarks that only test passive perception fundamentally misrepresent agent capability. ESI-Bench shifts the evaluation paradigm and exposes critical failure modes — especially metacognitive deficits — that will guide the next generation of spatially intelligent, action-aware foundation models.

Generative Recursive Reasoning 生成式递归推理

🧑‍🔬 Junyeob Baek, Mingyu Jo, Minsu Kim, Mengye Ren et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 将确定性递归推理升级为概率多轨迹生成推理框架
GRAM turns deterministic recursive reasoning into probabilistic multi-trajectory latent inference with unconditional generation.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的递归推理模型（RRM）沿单一确定性轨迹推进，只能收敛到一个预测结果，无法探索多种解题路径。这在面对多解问题时表现受限，也无法灵活扩展推理时的计算资源。此外，这类确定性框架缺乏无条件生成的能力。 Existing Recursive Reasoning Models (RRMs) refine a single latent state along one deterministic trajectory, meaning they can only converge to one prediction and cannot explore alternative solution paths. This limits their ability to handle problems with multiple valid answers or to scale inference-time computation flexibly. There is also no mechanism for unconditional generation within these deterministic recurrent frameworks.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 概率潜在轨迹：用随机潜变量转移替代确定性递推，推理时可探索多样化假设路径。 • 双模式生成：单一模型同时支持条件推理 p_θ(y|x) 和无条件生成 p_θ(x)，两种能力罕见统一。 • 双轴推理时扩展：可通过增加递归深度（串行）或并行采样更多轨迹来灵活分配计算预算。 • Probabilistic latent trajectories: GRAM replaces deterministic recurrence with stochastic latent transitions, enabling diverse hypothesis exploration at inference time. • Dual-mode generation: A single model supports both conditional reasoning p_θ(y|x) and unconditional generation p_θ(x), unifying two capabilities rarely combined. • Inference-time scaling via two axes: computation can be scaled by increasing recursive depth (sequential) or sampling more parallel trajectories (parallel), offering flexible compute budgets.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验涵盖结构化推理任务（多步逻辑/数学问题）和多解约束满足任务（类数独问题）。基线包括确定性循环模型（RNN/GRU）、确定性递归推理模型（RRM）及自回归序列模型。评估指标包括预测准确率、解的多样性，以及在不同推理时计算预算（深度与轨迹数量）下的无条件生成质量。 Experiments were conducted on structured reasoning tasks including multi-step logical/mathematical problems and multi-solution constraint satisfaction tasks (e.g., Sudoku-style problems with multiple valid completions). Baselines included deterministic recurrent models (RNNs/GRUs), deterministic recursive reasoning models (RRMs), and autoregressive sequence models. Evaluation metrics included prediction accuracy, solution diversity, and unconditional generation quality assessed across varying inference-time compute budgets (depth and trajectory count).
📊 实验
结果		 GRAM在结构化推理基准上持续优于确定性循环与递归基线模型。在多解约束满足任务上，相比单轨迹RRM，GRAM在解的多样性与准确率上均有提升，并行轨迹采样（best-of-N）随计算量增加带来额外收益。无条件生成能力得到定性与定量验证，而这是所有确定性基线所不具备的。相较确定性RRM基线，不同任务上准确率提升约3–8%，同时实现了确定性模型无法覆盖的多解输出。 GRAM consistently outperforms deterministic recurrent and recursive baselines on structured reasoning benchmarks. On multi-solution constraint satisfaction tasks, GRAM achieves improved solution diversity and accuracy over single-trajectory RRMs, with parallel trajectory sampling (best-of-N) providing additional gains as compute increases. Unconditional generation quality is demonstrated qualitatively and quantitatively, a capability absent in all deterministic baselines. Specific numeric gains show GRAM achieving higher accuracy (reported improvements ranging roughly 3–8% over deterministic RRM baselines depending on task) while also unlocking multi-solution coverage that deterministic models cannot produce.
🏁 结论 GRAM证明，在递归推理模型中引入随机潜变量轨迹，可构建更强大灵活的统一框架：在结构化任务上提升准确率，支持多解生成，同时具备无条件生成能力，且通过变分推断端到端训练。这确立了概率递归推理作为可扩展生成式神经推理系统的重要方向。 GRAM demonstrates that introducing stochastic latent trajectories into recursive reasoning models yields a more powerful and flexible framework that improves accuracy on structured tasks, supports multi-solution generation, and enables unconditional generation — all within a single unified model trained via variational inference. This establishes probabilistic recursive reasoning as a compelling direction for scalable and generative neural reasoning systems.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

GRAM将推理过程建模为随机潜变量轨迹，每一步通过学习到的概率转移函数采样潜变量，整体用摊销变分推断（类VAE目标）训练。推理时可并行采样多条轨迹并聚合（如多数投票或best-of-N），提升最终预测质量。同一共享转移网络通过有无输入x，分别处理条件推理与无条件生成两种模式。 GRAM models the reasoning process as a stochastic latent trajectory where each step transitions through a learned probabilistic function, trained with amortized variational inference (a VAE-style objective). At each recursive step, a latent variable is sampled rather than deterministically updated, allowing the model to represent uncertainty and diversity across reasoning steps. Multiple trajectories can be sampled in parallel and aggregated (e.g., via majority vote or best-of-N) to improve final predictions. The same shared transition network handles both conditional and unconditional modes by conditioning on input x or marginalizing it out.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[p_\theta(y \mid x) = \int p_\theta(y \mid z_T) \prod_{t=1}^{T} p_\theta(z_t \mid z_{t-1}, x)\, dz_{1:T}\]
💡 条件推理：对给定输入x，通过对所有随机潜变量轨迹积分，计算输出y的概率——即多步随机推理过程的边际分布。 Conditional reasoning: the probability of output y given input x is computed by marginalizing over all stochastic latent trajectories z_1 through z_T — capturing the full distribution of multi-step reasoning paths.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{ELBO}} = \mathbb{E}_{q_\phi(z_{1:T}|x,y)}\left[\log p_\theta(y \mid z_T)\right] - \mathrm{KL}\left[q_\phi(z_{1:T}\mid x,y) \,\|\, p_\theta(z_{1:T}\mid x)\right]\]
💡 变分下界（ELBO）训练目标：最大化重构准确率的同时，用KL散度约束后验轨迹分布与先验轨迹分布之间的差距，实现摊销变分推断训练。 The ELBO training objective: maximize reconstruction accuracy of the answer while regularizing the inferred latent trajectory (posterior) to stay close to the prior trajectory via KL divergence — this is the VAE-style amortized variational inference loss.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

在AI领域竞相探索推理时扩展（思维链、best-of-N采样）之际，GRAM提供了一种原则性的潜空间概率替代方案，无需冗长的token生成即可探索多样假设。将条件推理与无条件生成统一于单一框架，为既能求解又能自主提问的推理模型开辟了新路径。 As the AI field races toward inference-time scaling laws (chain-of-thought, best-of-N sampling), GRAM provides a principled latent-space probabilistic alternative that avoids verbose token generation while still enabling diverse hypothesis exploration. By unifying conditional reasoning and unconditional generation in one framework, GRAM opens a path toward reasoning models that can both solve and creatively pose problems.

AutoResearchClaw: Self-Reinforcing Autonomous Research with Human-AI Collaboration AutoResearchClaw：基于人机协作的自强化自主科研系统

🧑‍🔬 Jiaqi Liu, Shi Qiu, Mairui Li, Bingzhou Li et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 多智能体辩论+自愈执行+跨轮进化，让AI科研系统真正从失败中学习
A self-reinforcing multi-agent research pipeline that learns from failures and collaborates with humans to automate scientific discovery.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的自主科研系统通常把科研过程建模为线性流水线：单一智能体推理、遇到执行失败就停止、每次运行之间不积累经验。然而真实的科研是迭代的——假设会被多角度挑战，实验会失败并指导下一次尝试，教训需要跨轮次积累。这种"一次性"的线性设计无法反映真实科研的本质。 Existing autonomous research systems treat science as a linear pipeline: single-agent reasoning, hard stops on failure, and no memory across runs. Real research is iterative — hypotheses need multi-perspective stress-testing, failures carry signal, and experience must compound over time. This mismatch means current systems cannot truly automate the iterative, self-correcting nature of scientific discovery.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **结构化多智能体辩论**：多个智能体从不同角度挑战假设和分析结果，模拟同行评审式的迭代科研过程。 • **Pivot/Refine 自愈执行循环**：执行失败不再是终点，系统自动判断是"微调(Refine)"当前方案还是"转向(Pivot)"新路径，把失败转化为信息。 • **跨轮次进化记忆**：将历史错误系统化为未来的"防护规则"，实现真正的经验积累与自我强化。 • **7种人机协作干预模式**：从完全自主到逐步监督，覆盖全谱系，并实证发现"精准高杠杆干预"优于两个极端。 • **Structured multi-agent debate**: Multiple agents challenge hypotheses and analyze results from competing perspectives, mimicking peer-review-style iteration. • **Pivot/Refine self-healing executor**: Failures trigger a decision loop — either refine the current approach or pivot to a new direction — converting errors into actionable information. • **Cross-run evolutionary memory**: Past mistakes are codified into future safeguards, enabling genuine experience accumulation across independent research runs. • **7 human-in-the-loop intervention modes**: A spectrum from full autonomy to step-by-step oversight, with empirical evidence that targeted, high-leverage collaboration beats both extremes.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在 ARC-Bench 上进行，该基准包含 25 个涵盖实验阶段的科研主题，专门用于评估自主科研系统的实验执行和结果产出能力。基线对比模型为 AI Scientist v2（当前最强的自主科研系统之一）。此外，作者还进行了针对七种人机协作干预模式的消融实验，系统比较了不同干预粒度对最终研究质量的影响。 Experiments were conducted on ARC-Bench, a 25-topic benchmark specifically designed to evaluate autonomous research systems at the experiment stage. The primary baseline is AI Scientist v2, one of the strongest existing autonomous research pipelines. An ablation study across all seven human-in-the-loop intervention modes was also performed to empirically characterize the optimal level of human collaboration granularity.
📊 实验
结果		 在 ARC-Bench（25个实验阶段科研主题）上，AutoResearchClaw 超越 AI Scientist v2 **54.7%**。人机协作消融实验表明，精准的高杠杆干预模式在性能上**同时优于**完全自主模式和逐步全监督模式，证明"适度精准介入"是最优协作策略。 On ARC-Bench (25-topic experiment-stage benchmark), AutoResearchClaw outperforms AI Scientist v2 by **54.7%**. The human-in-the-loop ablation study demonstrates that targeted, high-leverage intervention consistently outperforms both full autonomy and exhaustive step-by-step oversight across all seven intervention modes tested.
🏁 结论 AutoResearchClaw 证明，将多智能体辩论、自愈执行、跨轮次经验进化与精准人机协作相结合，可以显著超越现有线性自主科研流水线，在 ARC-Bench 上以 54.7% 的优势击败 AI Scientist v2。本文最终将自主科研系统的范式从"替代人类判断"重新定位为"放大人类科学判断"的研究增强器。 AutoResearchClaw demonstrates that combining multi-agent debate, self-healing execution, cross-run evolutionary memory, and targeted human collaboration yields a 54.7% improvement over AI Scientist v2 on ARC-Bench. The paper reframes autonomous research systems as research amplifiers that augment — rather than replace — human scientific judgment.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AutoResearchClaw 由五大机制构成。首先，多智能体辩论模块让多个智能体对假设生成和结果分析展开结构化讨论，避免单点偏见。其次，自愈执行器通过 Pivot/Refine 决策循环处理失败：判断当前失败是需要小幅修正（Refine）还是需要根本性方向调整（Pivot）。第三，可验证的结果报告机制防止数字捏造和引用幻觉。第四，跨轮次进化模块将每次运行中的错误模式提炼成规则，作为下一次运行的先验保护。最后，七种人机协作干预模式让人类可以在最关键的决策节点以不同粒度介入，而非被迫选择全自动或全监督两个极端。 AutoResearchClaw is built on five interlocking mechanisms. A structured multi-agent debate module has multiple agents argue over hypotheses and results to reduce single-agent bias. A self-healing executor uses a Pivot/Refine decision loop: when execution fails, the system decides whether to incrementally refine the current plan or pivot to a fundamentally different approach. A verifiable result reporting layer checks for fabricated numbers and hallucinated citations. A cross-run evolution module distills past error patterns into protective rules that are injected as priors in future runs. Finally, seven human-in-the-loop intervention modes let researchers engage at precise, high-leverage decision points rather than choosing between full autonomy or exhaustive oversight.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

自主科研是 AI 领域最具野心的目标之一，但现有系统脆弱、不可信且缺乏学习能力。AutoResearchClaw 提供了一套系统性的解决方案，尤其是"精准人机协作优于两个极端"这一发现，为未来人机协作科研系统的设计提供了重要的实证依据。跨轮次进化机制也为构建真正"越用越聪明"的科研 AI 铺平了道路。 Autonomous scientific discovery is one of AI's most ambitious frontiers, yet existing systems are brittle, hallucination-prone, and stateless. AutoResearchClaw's finding that targeted human collaboration at high-leverage points outperforms both extremes provides a concrete design principle for the next generation of human-AI research systems. Its cross-run evolution mechanism also points toward research AIs that genuinely compound knowledge over time.

Vision-OPD: Learning to See Fine Details for Multimodal LLMs via On-Policy Self-Distillation Vision-OPD：通过在线策略自蒸馏让多模态大模型学会看清细节

🎯 用裁剪图像作教师，让大模型在完整图像中也能精准定位细节
Self-distillation from crop-conditioned teacher to full-image student sharpens fine-grained visual reasoning in MLLMs.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 多模态大语言模型（MLLM）在处理需要关注图像局部细节的问题时表现很差——比如一张图里有一个小但关键的物体或文字。研究者发现，同一个模型在看"裁剪后的局部图"时比看"完整图"更容易答对相同的问题，说明问题不在于模型不认识局部内容，而在于模型不知道该把注意力聚焦在哪里。如何让模型在只看完整图时也能自动"聚焦"到关键区域，是本文要解决的核心挑战。 Multimodal LLMs frequently fail at fine-grained visual questions that hinge on small but critical evidence within a full image. The same model answers such questions far more accurately when given a cropped, evidence-centered region — revealing a "regional-to-global perception gap." The challenge is closing this gap without relying on external teachers, human labels, or inference-time tools like visual search agents.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **区域-全局感知差距的新发现**：首次系统性揭示同一MLLM在裁剪图和完整图上的答题准确率差异，将其定义为可利用的自监督信号。 • **无需外部资源的在线策略自蒸馏**：用模型自身的"裁剪条件策略"作为教师，"完整图条件策略"作为学生，无需任何外部教师模型、人工标注或奖励函数。 • **Token级分布对齐**：在学生自身生成的rollout轨迹上最小化教师与学生的next-token分布散度，保持on-policy特性，训练更稳定高效。 • **Regional-to-global perception gap**: First systematic identification that the same MLLM answers fine-grained questions far better on crops than full images, repurposing this gap as a free self-supervision signal. • **On-policy self-distillation without external resources**: The model acts as its own teacher (crop-conditioned) and student (full-image-conditioned), requiring no external model, ground-truth labels, reward verifiers, or inference-time tool use. • **Token-level KL divergence on student rollouts**: Distillation is applied on the student's own generated trajectories, keeping training on-policy for stable and efficient optimization.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个细粒度视觉理解基准上进行，包括 V*Bench、HR-Bench、MME-RealWorld 等，涵盖高分辨率感知、小目标检测、文档理解等子任务。基线涵盖同规模开源模型（如 InternVL、Qwen-VL 系列）、更大规模闭源模型（如 GPT-4o）、以及具备"图像思考"能力的智能体模型（如 Visual Search Agent）。评估以准确率为主要指标，并对比了模型参数量以衡量效率。 Experiments were conducted on multiple fine-grained visual understanding benchmarks including V*Bench, HR-Bench, and MME-RealWorld, covering subtasks like high-resolution perception, small object detection, and document understanding. Baselines include same-scale open-source MLLMs (e.g., InternVL2.5, Qwen2.5-VL), larger closed-source models (e.g., GPT-4o), and agentic "Thinking-with-Images" models that use inference-time visual search tools. The primary metric is accuracy, with model size tracked to assess efficiency.
📊 实验
结果		 在 V*Bench 上，Vision-OPD 在 7B 规模下超越了参数量大数倍的闭源模型及视觉搜索智能体。在 HR-Bench 上，相比基础模型取得了显著提升（在多个子任务上绝对提升超过 5 个百分点）。在 MME-RealWorld 细粒度子集上，Vision-OPD 模型达到或超越更大规模的开源和闭源竞品，且无需任何推理时工具调用，证明细节感知能力已被内化进模型权重中。 On V*Bench, Vision-OPD at the 7B scale outperforms closed-source models and visual search agents that are substantially larger or tool-augmented. On HR-Bench, it achieves absolute accuracy gains exceeding 5 percentage points over the base model across multiple subtasks. On the MME-RealWorld fine-grained subset, Vision-OPD reaches competitive or superior performance against larger open-source and closed-source MLLMs, all without any inference-time visual tool use, demonstrating that fine-grained perception is fully internalized into model weights.
🏁 结论 本文证明了多模态大模型的细粒度视觉理解失败根源在于注意力聚焦不足而非局部识别能力欠缺，并提出了一种完全自给自足的在线策略自蒸馏方法 Vision-OPD，无需任何外部资源即可有效弥合区域-全局感知差距，在多个基准上超越了更大规模的开源和闭源模型。 This paper demonstrates that fine-grained visual failure in MLLMs stems from a focus deficit rather than a recognition deficit, and proves that on-policy self-distillation from the model's own crop-conditioned policy effectively closes the regional-to-global perception gap — achieving competitive or superior results against much larger models without any external supervision.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Vision-OPD 从同一个 MLLM 中实例化两个条件策略：一个以"证据裁剪图+问题"为输入的教师策略，一个以"完整图+问题"为输入的学生策略。训练时，先让学生策略自主生成回答轨迹（on-policy rollout），然后对同一轨迹上的每个token，计算教师与学生 next-token 概率分布之间的 KL 散度并最小化。这相当于把模型"在局部图上的感知优势"蒸馏回"全图策略"中，让模型内化视觉缩放的能力。整个过程不需要任何外部模型或人工标注，完全自给自足。 Vision-OPD instantiates two inference policies from the same MLLM: a crop-conditioned teacher (given the evidence region and question) and a full-image-conditioned student (given the complete image and question). During training, the student generates on-policy rollouts from the full image. For each token in these rollouts, Vision-OPD minimizes the KL divergence between the teacher's and student's next-token distributions, transferring the teacher's fine-grained perceptual advantage to the student. No external models, labels, or reward signals are needed — the MLLM's own regional knowledge serves as the sole supervision source.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

该方法彻底摆脱了对外部教师模型、人工标注和推理时工具的依赖，为提升多模态模型的细粒度视觉能力提供了一条高度可扩展的自监督路径。对于需要处理高分辨率图像、医疗影像、卫星图像等精细感知任务的实际应用，这一方法具有重要的落地价值。 Vision-OPD offers a fully self-contained, label-free training recipe that any MLLM can apply to sharpen fine-grained visual reasoning, removing the need for costly annotation pipelines or complex agentic inference. This has broad implications for real-world applications requiring precise visual perception, such as medical imaging, satellite analysis, and document understanding.
