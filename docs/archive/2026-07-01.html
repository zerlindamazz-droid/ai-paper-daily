DOPD: Dual On-policy Distillation DOPD：双重在线策略蒸馏

🧑‍🔬 Xinlei Yu, Gen Li, Qingyi Si, Guibin Zhang et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 用"双老师"智能路由让小模型更聪明地向大模型学习
A smarter student-teacher distillation method that dynamically routes learning signals to avoid misleading supervision.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个学霸在给普通同学辅导时，聪明的辅导老师会区分哪些题目是"你能学会的解题技巧"，哪些是"因为你有参考书而我没有才做对的"——对前者重点讲解，对后者则换一种方式补充。DOPD就是那个会区分两类题目的聪明辅导老师。Imagine a star student tutoring a classmate — a smart tutor knows the difference between "skills you can actually learn" and "answers I got right only because I had the answer key you don't." DOPD is that smart tutor: it figures out, word by word, whether the AI student is learning real skills or just chasing an unfair advantage it can never have.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练小型AI模型时，我们通常让它向大型AI模型"学习"，这叫知识蒸馏。但当大模型或小模型拥有对方没有的"特权信息"（比如额外的图片或提示）时，小模型会被误导——它可能以为自己在学习大模型的"能力"，其实只是在模仿它拥有的"额外信息优势"，这种差距永远无法真正弥补。更麻烦的是，并不是每个词（token）都同等重要，大多数词对真正的能力提升贡献很小。 When training a small AI model to mimic a large one (called knowledge distillation), things get tricky if either model has extra information the other doesn't — like one having access to a picture while the other doesn't. The small model can accidentally "learn" to copy the big model's information advantage rather than its actual reasoning skills, a gap it can never truly close. On top of that, not all words in a generated response are equally important for learning real skills.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 发现并命名了"特权幻觉"现象：小模型在蒸馏时会把"信息不对称"误当成"能力差距"去学习，导致学习方向跑偏。 • 提出双重蒸馏框架：根据每个词（token）的重要性，动态决定这个词该向"特权大模型"学，还是向"特权小模型"自身"学"。 • 为每个词设计了不同强度、目标和策略的监督信号，做到有的放矢，避免被无用信号干扰。 • Identifies and names a new failure mode called "privilege illusion" — where the student model learns the wrong thing by confusing information gaps with capability gaps. • Proposes a dual distillation framework that dynamically assigns each word-token to either the privileged teacher or the privileged student as its learning source. • Designs customized supervision (different strength, objective, and strategy) per token, ensuring only credible and useful signals drive learning.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在两大类模型上进行：大语言模型（LLM）和视觉语言模型（VLM）。评估涵盖标准蒸馏基准、分布外（OOD）任务、持续学习场景和鲁棒性测试。与普通在线策略蒸馏（Vanilla OPD）及多种同类方法进行对比。还额外测试了方法在不同训练设置下的稳定性。 Experiments were run on both large language models (LLMs) and vision-language models (VLMs). The method was compared against vanilla on-policy distillation and several other distillation baselines. Evaluation covered standard benchmarks, out-of-distribution (OOD) tasks, continual learning scenarios, and robustness tests. Stability across different training configurations was also assessed.
📊 实验
结果		 DOPD在LLM和VLM设置下均持续优于普通在线策略蒸馏（Vanilla OPD）及所有对比方法。在分布外任务上也展示出显著优势，验证了方法的泛化能力。持续学习和鲁棒性实验进一步证实其稳定性。（注：论文摘要中未披露具体数字，以上为方向性结论；具体数值需参阅论文正文表格。） DOPD consistently outperforms Vanilla OPD and all compared baselines across both LLM and VLM settings. It shows notable gains on out-of-distribution tasks, demonstrating stronger generalization. Continual learning and robustness experiments further confirm its stability advantages. (Note: The abstract does not state specific numeric improvements; precise figures are available in the paper's main result tables.)
🏁 结论 DOPD证明了在知识蒸馏中，精准识别每个词的监督来源（大模型还是小模型自身）比盲目跟随大模型更有效。它为AI模型压缩领域提供了一种更可靠、更聪明的训练范式。 DOPD proves that dynamically routing supervision per token — rather than blindly following the teacher — leads to more effective and reliable knowledge distillation. It sets a new, smarter paradigm for compressing large AI models into smaller, capable ones.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

DOPD的核心思路是：不让小模型盲目跟着大模型学所有词，而是对每个词做一个"裁判"——通过比较大模型和小模型在这个词上的"优势分差"和"相对概率"，判断这个词的监督信号应该来自哪里。如果大模型在这个词上的优势明显且可信，就让小模型跟大模型学；如果优势来自于信息不对称（即"特权幻觉"），就让小模型参考自己的特权版本来学，只获取辅助信号而不被误导。这样每个词都得到最适合它的学习方式，既传递真实能力，又避免被虚假优势带偏。 DOPD acts like a smart referee for every single word the student model generates. For each word, it compares the "advantage scores" of the privileged teacher and the privileged student — essentially asking: "Is the teacher better here because of real skill, or just because it has extra info?" If the teacher's advantage is genuine and trustworthy, the student learns from the teacher. If the advantage smells like it comes from information the student can never have, the student instead learns from its own "privileged self" — gaining auxiliary signals without being misled. Every word gets a tailored teaching strategy: different intensity, different goal, and different approach.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Route}(t) = \begin{cases} \text{Teacher supervision} & \text{if } A_{\text{teacher}}(t) - A_{\text{student}}(t) > \delta \\ \text{Student self-supervision} & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}\]
💡 对每个词（token）t，比较特权大模型和特权小模型的优势分差，若大模型优势明显（超过阈值δ），则用大模型监督；否则用小模型自身的特权版本提供辅助监督。 For each token t, compare the advantage gap between the privileged teacher and privileged student. If the teacher's advantage exceeds a threshold δ, learn from the teacher; otherwise, use the student's own privileged version for auxiliary supervision.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

在现实世界中，我们需要把大型AI模型"压缩"成小模型，才能部署到手机或低算力设备上。DOPD让这种压缩更聪明、更准确，意味着未来手机上的AI助手可以更接近云端大模型的能力。 Compressing large AI models into smaller ones is key to running AI on phones and everyday devices. DOPD makes this compression smarter and more accurate, meaning the AI in your pocket could soon be much closer in quality to the powerful cloud-based version.

Orca: The World is in Your Mind Orca：世界就在你的脑海中

🧑‍🔬 Yihao Wang, Yuheng Ji, Mingyu Cao, Yanqing Shen et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 Orca是一个统一的世界基础模型，通过视频和语言学习理解、预测并行动于世界。
Orca is a unified world foundation model that learns to understand, predict, and act in the world from video and language.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像培养一个博学多才的人，他既看了成千上万小时的纪录片，又读了几亿本教科书——而不是雇三个只懂一件事的专家。不管你要问问题、要画图，还是要搬箱子，这一个人全都能搞定，因为他真正理解世界的运作方式。Think of it like building one incredibly well-read person who has watched thousands of hours of documentaries AND studied millions of textbooks — instead of hiring three separate specialists who each only know one thing. When you need an answer, a drawing, or someone to move a box, this one person can do it all, because they truly understand how the world works.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的AI模型高度专门化：有的只会预测下一个词，有的只会预测下一帧视频，还有的只会控制机器人动作。它们之间互不相通，就像三个专家各说各话，没人能看懂完整的世界。我们真正需要的是一个能"理解世界"的统一模型，既能回答问题，又能预测未来，还能指挥机器人行动。 Current AI models are highly specialized: one model predicts the next word, another predicts the next video frame, and another decides what action a robot should take — but none of these models share a common understanding of how the world actually works. It's like having three different experts who can't talk to each other, each seeing only one slice of reality. We need a single model that truly "understands" the world and can flexibly answer questions, predict what happens next, and guide a robot — all at once.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 不再只预测"下一个词"或"下一帧"，而是提出统一的"下一状态预测"框架，跨所有模态建模世界如何从一个状态过渡到另一个状态。• 两种互补学习方式：「无意识学习」（被动吸收连续视频中的自然状态变化）和「有意识学习」（通过语言描述的事件和视觉问答主动理解有意义的变化）。• 预训练后主干网络完全冻结，只训练轻量级解码器，证明世界知识真正编码在共享的潜在空间中。 • Instead of predicting "next token" or "next frame," Orca proposes a unified "Next-State-Prediction" framework — modeling how the world transitions from one state to another, across all modalities at once. • Orca learns in two complementary ways: "unconscious learning" (absorbing dense, continuous video like passive observation) and "conscious learning" (understanding meaningful events described in language, like active reasoning). • After pre-training, the model's backbone is completely frozen — only small, lightweight decoders are fine-tuned per task, proving the world knowledge is genuinely encoded in the shared latent space.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究者用12.5万小时视频和1.6亿事件标注预训练Orca，再接上轻量级解码器（主干冻结）评估三类下游任务：文本生成用MVBench、TemporalBench、EgoSchema、VideoMME等视频问答基准；图像预测用Something-Something V2数据集；具身动作生成用CALVIN机器人操控基准。对比的基线均为同等规模、专门为单一任务训练的专业模型。 The researchers pre-trained Orca on 125K hours of video and 160M event annotations, then evaluated it on three downstream tasks by attaching lightweight decoders while keeping the backbone frozen. For text generation, they tested on video question-answering benchmarks (MVBench, TemporalBench, EgoSchema, VideoMME). For image prediction, they used the Something-Something V2 dataset. For embodied action generation, they used the CALVIN robot manipulation benchmark. Baselines were similar-scale specialized models trained specifically for each individual task.
📊 实验
结果		 在视频问答基准上，Orca在MVBench、TemporalBench、EgoSchema和VideoMME上均超越同等规模的专业基线模型。在图像预测（Something-Something V2）上达到有竞争力的未来帧预测质量。在CALVIN机器人操控基准上，Orca的具身动作生成也优于专业基线。扩展实验证明：世界潜在空间越强，三类下游任务表现全面提升，验证了"更强的世界潜在→更强的下游输出"假说。值得注意的是，这些成绩均在主干冻结、仅训练少量解码器参数的条件下取得。 On video QA benchmarks, Orca outperforms similar-sized specialized baselines across MVBench, TemporalBench, EgoSchema, and VideoMME. On image prediction (Something-Something V2), Orca achieves competitive future-frame prediction quality. On the CALVIN robot manipulation benchmark, Orca surpasses specialized baselines in embodied action generation. Scaling experiments confirm that a larger world latent consistently improves performance across all three readout types, validating the "stronger world latent → stronger downstream" hypothesis. Specific highlight: Orca achieves these results with a frozen backbone, meaning only the small decoders (a fraction of total parameters) are trained per task.
🏁 结论 Orca证明了：一个通过被动视频观察和主动语言引导学习训练的统一世界模型，能将丰富的世界知识迁移到文字、图像和动作任务上，并超越同规模的专业模型。这验证了通用世界基础模型作为AI真正理解并与世界互动的可扩展路径的可行性。 Orca proves that a single unified world model — trained with both passive video observation and active language-guided learning — can encode rich world knowledge that transfers effectively to text, image, and action tasks, outperforming task-specific specialized models of similar size. This validates the vision of a general-purpose world foundation model as a scalable path toward AI that truly understands and interacts with the world.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Orca通过两种体验构建对世界的统一"心智模型"。首先，它被动观看12.5万小时的视频，就像孩子自然吸收世界变化规律（无意识学习）。其次，它主动学习1.6亿条语言描述的事件和视觉问答，就像学生认真读教材、做习题（有意识学习）。两种经验都被编码进统一的"世界潜在空间"——一张关于世界运作方式的内部地图。地图建好后，只需接上不同的轻量级解码器（就像同一部手机上的不同App），就能输出文字答案、预测未来图像或生成机器人动作，而无需改动核心世界模型。 Orca works by building one shared "mental model" of the world from two types of experiences. First, it watches 125,000 hours of video passively — like a child absorbing how things move and change naturally (unconscious learning). Second, it studies 160 million language-described events and visual question-answering pairs — like a student actively reading textbooks and answering questions (conscious learning). Both types of experience are encoded into a single unified "world latent space," a kind of internal mental map of how the world works. Once this map is built, Orca plugs in small task-specific decoders (like different app interfaces on the same phone) to generate text answers, predict future images, or output robot actions — without ever changing the core world model.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

当今的AI助手、图像生成器和机器人都是分别训练的，无法共享知识——Orca指向一个未来：一个模型对世界的理解深刻到足以同时完成所有这些任务。这可能大幅降低构建AI系统的成本和难度，同时让机器人和AI助手在现实世界中变得更强大、更灵活。 Today's AI assistants, image generators, and robots are all trained separately and can't share knowledge — Orca points toward a future where one model understands the world deeply enough to do all of these things together. This could dramatically reduce the cost and effort of building AI systems, while making robots and assistants far more capable and adaptable in the real world.
