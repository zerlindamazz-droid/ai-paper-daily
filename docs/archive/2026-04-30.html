Incompressible Knowledge Probes: Estimating Black-Box LLM Parameter Counts via Factual Capacity 不可压缩知识探针：通过事实容量估计黑盒LLM参数量

🧑‍🔬 Bojie Li

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 用知识存储理论推算黑盒LLM参数量的事实基准
A factual benchmark that lower-bounds black-box LLM parameter counts using knowledge storage theory.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 闭源AI实验室（如OpenAI、Anthropic）不公开模型参数量。现有基于推理成本的估算方法因硬件和部署假设不同，误差超过2倍。目前没有可靠的、仅依赖模型本身的方法从外部估算模型大小。 Closed-source AI labs (like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) don't reveal how many parameters their models have. Existing estimates based on inference cost have over 2× uncertainty due to hardware and serving-stack assumptions. There's no reliable, model-intrinsic way to estimate model size from the outside.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **信息论下界**：存储F条事实至少需要F/(每参数比特数)个权重，模型"知道多少"直接决定它"至少有多少参数"。 • **IKP基准设计**：1400道跨7级稀有度的事实题，专门剔除可通过推理或架构优化压缩的知识，确保测的是纯粹的参数存储容量。 • **MoE关键发现**：对混合专家模型，总参数量预测事实知识能力(R²=0.79)远优于激活参数量(R²=0.51)。 • **推翻"密化定律"**：事实容量随时间的算法效率提升趋近于零，与Densing Law预测的持续压缩相矛盾。 • **Information-theoretic lower bound**: storing F facts requires at least F/(bits per parameter) weights — measuring what a model *knows* directly lower-bounds how many parameters it *has*. • **IKP Benchmark**: 1,400 factual questions across 7 tiers of obscurity, carefully designed to exclude facts derivable by reasoning or compressible by architecture tricks. • **MoE insight**: For Mixture-of-Experts models, *total* parameters predict factual knowledge (R²=0.79) far better than *active* parameters (R²=0.51), clarifying a long-debated question. • **Densing Law falsified**: Factual capacity does NOT improve with algorithmic efficiency over time — IKP scores show near-zero temporal drift, directly contradicting the "Densing Law."
🧪 实验
内容		 在89个开源模型（135M–1600B，来自19家厂商）上标定IKP并做留一法交叉验证，再对188个模型（含GPT-4、Claude、Gemini等闭源模型）进行参数量估算。与基于推理成本的估算方法对比，同时对96个有时间戳的开源模型做时间回归来验证"密化定律"，并对MoE模型分别分析总参数与激活参数的预测力差异。 The benchmark (IKP) was evaluated on 89 open-weight models from 19 vendors (135M–1,600B parameters) for calibration, and then applied to 188 total models from 27 vendors including proprietary models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, etc.). Baselines for comparison include inference-cost-based parameter estimates. Leave-one-out cross-validation was used to test generalization. A temporal regression across 96 dated open-weight models was run to test the "Densing Law" hypothesis. MoE models were analyzed separately comparing total vs. active parameter predictions.
📊 实验
结果		 89个开源模型标定的对数线性回归达到R²=0.917。留一法交叉验证：中位误差倍数1.59×，68.5%模型估算误差在2倍以内，87.6%在3倍以内。MoE模型：总参数预测R²=0.79，激活参数预测R²=0.51。IKP时间漂移系数−0.0010/月（95% CI [−0.0031, +0.0008]），统计上与零无异，以p<10⁻¹⁵的显著性拒绝密化定律预测的+0.0117/月。主要闭源前沿模型的参数估算与已泄露/传闻数字基本吻合，安全调优模型估算结果视为下界。 Log-linear calibration achieves R²=0.917 on 89 open-weight models. Leave-one-out cross-validation: median fold error 1.59×, 68.5% of models estimated within 2× of true size, 87.6% within 3×. For MoE models: total parameters R²=0.79 vs. active parameters R²=0.51. Temporal IKP drift: −0.0010/month (95% CI [−0.0031, +0.0008]) — statistically indistinguishable from zero, rejecting the Densing Law's predicted +0.0117/month at p < 10⁻¹⁵. Proprietary model estimates place major frontier models at sizes consistent with leaked/rumored figures, with safety-tuned models treated as lower bounds.
🏁 结论 IKP提供了一种有理论依据的、仅依赖模型本身的方法估算黑盒LLM参数量，中位误差约1.59倍，优于基于推理成本的方法；同时证明事实知识容量跨代际、跨厂商地与参数量保持对数线性关系，且不会随算法效率的提升而自动压缩。 IKP provides a principled, model-intrinsic method to estimate black-box LLM parameter counts with ~1.59× median error — tighter than inference-cost approaches — while also demonstrating that factual knowledge capacity scales log-linearly with parameters across generations and vendors, and does not compress with algorithmic improvements over time.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文推导了一个信息论下界：存储F条事实至少需要F/(每参数比特数)个权重，因此测量模型掌握的事实知识量可以给出参数量的下限。IKP基准包含1400道按7级稀有度分层的问题，从常识到极度冷僻的事实均有涵盖，确保答案无法通过推理得出、也难以被架构优化压缩。在89个开源模型（135M至1600B参数，来自19家厂商）上标定对数线性回归，将IKP准确率映射到参数量。再将此映射应用于188个模型（含主要闭源前沿模型）进行参数估算。 The paper derives an information-theoretic lower bound: since storing F facts requires at least F/(bits per parameter) model weights, measuring factual knowledge gives a floor on parameter count. IKP is a benchmark of 1,400 questions stratified across 7 obscurity tiers — from common to highly esoteric facts — chosen so correct answers cannot be inferred by reasoning alone and are unlikely to compress further with architectural improvements. A log-linear regression is calibrated on 89 open-weight models ranging from 135M to 1,600B parameters across 19 vendors, mapping IKP accuracy scores to parameter counts. This calibration is then applied to 188 models from 27 vendors, including major proprietary frontier models, to produce parameter estimates.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Parameters} \geq \frac{F}{\text{bits per parameter}}\]
💡 存储F条事实所需的最少参数量下界：模型参数量至少等于事实数量除以每个参数能存储的比特数。 The core information-theoretic lower bound: storing F facts requires at least F divided by the bits-per-parameter capacity of the model architecture.
\[\log(\hat{P}) = a \cdot \text{Acc}_{\text{IKP}} + b, \quad R^2 = 0.917\]
💡 对数线性映射：将IKP准确率映射到模型参数量的对数，在89个开源模型上拟合优度R²=0.917。 Log-linear calibration mapping IKP accuracy to log parameter count, achieving R²=0.917 across 89 open-weight models.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着前沿AI实验室越来越倾向于隐藏模型规模，IKP为研究社区提供了一种无需内部信息即可可靠审计和比较模型规模的工具。同时，它解决了关于规模化的核心争论：事实容量并未饱和，参数规模的提升仍是AI进步不可或缺的维度，而非已被算法效率所取代。 As frontier AI labs increasingly keep model sizes secret, IKP offers the research community a reliable, cost-effective tool to audit and compare model scales without insider access. It also resolves a fundamental debate about scaling: factual capacity is not saturating, meaning parameter scaling remains a necessary (not obsolete) axis of progress even as reasoning benchmarks plateau.

The Last Human-Written Paper: Agent-Native Research Artifacts 最后一篇人类撰写的论文：面向智能体的研究制品

🧑‍🔬 Jiachen Liu, Jiaxin Pei, Jintao Huang, Chenglei Si et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用机器可执行格式取代传统论文，让AI智能体能复现和拓展科研成果
A new machine-executable research format replaces traditional papers to let AI agents reproduce and extend science.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 传统科学论文是为人类读者设计的，把复杂的研究过程压缩成一条线性叙事，丢弃了失败实验、死胡同和关键实现细节。当AI智能体需要理解、复现或拓展已发表的研究时，这种"人类友好"的格式就成了严重障碍。随着AI越来越多地参与科研，这一问题亟待解决。 Traditional scientific papers are written for human readers and compress a messy, branching research process into a clean linear story. This discards failed experiments, dead ends, and implementation details that are critical for AI agents trying to understand, reproduce, or build on published work. As AI agents increasingly assist in science, this mismatch between human-readable papers and machine-actionable specifications becomes a serious bottleneck.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 明确提出传统论文的两大结构性成本："叙事税"（失败路径被丢弃）和"工程税"（实现细节对智能体不足）。 • 提出Ara（面向智能体的研究制品），以四层机器可执行结构取代叙事型论文。 • 配套三个工具：实时捕捉决策的Live Research Manager、将旧版PDF转换为Ara的编译器、以及自动化客观审查的Ara原生审稿系统。 • Identifies two structural costs in traditional papers: a "Storytelling Tax" (failed paths are discarded) and an "Engineering Tax" (implementation details are left unwritten for agents). • Introduces Ara (Agent-Native Research Artifact), a four-layer machine-executable research package replacing the narrative paper. • Proposes three supporting tools: a Live Research Manager to capture decisions in real time, an Ara Compiler to convert legacy PDFs/repos, and an Ara-native review system for automated objective checks.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在两个基准上评估Ara：PaperBench（测试智能体理解研究论文的问答基准）和RE-Bench（测试AI研究任务的复现与开放式拓展基准）。基线使用标准PDF论文和代码库，与Ara格式进行对比。针对拓展任务，作者还专门测试了探索图中保留的失败路径对五项开放式RE-Bench任务是否有助于或限制了有能力的智能体。 The authors evaluated Ara on two benchmarks: PaperBench (a question-answering benchmark measuring how well agents can understand research papers) and RE-Bench (a reproduction and open-ended extension benchmark for AI research tasks). Baselines used standard PDF papers and code repositories; Ara-formatted artifacts were compared against these. For extension tasks, the authors specifically tested whether preserving failure traces from the exploration graph helped or constrained capable agents on five open-ended RE-Bench tasks.
📊 实验
结果		 在PaperBench上，问答准确率从72.4%提升至93.7%（提升21.3个百分点）。在RE-Bench上，复现成功率从57.4%提升至64.4%（提升7个百分点）。在RE-Bench的五项开放式拓展任务中，Ara中保留的失败路径总体上能加速智能体进展，但对于能力较强的智能体，也可能将其限制在已有实验框架内，抑制更大胆的探索。 On PaperBench, Ara raises question-answering accuracy from 72.4% to 93.7% (+21.3 percentage points). On RE-Bench, reproduction success improves from 57.4% to 64.4% (+7 percentage points). On RE-Bench's five open-ended extension tasks, preserved failure traces in Ara generally accelerate agent progress, but can also constrain more capable agents by anchoring them to prior experimental runs rather than exploring novel directions.
🏁 结论 Ara证明了用结构化、机器可执行的研究制品替代叙事型论文，能显著提升AI智能体理解和复现科研成果的能力，同时也揭示了一个微妙的权衡：保留失败路径对能力较弱的智能体有益，但可能限制更强大的智能体。本文为AI原生科学出版的新范式奠定了基础。 Ara demonstrates that replacing narrative papers with structured, machine-executable research artifacts significantly improves AI agents' ability to understand and reproduce scientific work, while also surfacing a nuanced tradeoff: preserved failure traces help weaker agents but may constrain stronger ones. This work lays the groundwork for a new paradigm of AI-native scientific publishing.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Ara将研究制品分为四层：(1) 科学逻辑层，记录假设与推理；(2) 可执行代码层，含完整规格说明；(3) 探索图层，保留分支路径包括失败实验；(4) 证据层，将每个论断与原始输出挂钩。Live Research Manager在日常开发中被动记录决策和死胡同，避免信息丢失。Ara编译器利用大语言模型将已有PDF和代码库转译为Ara格式，实现对历史文献的向后兼容。Ara原生审稿系统自动完成客观核查（如代码能否运行、论断是否与证据一致），让人类审稿人专注于新颖性和重要性判断。 Ara structures a research artifact into four layers: (1) scientific logic capturing hypotheses and rationale, (2) executable code with full specifications, (3) an exploration graph that preserves branching paths including failures and dead ends, and (4) evidence grounding every claim in raw experimental outputs. A Live Research Manager passively logs decisions and dead ends during normal development so nothing is lost. An Ara Compiler uses LLMs to translate existing PDFs and code repositories into the Ara format, making the protocol backward-compatible with legacy literature. An Ara-native review system automates objective checks (e.g., code runs, claims match evidence), freeing human reviewers to focus on novelty and significance.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Storytelling Tax} + \text{Engineering Tax} \rightarrow \text{Agent Comprehension Gap}\]
💡 传统论文的两种结构性成本——叙事税（失败路径被丢弃）和工程税（实现细节缺失）——共同造成智能体理解鸿沟 The two structural costs of traditional papers — Storytelling Tax (failed paths discarded) and Engineering Tax (missing implementation details) — together create a gap that prevents AI agents from fully understanding published research.
\[\text{Ara} = \{\mathcal{L}_{\text{logic}},\ \mathcal{L}_{\text{code}},\ \mathcal{G}_{\text{explore}},\ \mathcal{E}_{\text{evidence}}\}\]
💡 Ara由四层组成：科学逻辑层、可执行代码层、探索图层（含失败路径）、证据层，共同构成完整的机器可执行研究制品 Ara is defined as a four-layer structure: scientific logic, executable code, an exploration graph (including failures), and evidence grounding — together forming a complete machine-executable research artifact.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着AI智能体在科研中承担越来越重要的角色，科学知识的载体形式本身也必须进化——Ara提供了一个具体可落地的转型协议。这项工作挑战了延续数百年的叙事型论文惯例，有望重塑AI辅助时代下研究成果的创作、共享、审稿与传承方式。 As AI agents take on larger roles in scientific research, the format of scientific knowledge itself must evolve — Ara offers a concrete, implementable protocol for that transition. This work challenges the centuries-old convention of the narrative paper and could reshape how research is created, shared, reviewed, and built upon in an AI-assisted future.

Agentic Harness Engineering: Observability-Driven Automatic Evolution of Coding-Agent Harnesses 智能体测试框架工程：可观测性驱动的编程智能体框架自动演化

🧑‍🔬 Jiahang Lin, Shichun Liu, Chengjun Pan, Lizhi Lin et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 三重可观测性柱自动演化编程智能体框架，超越人工设计基线
AHE automates coding-agent harness evolution using three observability pillars, beating human-designed harnesses on major benchmarks.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 编程智能体的性能很大程度上取决于"测试框架"（harness），即控制模型如何与代码仓库、工具和运行环境交互的脚手架系统。手动设计好的框架既耗时又依赖专家经验。自动化改进框架极为困难：动作空间庞大、评估信号稀疏且嘈杂、运行轨迹可达数百万token，且很难将某次编辑的效果归因到下一轮结果中。 Coding agents rely heavily on "harnesses" — the scaffolding that controls how a model interacts with code repositories, tools, and execution environments. Designing good harnesses manually is time-consuming and expert-intensive. Automating harness improvement is difficult because the action space is huge, evaluation signals are sparse and noisy, trajectories can span millions of tokens, and it's hard to attribute outcomes to specific edits.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **组件可观测性**：每个可编辑的框架组件都有文件级表示，使动作空间明确且可回滚，避免盲目修改。 • **经验可观测性**：将数百万原始轨迹token压缩为分层可钻取的证据语料库，演化智能体可真正消化和推理这些信息。 • **决策可观测性**：每次框架编辑都附带一个"自我声明的预测"，并在下一轮任务结果中验证，将每次编辑变成可证伪的契约，杜绝纯粹的试错式演化。 • **Component Observability**: Every editable harness component gets a file-level representation, making the action space explicit and easily revertible. • **Experience Observability**: Millions of raw trajectory tokens are distilled into a layered, drill-down evidence corpus that an evolving agent can actually consume and reason over. • **Decision Observability**: Each harness edit is paired with a self-declared prediction, which is later verified against real task-level outcomes — turning every edit into a falsifiable contract rather than blind trial-and-error.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在Terminal-Bench 2和SWE-bench-verified两个主流编程智能体基准上评估AHE，与人工设计框架（Codex-CLI）及自演化基线（ACE和TF-GRPO）进行对比。主要评估指标为pass@1（任务成功率）和token效率。此外，通过将演化后的框架冻结并应用于三个不同模型家族（不重新演化），测试跨模型族的迁移泛化能力。 The authors evaluated AHE on Terminal-Bench 2 and SWE-bench-verified, two widely used coding-agent benchmarks. They compared against human-designed harnesses (Codex-CLI), and self-evolving baselines (ACE and TF-GRPO). The primary metric was pass@1 (task success rate) and token efficiency. Cross-family transfer was tested by freezing the evolved harness and applying it to three alternative model families without re-evolution, to test generalization.
📊 实验
结果		 • Terminal-Bench 2：经过10次AHE迭代，pass@1从69.7%提升至77.0%（+7.3pp），超越人工设计框架Codex-CLI（71.9%）及自演化基线ACE和TF-GRPO。 • SWE-bench-verified：冻结的AHE框架在比种子框架少用12% token的情况下取得最高综合成功率。 • Terminal-Bench 2跨模型族迁移：在三个不同模型家族上获得+5.1至+10.1个百分点的提升，表明演化组件编码了通用工程经验而非基准特定调优。 • Terminal-Bench 2: pass@1 lifted from 69.7% → 77.0% after 10 AHE iterations (+7.3pp), surpassing human-designed Codex-CLI (71.9%) and self-evolving baselines ACE and TF-GRPO. • SWE-bench-verified: the frozen AHE harness achieved top aggregate success with 12% fewer tokens than the seed harness. • Cross-family transfer on Terminal-Bench 2: +5.1 to +10.1 percentage point gains across three alternate model families, demonstrating that evolved components encode general engineering experience rather than benchmark-specific tuning.
🏁 结论 AHE证明了可观测性驱动的自主框架演化是提升编程智能体的切实可行的通用路径，所产生的框架在性能上超越人工设计和自演化基线，并能跨模型族有效迁移。三支柱框架将不透明的试错过程转化为有原则、可证伪的持续改进循环。 AHE demonstrates that observability-driven autonomous harness evolution is a practical and general pathway for improving coding agents, producing harnesses that outperform both human-designed and self-evolving baselines while transferring effectively across model families. The three-pillar framework converts opaque trial-and-error into a principled, falsifiable improvement loop that keeps harnesses continually improving.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AHE（智能体测试框架工程）为框架工程循环的三个自然阶段分别配备了对应的可观测性支柱。第一，组件可观测性为框架中每个可编辑部分（提示词、工具、环境配置）赋予清晰的文件级身份，使编辑可追踪、可回滚。第二，经验可观测性将长智能体轨迹处理成分层证据语料库，提炼出模式、失败和成功案例，避免演化智能体被海量原始token淹没。第三，决策可观测性要求演化智能体在每次编辑前写下预测（如"此更改应提升文件编辑任务的工具使用成功率"），然后自动用下一轮结果验证该预测。三个支柱共同保障系统能够自主运行演化迭代，而不会退化为随机搜索。 AHE (Agentic Harness Engineering) instruments the three natural stages of any harness engineering loop with matched observability pillars. First, component observability gives every editable piece of the harness (prompts, tools, environment configs) a clear file-level identity so edits are trackable and revertible. Second, experience observability processes long agent trajectories into a layered evidence corpus — summarizing patterns, failures, and successes — so the evolving agent isn't overwhelmed by raw token volume. Third, decision observability requires the evolving agent to write down a prediction before each edit (e.g., "this change should improve tool-use success on file-editing tasks"), then automatically checks that prediction against next-round outcomes. These three pillars together allow the system to run autonomous evolution iterations without collapsing into random search.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{pass@1}_{\text{AHE}} = 77.0\% \quad (\uparrow 7.3\text{pp from seed } 69.7\%)\]

❓ 待解决
的问题		 标准Transformer虽然并行处理token，但在每个位置上计算深度受层数限制，"时间深度"有天花板。循环模型虽然理论上深度无限，却面临优化不稳定、难以充分利用现代加速器的问题。目前缺乏一种能兼顾两者优点的干净架构。 Standard Transformers process tokens in parallel, but at each position the "depth" of computation is capped by the number of layers — they are temporally shallow. Recurrent models can achieve unbounded temporal depth but are notoriously hard to optimize and poorly suited to modern GPU/TPU accelerators. There is no existing architecture that cleanly combines the strengths of both worlds.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **层级循环注意力**：每层对自身激活值计算的KV对做注意力（而非下一层的），形成每层独立的循环记忆，同时保持标准自回归解码的计算成本不变。 • **通用性强**：在温和假设下，新架构可模拟标准Transformer和token间RNN更新，兼具两类模型的表达能力。 • **分块算法降低内存带宽压力**：提出精确分块算法，将预填充/训练阶段的HBM访问量从Θ(N²)降至Θ(N log N)，有效算术强度从约1提升至Θ(N/log N)。 • **Layer-wise recurrent attention**: Each layer attends to key-value pairs derived from its *own* activations (not the layer below), creating a recurrent memory loop per layer while keeping autoregressive decoding cost identical to a standard Transformer. • **Universality**: The architecture can emulate both a standard Transformer and token-to-token RNN updates under mild assumptions, combining expressiveness of both paradigms. • **Tiling algorithm for efficient prefill**: A novel exact tiling algorithm reduces HBM (high-bandwidth memory) traffic during prefill/training from Θ(N²) to Θ(N log N), boosting effective arithmetic intensity from ~1 to Θ(N/log N).

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

核心改动简洁：标准Transformer中每层对"上一层"输出的KV做注意力；而循环Transformer改为每层对"自身"在前面token位置的激活值计算KV再做注意力，形成层级循环。解码时每个新token的前向计算成本与标准Transformer完全相同。训练和预填充阶段，这种循环依赖本会导致顺序计算瓶颈（算术强度约为1），作者为此推导出一种精确分块算法，在不改变数学计算结果的前提下重排计算顺序，将HBM访问量从Θ(N²)降至Θ(N log N)，使加速器能高效并行执行。 The key architectural change is simple: instead of each Transformer layer attending to key-value (KV) pairs produced by the *previous* layer, the Recurrent Transformer has each layer attend to KV pairs computed from *its own* activations at previous token positions. This creates a layerwise recurrent loop — the layer's output at token t depends on its own prior outputs — while the forward pass for decoding a new token remains identical in cost to a standard Transformer. To handle the sequential dependency that this recurrence introduces during training and prefill (which would otherwise serialize computation and create a bandwidth bottleneck with arithmetic intensity ≈ 1), the authors derive an exact tiling-based algorithm that reorders the computation. This reduces HBM memory traffic from Θ(N²) to Θ(N log N) for sequence length N, enabling efficient parallel execution on accelerators.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{HBM traffic: } \Theta(N^2) \rightarrow \Theta(N \log N)\]
💡 分块算法将预填充/训练阶段的高带宽内存（HBM）访问量从平方级降至准线性级，序列长度为N时效果显著。 The tiling algorithm reduces high-bandwidth memory (HBM) traffic during prefill/training from quadratic to near-linear in sequence length N.
\[\text{Arithmetic Intensity: } \sim 1 \rightarrow \Theta\left(\frac{N}{\log N}\right)\]
💡 有效算术强度（计算量与内存访问量之比）从约1大幅提升至Θ(N/log N)，意味着对于长序列，硬件计算单元被更充分地利用。 Effective arithmetic intensity (ratio of compute to memory access) grows from ~1 to Θ(N/log N), meaning compute hardware is far better utilized for long sequences.

本文直接针对大语言模型部署中最棘手的两大痛点——KV缓存内存消耗和推理延迟——提供了通过循环结构以宽换深的原则性解决方案。配套的分块算法是重要的系统级贡献，使架构对加速器友好，有望影响下一代LLM架构设计方向。 This work directly addresses two of the most pressing pain points in deploying large language models: KV cache memory cost and inference latency, by showing that recurrence can replace depth with width in a principled way. The tiling algorithm is a practically significant systems contribution that makes the architecture accelerator-friendly, potentially influencing next-generation LLM architecture design.
