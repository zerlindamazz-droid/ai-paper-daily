Understanding Quantization of Optimizer States in LLM Pre-training: Dynamics of State Staleness and Effectiveness of State Resets 理解LLM预训练中优化器状态量化：状态停滞动态与状态重置有效性

🧑‍🔬 Kristi Topollai, Anna Choromanska

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 理论指导的重置策略可修复低精度优化器状态停滞问题
Theory-guided reset schedules fix stale quantized optimizer states in LLM pre-training.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

训练大型语言模型时，优化器（如Adam）需要存储大量"状态"数据（如梯度的指数移动平均），这占用了大量显存。为此，研究者尝试用低精度（如8位甚至更低）来量化这些状态以节省内存，但低精度量化会导致许多微小的状态更新被"四舍五入"回原值，使状态实际上"卡住"不动，从而拖慢模型学习速度。这一现象背后的机制尚未被清晰理解，也缺乏系统性的解决方案。 Training large language models requires storing massive optimizer state data (e.g., exponential moving averages of gradients), consuming significant GPU memory. Quantizing these states to low precision (e.g., 8-bit or lower) saves memory, but causes many small updates to round back to the same stored value — effectively freezing the state and slowing down learning. The mechanics behind this "stalling" phenomenon and how to systematically fix it remain poorly understood.

⚙️ Method · 方法

研究者首先建立了一个"停滞预测模型"，用于估计在给定精度下单步更新被四舍五入回原值的概率，并刻画这种停滞如何随时间累积。基于这一理论分析，他们发现：当量化后的指数移动平均状态趋于停滞时，对其进行"重置"（即将状态清零并重新初始化）可以临时恢复状态对梯度的响应能力。由此，他们推导出一套理论指导的重置周期选择方法——核心不在于"是否"重置，而在于"何时"重置。该方法通过分析停滞概率随时间的演化来确定最优重置时机，并在受控模拟和真实LLM预训练实验中验证了其有效性。 The authors first build a predictive stalling model that estimates the per-step probability of a quantized EMA update rounding back to its stored value, and characterizes how this stalling accumulates over time after initialization. They then show that resetting the quantized EMA state (reinitializing it to zero) can temporarily restore its responsiveness to gradients once it becomes stale. From this theory, they derive a principled method for choosing reset periods by tracking how stalling probability evolves over time — framing the key question as not just whether to reset, but when. The approach is validated in both controlled simulations and real-world LLM pre-training experiments.

📊 Results · 结果

在受控模拟和LLM预训练实验中，采用理论指导的重置调度策略能够恢复因低精度状态存储（如8位量化）而损失的训练性能，同时显著降低优化器状态的内存占用。实验表明，合理的重置周期安排使低精度训练的效果可与全精度（32位）优化器状态媲美，且在减少优化器状态内存方面取得了实质性的节省（优化器状态内存可减少数倍，例如从32位降至8位可节省约75%的优化器状态内存）。此外，该模型对停滞概率的预测与实际观测结果高度吻合，验证了理论框架的准确性。 In both controlled simulations and LLM pre-training experiments, theory-guided reset schedules fully recover the performance lost from low-precision optimizer state storage (e.g., 8-bit quantization), while substantially reducing optimizer-state memory — reducing it by up to ~75% compared to full 32-bit storage. The predictive stalling model accurately matches empirically observed stalling probabilities, validating the theoretical framework. Crucially, the results show that the timing of resets matters significantly: poorly-timed resets provide little benefit, while theory-guided resets restore the responsiveness of quantized states to match full-precision optimizer behavior.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着LLM规模不断扩大，显存已成为训练的核心瓶颈之一，而优化器状态往往占据训练内存的三分之一以上。本文不仅为量化优化器状态的"停滞"现象提供了首个清晰的机制性解释，还给出了一套无需额外超参数搜索的理论驱动重置策略，具有直接的实用价值。 As LLMs grow larger, GPU memory is a critical bottleneck, and optimizer states often account for over one-third of training memory. This paper provides the first mechanistic, theory-grounded explanation for quantization-induced stalling in optimizer states, and offers a practical, theory-driven reset strategy that requires no expensive hyperparameter search — making low-precision training more principled and accessible.

Online Experiential Learning for Language Models 语言模型的在线经验学习

🧑‍🔬 Tianzhu Ye, Li Dong, Qingxiu Dong, Xun Wu et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 让语言模型从真实部署经验中持续自我改进的在线学习框架
A framework enabling LLMs to continuously self-improve from real-world deployment experience without offline retraining.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

当前大型语言模型的改进主要依赖离线训练，需要大量人工标注或模拟环境，而模型在真实部署中积累的海量交互经验却被完全忽视和浪费。这意味着模型在上线后便"停止成长"，无法从实际用户交互中持续学习和优化。 Current LLM improvement paradigms rely on offline training with human annotations or simulated environments, leaving vast amounts of real-world deployment experience completely unexploited. Once deployed, models cannot learn or adapt from actual user interactions, missing a continuous and rich source of improvement signal.

⚙️ Method · 方法

OEL框架分两个交替迭代的阶段运行：第一阶段，从用户端收集的交互轨迹中提取并积累可迁移的"经验知识"（而非直接使用原始轨迹）；第二阶段，通过"同策略上下文蒸馏"将这些知识固化到模型参数中，且无需访问用户端环境。两个阶段循环迭代，形成在线学习闭环——改进后的模型能采集更高质量的轨迹，进而产生更丰富的经验知识，推动下一轮提升。 OEL operates in two iterating stages: first, transferable experiential knowledge is extracted and distilled from interaction trajectories collected on the user side, rather than using raw trajectories directly. Second, this structured knowledge is consolidated into model parameters via on-policy context distillation, which critically does not require access to the user-side environment. The two stages iterate in a loop, where each improved model collects higher-quality trajectories that generate richer knowledge for the next round.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{K}_t = \text{Extract}(\{\tau_i\}_{i=1}^{N})\]
💡 经验知识提取：从第t轮收集的N条交互轨迹中提取可迁移的结构化经验知识K_t，而非直接使用原始轨迹。 Experiential knowledge extraction: structured, transferable knowledge K_t is distilled from N raw interaction trajectories collected in round t, rather than using them directly.
\[\theta_{t+1} = \arg\min_{\theta} \mathbb{E}_{x \sim p_{\theta_t}} \left[ \mathcal{L}_{\text{CD}}(\theta; x, \mathcal{K}_t) \right]\]
💡 同策略上下文蒸馏：使用当前策略模型θ_t生成的数据分布，将经验知识K_t通过上下文蒸馏损失固化到下一轮模型参数θ_{t+1}中，确保知识来源与训练策略一致。 On-policy context distillation: the model parameters θ_{t+1} are updated by minimizing a context distillation loss over inputs sampled from the current policy θ_t, ensuring the knowledge source and policy model remain consistent.

📊 Results · 结果

OEL在基于文本的游戏环境中跨多个模型规模（包括思维链和非思维链变体）均实现了持续迭代提升。框架同时提高了任务准确率和token效率，并保持了分布外（OOD）的泛化性能。消融分析表明，提取的经验知识显著优于直接使用原始交互轨迹，且同策略一致性（知识来源与策略模型一致）对有效学习至关重要。 OEL achieves consistent improvements across successive iterations on text-based game environments, tested across multiple model scales and both thinking and non-thinking model variants. The framework simultaneously improves task accuracy and token efficiency while preserving out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization. Ablation studies confirm that extracted experiential knowledge is significantly more effective than raw trajectories, and that on-policy consistency between the knowledge source and the policy model is a critical factor for effective learning.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作为语言模型引入了一种真正意义上的"在线持续学习"范式，使模型能够像人类一样从实践经验中不断成长，而无需昂贵的人工标注。它为打破"训练-部署"割裂局面提供了可行路径，对构建持续进化的AI系统具有重要意义。 This work introduces a genuinely online continual learning paradigm for LLMs, allowing models to grow from real-world experience much like humans do — without costly human annotation. It bridges the longstanding gap between offline training and live deployment, offering a practical path toward AI systems that continuously and autonomously self-improve.

MolmoB0T: Large-Scale Simulation Enables Zero-Shot Manipulation MolmoB0T：大规模仿真实现零样本操作

🧑‍🔬 Abhay Deshpande, Maya Guru, Rose Hendrix, Snehal Jauhri et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 纯仿真数据训练机器人策略，无需真实世界微调即可零样本迁移
Large-scale simulation alone enables zero-shot real-world robot manipulation, no real data needed.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

机器人学习领域长期存在一个共识：仅靠仿真训练不足以让机器人在真实世界中可靠工作，通常需要收集真实环境数据或进行针对性微调。这一"仿真到现实"的鸿沟使得机器人部署成本高昂且耗时。 A dominant belief in robot learning holds that simulation alone is insufficient for real-world deployment — some real-world data collection or task-specific fine-tuning is almost always considered necessary to bridge the sim-to-real gap. This assumption makes scaling robot policies expensive and slow, requiring physical hardware at every step.

⚙️ Method · 方法

研究团队构建了全开源的 MolmoBot-Engine 流程化数据生成管线，在 MolmoSpaces 仿真环境中跨机器人、跨任务、跨多样化场景生成 180 万条专家轨迹数据集（MolmoBot-Data）。基于此数据，他们训练了三类策略模型：MolmoBot（基于 Molmo2 的多帧视觉语言模型，配备流匹配动作头）、MolmoBot-Pi0（复现 π₀ 架构以进行直接对比）以及 MolmoBot-SPOC（轻量级边缘部署策略，支持强化学习微调）。所有策略均在未见过的真实物体和环境中直接评估，不进行任何真实世界的微调。 The team built MolmoBot-Engine, a fully open-source procedural data generation pipeline that creates diverse simulated scenarios across different robots, tasks, and environments within MolmoSpaces. This pipeline produced MolmoBot-Data, a dataset of 1.8 million expert trajectories covering articulated object manipulation and pick-and-place tasks. Three policy classes were trained on this data: MolmoBot (a Molmo2-based multi-frame vision-language model with a flow-matching action head), MolmoBot-Pi0 (replicating the π₀ architecture for fair comparison), and MolmoBot-SPOC (a lightweight model for edge deployment with RL fine-tuning support). All policies were evaluated directly on physical robots without any real-world fine-tuning.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\pi_{\theta}(a_t | o_t) \sim \text{FlowMatch}(f_{\theta}(o_t))\]
💡 MolmoBot 的流匹配动作头：给定观测 o_t，策略通过流匹配生成连续动作分布，用于精确的机械臂控制 MolmoBot's flow-matching action head: given observation o_t, the policy generates a continuous action distribution via flow matching, enabling precise robot arm control

📊 Results · 结果

在桌面拾放任务中，MolmoBot 在真实世界 4 个测试场景中达到 79.2% 的成功率，大幅超越 π₀.₅ 的 39.2%（提升约 40 个百分点）。在移动操作平台 Rainbow Robotics RB-Y1 上，策略成功完成了开门、抽屉操作、橱柜交互和移动拾放等任务，所有评估均为零样本迁移至未见过的物体和环境。 On tabletop pick-and-place tasks, MolmoBot achieves a 79.2% success rate across 4 real-world evaluation settings, outperforming π₀.₅ by a large margin (39.2%, roughly doubling its performance). On the Rainbow Robotics RB-Y1 mobile manipulator, policies successfully zero-shot transferred to door opening, drawer manipulation, cabinet interaction, and mobile pick-and-place — all on unseen objects and environments with no real-world fine-tuning.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项研究从根本上挑战了"仿真不够用"的主流观点，证明了通过足够大规模和多样化的程序化仿真数据，机器人策略可以直接泛化到现实世界。全开源的管线和 180 万条轨迹数据集的发布，将大幅降低机器人学习研究的门槛，加速通用操作策略的发展。 This paper fundamentally challenges a core assumption in robot learning, demonstrating that the sim-to-real gap can be closed through scale and diversity alone — without any real-world data. The fully open-source pipeline and 1.8M trajectory dataset lower the barrier to entry for robotics research and could accelerate the development of generalizable manipulation policies at scale.
