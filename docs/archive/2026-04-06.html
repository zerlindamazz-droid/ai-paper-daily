Steerable Visual Representations 可引导的视觉表征

🧑‍🔬 Jona Ruthardt, Manu Gaur, Deva Ramanan, Makarand Tapaswi et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 用自然语言动态引导视觉Transformer表征聚焦任意概念
Steer pretrained ViT features toward any concept using natural language via early text-visual fusion.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 DINOv2、MAE等预训练视觉Transformer能提取强大的通用图像特征，但它们天然聚焦于图像中最显眼的区域，无法被引导去关注用户真正感兴趣但不那么突出的概念。多模态大语言模型虽然可以用文字引导，但其表征过于以语言为中心，在纯视觉任务上效果下降。目前缺乏一种既能用自然语言引导、又不损害通用视觉表征质量的方法。 Pretrained Vision Transformers like DINOv2 and MAE produce powerful general-purpose image features, but they automatically focus on the most visually dominant regions, with no mechanism to redirect attention to less salient but user-relevant concepts. Multimodal LLMs can accept text guidance, but their representations are language-biased and degrade performance on purely visual tasks. There is no existing method that lets users steer generic visual features with natural language without sacrificing their general-purpose quality.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 早期融合跨注意力机制：通过轻量级跨注意力模块将文本直接注入冻结ViT编码器的中间层，而非像CLIP那样在编码后融合（晚期融合），让语言在特征层面重塑视觉表征。 • 无需重训主干网络：ViT主干保持冻结，只训练小型跨注意力适配器，参数高效且保留通用视觉能力。 • 引入可引导性评测基准：提出专用基准定量衡量表征被引导至目标概念的能力，填补了评估生态的空白。 • Early Fusion via Cross-Attention: Text is injected directly into intermediate layers of a frozen ViT encoder using lightweight cross-attention modules, rather than fused after encoding (late fusion like CLIP). This allows language to reshape visual features at the representational level. • Steerability Without Retraining: The base ViT backbone remains frozen; only small cross-attention adapters are trained, making the approach parameter-efficient and preserving the backbone's general-purpose quality. • New Steerability Benchmarks: The authors introduce dedicated benchmarks to quantitatively measure how well representations can be steered toward target concepts, filling a gap in the evaluation ecosystem.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在自行提出的可引导性基准以及图像检索、语义分割、异常检测和个性化物体判别等下游任务上进行评估。基线包括DINOv2、MAE、CLIP及多模态大语言模型。实验涵盖域内和域外（零样本）场景以评估泛化能力，评估指标包括检索准确率、分割质量（mIoU）、异常检测分数（AUROC）以及新基准上的可引导性专项指标。 The authors evaluate on steerability benchmarks they introduce themselves, as well as downstream tasks including image retrieval, semantic segmentation, anomaly detection, and personalized object discrimination. Baselines include DINOv2, MAE, CLIP, and multimodal LLMs. Experiments cover both in-distribution and out-of-distribution (zero-shot) settings to assess generalization. Evaluation metrics include retrieval accuracy, segmentation quality (mIoU), anomaly detection scores (AUROC), and steerability-specific metrics on the new benchmarks.
📊 实验
结果		 在提出的可引导性基准上，可引导视觉表征显著优于DINOv2和CLIP基线，能更精准地将注意力引导至目标概念。在异常检测任务上，方法媲美甚至超越专用模型。在个性化物体判别零样本场景中超越了先前方法。通用表征质量（检索、分割等）保持与冻结DINOv2骨干网络相当的水平，未出现明显退化。异常检测AUROC分数和可引导性专项指标均有具体数值提升，分割任务mIoU与原始DINOv2持平。 On the introduced steerability benchmarks, Steerable Visual Representations significantly outperform DINOv2 and CLIP baselines in directing attention to target concepts. On anomaly detection, the method matches or surpasses dedicated specialist models. On personalized object discrimination tasks, it outperforms prior approaches in zero-shot settings. General-purpose representation quality (e.g., retrieval and segmentation) is preserved at levels comparable to the frozen DINOv2 backbone, demonstrating no significant degradation from the steering mechanism. Specific numerical improvements are reported on steerability metrics and AUROC scores for anomaly detection benchmarks, with the method achieving competitive mIoU on segmentation tasks relative to unmodified DINOv2.
🏁 结论 本文证明，通过轻量级早期融合跨注意力适配器，预训练ViT的视觉表征可以被自然语言动态引导至用户指定的任意概念，同时不损害通用视觉质量。论文同时引入了框架和评测基准，开创了可控、任务自适应视觉表征的新范式。 This paper proves that visual representations from pretrained ViTs can be dynamically steered toward arbitrary user-specified concepts via natural language, using lightweight early-fusion cross-attention adapters, without sacrificing general-purpose visual quality. The work introduces both the framework and evaluation benchmarks, establishing a new paradigm for controllable, task-adaptive visual representations.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

该方法在冻结的预训练ViT（如DINOv2）的多个中间Transformer块中插入轻量级跨注意力层。用户输入的文本提示经文本编码器编码后，作为键（K）和值（V）注入跨注意力模块，视觉patch token作为查询（Q）。文本在每一层调制视觉特征，使全局（CLS token）和局部（patch token）表征都朝向目标概念偏移。仅训练跨注意力适配器，其余参数冻结，保留通用视觉质量。 The method adds lightweight cross-attention layers at multiple intermediate transformer blocks of a pretrained, frozen ViT (e.g., DINOv2). A text prompt describing the concept of interest is encoded by a text encoder and injected as keys and values into these cross-attention modules, while the visual patch tokens serve as queries. This allows the text to modulate visual features at each layer, steering both global (CLS token) and local (patch token) representations toward the described concept. Only the cross-attention adapters are trained, keeping the rest of the model frozen to preserve general-purpose visual quality.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Attn}(Q, K, V) = \text{softmax}\left(\frac{QK^\top}{\sqrt{d_k}}\right)V, \quad Q = f_{\text{vis}}(x),\ K=V=f_{\text{txt}}(t)\]
💡 跨注意力机制：视觉patch特征作为查询Q，文本编码作为键K和值V，通过注意力权重将文本信息注入视觉表征，实现早期文本-视觉融合。 Cross-attention mechanism where visual patch features act as queries (Q) and text encodings serve as keys (K) and values (V), injecting textual guidance directly into the visual feature space at intermediate layers.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

大多数视觉模型是静态的——无论下游用户关注什么，它们对图像的编码方式一成不变，导致需要针对任务进行微调或接受次优特征。可引导表征提供了一种灵活的中间路径：单个预训练模型的特征可以通过文字提示即时重定向，为检索、检测等任务解锁更高效、更具泛化性的流水线。 Most vision models are static — they encode images the same way regardless of what the downstream user cares about, forcing task-specific fine-tuning or accepting suboptimal features. Steerable representations offer a flexible middle ground: a single pretrained model whose features can be redirected on-the-fly with a text prompt, unlocking more efficient and generalizable pipelines for retrieval, detection, and beyond.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

SimpleStream采用滑窗策略：每个时刻只将最近N帧输入预训练的视觉-语言模型（VLM），无需任何记忆模块、压缩或检索机制。该方法不需要微调，可直接与现成VLM配合使用。作者还通过对帧数N和骨干模型的控制变量实验，分析上下文长度和模型类型对性能的影响。 SimpleStream uses a sliding-window strategy: at each time step, only the most recent N frames are passed to a pre-trained Vision-Language Model (VLM) with no additional memory, compression, or retrieval modules. The method requires no fine-tuning and works directly with off-the-shelf VLMs. The authors also run controlled ablations varying N (number of frames) and the backbone model to analyze how context length and model type affect performance.

The Compression Gap: Why Discrete Tokenization Limits Vision-Language-Action Model Scaling 压缩差距：为什么离散化分词限制了视觉-语言-动作模型的扩展

🧑‍🔬 Takuya Shiba

图 Figure · Page 1 of paper

🎯 离散动作分词造成信息瓶颈，使视觉编码器的升级对VLA模型失效。
Discrete action tokenization creates an information bottleneck that blocks vision encoder scaling gains in VLA models.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 在视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型中，研究者原本期望通过升级视觉编码器来提升机器人操作性能——就像在视觉语言任务中那样有效。但当动作被表示为离散token时，这种扩展收益神秘地消失了。此前没有人能从原理上解释，为什么更强的编码器无法带来更好的机器人表现。 Researchers expected that upgrading vision encoders in Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models would improve robot manipulation performance, just as it does in vision-language tasks. However, this scaling benefit mysteriously fails to materialize when actions are represented as discrete tokens. No one had a principled explanation for why encoder improvements don't translate to better robot performance in these settings.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **压缩差距原理**：提出信息论框架，指出流水线中最紧的瓶颈决定整体的扩展行为——为物理AI提供了全新且简洁的理论视角。 • **锁定码本为根源**：当动作通过固定容量码本（如OAT）离散化时，码本成为制约瓶颈，阻止更丰富的视觉表示改善下游性能。 • **因果验证**：通过码本大小消融实验作为"手术刀"式干预——扩大码本可部分恢复编码器的敏感性，提供了因果证据而非仅相关性证据。 • **Compression Gap principle**: Introduces an information-theoretic framework showing that the tightest bottleneck in a pipeline governs overall scaling behavior — a novel and clean theoretical lens for Physical AI. • **Identifies the codebook as the culprit**: When actions are discretized via a fixed-capacity codebook (e.g., OAT), it becomes the binding constraint that prevents richer visual representations from helping downstream performance. • **Causal validation**: Uses a codebook size ablation as a "surgical" intervention — enlarging the codebook partially restores encoder sensitivity, providing causal (not just correlational) evidence for the bottleneck hypothesis.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在LIBERO机器人操作评测基准上进行。研究对比了两种动作表示：扩散策略（连续）和OAT（离散分词）。在多个模型规模下测试了四种质量各异的视觉编码器，构成因子实验设计。同时进行码本大小消融实验，通过改变离散码的数量来探测编码器敏感性何时恢复。基线为弱/强编码器与两种动作表示类型的组合。 Experiments are conducted on the LIBERO benchmark, a standard robot manipulation evaluation suite. The study compares two action representations: Diffusion Policy (continuous) and OAT (discrete tokenization). Four vision encoders of varying quality are tested across multiple model scales, forming a factorial design. A codebook size ablation is also run, varying the number of discrete codes to probe when encoder sensitivity is restored. Baselines include combinations of weak and strong encoders crossed with both action representation types.
📊 实验
结果		 在LIBERO基准上，升级视觉编码器使扩散策略提升超过**21个百分点**，而OAT在所有模型规模下的收益均被大幅削弱。在按质量排序的四个编码器上绘制性能曲线时，扩散策略**单调地**跟随编码器质量提升，而OAT则**几乎保持平坦**。码本大小实验显示，扩大码本容量可**部分恢复**OAT对编码器质量的敏感性，为瓶颈假说提供了直接因果证据。 On LIBERO, upgrading the vision encoder improves Diffusion Policy by over **21 percentage points**, while OAT gains are substantially attenuated across all model scales. When plotting performance across four encoders ranked by quality, Diffusion Policy tracks encoder quality **monotonically**, while OAT remains **flat** regardless of encoder strength. The codebook size experiment shows that enlarging codebook capacity **partially recovers** OAT's sensitivity to encoder quality, providing direct causal support for the bottleneck hypothesis.
🏁 结论 本文证明离散动作分词会产生根本性的信息瓶颈——"压缩差距"——使视觉编码器的扩展无法改善VLA模型性能；物理AI中的扩展需要先定位瓶颈所在，而非盲目增大模型或数据规模。信息论框架为未来机器人学习系统的设计提供了有原则的诊断工具。 This paper proves that discrete action tokenization creates a fundamental information bottleneck — the "Compression Gap" — that prevents scaling vision encoders from improving VLA model performance, and that scaling in Physical AI requires diagnosing where bottlenecks lie rather than blindly increasing model or data size. The information-theoretic framing offers a principled diagnostic tool for designing future robot learning systems.

链接		 arXiv: 2604.03191v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文提出"压缩差距"这一信息论原理：在任何顺序流水线中，信息容量最小的组件成为制约瓶颈，其上游的改进无法传递下去。对于使用连续动作头的VLA模型（如扩散策略），视觉编码器是瓶颈，升级它可直接提升性能。对于使用离散动作分词的模型（如OAT），固定容量码本是瓶颈，无论编码器多强，信息在码本阶段都会丢失，无法传递到最终动作输出。作者通过系统性地改变编码器质量和码本容量的因子实验来验证这一原理。 The paper introduces the "Compression Gap" as an information-theoretic principle: in any sequential pipeline, the component with the smallest information capacity acts as the binding constraint, and improvements upstream of it cannot propagate. For VLA models with continuous action heads (Diffusion Policy), the vision encoder is the bottleneck; upgrading it directly improves performance. For models with discrete action tokenization (OAT), the fixed-capacity codebook is the bottleneck; no matter how good the encoder is, information is lost at the codebook stage and cannot reach the final action output. The authors validate this by systematically varying encoder quality and codebook capacity in controlled factorial experiments.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[I(X; Z) \leq \min_k C_k\]
💡 流水线中的信息传递量受限于各组件信息容量的最小值——即最紧的瓶颈决定整体上限。 The mutual information that can flow through a pipeline is bounded by the minimum capacity across all components — the tightest bottleneck sets the ceiling for the entire system.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

当机器人领域竞相借鉴大语言模型的扩展方法（更强编码器、更多数据）时，本文发出了重要警示：离散分词等架构选择可能悄然使扩展投入付诸东流。它将扩展问题从"扩展多少"重新定义为"在哪里扩展"，提供了一个诊断视角，有望节省巨大的计算资源并将研究引向更有价值的设计决策。 As the robotics community races to scale VLA models by borrowing recipes from LLMs (bigger encoders, more data), this paper issues a critical warning: architectural choices like discrete tokenization can silently nullify scaling investments. It reframes the scaling question from "how much" to "where," providing a diagnostic lens that could save enormous compute and redirect research toward more impactful design decisions.

Co-Evolution of Policy and Internal Reward for Language Agents 语言智能体的策略与内部奖励协同进化

🧑‍🔬 Xinyu Wang, Hanwei Wu, Jingwei Song, Shuyuan Zhang et al.

🎯 让语言智能体自主生成内部奖励，实现策略与奖励协同进化。
LLM agents learn to generate their own internal rewards, co-evolving policy and guidance simultaneously.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 在长程任务中训练语言智能体非常困难，因为奖励信号稀疏，往往只在任务结束时才出现。现有方法要么是事后进行信用分配，要么依赖独立的外部奖励模型，这些模型无法随策略一同改进，导致智能体在训练和推理阶段都缺乏有效引导。 Training LLM agents on long-horizon tasks is extremely difficult because rewards are sparse and often only arrive at the very end of a task. Existing solutions either assign credit after the fact or rely on separate reward models that don't improve alongside the policy, leaving agents poorly guided during both training and inference.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • Self-Guide是一种自生成的短文本信号，身兼双职：推理时引导下一步动作，训练时转化为逐步内部奖励——一个信号，两种用途。 • 协同进化循环是核心创新：更好的策略产生更高质量的引导信号，这些信号又提供更密集、更准确的内部奖励，进一步提升策略。 • 与外部奖励模型不同，Self-Guide无需额外模型和人工标注，作为智能体自身学习过程的一部分自动演化。 • Self-Guide is a self-generated short textual signal that serves a dual role: it steers the agent's next action at inference time AND becomes a step-level internal reward during training — one signal, two purposes. • The co-evolution loop is the core novelty: a better policy produces higher-quality guidance signals, which in turn provide denser and more accurate internal rewards that further improve the policy. • Unlike external reward models, Self-Guide requires no additional model, no human annotation, and evolves automatically as part of the agent's own learning process.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

智能体在每个动作步骤之前生成一个简短的Self-Guide信号，预测当前状态下良好的下一步动作应该是什么。在推理时，该信号直接作为条件引导动作选择，充当轻量级的前瞻提示。在训练时，每个Self-Guide信号的质量根据环境结果进行评估，并转化为密集的逐步奖励，替代或补充稀疏的环境奖励。整个系统使用GRPO（组相对策略优化）进行端到端训练，使策略和内部奖励生成器在统一的循环中共同进步。 The agent generates a short Self-Guide signal before each action step, predicting what a good next move looks like given the current state. At inference time, this signal directly conditions the agent's action selection, acting as a lightweight look-ahead hint. At training time, the quality of each Self-Guide signal is evaluated against environment outcomes and converted into a dense step-level reward, replacing or augmenting the sparse environment reward. The entire system is trained end-to-end using GRPO (Group Relative Policy Optimization), so the policy and the internal reward generator improve together in a single unified loop.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r_{\text{internal}}^t = f(\text{Self-Guide}_t, o_{t+1}, \ldots, o_T)\]
💡 第t步的内部奖励由当前生成的Self-Guide信号与后续环境观测共同决定，将自生成的引导信号转化为密集的逐步训练奖励。 The internal reward at step t is derived from the Self-Guide signal and subsequent environment observations, converting the self-generated guidance into a dense step-level training reward.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{GRPO}} = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t \min\left(\frac{\pi_\theta(a_t|s_t)}{\pi_{\text{old}}(a_t|s_t)} A_t,\ \text{clip}(\cdot) A_t\right)\right]\]
💡 GRPO目标函数使用组相对优势估计对策略进行优化，Self-Guide产生的内部奖励用于计算优势值A_t，实现密集的策略更新。 The GRPO objective optimizes the policy using group-relative advantage estimates; the internal reward from Self-Guide is used to compute the advantage A_t, enabling dense policy updates.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

稀疏奖励是训练能够胜任真实世界长程任务的语言智能体最根本的瓶颈之一，Self-Guide提供了一种优雅的自包含解决方案，随智能体本身一同扩展。通过将推理时引导和训练时监督统一为单一的协同进化信号，本工作为无需昂贵人类反馈或独立奖励模型的持续自我改进智能体指明了方向。 Sparse rewards are one of the most fundamental bottlenecks in training capable LLM agents for real-world, long-horizon tasks — Self-Guide offers an elegant, self-contained solution that scales with the agent itself. By unifying inference-time guidance and training-time supervision into a single co-evolving signal, this work points toward LLM agents that continuously self-improve without relying on costly human feedback or separate reward models.
