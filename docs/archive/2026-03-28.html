Revisiting On-Policy Distillation: Empirical Failure Modes and Simple Fixes 重访在线策略蒸馏：实证失败模式与简单修复方案

🧑‍🔬 Yuqian Fu, Haohuan Huang, Kaiwen Jiang, Yuanheng Zhu et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 修复在线蒸馏三大失效模式，实现更稳定的LLM后训练。
Simple fixes to on-policy LLM distillation eliminate three key failure modes for more stable training.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 在线策略蒸馏（OPD）是一种让强大的教师模型对学生模型自生成的输出进行评估和指导的训练方式，广泛用于大语言模型后训练。然而，最常用的"采样token"变体在数学推理、智能体等长序列任务中十分脆弱——它只依赖单个token的信号，当学生生成的内容偏离教师熟悉的分布时，指导信号会变得噪声大且不可靠。这导致训练不稳定、最终性能较差。 On-policy distillation (OPD) is a popular method for training large language models by having a powerful teacher model evaluate a student model's own generated outputs. However, the most common variant—sampled-token OPD—is brittle in long-generation tasks like math reasoning or agentic tasks: it relies on a single-token signal that becomes noisy and unreliable when the student's outputs drift far from what the teacher normally sees. This fragility causes unstable training and weaker final performance.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 从理论角度分析了token级与序列级反向KL目标之间的偏差-方差权衡，证明采样token OPD方差更低但在长序列中累积偏差更大。 • 明确识别出三大失效模式：①单token信号不平衡；②学生生成的前缀超出教师分布导致指导信号不可靠；③分词器/特殊token不匹配造成信号扭曲。 • 提出简洁有效的修复方案：基于截断反向KL的教师Top-K局部支撑匹配，结合top-p采样与特殊token掩码，实现稳定高效的蒸馏训练。 • Theoretically characterizes the bias-variance tradeoff between token-level and sequence-level reverse-KL objectives, showing sampled-token OPD has lower variance but accumulating bias in long rollouts. • Identifies three concrete failure modes: (1) imbalanced one-token signal, (2) unreliable teacher guidance on out-of-distribution student prefixes, and (3) tokenizer/special-token mismatch distortions. • Proposes a practical fix—teacher top-K local support matching via truncated reverse-KL with top-p rollout sampling and special-token masking—that is simple to implement yet highly effective.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验涵盖两个场景：①单任务数学推理，在标准数学数据集上训练，并在MATH500、AMC、AIME等基准上评测；②多任务智能体+数学联合训练，同时评测数学能力与工具使用/智能体任务。基线包括标准采样token OPD及其他KL散度变体。此外还设计了玩具梯度方差实验以单独验证偏差-方差权衡，并对每个修复组件（Top-K匹配、top-p采样、特殊token掩码）进行了消融分析。 Experiments cover two settings: (1) single-task math reasoning, training on standard math datasets and evaluating on benchmarks like MATH500, AMC, and AIME; (2) multi-task agentic-plus-math training, evaluating on both math and tool-use/agent benchmarks. Baselines include standard sampled-token OPD and other KL-divergence variants. The study also includes toy gradient-variance experiments to isolate the bias-variance tradeoff, and ablations on each proposed fix (top-K matching, top-p sampling, special-token masking).
📊 实验
结果		 所提截断反向KL方法在所有基准上均优于采样token OPD。在单任务数学推理中，MATH500、AMC、AIME等基准均有数个百分点的提升。在多任务智能体+数学联合训练场景下，该方法训练曲线更稳定，数学和智能体任务的最终性能均优于标准OPD基线。消融实验表明三项修复均有正向贡献，其中特殊token掩码和Top-K匹配效果尤为显著。 The proposed truncated reverse-KL method consistently outperforms sampled-token OPD across all benchmarks. On single-task math reasoning, it achieves notable gains on MATH500, AMC, and AIME (specific deltas reported in the paper show several percentage-point improvements over baselines). In the multi-task agentic-plus-math setting, the method delivers more stable training curves and better final performance on both math and agent tasks compared to standard OPD. Ablation studies confirm each of the three fixes contributes positively, with special-token masking and top-K matching being particularly impactful.
🏁 结论 本文证明了主流采样token在线蒸馏方法存在三个系统性失效模式（信号不平衡、分布不匹配、分词器错位），并通过截断反向KL+top-p采样+特殊token掩码的简单组合有效修复了这些问题。该方法在数学推理和多任务智能体训练中均实现了更稳定的优化和更好的下游性能。 This paper proves that the dominant sampled-token on-policy distillation approach has three systematic failure modes rooted in signal imbalance, distribution mismatch, and tokenizer misalignment, and that a simple combination of truncated reverse-KL, top-p sampling, and special-token masking reliably fixes them. The proposed method achieves more stable optimization and better downstream performance across both math reasoning and agentic multi-task settings.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者首先从理论上证明采样token OPD是序列级反向KL目标的有偏估计量，但其最坏情况方差界更紧。随后通过受控玩具实验和真实LLM训练验证了三大失效模式。针对这些问题，他们提出用截断反向KL损失替换原有采样token目标——只对教师模型概率质量最高的Top-K个token进行分布匹配（局部支撑匹配），忽略教师非常不确定的token。同时配合top-p核采样（使学生输出更贴近教师分布）以及特殊token掩码（消除不同模型分词器带来的对齐噪声），形成完整的修复方案。 The authors first conduct a theoretical analysis showing that sampled-token OPD is a biased estimator of the sequence-level reverse-KL objective, but enjoys a tighter worst-case variance bound compared to full-sequence methods. They then empirically study three failure modes using controlled toy experiments and real LLM training runs. To fix these issues, they propose replacing the sampled-token objective with a truncated reverse-KL loss that only matches the teacher's top-K probability mass (local support matching), effectively ignoring tokens the teacher is very uncertain about. This is paired with top-p nucleus sampling during rollout generation (to keep student outputs closer to the teacher's distribution) and masking out special tokens that may not align across different model tokenizers.

This work provides both a principled theoretical framework and drop-in practical fixes, lowering the barrier for practitioners to adopt more reliable distillation pipelines.

ShotStream: Streaming Multi-Shot Video Generation for Interactive Storytelling ShotStream：面向交互式叙事的流式多镜头视频生成

🧑‍🔬 Yawen Luo, Xiaoyu Shi, Junhao Zhuang, Yutian Chen et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 因果流式架构实现16FPS多镜头视频实时生成与交互叙事
A causal streaming architecture generates coherent multi-shot videos at 16 FPS with sub-second latency for real-time interactive storytelling.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 长篇多镜头叙事视频生成（例如包含多个场景的短片）需要在镜头间保持人物和环境的一致性，同时还要快速响应用户的指令。现有最先进的系统采用双向注意力模型，必须一次性处理所有帧，导致延迟极高，用户无法在生成过程中动态调整剧情走向，实时交互式叙事几乎无法实现。 Generating long, multi-shot narrative videos (like a short film with multiple scenes) requires keeping characters and environments consistent across shots while responding quickly to user instructions. Current state-of-the-art systems use bidirectional attention models that must process all frames together, leading to high latency and no ability for users to steer the story mid-generation. This makes real-time, interactive storytelling practically impossible.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **因果式逐镜头生成**：将多镜头视频生成重构为自回归"下一镜头预测"任务，实现流式输出，并支持用户在生成过程中任意时刻注入动态提示词。 • **双缓存记忆机制**：全局上下文缓存保存跨镜头一致性所需的参考帧，局部上下文缓存维护镜头内时序连贯性；RoPE不连续指示器显式区分两类缓存，消除位置编码歧义。 • **两阶段自强迫蒸馏策略**：先在真实历史帧上训练（镜头内自强迫），再在自生成历史帧上训练（镜头间自强迫），渐进式弥合训练与推理的分布差距，有效抑制自回归误差累积。 • **Causal next-shot generation**: Reformulates multi-shot video generation as an autoregressive "next-shot prediction" task, enabling streaming output and real-time user prompt injection at any point during generation. • **Dual-cache memory mechanism**: A global context cache preserves reference frames for cross-shot consistency, while a local context cache maintains intra-shot temporal coherence; a RoPE discontinuity indicator explicitly separates the two caches to eliminate positional ambiguity. • **Two-stage self-forcing distillation**: Progressively bridges the train-test gap by first training on ground-truth history (intra-shot), then on self-generated history (inter-shot), robustly suppressing error accumulation in autoregressive rollouts.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多镜头视频生成基准上进行，将ShotStream与双向基线模型（包括教师模型及其他最先进的多镜头视频生成系统）进行比较。评估指标涵盖视觉质量（FVD、CLIP分数）、镜头间人物/场景一致性，以及实际吞吐量（FPS和延迟）。消融实验验证了各组件的有效性，包括双缓存设计、RoPE不连续指示器以及两阶段自强迫蒸馏策略。 Experiments were conducted on multi-shot video generation benchmarks, comparing ShotStream against bidirectional baselines including the teacher model and other state-of-the-art multi-shot video generation systems. Evaluation metrics included visual quality (FVD, CLIP score), inter-shot character/scene consistency, and real-world throughput (FPS and latency). Ablation studies validated each component: the dual-cache design, the RoPE discontinuity indicator, and both stages of the self-forcing distillation strategy.
📊 实验
结果		 ShotStream在单张GPU上实现每秒**16帧（16 FPS）**的生成速度，每镜头延迟低于1秒，远快于双向模型。在标准多镜头生成基准上，其视频质量与更慢的双向模型持平甚至更优，证明因果流式方案不以牺牲一致性和视觉保真度为代价。消融实验表明，双缓存机制和两阶段自强迫蒸馏均对最终质量有显著贡献。 ShotStream achieves **16 FPS** on a single GPU with **sub-second latency** per shot, compared to bidirectional models that are significantly slower. It matches or exceeds the video quality of slower bidirectional counterparts on standard multi-shot benchmarks, demonstrating that the causal streaming approach does not sacrifice coherence or visual fidelity. Ablation results confirm that both the dual-cache mechanism and the two-stage self-forcing distillation each contribute meaningfully to final quality scores.
🏁 结论 ShotStream证明了高质量、跨镜头一致的多镜头视频生成可在因果流式范式下实现，通过蒸馏与新颖的双缓存记忆设计，以极低延迟达到甚至超越双向模型的质量。本文为实时交互式AI驱动叙事应用奠定了坚实的实践基础。 ShotStream proves that high-quality, consistent multi-shot video generation can be achieved in a causal streaming paradigm, matching bidirectional model quality at a fraction of the latency through distillation and a novel dual-cache memory design. This work establishes a practical foundation for real-time, interactive AI-driven storytelling applications.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ShotStream首先将现有的文本生成视频扩散模型（基于Wan2.1）微调为以历史帧为条件的双向下一镜头生成器（教师模型）。随后通过分布匹配蒸馏（DMD）将教师模型蒸馏为因果（单向）学生模型，使其无需等待未来上下文即可流式生成帧。推理时，双缓存系统向模型同时提供长程参考帧（全局缓存）和近期局部帧（局部缓存），并通过特殊的RoPE位置编码标记告知模型各帧来自哪个缓存。两阶段自强迫蒸馏策略先用真实镜头历史训练学生模型，再逐步切换为使用模型自身生成的历史作为上下文，使模型在长自回归展开中对自身误差具有鲁棒性。 ShotStream starts by fine-tuning an existing text-to-video diffusion model (based on Wan2.1) into a bidirectional next-shot generator that conditions on historical frames. This teacher model is then distilled into a causal (unidirectional) student model using Distribution Matching Distillation (DMD), so the student can generate frames in a streaming, left-to-right fashion without waiting for future context. During inference, a dual-cache system feeds the model both long-range reference frames (global cache) and recent local frames (local cache), with a special RoPE positional encoding flag to tell the model which cache is which. The two-stage self-forcing distillation first trains the student using ground-truth shot histories, then gradually switches to using its own previously generated shots as context, making the model robust to its own imperfections during long autoregressive rollouts.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[p_{\text{student}}(x_{t+1} | x_{\leq t}) \approx p_{\text{teacher}}(x_{t+1} | x_{\leq t})\]
💡 分布匹配蒸馏的核心目标：训练因果学生模型，使其在给定历史帧条件下生成下一镜头的分布，尽可能接近双向教师模型的分布。 The core objective of Distribution Matching Distillation: train the causal student so that its distribution over the next shot, given all past frames, matches the bidirectional teacher's distribution.
\[\text{Cache} = \{\underbrace{f_1, f_k, \ldots}_{\text{global (reference frames)}},\ \underbrace{f_{t-w}, \ldots, f_t}_{\text{local (recent frames)}}\}\]
💡 双缓存记忆机制：模型的上下文由全局缓存（跨镜头参考帧，用于保持一致性）和局部缓存（当前镜头内的近期帧，用于镜头内连贯性）共同构成。 The dual-cache memory: the model's context consists of a global cache of reference frames (for cross-shot identity/scene consistency) and a local cache of recent frames within the current shot (for intra-shot temporal coherence).

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者修改了标准的RL微调目标函数（类似GRPO/PPO风格训练），使模型在一次生成中输出一组包含置信度分数的多个候选答案。奖励函数由三部分组成：(1) 正确答案的覆盖率——正确答案是否出现在生成集合中；(2) 候选答案之间的多样性；(3) 校准性——模型对每个答案的置信度是否与该答案实际正确的频率一致。通过该奖励信号训练，模型学会在内部完成假设枚举和不确定性估计，从而大幅减少测试时重复采样的需要。 The authors modify the standard RL fine-tuning objective (similar to GRPO/PPO-style training) so that the model is prompted to output a structured set of multiple candidate answers with associated confidence scores in one generation. The reward function is a composite that measures: (1) whether the correct answer appears in the generated set (coverage), (2) diversity among the generated candidates, and (3) calibration — whether the model's stated confidence for each answer aligns with how often that answer is actually correct. By training with this reward signal, the model learns to internalize hypothesis enumeration and uncertainty estimation, reducing the need for repeated sampling at test time.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{R}(A_{1:k}, q) = \lambda_1 \cdot \text{Coverage}(A_{1:k}, q) + \lambda_2 \cdot \text{Diversity}(A_{1:k}) + \lambda_3 \cdot \text{Calibration}(A_{1:k}, q)\]
💡 多答案RL的复合奖励函数，由三部分加权组成：覆盖率（正确答案是否出现在生成集中）、多样性（答案之间的差异程度）和校准性（置信度与实际正确率的一致性），各项权重为λ1、λ2、λ3。 The composite reward for the multi-answer RL objective, combining coverage (does the correct answer appear in the set?), diversity (how different are the answers from each other?), and calibration (do the stated confidence scores match empirical accuracy?), weighted by λ1, λ2, λ3.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

现实世界中绝大多数LLM应用需要处理不确定性和歧义，而不只是输出一个自信的答案——本研究提供了一种训练层面的解决方案，将分布推理直接内化到模型中。通过用单次多答案生成替代昂贵的推理时采样，为医疗等高风险领域的更安全、更可靠的LLM部署打开了大门。 Most real-world deployments of LLMs require handling uncertainty and ambiguity, not just producing a single confident answer — this work provides a training-time solution that bakes distributional reasoning directly into the model. By replacing expensive inference-time sampling with a single-pass multi-answer model, it opens the door to safer and more calibrated LLM deployment in high-stakes domains like medicine.
