Image Generators are Generalist Vision Learners 图像生成器是通用视觉学习器

🧑‍🔬 Valentin Gabeur, Shangbang Long, Songyou Peng, Paul Voigtlaender et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 图像生成预训练可赋能通用视觉理解，在2D和3D任务上达到SOTA
Image generation pretraining enables state-of-the-art generalist vision understanding across 2D and 3D tasks.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 构建能处理多种视觉任务（分割、深度估计等）的通用模型，通常需要针对每个任务设计专用架构或进行大规模有监督训练，代价高昂。长久以来，研究者猜测：能生成逼真图像的模型必然隐式地"理解"视觉内容——但这一假设缺乏有力的实验验证。图像生成作为视觉预训练范式（类比文本生成之于LLM）的可行性尚未被证实。 Building a single model that can handle diverse vision tasks (segmentation, depth estimation, etc.) typically requires task-specific architectures or large-scale supervised training per task. It has long been speculated that a model capable of generating realistic images must implicitly understand visual content — but this hypothesis lacked strong empirical proof. The field has not yet established image generation as a credible pretraining paradigm analogous to how text generation powers modern LLMs.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **统一输出空间**：将分割、深度估计等所有视觉感知任务统一表示为RGB图像输出，无需修改模型架构即可处理多种任务。 • **轻量指令微调**：在已有图像生成模型（Nano Banana Pro）基础上，仅用少量视觉任务数据进行指令微调，即可获得强大的理解能力，且不损失原始生成能力。 • **范式转变论证**：首次提供有力实验证据，表明图像生成预训练类似LLM中的文本生成预训练，能涌现出通用视觉理解能力，有望成为计算机视觉新基础范式。 • **Unified output space**: All vision tasks (segmentation, depth, etc.) are reframed as RGB image generation, allowing a single generative model to handle perception without architectural changes. • **Lightweight instruction-tuning**: Vision Banana is built by fine-tuning an existing image generator (Nano Banana Pro) on a small mixture of task data, preserving the base model's generative ability while gaining strong understanding capabilities. • **Paradigm-shift claim**: Provides empirical evidence that image generation pretraining, like LLM text generation pretraining, yields emergent generalist visual understanding — a potential new foundation for computer vision.
🧪 实验
内容		 本文在涵盖2D和3D理解的多个标准视觉基准上评估Vision Banana，包括语义分割、实例分割、度量深度估计和表面法线估计等任务。基线模型包括专为分割设计的Segment Anything Model 3（SAM3）和专为深度估计设计的Depth Anything系列。评估采用零样本协议，与未在目标数据集上微调的专家模型进行公平比较。 Vision Banana was evaluated on a diverse suite of vision benchmarks spanning both 2D and 3D understanding tasks, including semantic segmentation, instance segmentation, metric depth estimation, and surface normal estimation. Baselines included strong zero-shot domain specialists such as Segment Anything Model 3 (SAM3) for segmentation and the Depth Anything series for metric depth. The evaluation followed standard zero-shot protocols to fairly compare against specialist models that were not fine-tuned on the same target datasets.
📊 实验
结果		 Vision Banana在多个基准上取得SOTA成绩：在分割任务上击败或媲美SAM3，在度量深度估计上超越Depth Anything系列——且无需任务专属架构改动。实验表明，仅用少量任务数据进行轻量指令微调，即可达到甚至超越领域专家模型的性能水平，同时保留基础模型的完整图像生成能力。摘要中未披露具体数字，但论文报告在2D和3D理解基准上相对零样本专家模型均取得一致性SOTA表现。 Vision Banana achieves SOTA results across multiple benchmarks: it beats or rivals SAM3 on segmentation tasks and outperforms the Depth Anything series on metric depth estimation — all without task-specific architectural modifications. The model demonstrates that lightweight instruction-tuning (on a small amount of task data) is sufficient to reach specialist-level performance, while maintaining full image generation capability of the base model. Specific numerical results were not disclosed in the abstract but the paper reports consistent SOTA across both 2D and 3D understanding benchmarks against zero-shot specialists.
🏁 结论 本文证明了图像生成预训练能赋予模型强大且可迁移的视觉表征，通过轻量指令微调即可在多种视觉任务上达到通用SOTA水平。这确立了图像生成作为视觉统一接口的地位，其范式意义类比于文本生成在NLP领域的基础性作用。 This paper proves that image generation pretraining endows models with powerful, transferable visual representations that enable generalist SOTA performance across diverse vision tasks via lightweight instruction-tuning. It establishes image generation as a universal interface for vision — a paradigm-level contribution analogous to text generation's foundational role in NLP.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者以预训练图像生成模型Nano Banana Pro（NBP）为基础，将其在原始生成训练数据与少量视觉任务标注数据的混合集上进行指令微调。核心思路是：将分割掩码、表面法线、深度图等所有感知任务的输出统一编码为RGB图像。这样，模型只需根据输入图像和文本指令"生成"对应的任务输出图像，无需任何任务专属解码头或适配层。最终得到的Vision Banana是一个单一通用模型，可无缝切换生成与理解两种模式。 The authors take a pre-trained image diffusion/generation model called Nano Banana Pro (NBP) and instruction-tune it on a mixture of its original generative training data and a small amount of labeled vision task data. The key insight is to parameterize all perception task outputs — such as segmentation masks, surface normals, and depth maps — as RGB images. This means the model simply "generates" a task-specific output image given an input image and a text instruction, requiring no task-specific heads or decoders. The resulting model, Vision Banana, is a single generalist that seamlessly switches between generation and understanding modes.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\hat{y} = G_{\theta}(x, t)\]
💡 Vision Banana的统一推理框架：给定输入图像 x 和任务文本指令 t，生成模型 G（参数为θ）直接输出对应的感知结果图像 ŷ（如深度图、分割掩码等）。 The unified inference framework of Vision Banana: given an input image x and a task text instruction t, the generative model G (with parameters θ) directly outputs the corresponding perception result image ŷ (e.g., depth map, segmentation mask).

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作打破了生成式与判别式视觉模型长期以来的壁垒，表明二者可在统一的生成预训练范式下融合——正如GPT式预训练统一了NLP领域。若该结论在更大规模上得到验证，将从根本上改变视觉基础模型的构建方式，使昂贵的任务专属预训练成为历史。 This work challenges the longstanding separation between generative and discriminative vision models, showing they can be unified under a single generative pretraining paradigm — just as GPT-style pretraining unified NLP. If validated at scale, this could fundamentally reshape how the community builds vision foundation models, making expensive task-specific pretraining obsolete.

Agent-World: Scaling Real-World Environment Synthesis for Evolving General Agent Intelligence Agent-World：面向通用智能体进化的真实环境合成扩展框架

🧑‍🔬 Guanting Dong, Junting Lu, Junjie Huang, Wanjun Zhong et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 自进化训练场，通过合成真实工具环境持续提升通用智能体能力
A self-evolving training arena that synthesizes real-world tool environments to continuously improve general LLM agents.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型越来越需要作为通用智能体与真实工具和API交互，但训练这类智能体面临两大瓶颈：一是缺乏真实、有状态的工具环境和任务；二是缺乏持续学习机制——训练数据一旦固定，智能体就无法继续成长。目前没有一种可扩展的方式能自动发现新环境、生成可验证的任务，并让智能体随时间不断进化。 Large language models increasingly need to act as general-purpose agents that call real-world tools and APIs, but training such agents is bottlenecked by the scarcity of realistic, stateful tool environments and tasks. Existing approaches lack principled mechanisms for lifelong learning — once training data is fixed, the agent cannot grow beyond it. There is no scalable way to automatically discover new environments, generate verifiable tasks, and keep improving an agent over time.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **自主环境-任务发现**：从数千个真实世界主题中自动探索MCP工具生态，合成可执行数据库并生成难度可控的可验证任务，无需人工标注。 • **持续自进化训练**：将多环境强化学习与动态竞技场结合，自动识别当前智能体的能力短板，针对性合成困难任务加以弥补，实现智能体策略与环境的协同进化。 • **扩展性规律揭示**：在23个智能体基准上验证了清晰的扩展规律——环境多样性越高、自进化轮次越多，性能提升越稳定。 • **Agentic Environment-Task Discovery**: Autonomously explores thousands of real-world MCP/tool ecosystem themes, synthesizes executable databases, and generates verifiable tasks with controllable difficulty — no human labeling needed. • **Continuous Self-Evolving Training**: Combines multi-environment RL with a dynamic arena that identifies capability gaps in the current agent and synthesizes targeted hard tasks to fill them, enabling co-evolution of both the agent policy and the environment. • **Scalability Insights**: Demonstrates clear scaling laws — more diverse environments and more self-evolution rounds both yield consistent, measurable performance gains across 23 agent benchmarks.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究团队在23个多样化智能体基准上评估了Agent-World-8B和Agent-World-14B两个模型，涵盖工具调用、API交互、数据库查询和多步推理等任务类型。基线包括强大的专有模型（如GPT-4级别）以及仅扩展训练数据规模但不含自进化机制的环境扩展基线。消融实验分析了环境多样性（主题数量）和自进化轮次对下游性能的影响，揭示了框架的扩展规律。 The models Agent-World-8B and Agent-World-14B were evaluated on 23 diverse agent benchmarks spanning tool use, API interaction, database queries, and multi-step reasoning tasks. Baselines include strong proprietary models (e.g., GPT-4 class) and environment-scaling baselines that scale up training data without the self-evolution mechanism. Ablation studies examined the effect of environment diversity (number of unique themes) and the number of self-evolution rounds on downstream performance, probing the scaling behavior of the framework.
📊 实验
结果		 Agent-World-8B和14B在全部23个智能体基准上均稳定超越强大的专有模型和环境扩展基线。研究观察到清晰的扩展规律：环境多样性提升和自进化轮次增加均带来单调递增的性能改善。Agent-World-14B在同等规模开源模型中达到最优，并在多个任务上超越GPT-4级别的专有模型。各基准的具体提升幅度不等，在多个工具调用和推理基准上相较强基线有数个百分点的稳定增益。 Agent-World-8B and Agent-World-14B consistently outperform strong proprietary models and environment-scaling baselines across all 23 agent benchmarks. The paper demonstrates clear scaling trends: increasing environment diversity and additional self-evolution rounds both yield monotonic performance improvements. Agent-World-14B achieves state-of-the-art results among open-weight models of comparable size on the evaluated benchmarks, surpassing GPT-4-class proprietary models on multiple tasks. Specific numerical gains are reported per benchmark (exact figures vary by task, with the framework showing consistent gains in the range of several percentage points over strong baselines across diverse tool-use and reasoning benchmarks).
🏁 结论 Agent-World证明了通过可扩展的自进化真实工具环境合成，结合多环境强化学习，能够训练出在广泛基准上超越强大专有模型的通用智能体。该框架建立了环境多样性与自进化轮次和智能体能力之间的清晰扩展规律，为持续提升通用智能体智能提供了可行路线图。 Agent-World demonstrates that scalable, self-evolving synthesis of real-world tool environments — combined with multi-environment reinforcement learning — can produce general-purpose agents that surpass strong proprietary models across a wide range of benchmarks. The framework establishes principled scaling laws linking environment diversity and self-evolution rounds to agent capability, providing a concrete roadmap for building continuously improving general agent intelligence.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Agent-World由两大核心模块构成。第一，**自主环境-任务发现**模块：从数千个真实世界主题中自动抓取并整理符合主题的数据库和工具生态（与MCP协议对齐），再合成难度可调的可验证任务，构建丰富多样的训练池。第二，**持续自进化智能体训练**模块：在多环境强化学习框架下，智能体反复与工具环境交互并获得可验证的奖励信号；"自进化竞技场"组件定期评估当前智能体，找出表现薄弱的能力盲区，动态合成针对性任务加以强化。该循环不断重复，使智能体策略与环境池经过多轮迭代协同进化。 Agent-World consists of two core modules. First, the Agentic Environment-Task Discovery module automatically crawls and organizes topic-aligned databases and tool ecosystems (aligned with the Model Context Protocol) from thousands of real-world themes, then synthesizes verifiable tasks with tunable difficulty levels — forming a rich, diverse training pool. Second, the Continuous Self-Evolving Agent Training module runs multi-environment reinforcement learning where the agent iteratively interacts with tool environments and receives verifiable rewards. A self-evolving arena component periodically evaluates the current agent, identifies where it underperforms (capability gaps), and dynamically synthesizes new, targeted tasks in those weak areas to drive further improvement. This loop repeats, allowing the agent and its environment pool to co-evolve over multiple rounds.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{E} = \{e_1, e_2, \ldots, e_N\}, \quad e_i \sim \text{Discovery}(\mathcal{T})\]
💡 环境池由N个环境组成，每个环境从真实世界主题集合T中通过自主发现模块采样生成 The environment pool consists of N environments, each sampled from a set of real-world themes T via the autonomous discovery module
\[\pi_{t+1} = \text{RL}(\pi_t, \mathcal{E}_t \cup \Delta\mathcal{E}_t)\]
💡 智能体策略在每轮自进化中，基于当前环境池加上新合成的针对性任务增量进行更新 The agent policy is updated each self-evolution round using the current environment pool plus newly synthesized targeted task increments that address identified capability gaps

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着LLM越来越多地被部署为调用真实API和工具的自主智能体，训练瓶颈已从模型能力转移到训练基础设施——Agent-World通过大规模自动化环境和任务创建直接解决了这一问题。自进化范式为静态数据集策划提供了原则性替代方案，指向无需持续人工干预即可不断自我改进的智能体。 As LLMs are increasingly deployed as autonomous agents calling real APIs and tools, the bottleneck has shifted from model capability to training infrastructure — Agent-World directly addresses this by automating environment and task creation at scale. The self-evolving paradigm offers a principled alternative to static dataset curation, pointing toward agents that can keep improving without continuous human intervention.

Generalization at the Edge of Stability 稳定性边缘的泛化

🧑‍🔬 Mario Tuci, Caner Korkmaz, Umut Şimşekli, Tolga Birdal

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用分形吸引子理论解释"稳定性边缘"训练为何能提升泛化
A fractal-attractor framework explains why training at the edge of stability improves generalization.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现代神经网络常使用较大的学习率训练，使优化过程进入"稳定性边缘"——一种混沌、震荡的状态。实验上这种训练方式往往能提升模型的测试性能，但背后的理论机制至今不明。以前的泛化界只用Hessian矩阵的迹或谱范数等简单指标来衡量，这些都无法充分刻画混沌区的复杂性。 Modern neural networks are often trained with large learning rates that push optimization into an "edge of stability" regime — a chaotic, oscillatory zone that empirically improves test performance, but has no solid theoretical explanation. Prior generalization bounds only considered simple curvature measures like the trace or spectral norm of the Hessian, which are insufficient to capture the full complexity of this chaotic regime.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出"锐度维度"（sharpness dimension）：受Lyapunov维数理论启发的全新复杂度度量，利用完整Hessian谱及其偏行列式结构，远超以往的迹或谱范数。 • 分形吸引子视角：将随机优化器建模为随机动力系统，其收敛至一个低内在维度的分形吸引子集，从几何角度解释了泛化现象。 • 严格泛化界：给出可证明的泛化上界，将分形吸引子的维度与测试性能直接挂钩，为大学习率训练的经验优势提供了数学基础。 • Novel "sharpness dimension": a new complexity measure inspired by Lyapunov dimension theory that captures the full Hessian spectrum and partial determinant structure — going far beyond trace/spectral norm. • Fractal attractor perspective: stochastic optimizers are modeled as random dynamical systems that converge to a fractal attractor with lower intrinsic dimension, providing a geometric explanation for generalization. • Rigorous generalization bound: a provable bound that links the fractal attractor's dimension to test performance, mathematically grounding the empirical benefits of large-learning-rate training.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在MLP和Transformer架构上展开，涵盖图像分类和序列建模任务。作者将提出的"锐度维度"与现有的平坦度/锐度代理指标（如Hessian迹、谱范数）进行对比，评估其与泛化差距的相关性。此外，他们还用模块化算术数据集研究了"grokking"现象（模型在过拟合很久之后突然获得泛化能力），以验证该理论框架的解释力。 Experiments were conducted on MLPs and Transformer architectures across image classification and sequence modeling tasks. The authors validated their theoretical sharpness dimension measure empirically by comparing it against prior flatness/sharpness proxies (trace, spectral norm) in terms of correlation with generalization gap. They also studied the "grokking" phenomenon — where models suddenly generalize long after overfitting — under the lens of their framework, using modular arithmetic datasets commonly used in grokking research.
📊 实验
结果		 在MLP和Transformer实验中，"锐度维度"与泛化差距的相关性始终优于现有指标（Hessian迹和谱范数）。在grokking实验中，锐度维度在模型从记忆转向泛化的关键时刻出现明显下降，给出了之前指标无法捕捉到的量化信号。总体上，锐度维度对应的泛化界更紧，相关性更高，验证了理论预测。 The sharpness dimension consistently showed stronger correlation with generalization gap than prior measures (trace and spectral norm of the Hessian) across MLP and Transformer experiments. In grokking experiments, the sharpness dimension exhibited a clear drop precisely at the moment the model transitions from memorization to generalization, providing a quantitative signal not captured by prior metrics. Specific numerical results vary by architecture, but the sharpness dimension's correlation with generalization was reported to be significantly higher (with tighter theoretical bounds) compared to baselines relying on trace or top-eigenvalue measures.
🏁 结论 本文证明了"稳定性边缘"的泛化性能由完整Hessian谱通过新提出的"锐度维度"所决定，首次为大学习率混沌训练提升泛化的现象提供了严格的理论解释。分形吸引子框架还对grokking等现象给出了新的理论洞见，推动了深度学习优化理论的发展。 This paper proves that generalization at the edge of stability is governed by the full Hessian spectrum through the novel "sharpness dimension," providing the first rigorous theoretical explanation for why chaotic large-learning-rate training improves generalization. The fractal attractor framework also offers new insights into phenomena like grokking, advancing the theoretical foundations of deep learning optimization.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者将带噪声的随机梯度优化器（如SGD）建模为随机动力系统。在该框架下，训练迭代不收敛到单个点，而是收敛到一个具有明确内在维度的分形吸引子集合。借鉴混沌理论中的Lyapunov维数理论，他们定义了"锐度维度"——该量依赖于Hessian矩阵的有序特征值及其偏行列式乘积。随后，他们以这一新维度为参数，证明了一个类PAC-Bayes风格的泛化界。该界表明，在混沌区域中，决定泛化的是Hessian的完整特征谱，而非仅仅是最大特征值或迹。 The authors model stochastic gradient optimizers (e.g., SGD with noise) as random dynamical systems. In this framework, the iterates of training do not converge to a single point but to a fractal attractor set with a well-defined intrinsic dimension. Drawing on Lyapunov dimension theory from chaos theory, they define the "sharpness dimension" — a quantity that depends on the ordered eigenvalues of the Hessian and their partial products (partial determinants). They then prove a PAC-Bayes-style generalization bound parameterized by this new dimension. This bound reveals that the full eigenspectrum of the Hessian, not just its top eigenvalue or trace, governs generalization in the chaotic regime.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[d_{\text{sharp}} = k + \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{k} \lambda_i}{|\lambda_{k+1}|}\]
💡 锐度维度：其中 λ_i 为Hessian矩阵的有序特征值，k 为使前k个特征值之和为非负的最大整数。该量综合了完整特征谱，而非仅依赖单个特征值或迹。 The sharpness dimension, where λ_i are the ordered eigenvalues of the Hessian and k is the largest integer such that the sum of the top-k eigenvalues is non-negative. It integrates the full spectrum rather than relying on a single eigenvalue or the trace.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{test}} \leq \mathcal{L}_{\text{train}} + \mathcal{O}\left(\sqrt{\frac{d_{\text{sharp}} \log n}{n}}\right)\]
💡 泛化界：测试损失不超过训练损失加上一个与锐度维度 d_sharp 和样本量 n 相关的项，直接将分形维度与泛化性能挂钩。 The generalization bound: test loss is bounded by training loss plus a term scaling with the sharpness dimension d_sharp and sample size n, directly linking the fractal dimension of the attractor to generalization performance.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

TEMPO在测试阶段交替执行两个步骤：（1）策略精炼步骤——模型在无标签测试问题上，利用自生成的推理轨迹和评论者（critic）提供奖励信号进行训练；（2）周期性评论者重校正步骤——使用少量有标签数据集重新校准奖励/评论者模型，防止其随策略模型的进化而偏移失真。这一交替过程在EM算法框架下得到严格证明：重校正对应E步（更新奖励分布估计），策略精炼对应M步（最大化期望奖励）。每次重校正都能收紧ELBO，从而实现持续性能提升，并在整个过程中保持解答的高多样性。 TEMPO alternates between two steps during test time: (1) a policy refinement step where the model trains on unlabeled test questions using self-generated rollouts and a critic for reward signals, and (2) a periodic critic recalibration step where a small labeled dataset is used to re-anchor the critic/reward model, preventing it from drifting with the evolving policy. This alternating procedure is formally grounded in the EM algorithm — the recalibration acts as the E-step (updating the reward distribution estimate) and policy refinement acts as the M-step (maximizing expected reward). By tightening the ELBO at each recalibration, the framework enables sustained performance gains instead of plateauing, while keeping solution diversity high throughout the process.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{ELBO}}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{q(z)}\left[\log p_\theta(x, z)\right] - \mathbb{E}_{q(z)}\left[\log q(z)\right]\]
💡 证据下界（ELBO）：TEMPO通过在重校正步骤中收紧该下界，确保每轮迭代后模型性能单调不降，从而实现持续提升。 The Evidence Lower Bound (ELBO): TEMPO tightens this bound at each recalibration step, guaranteeing monotonic improvement across iterations and enabling sustained test-time scaling.
\[\text{E-step: } q^{(t+1)}(z) = p_{\theta^{(t)}}(z | x, \mathcal{D}_{\text{calib}}) \quad \text{M-step: } \theta^{(t+1)} = \arg\max_\theta \mathbb{E}_{q^{(t+1)}}[\log p_\theta(x,z)]\]
💡 EM算法的两步交替：E步利用有标签校准数据更新评论者（奖励估计），M步在新奖励信号下更新策略模型。TEMPO正是通过这一交替实现持续优化。 The two alternating EM steps: the E-step updates the critic/reward estimate using labeled calibration data, and the M-step updates the policy to maximize expected reward under the refreshed critic — the core engine of TEMPO's sustained improvement.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着测试时计算扩展成为模型能力提升的新维度，TEMPO直击了悄然制约TTT潜力的核心瓶颈——奖励漂移问题。该工作同时提供了理论解释（EM/ELBO）和实践解决方案（周期性重校正），为无需更大模型或更多离线数据的可靠、可扩展推理时自适应打开了大门。 As the field moves toward test-time compute scaling as a new axis of model improvement, TEMPO addresses a critical bottleneck — reward drift — that has been quietly undermining TTT's potential. By providing both a theoretical explanation (EM/ELBO) and a practical fix (periodic recalibration), this work opens the door to reliable, scalable inference-time adaptation without requiring larger models or more offline data.
