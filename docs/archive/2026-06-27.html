Improved Large Language Diffusion Models 改进的大型语言扩散模型（iLLaDA）

🧑‍🔬 Shen Nie, Qiyang Min, Shaoxuan Xu, Zihao Huang et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 双向扩散语言模型首次在规模上与主流自回归大模型竞争
A bidirectional diffusion language model rivals top autoregressive LLMs at 8B scale.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy想象传统语言模型是一个写作文时只能从第一个字写到最后一个字、绝不能回头改的学生；而 iLLaDA 更像是先把整张卷子的空格全部填上草稿，再反复修改润色，最终交出一篇完整的文章——两种方式都能写出好文章，但思路完全不同！Think of traditional AI models like a student who must write an essay one word at a time — left to right — and can never erase or go back. iLLaDA is more like a student who fills in all the blanks with rough guesses first, then keeps refining the whole page until it makes perfect sense. Same great essay, totally different strategy!
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的大语言模型（比如ChatGPT、LLaMA）都是"从左到右"逐字生成文字的，就像一个人只能向前看、不能回头修改思路。这种方式被称为"自回归"，虽然效果很好，但它从根本上限制了模型同时利用上下文信息的能力。研究者们想知道：有没有一种完全不同的生成方式，也能训练出同样强大的语言模型？ Most powerful AI language models today — like GPT or LLaMA — generate text one word at a time, left to right, like reading a teleprompter they can never rewind. This "autoregressive" approach works well, but it means the model can't look ahead or revisit earlier decisions. Researchers wondered: could a completely different approach — one that fills in words more like solving a puzzle — reach the same level of power?
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出 iLLaDA，一个从零开始训练的 8B 参数双向掩码扩散语言模型，全程不使用自回归方式 • 预训练规模达 12T tokens，指令微调使用 25B token 语料库，训练 12 轮，规模创同类模型新高 • 引入"可变长度生成"提升推理效率，以及"基于置信度打分"用于多项选择题评估，解决扩散模型的实际落地难题 • 在数学、代码、常识推理等多个维度大幅超越前代 LLaDA 模型，并在多个榜单上逼近主流自回归模型 Qwen2.5 7B • Introduces iLLaDA, an 8B bidirectional masked diffusion LM trained entirely from scratch — no autoregressive tricks • Scales pre-training to 12 trillion tokens and fine-tunes on a 25B-token instruction dataset for 12 epochs — largest of its kind • Adds variable-length generation for speed and confidence-based scoring for multiple-choice tasks, making diffusion models more practical • Dramatically outperforms its predecessor LLaDA and stays competitive with the mainstream Qwen2.5 7B model across multiple benchmarks
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个权威基准测试上评估 iLLaDA，包括通用推理（BBH、ARC-Challenge）、数学（MATH）、代码生成（HumanEval）等，同时测试了基础版（Base）和指令微调版（Instruct）两种形态。主要对比基线包括前代扩散模型 LLaDA，以及主流自回归模型 Qwen2.5 7B 和 LLaMA3 8B。评估使用标准准确率指标，并引入了自研的置信度打分方法用于选择题场景。 The team tested iLLaDA on a wide range of standard AI benchmarks: general reasoning (BBH, ARC-Challenge), math (MATH), and code generation (HumanEval). They evaluated both a base pretrained version and an instruction-tuned "chat" version. The main competitors were the previous diffusion model LLaDA, and two popular autoregressive models — Qwen2.5 7B and LLaMA3 8B. They used standard accuracy metrics plus their own confidence-based scoring method for multiple-choice questions.
📊 实验
结果		 • iLLaDA-Base 在 BBH 上比 LLaDA 提升 21.6 分，在 ARC-Challenge 上提升 14.9 分 • iLLaDA-Instruct 在 MATH 数学榜上提升 14.5 分，在代码生成 HumanEval 上提升 16.5 分 • 在多个榜单上与自回归模型 Qwen2.5 7B 表现相当，证明扩散路线的竞争力 • iLLaDA-Base beats LLaDA by +21.6 points on BBH (general reasoning) and +14.9 points on ARC-Challenge • iLLaDA-Instruct improves by +14.5 points on MATH and +16.5 points on HumanEval (code generation) • Remains competitive with Qwen2.5 7B (a top autoregressive model) on multiple benchmarks, a first for diffusion-based LLMs at this scale
🏁 结论 本文证明了：不依赖传统"从左到右"生成方式，仅用双向掩码扩散从零训练，也能打造出与主流大模型比肩的强大语言模型。iLLaDA 是目前同类中规模最大、性能最强的扩散语言模型，为语言模型的多元化发展路径提供了有力证据。 This paper proves that you don't need the traditional left-to-right generation method to build a powerful language model — bidirectional masked diffusion trained from scratch can match mainstream autoregressive LLMs. iLLaDA is the strongest and largest diffusion-based language model to date, opening a credible alternative path for building future AI systems.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

iLLaDA 采用"掩码扩散"的思路：训练时随机遮住句子中的一些词，让模型学会同时利用左边和右边的上下文来猜出被遮住的词——就像做完形填空，而不是从头到尾默写课文。这种双向注意力机制让模型对全局语义的理解更全面。在生成文本时，模型从一段全部被遮住的序列出发，逐步"填充"出最终答案，类似于从一张全黑的图片慢慢还原成清晰照片。此外，研究者还设计了可变长度生成策略（避免浪费算力在无用的空位上）和置信度打分机制（让模型更好地判断多选题答案），进一步提升了实用性。 iLLaDA uses a technique called "masked diffusion": during training, it randomly hides some words in a sentence and makes the model guess them using both the words before AND after — like a fill-in-the-blank test where you can peek at the whole sentence. When generating new text, it starts with a completely blank sequence and gradually fills words in, like a photo developing from a blank frame into a sharp image. To make this practical, the researchers added two smart tricks: variable-length generation (so the model doesn't waste effort on empty slots) and confidence-based scoring (so it can better pick answers on multiple-choice questions).

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

目前几乎所有主流大模型（GPT、LLaMA、Qwen 等）都基于同一种自回归架构，这意味着整个 AI 行业高度依赖一种技术路线。iLLaDA 的成功说明扩散模型这条路同样走得通，未来的 AI 助手可能有更多样的底层架构，在并行生成、修改灵活性等方面带来新的可能性。 Almost every major AI language model today — GPT, LLaMA, Gemini — uses the same autoregressive recipe, meaning the whole industry is betting on one approach. iLLaDA's success shows that diffusion-based models are a real, competitive alternative, which could lead to future AI assistants that generate text faster, more flexibly, or in entirely new ways.

Qwen-Image-Agent: Bridging the Context Gap in Real-World Image Generation Qwen-Image-Agent：弥合真实世界图像生成中的上下文鸿沟

🧑‍🔬 Zekai Zhang, Jiahao Li, Jie Zhang, Kaiyuan Gao et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 AI图像助手，自动补全你描述不清的生图需求
An AI agent that fills in missing context to generate images from vague or incomplete user requests.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像你去咖啡馆，只说"给我来杯好喝的"，普通咖啡师会愣住，但一个超级贴心的老咖啡师会问你今天心情、口味偏好、天气冷不冷，然后给你调出最完美的一杯。Qwen-Image-Agent就是那个贴心的老咖啡师。Imagine you walk into a coffee shop and say "just make me something good." A regular barista would be confused, but a super-attentive one would ask about your mood, flavor preferences, and the weather — then craft the perfect cup. Qwen-Image-Agent is that attentive barista for AI image generation.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的文生图模型（比如你说"帮我画一张图"）需要你提供非常精确、完整的描述才能生成好图。但现实中大多数人描述都很模糊、不完整，或者需要最新的知识（比如"画最近那个很火的梗"）。这种用户说的话和模型真正需要的信息之间的落差，就是"上下文鸿沟"，导致生成的图往往不是你想要的。 Existing text-to-image AI models need very detailed, precise instructions to generate good images. But in real life, people often give vague, incomplete, or context-dependent requests — like "draw something for my friend's birthday" or "make a meme about that viral moment." This gap between what the user says and what the AI actually needs is called the "Context Gap," and it leads to disappointing, off-target results.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次明确提出"上下文鸿沟"这一核心问题，并将其作为图像生成智能体的设计核心 • 设计了"上下文感知规划"模块，让AI像人类助手一样主动分析你缺少哪些信息 • 整合了推理、搜索、记忆、反馈四大能力，让AI能从多个渠道自动补全生图所需的上下文 • 发布了专门评测图像生成智能体的新基准IA-Bench，填补了该领域的评测空白 • First to formally define the "Context Gap" problem and make it the central design challenge for image-generation agents • Introduces "Context-Aware Planning" — the AI figures out what info is missing before drawing anything • Combines reasoning, web search, memory, and user feedback into one unified system to fill in the blanks • Releases IA-Bench, a brand-new benchmark designed specifically to test image-generation agents across four key skills
🧪 实验
内容		 研究团队在三个基准测试上评估了Qwen-Image-Agent：他们自己新建的IA-Bench（覆盖规划、推理、搜索、记忆四大能力）、Mindbench（创意图像生成）以及WISE-Verified（知识密集型图像生成）。对比的基线模型包括多种现有的文生图智能体和大模型方案。评估采用自动化指标与人工评估相结合的方式。 The team tested Qwen-Image-Agent on three benchmarks: their newly created IA-Bench (testing Plan, Reason, Search, and Memory skills), Mindbench (creative image generation), and WISE-Verified (knowledge-intensive image generation). They compared against several strong existing text-to-image agent baselines and large model pipelines, using both automated metrics and human evaluation.
📊 实验
结果		 Qwen-Image-Agent在三个基准测试上均达到最优（SOTA）表现，超越所有对比基线。在IA-Bench上展示了在规划、推理、搜索、记忆四个维度的全面领先；在Mindbench和WISE-Verified上同样优于现有强基线（论文中具体数字详见原文，总体性能提升显著）。 Qwen-Image-Agent achieves state-of-the-art results across all three benchmarks — IA-Bench, Mindbench, and WISE-Verified — outperforming all compared baselines. It shows consistent improvements across all four capability dimensions (Plan, Reason, Search, Memory) in IA-Bench, and delivers top performance on knowledge-intensive and creative generation tasks (specific numerical results are detailed in the original paper).
🏁 结论 本文证明了将规划、推理、搜索、记忆和反馈统一集成到一个以"上下文"为核心的智能体框架中，可以有效弥合用户模糊需求与文生图模型所需精确描述之间的鸿沟，显著提升真实世界场景下的图像生成质量。 This paper proves that wrapping a text-to-image model inside a smart "context-completing" agent — one that plans, searches, reasons, and remembers — dramatically improves real-world image generation where user requests are vague or incomplete. The introduced IA-Bench also gives the community a new standard tool for evaluating such agents.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Qwen-Image-Agent把用户的模糊请求当作一个"不完整的拼图"，然后分两步来补全它。第一步叫"上下文感知规划"：AI先分析你说的话，找出哪些信息缺失、哪些需要查资料、哪些需要回忆之前的对话；第二步叫"上下文落地"：AI真正去推理、上网搜索、调取记忆或向用户追问，把缺失的碎片一块块补上。最后，把完整的上下文喂给文生图模型，才生成最终图像。整个过程就像一个聪明的助理帮你把需求整理清楚再交给设计师。 Qwen-Image-Agent treats your vague request as an incomplete puzzle and solves it in two steps. First, "Context-Aware Planning" — the AI reads your request and figures out what pieces are missing (maybe it needs to know the current date, search for a trending topic, or recall what you asked for last time). Second, "Context Grounding" — the AI actually goes and collects those missing pieces by reasoning, searching the web, pulling from memory, or asking you a follow-up question. Only once all the pieces are in place does it hand the full picture to the image generator. It's like having a smart personal assistant who preps a full creative brief before sending it to the designer.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项研究让普通用户不再需要学习复杂的"咒语"（提示词工程）才能生成好图，AI会主动理解并补全你的意图。对于AI产品落地而言，这意味着文生图工具将更接近真实用户的使用习惯，大幅降低使用门槛。 This research means everyday people won't need to master "prompt engineering" to get great AI-generated images — the AI will figure out what you mean on its own. For real-world AI products, this is a big step toward image tools that actually work the way people naturally talk and think.

Autodata: An agentic data scientist to create high quality synthetic data Autodata：作为高质量合成数据创造者的智能体数据科学家

🧑‍🔬 Ilia Kulikov, Chenxi Whitehouse, Tianhao Wu, Yixin Nie et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 训练AI智能体自动充当数据科学家，生成更高质量的训练数据
An AI agent trained to act as a data scientist that automatically creates high-quality training data for other AI models.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一位经验丰富的家教老师，不仅能给学生出题，还能根据学生的答题情况自动改进题目质量——越出越精准，越出越有针对性，最终让学生突飞猛进。Think of it like a super-tutor who not only writes practice exam questions for students, but also watches how students perform, learns which questions were most helpful, and keeps writing better and better questions — getting smarter at teaching with every round.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练AI模型需要大量高质量的数据，但人工标注数据既昂贵又缓慢。现有的自动生成数据的方法（称为"合成数据"）质量参差不齐，生成的数据往往不够好，导致训练出来的模型表现欠佳。就像用劣质食材做饭，无论厨艺多好，菜肴都难以美味。 Training AI models requires huge amounts of high-quality data, but collecting and labeling it by hand is slow and expensive. Existing automatic data generation methods often produce mediocre data — like trying to bake a cake with random ingredients grabbed off a shelf without knowing if they're any good.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出"智能体数据科学家"概念：让AI像真正的数据科学家一样，自主规划、编写代码、验证并生成高质量数据； • 引入"元优化"机制：让数据科学家智能体本身也能从经验中不断学习改进，越用越聪明； • 通过增加推理计算量来提升训练数据质量，打通了"推理算力→更好数据→更强模型"的新路径。 • Introduces the concept of an "agentic data scientist" — an AI that plans, writes code, verifies outputs, and curates its own training data like a real human expert would. • Proposes "meta-optimization": the data scientist agent learns from its own past performance and gets better over time at creating useful data. • Creates a new pipeline where spending more computing power at inference time directly translates into better training data and stronger AI models.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究者在三类任务上测试了Autodata：计算机科学研究任务、法律推理任务，以及数学对象推理任务。他们将Autodata与传统合成数据生成方法作为基线进行比较，并额外测试了经过元优化后的智能体版本。评估指标为模型在下游任务上的准确率表现。 Experiments were run on three types of tasks: computer science research reasoning, legal reasoning, and reasoning about mathematical objects. The baselines were classical synthetic data generation methods. They also tested the additional benefit of meta-optimizing the data scientist agent itself. Model accuracy on downstream tasks was the main evaluation metric.
📊 实验
结果		 Autodata在三类任务上均超越了传统合成数据生成基线方法。在此基础上，对数据科学家智能体进行元优化后，性能得到了"更大幅度的提升"（论文描述为"even larger performance uplift"）。虽然论文摘要未披露具体数字，但作者强调元优化带来的增益明显大于单纯使用智能体数据生成的增益。 Autodata outperformed classical synthetic data generation methods across all three task domains (CS research, legal reasoning, and mathematical reasoning). Meta-optimizing the data scientist agent itself yielded an "even larger performance uplift" on top of the baseline agentic approach. While exact benchmark numbers are in the full paper, the gains from meta-optimization were described as substantially larger than those from the base agentic method alone.
🏁 结论 本文证明了可以训练一个AI智能体充当"数据科学家"，让它自动创造高质量训练数据，并且通过对该智能体自身的元优化，能进一步大幅提升下游模型的性能。这为AI领域开辟了一条新路：用更多推理算力换取更好的训练数据，从而训练出更强的AI模型。 This paper proves that an AI agent can be trained to act as a data scientist, automatically producing high-quality training data that surpasses traditional methods — and that meta-optimizing the agent itself yields even bigger gains. It opens a new pathway: spend more compute at inference time to get better data, which in turn builds stronger AI models.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Autodata训练一个AI"数据科学家"智能体，让它像人类专家一样，主动思考需要什么样的数据、编写代码生成数据、检验数据质量，再把好的数据用于训练其他模型。更关键的是，系统还对这个智能体本身进行"元优化"——用它生成数据后的训练效果来反向改进智能体自身，让它越来越擅长创造有价值的数据。具体实现叫做"Agentic Self-Instruct"，是一种可实际落地的框架。 Autodata trains an AI agent to act like a human data scientist: it figures out what kinds of data are needed, writes code to generate examples, checks whether they're correct and useful, and delivers a curated dataset. The clever twist is "meta-optimization" — the system evaluates how well the data the agent created actually helped train downstream models, then uses that feedback to make the agent itself smarter at data creation. The practical version of this system is called "Agentic Self-Instruct."

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\theta^* = \arg\max_{\theta} \mathbb{E}_{d \sim p_\theta(D)} [R(M_d)]\]
💡 元优化目标：找到最优的数据科学家智能体参数θ，使得该智能体生成的数据集d训练出的模型M_d在下游任务上的奖励R最大化。 The meta-optimization objective: find the best agent parameters θ such that the model trained on the agent's generated dataset d achieves the highest reward R on downstream tasks.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

高质量数据是训练强大AI的命脉，而人工标注数据越来越难以满足需求。Autodata提供了一种让AI自己"养活自己"的方式，有望大幅降低数据生产成本，加速各类AI应用（法律、科研、数学等领域）的落地。 High-quality data is the fuel that powers every great AI model, and human-labeled data can't scale forever. Autodata offers a way for AI to bootstrap its own improvement, potentially slashing data production costs and accelerating AI applications in law, science, mathematics, and beyond.

🧑‍🔬 Wei Zhou, Xiongwei Zhu, Zelin Xu, Bo Dong et al.

🎯 让一个图像生成模型同时掌握多种能力且互不干扰的蒸馏框架
A single image-generation model that masters text-to-image, local editing, and global editing all at once without them fighting each other.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一位学厨师的学生，分别跟三位大师学中餐、西餐和甜点——每次上课只专注一位师傅，课后再把所有菜系的精髓融会贯通，最终一个人就能开一家全菜系餐厅，而且每道菜都地道。Imagine a student musician who learns guitar, piano, and drums from three separate teachers — each lesson focuses on just one instrument, but over time the student blends all three skills into one versatile performer. DanceOPD does the same for AI: it teaches one model to master multiple image tricks one at a time, so they all end up working together in harmony.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现代AI图像生成模型需要同时完成多项任务：根据文字生成图片、对图片局部进行修改、以及对整体风格进行调整。但这些能力往往互相"打架"——训练时强化某一项，就会削弱另一项，就像一个厨师练太多甜点就忘了怎么做主菜。目前还没有一种干净、有效的方式把这些能力统一到一个模型里。 Modern AI image generators need to do many things at once — create images from text, make small local tweaks, and apply big global style changes — but these abilities tend to clash. Training a model to do one thing well usually hurts its performance on the others, like a chef who gets so good at desserts that they forget how to cook a steak. There's no clean, principled way to combine all these capabilities into one model without trade-offs.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 每个训练样本只走一个"专家"能力通道，避免多种能力互相干扰。 • 将所有能力（包括人工定义的引导技巧）统一表示为同一数学空间中的"速度场"，天然可组合。 • 用学生模型自己生成的图像状态来查询专家（on-policy），减少训练与实际使用之间的差距。 • 整个训练目标极为简洁，只用速度MSE损失函数，易于实现和调参。 • Routes each training sample to one dedicated "expert" capability, preventing skills from clashing during training. • Treats every capability — including human-defined tricks like classifier-free guidance — as a velocity field in the same mathematical space, making them naturally composable. • Uses the student model's own generated outputs (on-policy) to query the experts, closing the gap between training and real-world use. • Achieves all this with a simple, elegant loss function (velocity MSE) that's easy to implement and tune.

They compared against state-of-the-art baselines including standard flow-matching distillation methods. Evaluation used standard image quality metrics such as FID (image realism), CLIP score (text-image alignment), and editing-specific metrics. This establishes a principled, practical framework for generative field distillation that could serve as a standard recipe for future multi-capability image generation systems.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

可以把DanceOPD想象成一所舞蹈学校：一位主学员（学生模型）同时向几位专家教练学习，每位教练只擅长一种风格（比如芭蕾、街舞、萨尔萨）。训练时不混合所有风格，而是每次课只专注一位教练。主学员先自己跳一段（on-policy生成），再问对应教练"从这个动作开始我该怎么动？"并根据反馈调整。久而久之，主学员自然融合了所有风格而互不干扰。在数学上，每种能力被表示为图像空间中的"速度场"，学生学会为不同任务跟随不同的场，包括吸收无分类器引导等技巧。 Think of DanceOPD like a dance school where one master teacher (the student model) learns from several specialist coaches (expert models), each great at one style — ballet, hip-hop, or salsa. Instead of confusingly mixing all styles in one class, each practice session is dedicated to just one coach. The master watches itself dance first (on-policy rollout), then asks the relevant coach "how should I move from here?" and adjusts based on that feedback. Over time, the master naturally blends all styles without letting them interfere. Mathematically, each capability is expressed as a "velocity field" — a map of directions to move in image space — and the student learns to follow the right field for the right task, including absorbing shortcuts like classifier-free guidance.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

目前，要打造一个"全能型"AI图像工具（既能生成、又能局部修图、还能全局换风格），往往需要痛苦的能力取舍或部署多个模型。DanceOPD提供了一套清晰的方法将这些技能融合进单一模型，未来的AI图像编辑器有望因此变得更快、更便宜、开箱即用能力更强。 Right now, building an AI image tool that's great at everything — generating, retouching, and restyling — requires painful compromises or separate models. DanceOPD offers a clean recipe to merge all these skills into one model without them fighting each other, which could make future AI image editors faster, cheaper, and more capable out of the box.
