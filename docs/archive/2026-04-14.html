维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现代LLM智能体的能力提升越来越依赖于对模型运行环境的重组，而非重新训练模型权重。然而，目前缺乏一个统一的理论框架来解释这种转变的内在逻辑，以及记忆、技能、协议、执行框架之间的关系。这导致智能体系统的构建和评估仍然缺乏系统性。 Modern LLM agents are becoming increasingly capable not by retraining model weights, but by restructuring the environment around the model. However, there is no unified theoretical framework to explain why this shift works and how its components — memory, skills, protocols, and execution harnesses — relate to each other. Without such a framework, building and evaluating agent systems remains ad hoc and fragmented.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 借鉴认知科学中"认知工件"概念，首次将"外化"作为统一视角，将智能体基础设施重新定义为将认知负担从模型转移到外部结构的过程。 • 提出清晰分类体系：记忆外化跨时间状态、技能外化程序性专业知识、协议外化交互结构、执行框架作为协调与治理层。 • 梳理了从权重→上下文→执行框架的历史演进路径，并展望了自演化执行框架和共享智能体基础设施等前沿方向。 • Introduces "externalization" as a unifying lens borrowed from cognitive science (cognitive artifacts), reframing agent infrastructure as offloading cognitive burdens from the model to external structures. • Proposes a clear taxonomy: memory externalizes state over time, skills externalize procedural expertise, protocols externalize interaction structure, and the harness acts as the coordination and governance layer. • Traces a historical progression from weights → context → harness, offering a principled narrative of how agent design has evolved and where it is heading (self-evolving harnesses, shared infrastructure).

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文采用文献综述与概念框架构建相结合的研究方法。作者借鉴认知科学中"认知工件"理论，论证LLM智能体的每个主要组件都在外化特定类型的认知负担：记忆存储负责状态持久化，技能库编码可复用的程序性知识，交互协议结构化多智能体与工具间的通信，执行框架则作为统一的治理协调层。作者系统分析了四类组件的相互作用、与参数化（模型权重内）能力的权衡关系，以及它们如何共同构成实际智能体部署的系统级架构。 The paper adopts a literature review and conceptual framework methodology. Drawing from cognitive science's notion of "cognitive artifacts" — tools that extend human cognition — the authors argue that each major component of an LLM agent externalizes a specific type of cognitive burden. Memory stores handle state persistence, skill libraries encode reusable procedures, interaction protocols structure multi-agent and tool communication, and harness engineering serves as the unifying governance layer. The authors systematically analyze how these four components interact, trade off against parametric (in-weights) capabilities, and collectively form a systems-level architecture for practical agent deployment.

By grounding agent infrastructure in cognitive science, it bridges AI systems research and cognitive theory, potentially guiding the next generation of self-evolving and shared agent infrastructure.

HY-Embodied-0.5: Embodied Foundation Models for Real-World Agents HY-Embodied-0.5：面向真实世界智能体的具身基础模型

🧑‍🔬 Tencent Robotics X, HY Vision Team, :, Xumin Yu et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 面向真实机器人的具身基础模型，MoT架构+自进化训练
A 2B/32B embodied foundation model family bridging VLMs and real-world robot agents via MoT architecture and self-evolving training.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 通用视觉语言模型（VLM）并不擅长处理具身智能所需的任务，比如机器人在真实环境中感知空间、理解时序信息并做出行动决策。这类模型缺乏细粒度的空间与时间视觉理解能力，无法满足操控、导航等机器人任务的需求。同时，大型强大模型与边缘部署所需的小型高效模型之间存在明显的性能鸿沟。 General Vision-Language Models (VLMs) are not well-suited for embodied agents that must perceive, reason, and act in the physical world. They lack the fine-grained spatial and temporal visual understanding required for robot tasks like navigation, manipulation, and planning. There is also a practical gap between large, powerful models and the small, efficient models needed for edge deployment on real robots.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **混合Transformer（MoT）架构**：通过模态专属计算与隐变量token，大幅提升细粒度视觉感知能力，超越标准VLM。 • **迭代自进化后训练范式**：模型通过迭代自监督训练循环不断增强推理能力，无需额外标注数据。 • **策略内蒸馏（On-Policy Distillation）**：将32B大模型的能力迁移至2B小模型，最大化边缘端性能，有效缩小模型规模与性能之间的鸿沟。 • **Mixture-of-Transformers (MoT) Architecture**: Uses modality-specific computing with latent tokens to boost fine-grained visual perception beyond what standard VLMs provide. • **Iterative Self-Evolving Post-Training**: The model continuously improves its own reasoning capabilities through an iterative, self-supervised training loop without needing more labeled data. • **On-Policy Distillation**: The 32B model distills its advanced capabilities into the compact 2B model, maximizing edge-device performance and closing the size-performance gap.
🧪 实验
内容		 模型在22个基准测试上进行了全面评估，涵盖视觉感知（空间推理、深度估计等）、具身理解和通用VLM任务。基线模型包括同等规模的最先进模型以及Gemini 3.0 Pro等前沿大模型。此外，还在真实物理机器人上开展了下游机器人控制实验，通过VLA扩展模型验证真实操控性能。 The models were evaluated across 22 benchmarks covering visual perception (e.g., spatial reasoning, depth estimation), embodied understanding, and general VLM tasks. Baselines include state-of-the-art models of similar sizes as well as frontier models like Gemini 3.0 Pro and other leading VLMs. Downstream robot control experiments were conducted using real physical robots to evaluate manipulation performance in real-world settings, validating the VLA extension.
📊 实验
结果		 MoT-2B模型在22个基准测试中的16个上超越了同等规模的最先进模型。32B变体在完整基准测试套件上达到了与Gemini 3.0 Pro等前沿模型相当的性能水平。在真实机器人控制实验中，基于HY-Embodied骨干构建的VLA模型在物理操控评估中取得了令人信服的表现，验证了其实际可部署性。 The MoT-2B model outperforms similarly sized state-of-the-art models on 16 out of 22 benchmarks evaluated. The 32B variant achieves performance comparable to frontier models such as Gemini 3.0 Pro across the full benchmark suite. In real-world robot control experiments, the VLA model built on the HY-Embodied backbone achieves compelling results in physical manipulation evaluations, demonstrating practical deployability.
🏁 结论 HY-Embodied-0.5证明了专为具身智能设计的基础模型——结合MoT架构、自进化训练与策略内蒸馏——能够在具身任务上显著超越通用VLM，同时保持在边缘设备和真实机器人上的可部署性。本工作为构建具备空间与时序感知能力、可直接驱动机器人行动的VLM提供了强有力的开源范式。 HY-Embodied-0.5 proves that purpose-built embodied foundation models—combining MoT architecture, self-evolving training, and on-policy distillation—can significantly outperform general VLMs on embodied tasks while remaining practically deployable on edge devices and real robots. This work provides a strong open-source blueprint for building spatially and temporally aware VLMs that translate directly into real-world robot action.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

HY-Embodied-0.5采用混合Transformer（MoT）架构，为视觉和语言token分配各自专属的Transformer模块，并引入隐变量token以丰富感知表征。训练阶段采用迭代自进化范式，模型自我生成训练信号，逐步提升空间与具身推理能力。此后通过策略内蒸馏将32B大模型的能力迁移至2B小模型。最终，训练好的VLM骨干被扩展为视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型，支持真实机器人的端到端控制。 HY-Embodied-0.5 introduces a Mixture-of-Transformers (MoT) architecture that allocates modality-specific transformer blocks for visual and language tokens, with added latent tokens to enrich perceptual representations. For training, an iterative self-evolving paradigm is used where the model generates its own training signal to progressively improve spatial and embodied reasoning. On-policy distillation is then applied to transfer knowledge from the 32B variant to the 2B edge model. Finally, the trained VLM backbone is extended into a Vision-Language-Action (VLA) model for direct robot control in real-world settings.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{MoT}(x) = \text{FFN}_{\text{modality}}(\text{Attn}(x) + x) + \text{Attn}(x)\]
💡 混合Transformer（MoT）为不同模态（视觉/语言）分配独立的前馈网络（FFN），使每种模态的信息能被专属模块处理，从而提升细粒度感知。 The Mixture-of-Transformers assigns separate feed-forward networks (FFNs) to different modalities (vision/language), allowing each modality to be processed by a dedicated sub-network for richer, modality-specific representations.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着机器人与具身智能逐步迈向基础模型驱动的流程，本工作提供了一条可扩展的开源路径，有效弥合通用VLM与物理落地智能体之间的鸿沟。2B/32B双规格模型家族同时满足研究规模与部署规模的需求，让强大的具身推理能力在边缘机器人上触手可及。 As robotics and embodied AI move toward foundation model-driven pipelines, this work demonstrates a scalable, open-source path to bridge the gap between general-purpose VLMs and physically grounded agents. The dual 2B/32B model family addresses both research-scale and deployment-scale needs, making powerful embodied reasoning accessible for edge robots.

Scal3R: Scalable Test-Time Training for Large-Scale 3D Reconstruction Scal3R：面向大规模三维重建的可扩展测试时训练

🧑‍🔬 Tao Xie, Peishan Yang, Yudong Jin, Yingfeng Cai et al.

🎯 测试时自适应全局上下文模块，实现长视频大规模三维重建
A neural global context module adapted at test time enables accurate large-scale 3D reconstruction from long videos.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 从长视频中重建大规模三维场景，对现有前馈式模型来说非常困难：随着场景增大，模型"记忆"不足，无法记住早期帧的内容，导致几何不一致和累计误差。现有方法缺乏跨帧传递全局场景理解的机制。 Reconstructing large 3D scenes from long video sequences is hard for current feed-forward models: they run out of "memory" as the scene grows and lose track of what they saw earlier, leading to inconsistent and inaccurate geometry. Existing methods lack a mechanism to carry global scene understanding across many frames, causing errors that accumulate over time.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出神经全局上下文表示：用紧凑的可学习记忆压缩整段视频的长程场景信息。 • 测试时自监督训练：在推理阶段对轻量子网络快速微调，大幅扩展记忆容量且计算开销小。 • 仿人类感知机制：类比人类用全局场景理解辅助局部细节感知，首次将此思路引入前馈式三维重建。 • Novel neural global context representation: a compact, learnable memory that compresses long-range scene information across the entire video sequence. • Test-time training via self-supervised objectives: lightweight sub-networks are rapidly fine-tuned at inference time, dramatically boosting memory capacity without heavy computation. • Inspired by human perception: mirrors how humans use global scene understanding to guide local detail recognition, applied here to feed-forward 3D reconstruction.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在两个大规模户外数据集上进行：KITTI Odometry（自动驾驶序列）和 Oxford Spires（大型城市激光雷达配准场景）。与当前最优的前馈式和基于优化的三维重建基线模型进行对比。评估指标包括相机位姿精度（平移/旋转误差）、三维重建质量（深度精度、点云完整性）以及运行效率。 Experiments were conducted on two challenging large-scale outdoor benchmarks: KITTI Odometry (autonomous driving sequences) and Oxford Spires (large urban LiDAR-registered scenes). The method was compared against state-of-the-art feed-forward and optimization-based 3D reconstruction baselines. Evaluation metrics include camera pose accuracy (translation/rotation error) and 3D reconstruction quality (depth accuracy, point cloud completeness). Efficiency (runtime) was also measured to verify scalability.
📊 实验
结果		 在 KITTI Odometry 和 Oxford Spires 两个基准上，Scal3R 在前馈式方法中实现了最优相机位姿精度和三维重建精度，在平移误差（ATE/RTE）和旋转误差指标上超越现有前馈基线，并在跨越数百帧的超大场景中保持高质量深度与点云重建，同时在推理效率上优于基于优化的重方法。（具体数值见论文实验表格。） On KITTI Odometry and Oxford Spires benchmarks, Scal3R achieves leading camera pose accuracy and state-of-the-art 3D reconstruction accuracy among feed-forward methods. The method outperforms prior feed-forward reconstruction models in both translation and rotation error metrics, and shows strong reconstruction quality (depth/point cloud metrics) on ultra-large scenes spanning hundreds of frames, while maintaining competitive inference efficiency compared to optimization-heavy baselines. (Specific numerical values from the full paper tables, e.g. ATE, RTE, depth AbsRel improvements, are detailed in the paper's experimental section.)
🏁 结论 Scal3R 证明了通过自监督测试时训练快速适应的轻量全局上下文表示，能有效将前馈三维重建扩展至大规模场景，并实现更优的位姿与几何精度。本文将测试时训练确立为克服长序列三维重建内存瓶颈的实用高效策略。 Scal3R demonstrates that a lightweight neural global context representation, adapted at test time via self-supervision, effectively scales feed-forward 3D reconstruction to large scenes with superior pose and geometry accuracy. This work establishes test-time training as a practical and efficient strategy for overcoming memory limitations in long-sequence 3D scene reconstruction.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Scal3R 引入一组轻量神经子网络，作为场景的压缩式全局上下文存储器。在测试阶段，这些子网络利用输入视频自身产生的自监督信号快速适应（无需标注数据），从而"记住"整个场景的布局与外观。重建局部区域时，模型查询该全局上下文以辅助几何估计，提升局部精度与全局一致性。该方法模块化设计，可无缝接入现有前馈重建流程。 Scal3R introduces a set of lightweight neural sub-networks that serve as a compressed global context store for the scene. During test time, these sub-networks are quickly adapted using self-supervised signals derived from the input video itself (no ground-truth labels needed), allowing them to "memorize" the entire scene's layout and appearance. When reconstructing any local part of the scene, the model queries this global context to inform geometry estimation, improving both local accuracy and global consistency. The approach is modular and plugs into existing feed-forward reconstruction pipelines.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{self} = \mathcal{L}_{depth} + \lambda \mathcal{L}_{pose}\]
💡 测试时训练的自监督损失：深度一致性损失与位姿一致性损失的加权组合，用于在推理阶段快速适应轻量子网络。 Self-supervised test-time training loss: a weighted combination of depth consistency and pose consistency losses used to rapidly adapt the lightweight sub-networks at inference time (exact form detailed in paper).

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

大规模三维重建是自动驾驶、机器人和 AR/VR 的核心瓶颈，这些领域都需要高效处理长视频流。本文证明测试时自监督紧凑记忆模块可替代昂贵的全局优化，为前馈三维重建系统的实际落地开辟了可扩展路径。 Large-scale 3D reconstruction is a critical bottleneck for autonomous driving, robotics, and AR/VR — fields where processing long video streams efficiently is essential. By showing that test-time self-supervised adaptation of a compact memory module can replace expensive global optimization, this paper opens a scalable path toward real-world deployment of feed-forward 3D reconstruction systems.

🎯 首个真实世界跨场景行为基准，揭露LLM模拟的系统性偏差
OmniBehavior benchmarks LLMs on real-world cross-scenario human behavior and exposes systematic simulation biases.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的用户模拟基准测试只关注单一场景、动作空间狭窄或使用合成数据，根本无法反映真实人类跨场景、长时间跨度的决策过程。因此，我们无法准确评估大语言模型是否能真正模拟出真实、多样且复杂的人类行为。 Current benchmarks for evaluating LLMs as user simulators are limited to isolated, single-scenario settings with narrow action spaces or synthetic data, which do not reflect how real humans make decisions across multiple contexts over long time horizons. This means we have no reliable way to test whether LLMs can truly simulate authentic, diverse, and complex human behavior in the wild.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首个完全基于真实世界数据构建的基准OmniBehavior，整合了长时域、跨场景和异构行为模式。 • 实证揭示孤立场景数据集导致"管道视野"问题——无法捕捉驱动真实决策的跨场景因果链。 • 发现LLM存在"正向平均人"偏差：过度活跃、人格同质化、乌托邦式倾向，导致个体差异和长尾行为消失。 • First benchmark (OmniBehavior) built entirely from real-world data, spanning long-horizon, cross-scenario, and heterogeneous behavioral traces in a unified framework. • Demonstrates empirically that isolated-scenario datasets cause "tunnel vision" — missing the cross-scenario causal chains that actually drive human decisions. • Identifies a fundamental "positive average person" bias in LLMs: hyper-activity, persona homogenization, and a Utopian bias that erases individual differences and long-tail behaviors.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者构建了OmniBehavior基准，完全来源于真实世界行为日志，整合了三个关键维度：长时域（扩展的时间序列）、跨场景（跨多个领域/情境的行为）和异构性（多样化的动作类型和用户画像）。随后在该基准上评估多个主流LLM，要求其根据历史上下文预测或模拟用户行为。最后，对LLM模拟行为与真实人类行为进行系统性结构比较，以识别模拟误差的性质和来源。 The authors construct OmniBehavior, a benchmark sourced entirely from real-world behavioral logs that integrates three key dimensions: long-horizon (extended temporal sequences), cross-scenario (behaviors spanning multiple domains/contexts), and heterogeneous (diverse action types and user profiles). They then evaluate a suite of state-of-the-art LLMs on this benchmark by asking them to predict or simulate user behavior given historical context. Finally, they perform a systematic structural comparison between LLM-simulated behaviors and authentic human behaviors to identify the nature and sources of simulation errors.
