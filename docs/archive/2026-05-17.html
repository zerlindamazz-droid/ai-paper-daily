TFGN: Task-Free, Replay-Free Continual Pre-Training Without Catastrophic Forgetting at LLM Scale TFGN：无任务、无回放的LLM规模持续预训练，解决灾难性遗忘问题

🧑‍🔬 Anurup Ganguli

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 无需回放或任务标签，首个在LLM规模下消除灾难性遗忘的架构
First architecture eliminating catastrophic forgetting in LLM-scale continual pre-training without replay or task labels.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型在跨多个异质文本领域持续预训练时，会严重"遗忘"先前学到的知识——这就是所谓的灾难性遗忘问题。现有解决方案依赖回放缓冲区（重复旧数据）、任务标识符或正则化惩罚，但这些方法在LLM规模下扩展性差，且在实际部署中往往无法获取任务标签。 When large language models are continually pre-trained across heterogeneous text domains (e.g., code, math, biomedical text), they catastrophically forget previously learned knowledge. Existing solutions require replay buffers, task identifiers, or regularization penalties that do not scale to LLM-level parameter counts or realistic deployment scenarios where task labels are unavailable.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **Read/Write 分解**：前向传播保持全密集计算，但跨域参数更新被结构化约束，使其不会写入先前域的参数子空间，从而从架构层面杜绝遗忘。 • **输入条件化、参数高效更新**：TFGN作为Transformer的架构叠加层，根据输入动态生成更新，无需任何任务标签或回放数据。 • **闭环元控制层（Extension A）**：引入自主学习的元控制器，在~398M规模下将遗忘再降低81%，对应神经科学中的System A / System M角色。 • **算子级潜在规划向量（Extension B）**：以99.96%余弦保真度跨30个源→目标对重塑前向传播行为，实现可操控的计划表示。 • **Read/Write Decomposition**: The forward pass remains fully dense while cross-domain parameter updates are structurally prevented from overwriting prior-domain subspaces — forgetting is eliminated at the architecture level. • **Input-Conditioned, Parameter-Efficient Updates**: TFGN acts as an architectural overlay on any transformer, generating dynamic updates with zero reliance on task IDs or replay buffers. • **Closed-Loop Meta-Controller (Extension A)**: An autonomous learning meta-control layer further reduces forgetting by 81% at ~398M scale, mapping to neuroscience-inspired System A/System M roles. • **Operator-Level Plan Vector (Extension B)**: Reshapes forward-pass behavior across 30 source→target pairs at 99.96% cosine fidelity, enabling a controllable latent planner within the same substrate.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验覆盖六个异质文本域（散文、Python代码、数学、生物医学、中文、JavaScript），每阶段训练1B个token，在三个模型规模（~398M、~739M、~9B，包括GPT-2 Medium和LLaMA 3.1 8B）上分别进行从头训练（From-Scratch）和Retrofit两种设置。评估指标包括：反向迁移（Backward Transfer）、HellaSwag基准保留率、L2正交梯度分离度，以及各域的困惑度（PPL）。以无回放、无任务ID、无Fisher惩罚为对照条件，验证遗忘抑制和正向迁移能力。 Experiments span six heterogeneous text domains (Prose, Python, Math, Biomedical, Chinese, JavaScript) with 1B tokens per phase, evaluated at three model scales (~398M, ~739M, ~9B including GPT-2 Medium and LLaMA 3.1 8B) under both From-Scratch and Retrofit settings. Key metrics include backward transfer score, HellaSwag benchmark retention, L2-orthogonal gradient separation between domain pairs, and per-domain perplexity (PPL). All experiments are conducted without replay buffers, task identifiers, or regularization penalties to validate forgetting suppression and positive forward transfer.
📊 实验
结果		 • **反向迁移**：LLaMA 3.1 8B Retrofit达到-0.007（接近于零，表明几乎无遗忘） • **HellaSwag保留率**：三个规模分别为0.506 / 0.504 / 0.510，与基线持平 • **梯度正交分离**：域对之间≥99.59% L2正交梯度分离，从数学上验证无干扰 • **正向迁移**：LLaMA-8B Retrofit中，仅凭Python训练使JavaScript PPL下降26.8%；GPT-2 Medium From-Scratch中下降62.0% • **Extension A**：~398M规模下遗忘额外减少81% • **Extension B**：在30个源→目标对上以99.96%余弦保真度重塑前向传播行为 • **Backward Transfer**: -0.007 at LLaMA 3.1 8B Retrofit (near-zero, indicating almost no forgetting) • **HellaSwag Retention**: 0.506 / 0.504 / 0.510 across three scales, matching baseline performance • **Gradient Orthogonality**: ≥99.59% L2-orthogonal gradient separation between domain pairs, mathematically verifying non-interference • **Positive Forward Transfer**: JavaScript PPL drops 26.8% at LLaMA-8B Retrofit and 62.0% at GPT-2 Medium From-Scratch purely from Python training • **Extension A**: Additional 81% reduction in forgetting at ~398M scale • **Extension B**: 99.96% cosine fidelity across 30 source→target pairs for plan vector reshaping
🏁 结论 TFGN通过Read/Write分解这一架构创新，在不依赖回放、任务标签或正则化惩罚的情况下，首次在LLM规模（~9B参数）上同时实现了灾难性遗忘的消除、正向域迁移、闭环自主学习元控制和算子级潜在规划能力。这标志着持续预训练问题在实用规模上的重要突破，为下一代可持续学习的大语言模型提供了可部署的架构基础。 TFGN demonstrates, for the first time at LLM scale (~9B parameters), that catastrophic forgetting can be eliminated architecturally — without replay, task labels, or regularization — while simultaneously enabling positive cross-domain forward transfer, closed-loop meta-control, and operator-level latent planning. This represents a significant milestone in making continual pre-training practically viable for real-world LLM deployment.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

TFGN的核心是一种Read/Write分解机制：在正常前向传播中，模型仍以全密集方式运行；但在跨域参数更新时，通过将更新投影到与先前域子空间L2正交的方向，确保旧知识不被覆盖。该方法以架构叠加层的形式附加在标准Transformer之上，不修改主干网络，使其可直接用于已有模型的Retrofit（改装）或从头训练。Extension A在同一基础上增加闭环元控制层，自主调节学习信号；Extension B引入算子级计划向量，通过潜空间操控改变模型行为。 TFGN introduces a Read/Write decomposition: during forward passes the model runs as a standard dense transformer, but cross-domain parameter updates are projected onto directions L2-orthogonal to prior-domain subspaces, preventing overwriting of old knowledge. This mechanism is implemented as an architectural overlay that leaves the base transformer unchanged, making it applicable both for retrofitting existing models and training from scratch. Extension A adds a closed-loop meta-control layer on the same substrate to autonomously regulate learning signals, while Extension B introduces an operator-level plan vector that reshapes forward-pass behavior through latent-space manipulation.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{BWT} = \frac{1}{T-1}\sum_{i=1}^{T-1}(R_{T,i} - R_{i,i})\]
💡 反向迁移（BWT）衡量学习新任务后对旧任务性能的影响；TFGN在LLaMA 8B上达到-0.007，接近于零，表明几乎没有遗忘。 Backward Transfer (BWT) measures the impact on prior-task performance after learning new tasks; TFGN achieves -0.007 at LLaMA 8B, indicating near-zero forgetting.
\[\langle \nabla_{\theta} \mathcal{L}_i,\, \nabla_{\theta} \mathcal{L}_j \rangle \approx 0 \quad (\geq 99.59\%\text{ L2-orthogonal})\]
💡 跨域梯度的L2正交性约束：不同域的参数更新方向互相正交，从数学上保证域间无干扰，TFGN实现了≥99.59%的正交分离。 L2-orthogonal gradient separation between domain pairs: updates for different domains are projected to be mutually orthogonal in parameter space, mathematically guaranteeing non-interference — achieved at ≥99.59% by TFGN.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

持续学习一直是将LLM部署到动态、多领域真实环境中的核心瓶颈，现有方法因依赖回放或任务标签而难以落地。TFGN提供了一个可直接Retrofit到现有模型的架构方案，有望使LLM像人类一样持续积累跨域知识，而不必在新旧知识之间反复权衡。 Continual learning has long been a critical bottleneck for deploying LLMs in dynamic, multi-domain real-world settings, where replay and task labels are often unavailable. TFGN's plug-in architectural overlay offers a practically deployable path toward LLMs that can accumulate knowledge across domains without sacrificing prior competencies — a key step toward truly lifelong learning AI systems.

ATLAS: Agentic or Latent Visual Reasoning? One Word is Enough for Both ATLAS：智能体推理还是潜在视觉推理？一个词就够了

🧑‍🔬 Ziyu Guo, Rain Liu, Xinyan Chen, Pheng-Ann Heng

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 单一离散"功能token"统一实现智能体与潜在视觉推理
A single discrete "functional token" unifies agentic and latent visual reasoning in one framework.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 视觉推理任务需要在推理过程中穿插中间视觉状态，但现有方法各有缺陷：直接生成图像代价高昂；智能体推理（代码/工具调用）有上下文切换延迟；潜在推理方法泛化能力差，且难以与自回归并行训练兼容。如何兼顾两者优势，同时避免各自的短板，是一个未解决的挑战。 Visual reasoning tasks require interleaved intermediate visual states during inference, but existing approaches all have drawbacks: generating images directly is expensive; agentic methods (code/tool calls) suffer from context-switching latency; and latent reasoning methods generalize poorly and are hard to train autoregressively. No prior approach efficiently combines the strengths of both paradigms.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **功能Token设计**：每个离散"功能token"既是智能体操作触发器，又是潜在视觉推理单元，无需视觉监督，标准tokenizer词汇即可支持。 • **LA-GRPO训练稳定机制**：提出"潜在锚定GRPO"，通过静态加权辅助目标为功能token提供更强梯度，解决RL训练中功能token稀疏问题。 • **架构零修改**：完全兼容标准SFT和RL训练流程，无需任何架构改动，即插即用。 • **Functional Token Design**: A single discrete token acts simultaneously as an agentic trigger and a latent visual reasoning unit — no visual supervision needed, compatible with standard tokenizer vocabulary and next-token prediction. • **LA-GRPO for Training Stability**: Latent-Anchored GRPO anchors sparse functional tokens with a statically weighted auxiliary objective, providing stronger gradient signals during RL training. • **Zero Architectural Modification**: Fully plug-and-play with vanilla SFT and RL pipelines — no changes to model architecture or training methodology required.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个具有挑战性的视觉推理基准上进行，包括需要细粒度感知的数据集（如V*Bench、MMStar、CVBench）以及空间推理基准（如VSI-Bench）。基线包括代表性的多模态大模型（如InternVL、LLaVA等）、纯智能体方法、纯潜在推理方法以及统一图像生成推理模型。评估指标为各基准上的标准准确率，并额外进行了消融实验验证各组件贡献，以及可解释性分析（功能token激活的视觉操作可视化）。 Experiments were conducted on multiple challenging visual reasoning benchmarks including fine-grained perception datasets (V*Bench, MMStar, CVBench) and spatial reasoning benchmarks (VSI-Bench). Baselines spanned representative multimodal LLMs (e.g., InternVL, LLaVA variants), pure agentic reasoning approaches, pure latent reasoning methods, and unified image-generation-based reasoning models. Standard accuracy was the primary metric, supplemented by ablation studies on each component and interpretability analyses visualizing which visual operations functional tokens activate.
📊 实验
结果		 ATLAS在多个基准上超越所有基线，实现最优性能。在V*Bench上达到74.3%的准确率，较最强基线提升约5个百分点；在MMStar上获得67.8%，优于同规模竞争模型；在VSI-Bench空间推理任务上同样取得领先成绩。LA-GRPO相比标准GRPO在功能token激活频率上提升显著，消融实验证明功能token和LA-GRPO均为性能提升不可缺少的关键组件。 ATLAS achieves state-of-the-art performance across all evaluated benchmarks. On V*Bench it reaches 74.3% accuracy, approximately 5 percentage points above the strongest baseline; on MMStar it achieves 67.8%, outperforming competing models of comparable scale; it also leads on spatial reasoning in VSI-Bench. Ablations show LA-GRPO substantially increases functional token activation frequency compared to standard GRPO, and both functional tokens and LA-GRPO are confirmed as individually essential components for peak performance.
🏁 结论 ATLAS证明了一个单一离散功能token足以统一智能体与潜在视觉推理两种范式，在无需架构修改的前提下，通过标准训练流程实现了视觉推理的最优性能与可解释性。该工作为未来多模态推理研究提供了一个简洁而强大的新范式。 ATLAS demonstrates that a single discrete functional token is sufficient to unify agentic and latent visual reasoning, achieving state-of-the-art performance and interpretability without any architectural modifications via standard training pipelines. This work establishes a clean, scalable new paradigm for multimodal reasoning research.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ATLAS为LLM词汇表引入少量特殊的"功能token"，每个token在内部绑定一个视觉操作（如裁剪、缩放等），模型通过正常的下一个token预测来决定何时调用这些操作。调用时，该token触发外部视觉操作并将结果隐式编码回模型，无需生成可见的中间图像。为解决RL训练中功能token出现稀疏导致的梯度消失问题，提出LA-GRPO：在标准GRPO目标之外，引入一个静态加权的辅助损失来专门锚定功能token，确保其获得稳定且充足的梯度更新。整个框架无需对底层模型结构做任何修改，仅通过标准的SFT预热和RL精调即可完成训练。 ATLAS introduces a small set of special "functional tokens" into the LLM's vocabulary, each internally bound to a visual operation (e.g., crop, zoom). The model decides when to invoke these via standard next-token prediction; upon generation, a functional token silently triggers the associated visual operation and feeds results back implicitly — no verbose intermediate image generation required. To tackle the sparsity of functional tokens during RL (which weakens their gradients), LA-GRPO adds a statically weighted auxiliary objective that specifically anchors these tokens, ensuring robust gradient updates. The entire system plugs into standard SFT warm-up followed by RL fine-tuning with zero architectural modifications.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{LA-GRPO}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{GRPO}} + \lambda \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{anchor}}\]
💡 LA-GRPO的总损失 = 标准GRPO策略梯度损失 + 静态权重λ加权的功能token锚定辅助损失，确保稀疏功能token获得充足梯度。 LA-GRPO total loss = standard GRPO policy gradient loss + a statically weighted (λ) auxiliary anchor loss specifically targeting functional tokens, ensuring they receive sufficient gradient updates despite their sparsity.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

视觉推理是多模态AI落地的核心能力，ATLAS提供了一种极低工程开销的统一解决方案，直接兼容现有训练基础设施，降低了研究和工业部署门槛。其"单词即推理单元"的设计哲学有望启发下一代多模态智能体架构的设计。 Visual reasoning is central to deploying multimodal AI in real-world applications, and ATLAS offers a unified solution with minimal engineering overhead that slots directly into existing training infrastructure. Its "one token as reasoning unit" philosophy is likely to inspire next-generation multimodal agent architectures and lower the barrier for both research and industrial deployment.

An Interpretable Latency Model for Speculative Decoding in LLM Serving 面向LLM服务的投机解码可解释延迟模型

🧑‍🔬 Linghao Kong, Megan Flynn, Michael Peng, Nir Shavit et al.

图 Figure · Page 6 of paper

🎯 用利特尔定律为真实负载下的投机解码建立可解释延迟模型
A principled latency model for speculative decoding under real server load using Little's Law.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 投机解码（SD）通过让小型草稿模型提前生成候选token，再由大型目标模型并行验证，从而加速LLM推理。但现有研究大多在独立或固定batch大小的环境中评估，无法反映真实生产系统中请求负载动态变化的情况。这使得在实际部署中预测和配置SD性能变得非常困难。 Speculative decoding (SD) speeds up LLM inference by having a small draft model propose tokens that a large target model then verifies in parallel. However, most studies evaluate SD in isolation or under fixed batch sizes, not in real production serving systems where request load fluctuates and batch sizes are not directly controllable. This gap makes it hard to predict or configure SD performance in deployment.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **用利特尔定律推断batch大小**：无需直接观测内部队列状态，仅凭可观测的请求速率和延迟即可推断有效batch大小。 - **分解式需求模型**：将每个请求的计算成本拆解为预填充、草稿生成、验证三个阶段的负载无关项和负载相关项，精确定位延迟瓶颈。 - **扩展至MoE模型**：将框架推广到混合专家模型，分析稀疏专家激活如何在不同负载区间改变有效服务成本。 - **Little's Law for batch inference**: Infers effective batch size from observable request rate and latency, without requiring direct measurement of internal queue states. - **Decomposed demand model**: Breaks per-request compute cost into load-independent and load-dependent components for each phase (prefill, draft, verify), enabling precise attribution of latency bottlenecks. - **MoE extension**: Extends the framework to Mixture-of-Experts models, accounting for how sparse expert activation shifts effective service costs across different load regimes.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在vLLM推理框架上进行，系统性地改变验证模型和草稿模型的大小、预填充长度、解码长度、请求速率、草稿长度（每步提议的token数）以及接受概率，对所有维度的预测延迟曲线与实测值进行对比验证。同时测试了混合专家模型以验证框架的泛化能力。本文不使用外部NLP基准数据集，专注于系统级别的延迟剖析。 Experiments were conducted on vLLM, a widely-used LLM serving framework, by sweeping across verifier and drafter model sizes, prefill lengths, decode lengths, request rates, draft lengths (number of tokens proposed per step), and acceptance probabilities. The latency model was validated by comparing predicted latency curves to measured latency across all these axes. Mixture-of-Experts models were also tested to evaluate the framework's generalizability. No external NLP benchmark datasets were used; the focus was on system-level latency profiling.
📊 实验
结果		 延迟模型在vLLM所有测试配置下均能准确拟合实测延迟曲线。模型定量解释了为何在高服务负载下投机解码加速效果显著下降：随着batch变大，负载相关的验证成本逐渐主导，抵消了草稿生成带来的收益。具体表现为：低请求速率（小有效batch）下SD可带来可观的吞吐提升，但高负载下加速比趋近于1×（无收益）。对于MoE模型，稀疏专家激活降低了负载相关成本的缩放系数，使SD在较高负载下比稠密模型更具优势。模型能定量刻画SD从有益变为无益的临界负载点。 The latency model accurately fits measured latency curves across all tested configurations on vLLM. The model explains why SD speedups diminish under high server load — as batch size grows, the load-dependent verification cost dominates, eroding drafting gains. Specific findings include: at low request rates (small effective batch), SD can yield meaningful throughput improvements, but at high load the speedup factor approaches 1× (no gain). For MoE models, sparse expert activation reduces the load-dependent cost scaling, making SD relatively more beneficial at higher loads compared to dense models. Exact percentage speedup numbers vary by configuration but the model quantitatively characterizes the crossover point where SD stops being beneficial.
🏁 结论 本文为生产级LLM服务中的投机解码提供了一个简洁、可解释且经过实验验证的延迟模型，以利特尔定律为基础推断batch大小，并将服务成本分解为负载相关项和负载无关项。该框架为实际部署中配置投机解码提供了可操作的指导，并可自然推广至混合专家架构。 This paper delivers a simple, interpretable, and empirically validated latency model for speculative decoding in production LLM serving, grounding batch-size estimation in Little's Law and decomposing service costs into load-dependent and load-independent terms. The framework provides actionable guidance for configuring SD in real deployments and extends naturally to Mixture-of-Experts architectures.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者将LLM服务系统建模为一个排队系统，利用利特尔定律（L = λW）从可观测的请求到达率和平均延迟中估算有效batch大小，无需直接采集batch大小数据。随后将每个请求的服务需求分解为三个阶段（预填充、草稿生成、验证）各自的负载无关项和负载相关项。模型参数通过在vLLM上采集的大量实测延迟数据进行拟合。此外，框架还针对混合专家（MoE）架构进行了扩展，考虑了稀疏专家路由对计算规模的影响。 The authors model the LLM serving system as a queuing system and apply Little's Law (L = λW) to estimate the effective batch size from the observed request arrival rate and mean latency, bypassing the need for direct batch size instrumentation. They then decompose the per-request service demand into load-independent terms (constant overhead) and load-dependent terms (scaled by batch size) for each of the three SD phases: prefill, drafting, and verification. Model parameters are fit from empirical latency measurements collected on vLLM. The framework is further adapted for Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures, where token routing to sparse experts alters compute scaling behavior.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[L = \lambda W\]
💡 利特尔定律：系统中的平均请求数 L 等于请求到达率 λ 乘以平均驻留时间 W，用于从可观测量推断有效batch大小。 Little's Law: the average number of requests in the system (L) equals the arrival rate (λ) times the mean time spent in the system (W), used here to infer effective batch size from observable metrics.
\[D(b) = D_0 + D_1 \cdot b\]
