GLM-5V-Turbo: Toward a Native Foundation Model for Multimodal Agents GLM-5V-Turbo：面向多模态智能体的原生基础模型

🧑‍🔬 GLM-V Team, :, Wenyi Hong, Xiaotao Gu et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 原生融合多模态感知与推理规划的智能体基础模型
GLM-5V-Turbo integrates multimodal perception natively into reasoning and agent execution, not just as an add-on.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 目前大多数基础模型把视觉能力当作语言模型的"附加插件"，而非核心组成部分，导致模型在处理图像、GUI界面、文档、视频等真实复杂环境时推理能力受限。AI智能体在现实中需要跨模态感知与执行，但现有架构很难做到原生的多模态融合。如何让多模态感知与语言推理、规划、工具调用无缝结合，是核心挑战。 Most current foundation models treat vision as a bolt-on feature added to a language backbone, which limits their ability to truly reason over images, GUIs, documents, and videos in real-world agentic settings. As AI agents are deployed in complex environments, they need to seamlessly perceive and act across heterogeneous modalities — something existing architectures struggle to do natively. The challenge is building a model where multimodal understanding is a first-class citizen alongside language reasoning, planning, and tool use.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **原生多模态架构**：多模态感知被设计为推理与规划的核心组件，而非辅助接口，实现真正的跨模态智能体行为。 • **层次化优化策略**：采用多模态预训练→监督微调→强化学习的分阶段训练流程，逐步提升智能体能力。 • **工具链与框架扩展**：与智能体框架深度集成，支持视觉工具调用、多模态代码生成和端到端GUI交互。 • **可靠的端到端验证**：强调可验证的智能体流水线，确保复杂多步骤任务的输出可被有效校验。 • **Native Multimodal Integration**: Multimodal perception is architected as a core component of reasoning and planning — not an auxiliary interface — enabling true cross-modal agent behavior. • **Hierarchical Optimization**: A staged training pipeline combining multimodal pre-training, supervised fine-tuning, and reinforcement learning is used to progressively sharpen agent capabilities. • **Toolchain & Framework Expansion**: The model is tightly integrated with agent frameworks and extended tool libraries, supporting visual tool use, multimodal coding, and end-to-end GUI interaction. • **Reliable End-to-End Verification**: Emphasis on verifiable agent pipelines ensures that outputs can be validated across complex multi-step agentic tasks.
🧪 实验
内容		 GLM-5V-Turbo在覆盖GUI交互、视觉工具调用、多模态代码生成和框架型智能体任务的多个基准上进行了评测。基线模型包括GPT-4o、Claude、Gemini等前沿闭源模型及主流开源多模态模型。评测同时涵盖纯文本编程基准（验证无性能退化）以及专为智能体场景设计的多模态基准，包括网页导航和文档理解任务。 GLM-5V-Turbo was evaluated on a range of multimodal agent benchmarks covering GUI interaction, visual tool use, multimodal coding, and framework-based agentic tasks. Baselines include leading frontier models such as GPT-4o, Claude, and Gemini variants, as well as open-source multimodal models. Evaluations span both text-only coding benchmarks (to ensure no regression) and multimodal benchmarks specifically designed for agentic settings, including web navigation and document understanding tasks.
📊 实验
结果		 作为技术报告，GLM-5V-Turbo在多模态代码生成、视觉工具调用和框架型智能体任务中表现优异，在多个智能体基准上与GPT-4o级别模型持平甚至更优。关键亮点是纯文本编程得分未出现下滑，证明原生多模态融合不会损伤语言能力。在GUI智能体评测和多模态框架任务中均取得领先成绩，模型定位为可实际部署的系统而非研究原型。（具体数字未在摘要中披露，详见正文基准表格。） While the paper is a technical report without exhaustive ablation tables, GLM-5V-Turbo is reported to achieve strong performance in multimodal coding, visual tool use, and framework-based agentic tasks, competitive with or surpassing GPT-4o-level models on several agent benchmarks. Importantly, it retains competitive text-only coding scores, demonstrating that native multimodal integration does not degrade language-only capabilities. Specific benchmark results include top-tier performance on GUI agent evaluations and multimodal agentic framework tasks, with the model positioned as a practical deployment-ready system rather than a research prototype.
🏁 结论 GLM-5V-Turbo证明，将多模态感知原生融入基础模型核心（而非附加模块）能显著增强GUI交互、代码生成和工具调用等智能体能力。本文为构建多模态智能体确立了实践原则：多模态感知优先、层次化优化、端到端可验证流水线。 GLM-5V-Turbo demonstrates that natively integrating multimodal perception into the core of a foundation model — rather than appending it as an auxiliary module — yields significantly stronger agentic capabilities across GUI, coding, and tool-use tasks. The work establishes practical principles for building multimodal agents: multimodal perception as a first-class component, hierarchical optimization, and end-to-end verifiable pipelines.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

GLM-5V-Turbo将多模态感知（图像、视频、GUI、文档、网页）直接嵌入模型的推理与规划模块，而非作为上游预处理器。训练流程采用层次化优化：首先通过大规模多模态预训练建立感知基础，再用监督微调对齐智能体任务，最后用强化学习强化在智能体场景下的决策能力。工具链扩展加入视觉工具支持，并与智能体执行框架深度集成，支持任务完成的端到端验证。代码生成、规划、工具调用与GUI交互均在统一的多模态上下文中运行。 GLM-5V-Turbo is designed so that multimodal perception — processing images, videos, GUIs, documents, and webpages — is embedded directly into the model's reasoning and planning modules rather than treated as an upstream preprocessor. The training pipeline follows a hierarchical optimization strategy: first, broad multimodal pre-training builds perceptual grounding; then supervised fine-tuning aligns the model to agent tasks; finally, reinforcement learning sharpens decision-making in agentic contexts. The toolchain is expanded to include visual tools, and the model is integrated with agent execution frameworks that support end-to-end verification of task completion. This design ensures that coding, planning, tool invocation, and GUI interaction all operate within a unified multimodal context.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着AI智能体从实验室走向现实世界——操控浏览器、在视觉上下文中编写代码、与GUI交互——多模态感知的架构集成方式变得至关重要。GLM-5V-Turbo提供了一个已落地的具体案例和可复现的方法论，证明原生多模态设计在智能体任务上优于主流的"视觉插件"范式。 As AI agents move from controlled benchmarks into real-world deployment — navigating browsers, writing code with visual context, operating GUIs — the architectural choice of how multimodal perception is integrated becomes critical. GLM-5V-Turbo offers a concrete, deployed example and a replicable recipe showing that native multimodal design outperforms the prevailing "vision as add-on" paradigm for agentic tasks.

MotionBricks: Scalable Real-Time Motions with Modular Latent Generative Model and Smart Primitives MotionBricks：基于模块化潜在生成模型与智能原语的可扩展实时动作控制

🧑‍🔬 Tingwu Wang, Olivier Dionne, Michael De Ruyter, David Minor et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 模块化生成框架实现35万+动作实时统一控制
A real-time modular generative framework unifying 350K+ motion clips with plug-and-play smart primitives for animation and robotics.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 生成式动作合成技术虽然发展迅速，但游戏和机器人中的实时交互动作控制仍依赖传统手工方法。核心问题有两个：一是生成模型在实时计算约束下质量和可扩展性严重下降；二是现有文本/标签驱动模型无法满足生产环境所需的细粒度多模态控制（速度指令、风格选择、精确关键帧）。 Generative motion synthesis has made great strides, but real-time interactive motion control in games and robotics is still dominated by hand-crafted traditional methods. Two core gaps exist: (1) generative models degrade badly in quality and scale under real-time compute budgets, and (2) existing text/tag-driven models can't handle the fine-grained multi-modal control (velocity, style, keyframes) that production applications need.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 模块化潜在生成骨干网络：单一模型支持35万+动作片段，通过模块化潜在编码分解动作，实现高质量实时生成。 • 智能原语（Smart Primitives）：统一的即插即用接口，将导航和物体交互的动作创作抽象化，无需动画专业知识。 • 极致实时效率：实现15,000 FPS吞吐量、2ms延迟，同时适用于游戏制作流水线和物理人形机器人控制。 • Modular Latent Generative Backbone: A single model scales to 350,000+ motion clips by decomposing motions into modular latent codes, enabling robust real-time generation. • Smart Primitives: A unified plug-and-play interface abstracting navigation and object interaction authoring — no expert animation knowledge required. • Extreme Real-Time Efficiency: Achieves 15,000 FPS throughput at 2ms latency, making it feasible for both game production pipelines and physical humanoid robots.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在开源动作数据集（如HumanML3D、基于AMASS的划分）和超过35万条片段的大规模私有数据集上进行。基线模型包括当前最先进的生成式动作模型。评估指标涵盖动作质量（FID、多样性、自然度）、实时性能（FPS、延迟）和可控性。同时构建了涵盖多种风格的完整生产级动画演示，并将模型部署到Unitree G1人形机器人上进行实时机器人控制评估。 Experiments were conducted on both open-source motion datasets (e.g., HumanML3D, AMASS-based splits) and large-scale proprietary datasets with over 350,000 clips. Baselines include state-of-the-art generative motion models. Evaluation metrics cover motion quality (FID, diversity, naturalness), real-time performance (FPS, latency), and controllability. A full production-level animation demo covering navigation and object-scene interaction across multiple styles was constructed. The model was also deployed on a Unitree G1 humanoid robot for real-time robotic control evaluation.
📊 实验
结果		 MotionBricks在开源及私有数据集上均达到最先进的动作质量。实时吞吐量达到15,000 FPS，延迟仅2ms，比此前生成方法快数个数量级。单一统一模型成功覆盖超过35万条多样化动作片段而无质量下降，在FID和自然度等指标上超越基线生成模型。 MotionBricks achieves state-of-the-art motion quality on both open-source and proprietary datasets of various scales. It reaches a real-time throughput of 15,000 FPS with only 2ms latency — orders of magnitude faster than prior generative methods. It successfully models a single unified model over 350,000 diverse motion clips without quality degradation, outperforming baseline generative models on FID and naturalness metrics across benchmarks.
🏁 结论 MotionBricks证明，单一模块化潜在生成模型能够统一海量动作数据集，同时满足实时性能与细粒度多模态控制需求，从而弥合了研究级生成合成与生产级交互动画及机器人控制之间的鸿沟。 MotionBricks proves that a single modular latent generative model can unify massive motion datasets with real-time performance and fine-grained multi-modal control, closing the gap between research-grade generative synthesis and production-level interactive animation and robotics.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

MotionBricks由两个核心组件构成。第一，模块化潜在生成骨干网络将超过35万条动作片段压缩为结构化的模块化潜在表示，使单一模型能够无损地处理多样化动作技能。第二，智能原语提供高层统一控制接口，同时接收速度指令、风格标签和关键帧约束，并将其转换为骨干网络的潜在查询。整个系统采用"积木拼装"的即插即用设计，将内容创作与模型内部解耦。最后，同一训练模型被部署到Unitree G1人形机器人上，验证其真实场景的泛化能力。 MotionBricks consists of two main components. First, a modular latent generative backbone compresses a massive dataset of over 350,000 motion clips into structured latent representations using a modular design, allowing a single model to handle diverse motion skills without quality collapse at scale. Second, smart primitives provide a high-level, unified control interface that accepts velocity commands, style tags, and keyframe constraints simultaneously, translating them into latent queries for the backbone. The system is designed so that new application behaviors can be assembled in a "brick-by-brick" plug-and-play fashion, decoupling content creation from model internals. Finally, the same trained model is deployed on a Unitree G1 humanoid robot to validate real-world generalization.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{FPS} = 15{,}000, \quad \text{Latency} = 2\text{ms}\]
💡 MotionBricks的实时性能指标：每秒生成15,000帧动作，单次推理延迟仅2毫秒。 MotionBricks real-time performance: generates 15,000 frames per second with only 2ms per inference, enabling true interactive real-time use.
\[\mathcal{M} = f_{\theta}(z_1, z_2, \ldots, z_K), \quad z_k \in \mathcal{Z}_{\text{modular}}\]
💡 模块化潜在生成模型：动作序列M由K个模块化潜在码z_k通过参数为θ的网络生成，每个潜在码对应一个可复用的动作模块。 The modular latent generative backbone: a motion sequence M is generated by composing K modular latent codes z_k, each representing a reusable motion module, decoded by a network with parameters θ.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本工作直接突破了生成式动作模型长期面临的生产落地瓶颈，证明其在质量和速度上均可与传统动画流水线媲美乃至超越。将同一模型部署于人形机器人，还打通了动画研究与真实世界机器人控制之间的壁垒，为动作智能提供了统一路径。 This work directly addresses the long-standing production barrier for generative motion models, showing they can finally compete with — and surpass — classical animation pipelines on both quality and speed. By deploying the same model on a humanoid robot, it also bridges the gap between animation research and real-world robotics, opening a unified path for motion intelligence.

World-R1: Reinforcing 3D Constraints for Text-to-Video Generation World-R1：通过强化学习约束文本生成视频中的三维一致性

🧑‍🔬 Weijie Wang, Xiaoxuan He, Youping Gu, Yifan Yang et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用强化学习让文生视频模型获得真实三维几何一致性
RL-based framework enforces 3D geometric consistency in text-to-video generation without architectural changes.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 文生视频模型虽然视觉效果惊艳，但经常出现三维几何错误——物体变形、深度不一致、场景结构跨帧不连贯。现有的修复方法需要大改模型架构，成本高、扩展性差。如何在不改变底层模型的前提下，让生成的视频符合真实的三维物理规律，是一个亟待解决的问题。 Text-to-video models produce visually impressive results but routinely violate basic 3D geometry — objects warp, depth is inconsistent, and scenes fail to hold structural coherence across frames. Existing fixes require expensive architectural overhauls that hurt scalability. There is a need for a lightweight, scalable method to bring physical 3D plausibility into video generation.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **零架构修改**：通过Flow-GRPO强化学习微调注入三维一致性约束，底层模型结构完全不变，扩展性强。 • **三维基础模型作为奖励信号**：利用预训练深度估计器和视觉语言模型提供反馈，无需昂贵的三维标注数据。 • **周期性解耦训练策略**：交替优化几何刚性约束与场景动态流畅性，避免联合训练时两者相互干扰的常见问题。 • **No architectural changes**: 3D consistency is injected purely via RL fine-tuning (Flow-GRPO), keeping the base model intact and scalable. • **Feedback from 3D foundation models**: Pre-trained depth estimators and vision-language models serve as reward signals, eliminating the need for costly labeled 3D data. • **Periodic decoupled training**: A novel training schedule alternates between enforcing rigid geometric constraints and preserving natural scene dynamics, avoiding the trade-off collapse common in joint training.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在标准视频生成基准上评估World-R1，包括专注几何一致性的指标和通用视觉质量指标。基线模型包括未经三维微调的底层视频基础模型以及其他注入三维先验的方法。评估手段包括预训练三维模型输出的深度一致性、结构连贯性自动指标，以及视觉语言模型评分和人类偏好研究。 The authors evaluate World-R1 on standard video generation benchmarks, including geometry-focused metrics and general visual quality metrics. Baselines include the underlying video foundation model (without 3D fine-tuning) and other methods that inject 3D priors. Evaluation uses automated metrics from pre-trained 3D models (depth consistency, structural coherence) and visual quality scores from VLMs, alongside human preference studies.
📊 实验
结果		 与底层视频基础模型相比，World-R1在三维一致性指标上取得显著提升，同时保持了有竞争力的视觉质量分数。周期性解耦训练有效恢复了纯几何优化会牺牲的视觉流畅性。论文报告的具体结果显示，在深度一致性和结构连贯性基准上相比基线有明显的指标提升，而基于VLM的视觉质量评分与原始模型持平甚至更优。 World-R1 significantly enhances 3D consistency metrics compared to the base video foundation model while maintaining competitive visual quality scores. The periodic decoupled training recovers visual fluidity that would otherwise be sacrificed by pure geometry optimization. Specific reported improvements include notable gains on depth consistency and structural coherence benchmarks; exact figures from the paper indicate meaningful margin improvements over baselines on 3D evaluation metrics while VLM-based quality scores remain on par with or exceed the original model.
🏁 结论 World-R1证明了强化学习在不修改任何模型架构的前提下，是为视频生成模型注入三维几何一致性的可扩展有效途径。本文实质性地缩小了当前视频合成能力与物理世界模拟需求之间的差距。 World-R1 demonstrates that reinforcement learning — without any architectural modification — is a scalable and effective pathway to instill 3D geometric consistency into video generation models. This work meaningfully bridges the gap between current video synthesis and the requirements of physically grounded world simulation.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

World-R1将三维一致性视为对齐问题，采用强化学习来解决。具体来说，使用Flow-GRPO（一种适配流匹配视频扩散模型的GRPO变体）对视频生成器进行微调，奖励信号来自预训练的三维基础模型（如深度估计器）和评估结构连贯性的视觉语言模型。同时，研究团队精心构建了一个专注于世界模拟场景的纯文本数据集，引导模型生成物理上合理的内容。最后，采用周期性解耦训练策略，交替优化几何一致性目标与视觉流畅性目标，防止两者相互损害。 World-R1 treats 3D consistency as an alignment problem and solves it with reinforcement learning. Specifically, it adopts Flow-GRPO — a variant of Group Relative Policy Optimization adapted for flow-matching video diffusion models — to fine-tune the video generator using reward signals from pre-trained 3D foundation models (e.g., depth estimators) and vision-language models that evaluate structural coherence. A specialized pure-text dataset focused on world simulation scenarios is curated to guide the model toward physically plausible content. Finally, a periodic decoupled training strategy alternates optimization between geometric consistency objectives and visual fluidity objectives, preventing each from degrading the other.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{R} = \lambda_1 \mathcal{R}_{\text{depth}} + \lambda_2 \mathcal{R}_{\text{VLM}}\]
💡 总奖励函数由深度一致性奖励和视觉语言模型质量奖励加权组合而成，分别用λ₁和λ₂平衡两者权重。 The total reward is a weighted combination of a depth consistency reward (from a 3D foundation model) and a visual quality reward (from a VLM), balanced by weights λ₁ and λ₂.
\[\theta^* = \arg\max_{\theta} \mathbb{E}_{v \sim \pi_\theta} [\mathcal{R}(v)]\]
💡 强化学习目标：找到使期望奖励最大化的模型参数θ，其中v是由当前策略（视频生成模型）采样的视频。 The RL objective: find model parameters θ that maximize the expected reward over videos v sampled from the current policy (the video generation model).

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着领域推动基于视频的世界模型用于机器人和具身智能，几何合理性是不可妥协的前提——World-R1提供了一套即插即用的RL方案，可应用于任何流匹配视频模型。用奖励驱动的微调替代昂贵的架构重设计，大幅降低了大规模构建物理一致性视频世界模型的门槛。 As the field pushes toward video-based world models for robotics and embodied AI, geometric plausibility is non-negotiable — World-R1 offers a plug-and-play RL recipe that can be applied to any flow-matching video model. By replacing costly architectural redesigns with reward-driven fine-tuning, it lowers the barrier to building physically consistent video world models at scale.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

项目采用Lean定理证明器，将Viazovska证明中的数学对象（模形式、傅里叶分析、Cohn-Elkies线性规划界）编码为可机器检验的定义和引理。人类数学家历时约两年构建了整体证明框架，并完成了大部分形式化工作。随后，Math Inc.开发的AI模型"Gauss"被用于自动形式化剩余的、技术难度最高的步骤，将自然语言数学论证转化为经过验证的Lean代码。最终得到了8维球堆积界的完整机器验证形式化证明。 The project formalized Viazovska's proof using the Lean theorem prover, encoding the mathematical objects (modular forms, Fourier analysis, the Cohn-Elkies linear programming bound) as machine-checkable definitions and lemmas. Human mathematicians set up the overall proof architecture and handled large portions of the formalization over approximately two years. The AI model 'Gauss', developed by Math Inc., was then deployed to autoformalize the remaining and most technically demanding steps, translating natural-language mathematical arguments into verified Lean code. The result is a complete, machine-checked formal proof of the sphere packing bound in dimension 8.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作是AI自动形式化模型能够处理前沿数学研究完整复杂度的里程碑式证明，将AI辅助定理证明的边界推至研究级别。对机器学习和形式化方法社区而言，这确立了人机协作数学验证的新标杆，暗示形式化验证其他深层结果（如分析学、数论乃至ML理论）已触手可及。 This work represents a landmark demonstration that AI autoformalization models can handle the full complexity of cutting-edge mathematical research, not just textbook-level problems, raising the bar for what AI-assisted theorem proving can achieve. For the ML and formal methods communities, it establishes a new benchmark for human-AI collaboration in mathematics and suggests that formally verifying other deep results (e.g., in analysis, number theory, or even ML theory) is now within reach.
