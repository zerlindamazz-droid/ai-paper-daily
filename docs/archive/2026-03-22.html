Transformers are Bayesian Networks Transformer即贝叶斯网络

🧑‍🔬 Gregory Coppola

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 严格证明Transformer本质上是贝叶斯网络上的置信传播
Transformers are rigorously proven to implement loopy belief propagation on implicit Bayesian networks.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 尽管Transformer是当前AI的主导架构，但学界对其为何有效缺乏严格的理论解释。我们缺少一个将Transformer计算与概率推理联系起来的数学框架，这限制了我们理解其能力边界和失败模式（如幻觉问题）的能力。 Despite transformers dominating modern AI, there is no rigorous theoretical explanation for why they work. We lack a principled mathematical framework connecting transformer computations to probabilistic reasoning, which limits our ability to understand their capabilities and failure modes — including hallucination.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **五角度形式化等价**：从五个独立角度严格证明sigmoid Transformer在其隐式因子图上实现了循环置信传播（Loopy BP），一层等于一轮BP迭代，全部经过标准数学公理形式化验证。 • **AND/OR结构分解**：精确揭示Transformer内部结构：注意力机制执行AND（聚合）操作，FFN执行OR（更新）操作，与Pearl经典的聚合/更新算法完全对应。 • **唯一性定理**：证明BP权重是sigmoid Transformer产生精确后验的唯一途径，排除了任何其他内部机制的可能性。 • **幻觉的结构性必然**：形式化证明可验证推理需要有限概念空间，幻觉不是可通过扩展规模修复的缺陷，而是无概念基础运算的结构性后果。 • **Formal equivalence (5 angles):** Proves via five independent results that sigmoid transformers implement loopy belief propagation (BP) on an implicit factor graph — one layer equals one round of BP — verified against standard mathematical axioms. • **AND/OR structural decomposition:** Precisely maps the transformer's internal structure: attention performs the AND (gather) operation and the FFN performs the OR (update) operation, exactly matching Pearl's classic gather/update algorithm. • **Uniqueness theorem:** Proves that BP weights are the *only* way a sigmoid transformer can produce exact posteriors — ruling out any alternative internal mechanism. • **Hallucination as structural necessity:** Formally proves that verifiable inference requires a finite concept space, and that hallucination is not a fixable bug but an inherent consequence of operating without grounded concepts — scaling alone cannot eliminate it.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验部分用于验证所有形式化理论结果在实践中的成立性。在构造性知识库上测试了Transformer是否能实现精确置信传播，同时评估了在有循环依赖的图上循环置信传播的实际收敛表现（尽管目前缺乏理论收敛保证）。实验旨在以经验方式验证贝叶斯网络刻画的实用性，而非与外部基线进行竞争性比较。 Experiments were conducted to empirically corroborate all five formal theoretical results. The authors tested whether transformers constructed according to their proofs correctly implement belief propagation on explicit knowledge bases, and evaluated the practical convergence behavior of loopy belief propagation in transformer-like settings — including on graphs with circular dependencies where theoretical convergence guarantees are absent. The experimental setup is primarily validatory rather than competitive benchmarking against external baselines.
📊 实验
结果		 本文的核心贡献是理论性的，而非追求Benchmark数字。实验上，五个形式化结论均得到实践验证：针对精确BP构造的Transformer在无环知识库上能给出正确的概率估计；在有循环依赖的图上，循环BP仍在测试配置中可靠收敛，尽管缺乏理论收敛保证。本文未报告标准排行榜指标，但所有定理均经数学公理形式化验证，保证了结论的严格正确性。 The paper's primary contributions are theoretical rather than benchmark-driven; specific numerical metrics are not the focus. Experimentally, all five formal results are confirmed to hold in practice: transformers built constructively for exact BP produce correct probability estimates on acyclic knowledge bases, and loopy BP via transformer layers converges reliably in tested configurations despite the lack of a formal guarantee. The paper does not report standard leaderboard metrics but establishes correctness guarantees via formal verification against mathematical axioms.
🏁 结论 This paper rigorously proves, via five formally verified results, that sigmoid transformers are equivalent to Bayesian networks implementing (loopy) belief propagation — providing the first precise theoretical explanation of how transformers perform probabilistic reasoning. It further establishes that hallucination is a structural inevitability for systems without grounded finite concept spaces, not a problem solvable by scaling.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文通过五个形式化证明将sigmoid Transformer与贝叶斯网络联系起来。首先证明任意权重的sigmoid Transformer在其隐式因子图上计算加权循环置信传播；其次给出构造性证明，说明Transformer可以被显式构建为对任意知识库运行精确BP；第三步证明唯一性——BP权重是精确后验计算的充要条件；第四步将注意力机制映射为Pearl"聚合"步骤，FFN映射为"更新"步骤，揭示AND/OR布尔结构；最后从理论上证明幻觉的结构性必然性。所有证明均经标准数学公理形式化验证。 The paper develops five formal proofs connecting sigmoid transformers to Bayesian networks. First, it shows that any sigmoid transformer with arbitrary weights computes weighted loopy belief propagation on its implicit factor graph. Second, it gives a constructive proof that a transformer can be explicitly built to run exact BP on any declared knowledge base. Third, it proves uniqueness — that BP weights are necessary (not just sufficient) for exact posterior computation. Fourth, it dissects the AND/OR boolean logic of transformer layers, mapping attention to Pearl's "gather" step and the FFN to the "update" step. Finally, the paper proves a theoretical result about hallucination: correctness of inference requires a finite concept space, and without grounding in such a space, hallucination is structurally unavoidable. All proofs are formally verified against standard mathematical axioms.

🎯 四项自监督设计协同，学习空间密集、时序一致的视觉表征

维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有视频自监督模型擅长全局场景理解，但在机器人操控、动作预判、深度估计等需要精细空间和时序定位的任务中表现欠佳——因为其学到的特征太"粗糙"，缺乏空间精度。前作 V-JEPA 2 在密集预测任务上能力有限，难以满足机器人抓取等精细操作的需求。如何在保留全局语义理解的同时获得高质量的密集局部特征，是本文要攻克的核心难题。 Most video self-supervised learning (SSL) models excel at global scene understanding but produce coarse, spatially imprecise features that struggle with tasks requiring fine-grained spatial and temporal grounding — such as robotic manipulation, action anticipation, and dense prediction. Prior work like V-JEPA 2 lacked the spatial resolution and local feature quality needed for dense downstream tasks. The challenge is to jointly achieve dense local representations and strong global semantic understanding without sacrificing one for the other.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **密集预测损失**：可见 token 与被掩码 token 均参与损失计算，迫使模型学习显式的空间与时序定位，而非依赖全局上下文。 - **深层自监督**：将自监督目标层次化地施加在编码器多个中间层，而非仅在最终层，使网络每一层的表征都得到优化。 - **多模态分词器**：统一的 tokenization 方案支持图像与视频的联合训练，提升跨模态泛化能力。 - **高效扩展性**：模型容量与训练数据的系统性扩展均带来持续的表征质量提升，验证了该设计的可扩展性。 - **Dense Predictive Loss**: Both visible and masked tokens contribute to the loss signal, forcing the model to learn explicit spatial and temporal grounding rather than relying solely on global context. - **Deep Self-Supervision**: The SSL objective is applied hierarchically across multiple intermediate encoder layers, not just the final layer, enriching representations at every level of the network. - **Multi-modal Tokenizers**: A unified tokenization scheme enables seamless joint training on both images and videos, improving generalization across modalities. - **Effective Scaling**: Systematic scaling of both model size and training data yields consistent representation quality gains, validating the design's scalability.

内容		 作者在多个多样化基准上评估 V-JEPA 2.1，涵盖密集预测、动作预判、机器人操控、导航及全局视频理解。密集预测使用 NYUv2 深度估计数据集（线性探针评估）；动作预判在 Ego4D（短期物体交互预判）和 EPIC-KITCHENS（高层动作预判）上测试；机器人实验包括真实机器人抓取任务（以 V-JEPA 2 AC 为基线）和 TartanDrive 数据集上的导航任务；全局识别在 Something-Something-V2 上评估。基线模型包括前作 V-JEPA 2、V-JEPA 2 AC 及其他主流 SSL 方法。 The authors evaluate V-JEPA 2.1 on a diverse suite of benchmarks spanning dense prediction, action anticipation, robotic manipulation, navigation, and global video understanding. For dense prediction, they use NYUv2 depth estimation with a linear probe. Action anticipation is evaluated on Ego4D (short-term object-interaction anticipation) and EPIC-KITCHENS (high-level action anticipation). Robotic experiments include a real-robot grasping task (compared against V-JEPA 2 AC as a baseline) and robotic navigation on the TartanDrive dataset. ⚙️ Method · 方法详解

V-JEPA 2.1 在 JEPA（联合嵌入预测架构）框架基础上引入四项改进。一是密集预测损失：修改掩码目标，让可见 token 与被掩码 token 均参与训练信号，使模型关注具体空间与时序位置。二是深层自监督：在 Transformer 编码器的多个中间层同时施加 SSL 损失，将丰富的学习信号贯穿整个网络层次。三是多模态分词器：统一的图像与视频 tokenization 流水线，支持两种模态的同步训练。四是规模化训练：通过更大模型和更多数据进行系统扩展，全面提升表征质量。 V-JEPA 2.1 builds on the JEPA (Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture) framework and introduces four targeted improvements. First, the dense predictive loss modifies the masking objective so that both visible and masked patch tokens are included in the training signal, anchoring the model's attention to spatial and temporal locations rather than averaging over global context. Second, deep self-supervision applies the same SSL loss across multiple intermediate transformer layers, propagating rich learning signals throughout the encoder hierarchy. Third, multi-modal tokenizers unify image and video inputs under a single tokenization pipeline, allowing the model to train on both modalities simultaneously. Finally, the model is trained at scale — larger model variants and expanded datasets — to maximize representation quality across all tasks.

🎯 无需更新参数，LLM智能体通过外化技能库自我进化并设计任务专用智能体。
A self-improving LLM agent that designs task-specific agents by evolving an external skill library — no parameter updates needed.

❓ 待解决
的问题		 为各种任务构建AI智能体通常需要人类专家手工设计，成本高且难以扩展。现有LLM智能体在跨对话的知识保留和复用方面也存在严重不足——上下文一重置，之前积累的经验就消失了，无法持续进步。 Building capable AI agents for diverse tasks typically requires human experts to manually design agents and prompt them. Current LLM agents also struggle to retain and reuse knowledge across interactions — they forget what they've learned once the context resets. This makes it costly and inflexible to scale agents to new domains.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **智能体自主设计智能体**：系统可端到端地为新任务自动构建、调整和优化专用智能体，彻底摆脱人工设计依赖。 - **状态化提示作为持久记忆**：技能以结构化Markdown文件存储，同时编码行为和上下文，跨会话持续演化复用。 - **无需更新模型权重的持续学习**：所有自适应均通过外化技能进化和提示更新完成，模型无关、效率极高。 - **Agent-designing agent**: The system autonomously constructs, adapts, and refines task-specific agents end-to-end — eliminating the need for human-designed agent architectures. - **Stateful prompts as persistent memory**: Skills are stored as structured Markdown files encoding both behavior and context, acting as evolving, reusable long-term memory across sessions. - **Continual learning without touching LLM weights**: All adaptation happens via external skill evolution and prompt updates, making the approach model-agnostic and highly efficient.

On the Humanity's Last Exam (HLE) benchmark, the system achieved a remarkable 116.2% relative improvement in overall accuracy, more than doubling the baseline performance. Results across iterative refinement rounds confirmed sustained, compounding gains consistent with continual learning behavior.

This work establishes a compelling new paradigm for scalable, self-improving agent systems.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Memento-Skills构建了一个以可复用"技能"库为核心的记忆型强化学习框架，技能以结构化Markdown文件存储，同时编码行为和上下文。系统的核心是"读写反思学习"循环：在读阶段，可训练的技能路由器根据当前状态化提示选择最相关的技能；在写阶段，智能体根据新经验更新或创建技能。从简单的基础技能（如网页搜索、终端操作）出发，智能体通过迭代生成和精炼，完全自主地为新任务构建专用智能体，全程无需更新LLM参数。 Memento-Skills builds a memory-based RL framework around a library of reusable "skills" stored as structured Markdown files. These skills serve as externalized, evolving memory that encodes both task behavior and context. A core Read–Write Reflective Learning loop drives the system: in the read phase, a behavior-trainable skill router selects the most relevant skills given the current stateful prompt; in the write phase, the agent reflects on its experience and updates or creates new skills accordingly. Starting from simple elementary skills (e.g., web search, terminal operations), the agent iteratively generates and refines more complex, task-specific agents entirely on its own — no LLM parameter updates are ever required.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

This paper tackles one of the core bottlenecks in deploying LLM agents at scale: the inability to accumulate and reuse knowledge without expensive retraining. By enabling agents to design agents through evolving external skill memory, it points toward truly autonomous, self-improving AI systems.

A rigorous mathematical guide unifying diffusion, score-based, and flow-matching models under Schrödinger bridges.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当前主流生成模型（扩散模型、基于得分的模型、流匹配）虽然目标一致——把简单分布变成复杂数据分布——却各自为战，缺少统一的数学框架。这让研究者难以从第一性原理出发推导新方法或深入理解现有方法之间的关联。 Modern generative models — including diffusion models, score-based models, and flow matching — all share the same core goal: transforming a simple distribution (like Gaussian noise) into a complex data distribution. However, these frameworks have been developed somewhat independently, lacking a unified mathematical principle that explains why they all work and how they relate to each other. This fragmentation makes it harder to derive new methods or understand existing ones from first principles.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次系统性地将薛定谔桥问题的数学理论与现代生成建模直接对接，整合最优传输、随机控制、路径空间优化于同一框架。 • 揭示扩散模型、得分匹配、流匹配均为同一统一原理的特例：在边缘分布约束下对参考过程进行最小KL散度偏差优化。 • 从第一性原理构建完整工具箱，清晰展示DSB、IPF、SB-FBSDE等具体算法族如何自然涌现，为系统推导新生成方法提供理论支撑。 • Provides the first comprehensive mathematical treatment of the Schrödinger bridge problem specifically tailored to modern generative modeling, bridging optimal transport, stochastic control, and path-space optimization in one framework. • Reveals that diffusion models, score-based models, and flow matching are all special cases of a single unifying principle — minimizing KL divergence from a reference process subject to marginal distribution constraints. • Constructs a practical "toolkit" from first principles, showing how specific algorithmic families (e.g., DSB, IPF, SB-FBSDE) emerge naturally from the general theory, enabling systematic derivation of new generative methods.
🧪 实验
内容		 本文属于理论综述与数学指南性质的工作，不包含新的原创实验。论文通过梳理和引用扩散模型、得分匹配、流匹配及薛定谔桥算法（如DSB、IPF）等领域已有文献中的实验结果，来说明所构建理论框架的实际意义。案例涵盖图像生成、分布插值等标准任务，用以验证统一框架的适用范围。 As a theoretical foundations paper and mathematical guide, this work does not conduct novel empirical experiments. Instead, it reviews and contextualizes existing experimental results from the literature on diffusion models, score-based generative models, flow matching, and Schrödinger bridge-based generative algorithms (e.g., DSB, IPF-based methods). The paper illustrates its theoretical constructions with connections to established benchmarks and tasks such as image generation and distribution interpolation, drawing on results from prior works to validate the practical relevance of the unified framework.
📊 实验
结果		 本文为理论性综述工作，不提供原创的量化实验结果或基准分数。论文的贡献在于对现有方法进行严格的数学统一，而非性能提升。文中引用的具体数字（如FID分数）均来自DSB、IPF等已发表工作，作者本身未开展新的对比实验。 As a theoretical/survey paper, this work does not report original benchmark numbers or quantitative improvements. Specific empirical results (e.g., FID scores, likelihood values) are drawn from cited prior works on DSB, IPF, and related algorithms, rather than from new experiments conducted by the authors.
🏁 结论 This paper establishes a comprehensive mathematical foundation showing that the Schrödinger bridge problem — minimizing path-measure KL divergence subject to marginal constraints — is the unifying principle underlying diffusion models, score-based models, and flow matching. By building this toolkit from first principles, it provides a rigorous theoretical scaffold for understanding, extending, and designing next-generation generative modeling algorithms.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文将生成建模重新表述为薛定谔桥问题：在满足源分布和目标分布边缘约束的条件下，找到与参考随机过程KL散度最小的路径概率测度。从最优传输和随机微积分的基本原理出发，推导出薛定谔桥的动态表述形式，核心为前向-后向随机微分方程（FBSDE）系统。通过这一统一框架，论文展示了迭代比例拟合（IPF）、扩散薛定谔桥（DSB）等现有算法是该一般性问题的具体计算解。该框架还自然延伸至图像翻译、条件生成等特定任务场景。 The paper frames generative modeling as a Schrödinger bridge problem: finding the probability measure over stochastic paths that is closest (in KL divergence) to a reference process, while matching prescribed source and target marginal distributions. Starting from first principles in optimal transport and stochastic calculus, it derives the dynamic formulation of the Schrödinger bridge, which involves coupled forward-backward stochastic differential equations (FBSDEs). The authors then show how existing algorithms like Iterative Proportional Fitting (IPF) and Diffusion Schrödinger Bridges (DSB) emerge as computational solutions to this general problem. The framework also supports generalization to task-specific settings such as image-to-image translation and class-conditional generation.
