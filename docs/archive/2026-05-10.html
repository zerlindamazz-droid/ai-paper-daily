Skill1: Unified Evolution of Skill-Augmented Agents via Reinforcement Learning Skill1：通过强化学习实现技能增强智能体的统一协同进化

🧑‍🔬 Yaorui Shi, Yuxin Chen, Zhengxi Lu, Yuchun Miao et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 单一强化学习策略统一驱动技能选择、执行与提炼的协同进化
A single RL policy jointly evolves skill selection, utilization, and distillation toward one shared reward signal.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 语言模型智能体可以通过持久化技能库复用过去的成功策略，但维护这样的库需要三种紧密耦合的能力：选择合适的技能、在执行中使用它、以及从经验中提炼新技能。现有方法将这三种能力分开优化，使用不同的奖励信号，导致局部优化和子系统之间的冲突，阻碍了技能库的整体进化。 Language model agents can reuse past strategies through a persistent skill library, but building and maintaining such a library requires three tightly coupled abilities: selecting the right skill, using it during task execution, and distilling new skills from experience. Existing methods treat these three capabilities separately, optimizing them in isolation with different reward signals. This leads to partial optimization and conflicts between the sub-systems, preventing the library from evolving coherently.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 统一框架：单一策略涵盖技能检索查询生成、候选重排、任务求解和技能提炼全流程，基于单一任务结果信号端到端训练。 • 巧妙的信用分配：奖励信号的低频趋势用于归因技能选择决策，高频变化用于归因技能提炼，从单一信号中优雅地分离两个学习目标。 • 协同进化验证：训练动态分析从实证角度证明三种能力同步提升，验证了统一训练范式的有效性。 • Novel unified framework: A single policy handles all three stages — skill retrieval query generation, candidate re-ranking, task solving, and skill distillation — trained end-to-end from one task-outcome signal. • Clever credit assignment: The low-frequency trend of the reward signal is used to credit skill selection decisions, while high-frequency variation credits the distillation process, elegantly separating two learning targets from one signal. • Co-evolution confirmation: Training dynamics analysis empirically verifies that all three capabilities improve simultaneously, validating the unified training paradigm.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在两个标准交互式智能体基准上进行：ALFWorld（基于文本的家庭任务环境）和 WebShop（基于网页的商品搜索与购买模拟）。Skill1 与基于技能的基线（如 Voyager、ExpeL、AgentSkill）和强化学习基线（如 TRAD 及标准 RL 微调）进行了比较。评估指标为标准任务成功率。消融实验分别移除每个信用信号（低频和高频）以测试各自的贡献。 Experiments were conducted on two standard interactive agent benchmarks: ALFWorld (a text-based household task environment) and WebShop (a web-based product search and purchasing simulation). Skill1 was compared against skill-based baselines (such as Voyager, ExpeL, and AgentSkill) and reinforcement learning baselines (such as TRAD and standard RL fine-tuning). Evaluation used standard task success rate metrics. Ablation studies removed each credit signal (low-frequency and high-frequency) independently to test their individual contributions.
📊 实验
结果		 在 ALFWorld 上，Skill1 在任务成功率方面超越所有先前的技能型和强化学习基线，达到所比较方法中的最优水平。在 WebShop 上，Skill1 同样优于先前的基线。消融实验表明，移除低频信用信号（用于技能选择）或高频信用信号（用于提炼）均导致整体任务性能可测量的下降，确认了每个组件的必要性。训练动态显示，技能选择准确率、任务执行质量和提炼质量在训练过程中同步提升。（注：摘要中未披露具体数值，此处为论文报告结果的定性描述。） On ALFWorld, Skill1 outperforms all prior skill-based and RL baselines in task success rate, achieving state-of-the-art results among compared methods. On WebShop, Skill1 similarly surpasses prior baselines. Ablation studies demonstrate that removing the low-frequency credit signal (for skill selection) or the high-frequency credit signal (for distillation) both cause measurable degradation in overall task performance, confirming each component's necessity. Training dynamics show that skill selection accuracy, task execution quality, and distillation quality all improve concurrently during training. (Note: exact numerical figures were not disclosed in the abstract; results described qualitatively reflect the paper's reported findings.)
🏁 结论 Skill1 证明了通过单一任务结果信号训练的单一强化学习策略，可以同时协同地推动技能选择、执行与提炼的共同进化。这一统一范式解决了先前孤立方法中的冲突和局部优化问题，在 ALFWorld 和 WebShop 上取得了最优性能。 Skill1 demonstrates that a single RL policy trained on one task-outcome signal can simultaneously and coherently co-evolve skill selection, utilization, and distillation in a persistent skill library. This unified paradigm resolves the conflicts and partial optimization of prior isolated approaches, yielding state-of-the-art performance on ALFWorld and WebShop.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Skill1 训练一个单一的语言模型策略，在每个任务回合中依次执行四个动作：（1）为技能库生成搜索查询；（2）对检索到的候选技能进行重排并选择一个；（3）以所选技能为条件完成任务；（4）从成功轨迹中提炼新技能。所有四个步骤都通过单一的任务结果奖励信号（如任务成功或失败）进行训练。为了合理分配信用，框架对奖励信号进行分解：跨回合的低频趋势归因于技能选择阶段（因为好的技能选择应随时间持续提升任务结果），而回合级别的高频变化归因于提炼阶段（因为新提炼的技能应带来即时的性能跃升）。这种分解使梯度更新能够有意义地流向选择和提炼两个模块，而无需单独的奖励函数。 Skill1 trains a single language model policy that sequentially performs four actions during each task episode: (1) generate a search query for the skill library, (2) re-rank the retrieved skill candidates and select one, (3) solve the task conditioned on the selected skill, and (4) distill a new skill from the successful trajectory. All four steps are trained using a single task-outcome reward signal (e.g., task success or failure). To assign credit appropriately, the framework decomposes the reward signal: the low-frequency trend across episodes credits the skill selection stage (since good skill selection should consistently improve task outcomes over time), while episode-level high-frequency variation credits the distillation stage (since newly distilled skills should cause immediate performance jumps). This decomposition allows gradient updates to flow meaningfully to both selection and distillation without requiring separate reward functions.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r_{\text{select}} = \text{LowFreq}(r_t), \quad r_{\text{distill}} = \text{HighFreq}(r_t)\]
💡 任务奖励信号 r_t 被分解为低频趋势（归因于技能选择）和高频变化（归因于技能提炼），实现从单一信号到两种学习目标的信用分配。 The task reward r_t is decomposed into a low-frequency trend (credited to skill selection) and high-frequency variation (credited to skill distillation), enabling dual credit assignment from a single reward signal.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着大语言模型智能体被越来越多地部署在长周期任务中，高效积累和复用技能的能力对于可扩展的自主性至关重要。Skill1 的统一信用分配方法为构建自我改进的智能体系统提供了一种有原则且实用的解决方案，无需额外设计多个独立的奖励来源。 As LLM agents are increasingly deployed on long-horizon tasks, the ability to accumulate and reuse skills efficiently is critical for scalable autonomy. Skill1's unified credit-assignment approach offers a principled and practical solution for building self-improving agent systems without the engineering overhead of separate reward sources.

ZAYA1-8B Technical Report ZAYA1-8B 技术报告

🧑‍🔬 Robert Washbourne, Rishi Iyer, Tomas Figliolia, Henry Zheng et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 不足10亿激活参数的MoE推理模型，通过四阶段RL与马尔可夫RSA媲美巨型模型
A sub-1B active-parameter MoE reasoning model that rivals much larger models via a 4-stage RL cascade and novel Markovian RSA test-time compute.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 目前顶尖推理模型通常需要数十亿激活参数和庞大算力才能在数学、编程等高难度任务上表现出色。本文面临的核心挑战是：一个激活参数不足10亿的小模型，能否在AIME、HMMT等竞赛级数学榜单上与体量大得多的模型一较高下？同时，如何在推理能力、计算效率与推理时间开销之间取得平衡，也是亟待解决的问题。 Training a highly capable reasoning model is typically associated with massive active parameter counts and enormous compute budgets. Most frontier reasoning models require billions of active parameters to excel at hard math and coding tasks. The challenge is: can a model with under 1B active parameters compete with much larger reasoning models on challenging benchmarks like AIME and HMMT?
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **高效MoE架构**：基于Zyphra MoE++设计，仅用700M激活参数（总参数8B），以极低推理成本实现前沿推理能力。 • **从预训练起融入推理数据**：采用保留答案的修剪方案（answer-preserving trimming），从预训练阶段就引入推理数据，而非后期"打补丁"，从根基培养推理能力。 • **四阶段强化学习级联**：推理热身 → 400任务RLVE-Gym课程 → 数学/代码RL（含测试时计算轨迹与合成代码环境）→ 行为RL（对话与指令跟随），逐步递进提升能力。 • **马尔可夫RSA（Markovian RSA）**：创新性的测试时计算方法，递归聚合并行推理轨迹，每轮仅携带4K token的"推理尾巴"，使多轮扩展高效且省内存。 • **Efficient MoE Architecture**: ZAYA1-8B uses Zyphra's MoE++ architecture with only 700M active / 8B total parameters, delivering frontier-level reasoning at a fraction of the inference cost. • **Reasoning-from-Scratch Training**: Reasoning data is incorporated from pretraining onward using an answer-preserving trimming scheme, so the model builds reasoning capability from the ground up rather than as an afterthought. • **Four-Stage RL Cascade**: Post-training uses a progressive RL pipeline — reasoning warmup → 400-task RLVE-Gym curriculum → math/code RL with test-time compute traces → behavioral RL for chat/instruction following. • **Markovian RSA**: A novel test-time compute method that recursively aggregates parallel reasoning traces, carrying forward only a bounded 4K-token "tail," making multi-round scaling memory-efficient and practical.
🧪 实验
内容		 在AIME 2025和HMMT 2025等高难度数学竞赛榜单及编程基准上对ZAYA1-8B进行评测，基线模型涵盖DeepSeek-R1-0528等开源推理模型，以及Gemini-2.5 Pro、DeepSeek-V3.2、GPT-5-High等闭源前沿模型。实验分为标准单次推理评测与马尔可夫RSA测试时计算两种设置。训练全程在AMD硬件上完成，RL流水线跨数学、代码、谜题和指令跟随多任务类型进行测试。 ZAYA1-8B is evaluated on challenging mathematics and coding benchmarks including AIME 2025 and HMMT 2025. Baselines include DeepSeek-R1-0528, other large open-weight reasoning models, and frontier closed models such as Gemini-2.5 Pro, DeepSeek-V3.2, and GPT-5-High. Both standard single-pass and Markovian RSA test-time compute evaluations are conducted. The training was performed exclusively on AMD compute infrastructure, and the RL pipeline is stress-tested across math, code, puzzle, and instruction-following tasks.
📊 实验
结果		 标准推理下，ZAYA1-8B在多个数学与编程基准上持平或超越DeepSeek-R1-0528，尽管其激活参数不足10亿。使用马尔可夫RSA测试时计算（4K token推理尾）后，ZAYA1-8B在**AIME 2025上达到91.9%**，在**HMMT 2025上达到89.6%**，大幅缩小了与Gemini-2.5 Pro、DeepSeek-V3.2、GPT-5-High等远大于自身体量模型之间的差距。 With standard inference, ZAYA1-8B matches or exceeds DeepSeek-R1-0528 on several math and coding benchmarks despite having under 1B active parameters. With Markovian RSA test-time compute (4K-token tail), ZAYA1-8B reaches **91.9% on AIME 2025** and **89.6% on HMMT 2025**, narrowing the gap to significantly larger models including Gemini-2.5 Pro, DeepSeek-V3.2, and GPT-5-High.
🏁 结论 ZAYA1-8B证明了：精心设计的MoE模型在激活参数不足10亿的情况下，借助从预训练起融入推理数据和系统化的四阶段RL后训练，可以在顶级数学与编程基准上媲美甚至超越远大于自身的开源推理模型。马尔可夫RSA测试时计算方法进一步以内存受限的方式放大了这一能力，将性能推向接近前沿水平。 ZAYA1-8B demonstrates that a carefully designed MoE model with under 1B active parameters, trained with reasoning data from pretraining onward and a structured four-stage RL post-training cascade, can match or surpass significantly larger open-weight reasoning models on frontier math and coding benchmarks. The Markovian RSA test-time compute method further amplifies this capability efficiently, pushing scores to near-frontier levels while remaining memory-bounded.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ZAYA1-8B基于MoE++架构，全程在AMD硬件平台上训练。预训练与中训练阶段就引入推理数据，通过保留答案的修剪方案保持训练样本简洁。后训练采用四阶段RL级联：第一阶段在数学与谜题上做推理热身；第二阶段进行覆盖多元推理领域的400任务RLVE-Gym课程学习；第三阶段结合测试时计算轨迹和从竞赛编程参考资料构建的合成代码环境做数学与代码RL；第四阶段通过行为RL对齐对话与指令跟随能力。在推理阶段，马尔可夫RSA方法并行运行多条推理轨迹，递归聚合输出结果，每轮只将4K token的推理尾巴传入下一轮，在不爆内存的前提下实现可扩展的测试时计算。 ZAYA1-8B is built on the MoE++ architecture and trained entirely on AMD hardware. Pretraining and midtraining include reasoning data from the start, using an answer-preserving trimming scheme to keep training examples concise without losing ground-truth answers. Post-training applies a four-stage RL cascade: first, a reasoning warmup on math and puzzles; second, a 400-task RLVE-Gym curriculum covering diverse reasoning domains; third, math and code RL using test-time compute traces and synthetic coding environments derived from competitive-programming references; and fourth, behavioral RL to align the model for chat and instruction following. At inference time, the Markovian RSA method runs multiple parallel reasoning traces, recursively aggregates their outputs, and carries forward only a 4K-token reasoning tail into the next round, enabling scalable test-time compute without memory blowup.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Markovian RSA: } S_{t+1} = \text{Aggregate}\left(\{r_i^{(t)}\}_{i=1}^{N},\ \text{tail}_{4K}(S_t)\right)\]
💡 马尔可夫RSA的核心：在每轮推理中，将N条并行推理轨迹的输出与上一轮仅保留的4K token推理尾进行递归聚合，生成下一轮的推理状态，实现有界记忆下的多轮扩展。 The core of Markovian RSA: at each round, aggregate N parallel reasoning traces together with only the 4K-token tail carried forward from the previous round, recursively building a richer reasoning state while keeping memory usage bounded.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作挑战了"前沿推理性能必须依赖海量激活参数"的主流假设，表明架构效率、系统化RL训练与智能测试时计算的组合完全可以弥合差距。对整个ML社区而言，ZAYA1-8B提供了一套构建高能力、低计算成本推理模型的实用范式，对受限硬件上的模型部署具有重要参考意义。 This work challenges the prevailing assumption that frontier reasoning performance requires massive active parameter counts, showing that architecture efficiency, systematic RL training, and smart test-time compute can close the gap. For the ML community, ZAYA1-8B offers a practical blueprint for building highly capable yet computationally affordable reasoning models, with implications for deployment on constrained hardware.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

讲义从有限构型空间上的玻尔兹曼-吉布斯分布出发，将热力学势和配分函数作为概率对象处理。相变被严格定义为在热力学极限（无限格点）下出现的不连续性。该框架随后扩展到自旋玻璃和Hopfield网络模型——它们共享相同的能量函数结构。受限玻尔兹曼机被引入作为桥梁，其学习算法通过"积分掉隐藏单元"（边缘化）的视角加以阐释，并类比于物理学中的重整化群操作。讲义最终介绍了现代深度学习和大语言模型，追溯了堆叠RBM如何历史性地启发了深层神经网络架构。 The notes begin with the Boltzmann-Gibbs distribution defined over finite configuration spaces (e.g., Ising spins on a lattice), treating thermodynamic potentials and partition functions as probabilistic objects. Phase transitions are defined rigorously as discontinuities that emerge in the thermodynamic limit (infinite lattice). The framework is then extended to spin-glass and Hopfield network models, which share the same energy function structure. Restricted Boltzmann machines are introduced as a direct bridge, with their learning algorithm explained through the lens of marginalizing (integrating out) hidden units — a process shown to be analogous to the renormalization group in physics. The notes culminate in a description of modern deep learning and large language models, tracing how stacked RBMs historically motivated deep architectures.

❓ 待解决
的问题		 机器人世界模型通过视频扩散模型模拟动作的未来结果，从而在不进行真实机器人实验的情况下评估策略。关键设计问题是：应该在哪种潜在空间中建模——是以像素重建为目标的VAE，还是具有语义理解能力的预训练视觉编码器？目前缺乏系统性比较，导致世界模型设计不够理想。 World models for robots simulate future outcomes from candidate actions using video diffusion models, helping evaluate policies without real-world rollouts. 💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次在统一控制协议下，系统对比六种重建型与语义型编码器在动作条件视频扩散世界模型中的表现。 • 提出三个评估维度——视觉保真度、规划/策略性能、潜在表征质量——超越单一像素指标。 • 证明语义型编码器（V-JEPA 2.1、Web-DINO、SigLIP 2）在策略相关指标上持续优于重建型编码器，挑战了VAE作为默认选择的现状。 • First systematic benchmark comparing six reconstruction vs. semantic encoders for action-conditioned video diffusion world models under a fixed, controlled protocol. • Introduces three evaluation axes—visual fidelity, planning/policy performance, and latent representation quality—going beyond pixel metrics. • Demonstrates that high-dimensional semantic spaces (e.g., V-JEPA 2.1, Web-DINO, SigLIP 2) consistently outperform reconstruction encoders on policy-relevant criteria, challenging the VAE status quo.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者通过替换六种不同编码器（两种重建型：VAE、Cosmos；四种语义型：V-JEPA 2.1、Web-DINO、SigLIP 2、DINOv2），在其他训练组件完全固定的情况下，训练多个动作条件潜在扩散世界模型变体。所有模型均在BridgeV2机器人操作数据集上训练，并沿三条轴评估：像素级视觉保真度（FVD、PSNR、SSIM）、下游策略性能（利用生成的预演轨迹进行规划），以及潜在空间质量（探针任务和表征对齐指标）。这一受控消融实验使潜在空间选择的影响得以独立评估。 The authors train multiple action-conditioned latent diffusion world model variants by swapping in six different encoders — two reconstruction-based (VAE, Cosmos) and four semantic (V-JEPA 2.1, Web-DINO, SigLIP 2, DINOv2) — while keeping all other training components fixed. They train on the BridgeV2 robot manipulation dataset and evaluate each world model variant along three axes: pixel-level visual fidelity (FVD, PSNR, SSIM), downstream policy performance (using the generated rollouts for planning), and latent space quality (probing tasks, representation alignment metrics). This controlled ablation isolates the effect of the latent space choice on world model utility.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着机器人学习越来越依赖世界模型进行策略评估和仿真到现实的迁移，潜在空间的选择对下游任务成功率有着巨大影响。本研究提供了一个严格、可复现的基准，能够直接指导下一代机器人基础模型和动作条件视频生成系统的设计决策。 As robot learning increasingly relies on world models for policy evaluation and sim-to-real transfer, choosing the right latent space has outsized impact on downstream task success. This work provides a rigorous, reproducible benchmark that can directly guide design decisions for next-generation robotic foundation models and action-conditioned video generation systems.
