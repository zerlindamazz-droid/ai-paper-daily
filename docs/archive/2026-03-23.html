Beyond Single Tokens: Distilling Discrete Diffusion Models via Discrete MMD 超越单Token：通过离散MMD蒸馏离散扩散模型

🧑‍🔬 Emiel Hoogeboom, David Ruhe, Jonathan Heek, Thomas Mensink et al.

图 Figure · Page 1 of paper

🎯 D-MMD让离散扩散模型高质量快速蒸馏成为可能
D-MMD enables fast, high-quality distillation of discrete diffusion models for text and images without quality collapse.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 离散扩散模型（用于文本生成和离散图像token）采样速度慢，需要很多步才能生成高质量结果。连续扩散模型（如图像扩散）已有成熟的蒸馏技术，可以将采样步骤压缩到寥寥几步，但离散扩散模型在这方面严重滞后。此前的离散蒸馏方法容易出现质量崩塌和多样性丧失的问题，难以实际使用。 Discrete diffusion models (used for text and discrete image tokens) require many sampling steps to generate high-quality outputs, making them slow at inference. Unlike continuous diffusion models — which have many well-developed distillation techniques to reduce sampling steps to just a few — discrete diffusion models have lacked effective distillation methods. Prior attempts at discrete distillation suffer from quality and diversity collapse, rendering them impractical.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - **序列级分布匹配**：D-MMD通过最大均值差异（MMD）对整个token序列的分布进行匹配，而非逐token独立匹配，有效捕捉token之间的依赖关系，克服了先前方法的崩塌问题。 - **成功跨域迁移**：将连续扩散领域已被验证的矩匹配蒸馏思想，巧妙地适配到本质上完全不同的离散空间，技术迁移难度大、创新性强。 - **学生超越老师**：蒸馏后的模型在足够采样步骤下，生成质量可以超越其教师模型，这在蒸馏方法中极为罕见，证明蒸馏过程带来了额外的优化增益。 - **Novel matching objective**: D-MMD matches distributions of entire token sequences (via Maximum Mean Discrepancy over discrete distributions) rather than single tokens independently, capturing inter-token dependencies that prior methods miss. - **Bridging continuous and discrete worlds**: Successfully adapts moment-matching distillation ideas proven in continuous diffusion to the fundamentally different discrete setting, overcoming the collapse issues of prior discrete distillation. - **Student can surpass teacher**: The distilled model is not merely a compressed copy — under sufficient steps, it can actually outperform its teacher model in generation quality, a rare and striking property for distillation methods.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验覆盖文本生成和离散图像生成两类任务。文本实验以预训练离散扩散语言模型为教师，在标准语言建模基准上进行评估；图像实验使用将图像编码为离散codebook token的生成模型。基线方法包括使用完整采样步数的教师模型，以及已有的离散扩散蒸馏方法。评估指标包括文本困惑度（perplexity）、图像生成质量（FID）及多样性指标，并在不同采样步数下绘制质量-速度权衡曲线，全面衡量蒸馏效果。 Experiments were conducted on both text generation and discrete image generation tasks. For text, the authors evaluated on standard language modeling benchmarks using pre-trained discrete diffusion language models as teachers. For images, they used discrete image token models (where images are encoded into discrete codebooks). Baselines included the original teacher discrete diffusion models (with full sampling steps) and prior discrete distillation approaches. Evaluation metrics included perplexity and generative quality scores (such as FID for images and standard NLP metrics for text), as well as diversity measures, across varying numbers of sampling steps to profile the quality-speed tradeoff.
📊 实验
结果		 D-MMD achieves strong generation quality with significantly fewer sampling steps than the teacher. On text benchmarks, the distilled model matches or surpasses teacher perplexity with a fraction of the steps (e.g., reducing from 1000 steps to ~10–50 steps). On image generation, D-MMD achieves competitive FID scores — and in several settings the student model outperforms the teacher in FID, demonstrating the "beyond-teacher" effect. Prior discrete distillation baselines suffer visible quality and diversity collapse at reduced step counts, while D-MMD maintains generation diversity throughout. Exact benchmark numbers vary by setting, but improvements over prior distillation baselines are consistently substantial across both modalities.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

D-MMD将蒸馏问题转化为一个分布匹配问题，使用最大均值差异（MMD）度量学生模型与教师模型在离散token序列上的分布差异。与以往逐token独立匹配不同，该方法在每个去噪步骤中对多token的联合分布进行匹配，保留了token间的结构依赖。MMD目标函数通过在嵌入空间中使用核函数，实现对离散分布的可微分损失计算。学生模型通过学习"跳过"多个去噪步骤，复现教师模型的多步去噪路径，从而在更少步骤内达到相近甚至更高的生成质量。整体思路借鉴了连续域中的一致性蒸馏（consistency distillation），但专门为离散/类别型数据进行了适配。 D-MMD frames distillation as a distribution-matching problem using Maximum Mean Discrepancy (MMD) over discrete token sequences. Instead of trying to match single-token predictions independently (which loses inter-token structure), the method matches the joint distribution of multi-token outputs between the student and teacher at each denoising step. The MMD objective provides a principled, differentiable loss over discrete distributions by using kernel functions in the embedding space. This allows the student model to learn to "jump" multiple denoising steps at once, mirroring consistency distillation ideas from the continuous domain but adapted for categorical/discrete outputs. The training signal is derived from the teacher's multi-step trajectory, guiding the student to replicate longer denoising paths in fewer steps without quality collapse.

Var-JEPA: A Variational Formulation of the Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture -- Bridging Predictive and Generative Self-Supervised Learning Var-JEPA：联合嵌入预测架构的变分形式化——连接预测式与生成式自监督学习

🧑‍🔬 Moritz Gögl, Christopher Yau

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 用ELBO统一JEPA与变分生成模型，无需启发式防坍缩技巧
Var-JEPA unifies JEPA and variational generative models under a single ELBO objective, enabling principled uncertainty quantification without ad-hoc collapse fixes.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 JEPA（联合嵌入预测架构）通常被视为与生成式/概率式自监督学习截然不同的范式——在隐空间中预测而非重建原始观测。然而这一区分导致实践者依赖各种临时技巧（架构启发式、停止梯度等）来防止表征坍缩，缺乏原则性的概率基础。此外，JEPA无法对学到的表征进行不确定性量化。 JEPA (Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture) is widely presented as a fundamentally different paradigm from generative/probabilistic self-supervised learning — predicting in latent space rather than reconstructing raw observations. However, this distinction has led practitioners to rely on ad-hoc tricks (architectural heuristics, stop-gradients, etc.) to prevent representation collapse, with no principled probabilistic grounding. Additionally, JEPA offers no mechanism for uncertainty quantification in its learned representations.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **"区分只是修辞"的大胆论断**：论文从理论层面指出，JEPA的编码器-预测器结构并非独立范式，而是隐式地对应于耦合隐变量模型中的变分后验与条件先验。 • **ELBO作为统一目标**：通过从单一ELBO目标推导Var-JEPA，用有原则的概率损失替代了所有临时性防坍缩正则化手段，无需任何架构技巧。 • **免费获得不确定性量化**：显式的概率隐空间结构天然支持表征空间内的不确定性估计，这是标准JEPA所不具备的能力。 • **表格数据实例化（Var-T-JEPA）**：将框架具体应用于较少被JEPA类方法探索的表格数据领域，验证了其实际可用性。 • **The "separation is rhetorical"**: The paper makes a bold theoretical claim — JEPA's encoder-predictor structure is not conceptually distinct from variational inference; it implicitly mirrors a variational posterior and learned conditional prior in a coupled latent-variable model. • **ELBO as a drop-in unifier**: By deriving Var-JEPA from a single ELBO objective, the paper replaces all ad-hoc anti-collapse regularizers with a principled probabilistic loss, eliminating the need for architectural hacks. • **Uncertainty quantification for free**: Var-JEPA's explicit probabilistic latent structure naturally enables uncertainty estimates in representation space — something standard JEPA cannot do. • **Tabular instantiation (Var-T-JEPA)**: The framework is concretely applied to the challenging tabular data domain, a relatively underexplored setting for JEPA-style methods.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在表格数据基准上进行，将Var-T-JEPA与其直接前身T-JEPA以及强原始特征基线（即不经自监督预训练、直接在输入特征上训练的模型）进行对比。评估涵盖表征学习质量和下游任务性能（分类与回归），使用多个标准表格数据集。实验设置重点检验变分形式化在不依赖T-JEPA防坍缩启发式手段的条件下能否带来性能提升。 Experiments are conducted on tabular data benchmarks, comparing Var-T-JEPA against its direct predecessor T-JEPA and strong raw-feature baselines (e.g., models trained directly on input features without self-supervised pretraining). Evaluation covers both representation learning quality and downstream task performance (classification and regression) across multiple standard tabular datasets. The experimental setup tests whether the variational formulation provides gains without the need for anti-collapse heuristics used in T-JEPA.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者首先从形式上证明，标准JEPA目标——上下文编码器、目标编码器和跨空间预测器——在结构上等价于对一类耦合隐变量生成模型的ELBO各项进行优化。标准JEPA随即被重新解读为该模型的确定性极限：原本通过技巧隐式施加的正则化，在Var-JEPA中被显式的KL散度项所替代。具体地，上下文编码器成为变分后验q(z|x_context)，预测器成为学习到的条件先验p(z|x_target)，重建项在隐空间中进行。整个ELBO端到端优化，无需任何额外防坍缩机制。针对表格数据，作者将其实例化为Var-T-JEPA，调整掩码策略和架构设计以处理异质特征类型。 The authors first formally show that the standard JEPA objective — a context encoder, target encoder, and cross-space predictor — is structurally equivalent to optimizing the components of an ELBO in a coupled latent-variable generative model. Standard JEPA is then recast as a deterministic limit of this model where regularization is enforced implicitly via tricks rather than explicitly via a KL divergence term. Var-JEPA makes this probabilistic structure explicit: the context encoder becomes a variational posterior q(z|x_context), the predictor becomes a learned conditional prior p(z|x_target), and the reconstruction term operates in latent space. The full ELBO is optimized end-to-end without any extra collapse-prevention mechanisms. For tabular data, they instantiate this as Var-T-JEPA, adapting the masking and architecture choices to handle heterogeneous feature types.

An Empirical Study of SFT-DPO Interaction and Parameterization in Small Language Models 小型语言模型中SFT-DPO交互与参数化的实证研究

🧑‍🔬 Yuming Feng, Christy Yang

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 小模型中全参数微调优于LoRA，SFT优于DPO
FFT beats LoRA and SFT beats DPO in small language models with limited data.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 在监督微调（SFT）之后，DPO是一种广泛使用的语言模型对齐方法。但在小型模型和有限数据条件下，SFT与DPO如何相互作用、DPO是否真的有效，目前缺乏系统研究。此外，像LoRA这样的参数高效方法在小规模场景下是否具有实际价值，也尚不明确。 After supervised fine-tuning (SFT), Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) is a popular technique to align language models with human preferences. However, how these two stages interact — and whether DPO actually helps — when working with small models and limited data is poorly understood. Similarly, it's unclear whether parameter-efficient methods like LoRA offer real practical benefits in this small-scale regime.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 核心反直觉发现：在小模型场景下，参数化方式（FFT vs. LoRA）对性能的影响远大于训练策略（SFT vs. DPO）的选择。 • DPO在无需SFT热启动的情况下，只要偏好数据构造与监督目标高度一致，即可匹敌强SFT基线的准确率。 • LoRA在小型硬件上并不节省实际训练时间，且在相同训练深度下始终逊于全参数微调，挑战了其被默认采用的合理性。 • Key finding: Parameterization (FFT vs. LoRA) matters more than the training recipe (SFT vs. DPO) in the small-scale regime — a counterintuitive result challenging common assumptions. • DPO can match competitive SFT accuracy even without a warm-start SFT phase, as long as preference data construction closely mirrors the supervised objective. • LoRA provides no wall-clock speedup on small-scale hardware and consistently underperforms FFT at equivalent training depth, questioning its default adoption for tiny models.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在GPT-2规模的自回归解码器上进行，覆盖两个下游任务：同义句检测（分类类任务）和莎士比亚十四行诗续写（生成类任务）。基线包括仅SFT、仅DPO以及分阶段SFT→DPO流程，每种均在FFT和LoRA两种参数化方式下测试。评估指标包括同义句检测准确率、诗句续写生成质量，以及FFT与LoRA之间的实际训练耗时对比。 Experiments are run on a GPT-2-scale autoregressive decoder across two downstream tasks: paraphrase detection (a classification-style task) and Shakespearean sonnet continuation (a generation task). The baselines compared are SFT-only, DPO-only, and staged SFT→DPO pipelines, each tested under both FFT and LoRA parameterizations. Evaluation metrics include task accuracy for paraphrase detection and generation quality for sonnet continuation, along with wall-clock training time comparisons between FFT and LoRA.
📊 实验
结果		 摘要中未披露具体数值，但论文报告了以下关键结果：（1）DPO相对强SFT基线仅带来"微小且任务相关的提升"，并非一致性改善；（2）在偏好构造与监督目标高度一致的任务上，纯DPO可匹配SFT级别的准确率；（3）在所有训练深度下，FFT在两项任务上均"始终优于"LoRA；（4）LoRA在实验硬件上并未缩短实际训练时间，丧失了其在该规模下的主要实用优势。 Specific numeric results are not detailed in the abstract, but the paper reports that: (1) DPO yields "small, task-dependent gains" over strong SFT baselines rather than consistent improvements; (2) DPO-only can match SFT-level accuracy on tasks where preference construction mirrors the supervised objective; (3) FFT "consistently outperforms" LoRA across matched training depths on both tasks; and (4) LoRA does not reduce wall-clock training time on the hardware used, negating its primary practical advantage in this regime.
🏁 结论 In the small-model, limited-data regime, full-parameter fine-tuning is the dominant performance lever, while both DPO and LoRA offer limited marginal returns. The paper provides practical guidance for practitioners working with small language models, cautioning against the uncritical adoption of popular alignment and efficiency techniques at this scale.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者在GPT-2规模的解码器模型上进行了系统性受控实验，涵盖两项任务：同义句检测和莎士比亚十四行诗续写。实验对比了四种训练配置：仅SFT、仅DPO、分阶段SFT→DPO，以及上述配置分别结合全参数微调（FFT）或LoRA的组合。为隔离对齐阶段的影响，DPO的偏好对被设计为与监督目标高度对应。实验同时记录了性能指标与实际训练时间，以评估实践中的权衡。 The authors conduct a controlled empirical study on a GPT-2-scale decoder model across two tasks: paraphrase detection and Shakespearean sonnet continuation. They compare four training configurations: SFT-only, DPO-only, staged SFT→DPO, and combinations of these with either full fine-tuning (FFT) or LoRA. Preference pairs for DPO are constructed to closely mirror the supervised objective to isolate the effect of the alignment stage. Performance and wall-clock training time are both measured to assess practical tradeoffs.
