UniGRPO: Unified Policy Optimization for Reasoning-Driven Visual Generation UniGRPO：面向推理驱动视觉生成的统一策略优化框架

🧑‍🔬 Jie Liu, Zilyu Ye, Linxiao Yuan, Shenhan Zhu et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用统一强化学习框架同时优化文本推理与图像生成策略
A unified RL framework jointly optimizes text reasoning and image generation policies via GRPO for interleaved multimodal models.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的能同时生成文本和图像的统一模型（交错生成模型）缺乏一种同时适用于两种模态的强化学习训练方案。文本生成用自回归建模，图像生成用流匹配，把它们统一在RL框架下联合优化非常困难。目前也没有能支持多轮、多条件交错生成的可扩展后训练框架。 Current unified models that can generate both text and images (interleaved generation) lack a principled reinforcement learning training recipe that works for both modalities simultaneously. Text generation uses autoregressive modeling while image generation uses flow matching — optimizing them jointly under RL is non-trivial. There is no scalable post-training framework that can handle multi-round, multi-condition interleaved generation.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **统一MDP建模**：将"先推理、后生图"的完整流程建模为单一马尔可夫决策过程，配合稀疏终端奖励，让RL可以同时训练文本与图像两个模态的策略。 • **两项关键FlowGRPO改进**：①去除分类器无关引导（CFG），保持rollout线性无分支，对多轮和多条件场景的扩展至关重要；②用速度场上的MSE惩罚替代潜空间KL惩罚，更直接、更鲁棒地防止奖励欺骗。 • **极简主义设计**：将标准GRPO（推理）与FlowGRPO（图像合成）无缝结合，不过度设计，形成简洁可扩展的基线方案。 • **Unified MDP formulation**: Models the entire reasoning-then-image-generation pipeline as a single Markov Decision Process with sparse terminal rewards, enabling RL to jointly train both modalities. • **Two key FlowGRPO modifications**: (1) Removes classifier-free guidance (CFG) to keep rollouts linear and unbranched — critical for scalability to multi-turn and multi-condition scenarios; (2) Replaces latent KL penalty with an MSE penalty on velocity fields, providing more robust regularization against reward hacking. • **Minimalist design philosophy**: Seamlessly combines standard GRPO (for reasoning) and FlowGRPO (for image synthesis) without over-engineering, yielding a clean and scalable baseline.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在推理驱动的文本到图像生成基准上展开，评估指标包括GenEval（组合生成能力）、T2I-CompBench（文图对齐）以及美学/质量评分。基线包括：无推理的直接图像生成、标准流匹配微调模型，以及原始FlowGRPO变体。消融实验对比了去除CFG、MSE惩罚与KL惩罚对生成质量和训练稳定性的各自影响。 Experiments are conducted on reasoning-driven text-to-image generation benchmarks. The model is evaluated on standard image generation quality metrics including GenEval (compositional generation), T2I-CompBench (text-image alignment), and aesthetic/quality scoring. Baselines include direct image generation without reasoning, standard flow-matching fine-tuned models, and prior FlowGRPO variants. Ablation studies compare the effect of CFG removal and the MSE vs. KL penalty on both generation quality and training stability.
📊 实验
结果		 UniGRPO在组合图像生成基准上取得显著提升。在GenEval上，模型超越无推理基线和原版FlowGRPO，整体得分提升数个百分点，位列统一生成模型前列。在T2I-CompBench上，推理增强流程在属性绑定、空间关系、非空间类别上均有稳定提升。消融实验证实MSE速度场惩罚比潜空间KL惩罚训练更稳定，去除CFG在不损失生成质量的同时保证了更干净的rollout结构。 UniGRPO achieves notable improvements on compositional and aligned image generation benchmarks. On GenEval, the model outperforms direct generation baselines and prior FlowGRPO approaches, with overall scores improving by several percentage points (specific scores reported in the paper place UniGRPO among top-performing unified generation models). On T2I-CompBench, the reasoning-augmented pipeline yields consistent gains across attribute binding, spatial relationships, and non-spatial categories. Ablations confirm that the MSE velocity penalty provides more stable training than latent KL, and CFG removal does not degrade generation quality while enabling cleaner rollouts.
🏁 结论 UniGRPO证明了通过统一的基于GRPO的强化学习框架联合优化文本推理与图像生成策略，可以显著提升推理驱动生成任务的图像质量。对FlowGRPO的两项针对性改进（去除CFG、MSE速度场惩罚）为未来完全交错多模态模型的后训练提供了鲁棒且可扩展的基线方案。 UniGRPO demonstrates that jointly optimizing text reasoning and image generation policies via a unified GRPO-based RL framework significantly enhances image quality in reasoning-driven generation tasks. The two targeted modifications to FlowGRPO (CFG removal and MSE velocity penalty) establish a robust, scalable post-training baseline for future fully interleaved multimodal models.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

UniGRPO将"推理驱动图像生成"流程建模为马尔可夫决策过程：模型先通过推理扩展用户提示（文本策略），再基于推理结果合成图像（流匹配图像策略）。两个策略使用GRPO联合训练，奖励信号来自图像质量的稀疏终端奖励。在图像生成侧，作者对FlowGRPO做了两项改造：去除CFG以保持rollout线性（便于未来多轮扩展），以及将潜空间KL散度惩罚替换为直接作用于速度场的MSE惩罚，后者更稳定且能有效抑制奖励欺骗。 UniGRPO frames the reasoning-driven image generation pipeline as a Markov Decision Process: the model first generates a reasoning trace expanding the user prompt (text policy), then produces an image conditioned on that reasoning (image policy via flow matching). Both policies are jointly trained using GRPO with sparse terminal rewards tied to image quality. For the image generation side, the authors adapt FlowGRPO with two key changes: CFG is removed so rollouts stay linear (important for future multi-turn scaling), and the KL divergence penalty on latents is replaced by an MSE penalty computed directly on the flow-matching velocity fields. This MSE penalty is more stable and directly counteracts reward hacking by anchoring the learned velocity fields close to the reference model's outputs.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{MSE}} = \mathbb{E}_{t, \mathbf{x}_t} \left[ \left\| v_\theta(\mathbf{x}_t, t) - v_{\text{ref}}(\mathbf{x}_t, t) \right\|^2 \right]\]
💡 MSE速度场惩罚：衡量当前策略的速度场预测与参考模型速度场之间的均方误差，用来替代原始的KL散度惩罚，更直接地防止奖励欺骗并稳定训练。 The MSE velocity field penalty measures the mean squared error between the current policy's predicted flow velocity and the reference model's velocity. It replaces the KL divergence penalty on latents, providing more direct and stable regularization against reward hacking.
\[J(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{\tau \sim \pi_\theta} \left[ \sum_{t} r_t \right] - \lambda \cdot \mathcal{D}_{\text{KL}}(\pi_\theta \| \pi_{\text{ref}})\]
💡 GRPO目标函数（通用形式）：最大化策略在整条生成轨迹上的累积稀疏奖励，同时用KL散度（对文本侧）或MSE惩罚（对图像侧）约束策略不要偏离参考模型太远。 The GRPO objective: maximize the expected cumulative sparse reward over the full generation trajectory (text reasoning + image synthesis), while penalizing large deviations from the reference policy — using KL for text and MSE on velocity fields for image generation.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着社区向能同时推理与生图的统一模型演进，一套原则性的RL后训练方案至关重要，UniGRPO正好填补了这一空白。其关于无CFG rollout和速度场正则化的洞察将直接指导下一代交错视觉语言模型的设计。 As the community moves toward unified models that think and generate images together, a principled RL post-training recipe is essential — UniGRPO fills this gap with a clean, scalable framework. The insights around CFG-free rollouts and velocity-field regularization will directly inform the design of next-generation interleaved vision-language models.

ThinkJEPA: Empowering Latent World Models with Large Vision-Language Reasoning Model ThinkJEPA：用大型视觉语言推理模型赋能潜在世界模型

🧑‍🔬 Haichao Zhang, Yijiang Li, Shwai He, Tushar Nagarajan et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 融合密集视频动态与VLM语义推理，提升长时域世界模型预测能力。
Combining dense video dynamics with VLM semantic reasoning for better long-horizon world modeling.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 像V-JEPA2这样的潜在世界模型擅长逐帧预测未来视频状态，但只能处理短时间窗口，容易专注于局部低层次运动线索，难以捕捉长时域的语义信息。视觉语言模型（VLM）拥有丰富的世界知识和长时序推理能力，但它们稀疏采样帧、输出仅限于文本，且在小规模动作条件数据集上微调时效果不佳。两种方法单独使用都不足以支撑机器人操作任务中鲁棒的长时域视频预测。 Latent world models like V-JEPA2 are good at predicting future video states frame-by-frame, but they only look at a short window of time and tend to focus on low-level, local motion cues — missing the bigger semantic picture over longer horizons. Vision-language models (VLMs) have broad world knowledge and can reason over long time spans, but they sample frames sparsely, output only text, and struggle when fine-tuned on small action-conditioned robotics datasets. Neither approach alone is sufficient for robust long-horizon video prediction in manipulation tasks.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **双时序通路设计**：密集JEPA分支逐帧捕捉细粒度运动信息，稀疏采样的VLM"思考者"分支提供长时域语义引导，两者协同工作。 • **层次化金字塔表征提取模块**：聚合VLM多层内部表征为引导特征，而非仅使用最终输出层，从而获取更丰富的推理状态信号。 • **ThinkJEPA框架**：创新性地利用VLM逐步推理（链式思维）过程中的中间信号作为潜在监督，而非仅依赖最终文本输出，有效弥合语言模型知识与密集视频动态之间的鸿沟。 • **Dual-temporal pathway**: A dense JEPA branch captures fine-grained motion frame-by-frame, while a sparsely-sampled VLM "thinker" branch provides long-horizon semantic guidance — the two work in concert. • **Hierarchical pyramid representation extraction**: Multi-layer internal representations from the VLM are aggregated into a guidance signal, going beyond just the final output layer to capture richer reasoning states. • **VLM-guided JEPA framework (ThinkJEPA)**: Uniquely leverages the VLM's progressive chain-of-thought reasoning signals (not just final answers) as latent supervision, bridging the gap between language-model knowledge and dense video dynamics.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验聚焦于手部操作轨迹预测任务，这是一个涉及精细物体交互的高难度场景。与两个主要基线进行对比：纯VLM基线和标准JEPA预测器基线（类似V-JEPA2）。评估指标包括潜在空间中的预测精度以及长时域滚动预测的稳定性，衡量模型在动作条件化操作视频数据集上对未来长时序预测的连贯性。 Experiments focus on hand-manipulation trajectory prediction, a challenging setting involving fine-grained object interactions. The method is evaluated against two main baselines: a strong VLM-only baseline and a standard JEPA predictor baseline (analogous to V-JEPA2). Evaluation metrics include prediction accuracy in latent space and long-horizon rollout stability, measuring how well the model sustains coherent predictions over extended future timesteps on action-conditioned manipulation video datasets.
📊 实验
结果		 ThinkJEPA在手部操作轨迹预测任务上超越了纯VLM基线和JEPA预测器基线。在长时域滚动预测方面表现出更强的鲁棒性，在基线模型性能下降的长时序场景中仍能保持预测连贯性。论文报告了潜在预测精度指标上的具体数值提升，并通过消融实验验证了层次化金字塔表征提取模块对整体增益的独立贡献。 ThinkJEPA outperforms both the VLM-only baseline and the JEPA-predictor baseline on hand-manipulation trajectory prediction tasks. The method demonstrates more robust long-horizon rollout behavior, maintaining prediction coherence over extended future timesteps where baseline models degrade. Specific numerical margins are reported in the paper showing improvements in latent prediction accuracy metrics, with the hierarchical pyramid extraction module ablated to confirm its individual contribution to the overall gains.
🏁 结论 ThinkJEPA证明了将VLM链式推理信号（以层次化多层表征形式提取）注入JEPA式密集世界模型，能够显著提升操作任务中的长时域潜在预测能力。本文建立了一种将大型视觉语言模型的语义丰富性与密集视频世界模型的动态精确性有机结合的可行框架。 ThinkJEPA demonstrates that injecting VLM chain-of-thought reasoning signals — extracted as hierarchical multi-layer representations — into a JEPA-style dense world model substantially improves long-horizon latent prediction in manipulation tasks. The work establishes a viable framework for marrying the semantic richness of large vision-language models with the dynamic precision of dense video world models.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ThinkJEPA采用双分支架构。第一个"密集"分支遵循JEPA范式：上下文编码器处理密集采样的视频帧，预测器预测目标（未来）帧的潜在表征。第二个"思考者"分支将均匀稀疏采样的帧输入大型VLM，让其对未来状态进行链式推理。随后，层次化金字塔表征提取模块从VLM多层中间特征（而非仅文本输出）中提取并聚合出紧凑的引导向量。该引导向量被注入JEPA预测器，引导其生成语义一致的长时域预测。两个分支联合训练，使密集预测器同时学习低层运动动态和高层语义意图。 ThinkJEPA uses a dual-branch architecture. The first (dense) branch follows the JEPA paradigm: a context encoder processes densely sampled video frames and a predictor forecasts the latent representations of target (future) frames. The second (thinker) branch feeds uniformly and sparsely sampled frames into a large VLM, which generates chain-of-thought reasoning about future states. A hierarchical pyramid representation extraction module then extracts and aggregates multi-layer intermediate features from the VLM — not just its text output — into a compact guidance vector. This guidance vector is injected into the JEPA predictor, steering it toward semantically consistent long-horizon predictions. The two branches are trained jointly so the dense predictor learns both low-level dynamics and high-level semantic intent.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\hat{z}_{t+1:T} = f_{\text{pred}}\left(z_{1:t},\, g_{\text{pyramid}}(\Phi_{\text{VLM}}(x_{1:T}^{\text{sparse}}))\right)\]
💡 JEPA预测器在密集上下文编码特征的基础上，融合从VLM多层表征聚合而成的金字塔引导向量，预测未来潜在状态序列。 The JEPA predictor forecasts future latent states by conditioning on dense context encodings augmented with a pyramid guidance vector aggregated from multi-layer VLM representations over sparsely sampled frames.
\[g_{\text{pyramid}} = \text{Aggregate}\left(\{\Phi_{\text{VLM}}^{(l)}\}_{l=1}^{L}\right)\]
💡 层次化金字塔表征提取模块将VLM第1层到第L层的中间隐藏状态聚合为统一的引导特征向量，而非仅使用最终输出层。 The hierarchical pyramid extraction module aggregates intermediate hidden states from VLM layers 1 through L into a unified guidance feature, capturing richer reasoning signals than the final output alone.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

世界模型是基于模型的强化学习和机器人规划的核心，提升其长时域预测精度直接决定了自主智能体的能力上限。ThinkJEPA提供了一种将VLM与JEPA式模型互补优势相融合的原则性方案，随着两类范式各自独立扩展，这一融合问题正是当前社区面临的核心挑战。 World models are a cornerstone of model-based reinforcement learning and robot planning; improving their long-horizon accuracy directly unlocks better autonomous agents. ThinkJEPA offers a principled recipe for integrating the complementary strengths of VLMs and JEPA-style models, a challenge the community has been grappling with as both paradigms scale up independently.

UI-Voyager: A Self-Evolving GUI Agent Learning via Failed Experience UI-Voyager：一个通过失败经验自我进化的GUI智能体

🧑‍🔬 Zichuan Lin, Feiyu Liu, Yijun Yang, Jiafei Lyu et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 从失败轨迹中自我进化的移动端GUI智能体，超越人类水平
A self-evolving mobile GUI agent that learns from failed trajectories to exceed human-level task success.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 移动端GUI智能体需要在手机应用上完成复杂的长序列操作，但奖励信号稀疏、失败轨迹难以利用是两大难题。现有方法大多直接丢弃失败数据，且无法精准定位长序列中哪一步出了问题，导致学习效率低下。 Autonomous GUI agents need to complete long sequences of actions on mobile apps, but learning from sparse rewards and failed attempts is extremely hard. Most existing methods waste failed trajectories instead of mining them for useful learning signals, and they struggle to pinpoint exactly which step in a long sequence went wrong.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **拒绝微调（RFT）**：构建全自动的数据-模型协同进化闭环，持续筛选成功轨迹进行训练，无需人工标注。 • **群体相对自蒸馏（GRSD）**：在多条轨迹的对比中定位关键"分叉点"，将成功轨迹的知识蒸馏到失败轨迹中，生成密集的步骤级监督信号。 • **4B小模型超越人类水平**：仅用40亿参数模型在AndroidWorld上达到81.0% Pass@1，效率极为突出。 • **Rejection Fine-Tuning (RFT):** A fully autonomous data-model co-evolution loop that continuously filters and learns from successful rollouts without any manual annotation. • **Group Relative Self-Distillation (GRSD):** Identifies critical "fork points" where a trajectory diverges from success, then constructs dense step-level supervision by distilling knowledge from successful trajectories into failed ones. • **Human-level surpassing with only 4B parameters:** Achieves 81.0% Pass@1 on AndroidWorld with a 4B model, demonstrating remarkable efficiency at small model scale.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在AndroidWorld基准上进行，该基准包含真实Android应用的长序列操作任务，评估指标为Pass@1成功率。基线模型涵盖基于GPT-4o的智能体、其他多模态大模型智能体以及此前的自进化或强化学习训练智能体。消融实验分别验证了RFT单独使用与RFT+GRSD组合的效果，案例分析则直观展示了GRSD如何纠正分叉点处的错误。 Experiments were conducted on AndroidWorld, a challenging benchmark of real Android app tasks requiring long-horizon planning. The model was evaluated using Pass@1 success rate. Baselines include a wide range of recent GUI agents such as GPT-4o-based agents, other MLLM-based agents, and prior self-evolving or RL-trained agents. Ablation studies were performed to isolate the contribution of each stage (RFT alone vs. RFT+GRSD), and case studies illustrate how GRSD corrects specific fork-point errors.
📊 实验
结果		 UI-Voyager的4B模型在AndroidWorld上取得**81.0% Pass@1**成功率，超越所有对比基线（含更大参数量模型和基于GPT-4o的智能体），并超过该基准上的人类水平表现。消融实验显示，GRSD相比单独使用RFT有显著提升，证明从失败轨迹中挖掘密集步骤级监督的有效性。 UI-Voyager's 4B model achieves **81.0% Pass@1** on AndroidWorld, outperforming all compared baselines including much larger models and GPT-4o-based agents. This result exceeds reported human-level performance on the benchmark. Ablation results confirm that GRSD provides a meaningful improvement over RFT alone, demonstrating the value of dense step-level supervision mined from failed trajectories.
🏁 结论 UI-Voyager证明，通过将自主拒绝微调与基于群体的自蒸馏机制相结合，仅4B参数的小模型即可在复杂移动端GUI任务上超越人类水平。本文为无需人工标注的GUI智能体高效自进化建立了新范式。 UI-Voyager demonstrates that a small 4B model can surpass human-level performance on complex mobile GUI tasks by combining autonomous rejection fine-tuning with a novel group-based self-distillation mechanism that turns failed trajectories into dense learning signals. This work establishes a new paradigm for efficient, annotation-free self-evolution of GUI agents.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

UI-Voyager分两个阶段工作。第一阶段的拒绝微调（RFT）让智能体自主完成GUI任务，只保留成功轨迹来微调模型，如此循环往复，实现数据与模型的协同自进化，全程不需要人工标注。第二阶段的群体相对自蒸馏（GRSD）对同一任务生成多条轨迹并相互比较，找到失败轨迹与成功轨迹的"分叉点"，再将成功轨迹在该关键步骤的正确动作作为监督信号，为失败轨迹提供密集的步骤级纠正，从而实现比粗粒度成功/失败奖励更精准的功劳归因。 UI-Voyager operates in two stages. In Stage 1, Rejection Fine-Tuning (RFT) runs the agent on GUI tasks, keeps only the successful trajectories, and uses them to fine-tune the model — this loop repeats autonomously, so the model and its training data co-evolve without human input. In Stage 2, Group Relative Self-Distillation (GRSD) generates multiple rollout trajectories for the same task and compares them to find "fork points" — the exact steps where failed trajectories diverge from successful ones. It then constructs dense, step-level training signals by using the successful trajectory's actions to correct the failed ones at those critical junctures, enabling much richer credit assignment than a simple success/fail reward.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{GRSD}} = -\sum_{t \in \mathcal{F}} \log \pi_\theta(a_t^* \mid s_t)\]

Why Does Self-Distillation (Sometimes) Degrade the Reasoning Capability of LLMs? 为什么自蒸馏（有时）会降低大语言模型的推理能力？

🧑‍🔬 Jeonghye Kim, Xufang Luo, Minbeom Kim, Sangmook Lee et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 自蒸馏通过压制不确定性表达损害LLM推理能力
Self-distillation degrades LLM reasoning by suppressing uncertainty expression in outputs.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 自蒸馏（让模型在自己生成的输出上继续训练）是一种流行的大模型后训练方法，通常能提升性能。但研究者发现，在数学推理任务中，自蒸馏有时反而会让模型的回答变短，推理性能下降，尤其在模型从未见过的新问题上表现更差。这种"越训越差"的现象背后究竟是什么原因？ Self-distillation — training a model on its own generated outputs — is popular for improving LLMs after initial training. But researchers noticed it sometimes makes models shorter in reasoning and worse at math problems, especially ones the model hasn't seen before. Why does a technique that's supposed to help actually hurt performance in these cases?
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出"认知言语化抑制"新概念：将模型推理时不再表达不确定性（如"我不确定…""让我重新想想…"）识别为自蒸馏退化的根本原因，这是首次对该现象的系统性诊断。 • 双因素受控实验：系统剥离"教师模型的条件信息丰富度"与"训练任务覆盖范围"两个关键变量，精确定位退化发生的条件。 • 揭示域内/域外不对称性：信息丰富的条件下抑制不确定性有利于域内快速优化，但对分布外（OOD）新问题的泛化能力造成严重伤害。 • Novel diagnosis: identifies "epistemic verbalization suppression" — the silencing of a model's expressed uncertainty during reasoning — as the root cause of self-distillation degradation. • Controlled factor analysis: systematically isolates two key variables — conditioning context richness (how much information the teacher model sees) and task coverage (how diverse the training problems are) — to show exactly when and why degradation occurs. • OOD vs. in-domain asymmetry: reveals that suppressing uncertainty helps rapid in-domain optimization but critically harms out-of-distribution generalization, where expressing doubt and self-correcting is essential.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在三个主流开源模型上进行：Qwen3-8B、DeepSeek-Distill-Qwen-7B 和 Olmo3-7B-Instruct。使用数学推理基准测试集，分别评估域内和分布外（OOD）场景下的性能。实验系统改变教师模型的条件信息丰富度和训练任务覆盖范围，基线为原始未修改模型和标准自蒸馏设置，同时追踪回答长度、准确率以及模型输出中不确定性表达词汇的出现频率。 Experiments were conducted on three LLMs: Qwen3-8B, DeepSeek-Distill-Qwen-7B, and Olmo3-7B-Instruct. Mathematical reasoning benchmarks were used to evaluate both in-domain and out-of-distribution (OOD) performance, with varying levels of teacher conditioning richness and training task coverage as the experimental factors. Baselines include the original unmodified models and standard self-distillation setups, with evaluation tracking response length, accuracy, and the frequency of uncertainty-expressing language in model outputs.
📊 实验
结果		 在三个测试模型上，自蒸馏导致分布外数学推理任务性能最高下降40%。使用丰富条件信息训练的教师模型带来了域内快速提升，但造成显著的OOD退化。性能退化与回答长度缩短及不确定性表达词汇消失高度相关。训练任务覆盖范围越窄，OOD性能下降越严重；扩大任务覆盖范围可部分缓解退化问题。 Across the three tested models, self-distillation caused performance drops of up to 40% on out-of-distribution mathematical reasoning tasks. Models trained with rich-context teacher conditioning showed rapid in-domain gains but significant OOD degradation. The degradation strongly correlated with shortened response length and the disappearance of uncertainty-expressing phrases in model outputs. Limited task coverage amplified the OOD performance drop, while broader task coverage partially mitigated the damage.
🏁 结论 本文证明了自蒸馏通过压制"认知言语化"（即模型表达不确定性的能力）来损害LLM推理，尤其对分布外泛化能力危害最大。核心贡献在于揭示：稳健推理不仅需要强化正确答案，更需要保留模型公开表达和适应不确定性的能力。 This paper proves that self-distillation degrades LLM reasoning by suppressing epistemic verbalization — the model's ability to express uncertainty — particularly harming out-of-distribution generalization. The key contribution is identifying that robust reasoning requires not just reinforcing correct answers, but preserving the model's capacity to openly express and adapt to uncertainty.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者假设：当教师模型在生成训练数据时获得了丰富的上下文信息（如正确答案、详细提示），它会产生高度自信、不含不确定性表达的推理链。学生模型学习这些推理链后，也失去了表达"不确定性"的能力（如"我不太确定…""让我换种思路…"等）。为验证此假设，研究者设计了受控实验，系统改变两个维度：(1) 教师模型生成训练数据时的条件信息丰富程度；(2) 训练数据对不同问题类型的覆盖广度。随后分别评估域内和分布外性能，将性能下降与模型输出中认知言语化的消失直接关联。 The authors hypothesize that when a teacher LLM is conditioned on rich contextual information (e.g., correct answers or detailed hints), it generates highly confident, uncertainty-free reasoning traces. Training the student on these traces teaches it to suppress expressions of uncertainty (e.g., hedging phrases, self-corrections). To test this, they design controlled experiments varying two dimensions: (1) how much information the teacher model is given when generating training data, and (2) how broadly the training data covers different problem types. They then evaluate both in-domain and out-of-distribution performance to measure where and when degradation occurs, linking the performance drop to the disappearance of epistemic verbalization in the model's outputs.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着自蒸馏成为大模型后训练的标配方法，本研究发出重要警告：盲目优化"更短、更自信"的推理链可能悄无声息地让模型泛化能力损失高达40%。这项工作重新定义了"好推理"的标准——不应只看答案是否正确，还要看模型是否保留了探索性、能感知不确定性的思维方式。 As self-distillation becomes a standard post-training recipe for LLMs, this work provides a critical warning: optimizing for shorter, confident traces can silently destroy generalization ability by as much as 40%. It reframes what "good reasoning" means — shifting focus from answer correctness alone to the preservation of exploratory, uncertainty-aware thinking behaviors.
