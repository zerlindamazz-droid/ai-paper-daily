⚙️ Method · 方法详解

HoloAgent-0 的核心思路是给机器人配一个"大脑操作系统"。当你对机器人说出一个指令时，系统首先用大语言模型把指令拆解成一系列步骤，形成一张"任务图"；接着，它会从三维空间记忆中查找相关物体和位置；然后，把具体动作分配给合适的机器人模块（比如手臂、底盘、视觉感知）去执行；最后，系统持续监控执行过程，一旦出现问题（比如抓取失败），立刻重新规划。这三层结构——操作系统、空间记忆、具身技能——紧密耦合，形成一个闭环的真实世界执行系统。 Think of HoloAgent-0 as giving a robot a full "operating system" for its brain. When you give it a language command, a large language model first breaks it into a step-by-step task graph — like a recipe. The robot then consults its 3D spatial memory to find where relevant objects are in the room. It assigns each step to the right robotic skill (arm movement, wheel navigation, object detection), and a supervisor layer watches the execution in real time. If something goes wrong — say, a grasp fails — the system automatically detects the problem and re-plans on the fly. Three tightly connected layers (OS, spatial memory, skills) keep everything coordinated.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项研究向"真正听话的机器人"迈出了关键一步——未来的家庭机器人、工厂助手或医疗护理机器人，都需要这样一个能统筹全局、自我纠错的"大脑"。它把过去各自为战的机器人AI模块整合成了一个协调运作的系统，大大降低了将语言指令落地为真实机器人行动的门槛。 This research is a key step toward robots that can truly follow everyday spoken instructions — the kind of system needed for home assistants, hospital helpers, or factory robots. By unifying previously fragmented robot AI modules into one coordinated framework, it lowers the barrier to building real-world robots that understand, remember, and act reliably.

🧑‍🔬 Yutian Cheng, Xiaojian Ma, Xianhao Wang, Min Yang et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 让AI机器人"按需重规划"，大幅减少计算消耗同时保持任务表现
A smart wrapper that makes AI robots replan only when truly necessary, slashing compute without hurting performance.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个有经验的司机开车时不需要每秒都重新查看导航——只有当实际路况和导航预测明显不符时（比如突然遇到堵车或施工），才会重新规划路线。AdaReP就是这个"经验老道的司机直觉"，让AI机器人学会按需重规划，而不是每步都傻傻地重算一遍。Think of it like an experienced driver who doesn't check the GPS every single second — they only reroute when something unexpected happens, like a sudden traffic jam. AdaReP gives AI robots that same smart instinct: coast along the existing plan when things are on track, and only replan when reality starts to diverge from the prediction.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 AI机器人在执行任务时，通常依赖一个"世界模型"来预测未来，并在每一步都重新规划下一步行动。但这种"每步都重新计算"的方式非常耗时耗力。如果直接复用旧计划，误差又会越积越大，导致任务失败。如何在"频繁重规划（准确但慢）"和"复用旧计划（快但可能出错）"之间找到最佳平衡，是一个难题。 AI robots that use "world models" to predict the future typically replan their actions at every single step to stay accurate — but this is extremely slow and computationally expensive. On the other hand, reusing an old cached plan saves time but lets errors accumulate, potentially causing task failures. The challenge is finding the sweet spot: replan only when truly needed, not every single step.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次用"扰动动态遗憾"理论框架，严格分析了"复用旧计划"的代价如何随误差积累和环境敏感度变化 • 提出AdaReP：一个无需修改原有模型或规划器的"即插即用"包装器，在线自适应决定何时重规划 • 同时适用于图像空间规划、潜在空间控制和真实机器人操控任务，泛化能力强 • 在真实物理机器人上实现超过80%的重规划次数减少，极具实用价值 • First theoretical framework (perturbation-based dynamic regret) to rigorously quantify the cost of reusing stale plans based on error accumulation and environment sensitivity. • AdaReP is a plug-and-play wrapper — no retraining or modifying the existing world model or planner needed. • Works across image-space planning, latent-space control, and real physical robot tasks. • Achieves over 80% reduction in replanning queries on a real robot with no meaningful drop in task success.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验覆盖三大场景：(1) 图像空间规划（基于视觉像素直接规划的任务）；(2) 潜在空间控制（在压缩的隐变量空间中规划，如DreamerV3等方法）；(3) 真实物理机器人操控实验（50次真实机器人操作试验）。对比基线包括"每步都重规划"的标准MPC方法，以及固定频率重规划策略。评估指标包括任务成功率和规划器查询次数（计算开销）。 Experiments were conducted in three settings: (1) image-space planning tasks where the AI plans directly from raw visual pixels; (2) latent-space control tasks where planning happens in a compressed internal representation (like DreamerV3-style models); and (3) a 50-trial physical robot manipulation study with a real robotic arm. Baselines included the standard "replan every step" MPC approach and fixed-frequency replanning strategies. Key metrics were task success rate and number of planner queries (a proxy for computational cost).
📊 实验
结果		 在真实物理机器人实验（50次试验）中，AdaReP将重规划查询次数减少超过80%，同时保持与每步重规划相当的任务成功率。在图像空间和潜在空间的仿真任务中，AdaReP同样大幅削减计算开销，任务表现与全量重规划基线相当甚至持平，显著优于固定频率重规划策略。 On the 50-trial physical robot study, AdaReP reduced replanning queries by over 80% while maintaining task success rates comparable to replanning at every step. In simulation tasks across both image-space and latent-space settings, AdaReP substantially cut computational overhead while matching the performance of full replanning baselines, and outperformed fixed-frequency replanning strategies.
🏁 结论 本文证明了：通过在线监测预测偏差和环境敏感度，AI规划系统可以智能地决定"什么时候才真正需要重规划"，从而将计算开销减少80%以上，同时保持任务成功率不下降。AdaReP为神经世界模型控制的高效部署提供了一个通用、免训练的实用解决方案。 This paper proves that AI planners don't need to replan at every single step — by smartly monitoring prediction drift and local environment sensitivity, they can replan only when truly necessary, cutting computation by over 80% with no meaningful loss in task success. AdaReP offers a practical, training-free solution for deploying neural world-model controllers more efficiently in the real world.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AdaReP的核心思想是：不再每步都重新规划，而是实时监测"当前实际状态"与"之前计划预测的状态"之间的偏差，同时估计当前环境的"局部敏感度"（即环境变化有多剧烈）。如果偏差很小、环境也比较平稳，就继续复用旧计划；一旦偏差超过动态调整的容忍阈值，才触发重规划。这个容忍阈值本身也会根据实时信息自动更新，做到真正的"按需重规划"。整个过程不需要修改已有的世界模型或规划算法，像给现有系统套了一层智能外壳。 AdaReP acts like a watchful supervisor sitting on top of an existing AI planner. At each step, it measures how far the robot's actual situation has drifted from what the old plan predicted, and also estimates how "turbulent" the current environment is (i.e., how sensitive small errors are to blowing up). If things are still close to the predicted path and the environment is stable, it says "keep using the old plan." Only when the drift exceeds a dynamically adjusted tolerance threshold does it trigger a fresh replan. Crucially, this tolerance threshold updates itself in real time — no manual tuning needed — and nothing about the underlying world model or planner is changed.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Regret}(T) \leq \sum_{t=1}^{T} \left( \epsilon_t \cdot \kappa_t \cdot \Delta_t \right)\]
💡 复用旧计划的代价（遗憾）随三个因素增大：容忍阈值ε（允许多大偏差）、局部动态敏感度κ（环境变化有多剧烈）、以及自上次重规划以来的累积预测误差Δ。AdaReP通过在线估计κ和Δ来动态调整ε，从而控制总代价。 The penalty for reusing a stale plan grows with three factors: the reuse tolerance ε (how much drift you allow), the local dynamics sensitivity κ (how chaotic the environment is), and the accumulated mismatch Δ since the last replan. AdaReP monitors κ and Δ online to adaptively tune ε and keep the total penalty bounded.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

机器人和自动驾驶等实时AI系统对计算速度要求极高，每步都重规划在真实部署中往往不可行。AdaReP让这些系统在不牺牲性能的前提下大幅提速，是将世界模型从实验室推向现实应用的重要一步。 Real-world robots and autonomous vehicles can't afford to spend huge compute on replanning every millisecond. AdaReP makes powerful AI planning systems dramatically faster and more practical to deploy, bringing smart world-model controllers one step closer to everyday real-world use.

维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现代AI助手越来越多地被要求从海量工具库中挑选合适的工具，一步一步完成复杂任务——比如一个购物助手需要依次搜索商品、查库存、用优惠券、安排配送。但现有的AI测试只用了少量工具和简单任务，根本不能反映真实世界的复杂度。这篇论文要问的是：当工具库巨大、任务漫长、某些工具还坏掉或会误导AI时，会发生什么？ AI assistants are increasingly asked to complete complex, multi-step tasks by choosing and using the right tools from a massive library — think of a shopping assistant that must search products, check inventory, apply coupons, and arrange delivery all in sequence. ⚙️ Method · 方法详解

研究者构建了一个名为PlanBench-XL的交互式基准，包含327个零售类任务（如处理退货、寻找替代品、管理订单），对应1665个可用工具。关键在于，AI智能体不能一次看到所有工具，必须像人用搜索引擎一样逐步检索，边做边找。在此基础上，基准还可以选择性地加入"阻断"：某些工具可能被移除（缺失）、损坏（故障）或被看似正确但实际错误的工具替代（干扰）。AI必须察觉计划被打乱，并重新规划路线——就像导航遇到封路后自动绕行。 The researchers built an interactive benchmark called PlanBench-XL with 327 retail tasks (like handling returns, finding substitutes, or managing orders) spread across a pool of 1,665 tools. Crucially, agents cannot see all tools at once — they must search and retrieve relevant ones step by step, just like a person using a search engine rather than memorizing an entire manual. On top of this, the benchmark optionally introduces "blocks": some tools may be removed (missing), broken (failing), or replaced with a convincing but wrong alternative (distracting). Agents must recognize when their planned path is disrupted and reroute — like a GPS finding a detour when a road is closed.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

团队训练了一个90亿参数的AI模型（DataClaw-0），让它像一个聪明的数据编辑员一样工作。首先，他们解决了"先有鸡还是先有蛋"的难题——通过把AI生成的内容锚定到真实的事实上，在五个领域（物理和数字世界）生成大规模合成训练数据，确保数据准确不乱编。然后分两步训练模型：第一步用监督学习教会基本的数据整理技能；第二步用强化学习（GRPO）让模型更精准地理解并执行用户的复杂需求。最终，这个模型能把杂乱的原始多模态数据，智能地重组成适合特定任务的高质量数据集。 The team trains a 9-billion-parameter AI model (DataClaw-0) to act like a smart data editor. First, they solve the "chicken-and-egg" problem of needing training data to build the system: they create a large synthetic dataset by anchoring AI-generated content to verified real facts across five domains (physical and digital), so the generated examples stay accurate. Then, the model is trained in two stages — first with standard supervised learning to teach basic data refinement skills, then with reinforcement learning (GRPO) to sharpen its ability to follow complex, specific instructions from users. The result is a model that can take raw, noisy multimodal data and intelligently restructure it for whatever downstream task a user needs.
