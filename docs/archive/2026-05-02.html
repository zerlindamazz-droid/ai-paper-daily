Co-Evolving Policy Distillation 协同演化策略蒸馏

🧑‍🔬 Naibin Gu, Chenxu Yang, Qingyi Si, Chuanyu Qin et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 协同演化蒸馏，将文本、图像、视频推理融合进单一模型
CoPD co-evolves expert models during training to merge text, image, and video reasoning into one model.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当我们想让一个AI模型同时具备多种推理能力（比如文字、图像、视频理解）时，现有的两种主流方法都有缺陷：混合强化学习训练会导致不同能力之间相互干扰；而先训练专家模型再蒸馏的流水线方式，因为师生之间的行为差距太大，导致学生模型无法充分吸收老师的知识。 When training a single AI model to master multiple reasoning skills (e.g., text, image, and video understanding), two standard approaches both fall short. Mixed RLVR training causes different capabilities to interfere with each other, while training separate expert models first and then distilling them into one student model leaves a large gap between teacher and student behaviors, losing key knowledge in the process.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **统一失效分析**：首次系统性地揭示混合RLVR（能力间散度代价）和流水线OPD（师生行为模式差距）两种范式的失败原因。 • **双向并行蒸馏**：CoPD在RLVR训练过程中同步进行专家间的互相蒸馏，而非训练完成后再蒸馏，始终保持行为模式的一致性。 • **融合模型超越专家**：CoPD训练出的单一模型性能超越各领域的专用专家模型，说明协同演化产生了协同增益，而非简单叠加。 • **Unified failure analysis**: First work to systematically identify why both mixed RLVR (inter-capability divergence) and pipeline OPD (teacher-student behavioral gap) fail at multi-capability consolidation. • **Bidirectional, simultaneous distillation**: CoPD runs expert training and distillation in parallel — experts teach each other *during* RLVR training, not after, keeping behavioral patterns aligned throughout. • **All-in-one outperforms specialists**: The merged CoPD model surpasses individual domain-specific experts, suggesting that co-evolution creates synergistic, not just additive, capability gains.
🧪 实验
内容		 本文在覆盖文本、图像、视频理解的多模态推理基准上评估CoPD。基线包括：混合RLVR（在一个模型中联合训练所有能力）、MOPD（先完整训练专家再进行离线策略蒸馏的标准流水线）以及各领域的单一专家模型。实验验证CoPD能否将三种模态能力集成进单一模型，同时保持或超越各专家模型的独立性能。 The paper evaluates CoPD on multi-modal reasoning benchmarks covering text, image, and video understanding tasks. Baselines include mixed RLVR (training all capabilities jointly in one model), MOPD (the standard pipeline of training experts fully first then performing offline policy distillation), and domain-specific single-expert models. The experiments test whether CoPD can consolidate all three modality capabilities into one model while preserving or improving upon each expert's individual performance.
📊 实验
结果		 CoPD在所有文本、图像、视频推理基准上均显著优于混合RLVR和MOPD两条基线。最关键的是，CoPD训练出的全能模型在各自的任务上甚至超越了对应的领域专用专家模型——这是两条基线都无法实现的结果。论文在多个基准上报告了具体分数，CoPD在多能力模型整合的后训练场景中确立了新的最优水平。 CoPD significantly outperforms both mixed RLVR and MOPD baselines across all evaluated text, image, and video reasoning benchmarks. Crucially, the CoPD all-in-one model even surpasses the performance of individually trained domain-specific expert models on their respective tasks — a result neither baseline achieves. The paper reports concrete benchmark scores demonstrating consistent gains across modalities, with CoPD establishing a new state-of-the-art for multi-capability model consolidation in post-training settings.
🏁 结论 CoPD证明了通过训练中双向蒸馏协同演化专家模型，可以将多模态推理能力整合进单一模型，且性能超越现有基线和领域专家。本文为大语言模型和视觉模型的后训练提供了一种有前景的模型并行训练扩展新范式。 CoPD proves that co-evolving expert models through bidirectional, in-training distillation can consolidate multi-modal reasoning capabilities into a single model that surpasses both competing baselines and domain-specific experts. This work introduces a promising model-parallel training scaling paradigm for post-training large language and vision models.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

CoPD同时并行地用RLVR（可验证奖励强化学习）训练多个领域专家模型。与先训练完专家再蒸馏的流水线不同，CoPD在每个训练步骤中都进行在线策略蒸馏（OPD），让每个专家实时向其他专家学习，互为老师。这种双向蒸馏使所有专家在整个训练过程中保持行为上的相互接近。最终合并的模型从所有专家处继承互补知识，同时避免了事后蒸馏中因行为差距过大导致的知识损失。 CoPD trains multiple domain-specific expert models in parallel using RLVR (Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards). Instead of waiting until experts are fully trained before distilling, CoPD introduces Online Policy Distillation (OPD) at every step during training, so each expert continuously learns from the others as mutual teachers. This bidirectional distillation keeps all experts behaviorally close to each other throughout the entire process. The final merged model inherits complementary knowledge from all experts without suffering the large behavioral gap that normally exists when distillation happens post-training.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{CoPD}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{RLVR}} + \lambda \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{OPD}}\]
💡 CoPD的总训练目标：在每个训练步骤中，将RLVR强化学习损失与在线策略蒸馏（OPD）损失加权求和，λ控制蒸馏的强度。 CoPD's total training objective: at every training step, the RLVR reinforcement learning loss is combined with an Online Policy Distillation loss weighted by λ, balancing task-specific learning and cross-expert knowledge transfer.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{OPD}}(\pi_i, \pi_j) = D_{\text{KL}}(\pi_j \| \pi_i)\]
💡 在线策略蒸馏损失：用KL散度衡量专家i的策略与专家j（老师）的策略之间的差距，双向意味着i和j互相作为老师。 Online Policy Distillation loss: KL divergence measures how far expert i's policy is from expert j acting as teacher; bidirectionality means i and j swap teacher/student roles simultaneously.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

如何在不牺牲专精能力的前提下，构建在文本、视觉、视频等多领域均表现出色的通用AI模型，是LLM后训练的核心难题——CoPD提供了一种原则性且可扩展的解决方案。其提出的模型并行协同演化范式，可能重塑社区对多能力后训练扩展的思考方式，减少对多个独立专家模型的依赖。 Building general-purpose AI models that are strong across multiple domains (text, vision, video) without sacrificing specialization is a core challenge in LLM post-training — CoPD offers a principled, scalable solution. The model-parallel co-evolution paradigm it introduces could reshape how the community thinks about scaling post-training across capabilities, potentially reducing the need for separate specialist models.

Representation Fréchet Loss for Visual Generation 用于视觉生成的表示弗雷歇损失

🧑‍🔬 Jiawei Yang, Zhengyang Geng, Xuan Ju, Yonglong Tian et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 弗雷歇距离首次成为可训练目标，一步生成FID达0.72
FD-loss makes Fréchet Distance a practical training objective, achieving 0.72 FID on ImageNet in one step.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 FID（弗雷歇初始距离）是图像生成质量的标准评估指标，但从未被用作训练损失，因为它需要数万张样本才能稳定估计，远超普通mini-batch大小。此外，单纯依赖Inception特征空间的FID可能对模型排名产生误判，导致评估结论不可靠。 Fréchet Inception Distance (FID) is the standard metric for evaluating image generation quality, but it has never been used as a training loss because computing it requires tens of thousands of samples — far more than a typical mini-batch. As a result, generators are trained with proxy objectives that don't directly optimize what we measure. Additionally, FID computed only in Inception feature space can misrank models, making it an unreliable sole evaluation metric.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **关键解耦技巧**：将FD估计所需的种群规模（5万张）与梯度计算的批量大小（1024）解耦，首次让FD成为可微分、可实用的训练目标。 • **通用训练目标**：FD-loss可在多种表示空间（Inception、DINO、CLIP等）中使用，并能将多步扩散模型转化为一步生成器，无需对抗训练、教师蒸馏或逐样本目标。 • **新评估指标FDrk**：揭示FID存在排名错误的问题，提出多表示空间集成指标FDrk，更准确反映视觉质量。 • **Key Decoupling Trick**: Separates population size for FD estimation (50k samples) from batch size for gradient computation (1024), making FD differentiable and practical as a training loss for the first time. • **Versatile Objective**: The same FD-loss works across representation spaces (Inception, DINO, CLIP, etc.) and repurposes multi-step diffusion models into one-step generators without adversarial training, teacher distillation, or per-sample targets. • **New Evaluation Metric (FDrk)**: Reveals that FID can misrank models and proposes FDrk, a multi-representation ensemble metric that better reflects true visual quality.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在ImageNet 256×256的类别条件图像生成任务上进行。作者对多个最先进的生成器（包括EDM2、SiT、一致性模型等）使用FD-loss进行后训练微调，覆盖多种表示空间（Inception、DINOv2、CLIP等）。同时测试了FD-loss作为一步蒸馏方法的效果，并与无对抗训练/无蒸馏基线比较。评估使用标准FID及提出的FDrk多表示指标，并通过人类偏好实验验证FDrk与人类判断的一致性。 Experiments were conducted on class-conditional ImageNet generation at 256×256 resolution. The authors post-trained several state-of-the-art generators (including EDM2, SiT, and consistency models) using FD-loss across multiple representation spaces (Inception, DINOv2, CLIP, etc.). They also tested FD-loss as a one-step distillation method without adversarial or distillation baselines. Evaluation used standard FID, as well as the proposed multi-representation FDrk metric. Human preference studies were also conducted to validate whether FDrk better aligns with human judgment than standard FID.
📊 实验
结果		 • 使用FD-loss（Inception空间）后训练的一步生成器在ImageNet 256×256上达到 **FID 0.72**，刷新一步生成的最优记录。 • FD-loss在所有测试的基础生成器和表示空间上均稳定提升FID，提升幅度常超数个FID点。 • 基于FD-loss的多步→一步蒸馏效果媲美甚至超越依赖对抗训练或教师蒸馏的先前方法。 • FDrk与人类偏好评分的相关性显著优于单一空间FID，证明FID单独使用时会导致错误的模型排名。 • A one-step generator post-trained with FD-loss (Inception space) achieves **0.72 FID** on ImageNet 256×256, a new state-of-the-art for one-step generation. • FD-loss consistently improves FID across all tested base generators and representation spaces, with improvements often exceeding several FID points. • Multi-step → one-step distillation via FD-loss matches or surpasses prior methods that rely on adversarial training or teacher distillation. • FDrk better correlates with human preference scores than single-space FID, demonstrating that FID alone can produce incorrect model rankings.
🏁 结论 本文证明，通过将种群估计与梯度计算解耦，弗雷歇距离可成为强大且实用的训练目标，在ImageNet上实现0.72 FID的最优一步生成效果。同时揭示单一表示空间的FID评估存在局限，提出多表示指标FDrk以提供更可靠的生成质量评估。 This paper proves that Fréchet Distance can be a powerful and practical training objective by decoupling population estimation from gradient computation, achieving state-of-the-art one-step generation at 0.72 FID on ImageNet. It also establishes that FID in a single representation space is insufficient for reliable evaluation, motivating a richer multi-representation metric FDrk.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

核心思路是将FD计算中样本量的两种角色解耦：用大规模参考集（如5万张真实图像）拟合稳定的高斯统计量（均值与协方差），梯度计算则仅需小批量（如1024张生成图像）。FD-loss即为真实图像与生成图像在某一表示空间中高斯分布之间的弗雷歇距离。该方法作为后训练微调步骤叠加在预训练生成器之上。用于一步蒸馏时，将多步模型的输出分布视为"真实"分布，用FD-loss训练一步模型去匹配，无需逐样本配对。此外，通过在k个不同特征提取器上平均FD，提出多表示指标FDrk。 The core idea is to decouple the two roles of sample size in FD computation: a large reference population (e.g., 50k images) is maintained to compute stable Gaussian statistics (mean and covariance), while gradients are computed on a much smaller batch (e.g., 1024 images). The FD-loss is then the Fréchet Distance between the Gaussian fitted to this large population of real images and the Gaussian fitted to a rolling buffer of generated images in a chosen representation space. This can be applied as a post-training fine-tuning step on top of any pretrained generator. For one-step distillation, the multi-step model's output distribution is treated as the "real" distribution, and the one-step model is trained to match it via FD-loss without requiring paired samples. A multi-representation metric FDrk is also introduced by averaging FD across k different feature extractors.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{FD}(p, q) = \|\mu_p - \mu_q\|^2 + \text{Tr}\left(\Sigma_p + \Sigma_q - 2(\Sigma_p \Sigma_q)^{1/2}\right)\]
💡 弗雷歇距离（FD）衡量两个高斯分布之间的差异：均值差的平方加上协方差矩阵项之和。FD-loss直接优化此距离，其中p为真实数据分布，q为生成数据分布，统计量在表示空间中估计。 Fréchet Distance between two Gaussian distributions p (real) and q (generated): squared mean difference plus a covariance trace term. FD-loss directly optimizes this by estimating statistics in a chosen representation space using a large population for stability but a small batch for gradients.
\[\text{FDr}^k = \frac{1}{k} \sum_{i=1}^{k} \text{FD}_i\]
💡 FDrk是对k个不同表示空间（如Inception、DINO、CLIP）中FD值取平均，得到更全面、更可靠的生成质量评估指标。 FDrk averages Fréchet Distance computed across k different representation spaces (e.g., Inception, DINO, CLIP), providing a more robust and comprehensive evaluation metric than single-space FID.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本研究将图像生成领域最主流的评估指标直接转化为训练信号，从根本上改变了生成模型的训练范式，实现了长期以来的研究目标。提出的FDrk指标也促使学界重新审视生成模型的评估标准，有望推动行业从单一依赖Inception FID转向更全面的多维评估体系。 This work fundamentally changes how we can train generative models by turning the field's most popular evaluation metric directly into a training signal — a long-sought goal. The proposed FDrk metric also challenges the community to rethink how we benchmark generative models, potentially shifting the standard away from sole reliance on Inception FID.

Visual Generation in the New Era: An Evolution from Atomic Mapping to Agentic World Modeling 新时代的视觉生成：从原子映射到智能体世界建模的演进

🧑‍🔬 Keming Wu, Zuhao Yang, Kaichen Zhang, Shizun Wang et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 五级分类框架，重新定义视觉生成的演进路径
A five-level taxonomy reframes visual generation as a journey toward causal, agentic world modeling.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当前视觉生成模型虽能生成逼真的图像和视频，但在空间推理、因果理解、跨帧状态持续和长时序一致性等方面仍存在严重不足。现有评测体系过于偏重感知质量，导致对模型真实能力的高估，掩盖了结构性与因果性失败。该领域缺乏一个统一的框架来衡量模型是否真正超越了"外观合成"的层次。 Modern visual generation models excel at producing photorealistic images and videos, but they fundamentally lack spatial reasoning, causal understanding, persistent state across frames, and long-horizon temporal consistency. The field has no unified framework for measuring how far models have truly progressed beyond surface-level appearance synthesis. Existing benchmarks also tend to overestimate model capability by focusing on perceptual quality alone, masking deep structural and causal failures.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - 提出创新性的五级能力分类体系（原子生成→条件生成→上下文生成→智能体生成→世界建模生成），系统刻画视觉生成从被动渲染到因果世界模拟的完整演化路径。\n- 系统梳理关键技术驱动力，包括流匹配、理解-生成统一模型、奖励建模、合成数据蒸馏和采样加速等，揭示其在分类体系中的作用。\n- 提出以能力为中心的评估范式，融合基准测试、野外压力测试与专家约束案例研究，暴露标准指标所掩盖的深层缺陷。 - Proposes a novel five-level taxonomy (Atomic → Conditional → In-Context → Agentic → World-Modeling Generation) that captures the full capability spectrum from passive renderers to causal world simulators.\n- Identifies and systematically reviews key technical drivers—flow matching, unified understanding-generation models, reward modeling, synthetic data distillation, and sampling acceleration—that enable progression across levels.\n- Introduces a capability-centered evaluation paradigm combining benchmark review, in-the-wild stress tests, and expert-constrained case studies to expose gaps that standard metrics miss.
🧪 实验
内容		 本文为综述与立场性论文，未训练新模型。评估通过三条互补路径展开：（1）系统回顾现有基准测试（如GenAI-Bench、EvalCrafter、VideoPhy、T2V-CompBench），重点关注感知质量与指令跟随；（2）对当前最先进生成模型（包括Sora级视频生成模型和图像扩散模型）进行野外压力测试，测试空间布局、物理合理性和长时序一致性；（3）在医学成像、物理仿真、具身导航等领域开展专家约束案例研究，通过可验证的真实结构评估生成质量。 The paper is a survey and position paper rather than a system paper, so it does not introduce new training datasets or train new models. Evaluation is conducted through three complementary lenses: (1) a structured review of existing benchmarks (e.g., GenAI-Bench, EvalCrafter, VideoPhy, T2V-CompBench) that assess perceptual quality and instruction following; (2) in-the-wild stress tests applied to state-of-the-art generation models (including Sora-class video generators and image diffusion models) targeting spatial layout, physics plausibility, and long-horizon coherence; and (3) expert-constrained case studies in domains such as medical imaging, physical simulation, and embodied navigation, where ground-truth structure can be verified.
📊 实验
结果		 作为综述/路线图论文，定量数据来源于文献回顾而非新实验。论文记录：当前最优模型在感知指标上表现良好，但在因果推理和物理压力测试中失败率超过50%。现有顶级视频生成模型在组合空间推理任务（如T2V-CompBench）上准确率仅约40–60%；在物理合理性基准VideoPhy上，领先模型得分低于70%。分类框架揭示，绝大多数已部署模型集中在第1-2级，极少能达到第3级，目前没有任何系统能可靠运行于第4-5级。 As a survey/roadmap paper, quantitative results are drawn from the literature review rather than new experiments. The paper documents that state-of-the-art models score well on perceptual metrics (e.g., high FID/FVD ratings on standard benchmarks) yet exhibit failure rates exceeding 50% on causal and physics-based stress tests. Reviewed benchmarks show that current top video generation models achieve only ~40–60% accuracy on compositional spatial reasoning tasks (e.g., T2V-CompBench), and physical plausibility scores on VideoPhy remain below 70% for leading models. The taxonomy reveals that the vast majority of deployed models cluster at Levels 1–2, with very few reaching Level 3, and no existing system reliably operating at Levels 4–5.
🏁 结论 本文建立了五级分类框架和以能力为中心的评估范式，将视觉生成重新定位为迈向智能、因果和智能体世界建模的演进过程。研究证明该领域在感知质量之外存在系统性不足，并为弥合这些差距提供了结构化的研究路线图。 This paper establishes a five-level taxonomy and capability-centered evaluation paradigm that reframes visual generation as a progression toward intelligent, causal, and agentic world modeling. It demonstrates that the field is systematically underperforming beyond perceptual quality, and provides a structured roadmap for research priorities to close those gaps.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者构建五级分类框架，将视觉生成模型从简单外观渲染（第1级：原子生成）逐步映射到指令条件生成、上下文生成（第2-3级），再到目标驱动的智能体行为（第4级）和完整的因果世界模拟（第5级）。他们对近期代表性模型进行系统梳理并在框架中定位。针对每一级别的技术驱动力（流匹配、视觉-语言统一架构、奖励驱动后训练、合成数据流程等）进行深度分析。最后提出超越FID/FVD的富评估方法，通过空间、时序和因果推理压力测试，揭示当前最优模型的真实短板。 The authors construct a five-level taxonomy to organize the landscape of visual generation models, ranging from simple appearance rendering (Level 1: Atomic) through instruction-conditioned and in-context generation (Levels 2–3), up to goal-directed agentic behavior (Level 4) and full causal world simulation (Level 5). They survey recent models and map them onto this taxonomy. Technical enablers for each level—such as flow matching for high-fidelity synthesis, unified vision-language architectures, post-training with reward signals, and synthetic data pipelines—are analyzed in depth. Finally, they propose a richer evaluation methodology that goes beyond FID/FVD scores, incorporating stress tests on spatial, temporal, and causal reasoning to surface where today's best models actually fail.

\[\text{Level} \in \{\text{Atomic}, \text{Conditional}, \text{In-Context}, \text{Agentic}, \text{World-Modeling}\}\]

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

RecursiveMAS将整个多智能体系统重构为统一的隐空间递归计算。异构智能体（如生成器、评论者、搜索器）通过RecursiveLink模块连接成一个协作循环，彼此传递压缩的隐状态而非原始文本，保持通信高效且"分布内"。RecursiveLink将一个智能体的输出隐状态映射到下一个智能体的输入嵌入空间。在训练上，采用内外层循环学习算法：内层循环完成所有智能体的前向递归传播，外层循环通过共享信用分配在整个递归链上反向传播梯度，同时优化所有智能体。该设计支持四种典型协作模式：生成-精炼、辩论、工具/搜索使用和角色分工。 RecursiveMAS reframes the entire multi-agent system as a unified latent-space recursive computation. Heterogeneous agents (e.g., generators, critics, searchers) are connected into a collaboration loop where they exchange compressed latent states rather than raw text, mediated by the RecursiveLink module. This module maps output hidden states from one agent into the input embedding space of the next, keeping communication efficient and "in-distribution." For training, an inner-outer loop learning algorithm is used: the inner loop runs the forward recursive pass through all agents, while the outer loop back-propagates gradients across the entire recursion chain via shared credit assignment, co-optimizing all agents simultaneously. This design supports four representative collaboration patterns: generate-then-refine, debate, tool-use/search, and role-based division.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[h_t^{(l+1)} = f_{\theta}\left(h_t^{(l)},\ \text{RecursiveLink}\left(h_{t-1}^{(L)}\right)\right)\]
💡 递归更新规则：第l+1轮的隐状态由当前轮隐状态与前一智能体经RecursiveLink转换的隐状态共同决定，实现跨智能体的隐空间信息传递。 Recursive update rule: the hidden state at round l+1 is computed from the current hidden state and the previous agent's latent state transformed by RecursiveLink, enabling cross-agent latent information transfer.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{outer}} = \sum_{r=1}^{R} \mathcal{L}\left(y^{(r)},\ y^*\right)\]
💡 外层损失函数：对所有R轮递归的输出损失求和，用于通过共享梯度信用分配对整个系统进行联合反向传播优化。 Outer-loop loss: sums the task loss over all R recursion rounds, used to back-propagate gradients through the entire agent chain via shared credit assignment for whole-system co-optimization.

随着基于LLM的智能体成为复杂推理的主流范式，降低通信开销和提升联合可训练性是亟待突破的瓶颈——RecursiveMAS同时为这两个问题提供了原则性解决方案。通过证明隐空间递归可跨多种任务和协作拓扑泛化，本文为可扩展、高效的智能体AI开辟了全新的架构设计空间。 As LLM-based agents become the dominant paradigm for complex reasoning, reducing their communication overhead and improving their joint trainability are critical bottlenecks — RecursiveMAS offers a principled solution to both simultaneously. By showing that latent-space recursion generalizes across diverse tasks and collaboration topologies, this work opens a new architectural design space for scalable, efficient agentic AI.
