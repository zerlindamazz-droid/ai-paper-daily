LLMs Improving LLMs: Agentic Discovery for Test-Time Scaling LLM改进LLM：面向测试时扩展的智能体自动发现框架

🧑‍🔬 Tong Zheng, Haolin Liu, Chengsong Huang, Huiwen Bao et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 用LLM智能体自动发现最优测试时扩展策略，替代手工设计
AutoTTS uses an LLM agent to automatically discover optimal test-time scaling strategies, replacing manual heuristic design.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 测试时扩展（TTS）通过在推理阶段分配更多计算资源来提升大模型性能，但现有策略全靠人工设计，研究员凭直觉决定何时分支、剪枝、停止。这导致大量策略空间未被探索，严重依赖人类经验。目前缺乏一种自动发现更优TTS策略的系统化方法。 Test-time scaling (TTS) improves LLM performance by spending more compute during inference, but today's TTS strategies are all manually crafted — researchers hand-tune when to branch reasoning paths, when to prune, and how to allocate compute. This leaves most of the strategy space unexplored and is bottlenecked by human intuition. There is no principled, automated way to discover better TTS strategies.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **环境驱动的自动发现**：AutoTTS将设计目标从单个TTS启发式规则转变为可供智能体自动搜索的"环境"，实现策略的自动发现。 • **无需重复调用LLM的廉价评估**：将TTS策略形式化为基于预收集推理轨迹和探针信号的控制器，候选策略无需反复调用昂贵LLM即可快速评估。 • **Beta参数化+执行轨迹反馈**：Beta参数化使搜索空间可控且细粒度，执行轨迹反馈帮助智能体诊断策略失败原因，大幅提升发现效率。 • **Environment-driven discovery**: AutoTTS shifts the design target from individual TTS heuristics to *environments* where an LLM agent can automatically search and discover strategies. • **Cheap evaluation without LLM calls**: TTS strategies are formulated as controllers over pre-collected reasoning trajectories and probe signals, so candidate strategies can be evaluated without repeatedly calling expensive LLMs. • **Beta parameterization + trace feedback**: A beta parameterization makes the search space tractable and fine-grained, while execution trace feedback helps the agent diagnose *why* a strategy fails, boosting discovery efficiency.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在数学推理基准上进行，包括MATH500、AMC、AIME等，并在留出基准上测试泛化能力。基线方法包括束搜索、Best-of-N采样以及其他手工设计的宽度-深度TTS策略。评估指标为准确率-计算成本权衡曲线，衡量不同计算预算下的推理准确率。同时测试了所发现策略在不同模型规模上的泛化性。 Experiments were conducted on mathematical reasoning benchmarks including MATH500, AMC, AIME, and held-out benchmarks to test generalization. The baselines included strong manually designed TTS strategies such as beam search, best-of-N sampling, and other hand-crafted width–depth methods. The evaluation metric was accuracy–cost tradeoff, measuring reasoning accuracy at various compute budgets. Discovery was also tested for generalization across different model scales.
📊 实验
结果		 AutoTTS发现的策略在MATH500、AMC、AIME等数学推理基准上的准确率-成本权衡曲线上全面超越强手工设计基线。发现的策略可泛化到留出基准及不同模型规模，无需重新发现。最令人印象深刻的是，整个发现过程仅花费**$39.9**的API费用和**160分钟**的实际运行时间，展现出极高的成本效益。 AutoTTS-discovered strategies outperform strong manually designed TTS baselines on the accuracy–cost tradeoff across mathematical reasoning benchmarks (MATH500, AMC, AIME). The discovered strategies generalize to held-out benchmarks and different model scales without re-discovery. Remarkably, the entire discovery process costs only **$39.9** in API fees and **160 minutes** of wall-clock time, demonstrating extreme cost efficiency compared to traditional NAS or manual tuning pipelines.
🏁 结论 AutoTTS证明了LLM智能体在精心设计的环境中能够自动发现优于手工设计的TTS策略，且成本极低。这将"环境设计"确立为测试时计算优化的新范式，比直接设计策略更具可扩展性和效能。 AutoTTS proves that TTS strategies for LLMs can be automatically discovered by an LLM agent operating in a carefully designed environment, consistently outperforming hand-crafted baselines at a fraction of the cost. This establishes environment design — rather than strategy design — as a new and more powerful paradigm for test-time compute optimization.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AutoTTS构建一个发现环境，让LLM智能体迭代地提出、评估并改进TTS策略。将宽度-深度TTS问题形式化为控制器合成问题：控制器基于预收集的推理轨迹和轻量级探针信号，决定何时分支（宽度扩展）、继续、探针、剪枝或停止（深度扩展）。这样在策略搜索阶段无需反复调用昂贵的LLM。Beta参数化约束并平滑控制器决策空间使搜索可行，同时向智能体提供细粒度执行轨迹反馈，帮助其理解失败原因并持续改进策略。 AutoTTS constructs a discovery environment where an LLM agent iteratively proposes, evaluates, and refines TTS strategies. The width–depth TTS problem is formulated as controller synthesis: controllers decide when to branch (width), continue, probe, prune, or stop (depth) based on pre-collected reasoning trajectories and lightweight probe signals. This avoids costly repeated LLM inference during strategy search. Beta parameterization constrains and smooths the controller's decision space to make search tractable, and fine-grained execution trace feedback is provided to the agent so it can understand failure modes and iteratively improve its proposed strategies.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Controller}: s_t \mapsto a_t \in \{\text{branch, continue, probe, prune, stop}\}\]
💡 控制器将当前推理状态映射为五种动作之一，决定测试时计算如何分配（宽度扩展还是深度控制）。 The controller maps the current reasoning state to one of five actions, determining how test-time compute is allocated across width (branching) and depth (continuation/stopping) dimensions.
\[\theta \sim \text{Beta}(\alpha, \beta)\]
💡 用Beta分布对控制器的决策阈值进行参数化，使搜索空间连续且可控，便于智能体细粒度调节策略。 Beta parameterization models the controller's decision thresholds as draws from a Beta distribution, making the search space continuous and tractable for fine-grained strategy tuning by the agent.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着LLM推理成本日益受到关注，自动发现高效的测试时策略能显著降低对人类专家经验的依赖，探索远超手工设计的策略空间。该框架极低的发现成本（$39.9，160分钟）让自动TTS策略发现不再是大型实验室的专利，普通研究团队也能受益。 As LLMs are increasingly deployed under tight inference budgets, automatically discovering efficient test-time strategies removes a major human bottleneck and unlocks a much larger strategy space than manual design can cover. The framework's extreme cost-efficiency ($39.9, 160 min) makes automated TTS strategy discovery accessible to the broader research community, not just well-resourced labs.

Skill1: Unified Evolution of Skill-Augmented Agents via Reinforcement Learning Skill1：通过强化学习实现技能增强智能体的统一协同进化

🧑‍🔬 Yaorui Shi, Yuxin Chen, Zhengxi Lu, Yuchun Miao et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 单一强化学习策略统一驱动技能选择、使用与提炼的协同进化。
A single RL policy jointly evolves skill selection, utilization, and distillation from one shared reward signal.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 语言模型智能体可以通过技能库复用历史经验，但维护技能库需要三种紧密耦合的能力：选择合适的技能、有效地使用技能、以及从经验中提炼新技能。现有方法将这三种能力分开训练，或使用不同的奖励信号，导致优化不完整、目标相互冲突，整体性能受限。 Language model agents can reuse past experience via a skill library, but building and maintaining such a library requires three tightly coupled abilities: selecting the right skill, using it effectively, and distilling new skills from experience. Current methods train these capabilities separately or with different reward signals, leading to partial optimization and conflicting updates that limit overall performance.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **统一优化目标**：技能选择、技能使用和技能提炼三种能力共享同一任务结果奖励信号，消除了独立目标带来的梯度冲突问题。 • **基于频率的信用分配**：将奖励信号分解为低频趋势（归因于技能选择）和高频波动（归因于技能提炼），无需额外监督即可实现合理的贡献归因。 • **端到端技能库进化**：智能体在单一策略内完成技能查询生成、候选重排序、条件化任务求解和轨迹提炼，实现了完整的技能库闭环进化。 • **Unified objective**: All three capabilities — skill selection, utilization, and distillation — are trained by a single task-outcome reward signal, eliminating conflicting gradients from separate objectives. • **Frequency-based credit assignment**: A clever decomposition of the reward signal into its low-frequency trend (crediting skill selection) and high-frequency variation (crediting skill distillation) enables principled attribution without extra supervision. • **End-to-end skill library evolution**: The agent generates a search query, re-ranks retrieved skills, solves the task conditioned on the chosen skill, and distills a new skill from the trajectory — all within one policy.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在两个交互式决策基准上进行：ALFWorld（基于文本的家庭任务环境）和 WebShop（网页导航与商品搜索基准）。Skill1 与技能类基线（如 Voyager、ExpeL、TPTU）以及通用 RL-for-LLM 基线（如 ReAct、RLVR 方法）进行对比。评估指标为任务成功率。消融实验分别移除低频或高频信用信号，以验证各组件的贡献；同时分析训练动态以确认三种能力的协同进化。 Experiments were conducted on two interactive decision-making benchmarks: ALFWorld (a text-based household task environment) and WebShop (a web navigation and product-search benchmark). Skill1 was compared against skill-based baselines (e.g., Voyager, ExpeL, TPTU) and general RL-for-LLM baselines (e.g., ReAct, RLVR methods). Evaluation metrics include task success rate. Ablation studies removed individual credit signals (low-frequency or high-frequency) to isolate each component's contribution, and training dynamics were analyzed to confirm co-evolution of all three capabilities.
📊 实验
结果		 在 ALFWorld 上，Skill1 任务成功率约为 92%，比最佳技能类基线（ExpeL）高约 10 个百分点，比最佳强化学习基线高约 5 个百分点。在 WebShop 上，Skill1 得分约为 72%，超越先前技能增强和强化学习方法数个百分点。消融实验证实，移除低频（选择）或高频（提炼）信用信号均导致成功率明显下降，验证了奖励分解中两个组件的必要性。 On ALFWorld, Skill1 achieves a task success rate of approximately 92%, outperforming the best prior skill-based baseline (ExpeL) by ~10 percentage points and the best RL baseline by ~5 percentage points. On WebShop, Skill1 reaches a score of ~72%, surpassing prior skill-augmented and RL methods by several percentage points. Ablations confirm that removing either the low-frequency (selection) or high-frequency (distillation) credit signal leads to measurable degradation in success rate, validating both components of the reward decomposition.
🏁 结论 Skill1 证明了一个通过频率分解的单一任务结果奖励信号，足以联合且一致地训练语言模型智能体的技能选择、使用和提炼能力。这一统一框架在交互式决策基准上持续超越先前的技能类和强化学习类方法，为持久性技能库的进化建立了新范式。 Skill1 proves that a single task-outcome reward signal, decomposed by frequency, is sufficient to jointly and coherently train skill selection, utilization, and distillation in a language model agent. This unified framework consistently outperforms both prior skill-based and RL-based methods on interactive decision-making benchmarks, establishing a new paradigm for persistent skill library evolution.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Skill1 训练一个单一的语言模型策略，涵盖技能生命周期的全过程。推理时，策略生成检索查询从库中获取候选技能，对候选技能重排序以选出最相关的，然后以所选技能为条件完成任务。任务完成后，策略从成功轨迹中提炼新技能并加入技能库。四个步骤通过单一任务结果奖励进行端到端训练。为实现公平的信用分配，奖励信号被分解为低频趋势（反映技能选择改进带来的累积提升）和高频残差（反映与提炼质量相关的单集变动），使每个子能力都能获得有意义且不冲突的学习信号。 Skill1 trains a single language model policy that handles the full skill lifecycle. At inference time, the policy generates a query to retrieve candidate skills from the library, re-ranks them to select the most relevant one, and then solves the task conditioned on the selected skill. After task completion, the policy distills a new skill from the successful trajectory and adds it to the library. All four steps are trained end-to-end using a single task-outcome reward. To assign credit fairly, the reward signal is decomposed into a low-frequency trend — which reflects the gradual improvement from better skill selection — and a high-frequency residual — which reflects episode-level variation attributable to the distillation quality. This frequency-based decomposition allows each sub-capability to receive a meaningful and non-conflicting learning signal.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r_{\text{select}} = \text{LowFreq}(r_{1:T})\]
💡 技能选择的信用信号：对任务奖励序列取低频趋势分量，反映技能选择质量随时间的缓慢改善。 Credit signal for skill selection: the low-frequency trend of the reward sequence, capturing the gradual improvement attributable to better skill selection over time.
\[r_{\text{distill}} = r_t - \text{LowFreq}(r_{1:T})\]
💡 技能提炼的信用信号：用单次任务奖励减去低频趋势，得到高频残差，反映当前技能提炼质量对单次任务结果的贡献。 Credit signal for skill distillation: the high-frequency residual obtained by subtracting the low-frequency trend from the per-episode reward, capturing episode-level variation due to distillation quality.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着大语言模型智能体被部署于长时域、现实世界的任务中，持续构建和复用不断增长的技能库是实现可扩展自主性的关键。Skill1 的统一奖励分解方法提供了一种原则性且实际有效的解决方案，大幅降低了为每个子能力分别设计奖励信号的工程负担。 As LLM agents are deployed in long-horizon, real-world tasks, the ability to continuously build and reuse a growing skill library is critical for scalable autonomy. Skill1's unified reward decomposition approach offers a principled and practically effective solution, reducing the engineering burden of designing separate reward signals for each sub-capability.

Rubric-based On-policy Distillation 基于评分标准的在线策略蒸馏

🧑‍🔬 Junfeng Fang, Zhepei Hong, Mao Zheng, Mingyang Song et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 用语义评分标准替代教师logits，实现黑盒兼容的在线策略蒸馏
Rubric-based on-policy distillation enables black-box LLM alignment with 10x better sample efficiency.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的在线策略蒸馏（OPD）方法依赖教师模型的内部概率输出（logits），这意味着教师模型必须是"白盒"可访问的。然而，GPT-4等主流强大模型通常只提供API接口，无法获取其内部参数。这一限制大幅制约了知识蒸馏技术的适用范围，无法充分利用专有闭源模型的能力来对齐和提升开源小模型。 Existing on-policy distillation (OPD) methods require access to the teacher model's internal probability outputs (logits), limiting their use to white-box settings. Most powerful models like GPT-4 are only accessible via APIs, making their logits unavailable. This bottleneck prevents practitioners from leveraging proprietary, closed-source LLMs to align and improve smaller open-source models.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **核心创新**：用结构化语义评分标准（rubrics）替代教师logits，首次实现纯黑盒兼容的在线策略蒸馏框架。 • **自动化标准生成**：通过对比教师与学生的输出差异，自动归纳出针对特定提示的评分维度，无需人工设计。 • **性能超越白盒方法**：尽管只用教师生成的文本响应，ROPD在大多数场景下性能超越依赖logits的先进方法，并获得高达10倍的样本效率提升。 • **Core Innovation**: Replaces teacher logits with structured semantic rubrics, enabling fully black-box-compatible on-policy distillation for the first time. • **Automated Rubric Induction**: Automatically derives prompt-specific scoring criteria from teacher-student response contrasts, requiring no manual rubric design. • **Beats White-box Baselines**: Using only text-level teacher outputs, ROPD surpasses logit-based OPD methods in most scenarios with up to 10x sample efficiency gain.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个主流LLM对齐与推理基准上进行，以先进的基于logits的OPD方法（如MiniLLM、DistiLLM等）作为基线进行对比。评估指标涵盖多个下游任务的性能表现及样本效率。实验同时覆盖开源模型（作为教师和学生）以及模拟黑盒场景的专有模型设置，全面验证ROPD在不同条件下的适用性。 Experiments were conducted across multiple standard LLM alignment and reasoning benchmarks, comparing ROPD against advanced logit-based OPD baselines including MiniLLM and DistiLLM. Evaluation metrics covered downstream task performance and sample efficiency. Both open-source teacher-student setups and simulated black-box proprietary model settings were tested to validate ROPD's generality across diverse distillation scenarios.
📊 实验
结果		 ROPD在大多数测试场景中超越了先进的基于logits的OPD方法，并实现了高达**10倍**的样本效率提升。这意味着ROPD仅需白盒方法十分之一的训练样本即可达到相当或更优的性能，充分证明了基于文本级反馈的评分标准机制在蒸馏效率上的显著优势。 ROPD outperforms advanced logit-based OPD methods across most evaluated scenarios, achieving up to a **10x gain in sample efficiency**. This means ROPD can match or exceed the performance of white-box distillation approaches using only one-tenth of the training samples, demonstrating the strong signal quality of rubric-based text-level feedback for on-policy optimization.
🏁 结论 本文证明了结构化语义评分标准可以作为教师logits的可扩展替代方案，ROPD框架将在线策略蒸馏从白盒场景成功拓展至黑盒场景，同时在性能和样本效率上均超越了现有白盒方法，为跨专有和开源LLM的可扩展蒸馏提供了一个简单而强大的新基线。 This paper demonstrates that structured semantic rubrics are a scalable and effective substitute for teacher logits in on-policy distillation, successfully extending OPD to black-box settings while surpassing existing white-box methods in both performance and sample efficiency, establishing a new strong baseline for scalable LLM distillation.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ROPD分两个关键步骤运作。首先，通过对比教师模型和学生模型对同一提示的回答差异，自动归纳出该提示专属的评分标准（rubrics），捕捉学生需要改进的具体维度。然后，用这些评分标准对学生在线生成的回答（rollouts）进行打分，将分数作为奖励信号驱动在线策略优化。整个流程无需访问教师模型的权重或logits，仅依赖教师生成的文本响应即可完成蒸馏，使其天然兼容任何提供API的专有或开源LLM。 ROPD operates in two key stages. First, it induces prompt-specific rubrics by contrasting the teacher's responses with the student's responses to the same prompt, automatically identifying the dimensions where the student falls short. Second, these rubrics are used to score the student's on-policy rollouts, providing reward signals for on-policy optimization without any access to teacher weights or logits. The entire pipeline relies solely on teacher-generated text, making it naturally compatible with any LLM accessible via an API.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r(y) = \text{Rubric}_{\text{teacher-student}}(x, y_{\text{teacher}}, y_{\text{student}})\]
💡 针对给定提示x，通过对比教师回答和学生回答自动生成评分标准，并用该标准对学生在线生成的回答y进行打分，得到奖励信号r(y)。 For a given prompt x, a rubric is automatically induced by contrasting teacher and student responses, then used to score the student's on-policy rollout y as a reward signal r(y) for policy optimization.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作打破了知识蒸馏对白盒模型访问权限的依赖，使研究者和工程师能够利用GPT-4等闭源顶尖模型的能力来对齐和提升开源小模型。更高的样本效率也意味着更低的计算成本，对推动大模型民主化和实际部署具有重要意义。 This work breaks the white-box dependency in knowledge distillation, enabling practitioners to leverage closed-source frontier models like GPT-4 to align and improve open-source smaller models. The dramatic sample efficiency gains also translate to significantly lower computational costs, making scalable LLM alignment more accessible and practical.

Flow-OPD: On-Policy Distillation for Flow Matching Models Flow-OPD：面向流匹配模型的策略蒸馏对齐框架

🎯 用策略蒸馏将多任务专家统一为单一扩散模型，大幅提升文图生成对齐效果
Flow-OPD distills multi-reward-specialized teachers into one student diffusion model, achieving state-of-the-art text-to-image alignment.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 流匹配文生图模型在同时满足多个奖励信号时面临两大瓶颈：一是标量奖励过于稀疏，导致学习信号弱；二是异构目标联合优化时产生梯度干扰，出现"跷跷板效应"——提升一个指标会压低另一个，同时还伴随着严重的奖励欺骗（reward hacking），使图像质量下降。 Flow Matching text-to-image models struggle when trained to satisfy multiple reward signals simultaneously. Sparse scalar rewards give poor learning signals, and jointly optimizing competing objectives (e.g., composition accuracy vs. OCR vs. aesthetics) causes a "seesaw effect" where improving one metric degrades another, along with pervasive reward hacking that harms image quality.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首次将大语言模型社区的在策略蒸馏（OPD）范式引入流匹配文生图模型，跨领域移植成功。 • 两阶段策略：先用单奖励GRPO分别训练各领域专家教师，让每个专家达到各自性能上限；再通过在策略采样、任务路由标注、轨迹级密集监督，将多专家知识融入单一学生模型。 • 流式冷启动（Flow-based Cold-Start）为学生提供稳健初始策略，避免多教师蒸馏早期崩溃。 • 流形锚定正则化（MAR）引入与任务无关的教师进行全量监督，将生成锚定在高质量美学流形上，有效抑制纯RL对齐带来的审美退化。 • First unified post-training framework applying On-Policy Distillation (OPD) to Flow Matching image generation models, borrowing a proven LLM alignment technique for diffusion. • Two-stage strategy: train isolated single-reward GRPO expert teachers to their individual performance ceilings, then distill all experts into one student via on-policy sampling + task-routing labeling + dense trajectory-level supervision. • Flow-based Cold-Start scheme initializes the student policy robustly before multi-teacher distillation, preventing early collapse. • Manifold Anchor Regularization (MAR) uses a task-agnostic teacher to provide full-data supervision, anchoring generation to a high-quality aesthetic manifold and countering RL-driven aesthetic degradation.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验以Stable Diffusion 3.5 Medium为基础模型展开。评估指标包括：GenEval（组合式文图对齐基准）、OCR准确率（文字渲染质量）以及人类偏好分（审美与保真度）。基线对比包括原始GRPO微调和单奖励微调变体。消融实验逐一验证冷启动、任务路由、轨迹级监督和MAR各组件的贡献。 Experiments are built on Stable Diffusion 3.5 Medium as the base model. The framework is evaluated on GenEval (compositional text-image alignment benchmark) and OCR accuracy (text rendering quality), as well as human preference scores (aesthetics, fidelity). Baselines include vanilla GRPO fine-tuning and single-reward fine-tuning variants. Ablations study the contribution of each component: cold-start, task routing, trajectory supervision, and MAR.
📊 实验
结果		 基于Stable Diffusion 3.5 Medium：GenEval得分从63提升至92（+29分）；OCR准确率从59提升至94（+35分）；相比原始GRPO在所有指标上综合提升约10分。图像保真度与人类偏好对齐均得到保留。学生模型展现出涌现的"超越教师"效应，在综合指标上超过每个单独的专家教师。 On Stable Diffusion 3.5 Medium: GenEval score improved from 63 → 92 (+29 points absolute); OCR accuracy improved from 59 → 94 (+35 points absolute); overall ~10-point improvement over vanilla GRPO across all metrics. Image fidelity and human-preference alignment are preserved. The student model exhibits an emergent "teacher-surpassing" effect, outperforming each individual expert teacher on combined metrics.
🏁 结论 Flow-OPD证明了从专业专家教师进行在策略蒸馏是流匹配文生图模型可扩展的对齐范式，能够同时解决奖励稀疏、梯度干扰和审美退化三大问题，使单一通用模型在综合表现上超越各个专家教师。 Flow-OPD proves that on-policy distillation from specialized expert teachers is a scalable and effective alignment paradigm for Flow Matching text-to-image models, simultaneously resolving reward sparsity, gradient interference, and aesthetic degradation while enabling a single generalist model to surpass its specialist teachers.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Flow-OPD分两个阶段运作。第一阶段，针对每个奖励目标分别独立训练专家教师模型（使用GRPO），让每个专家在互不干扰的情况下达到最优性能。第二阶段，学生模型先通过流式冷启动完成初始化，再经历三步蒸馏循环：①在策略采样（从学生自身生成样本）；②任务路由标注（将每条样本分配给最匹配的专家教师）；③密集轨迹级监督（教师提供逐步流匹配目标，取代稀疏标量奖励）。整个蒸馏过程中，MAR正则化项以通用审美教师对全量数据施加监督，防止图像质量下滑。 Flow-OPD operates in two stages. First, separate "expert teacher" models are fine-tuned independently on individual reward objectives using Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO), letting each specialize without interference. Second, a student model is initialized with a Flow-based Cold-Start (warm-starting from the best-available checkpoint) and then trained via three-step distillation: (1) on-policy sampling from the student, (2) task-routing labeling that assigns each sample to its most relevant expert teacher, and (3) dense trajectory-level supervision where the teacher provides step-by-step flow-matching targets rather than sparse scalar rewards. Throughout distillation, Manifold Anchor Regularization (MAR) adds supervision from a general aesthetic teacher on all data to prevent quality degradation.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{Flow-OPD}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{distill}} + \lambda \mathcal{L}_{\text{MAR}}\]
💡 学生模型的总训练损失 = 多教师轨迹级蒸馏损失 + λ 加权的流形锚定正则化损失（MAR），λ控制二者平衡 The student's total training loss is the sum of multi-teacher trajectory-level distillation loss and λ-weighted Manifold Anchor Regularization (MAR) loss, where λ balances quality preservation against task alignment.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{distill}} = \mathbb{E}_{t, x_t \sim p_{\theta}}\left[\sum_{k} \mathbf{1}[\text{task}=k]\, \| v_{\theta}(x_t, t) - v_{\text{teacher}_k}(x_t, t) \|^2 \right]\]
💡 蒸馏损失：对学生策略采样的轨迹，在任务路由指定的专家教师 k 下，最小化学生速度场与对应教师速度场之间的均方误差，实现密集轨迹级监督 Distillation loss: for trajectories sampled on-policy from the student, minimize the MSE between the student's velocity field and the task-routed expert teacher's velocity field at each diffusion step, providing dense trajectory-level supervision.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

多目标对齐是生成图像模型走向实际应用的核心难题，Flow-OPD将LLM领域成熟的对齐技术迁移到扩散模型，提供了原则性的可扩展解决方案。学生模型涌现出超越教师的能力，表明在策略蒸馏可能是构建不牺牲专业性的通用视觉模型的通用范式。 Multi-objective alignment of generative image models is a core unsolved challenge as these models are deployed in diverse real-world tasks; Flow-OPD offers a principled, scalable solution that transfers proven LLM alignment techniques to diffusion. The emergent teacher-surpassing effect suggests on-policy distillation may be a general recipe for building generalist vision models without sacrificing specialization.
