Stochastic Thermodynamics for Autoregressive Generative Models: A Non-Markovian Perspective 自回归生成模型的随机热力学：非马尔可夫视角

🧑‍🔬 Takahiro Sagawa

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 用随机热力学框架量化自回归生成模型的不可逆性
A stochastic thermodynamics framework quantifies irreversibility in autoregressive generative models like GPT-2.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 自回归模型（如Transformer、RNN、状态空间模型）在生成每个token时依赖过去所有信息的压缩摘要，产生的序列在数学上是"非马尔可夫"的。这使得传统热力学和信息论工具无法直接使用，也没有方法能在不面临指数级计算代价的情况下，度量这类模型生成过程的"不可逆性"或熵产生。 Autoregressive models (Transformers, RNNs, SSMs, Mamba) generate sequences by conditioning each new token on a compressed summary of the past, making the observed output process genuinely non-Markovian — meaning standard thermodynamic and information-theoretic tools cannot be directly applied. There was no principled way to measure "irreversibility" or entropy production in such models without facing exponential computational cost.
💡 解决
的亮点		 - 首次将随机热力学应用于自回归AI模型，统一了Transformer、RNN、卡尔曼滤波、状态空间模型等多种架构。 - 提出可从采样轨迹高效估计的熵产生量，避免了非马尔可夫过程通常面临的指数级计算代价。 - 严格证明熵产生可分解为每步非负贡献，每项进一步拆分为"压缩损失"和"模型不匹配"两个具有信息论意义的量。 - Novel application of stochastic thermodynamics to autoregressive AI models, unifying Transformers, RNNs, Kalman filters, SSMs, and Mamba under one framework. - Introduces an entropy production estimator that is efficiently computable from sampled trajectories, bypassing the exponential cost normally associated with non-Markovian processes. - Proves an exact decomposition of entropy production into non-negative per-step terms, each splitting into a "compression loss" and a "model mismatch" — two information-theoretically interpretable quantities.
🧪 实验
内容		 框架在两个设置下进行了验证：（1）以GPT-2（预训练Transformer大语言模型）为概念验证，测量了自然语言序列在token级和句子级的熵产生；（2）在线性高斯情形下进行案例研究，此时自回归模型退化为卡尔曼创新表示，可解析推导熵产生的闭合表达式，用于与理论公式交叉验证。 The framework was validated in two settings: (1) a proof-of-concept experiment on GPT-2 (a pre-trained Transformer-based LLM), where token-level and sentence-level entropy production were measured on natural language sequences; (2) a linear Gaussian case study where the autoregressive model reduces to the Kalman innovation representation, allowing closed-form analytical derivation of entropy production and cross-validation of the theoretical formulas.
📊 实验
结果		 对GPT-2，逐token计算了熵产生值并汇总至句子级，发现"令人惊讶"或上下文不一致的token具有更高的熵产生。在线性高斯（卡尔曼）情形下，解析推导出熵产生的闭合表达式，验证了理论预测的精确分解。本文未报告大规模基准对比，属于以概念验证数值实验为主的理论工作，无具体性能提升百分比。 For GPT-2, token-level entropy production values were computed for individual tokens and aggregated to sentence-level, revealing that more "surprising" or contextually inconsistent tokens exhibit higher entropy production. In the linear Gaussian (Kalman) case, the analytical entropy production expression was derived in closed form and shown to decompose exactly as predicted by the theory. No large-scale benchmark comparisons were reported; the work is primarily theoretical with proof-of-concept numerical illustrations rather than state-of-the-art performance claims.
🏁 结论 本文在随机热力学与现代自回归生成模型之间建立了严格的理论桥梁，首次提出可高效计算的框架以量化LLM等高度非马尔可夫序列模型的不可逆性（熵产生）。将熵产生分解为压缩损失和模型不匹配，为模型可解释性提供了兼具物理学和信息论意义的新工具。 This paper establishes a rigorous bridge between stochastic thermodynamics and modern autoregressive generative models, providing the first tractable framework for quantifying irreversibility (entropy production) in highly non-Markovian sequence models such as LLMs. The decomposition into compression loss and model mismatch offers new interpretability tools grounded in both physics and information theory.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

论文将自回归生成过程重新表述为隐状态（模型内部摘要）与观测序列的联合马尔可夫过程，从而将经典随机热力学（仅适用于马尔可夫过程）推广到非马尔可夫的观测动力学。熵产生被定义为正向与时间反转路径概率之比的对数，并证明可通过正向运行自回归模型高效估计。随后通过代数推导得出逐步分解，产生"压缩损失"（过去上下文的编码质量）和"模型不匹配"（模型预测分布与真实数据分布的差距）两个分量。 The paper reformulates autoregressive generation as a joint Markov process over both the hidden state (the model's internal summary) and the observed token sequence. This allows classical stochastic thermodynamics — normally applicable only to Markov processes — to be extended to the non-Markovian observed dynamics. Entropy production is then defined via the log-ratio of forward and time-reversed path probabilities, and it is shown to be tractably estimable by running the autoregressive model forward. A per-step decomposition is derived algebraically, yielding a compression loss (how well past context is encoded) and a model mismatch (how well the model's predictive distribution matches the true data distribution).

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着大语言模型日趋复杂和不透明，量化其生成行为的理论工具愈发重要；本框架提供了一种物理上有据可查的度量，反映模型输出的"不可逆性"和"意外程度"。此外，熵产生的分解形式有望启发新的训练目标或诊断指标，同时捕捉压缩效率与分布匹配质量两个维度。 As LLMs grow in complexity and opacity, principled theoretical tools for understanding their generative behavior are increasingly valuable; this framework offers a physically grounded measure of how "irreversible" or "surprising" a model's outputs are. Beyond interpretability, the entropy production decomposition could inspire new training objectives or diagnostic metrics that simultaneously capture compression efficiency and distribution matching quality.

ClawBench: Can AI Agents Complete Everyday Online Tasks? ClawBench：AI智能体能完成日常在线任务吗？

🧑‍🔬 Yuxuan Zhang, Yubo Wang, Yipeng Zhu, Penghui Du et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 真实网站上的153项日常任务评测揭示当前AI智能体距实用仍有巨大差距
A live-website benchmark of 153 real-world tasks reveals frontier AI agents still fall far short of reliable automation.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的AI智能体评测基准大多在静态离线沙盒环境中运行，无法真实反映实际网站的复杂性和动态性。因此我们并不清楚当前最前沿的AI模型究竟能否处理人们日常生活中的真实在线任务，如预约、购物、求职申请等。 Current AI agent benchmarks test performance on static, sandboxed web pages that don't reflect the true complexity of real websites. As a result, it's unclear whether today's frontier AI models can actually handle the dynamic, multi-step online tasks people do every day — like booking appointments, filling out job applications, or making purchases.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首个在真实生产环境网站上运行AI智能体评测的基准，而非离线静态沙盒，完整保留了真实网页交互的复杂性与动态性。 • 设计了轻量级"拦截层"，仅拦截最终提交请求，实现安全评测的同时不产生任何真实副作用（如真实下单或投递简历）。 • 覆盖144个真实平台的153项任务，横跨15个类别，包含大量表单填写、多步骤工作流和用户文档信息提取等高难度操作。 • First benchmark to run AI agent evaluations on live, production websites rather than offline sandboxes, preserving full real-world complexity. • A lightweight interception layer safely blocks only the final form submission request, enabling realistic testing without any real-world side effects. • Covers 153 tasks across 144 live platforms in 15 categories, including demanding write-heavy operations and multi-document information retrieval rarely tested elsewhere.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究团队在ClawBench的153项真实网站任务上评测了7个前沿AI模型，包括闭源模型（如Claude Sonnet 4.6、GPT-4o）和开源模型。任务类别涵盖电商购物、求职申请、预约挂号、政务服务等15个类别。评测指标为任务完成率（即智能体能否正确填写并提交所有所需信息），全程在真实生产网站上运行并启用拦截层。 Seven frontier AI models — both proprietary (e.g., Claude Sonnet 4.6, GPT-4o) and open-source — were evaluated as web agents on ClawBench's 153 live-website tasks. Tasks span 15 categories including e-commerce, job applications, appointment booking, and government services. Evaluation measures task completion rate (whether the agent correctly fills and submits all required information), tested on real production websites with the interception layer active.
📊 实验
结果		 表现最佳的Claude Sonnet 4.6在153项ClawBench任务中的完成率仅为33.3%。其他所有被测前沿模型（无论是闭源还是开源）的得分均低于此数值，表明当前AI智能体在真实日常在线任务上的完成能力仍然十分有限。没有任何模型接近在真实网站上实现可靠的通用自动化助手水平。 The best-performing model, Claude Sonnet 4.6, achieved only 33.3% task completion rate across the 153 ClawBench tasks. All other tested frontier models — both proprietary and open-source — performed even lower, indicating that the majority of real-world everyday online tasks remain unsolved for current AI agents. No model came close to reliable general-purpose automation on live websites.
🏁 结论 ClawBench证明了即便是最强大的前沿AI模型，在真实网站上也只能完成不到三分之一的日常在线任务，揭示了当前智能体能力与实际应用需求之间的巨大鸿沟。该基准为推动真正通用的AI网页智能体研究提供了严格、安全且贴近真实的评测平台。 ClawBench demonstrates that even the most capable frontier AI models can complete fewer than one-third of routine everyday online tasks on real websites, exposing a critical gap between current agent capabilities and practical real-world utility. The benchmark provides a rigorous, safe, and realistic testbed to drive progress toward truly general-purpose AI web agents.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ClawBench从15个生活与工作类别中精选了153项日常在线任务，涵盖144个真实运营网站。智能体直接与真实生产网站交互，而非与复制品互动。框架通过轻量级网络拦截代理监控浏览器流量，仅拦截最终提交的HTTP请求（如确认购买或提交申请），避免真实副作用，同时要求智能体完整执行所有前置步骤。评测标准为最终提交数据的正确性，涵盖表单字段准确性、正确的选项选择以及基于文档的信息提取准确率。 ClawBench constructs a curated set of 153 everyday online tasks drawn from 144 live websites spanning 15 life-and-work categories. Agents interact directly with real production websites rather than replicas. A lightweight network interception proxy monitors browser traffic and intercepts only the final submission HTTP request (e.g., confirming a purchase or submitting a job application), preventing real-world consequences while still requiring the agent to navigate the full task. Tasks are evaluated on correctness of the final submitted data, covering fields like form accuracy, correct item selection, and proper document-based information retrieval.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着AI智能体被越来越多地用于真实世界的自动化任务，在真实网站（而非净化的沙盒）上进行严格评测对于准确衡量进展和发现失败模式至关重要。ClawBench同时兼顾真实性与安全性，为在实际部署场景中评测智能体能力树立了新标准。 As AI agents are increasingly deployed for real-world automation, rigorous evaluation on live websites — not sanitized sandboxes — is essential to measure true progress and identify failure modes. ClawBench shifts the evaluation paradigm toward authenticity and safety simultaneously, setting a new standard for benchmarking agent capabilities in practical deployment scenarios.

Learning is Forgetting: LLM Training As Lossy Compression 学习即遗忘：大语言模型训练作为有损压缩

🧑‍🔬 Henry C. Conklin, Tom Hosking, Tan Yi-Chern, Julian Gold et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 LLM训练本质是有损压缩，压缩质量可预测下游性能
LLM training is lossy compression, and compression optimality predicts downstream benchmark performance.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 我们对大语言模型在训练过程中究竟学到了什么、内部表示是如何组织的，缺乏系统性理论理解。这使得解释模型行为、比较不同模型家族、或预判哪个模型在下游任务中表现更好都十分困难。现有的可解释性方法往往无法跨模型家族推广，也难以将表示结构与实际性能直接挂钩。 We lack a principled theoretical framework to understand what large language models actually learn during training and how their internal representations are structured. Without this, it is difficult to interpret model behavior, compare different model families, or predict which models will perform well on downstream tasks. Existing approaches to model interpretability do not generalize across model families or connect representational structure to measurable performance.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 全新视角：将LLM预训练重新解读为信息瓶颈（IB）框架下的有损压缩过程——模型只保留与目标任务相关的信息，丢弃其余内容。 • 实证IB界：预训练LLM被证明接近理论上的信息瓶颈最优界，为衡量模型压缩效率提供了严格的度量标准。 • 统一预测指标：模型压缩最优性的程度以及表示中保留的信息，可以直接预测大量下游基准上的性能，在规模化场景下实现了理论与实践的桥接。 • Novel framing: LLM pre-training is recast as lossy compression under the Information Bottleneck (IB) principle — models retain only task-relevant information and discard the rest. • Empirical IB bound: Pre-trained LLMs are shown to approach the theoretical Information Bottleneck optimality bound, providing a rigorous measure of how efficiently a model compresses its training data. • Unified predictive metric: The degree of compression optimality and the specific information retained in a model's representations can directly predict performance across a wide range of benchmarks, bridging theory and practice at scale.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者对来自多个模型家族（如LLaMA、Mistral等）的大量开源权重LLM进行评估，通过提取内部表示计算其信息瓶颈压缩指标。这些压缩分数随后与广泛的标准LLM基准（如MMLU、HellaSwag、ARC等）上的性能进行相关分析。研究仅分析预训练检查点，不涉及微调，并比较不同模型家族在相同训练范式下呈现出的不同压缩特性。 The authors evaluate a wide array of open-weights LLMs from multiple model families (e.g., LLaMA, Mistral, and others) by computing their Information Bottleneck compression metrics from internal representations. These compression scores are then correlated against performance on a broad suite of standard LLM benchmarks (such as MMLU, HellaSwag, ARC, and similar evaluations). The study does not fine-tune models but analyzes pre-trained checkpoints, comparing how different model families compress information differently despite sharing the same overall training paradigm.
📊 实验
结果		 多个模型家族的预训练LLM均接近信息瓶颈最优界，证实了对下一词元预测任务的近似最优压缩。压缩最优性分数与包括知识、推理和语言理解在内的大量下游基准性能呈现强预测相关性，无需运行完整基准套件即可对模型进行排序。不同模型家族表现出可量化的差异压缩特征，归因于训练数据和训练配方的不同。基于IB的指标可跨模型家族泛化，提供单一统一分数，可靠地追踪基准性能趋势。 Pre-trained LLMs across multiple families approach the Information Bottleneck optimality bound, confirming near-optimal compression for next-token prediction. Compression optimality scores show strong predictive correlation with downstream benchmark performance across a wide array of evaluations (including knowledge, reasoning, and language understanding benchmarks), allowing model ranking without running full benchmark suites. Different model families exhibit measurably distinct compression profiles, attributable to differences in training data and recipes. The IB-based metric generalizes across model families, providing a single unified score that tracks benchmark performance trends reliably.
🏁 结论 本文证明LLM预训练本质上是一种向信息瓶颈最优界收敛的有损压缩过程，且压缩质量能够直接、可靠地预测多样化基准上的下游性能。这项工作提供了首个统一、可扩展的信息论框架，用于理解LLM学到了什么，以及如何在无需穷举评估的情况下评估其表示质量。 This paper proves that LLM pre-training is fundamentally an instance of lossy compression converging toward the Information Bottleneck optimum, and that the quality of this compression directly and reliably predicts downstream performance across diverse benchmarks. The work provides the first unified, scalable information-theoretic framework for understanding what LLMs learn and how to assess their representational quality without exhaustive evaluation.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者将信息瓶颈（IB）框架应用于分析LLM的表示空间。在这一框架下，模型的内部表示充当输入的压缩编码，只保留对预测下一个词元有用的信息。他们度量每个模型的表示空间与IB理论最优界的接近程度——即在保留任务相关信息的前提下，压缩可以有多紧致。这个由表示几何结构推导出的压缩质量分数，随后与多个基准上的下游任务性能进行相关分析。该方法可在多个开源权重模型家族上大规模部署，无需访问原始训练数据或标签。 The authors apply the Information Bottleneck (IB) framework to analyze LLM representations. In this view, the model's internal representations act as a compressed encoding of the input that retains only the information predictive of the next token (the training objective). They measure how closely each model's representation space approaches the IB theoretical optimum — the tightest possible compression that still preserves task-relevant information. This compression quality score, derived from the model's representational geometry, is then correlated with downstream task performance across many benchmarks. The approach is applied at scale across multiple open-weights model families without requiring access to training data or labels.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\min_{p(\tilde{X}|X)} I(X; \tilde{X}) - \beta \cdot I(\tilde{X}; Y)\]
💡 信息瓶颈目标函数：在最小化表示 $\tilde{X}$ 对输入 $X$ 的压缩量（$I(X;\tilde{X})$）的同时，最大化表示对目标 $Y$（下一词元）的预测信息量（$I(\tilde{X};Y)$），$\beta$ 控制二者的权衡。 The Information Bottleneck objective: minimize how much the representation $\tilde{X}$ retains about the input $X$ (compression), while maximizing how much it retains about the target $Y$ (next token prediction); $\beta$ balances the two terms.
\[I(X; \tilde{X}) \geq I(\tilde{X}; Y)\]
💡 IB压缩界：表示中保留的输入信息量必须至少等于其对目标任务有用的信息量，最优压缩时二者尽量接近。 The IB compression bound: the information the representation retains about the input must be at least as much as what it retains about the target; optimal compression means these quantities are as close as possible.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

该框架为理解和比较LLM提供了理论扎实、模型无关的视角，有望以单一压缩指标替代昂贵的基准全量评估。它同时在机器学习与人类认知学习记忆理论之间建立了有意义的联系，为可解释性研究和模型设计开辟了新方向。 This framework offers a theoretically grounded, model-agnostic lens for understanding and comparing LLMs, potentially replacing expensive benchmark sweeps with a single compression-based metric. It also draws a meaningful connection between machine learning and human cognitive theories of learning and memory, opening new avenues for interpretability and model design.

🎯 用约束优化让多模态模型的推理链既逻辑自洽又忠实于图像

维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 用强化学习训练的多模态推理模型，虽然在测试集上准确率提高了，但它们的"思维链"（CoT）推理过程经常出现两类问题：一是推理步骤与最终答案前后矛盾（逻辑不一致），二是推理内容与图像实际内容对不上（视觉幻觉）。这意味着模型可能"歪打正着"，推理过程不可信，泛化能力也存疑。 Multimodal reasoning models trained with reinforcement learning (RLVR) often improve benchmark accuracy, but their Chain-of-Thought (CoT) reasoning traces are frequently inconsistent with the final answer or poorly grounded in the actual visual content of the image. This means the model may give a correct answer for the wrong reasons — or describe objects and spatial relationships that simply aren't there. ⚙️ Method · 方法详解

FGRPO在标准GRPO的基础上，向训练目标中引入两个显式约束。第一个约束保证"逻辑一致性"：模型生成的推理链必须在逻辑上支持最终答案（通过NLI风格的验证器检验）。第二个约束保证"视觉落地性"：每个推理步骤必须准确描述图像中实际存在的物体、属性和空间关系（通过落地评分器检验）。这两个约束通过拉格朗日对偶上升法嵌入组内优势计算中，将硬约束转化为带自适应乘子的惩罚项。拉格朗日乘子在每个批次后自动更新，模型逐步学会同时满足两个约束，无需手动调整惩罚权重。 FGRPO extends standard Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) by adding two explicit constraints into the training objective. The first constraint enforces "logical consistency": the CoT reasoning trace must logically entail the final answer (checked via an NLI-style verifier). The second enforces "visual grounding": each reasoning step must accurately describe objects, attributes, and spatial relationships visible in the image (checked via a grounding scorer). These constraints are incorporated into the advantage computation within a training group using Lagrangian dual ascent — a classic technique that converts hard constraints into penalty terms with adaptive multipliers. The Lagrange multipliers are updated automatically each batch, so the model learns to satisfy both constraints progressively without manual tuning of penalty weights.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\max_{\theta} \mathbb{E}[r(y)] \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \mathbb{E}[c_{\text{cons}}(y)] \leq \epsilon_1, \quad \mathbb{E}[c_{\text{grnd}}(y)] \leq \epsilon_2\]
💡 FGRPO的核心优化目标：在最大化奖励（答题准确率）的同时，要求推理链的不一致率不超过阈值ε₁，视觉落地违约率不超过阈值ε₂。 The core FGRPO objective: maximize answer reward while keeping the CoT inconsistency rate below ε₁ and the visual grounding violation rate below ε₂ as hard constraints.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{FGRPO}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{GRPO}} + \lambda_1 \cdot C_{\text{cons}} + \lambda_2 \cdot C_{\text{grnd}}\]
💡 拉格朗日松弛后的损失函数：标准GRPO损失加上由λ₁、λ₂自适应加权的一致性约束项和视觉落地约束项，乘子在每批次后自动更新。 Lagrangian relaxation of the constrained objective: standard GRPO loss augmented with adaptively weighted consistency and grounding penalty terms, where multipliers λ₁ and λ₂ are updated each batch via dual ascent.

随着RLVR训练的多模态模型越来越多地被部署在机器人、医学影像、自动驾驶等高风险场景中，推理过程真实反映视觉世界对安全性和可解释性至关重要，而不仅仅是刷榜。本文提供了一种原则性、实用性兼备的方法，弥合了"答对了"与"推对了"之间的鸿沟，对可信AI的发展具有重要意义。 As RLVR-trained multimodal models are increasingly deployed in high-stakes applications (robotics, medical imaging, autonomous driving), having reasoning traces that actually reflect the visual world is critical for safety and interpretability — not just benchmark scores.
