AnyFlow: Any-Step Video Diffusion Model with On-Policy Flow Map Distillation AnyFlow：基于在策略流图蒸馏的任意步数视频扩散模型

🧑‍🔬 Yuchao Gu, Guian Fang, Yuxin Jiang, Weijia Mao et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 首个支持任意步数推理的视频扩散蒸馏框架，兼顾少步快速生成与多步质量提升
AnyFlow is the first any-step video diffusion distillation framework that scales quality with sampling steps via flow-map learning.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的一致性蒸馏方法（Consistency Distillation）可以加速视频生成，但存在一个关键缺陷：随着推理步数增加，生成质量反而下降或不再提升，无法像原始ODE采样那样"步数越多越好"。这限制了模型在需要高质量输出时的灵活性。根本原因在于一致性蒸馏用"一致性采样轨迹"替代了原始的概率流ODE轨迹，破坏了ODE采样天然具备的测试时扩展能力。 Existing consistency distillation methods speed up video generation but suffer a critical flaw: adding more sampling steps at inference time does not improve — and can even hurt — output quality. This breaks the desirable "more steps = better results" scaling behavior of the original ODE sampler. The root cause is that consistency distillation replaces the true probability-flow ODE trajectory with a surrogate consistency-sampling path, discarding the ODE's natural test-time scaling property.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **流图转换学习**：将蒸馏目标从端点一致性映射（z_t → z_0）改为任意时间区间上的流图转换（z_t → z_r），使模型能精确学习ODE轨迹的局部段而非直接跳到终点。 • **流图反向模拟（Flow Map Backward Simulation）**：将完整的Euler展开分解为多个短程流图转换，实现高效的在策略蒸馏，同时消减少步采样的离散化误差和因果生成的曝光偏差两大问题。 • **架构通用性**：同时支持双向（Bidirectional）和因果（Causal）两种主流视频扩散架构，参数规模从1.3B到14B均验证有效，适用范围广。 • **Flow-Map Transition Learning**: Shifts the distillation target from endpoint consistency mapping (z_t → z_0) to flow-map transitions (z_t → z_r) over arbitrary time intervals, teaching the model to accurately track ODE segments rather than leap to the endpoint. • **Flow Map Backward Simulation**: Decomposes a full Euler rollout into shortcut flow-map transitions for efficient on-policy distillation, simultaneously reducing discretization error in few-step sampling and exposure bias in causal generation. • **Architecture Universality**: Validated on both bidirectional and causal video diffusion architectures at 1.3B–14B scales, making it broadly applicable to state-of-the-art video generation pipelines.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在两类主流视频扩散架构上展开：双向架构（以CogVideoX为基础）和因果架构（以Wan为基础），参数规模覆盖1.3B、5B和14B。评估基准包括VBench（视频质量综合评估）和EvalCrafter，与一致性蒸馏基线（如AnimateLCM、CogVideoX-Flash等）以及教师模型在不同推理步数（1步、2步、4步、8步等）下进行对比，重点考察在不同步数预算下的性能变化趋势。 Experiments were conducted on two mainstream video diffusion architectures: bidirectional (CogVideoX-based) and causal (Wan-based), across model scales of 1.3B, 5B, and 14B parameters. Benchmarks used include VBench (comprehensive video quality) and EvalCrafter. AnyFlow is compared against consistency distillation baselines (e.g., AnimateLCM, CogVideoX-Flash) and the teacher models across varying inference step counts (1, 2, 4, 8+ steps), with a focus on how performance scales with step budget.
📊 实验
结果		 在VBench基准上，AnyFlow在少步（4步）区间与一致性蒸馏对标模型持平或超越，同时随步数增加性能持续提升（一致性蒸馏方法在4步以上即趋于饱和甚至下降）。以Wan-1.3B因果架构为例，AnyFlow在4步时VBench总分达到83.2%，超过AnimateLCM等基线；在8步时进一步提升至84.1%，而基线方法几乎无提升。在14B规模的Wan模型上，AnyFlow同样实现了与教师模型相当的质量同时推理速度显著加快（约4–8倍加速）。EvalCrafter评分亦呈现类似趋势，步数扩展优势在所有架构和规模上一致成立。 On VBench, AnyFlow matches or surpasses consistency distillation baselines in the few-step regime (4 steps) while continuing to improve with more steps — a property consistency-distilled models lack. For the Wan-1.3B causal model, AnyFlow achieves a VBench total score of 83.2% at 4 steps (outperforming AnimateLCM and similar baselines) and further improves to 84.1% at 8 steps, whereas baselines plateau or degrade. At the 14B scale, AnyFlow delivers teacher-comparable quality with approximately 4–8× inference speedup. EvalCrafter scores show consistent trends across all architectures and model sizes, confirming the generality of test-time step scaling.
🏁 结论 AnyFlow证明了通过流图转换学习和在策略反向模拟，可以在保留ODE采样"步数越多越好"特性的同时实现高效蒸馏，首次将少步生成效率与任意步数质量扩展统一到同一框架中。这为视频扩散模型的实际部署提供了兼顾速度与质量的新范式。 AnyFlow proves that flow-map transition learning combined with on-policy backward simulation can preserve the ODE's test-time scaling property while achieving the speed benefits of distillation, unifying few-step efficiency and any-step quality scaling in a single framework for the first time. This establishes a new deployment paradigm for video diffusion models that does not sacrifice quality for speed.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AnyFlow的核心思路是重新定义蒸馏目标：不再要求模型从任意噪声水平直接预测最终干净视频（z_t → z_0），而是学习在任意时间区间内沿ODE轨迹走一段（z_t → z_r），称为"流图"（flow map）。训练时，利用"流图反向模拟"将一次完整的多步Euler积分分解为若干短程流图转换的串联，并以在策略（on-policy）方式采样训练数据，使训练分布与推理分布保持一致，避免曝光偏差。在推理时，用户可以自由选择任意步数：少步时享受高效生成，多步时获得更高质量，性能随步数单调提升。 AnyFlow redefines the distillation target: instead of mapping any noisy latent directly to the clean endpoint (z_t → z_0), the model learns to advance along the ODE trajectory over an arbitrary time interval (z_t → z_r), called a "flow map." During training, Flow Map Backward Simulation decomposes a full multi-step Euler integration into a chain of short flow-map transitions, and samples training data on-policy so the training distribution matches inference, eliminating exposure bias. At inference, users freely choose any number of steps — few steps for speed, more steps for quality — with performance monotonically improving as the step budget grows.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[z_r = z_t + \int_t^r v_\theta(z_s, s)\, ds\]
💡 流图定义：从时刻t的噪声潜变量z_t出发，沿ODE速度场v_θ积分到时刻r，得到中间状态z_r。AnyFlow的蒸馏目标正是学习这一任意区间的转换，而非直接跳到z_0。 Flow map definition: starting from noisy latent z_t at time t, integrate along the ODE velocity field v_θ to reach z_r at time r. AnyFlow's distillation target is to learn this arbitrary-interval transition instead of jumping directly to the clean endpoint z_0.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{AnyFlow}} = \mathbb{E}_{t,r}\left[\| \hat{z}_r^{\text{model}} - \hat{z}_r^{\text{teacher}} \|^2\right]\]
💡 AnyFlow的蒸馏损失：最小化学生模型预测的流图转换终点与教师模型（通过Euler模拟）得到的目标终点之间的均方误差，训练数据通过在策略采样生成以匹配推理分布。 AnyFlow distillation loss: minimize the MSE between the student model's predicted flow-map endpoint and the teacher's target endpoint (computed via Euler simulation), with training data sampled on-policy to match the inference distribution.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

视频生成模型的实用化部署面临"快速推理 vs. 高质量输出"的两难困境，现有蒸馏方法无法同时满足两者。AnyFlow打破了这一瓶颈，让同一个模型能够根据算力预算灵活调整步数，对工业界落地和研究社区均具有重要意义。 Practical deployment of video generation models has long faced a hard trade-off between fast inference and high output quality that existing distillation methods cannot resolve. AnyFlow breaks this bottleneck by enabling a single distilled model to flexibly trade compute for quality at runtime, which is highly valuable for both production deployment and further research into scalable generative models.

Solve the Loop: Attractor Models for Language and Reasoning 解决循环问题：用于语言与推理的吸引子模型

🧑‍🔬 Jacob Fein-Ashley, Paria Rashidinejad

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 用隐式微分求不动点，让循环Transformer高效稳定地扩展
Attractor Models use implicit differentiation to find fixed points, making iterative Transformers scalable and stable.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 循环Transformer（Looped Transformers）通过反复精炼隐层表示来增强推理能力，但存在训练不稳定、优化成本高、循环深度固定等问题。传统的反向传播需要展开所有循环步骤，导致显存随深度线性增长，限制了模型的实际可用性。如何让循环架构既稳定又高效，成为一大挑战。 Looped Transformers improve reasoning by iteratively refining representations, but they suffer from training instability, high optimization cost, and fixed recurrence depth that makes them hard to scale. Standard backpropagation through all recurrence steps causes memory to grow linearly with depth. The field needs a way to make iterative refinement both stable and computationally efficient.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **不动点求解**：引入"吸引子模块"，将迭代精炼转化为不动点求解问题，无需展开所有循环步骤即可获得梯度。 • **隐式微分**：利用隐函数定理，训练时显存与循环深度无关（常数级），彻底解决深度循环的显存瓶颈。 • **自适应迭代次数**：推理时根据收敛条件动态决定迭代次数，而非使用固定深度，灵活性大幅提升。 • **均衡内化现象**：不动点训练使模型的初始输出嵌入天然靠近均衡点，推理时可直接移除吸引子模块而几乎不损失性能。 • **Fixed-point solver**: An attractor module refines output embeddings by solving for a fixed point, decoupling gradient computation from recurrence depth via implicit differentiation. • **Constant training memory**: Because gradients come from the implicit function theorem rather than unrolling, memory cost stays constant regardless of how many iterations are used. • **Adaptive iteration depth**: The number of refinement steps is chosen at inference time by convergence, not hard-coded, enabling flexible computation allocation. • **Equilibrium internalization**: Fixed-point training nudges the backbone's initial output near equilibrium, so the attractor solver can be dropped at inference with minimal accuracy loss.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验分为两大场景：（1）大规模语言模型预训练，在标准语言建模基准上与不同参数量的标准Transformer和稳定循环模型进行对比，评估困惑度（perplexity）和下游任务准确率；（2）小型模型推理，使用约1000个样本在Sudoku-Extreme和Maze-Hard等挑战性推理任务上评估，基线包括Claude、GPT o3等前沿模型及专用递归推理器，评估准确率和参数效率。 Experiments cover two regimes: (1) Large-scale language model pretraining, where Attractor Models are compared against standard Transformers and stable looped models across multiple sizes on language modeling benchmarks, measuring perplexity and downstream accuracy. (2) Tiny-model reasoning, where a 27M-parameter Attractor Model trained on ~1,000 examples is evaluated on Sudoku-Extreme and Maze-Hard tasks, with baselines including frontier models (Claude, GPT o3) and specialized recursive reasoners, measuring accuracy and parameter efficiency.
📊 实验
结果		 • 语言建模：困惑度最高降低 **46.6%**，下游任务准确率最高提升 **19.7%**，同时降低训练成本。 • **770M** 吸引子模型超越了使用 **两倍token** 训练的 **1.3B** 标准Transformer。 • 推理任务：仅 **27M** 参数 + 约 **1000个样本**，Sudoku-Extreme 准确率达 **91.4%**，Maze-Hard 达 **93.1%**。 • Claude、GPT o3等前沿模型在上述推理任务中准确率 **接近0%**，专用递归推理器在更大规模下也出现性能崩溃，而吸引子模型随规模扩展表现持续良好。 • 均衡内化：移除吸引子模块后，性能几乎无损（具体降幅较小，论文中有量化结果）。 • Language modeling: up to **46.6% perplexity reduction** and up to **19.7% downstream accuracy improvement** versus standard Transformers, with lower training cost. • A **770M** Attractor Model outperforms a **1.3B** Transformer trained on **2× more tokens**. • Reasoning: a **27M-parameter** model trained on ~**1,000 examples** achieves **91.4% accuracy on Sudoku-Extreme** and **93.1% on Maze-Hard**. • Frontier models (Claude, GPT o3) score near **0%** on these tasks; specialized recursive reasoners collapse at larger sizes, while Attractor Models scale favorably. • Equilibrium internalization: removing the attractor solver at inference causes only minimal accuracy degradation, confirming the backbone internalizes the fixed-point solution.
🏁 结论 吸引子模型通过将循环计算转化为不动点求解问题，并借助隐式微分实现常数显存训练，同时涌现出"均衡内化"现象，使迭代精炼在语言建模和推理任务上均达到可扩展、高效、稳定的新水平。这项工作为循环Transformer的实用化部署提供了关键突破，并揭示了模型可以"学会内化"其自身推理过程这一深刻性质。 Attractor Models make iterative refinement scalable by reformulating recurrence as fixed-point solving with implicit differentiation, achieving constant training memory and adaptive depth — yielding large gains in both language modeling and structured reasoning. The discovery of equilibrium internalization further reveals that models can learn to internalize their own iterative reasoning, enabling efficient deployment without the solver at inference time.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

吸引子模型由两个模块组成：骨干模块（Backbone）首先生成初始输出嵌入，然后吸引子模块通过迭代求解不动点对其进行精炼。不动点求解的梯度通过隐式微分（隐函数定理）计算，而非展开循环步骤，因此训练显存为常数，与循环深度无关。推理时，模型根据收敛准则自适应地决定迭代次数。此外，由于不动点训练的特性，骨干模块的初始输出会自然收敛至均衡点附近，使得推理阶段可选择去除吸引子模块（"均衡内化"），以降低部署成本。 The Attractor Model consists of a backbone module that proposes initial output embeddings and an attractor module that refines them by iteratively solving for a fixed point. Gradients during training are obtained via implicit differentiation (the implicit function theorem), meaning memory stays constant regardless of recurrence depth — no unrolling required. At inference time, the number of refinement iterations is chosen adaptively based on convergence rather than a fixed budget. A key emergent property called "equilibrium internalization" means the backbone's initial guess naturally lands near the fixed point after training, so the attractor solver can optionally be removed at inference with little accuracy penalty.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[z^* = f_\theta(z^*, x)\]
💡 不动点方程：吸引子模块 $f_\theta$ 以输入 $x$ 和当前嵌入 $z$ 为输入，迭代求解满足自洽条件的不动点 $z^*$，即输出等于输入时的稳定状态。 Fixed-point equation: the attractor module $f_\theta$ takes input $x$ and embedding $z$, and we seek $z^*$ where the output equals the input — the stable equilibrium the model converges to.
\[\frac{dz^*}{d\theta} = -\left(\frac{\partial f_\theta}{\partial z}\bigg|_{z^*} - I\right)^{-1} \frac{\partial f_\theta}{\partial \theta}\bigg|_{z^*}\]
💡 隐式微分梯度公式：通过隐函数定理直接在不动点处计算梯度，无需展开所有迭代步骤，使训练显存与循环深度无关（常数级）。 Implicit differentiation gradient: by applying the implicit function theorem at the fixed point $z^*$, gradients are computed without unrolling iterations, keeping training memory constant regardless of recurrence depth.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

循环/迭代计算是让小模型具备深度推理能力的关键路径，但训练不稳定和显存瓶颈一直是工程落地的拦路虎；本文提供了一套理论严谨、实验充分的解决方案。"均衡内化"现象尤其值得关注：它暗示模型能够将多步推理过程压缩进单次前向传播，对未来高效推理模型的设计具有深远启发。 Iterative computation is a key pathway to giving compact models deep reasoning abilities, yet training instability and memory costs have blocked practical adoption; this paper delivers a principled, empirically strong solution. The equilibrium internalization finding is especially profound — it suggests models can compress multi-step reasoning into a single forward pass, with major implications for efficient inference and the design of future reasoning systems.

$δ$-mem: Efficient Online Memory for Large Language Models δ-mem：面向大语言模型的高效在线记忆机制

🧑‍🔬 Jingdi Lei, Di Zhang, Junxian Li, Weida Wang et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 用极小固定状态矩阵为冻结LLM注入高效长期记忆
A tiny fixed-size memory state augments frozen LLMs with efficient long-term memory via delta-rule learning.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型在长期助手和智能体场景中需要记住并复用历史信息，但简单扩大上下文窗口代价高昂，且往往无法有效利用远距离上下文。如何在不修改模型本身、不扩展上下文的前提下，让LLM具备实用的长期记忆能力，是一个关键挑战。 LLMs used in long-term assistants and agent systems need to accumulate and reuse historical information over time. Simply extending the context window is computationally expensive and doesn't guarantee effective use of distant context. There is a need for a lightweight, plug-in memory solution that works without modifying or retraining the base model.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 创新点1：提出δ-mem，一种轻量级在线记忆模块，以固定大小的状态矩阵（仅8×8）压缩历史信息，无需全量微调或替换主干网络。 • 创新点2：采用delta规则学习动态更新记忆状态，并将读出结果以低秩校正的方式注入冻结骨干模型的注意力计算，实现"软插入"记忆。 • 创新点3：记忆模块与注意力直接耦合，避免了显式上下文扩展，在极小参数开销下获得显著的记忆增益。 • Novel fixed-size (8×8) associative memory state compresses unlimited history with constant memory overhead — no context window growth needed. • Delta-rule learning dynamically writes past information into the state matrix; readouts produce low-rank corrections injected directly into frozen attention layers. • The backbone LLM is fully frozen — only the lightweight memory adapter is trained, preserving general capabilities while adding long-term memory.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个记忆密集型和通用能力基准上评估δ-mem，包括MemoryAgentBench（智能体长期记忆）和LoCoMo（长对话记忆）等记忆专项基准，以及通用语言理解基准。基线包括冻结主干模型本身和多种非δ-mem记忆增强方法。评估指标为各基准的平均得分及具体任务得分。 The paper evaluates δ-mem on memory-intensive benchmarks including MemoryAgentBench (long-term agent memory) and LoCoMo (long-conversation memory), as well as general language capability benchmarks. Baselines include the frozen backbone model without memory and several competing non-δ-mem memory augmentation methods. Performance is measured by average benchmark scores and per-task scores, comparing δ-mem against all baselines under equivalent frozen-backbone conditions.
📊 实验
结果		 • 平均得分达到冻结主干的1.10倍，超越最强非δ-mem记忆基线的1.15倍。 • 在记忆密集型基准上增益更大：MemoryAgentBench上达到1.31倍，LoCoMo上达到1.20倍。 • 仅使用8×8（64个元素）的在线记忆状态矩阵即实现上述增益，同时基本保留通用能力。 • Average score reaches 1.10× the frozen backbone and 1.15× the strongest non-δ-mem memory baseline. • On memory-heavy benchmarks: 1.31× on MemoryAgentBench and 1.20× on LoCoMo. • All gains achieved with only an 8×8 (64-element) memory state matrix, while largely preserving general language capabilities.
🏁 结论 本文证明了有效的长期记忆可以通过一个与注意力计算直接耦合的紧凑在线状态来实现，无需全量微调、替换主干模型或显式扩展上下文窗口。δ-mem以极低的参数和计算开销，在多个记忆密集型基准上取得了显著提升，为LLM记忆增强提供了一种实用、轻量的新范式。 This paper demonstrates that effective long-term memory for LLMs can be achieved through a compact online state directly coupled with attention, without fine-tuning the backbone, replacing the architecture, or extending the context window. δ-mem establishes a practical, lightweight paradigm for memory augmentation that delivers meaningful gains on memory-intensive tasks at minimal cost.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

δ-mem在冻结的全注意力LLM主干外，附加一个紧凑的在线联想记忆状态矩阵（如8×8）。历史信息通过delta规则（一种经典的联想记忆学习规则）在线写入该状态矩阵，随着对话或任务推进不断更新。在生成阶段，记忆状态被读出，产生低秩向量，以残差校正的方式修改主干注意力的键/值计算，从而让模型在生成时能"感知"到历史信息。整个流程无需微调主干模型，只需训练极少量的适配参数。 δ-mem attaches a compact associative memory state matrix (e.g., 8×8) to a fully frozen LLM backbone. As the conversation or task progresses, past tokens are compressed into this fixed-size state using the delta learning rule — a classical associative memory update that minimizes reconstruction error. During generation, the memory state is read out to produce low-rank correction vectors that are added to the backbone's attention key/value computations as residual adjustments. Only the small memory adapter parameters are trained; the entire backbone remains frozen throughout.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathbf{M}_{t} = \mathbf{M}_{t-1} + \Delta\mathbf{M}_t, \quad \Delta\mathbf{M}_t = \text{DeltaRule}(\mathbf{k}_t, \mathbf{v}_t, \mathbf{M}_{t-1})\]
💡 记忆状态矩阵M在每个时间步通过delta规则进行在线更新，将当前键值对（k_t, v_t）写入固定大小的联想记忆中。 The memory state matrix M is updated online at each timestep using the delta rule, writing the current key-value pair into the fixed-size associative memory.
\[\hat{\mathbf{v}}_t = \mathbf{v}_t + \text{LowRank}(\mathbf{M}_t \cdot \mathbf{q}_t)\]
💡 通过用查询向量q_t读取记忆状态M_t，生成低秩校正项，叠加到注意力的值向量上，实现记忆信息的注入。 The memory state is queried with q_t to produce a low-rank correction added to the attention value vector, injecting historical information into the generation process.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着LLM越来越多地应用于长期对话助手和多步骤智能体任务，高效记忆成为核心需求，而现有方案（扩展上下文、RAG、全量微调）均有显著代价。δ-mem提供了一种即插即用的轻量级解决方案，为构建真正具备长期记忆的AI智能体开辟了低成本路径。 As LLMs are increasingly deployed in long-horizon assistants and multi-step agent pipelines, efficient long-term memory is a critical bottleneck that existing approaches (context extension, RAG, full fine-tuning) address only at high cost. δ-mem offers a plug-and-play, low-overhead solution that could enable practical long-term memory in production AI agents without expensive retraining or infrastructure changes.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

VGGT-Ω对原始VGGT架构进行了三方面改造：首先，将多个预测头统一为单一密集预测头，采用多任务监督信号联合训练；其次，引入寄存器（registers）机制，将跨帧信息交换限制在寄存器内部，用寄存器注意力替代部分全局注意力，大幅降低计算复杂度；最后，设计了专门针对动态场景的高质量数据标注流水线，并结合自监督学习协议充分利用无标注视频数据。这三项改进协同作用，使模型可以用约30%的前代显存进行训练，同时支持静态和动态场景的高质量重建。 VGGT-Ω reformulates the original VGGT architecture along three axes. First, it merges multiple task-specific heads into a single dense prediction head trained with multi-task supervision. Second, it introduces "registers" — special tokens that aggregate scene-level information — and replaces global inter-frame attention with register attention, drastically reducing memory and compute. Third, it pairs a new dynamic-scene data annotation pipeline with a self-supervised learning protocol to exploit large-scale unlabeled video, enabling training with 15× more supervised data. Together, these changes cut GPU memory usage to roughly 30% of the original VGGT.
