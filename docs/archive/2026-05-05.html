UniVidX: A Unified Multimodal Framework for Versatile Video Generation via Diffusion Priors UniVidX：基于扩散先验的多功能视频生成统一多模态框架

🧑‍🔬 Houyuan Chen, Hong Li, Xianghao Kong, Tianrui Zhu et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 单一扩散模型统一处理多种视频模态生成任务
A unified diffusion framework handles multiple video modalities with one model instead of separate per-task models.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有视频扩散模型通常针对每个任务（如深度估计、法线预测、Alpha抠图）分别训练，导致输入输出映射固定、模型无法学习跨模态关系。这种方式既低效又缺乏灵活性，也错失了利用跨模态信息提升整体质量的机会。 Existing video diffusion models (VDMs) are typically trained separately for each task (e.g., depth estimation, normal prediction, alpha matting), which fixes the input-output mapping and prevents the model from learning relationships across modalities. This approach is wasteful, inflexible, and misses opportunities for cross-modal information sharing that could improve quality across all tasks.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **随机条件遮蔽（SCM）**：训练时随机将模态划分为干净条件或噪声目标，实现任意到任意的条件生成，打破固定映射限制。 • **解耦门控LoRA（DGL）**：仅在某模态作为生成目标时激活对应的LoRA适配器，在适配新模态分布的同时保留VDM主干的原始先验。 • **跨模态自注意力（CMSA）**：跨模态共享键值（K/V），保留各模态独立的查询（Q），高效促进跨模态对齐与信息交互。 • **Stochastic Condition Masking (SCM)**: randomly assigns modalities as either clean conditions or noisy targets during training, enabling any-to-any conditional generation without fixed mappings. • **Decoupled Gated LoRA (DGL)**: activates per-modality LoRA adapters only when that modality is a generation target, preserving the VDM backbone's original priors while adapting to new modality distributions. • **Cross-Modal Self-Attention (CMSA)**: shares keys and values across modalities while keeping modality-specific queries, efficiently promoting cross-modal alignment and information exchange.
🧪 实验
内容		 UniVid-Intrinsic在IIW（反照率）、MIT Intrinsics及视频法线估计等内在分解基准上评估，与Intrinsic Image Diffusion、GeoWizard、StableNormal等专用模型对比。UniVid-Alpha在VideoMatte240K和BG-20k等视频抠图基准上评估，与RobustVideoMatting及视频Alpha扩散模型等方法对比。两个模型还在野外场景中进行了泛化性定性评测，值得注意的是训练数据不足1000个视频。 UniVid-Intrinsic was evaluated on intrinsic decomposition benchmarks including IIW (albedo), MIT Intrinsics, and video normal estimation datasets, compared against state-of-the-art specialized models like Intrinsic Image Diffusion, GeoWizard, and StableNormal. UniVid-Alpha was evaluated on video matting benchmarks such as VideoMatte240K and BG-20k against methods like RobustVideoMatting and video alpha diffusion models. Both models were also tested on in-the-wild generalization with qualitative assessments, notably trained on fewer than 1,000 videos.
📊 实验
结果		 UniVid-Intrinsic在反照率估计（IIW WHDR指标）、法线估计（平均角误差）和辐照度预测上均达到或超越专用单任务模型水平。UniVid-Alpha在VideoMatte240K上的SAD和MSE指标与RobustVideoMatting等专用抠图模型持平或更优。两个模型在不足1000个训练视频的条件下，均展现出强劲的野外泛化能力，以统一模型在4个以上不同任务上匹配甚至超越各任务专属的最先进方法。 UniVid-Intrinsic achieves competitive or superior performance on albedo estimation (IIW WHDR reduced vs. baselines), normal estimation (mean angular error improved), and irradiance prediction compared to specialized single-task models. UniVid-Alpha achieves competitive alpha matting quality on VideoMatte240K (SAD and MSE metrics on par with or better than RobustVideoMatting and dedicated matting models). Both models demonstrate strong in-the-wild generalization despite being trained on fewer than 1,000 videos, outperforming or matching task-specific state-of-the-art methods across 4+ distinct tasks within a single unified model.
🏁 结论 UniVidX证明了基于视频扩散先验构建的单一统一多模态框架，即便在极少训练数据下，也能在多种像素对齐视频生成任务上匹配甚至超越各任务专属模型。其三大核心设计——SCM、DGL和CMSA——共同实现了灵活的全向生成、模态专属适配与跨模态一致性，同时保留了主干模型的原始生成能力。 UniVidX proves that a single unified multimodal framework built on video diffusion priors can match or exceed specialized per-task models across diverse pixel-aligned video generation tasks, even with very limited training data. Its three core designs—SCM, DGL, and CMSA—together enable flexible omni-directional generation, modality-specific adaptation, and cross-modal consistency without sacrificing the backbone's original generative strength.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

UniVidX将所有像素对齐的多模态视频任务统一表述为共享多模态空间中的条件生成问题，构建于预训练视频扩散主干之上。训练时，SCM随机将模态分配为条件（干净）或目标（加噪），使模型同时学习所有方向的生成路径。DGL引入轻量级的每模态LoRA适配器，仅在该模态被生成时激活，保证模态专属适配的同时不破坏主干先验。CMSA在每个注意力层跨模态共享K/V张量，使任意模态都能关注到其他模态的信息，而查询向量保持模态专属。文章实例化了两个系统：UniVid-Intrinsic（RGB+反照率+辐照度+法线）和UniVid-Alpha（混合RGB+RGBA图层）。 UniVidX formulates all pixel-aligned multimodal video tasks as conditional generation within a shared multimodal space built on top of a pretrained video diffusion backbone. During training, SCM randomly partitions modalities into conditions (clean) and targets (noisy), so the model learns all directional generation paths simultaneously. DGL introduces lightweight per-modality LoRA adapters gated by whether that modality is being generated, ensuring modality-specific adaptation without corrupting the backbone's strong priors. CMSA injects cross-modal attention by sharing K/V tensors across modalities at each attention layer, letting any modality attend to information from all others while queries remain modality-specific. Two instantiations are demonstrated: UniVid-Intrinsic (RGB + albedo + irradiance + normal) and UniVid-Alpha (blended RGB + RGBA layers).

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L} = \mathbb{E}_{t, \epsilon}\left[\left\| \epsilon - \epsilon_\theta\left(z_t^{\text{tgt}}, t, z_0^{\text{cond}}\right) \right\|^2\right]\]
💡 统一扩散训练目标：模型学习从加噪目标模态 z_t^{tgt} 和干净条件模态 z_0^{cond} 中预测噪声，SCM随机决定哪些模态作为目标、哪些作为条件。 Unified diffusion training objective: the model learns to predict noise from a noisy target modality z_t^{tgt} given a clean condition modality z_0^{cond}; SCM randomly determines which modalities are targets vs. conditions each step.
\[\text{Attn}(Q_i, K, V) = \text{softmax}\left(\frac{Q_i K^\top}{\sqrt{d}}\right)V, \quad K = V = \text{concat}(K_1, \ldots, K_M)\]
💡 跨模态自注意力（CMSA）：每个模态 i 使用自身的查询 Q_i，但键 K 和值 V 由所有模态拼接而成，使模态间可相互参考信息。 Cross-Modal Self-Attention (CMSA): each modality i uses its own query Q_i but attends to keys and values concatenated from all M modalities, enabling cross-modal information sharing within a single attention operation.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本文挑战了多模态视频理解与生成领域"一任务一模型"的主流范式，证明统一方法既更高效又更强大。对更广泛的机器学习社区而言，SCM+DGL+CMSA的设计组合提供了一套可复用的方法论，可应用于任意预训练视频扩散模型，以极少数据解锁多任务生成能力。 This work challenges the prevailing paradigm of training one model per task in multimodal video understanding and generation, showing that a unified approach is both more efficient and more capable. For the broader ML community, it offers a principled recipe—SCM + DGL + CMSA—that can be applied to any pretrained VDM to unlock versatile multi-task generation with minimal data.

Mamoda2.5: Enhancing Unified Multimodal Model with DiT-MoE Mamoda2.5：使用DiT-MoE增强统一多模态模型

🧑‍🔬 Yangming Shi, Shixiang Zhu, Tao Shen, Zhimiao Yu et al.

🎯 AR-扩散统一框架结合MoE，实现顶级视频编辑，推理提速95.9倍。
A unified AR-Diffusion model with DiT-MoE achieves top-tier video editing at 95.9× faster inference.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 构建一个能同时处理多模态理解和高质量视频生成的统一模型，计算代价极高且推理速度慢。现有方法难以在不大幅增加训练成本的情况下扩展模型容量，视频编辑还需要30步扩散迭代，导致实际部署延迟过高。 Building a single model that handles both multimodal understanding and high-quality video/image generation is extremely resource-intensive and slow at inference time. Existing unified models struggle to scale capacity affordably, and video editing in particular requires many diffusion steps (e.g., 30), making real-world deployment impractical due to latency.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 将DiT-MoE引入扩散Transformer骨干：128个专家、Top-8路由，总参数25B但仅激活3B，以极低计算量实现超大模型容量。 • 联合少步蒸馏与强化学习，将30步编辑模型压缩为4步，推理速度较开源基线最高提升95.9倍。 • 在单一架构中无缝统一自回归（AR）理解与扩散生成，并在广告视频编辑场景中成功部署，内部成功率达98%。 • Introduces DiT-MoE into the Diffusion Transformer backbone: 128 experts with Top-8 routing, scaling to 25B total parameters while activating only 3B — massive capacity at a fraction of the compute cost. • Combines joint few-step distillation and reinforcement learning to compress a 30-step editing model into a 4-step model, achieving up to 95.9× inference speedup over open-source baselines. • Seamlessly unifies autoregressive (AR) understanding and diffusion-based generation in one architecture, with a demonstrated real-world deployment in advertising video editing at 98% success rate.
🧪 实验
内容		 模型在VBench 2.0上评估整体视频生成质量，在OpenVE-Bench上评估视频编辑质量，对比对象包括开源模型和Kling O1等商业顶级系统。同时对MoE设计（专家数量、路由方式）进行消融实验。推理速度与开源视频编辑基线对比。实际部署在广告视频编辑内部场景中验证任务成功率。 The model was evaluated on VBench 2.0 for overall video generation quality, and on OpenVE-Bench for video editing quality, comparing against open-source models and proprietary systems like Kling O1. Ablations were conducted on the MoE design choices (number of experts, routing). Inference speed was benchmarked against open-source video editing baselines. Real-world deployment was evaluated in an internal advertising video editing scenario measuring task success rate.
📊 实验
结果		 • VBench 2.0：达到顶级生成性能，超越所有评估的开源模型。 • OpenVE-Bench：视频编辑质量创新纪录，与商业顶级模型Kling O1持平。 • 推理速度：相比开源基线最高提速95.9倍（30步→4步，通过蒸馏+强化学习实现）。 • 实际部署：广告视频编辑内部场景成功率达98%。 • VBench 2.0: top-tier generation performance, surpassing all evaluated open-source models. • OpenVE-Bench: sets a new record in video editing quality, matching proprietary Kling O1. • Inference speed: up to 95.9× faster video editing inference vs. open-source baselines (30-step → 4-step via distillation + RL). • Real-world deployment: 98% success rate in internal advertising video editing scenarios.
🏁 结论 Mamoda2.5证明，在统一AR-扩散框架中结合DiT-MoE与联合少步蒸馏和强化学习，能以极低计算和延迟代价实现顶级视频生成与编辑质量。其在工业场景的成功部署验证了该方法在实际多模态视频应用中的可行性。 Mamoda2.5 proves that combining DiT-MoE with joint few-step distillation and RL within a unified AR-Diffusion framework can deliver top-tier video generation and editing quality at a fraction of the compute and latency cost of prior methods. Its real-world deployment success demonstrates practical viability for industrial multimodal video applications.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Mamoda2.5基于统一的AR-扩散框架，扩散Transformer（DiT）负责图像和视频生成，自回归模块负责多模态理解。为高效扩展模型容量，在DiT中引入细粒度MoE层，配置128个专家和Top-8路由，每次前向传播仅激活25B参数中的3B。为加速推理，作者联合训练少步蒸馏目标与强化学习信号，使模型仅需4步扩散即可产出高质量编辑结果（原为30步），在不牺牲质量的前提下大幅降低延迟。 Mamoda2.5 builds on a unified AR-Diffusion framework where a Diffusion Transformer (DiT) backbone handles image and video generation while an autoregressive module manages multimodal understanding. To scale model capacity efficiently, a fine-grained Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) layer is inserted into the DiT, with 128 experts and Top-8 routing — the model activates only 3B of its 25B parameters per forward pass. For fast inference, the authors jointly train a few-step distillation objective and a reinforcement learning signal to teach the model to produce high-quality edits in just 4 diffusion steps instead of 30, dramatically cutting latency without sacrificing quality.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Active Params} = \frac{\text{Top-}k}{N_{\text{experts}}} \times \text{Total Params} \approx \frac{8}{128} \times 25\text{B} \approx 3\text{B}\]
💡 MoE路由机制：128个专家中每次仅激活Top-8，使25B总参数模型每步实际计算量仅约3B，大幅降低训练与推理成本。 MoE routing: only Top-8 out of 128 experts are activated per token, so the 25B-parameter model effectively computes with ~3B active parameters per forward pass, drastically cutting costs.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本研究用优雅的工程方案（MoE+蒸馏+强化学习）同时解决了统一多模态模型长期面临的两大瓶颈：扩展成本过高和扩散推理过慢，并交出了工业级成绩。95.9倍加速和98%实际成功率表明，统一AR-扩散模型已具备生产部署条件，将开源系统的天花板提升至可与Kling O1等商业巨头抗衡的水平。 This work addresses two longstanding bottlenecks in unified multimodal models — prohibitive scaling costs and slow diffusion inference — with elegant engineering solutions (MoE + distillation + RL) that deliver industry-grade results. The 95.9× speedup and real-world 98% success rate signal that unified AR-Diffusion models are ready for production deployment, raising the bar for what open-source systems can achieve against proprietary giants like Kling O1.

Let ViT Speak: Generative Language-Image Pre-training 让ViT开口说话：生成式语言-图像预训练

🧑‍🔬 Yan Fang, Mengcheng Lan, Zilong Huang, Weixian Lei et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 生成式预训练让ViT直接预测语言Token，更好地对齐多模态大模型
GenLIP trains Vision Transformers to directly predict language tokens, replacing contrastive pretraining for better LLM alignment.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有视觉编码器（如CLIP）使用对比学习训练，其目标函数与大语言模型（LLM）自回归预测的本质存在不一致。这种"目标错位"可能限制视觉表示与语言模型的深度融合。此外，对比学习需要大批次构建和独立的文本编码器，流程复杂。 Current vision encoders for multimodal LLMs (like CLIP) are trained with contrastive objectives that don't naturally align with the autoregressive next-token prediction paradigm of LLMs. This mismatch may limit how well the visual representations integrate with language models downstream. Additionally, contrastive training requires large, carefully constructed batches and a separate text encoder, adding complexity.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **无对比损失、无额外解码器**：GenLIP用标准语言建模目标训练，ViT在单一Transformer中直接从视觉Token预测文本Token，流程极简。 • **自回归对齐**：与LLM相同的next-token预测损失，使视觉编码器天然对齐下游语言模型的解码方式。 • **多分辨率原始宽高比预训练**：在原始分辨率图像上继续预训练，大幅提升OCR、图表理解等细节敏感任务的表现。 • **No contrastive loss, no extra decoder**: GenLIP uses a plain language modeling objective — the ViT directly predicts text tokens from visual tokens in a single transformer, radically simplifying the pipeline. • **Autoregressive alignment**: By training with the same next-token prediction loss as LLMs, the vision encoder is natively aligned with downstream language model decoding. • **Multi-resolution native aspect ratio pretraining**: Continued pretraining on images at native resolutions gives strong gains on detail-sensitive tasks like OCR and chart understanding.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在Recap-DataComp-1B的最多80亿样本上预训练GenLIP，将其视觉编码器接入多模态LLM框架进行评测。评测基准涵盖通用视觉问答（MMBench、MMStar、MMMU、SeedBench）、OCR与文档理解（OCRBench、DocVQA、ChartQA、TextVQA）以及幻觉评测（POPE、HallusionBench）。基线模型包括SigLIP、EVA-CLIP等强对比学习视觉编码器。此外还探究了数据规模和模型规模的扩展效果。 The authors pretrain GenLIP on up to 8B samples from Recap-DataComp-1B and evaluate the resulting vision encoder by plugging it into multimodal LLM pipelines. Benchmarks span general visual question answering (MMBench, MMStar, MMMU, SeedBench), OCR and document understanding (OCRBench, DocVQA, ChartQA, TextVQA), and hallucination (POPE, HallusionBench). Baselines include strong CLIP-style contrastive models such as SigLIP and EVA-CLIP, which also serve as vision backbones for MLLMs. The effect of data scale and model size scaling is also investigated.
📊 实验
结果		 GenLIP在使用显著更少预训练数据的情况下，在大多数基准上与SigLIP、EVA-CLIP等强基线持平或超越。多分辨率继续预训练后，OCR敏感任务提升显著，在OCRBench、DocVQA、ChartQA上均优于基础版GenLIP。在MMBench、MMStar、MMMU、SeedBench等通用基准上也与顶级对比模型竞争力相当。随着数据规模（最多80亿）和模型规模增大，性能持续提升，验证了可扩展性。与同等数据预算的SigLIP基线相比，GenLIP在OCRBench和ChartQA上取得更优结果。 GenLIP matches or surpasses strong contrastive baselines (SigLIP, EVA-CLIP) across most benchmarks while using substantially less pretraining data. On OCR-sensitive benchmarks, the multi-resolution continued pretraining variant shows notable gains: e.g., improvements on OCRBench, DocVQA, and ChartQA over the base GenLIP. On general benchmarks like MMBench, MMStar, MMMU, and SeedBench, GenLIP achieves competitive scores with top contrastive models. The model also demonstrates consistent improvements as both data scale (up to 8B samples) and model size increase, confirming scalability. Specific numbers reported include competitive or superior performance on OCRBench and ChartQA compared to SigLIP-based baselines at comparable or smaller data budgets.
🏁 结论 GenLIP证明了用标准语言建模目标训练ViT的极简生成式预训练框架，能在多模态LLM任务上与甚至超越对比学习基线，同时流程更简单、与LLM架构天然对齐。本文将生成式语言-图像预训练确立为构建MLLM视觉编码器的可行且可扩展的替代方案。 GenLIP demonstrates that a minimalist generative pretraining framework — training ViTs with a standard language modeling objective — can match or surpass contrastive pre-training baselines for multimodal LLMs, while being simpler and more naturally aligned with LLM architectures. This work establishes generative language-image pretraining as a viable and scalable alternative to contrastive approaches for building vision encoders in MLLMs.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

GenLIP将视觉Transformer与标准语言建模目标结合：ViT提取视觉Token后，模型自回归地预测对应的文字Token，损失函数即普通的交叉熵，与训练LLM完全一致。整个框架只有一个Transformer，无需对比批次构建、动量编码器或独立文本解码器。模型在Recap-DataComp-1B的80亿图文对上进行预训练，随后在原始宽高比的多分辨率图像上继续训练，以增强对细节信息（如OCR、图表）的感知能力。 GenLIP takes a Vision Transformer and trains it to autoregressively predict language tokens conditioned on visual tokens, using a standard language modeling (cross-entropy) loss — the same objective used to train LLMs. There is no contrastive batch construction, no momentum encoder, and no separate text decoder; a single transformer handles both modalities. The model is pre-trained on 8 billion image-text pairs from the Recap-DataComp-1B dataset. After initial pretraining, the model undergoes continued pretraining on multi-resolution images at native aspect ratios to improve fine-grained visual understanding for tasks like OCR and chart reading.
