Wan-Streamer v0.1: End-to-end Real-time Interactive Foundation Models Wan-Streamer v0.1：端到端实时交互基础模型

🧑‍🔬 Lianghua Huang, Zhifan Wu, Wei Wang, Yupeng Shi et al.

🎯 单一模型实现视听说全双工实时交互，响应延迟低至200毫秒。
A single AI model that sees, hears, speaks, and responds in real-time under 200ms — no assembly required.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy想象一下老式AI助手就像"传话游戏"——你的话要依次经过5个人：听话的、记录的、思考的、说话的、表演的，每个人都会耽误时间。Wan-Streamer把这5个人换成了一个全能天才，他能同时听、想、说——就像真人一样。Think of old AI assistants like a game of telephone played through five different people — your words get passed from a listener, to a transcriber, to a thinker, to a speaker, to an animator. Each person adds a delay. Wan-Streamer replaces all five people with one genius who listens, thinks, and talks all at the same time — like a real human does.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的AI语音和视频助手就像接力赛——一个模块负责识别语音，另一个理解意思，再一个生成回答，还有一个转换成声音，最后一个让虚拟形象开口说话。每次"交棒"都会增加延迟和错误。结果就是对话卡顿别扭，完全不像和真人说话。如何让AI在同一个模型里同时处理声音、视频和文字，实现真正自然流畅的实时对话，一直是个难题。 Existing AI voice and video assistants are like a relay race — one module transcribes your speech, another understands it, another generates a response, another turns it into audio, and yet another animates a face. Each handoff adds delay and error. The result is a laggy, awkward conversation that feels nothing like talking to a real person. Building a truly natural, real-time AI conversation partner that handles audio, video, and language all at once — without stitching together separate tools — has remained an unsolved challenge.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 一个模型包揽一切：语言理解、语音识别、回复生成、文字转语音、视频动画全部在同一个Transformer模型里联合学习，无需任何外部模块。 • 超低延迟：模型侧响应约200毫秒，加上网络传输后总延迟约550毫秒，足以实现真实感的对话体验。 • 为流式而生：整个架构从零重新设计，采用因果编解码器和智能多模态令牌调度，支持以160毫秒为单位、每秒25帧的实时音视频流式处理。 • One model does everything: language understanding, speech recognition, response generation, text-to-speech, and video animation are all learned together inside a single Transformer — no external modules needed. • Ultra-low latency: the system responds in about 200ms on the model side, and under 550ms total including network travel — fast enough to feel like a real conversation. • Streaming by design: the entire architecture was rebuilt from scratch with "causal" (one-direction) encoders and decoders, plus a smart token scheduling system, so it can process and generate audio/video in tiny 160ms chunks at 25 frames per second.
🧪 实验
内容		 论文评估了模型侧响应延迟和端到端交互延迟，在真实网络条件下（含350毫秒双向网络延迟）测试了全双工音视频对话能力。与传统的级联系统（将VAD、ASR、语言模型、TTS、音频驱动动画等多个模块串联）进行了对比，重点考察流畅度、响应时机和跨模态同步质量。系统在25fps视频输出、160毫秒最小流式单元的设置下进行测试。 The paper evaluates model-side response latency and full end-to-end interaction latency under realistic network conditions with 350ms of bidirectional network delay. It compares against traditional cascaded pipeline systems — those that chain together separate voice activity detection (VAD), speech recognition (ASR), language model, text-to-speech (TTS), and avatar animation modules. The system is tested at 25 fps video output with streaming chunks as small as 160ms, measuring real-time duplex audio-visual communication quality and synchronization.
📊 实验
结果		 Wan-Streamer实现了约200毫秒的模型侧响应延迟，加上350毫秒的双向网络延迟后，总端到端交互延迟约为550毫秒。系统支持最短160毫秒的流式单元，以25fps运行，实现亚秒级全双工音视频通信。与多模块级联系统相比，彻底消除了模块间的流水线延迟和错误累积。 Wan-Streamer achieves approximately 200ms model-side response latency and approximately 550ms total end-to-end interaction latency (including 350ms bidirectional network latency). The system supports streaming units as short as 160ms at 25 fps, enabling sub-second full-duplex audio-visual communication. By eliminating cascaded modules, it significantly reduces pipeline latency and error accumulation compared to multi-module systems.
🏁 结论 Wan-Streamer证明了单一统一的Transformer模型能够处理实时音视频对话的全部环节——感知、推理、生成和时序控制——无需任何外部模块。这表明端到端流式多模态交互可以在亚秒级延迟下实现，为真正自然的AI对话伙伴铺平了道路。 Wan-Streamer proves that a single unified Transformer model can handle all aspects of real-time audio-visual conversation — perception, reasoning, generation, and timing — without relying on any external modules. This demonstrates that end-to-end streaming multimodal interaction is achievable at sub-second latency, paving the way for truly natural AI conversation partners.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Wan-Streamer将语言、音频和视频全部塞进一个大型Transformer模型，让它同时读写三种模态。可以把对话想象成一卷交织着文字、音频片段和视频帧的长卷轴——模型从左到右处理这卷轴，始终向前，从不"倒带"。一种叫"块因果注意力"的特殊机制让模型每次只看一小块新输入，使其在用户还没说完时就能开始响应。从音频编码器到视频解码器，每个组件都被重新设计为流式、单向工作模式，还有一个智能调度系统决定何时输出文字、音频或视频，保持一切同步。 Wan-Streamer packs language, audio, and video into one giant Transformer model that reads and writes all three at the same time. Think of the conversation as a single long scroll of interleaved text, audio clips, and video frames — the model processes this scroll left-to-right, always moving forward, never needing to "rewind" to understand context. A special attention mechanism called "block-causal attention" lets the model peek at small chunks of incoming data at a time, so it can start responding before the user even finishes speaking. Every component — from the audio encoder to the video decoder — was redesigned to work in this streaming, forward-only fashion, and a smart scheduling system decides when to output text, audio, or video tokens to keep everything in sync.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项研究让我们距离能够进行真正自然、面对面式实时对话的AI助手更近了一步——想象一下与AI视频通话，对方反应和真人一样快。通过消除笨重的多模块串联，它使实时多模态AI的构建和大规模部署变得大大简化。 This research brings us much closer to AI assistants that can hold truly natural, face-to-face-style conversations in real time — think video calls with an AI that reacts as fast as a human. By eliminating the clunky chain of separate modules, it makes real-time multimodal AI dramatically simpler to build and deploy at scale.

维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现在的AI"智能体"可以自主执行任务，比如写代码、浏览网页、操作工具，但如何准备让它们全面发展的训练数据，几乎没有公开资料。现有的开源项目往往只针对一个特定测试场景训练，换个任务就表现很差。这就像一个运动员只练了一项运动，换个项目就束手无策。研究者和开发者苦于找不到训练通用型智能体的方法。 Modern AI "agents" — models that take actions, use tools, and solve multi-step tasks like coding or web browsing — are increasingly powerful, but nobody publicly shares how to build the training data that makes them work well across many different tasks. Existing open efforts only train models to be good at one specific benchmark, so they fail when asked to do something different. This leaves researchers and developers in the dark about how to create truly versatile AI agents.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首个完全开放的智能体数据筛选流水线，专为训练能跨多类任务泛化的AI智能体而设计，而非只针对单一测试。 • 进行了100多项对照实验，系统摸清了哪些数据"配料"最关键，就像厨师反复试菜找到最佳配方。 • 构建了10万条高质量多样化训练样本，最终模型在7个智能体基准上平均准确率44.8%，比之前最强开源模型高出3.9个百分点。 • 数据配方具有优秀的扩展性，在相同计算量下，任意数据规模下都优于竞争对手数据集。 • First fully open data curation pipeline specifically designed to train AI agents that generalize across many different task types — not just one benchmark. • Conducted 100+ controlled experiments to figure out exactly which data ingredients matter most, like a chef systematically testing recipes. • Assembled a 100K example training set with strong diversity, resulting in a model that beats the previous best open-data agent by nearly 4 percentage points. • Shows that their data recipe scales better than competitors — even with less data, it wins.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究团队在7个不同的智能体基准测试上评估了他们训练的模型，覆盖了软件工程、工具使用、交互问题求解等多类任务。对比基线包括Nemotron-Terminal-32B、SWE-Smith、SERA等现有最强开源智能体模型。他们还在多个训练集规模下进行了计算量控制的对比实验，验证数据流水线的扩展性。此外，100多项消融实验系统测试了不同任务来源、数据多样性策略和过滤方法的重要性。 The researchers tested their trained model on seven different agentic benchmarks covering a range of tasks like software engineering, tool use, and interactive problem-solving. They compared against strong baselines including Nemotron-Terminal-32B, SWE-Smith, and SERA — all existing open-data agentic models. They also ran compute-controlled comparisons at multiple training set sizes to check whether their data pipeline scales better than alternatives. The 100+ ablation studies examined which task sources, data diversity strategies, and filtering methods mattered most.
📊 实验
结果		 OT-Agent模型（基于Qwen3-32B微调）在7个智能体基准测试上的平均准确率为44.8%。相比此前最强开源智能体模型Nemotron-Terminal-32B（40.9%），提升了3.9个百分点。在不同训练集规模的计算量控制对比中，OT-Agent数据流水线在每个数据量级上均优于所有其他开源数据集。10万条训练样本展现出良好的数据扩展性，随着数据增加性能持续稳定提升。 The OT-Agent model (fine-tuned Qwen3-32B) achieved an average accuracy of 44.8% across seven agentic benchmarks. This beats the previous best open-data agentic model, Nemotron-Terminal-32B, by 3.9 percentage points (40.9% → 44.8%). In compute-controlled comparisons across all training set sizes, OT-Agent's data pipeline outperformed every alternative open dataset tested. The 100K training set showed strong data scaling properties, meaning performance kept improving reliably as more data was added.
🏁 结论 本文证明，通过精心设计、多样化且完全开放的数据流水线，可以训练出能跨多类任务泛化的AI智能体，而不仅仅局限于某一特定场景。该工作刷新了开源智能体模型的性能记录，并为整个研究社区提供了可复用的"数据配方"。 This paper proves that with a carefully designed, diverse, and fully open data pipeline, you can train an AI agent that generalizes well across many different tasks — not just one. It sets a new state-of-the-art for open-data agentic models and provides the community with a reusable recipe for building better AI agents.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

研究团队从零开始构建了一套数据流水线，从多种不同类型的智能体任务（编程、工具使用、网页操作等）中收集训练样本，而不是只盯着一类任务。他们做了100多项"消融实验"，就像做科学实验一样，每次换掉或去掉某个数据"配料"，看性能是高了还是低了，最终找出最优组合。确定最佳配方后，他们整理了10万条高质量训练样本，用来微调Qwen3-32B这个强大的基础模型。所有数据、代码和模型都完全公开发布。 The team built a data pipeline from scratch that collects training examples from many different agentic tasks — coding, tool use, web navigation, and more — rather than just one type. They ran over 100 "ablation" experiments, which means they systematically swapped out or removed different ingredients (task sources, diversity levels, data quality filters) to measure what actually helps performance. Think of it like a scientific recipe test: try it with oregano, try it without, see what tastes better. After figuring out the best combination, they assembled 100,000 high-quality training examples and used them to fine-tune a powerful base model (Qwen3-32B), releasing everything — data, code, models — to the public.

AI智能体正在成为编程助手、自动化研究工具、数字员工等产品的核心技术，但训练它们的方法此前几乎是商业秘密。本研究将完整的"训练配方"公开，让初创公司、学术机构和独立开发者都能直接使用，大大降低了构建强大AI智能体的门槛。 AI agents are quickly becoming the backbone of products like coding assistants, automated research tools, and digital workers — but building them well has been a closely guarded secret. By making the entire recipe open and public, this work lets anyone — startups, academics, independent developers — train their own capable agents without starting from scratch.

Unlimited OCR Works 无限OCR之作

🧑‍🔬 Youyang Yin, Huanhuan Liu, YY, Qunyi Xie et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 恒定内存占用，单次推理识别数十页文档的高效OCR模型
A constant-memory OCR model that reads dozens of document pages in one pass without slowing down.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个抄书的人，普通人每写一个字都要把前面全部重读一遍（越写越累、桌子越堆越满），而这个新方法让抄书员只需瞄一眼刚写的几行和原稿，桌子永远干净整洁，抄多少页都不会变慢。Think of it like a copyist who, instead of re-reading the entire book every time they write a new word, only glances at the last sentence and the original page — so they never get tired or run out of desk space, no matter how long the book is.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的AI文字识别（OCR）模型在处理长文档时，越识别越慢、越用越多内存——就像一个人抄写越多越累、越来越健忘。以DeepSeek OCR为代表的顶尖模型虽然精度高，但随着识别内容变长，内存消耗和速度问题愈发突出，难以一次性处理几十页的长文档。 Existing AI OCR models slow down and use more and more memory the longer the document they are reading — like a person who gets progressively more tired and forgetful the more they copy. This makes it impractical to scan long documents (many pages) in one go. Current top models like DeepSeek OCR are powerful but suffer from this exact problem as output sequences grow longer.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出"参考滑动窗口注意力"（R-SWA），让内存占用在整个识别过程中保持恒定，不随文档长度增加而膨胀。• 将解码器中所有注意力层替换为R-SWA，无需大改模型架构即可实现"无限长度"文字识别。• 在标准32K最大长度限制下，单次推理即可识别数十页文档，效率远超现有方案。 • Introduces Reference Sliding Window Attention (R-SWA), which keeps memory usage flat no matter how long the document is — like a reader who only keeps a small notepad of recent context instead of memorizing everything. • Replaces all attention layers in the decoder with R-SWA, enabling truly "unlimited" length OCR without redesigning the whole model. • Can transcribe dozens of pages in a single forward pass within the standard 32K token limit, something previous models could not do efficiently.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者以DeepSeek OCR为基线，与其他主流OCR模型在标准OCR评测集上进行对比，涵盖文档理解、场景文字、手写体及多页文档等任务。评测指标包括识别准确率，以及在不同文档长度下的内存消耗和推理速度，以验证恒定KV缓存设计的效率优势。 The authors tested Unlimited OCR against DeepSeek OCR (the baseline) and other leading OCR models on standard OCR benchmarks covering document understanding, scene text, handwriting, and multi-page document tasks. They evaluated using standard OCR accuracy metrics and also measured memory consumption and inference speed across documents of varying lengths to demonstrate the efficiency gains of constant KV cache design.
📊 实验
结果		 Unlimited OCR在标准评测基准上保持了与DeepSeek OCR相当的识别精度，同时将KV缓存大小控制为恒定值，不随文档长度增长。在32K标准最大长度限制内，可单次完成数十页文档的识别，而普通注意力模型在同等条件下会出现内存溢出或严重变慢。R-SWA彻底消除了长序列OCR中内存和计算量随长度平方级增长的问题（详细数值指标见GitHub技术报告）。 Unlimited OCR maintains accuracy on par with DeepSeek OCR across standard benchmarks while achieving a constant KV cache size regardless of document length. Within the standard 32K token limit, it can process dozens of pages in a single forward pass — a task that would cause memory overflow or severe slowdown in standard attention-based models. The R-SWA mechanism eliminates the quadratic growth in memory and computation that normally plagues long-sequence OCR, though specific numeric benchmark scores were not fully detailed in the abstract (refer to the full technical report at GitHub for exact figures).
🏁 结论 本文证明了：只需将解码器的注意力机制替换为滑动窗口方式，即可让OCR在处理超长文档时保持高速、低内存，且不损失精度。这一方案不仅适用于OCR，还可推广到语音识别、翻译等各类序列解析任务，具有广泛的实用价值。 This paper proves that replacing the decoder's attention mechanism with a sliding-window approach keeps OCR fast and memory-efficient even on very long documents, without sacrificing accuracy. It offers a practical upgrade path for any sequence-parsing AI task — not just OCR, but also speech recognition and translation.
🔗 论文
链接		 arXiv: 2606.23050v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

普通OCR大模型在解码时会把已经识别过的所有内容都存进一个"记忆本"（KV缓存），文档越长，记忆本越厚，速度越慢、内存越贵。Unlimited OCR的解决方案是用"参考滑动窗口注意力"（R-SWA）替换解码器中的所有注意力层。打个比方：就像一个抄写员只需回看刚写的几行，加上瞄一眼原稿，而不是每次都从头重读整篇文章。R-SWA用一个固定大小的小窗口在文档上"滑动"，记忆本的大小始终不变。DeepSeek OCR强大的图像编码器保持不动，只换掉解码器的注意力层，改造成本低、效果好。 Normal OCR models using large language models keep a growing "memory log" (called a KV cache) of everything they've read so far — so the longer the document, the more memory they need and the slower they get. Unlimited OCR fixes this with a new attention mechanism called R-SWA. Think of it like a skilled human copyist who only glances back at the last few lines they wrote, plus a quick reference to the original source, rather than re-reading the entire document from the start every time. R-SWA slides a small fixed-size window over the document, keeping memory use constant throughout. The powerful image encoder from DeepSeek OCR is kept intact; only the decoder's attention layers are swapped out for R-SWA, making the upgrade relatively easy to apply.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{KV Cache Size} = O(1) \text{ (R-SWA)} \quad \text{vs} \quad O(n) \text{ (Standard Attention)}\]
💡 标准注意力机制的KV缓存随序列长度n线性增长，而R-SWA将其控制为恒定大小O(1)，不随文档长度变化。 Standard attention's memory grows linearly with document length (n), but R-SWA keeps it constant (O(1)) no matter how long the document gets.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

对企业和开发者来说，AI文档扫描工具从此可以一次性处理整份合同、整本书或完整报告，无需昂贵的GPU内存，也不会因为文档变长而越等越久。R-SWA技术具有通用性，可加速所有处理长序列的AI系统，从字幕生成到法律文书、医疗记录，应用前景广泛。 For businesses and developers, this means AI document scanning tools can now handle entire contracts, books, or reports in one shot — without needing expensive GPU memory or waiting longer for longer documents. The underlying R-SWA technique is general enough to speed up any AI that reads long sequences, from subtitles to legal documents to medical records.

Qwen-AgentWorld: Language World Models for General Agents Qwen-AgentWorld：面向通用智能体的语言世界模型

🧑‍🔬 Yuxin Zuo, Zikai Xiao, Li Sheng, Fei Huang et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 用语言模型模拟真实环境，训练出更强大的通用AI智能体
A language-based world model that simulates AI agent environments, enabling smarter training and planning for general-purpose agents.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像游戏里的"沙盒模式"——新手玩家不用在真实服务器上冒险，可以先在沙盒里练习各种操作、积累经验，再上真实战场。Qwen-AgentWorld就是AI智能体的沙盒：用语言模拟出逼真的"练习关卡"，让AI反复试错、越练越强，而不用每次都在真实环境里折腾。Think of it like a flight simulator for AI agents — instead of crashing real planes to learn, pilots practice in a super-realistic virtual cockpit. Qwen-AgentWorld is that cockpit: it lets AI agents practice thousands of real-world tasks in a safe, cheap, language-powered simulation before ever touching the real thing.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 AI智能体（比如自动操作电脑、浏览网页的机器人）想要变强，就需要大量练习。但在真实环境中练习又慢又贵，而且不可控。现有的AI模型无法准确预测"智能体采取某个动作后会发生什么"，导致智能体无法高效地规划和训练。 AI agents (like computer-using bots or web browsing assistants) need to practice in real environments to get better — but real environments are slow, expensive, and hard to control. Existing AI models can't reliably predict "what happens next" after an agent takes an action, which limits how well agents can plan or train efficiently.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首个能模拟7类真实智能体环境的语言世界模型，支持长链式推理，填补了该领域的空白。 • 三阶段训练流水线（持续预训练→监督微调→强化学习），系统性地让模型学会高保真地预测环境状态变化。 • 提出AgentWorldBench新评测基准，基于5个前沿模型在9个已有基准上的真实交互数据构建，评估更客观公正。 • 双重应用范式：既可作为独立模拟器为强化学习智能体提供训练场，也可作为基础模型预热来提升下游任务表现。 • First-ever language world models (Qwen-AgentWorld) that can simulate 7 different types of real-world AI agent environments using long chain-of-thought reasoning. • A three-stage training pipeline (pre-training → supervised fine-tuning → reinforcement learning) that systematically teaches the model to predict environment dynamics with high fidelity. • Introduces AgentWorldBench, a new benchmark built from real interactions of 5 frontier AI models across 9 established benchmarks to fairly evaluate world models. • Shows two powerful use cases: using the world model as a training simulator for RL agents, and as a warm-up pre-trainer to boost downstream agent performance.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究团队在自建的AgentWorldBench上评测Qwen-AgentWorld，该基准由5个前沿模型（含GPT-4o、Claude等）在9个智能体基准上的真实交互数据构成，覆盖7个领域。对比基线包括业内顶尖的闭源大模型。在两个下游应用上，分别测试了：以世界模型为模拟器进行强化学习训练的效果，以及世界模型预热后在7个智能体基准上的下游性能提升。 The team evaluated Qwen-AgentWorld on AgentWorldBench — a new benchmark they built from real agent interactions of 5 frontier models (including GPT-4o, Claude, etc.) across 9 established agentic benchmarks spanning 7 domains. They compared against strong baseline language models including frontier closed-source models. For the two downstream applications, they tested RL training with the world model as a simulator, and compared agent performance after world-model warm-up versus standard baselines, across 7 agentic benchmarks.
📊 实验
结果		 Qwen-AgentWorld在AgentWorldBench上的模拟保真度显著超越所有现有前沿模型。作为强化学习模拟器时，其训练出的智能体性能超过仅在真实环境中训练的结果。作为预热基础模型时，在全部7个智能体基准上均有性能提升。397B-A17B达到最优水平，即便更小的35B-A3B模型在模拟精度上也超过了更大的闭源竞品。 Qwen-AgentWorld significantly outperforms all existing frontier models on AgentWorldBench in world-model simulation fidelity. When used as an RL simulator, it yields agent gains that surpass training in real environments alone. As a warm-up pre-trainer, world-model training improves downstream agent performance across all 7 agentic benchmarks tested. The 397B-A17B model achieves state-of-the-art results, and even the smaller 35B-A3B model outperforms much larger closed-source competitors on simulation accuracy.
🏁 结论 本文证明了语言模型可以高保真地模拟真实AI智能体环境，并且无论是将其用作训练模拟器还是预热基础模型，都能稳定提升智能体在多种任务上的表现。Qwen-AgentWorld为可扩展、低成本的智能体训练开辟了新范式。 This paper proves that a language model can serve as a high-fidelity simulator of real-world AI agent environments, and that training with such a world model — either as a simulator or as a warm-up — consistently makes agents smarter and more capable across diverse tasks. Qwen-AgentWorld establishes a new paradigm for scalable, cost-effective agent training.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

想象一下给AI智能体造一个超逼真的"飞行模拟器"：飞行员不用开真飞机就能练习，AI也不用在真实环境中反复试错。研究团队先收集了超过1000万条智能体在7个领域（网页浏览、代码操作、设备控制等）的真实交互记录。然后用三步法训练两个大模型（350亿和3970亿参数）：第一步，注入"环境如何变化"的广泛知识（持续预训练）；第二步，教模型一步步推理"下一步会发生什么"（监督微调）；第三步，用定制奖励信号做强化学习，让模拟更逼真。训练好的世界模型既能当便宜可扩展的训练模拟器，也能直接作为强大的智能体基础模型来用。 Think of it like building a super-realistic flight simulator for AI agents. Instead of flying real planes (expensive and risky), pilots (AI agents) practice in a simulator that faithfully mimics real conditions. The team first collected over 10 million real interaction recordings from AI agents working across 7 domains (like web browsing, coding, device control). They then trained two large language models — one ~35B and one ~397B parameters — using a three-step process: first, inject broad knowledge about how environments change (pre-training); second, teach the model to reason step-by-step about what happens next (supervised fine-tuning); third, fine-tune with reinforcement learning using custom reward signals that measure simulation accuracy. The resulting world models can then either serve as cheap, scalable training simulators for new agents, or be adapted directly as strong agent foundation models.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{total}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{rubric}} + \lambda \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{rule}}\]
💡 强化学习阶段的混合奖励函数：将基于评分标准的奖励（rubric）和基于规则的奖励（rule）加权组合，衡量世界模型模拟的保真度 The hybrid reward function used in RL training: combines rubric-based rewards (judging overall simulation quality) and rule-based rewards (checking specific accuracy criteria), weighted together to sharpen the world model's simulation fidelity

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

在真实环境中训练AI智能体既慢又贵，这项工作证明可以用纯语言模型构建一个廉价快速的"练习场"，大幅降低开发优秀AI助手所需的时间和资源。这将加速网页浏览、代码操作、设备控制等日常任务AI助手的研发进程，让真正实用的AI帮手离我们更近一步。 Training AI agents in the real world is slow and costly — this work shows you can build a cheap, fast "practice arena" entirely in language, slashing the time and resources needed to develop capable AI assistants. This could accelerate the development of reliable AI agents for everyday tasks like browsing, coding, and device control, bringing us closer to truly useful AI helpers.
