🎯 首个评估多智能体3D游戏第一视角视频理解能力的基准框架
A new benchmark exposing major gaps in MLLMs' ability to understand multi-agent 3D gameplay from a first-person perspective.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 多模态大语言模型越来越多地被用作3D环境中自主智能体的感知引擎，但现有基准无法测试关键能力，例如追踪快速状态变化、将动作归因到正确的智能体、或从第一人称视角推理并发的多智能体行为。现有评估工具的缺失让我们无法有效衡量并修复当前模型的感知盲区。 Multimodal LLMs are increasingly used as perception engines for autonomous agents in 3D environments, but existing benchmarks don't test critical skills like tracking rapid state changes, attributing actions to the right agent, or reasoning about simultaneous multi-agent behaviors from a first-person view. Without rigorous evaluation tools, we can't measure or fix these blind spots in current models. This is especially important as AI agents are deployed in complex environments like games, robotics, and virtual simulations.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **三元标注体系**：将标注组织为"自身（Self）、其他智能体（Other Agents）、世界（World）"三类，贴合多智能体环境的自然结构。\n• **高密度决策标注**：以每秒1.22个标签的密度捕捉多人3D游戏中快速、并发的事件。\n• **结构化干扰项分类体系**：精心设计的错误选项可精准暴露模型的具体失败模式（如智能体角色归因错误、时序定位幻觉等）。\n• **三级认知复杂度**：2400个QA对按推理难度分为三个层次，从基础感知到跨视频多智能体复杂推理。 • **Triadic annotation system**: Videos are annotated around three entity types — Self, Other Agents, and the World — mirroring how multi-agent environments are naturally structured.\n• **Decision-dense labeling**: Achieves a high annotation density of 1.22 labels/second, capturing the rapid, concurrent events in multiplayer 3D gameplay.\n• **Structured distractor taxonomy**: QA distractors are carefully designed to expose specific failure modes (e.g., hallucination about agent roles, temporal grounding errors), enabling fine-grained model diagnosis.\n• **Cognitive complexity tiers**: 2,400 QA pairs are organized into three levels of reasoning difficulty, from basic perception to complex cross-video multi-agent reasoning.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在GameplayQA基准上评估了多个前沿多模态大语言模型（MLLMs），包括GPT-4o等主流模型。评估任务涵盖三个认知复杂度级别的多项选择QA，涉及第一人称视角的时序理解、跨视频多智能体行为推理、以及智能体角色归因。人类表现作为参照上界，模型的错误类型通过结构化干扰项分类体系进行细粒度分析。 Frontier MLLMs (including GPT-4o-class models) were evaluated on the 2,400 QA pairs in GameplayQA across three cognitive complexity levels. Tasks include multiple-choice questions requiring temporal video grounding, cross-video multi-agent reasoning, and agent-role attribution from first-person 3D gameplay footage. Human performance was established as an upper-bound reference, and model errors were analyzed using the structured distractor taxonomy to identify specific failure categories.
📊 实验
结果		 前沿多模态大语言模型在GameplayQA三个认知复杂度级别上均与人类表现存在显著差距。模型在时序定位、跨视频推理、智能体角色归因以及处理游戏高决策密度方面频繁出错。摘要未披露具体准确率数字，但结果表明当前最先进MLLMs在智能体第一视角多视频任务上表现远逊于人类，揭示了明显的能力天花板。 Frontier MLLMs show a substantial gap compared to human performance across all three cognitive complexity levels on GameplayQA. Models frequently fail at temporal and cross-video grounding, agent-role attribution, and handling the high decision density of gameplay. While exact model accuracy numbers are not fully detailed in the abstract, the benchmark reveals that current state-of-the-art MLLMs struggle significantly on agentic first-person multi-video tasks, with human performance representing a considerably higher ceiling than any evaluated model achieves.
🏁 结论 GameplayQA建立了一个严格且诊断能力丰富的基准，揭示了前沿多模态大语言模型在3D多智能体环境中的智能体感知能力与人类水平之间的显著差距，为具身AI、时序推理和世界建模领域的进步提供了结构化评估工具。 GameplayQA establishes a rigorous, diagnostically rich benchmark revealing that frontier MLLMs fall significantly short of human-level agentic perception in 3D multi-agent environments. The framework provides the community with structured tools to measure and drive progress in embodied AI, temporal reasoning, and world modeling.

链接		 arXiv: 2603.24329v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者录制了多人3D游戏（第一人称视角）并以每秒1.22个标签的密度进行时间同步的密集标注。标注采用"自身/其他智能体/世界"三元结构。基于这些标注，构建了2400个诊断性QA对，分为三个认知复杂度层次。设计了结构化干扰项分类体系，使错误选项与已知失败模式一一对应，从而实现对模型错误的精准溯源。最终用前沿多模态大语言模型在该基准上进行评估，并与人类表现进行对比。 The authors built GameplayQA by recording multiplayer 3D gameplay (first-person perspective) and densely annotating videos with time-synced captions at 1.22 labels/second. Annotations follow a triadic structure covering the agent itself, other agents, and world events. From these rich annotations, 2,400 diagnostic QA pairs were constructed across three cognitive complexity levels. A structured distractor taxonomy was designed so that wrong answer choices correspond to specific, known failure modes, allowing precise analysis of where and how models go wrong. Finally, frontier multimodal LLMs were evaluated on this benchmark and compared against human performance.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Annotation Density} = 1.22 \text{ labels/second}\]
💡 视频标注密度：每秒平均产生1.22个标注标签，反映了对游戏快速事件的高频覆盖能力。 The annotation density of the benchmark: 1.22 labels are assigned per second of video, capturing the rapid, decision-dense nature of 3D multiplayer gameplay.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着AI智能体被部署到机器人、虚拟世界等越来越复杂的3D环境中，第一视角多智能体感知成为亟待突破的核心能力。GameplayQA为研究社区提供了一个原则性、细粒度的诊断工具，能精准定位当前MLLMs的失败点，有力推动具身智能和智能体感知的定向改进。 As AI agents are deployed in increasingly complex 3D environments — from robotics to autonomous virtual agents — robust first-person, multi-agent perception is a critical unsolved capability. GameplayQA provides the research community with a principled, fine-grained diagnostic tool to measure exactly where current MLLMs fail, accelerating targeted improvements in embodied and agentic AI.

Repurposing Geometric Foundation Models for Multi-view Diffusion 将几何基础模型重用于多视图扩散

🧑‍🔬 Wooseok Jang, Seonghu Jeon, Jisang Han, Jinhyeok Choi et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 用几何基础模型特征空间替代VAE隐空间，实现一致多视图扩散生成
Repurpose geometric foundation model features as a latent space for geometrically consistent multi-view diffusion.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 新视角合成（NVS）需要从不同相机角度生成几何一致的图像。现有扩散模型大多使用为单图生成设计的VAE隐空间，缺乏对3D几何和跨视图关系的内在理解。这种不匹配使模型难以产生空间连贯、几何一致的多视图输出。 Novel view synthesis (NVS) requires generating images of an object from new camera angles that are geometrically consistent with each other. Most existing diffusion-based approaches use a standard VAE latent space that was designed for single-image generation and has no built-in understanding of 3D geometry or cross-view relationships. This mismatch makes it harder for the model to produce coherent 3D-consistent outputs.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 核心创新：将DUSt3R/MASt3R等几何基础模型的特征空间直接作为扩散模型的隐空间，几何一致性"天然内建"而非额外学习。 • 无需在扩散模型中引入额外几何监督信号，设计简洁优雅，训练效率高。 • 相比VAE隐空间训练速度提升4.4倍以上，结构化特征空间显著降低学习难度。 • 无需大规模文本-图像预训练，从零训练即可媲美依赖大规模预训练的SOTA方法。 • Key insight: geometric foundation models (e.g., DUSt3R/MASt3R) already encode strong cross-view correspondences in their feature space — repurposing this as the diffusion latent space is novel and elegant. • GLD's latent space is geometry-aware by design, not by training, eliminating the need for expensive geometric supervision in the diffusion model itself. • Achieves 4.4× training speedup over VAE-based baselines, likely due to the more structured and compact geometric feature space. • Competitive with state-of-the-art methods that rely on large-scale text-to-image pretraining, despite training from scratch.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在标准多视图/NVS基准数据集上评估GLD，与VAE隐空间、RAE隐空间的扩散基线以及依赖大规模文本-图像预训练的SOTA方法进行比较。评估指标包括2D图像质量指标（如PSNR、SSIM、LPIPS）和3D一致性指标。同时测量了训练效率。GLD的扩散模型从零训练，不使用任何生成式预训练。 The authors evaluated GLD on standard multi-view/NVS benchmarks, comparing against VAE-based and RAE-based latent diffusion baselines, as well as state-of-the-art NVS methods that use large-scale text-to-image pretraining. Evaluation metrics included 2D image quality metrics (e.g., PSNR, SSIM, LPIPS) and 3D consistency metrics. Training efficiency was also measured. The diffusion model in GLD was trained from scratch without any generative pretraining.
📊 实验
结果		 GLD在2D图像质量指标（PSNR、SSIM、LPIPS）和3D一致性指标上均优于VAE和RAE隐空间基线。相比VAE隐空间基线，训练速度提升超过4.4倍。尽管扩散模型从零训练、未使用大规模文本-图像预训练，GLD仍能与依赖大规模预训练的SOTA方法保持竞争力，在大幅降低训练成本的同时保持了顶尖性能。 GLD outperforms both VAE and RAE latent spaces on 2D image quality metrics (PSNR, SSIM, LPIPS) and 3D consistency metrics. Training is accelerated by more than 4.4× compared to the VAE latent space baseline. Despite training its diffusion model from scratch without large-scale text-to-image pretraining, GLD remains competitive with state-of-the-art methods that leverage such pretraining — a significant result given the substantially lower training cost.
🏁 结论 GLD证明了预训练几何基础模型的特征空间是多视图扩散的优质隐空间，在图像质量、3D一致性和训练效率上全面超越VAE/RAE隐空间。本文为新视角合成开辟了一条无需大规模生成预训练、直接借助几何先验的新范式。 GLD proves that the feature space of pretrained geometric foundation models constitutes a superior latent space for multi-view diffusion, outperforming conventional VAE/RAE latent spaces in both quality and training efficiency. This work establishes a new paradigm for NVS that leverages geometric priors without requiring large-scale generative pretraining.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

GLD用预训练几何基础模型（如DUSt3R/MASt3R）的内部特征表示替代标准VAE隐空间。由于几何基础模型在训练时就需要跨视图推理3D结构，其特征天然编码了跨视图几何对应关系。在这些特征之上训练一个解码器以重建高保真RGB图像。多视图扩散模型完全在这个几何特征空间中运行，学习去噪并生成几何一致的新视角图像，无需在扩散训练阶段引入额外的3D监督信号。 GLD replaces the standard VAE latent space used in diffusion models with the internal feature representations of a pretrained geometric foundation model (such as DUSt3R or MASt3R). These features naturally encode cross-view geometric correspondences because the foundation model was trained to reason about 3D structure across multiple views. A decoder is trained on top of these features to reconstruct high-fidelity RGB images. The multi-view diffusion model then operates entirely within this geometric feature space, learning to denoise and generate geometrically consistent novel views without needing explicit 3D supervision during diffusion training.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathbf{z} = f_{\text{geo}}(\mathbf{x})\]
💡 将输入图像 x 通过几何基础模型编码器映射为几何感知特征 z，作为扩散模型的隐空间表示，替代传统VAE编码 Input image x is encoded into geometry-aware features z by the geometric foundation model encoder, replacing the traditional VAE encoding as the latent space for diffusion.
\[\hat{\mathbf{x}} = D(\mathbf{z})\]
💡 解码器 D 将几何特征 z 重建为高保真RGB图像，确保几何隐空间可被用于图像生成 Decoder D reconstructs high-fidelity RGB images from geometric features z, ensuring the geometric latent space supports image generation.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本文挑战了"VAE隐空间是所有生成任务最优选择"的默认假设，证明针对任务的几何先验能够同时大幅提升质量和效率。这为机器人、AR/VR和自动驾驶等需要可靠3D场景理解的应用提供了一条无需昂贵大规模预训练即可实现高质量3D一致生成的实用路径。 This paper challenges the default assumption that VAE latent spaces are optimal for all generative tasks, showing that task-specific geometric priors can dramatically improve both quality and efficiency. It opens a practical path to high-quality 3D-consistent generation without the prohibitive cost of large-scale pretraining, which is especially relevant for robotics, AR/VR, and autonomous driving applications that need reliable 3D scene understanding.

AVO: Agentic Variation Operators for Autonomous Evolutionary Search AVO：用于自主进化搜索的智能体变异算子

🧑‍🔬 Terry Chen, Zhifan Ye, Bing Xu, Zihao Ye et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 用LLM自主智能体替代进化算法的固定变异算子，自动发现更快的GPU内核
Autonomous LLM agents replace fixed mutation operators in evolutionary search to discover faster GPU kernels.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 传统进化搜索依赖人工设计的变异和交叉算子，灵活性差、依赖领域专家知识。之前把大语言模型嵌入进化流程的做法，只是把LLM当作候选方案生成器，无法深度推理或自我纠错。在GPU内核优化这类高度依赖微架构知识的任务上，这种局限性尤为突出。 Classical evolutionary search relies on hand-crafted mutation and crossover operators that are rigid and domain-specific, limiting the depth and creativity of code optimization. Prior LLM-in-the-loop approaches treat language models merely as candidate generators within a fixed pipeline, preventing them from reasoning deeply or self-correcting. This bottleneck is especially painful for GPU kernel optimization, where micro-architectural tuning demands expert-level insight.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • AVO将LLM从被动的候选生成器升级为完整的自主变异算子，能够在自导循环中规划、批判、修复和验证自身的代码编辑。 • 智能体可查阅当前代码谱系、领域知识库和实时执行反馈，实现远超单次生成的迭代推理能力。 • 发现的优化可跨注意力变体迁移（如MHA→GQA），仅需30分钟额外搜索，展现出强大的泛化能力。 • AVO elevates the LLM from a passive candidate generator to a full autonomous variation operator — an agent that plans, critiques, repairs, and verifies its own edits in a self-directed loop. • The agent can consult the current code lineage, a domain-specific knowledge base, and live execution feedback, enabling iterative reasoning far beyond single-shot generation. • Discovered optimizations transfer across attention variants (e.g., MHA → GQA) with minimal additional search time (30 minutes), demonstrating strong generalization.
🧪 实验
内容		 AVO在NVIDIA Blackwell（B200）GPU上对注意力内核进行优化评估，这是AI领域竞争最激烈的优化目标之一。基线对比方法为cuDNN和FlashAttention-4，均为业界顶尖的专家手工实现。主要测试对象为多头注意力（MHA），进行了连续7天的搜索；同时将发现的优化迁移到分组查询注意力（GQA），仅用30分钟额外适配。评估指标为多种序列长度和批大小配置下的内核执行速度。 AVO was evaluated on GPU attention kernel optimization — one of the most heavily optimized targets in AI — running on NVIDIA Blackwell (B200) GPUs. The baselines compared were cuDNN and FlashAttention-4, both state-of-the-art expert-engineered implementations. Two attention variants were tested: multi-head attention (MHA) as the primary target with 7 days of continuous search, and grouped-query attention (GQA) as a transfer target with only 30 minutes of additional adaptation. Performance was measured as kernel execution speed across multiple sequence length and batch size configurations.
📊 实验
结果		 在多头注意力（MHA）上经过7天搜索：AVO在各测试配置下比cuDNN最高提升3.5%，比FlashAttention-4最高提升10.5%。在分组查询注意力（GQA）上仅经30分钟迁移适配：比cuDNN最高提升7.0%，比FlashAttention-4最高提升9.3%。所有结果均在NVIDIA B200 GPU上取得，超越了高度优化的专家级库实现。 On multi-head attention (MHA) after 7 days of search: AVO outperforms cuDNN by up to 3.5% and FlashAttention-4 by up to 10.5% across evaluated configurations. On grouped-query attention (GQA) after 30 minutes of transfer adaptation: AVO achieves gains of up to 7.0% over cuDNN and up to 9.3% over FlashAttention-4. All results are on NVIDIA B200 GPUs, surpassing both highly-tuned library implementations.
🏁 结论 AVO证明，将LLM智能体从简单的候选生成器提升为完整的自主变异算子，能够让进化搜索发现超越顶尖专家手工实现的GPU内核。这确立了"智能体变异"作为自主性能工程的全新强大范式。 AVO demonstrates that elevating LLM agents from mere candidate generators to full autonomous variation operators enables evolutionary search to discover GPU kernels that surpass state-of-the-art expert-engineered implementations. This establishes agentic variation as a powerful new paradigm for autonomous performance engineering.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

AVO用自导型LLM智能体循环替代进化算法中的固定变异算子。在每个进化步骤中，智能体读取当前代码种群/谱系，查询领域知识库，提出代码修改方案，执行后获取运行时反馈，并迭代地批评和修复自己的提案，直到产生经验证的改进候选方案。该系统在NVIDIA Blackwell（B200）GPU上持续自主运行，以多天为搜索周期，专注于注意力内核优化任务。 AVO replaces the fixed variation operators in evolutionary algorithms with a self-directed LLM agent loop. At each evolutionary step, instead of applying a preset mutation rule, the agent reads the current code population/lineage, queries a domain-specific knowledge base, proposes code edits, executes them, receives runtime feedback, and iteratively critiques and repairs its proposals. This agentic loop continues until a verified, improved candidate is produced. The system runs continuously and autonomously on NVIDIA Blackwell (B200) GPUs, targeting attention kernel optimization over multi-day search horizons.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{AVO: } x_{t+1} = \text{AgentLoop}(x_t, \mathcal{L}_t, \mathcal{K}, f_{\text{exec}})\]
💡 AVO的核心思想：下一代候选方案 x_{t+1} 由智能体循环生成，输入为当前候选 x_t、代码谱系 L_t、领域知识库 K 以及执行反馈函数 f_exec，而非固定的变异规则。 AVO's core idea: the next candidate x_{t+1} is produced by an agent loop that takes the current candidate, the code lineage, a knowledge base, and execution feedback — replacing any fixed mutation rule.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

GPU内核正日益成为AI训练和推理的关键瓶颈，自动发现超越人类专家水平的微架构优化是重大开放挑战。AVO表明，自主智能体能够弥合乃至超越多年专家工程积累的性能差距，预示着AI硬件优化本身将大幅走向自动化的未来。 As GPU kernels become increasingly critical bottlenecks in AI training and inference, automated discovery of micro-architectural optimizations beyond what human experts can craft is a major open challenge. AVO shows that autonomous agents can close — and even surpass — the performance gap with years of expert engineering, pointing toward a future where AI hardware optimization is itself largely automated.

Claudini: Autoresearch Discovers State-of-the-Art Adversarial Attack Algorithms for LLMs Claudini：自动化科研发现大语言模型最先进对抗攻击算法

🧑‍🔬 Alexander Panfilov, Peter Romov, Igor Shilov, Yves-Alexandre de Montjoye et al.

🎯 LLM智能体自动发现超越现有所有对抗攻击算法的越狱方法
An LLM agent autonomously discovers novel jailbreak algorithms that beat all 30+ existing methods.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 如何让大语言模型（LLM）产生有害输出（"越狱"）是一个重要的安全挑战。目前，设计有效的对抗攻击算法需要大量人工专业知识和反复实验。本文探讨这一研究过程本身能否被自动化。 Jailbreaking LLMs — tricking them into producing harmful outputs — is an active security challenge.
