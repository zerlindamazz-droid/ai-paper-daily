Vero: An Open RL Recipe for General Visual Reasoning Vero：面向通用视觉推理的开放强化学习方案

🧑‍🔬 Gabriel Sarch, Linrong Cai, Qunzhong Wang, Haoyang Wu et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 首个开放式RL训练方案，600K多样视觉数据超越闭源模型
A fully open RL recipe with 600K diverse visual data beats proprietary VLMs across 30 benchmarks.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当前最强的视觉语言模型（VLM）能处理图表理解、科学推理、空间推理等多种任务，但其背后的训练方案是闭源的。研究人员无法复现或改进这些系统，因为数据、奖励设计和强化学习流水线都不对外公开。目前缺乏一个覆盖多样视觉推理任务的强力开放基准。 The best vision-language models (VLMs) can handle a wide range of visual reasoning tasks — from reading charts to spatial reasoning — but the training recipes behind them are proprietary and not publicly available. Researchers and practitioners cannot reproduce or build upon these systems because the data, reward design, and RL pipelines are locked away. There is no strong open baseline that covers diverse visual reasoning at scale.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • Vero-600K：从59个公开数据集聚合而成的60万样本RL训练集，覆盖6大视觉任务类别（图表、科学、空间、数学、OCR、开放任务），是目前最多样的开放视觉RL数据集。 • 任务路由奖励：动态将不同答案格式（选择题、数值、自由文本）路由到对应验证器，使单一RL流程能跨任务类型统一训练。 • VeroEval：全新的30个挑战性基准评测套件，专为衡量广泛视觉推理能力而设计，为社区提供严格可复现的评估标准。 • 广泛覆盖是关键：消融实验证明，数据多样性——而非某单一任务类别——才是强化学习扩展性能的核心驱动力。 • Vero-600K: A 600K-sample RL training dataset aggregated from 59 public datasets across 6 broad visual task categories (charts, science, spatial, math, OCR, open-ended), making it the most diverse open visual RL dataset to date. • Task-Routed Rewards: A reward design that dynamically routes heterogeneous answer formats (multiple choice, numeric, free-form) to appropriate verifiers, enabling a single RL pipeline to train across wildly different task types. • VeroEval: A new evaluation suite of 30 challenging benchmarks specifically designed to measure broad visual reasoning, providing a rigorous and reproducible standard for the community. • Broad Coverage as the Key Driver: Ablations demonstrate that diverse data coverage — not any single task category — is the primary factor enabling strong RL scaling, with isolated task categories transferring poorly to others.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在四个不同基础VLM（主要为Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct）上使用Vero-600K数据集和RL流水线进行训练。在VeroEval（30个涵盖图表、科学、数学、空间、OCR和开放任务的挑战性基准）上评测。基线包括强力开放权重模型和闭源增强思维模型（如Qwen3-VL-8B-Thinking）。消融实验对比Vero-600K与现有开放RL数据集，并系统测试不同任务类别子集对性能的影响。 The authors train Vero on four different base VLMs (including Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct as the primary model) using their Vero-600K dataset and RL pipeline. They evaluate on VeroEval, a newly constructed suite of 30 challenging benchmarks spanning charts, science, math, spatial, OCR, and open-ended tasks. Baselines include strong open-weight models and proprietary thinking-augmented models such as Qwen3-VL-8B-Thinking. Ablation studies compare Vero-600K against existing open RL datasets, and systematically test training with subsets of task categories to understand data composition effects.
📊 实验
结果		 Vero在VeroEval的30个基准上，相比四个基础模型平均提升3.6–5.3分。以Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct为起点，Vero在30个基准中的23个上超越闭源的Qwen3-VL-8B-Thinking模型，且未使用任何专有思维链数据。在相同基础模型下，Vero-600K在各任务类别上均优于所有现有开放RL数据集。消融实验显示，移除任意单一任务类别均导致可观的性能下降，而孤立任务类别训练迁移性差，证实广泛数据覆盖是扩展性能的主要驱动力。 Vero improves over four base models by an average of 3.6–5.3 points across VeroEval's 30 benchmarks. Starting from Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct, Vero outperforms the proprietary Qwen3-VL-8B-Thinking model on 23 out of 30 benchmarks — without using any proprietary thinking data. When trained from the same base model, Vero-600K surpasses all existing open RL datasets across task categories in head-to-head comparisons. Ablations show that removing any single task category causes measurable drops, while training on isolated categories transfers poorly to others, confirming broad data coverage as the primary scaling driver.
🏁 结论 Vero证明，一套完全开放的RL方案——结合涵盖六大视觉任务类别的60万规模多样数据与任务路由奖励——能够在广泛视觉推理上达到或超越闭源VLM。核心发现是：数据多样性而非特定任务设计，才是视觉推理RL扩展的主要驱动力。 Vero demonstrates that a fully open RL recipe — combining diverse 600K-scale data across six visual task categories with task-routed rewards — can match or exceed proprietary VLMs on broad visual reasoning. The key finding is that data diversity, not task-specific design, is the primary driver of strong RL scaling for visual reasoning.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Vero在预训练VLM基础上，使用GRPO风格的策略优化进行强化学习训练，核心训练数据为Vero-600K。该数据集从59个公开数据集中精心整理，覆盖六大视觉任务类别，并经过去重和质量过滤。Vero采用任务路由奖励机制：对每条训练样本，自动检测答案格式并路由到对应验证器（如选择题用精确匹配，数值题用容差比较，开放题用LLM裁判）。这使得RL信号在异质任务间保持准确有效。最终模型在VeroEval的30个基准上评测，并通过系统消融实验分析各类别数据的贡献。 Vero applies reinforcement learning (specifically GRPO-style policy optimization) on top of pre-trained VLMs using a large and diverse dataset called Vero-600K. This dataset is curated from 59 existing public datasets spanning six visual task categories, with careful deduplication and quality filtering. Instead of a single monolithic reward function, Vero uses task-routed rewards: for each training sample, the answer format is detected and routed to an appropriate verifier (e.g., exact match for multiple choice, tolerance-based numeric comparison, or LLM-judged free-form evaluation). This allows the RL signal to remain accurate and meaningful across heterogeneous tasks. The resulting models are evaluated on VeroEval, a new 30-benchmark suite, and ablations systematically vary data composition to understand which categories contribute most to generalization.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r(y, y^*) = \begin{cases} r_{\text{exact}}(y, y^*) & \text{if format} = \text{MCQ} \\ r_{\text{numeric}}(y, y^*) & \text{if format} = \text{numeric} \\ r_{\text{LLM}}(y, y^*) & \text{if format} = \text{free-form} \end{cases}\]
💡 任务路由奖励函数：根据答案格式将样本路由到不同的验证器（选择题用精确匹配，数值题用容差比较，开放题用LLM裁判），以在异质任务间提供准确的RL信号。 Task-routed reward function: routes each training sample to a different verifier based on its answer format — exact match for multiple choice, tolerance-based comparison for numeric answers, and an LLM judge for free-form responses — ensuring accurate RL signals across heterogeneous tasks.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

Vero完全开源数据、代码和模型，打破了视觉强化学习领域长期存在的可复现性壁垒，让更多研究者能平等获取顶尖训练方案。其"广泛数据覆盖是RL扩展核心驱动力"的发现，为未来多模态推理系统提供了清晰且可操作的设计原则。 By fully open-sourcing the data, code, and models, Vero democratizes access to state-of-the-art visual RL training and removes a major reproducibility barrier in the field. Its finding that broad data coverage — not clever reward engineering alone — drives RL scaling provides a clear and actionable design principle for future multimodal reasoning systems.

TriAttention: Efficient Long Reasoning with Trigonometric KV Compression TriAttention：基于三角函数KV压缩的高效长推理方法

🧑‍🔬 Weian Mao, Xi Lin, Wei Huang, Yuxin Xie et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 利用RoPE前空间几何特性压缩KV缓存，实现10倍显存节省同等精度。
TriAttention compresses KV cache using pre-RoPE geometry, achieving full accuracy at 10x memory reduction.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当大语言模型进行长链推理（比如解复杂数学题）时，KV缓存会急剧膨胀，导致显存不足。现有压缩方法用最近的查询向量（经过RoPE旋转后）来估计哪些Key重要，但由于RoPE会随位置旋转查询向量，代理查询不可靠，导致错误的Key选择和不稳定的推理结果。 When LLMs perform extended reasoning (e.g., solving hard math problems with long chain-of-thought), the KV cache grows very large and causes memory bottlenecks. Existing compression methods estimate which keys are important by using recent post-RoPE queries as proxies, but because RoPE rotates query vectors with their positions, these proxies are unreliable and lead to wrong key selections and unstable reasoning quality.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 发现"Q/K集中性"现象：在RoPE变换前的空间中，Q和K向量围绕固定非零中心聚集，且跨位置保持稳定，为可靠的重要性估计提供基础。 • 三角级数洞察：这些稳定中心决定了模型对特定相对距离的偏好规律，可通过三角函数级数精确刻画。 • TriAttention仅凭Key的位置（距离偏好）和Q/K范数即可打分，无需对大量查询计算注意力分数，计算开销极低。 • Novel observation of "Q/K concentration": in pre-RoPE space, Q and K vectors cluster around stable, non-zero centers regardless of position, enabling reliable importance estimation. • Trigonometric series insight: the stable centers govern a distance-preference pattern — the model systematically prefers keys at certain relative distances, predictable via a trigonometric decomposition. • TriAttention scores key importance purely from their positions (via distance preference) plus Q/K norms, requiring no expensive attention score computation over many queries.
🧪 实验
内容		 主要基准测试为AIME 2025（高难度数学竞赛数据集），测试生成长达32K token的推理链时的表现。基线方法包括Full Attention和当前最优的KV缓存压缩方法（如SnapKV、PyramidKV等）。评估指标涵盖推理准确率（AIME题目通过率）、推理吞吐量（token/秒）和KV缓存显存占用。此外还在单张消费级GPU上部署OpenClaw长文本推理模型，验证实际可行性。 The primary benchmark is AIME 2025 (a hard math competition dataset), testing long-context reasoning with up to 32K tokens of generated chain-of-thought. Baselines include Full Attention and leading KV cache compression methods (e.g., SnapKV, PyramidKV, and other recent approaches). Evaluation metrics are reasoning accuracy (pass rate on AIME problems), inference throughput (tokens/second), and KV memory usage. The method is also deployed on a real consumer GPU (single GPU setup) to test practical feasibility with OpenClaw, a long-context reasoning model.
📊 实验
结果		 在AIME 2025（32K token生成）测试中：TriAttention在保持与Full Attention相同准确率的前提下，实现2.5倍推理吞吐量提升或10.7倍KV缓存显存压缩。同等效率下，其他主流压缩基线仅能达到Full Attention约50%的准确率。TriAttention使OpenClaw模型得以在单张消费级GPU上运行，而Full Attention在同等长文本设置下会直接显存溢出。 On AIME 2025 with 32K-token generation: TriAttention matches Full Attention accuracy while achieving 2.5× higher throughput or 10.7× KV memory reduction. Leading baseline compression methods achieve only ~50% of Full Attention's accuracy at the same efficiency level. TriAttention enables OpenClaw deployment on a single consumer GPU, whereas Full Attention causes out-of-memory under the same long-context setting.
🏁 结论 TriAttention证明了RoPE前空间中Q/K的集中性为长推理LLM的KV缓存压缩提供了原理清晰、稳定高效的信号，在准确率和效率上大幅超越现有方法。本文为基于Transformer注意力几何结构的无损KV压缩建立了新范式。 TriAttention proves that pre-RoPE Q/K concentration provides a principled, stable, and highly effective signal for KV cache compression in long reasoning LLMs, dramatically outperforming prior methods in both accuracy and efficiency. This work establishes a new paradigm for lossless-quality KV compression grounded in the geometric structure of transformer attention.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者首先发现：在RoPE变换之前的空间中，Q和K向量在各层各注意力头中都紧密聚集在固定的、与位置无关的中心附近。他们通过数学推导证明，这种集中性使得注意力天然偏向特定相对距离的Key，且这种偏好可用三角函数级数表达。TriAttention利用这些学习到的中心，为每个Key计算基于位置的重要性分数，判断模型可能关注哪些Key。再结合Q/K向量范数作为补充信号，最终只保留评分最高的Key，大幅削减KV缓存显存占用，同时保持推理精度。 The authors first identify that in the pre-RoPE space, both Q and K vectors are tightly clustered around fixed, position-independent centers across layers and heads. They mathematically derive that this concentration causes attention to prefer keys at specific relative distances from the query, expressible as a trigonometric series over those distances. TriAttention uses these learned centers to compute a position-based importance score for each key, capturing which keys the model will likely attend to by distance alone. It further combines Q/K vector norms as a complementary importance signal, then retains only the top-ranked keys in the KV cache, dramatically reducing memory while preserving reasoning fidelity.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{score}(k_i) = \sum_{n} a_n \cos(n \cdot \Delta p_i) + b_n \sin(n \cdot \Delta p_i)\]
💡 基于三角级数的Key重要性评分公式：利用Query与Key之间的相对位置距离Δp，通过三角函数级数（由Q/K中心向量决定的系数a_n, b_n）计算每个Key被关注的可能性。 Key importance score via trigonometric series: the relative position distance Δp between query and key is decomposed into a trigonometric series, where coefficients a_n, b_n are determined by the pre-RoPE Q/K centers, capturing the model's distance preference.
\[\bar{q} = \mathbb{E}[q], \quad \bar{k} = \mathbb{E}[k]\]
💡 Q/K集中性的核心：Q和K向量的均值（中心）在预RoPE空间中跨位置保持稳定非零，是整个方法的理论基础。 Q/K concentration: the mean (center) vectors of Q and K in pre-RoPE space are stable and non-zero across positions, forming the theoretical foundation of TriAttention.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着LLM被广泛用于数学、编程等复杂多步推理任务，KV缓存显存成为制约上下文长度和硬件门槛的核心瓶颈——TriAttention在不损失精度的前提下实现10倍压缩，直接突破了这一障碍。本文揭示的RoPE注意力中Q/K集中性这一基本几何特性，有望启发超越KV缓存的新一代高效注意力研究。 As LLMs are increasingly deployed for complex, multi-step reasoning tasks (math, coding, science), KV cache memory is a critical bottleneck that limits either context length or accessible hardware — TriAttention's 10× compression without accuracy loss directly addresses this barrier. This work also reveals a fundamental geometric property of RoPE-based attention (Q/K concentration) that could inspire a new line of efficient attention research beyond just KV caching.

SkillX: Automatically Constructing Skill Knowledge Bases for Agents SkillX：为智能体自动构建技能知识库

🧑‍🔬 Chenxi Wang, Zhuoyun Yu, Xin Xie, Wuguannan Yao et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 自动构建可复用的层次化技能知识库，提升弱智能体跨任务表现
SkillX automatically builds a reusable, hierarchical skill knowledge base that boosts weaker LLM agents across diverse tasks.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 目前LLM智能体各自为战，每个智能体都需要从零开始重新摸索行为，导致大量重复探索和泛化性差的问题。没有高效的机制来跨智能体和环境地捕获、整理和共享经验，这使得构建有能力的智能体变得缓慢且代价高昂。 LLM agents today learn in isolation — each agent rediscovers behaviors from scratch, leading to redundant exploration and poor generalization. There is no efficient mechanism to capture, organize, and share experience across agents and environments. This bottleneck makes scaling capable agents slow and costly.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 多层级技能设计：将原始轨迹提炼为三层层次结构——战略计划、功能性技能和原子技能，实现高层推理与底层执行的双重复用。 • 迭代式技能精炼：基于真实执行反馈自动修正技能，无需人工干预即可持续提升知识库质量。 • 探索性技能扩展：系统主动生成并验证种子数据之外的新技能，扩大覆盖范围，避免知识盲区。 • Multi-Level Skills Design: raw trajectories are distilled into a three-tier hierarchy — strategic plans, functional skills, and atomic skills — enabling both high-level reasoning and low-level execution reuse. • Iterative Skills Refinement: skills are automatically revised based on real execution feedback, creating a self-improving quality loop without human intervention. • Exploratory Skills Expansion: the system proactively generates and validates novel skills beyond the seed training data, increasing coverage and preventing knowledge gaps.

内容		 实验在三个具有挑战性的长时程和用户交互基准上进行：AppWorld（基于应用的任务自动化）、BFCL-v3（函数调用与工具使用）和τ²-Bench（多轮用户交互）。由GLM-4.6构建的SkillKB被插入多个较弱的基础智能体以评估可迁移性。基线为不使用技能库的相同基础智能体，评估指标包括任务成功率和执行效率。 Experiments are conducted on three challenging long-horizon and user-interactive benchmarks: AppWorld (app-based task automation), BFCL-v3 (function-calling and tool-use), and τ²-Bench (multi-turn user interaction). The SkillKB built by GLM-4.6 is plugged into several weaker base agents to evaluate transferability. Baselines include the same base agents without the skill library, and comparisons are made on task success rate and execution efficiency metrics.
📊 实验
结果		 在AppWorld、BFCL-v3和τ²-Bench三个基准上，将SkillKB插入较弱基础智能体后，任务成功率和执行效率均持续提升。由GLM-4.6构建的技能库可正迁移至能力弱于构建者的智能体，展示了跨智能体泛化能力。摘要中未完整披露具体数字，但论文强调在所有评估设置中成功率和步骤效率均有一致提升。 SkillKB consistently improves task success rate and execution efficiency when plugged into weaker base agents across all three benchmarks (AppWorld, BFCL-v3, τ²-Bench). The skill library built by GLM-4.6 transfers positively to agents that are weaker than the builder, demonstrating cross-agent generalization. Specific numeric gains are not fully reported in the abstract, but the paper emphasizes consistent improvements in both success rate and step efficiency across all evaluated settings.
🏁 结论 SkillX证明了由强智能体自动构建的层次化技能知识库可被较弱智能体复用，从而在任务成功率和执行效率上取得一致性提升。这验证了结构化、可共享的经验表示是实现可泛化LLM智能体学习的可扩展路径。 SkillX demonstrates that a fully automated, hierarchically structured skill knowledge base can be built by a strong agent and reused by weaker agents to achieve consistent gains in task success and efficiency. This validates that structured, sharable experience representations are a scalable path toward generalizable LLM agent learning.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

SkillX是一个全自动流水线，首先从强骨干智能体（GLM-4.6）收集轨迹，并将其提炼为三层技能层次：原子技能（基础动作）、功能性技能（可组合的子任务例程）和战略计划（高层任务蓝图）。迭代精炼循环会重新执行技能并根据成功/失败反馈进行更新。探索性扩展模块随后生成假设性新技能场景，执行后将验证通过的技能加入库中。最终生成的即插即用SkillKB可在推理时注入任何较弱的基础智能体，无需重新训练。 SkillX is a fully automated pipeline that first collects trajectories from a strong backbone agent (GLM-4.6) and distills them into a three-tiered skill hierarchy: atomic skills (primitive actions), functional skills (composable sub-task routines), and strategic plans (high-level task blueprints). An iterative refinement loop re-executes skills and updates them based on success/failure feedback. An exploratory expansion module then generates hypothetical new skill scenarios, executes them, and adds validated ones to the library. The resulting plug-and-play SkillKB can be injected into any weaker base agent at inference time without retraining.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{SkillKB} = \{\mathcal{S}_{\text{atomic}}, \mathcal{S}_{\text{functional}}, \mathcal{S}_{\text{strategic}}\}\]
💡 技能知识库由三层技能组成：原子技能（基础动作）、功能性技能（子任务例程）和战略计划（高层蓝图），形成多级技能层次结构。 The skill knowledge base is composed of three tiers: atomic skills (primitive actions), functional skills (composable sub-task routines), and strategic plans (high-level task blueprints), forming a multi-level skill hierarchy.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

本文将神经网络参数建模为均场体制下的概率测度，通过将标准欧氏代价替换为正半定矩阵上某个范数（如Schatten-p范数）所导出的代价，构造谱Wasserstein距离族。对静态Kantorovich公式和动态Benamou-Brenier公式分别建立理论，并证明在单调范数下二者等价。对高斯边际，问题化归为协方差矩阵上的约束优化，推广经典Bures度量并对可交换协方差给出闭合解。对能量泛函求谱Wasserstein梯度流，则恢复归一化矩阵更新规则（含Muon）作为精确有限粒子极限。 The paper models neural network parameters as probability measures in a mean-field regime, then constructs a family of Spectral Wasserstein distances by replacing the standard Euclidean cost with a cost derived from a norm on positive semidefinite matrices (e.g., Schatten-p norms). Static Kantorovich and dynamic Benamou-Brenier formulations are developed and shown to be equivalent for monotone norms. For Gaussian marginals, the problem reduces to constrained optimization over covariance matrices, generalizing the classical Bures metric and yielding closed-form solutions for commuting covariances. The induced gradient flow of an energy functional over this geometry then recovers normalized matrix update rules, including Muon, as exact finite-particle limits.
