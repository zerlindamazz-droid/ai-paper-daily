VIMPO: Value-Implicit Policy Optimization for LLMs VIMPO：面向大语言模型的隐式价值策略优化

🧑‍🔬 Zhewei Kang, Aosong Feng, Sergey Levine, Dawn Song et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 无需评论网络，从策略本身推导出价值函数来提升LLM推理
A critic-free RL method that extracts value signals directly from the policy to sharpen LLM reasoning.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个学生做完一道大题后只知道"整体答错了"，却不知道哪一步出了问题——GRPO 就是这种粗糙的批改方式。而 VIMPO 就像一位细心的老师，能从学生解题过程本身推断出每一步的质量，给出逐步点评，帮学生更快找到错误所在——而且这位老师不需要额外配备"助教"。Imagine a teacher grading a math test by only saying "wrong" at the very end, giving the same mark to every step — that's how simpler RL methods work. VIMPO is like a clever teacher who can figure out which specific step went wrong just by reading how confidently the student wrote each line, without needing a separate teaching assistant to check every step.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 用强化学习训练AI做数学推理时，有两种主流方法：一种简单但"粗放"——对整段回答只给一个笼统的奖励分数；另一种更精细但需要额外训练一个"评委模型"，容易不稳定、难以维护。现有方法要么精度不够，要么太复杂，很难两全其美。 When training AI to reason through math problems using reinforcement learning, existing methods face a painful trade-off: simple methods (like GRPO) give the same reward score to every word in a response, which is a bit like grading every sentence of an essay the same regardless of quality. More precise methods need a separate "critic" model to score each step, but that critic is finicky and hard to train stably. Nobody had a clean way to get fine-grained credit assignment without the extra complexity.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 无需"评委模型"：VIMPO 直接从语言模型自身的输出概率中推导出一个隐式价值函数，省去了单独训练评论网络的麻烦。 • 精细信用分配：通过"策略暗含价值递推"，每一个生成的词都能获得更有针对性的学习信号，而不是整段话共享同一个奖励。 • 噪声鲁棒性强：在奖励信号不准确（有噪声）的情况下，VIMPO 仍能保持稳定优势，说明其信用分配机制更可靠。 • No separate critic needed: VIMPO derives a value function purely from the policy model's own output probabilities — no extra model, no extra instability. • Finer credit assignment: Each generated token gets a more meaningful learning signal, rather than every word in a response sharing one blunt reward score. • Robust to noisy rewards: Even when reward signals are imperfect or noisy, VIMPO consistently outperforms simpler baselines, showing its value signal is genuinely more informative.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在数学推理基准上评测，包括 MATH-500、AIME 2024、AIME 2025 和 OlympiadBench 四个数据集，涵盖从标准数学题到顶级竞赛题的不同难度层次。基线方法主要是 GRPO（一种主流的无评论网络强化学习方法）。此外，还专门设计了噪声奖励实验，验证方法在奖励信号不可靠时的鲁棒性。 The team tested VIMPO on four math reasoning benchmarks: MATH-500 (standard competition math), AIME 2024 and AIME 2025 (top-tier U.S. math olympiad problems), and OlympiadBench (international olympiad-level questions). The main baseline was GRPO, a widely-used critic-free RL method. They also ran a separate set of noisy-reward experiments, deliberately injecting incorrect reward signals to test which method holds up better under imperfect feedback.
📊 实验
结果		 VIMPO 在全部四个基准上均超越 GRPO，在竞赛级别题目上优势尤为明显：AIME 2024 和 AIME 2025 上的提升幅度显著大于 MATH-500，说明越难的题目受益越多。在噪声奖励实验中，VIMPO 相对 GRPO 保持了一致且稳定的性能领先，证明其隐式价值机制在信号不完整时依然有效。（具体绝对分数因模型和设置不同而异，论文报告了各基准的 pass@1 准确率对比。） VIMPO outperforms GRPO on all four benchmarks — MATH-500, AIME 2024, AIME 2025, and OlympiadBench — with the largest gains on competition-style problems (AIME and OlympiadBench), suggesting harder tasks benefit most from finer credit assignment. In noisy-reward settings, VIMPO consistently maintains its advantage over GRPO, demonstrating robustness. Concrete pass@1 accuracy improvements vary by model and benchmark, with competition benchmarks showing especially notable margins.
🏁 结论 VIMPO 证明了无需单独训练评论模型也能实现精细的信用分配：只需从策略模型自身的概率中推导出隐式价值函数，即可在数学推理任务上稳定超越现有简单方法，同时保持训练的简洁性。 VIMPO proves that fine-grained credit assignment in LLM reinforcement learning doesn't require a separate critic — by deriving an implicit value function directly from the policy itself, it consistently beats simpler baselines on math reasoning while keeping training clean and stable.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

VIMPO 的核心思路是：在 KL 正则化强化学习的数学框架下，最优策略自身其实已经"隐含"了一个价值函数——就像一位高手下棋，他每一步的落子概率本身就反映了他对局势好坏的判断。VIMPO 用当前模型与参考模型输出概率的对数比值，从后往前递推计算出每个位置的"隐式价值"，并用最终奖励作为锚点（因为句子结束时没有未来收益了）。训练时分两部分：一个价值损失（让推导出的价值与实际奖励对齐）和一个类 PPO 的演员更新（利用计算出的优势函数改进策略），两者完全依赖同一个模型，无需额外评委。 The key insight is mathematical: under a standard RL framework with a KL penalty, the optimal policy secretly encodes a value function inside its own probabilities. VIMPO "unlocks" this hidden value by computing log-ratios between the current model's output and a fixed reference model, then chaining these ratios backwards from the end of each response (where the reward is known) to estimate how valuable each token position was. This gives a value loss (to align the implied value with actual rewards) and an advantage estimate (to update the policy), all without ever training a separate critic model — just one model doing double duty.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

更好的信用分配意味着 AI 能更高效地从对错反馈中学习，未来的数学、编程、科学推理 AI 助手可以用更少的训练成本达到更高水平。这种方法的简洁性也意味着它更容易被工业界采用，推动 AI 推理能力的普及。 Better credit assignment means AI can learn more efficiently from right/wrong feedback, which directly improves AI tutors, coding assistants, and scientific reasoning tools — all while being simpler to train. This kind of clean, scalable method is the kind the industry actually adopts, so its real-world impact could be substantial.

的问题		 训练机器人"大脑"需要大量机器人操作数据，但这类数据收集成本极高、速度极慢、种类极少——就像你只能靠真实驾驶积累经验，却买不起车。研究者希望找到更便宜、更丰富的替代数据，让机器人学得更好、更快。 Training robot AI models requires massive amounts of real robot demonstration data — but collecting it is slow, expensive, and produces very limited variety of behaviors. ⚙️ Method · 方法详解

研究者把互联网上大量第一人称人类操作视频（比如做饭、整理物品时手部的视角）当作"预训练教材"。他们先用一套筛选流水线过滤掉质量差的片段，再自动为每段视频标注"动作信息"。然后用这些视频预训练一个具身基础模型，让它学会丰富的"世界常识"（物体如何移动、手怎么抓握等）。最后只用少量真实机器人数据做微调，帮助模型把人类动作"翻译"成机器人能执行的指令。 The team collected large amounts of first-person human video from the internet — think cooking or tidying-up clips filmed from a head-mounted camera. They built a filtering pipeline to remove low-quality footage and automatically attached action labels to each clip. This processed video was used to pretrain an embodied AI model, teaching it rich "world knowledge" about how objects behave and how hands interact with them. Finally, only a small batch of real robot data was used to fine-tune the model, bridging the gap between human and robot actions.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

机器人数据的高昂采集成本一直是具身AI规模化的最大瓶颈，本研究表明这一瓶颈可以用人类日常视频绕开。这意味着未来的家用机器人、工厂自动化机器人可以用互联网上海量的人类操作视频来"低成本"训练，大幅加速机器人智能的普及。 The high cost of collecting robot data has been the biggest bottleneck for scaling embodied AI — this work shows that bottleneck can be bypassed using everyday human video freely available online. This could dramatically speed up and cheapen the development of capable home robots, factory automation, and other embodied AI products.

Current World Models Lack a Persistent State Core 当前世界模型缺乏持久状态核心

🧑‍🔬 Jinpeng Lu, Dexu Zhu, Haoyuan Shi, Linghan Cai et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 新基准揭示：AI世界模型"看不见就忘记"，无法追踪镜头外的世界演变。
A new benchmark reveals that AI world models forget what happens off-screen, like a video game that resets when you look away.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy想象一个只记得当前画面的保安：摄像头一转开正在燃烧的蜡烛，他就忘了蜡烛的存在。等摄像头转回来，他把蜡烛恢复成原来的样子——一点都没燃烧过。现在所有AI世界模型都是这个保安：盯着屏幕很厉害，但一旦没人看，就完全不记得世界还在继续转动。Imagine a security guard who only remembers what's on screen right now — the moment a camera pans away from a burning candle, they forget the candle exists. When the camera swings back, they just show the candle exactly as it was before, not burned down at all. Today's AI world models are exactly that guard: great at watching, terrible at remembering that the world keeps going when no one's looking.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 AI"世界模型"试图模拟真实世界的运作方式，就像在脑海中预演接下来会发生什么。但现有模型有一个隐藏的大缺陷：它们只追踪镜头正在拍摄的内容。一旦摄像机转向别处，模型就会"忘记"之前发生的事，等镜头回来时只是恢复到原来的样子，而不是让事件继续发展。更糟的是，现有的评测标准根本不检查这个问题。 AI "world models" are systems that try to simulate how the real world works — like a mental movie of what happens next. But current ones have a big hidden flaw: they only keep track of things while the camera is watching. The moment you look away (like panning the camera elsewhere), the model forgets what was happening and just restores the scene to how it looked before, instead of letting events continue as they would in reality. Existing tests for these models never actually check for this problem.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首个专门测试AI世界模型能否追踪"镜头外事件"的基准——WRBench，填补了此前所有评测的空白。 • 巧妙地将摄像机运动作为"测试探针"：镜头转开再转回，检验世界是否正确地继续演变。 • 横跨23个模型、9600段视频，是迄今最大规模的此类缺陷研究。 • 证明这一缺陷与模型大小、图像质量、控制方式无关——这是根本性的设计盲点，不是靠堆参数能解决的。 • First benchmark (WRBench) that specifically tests whether AI world models can keep track of off-screen events — something no prior benchmark has measured. • Uses camera movement as a clever "test probe": pan away, then return, and check if the world moved on correctly. • Tested 23 different models across 9,600 videos, making it the most comprehensive study of this failure mode. • Shows this flaw persists regardless of model size, image quality, or control method — it's a fundamental design gap, not just a scaling problem.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究人员测试了23个AI视频生成和世界模型系统，涵盖四种控制范式（文本控制、动作控制、轨迹控制和混合控制）。他们生成了9600段视频，场景涵盖各种物理过程——物体坠落、液体倾倒、火焰燃烧等——这些场景的"正确续接"有明确预期。人类评估者为"镜头回归后场景应该是什么样"设定了基准答案，模型在摄像机执行、场景连续性以及最关键的"镜头外事件是否正确推进"三个维度上被打分。 The researchers tested 23 AI video generation and world model systems spanning four different control paradigms (text-based, action-based, trajectory-based, and hybrid controls). They generated 9,600 videos across a range of physical scenarios — things like objects falling, liquids pouring, or fires burning — where it's clear what "correct" off-screen continuation looks like. Human evaluators set the ground truth for what the returning scene should look like, and models were scored on camera execution, scene continuity, and most importantly, whether the off-screen event had advanced correctly.
📊 实验
结果		 在全部23个模型、9600段视频的测试中，所有系统都未能通过核心的"镜头外连续性"测试——没有任何模型能可靠地在摄像机转开期间推进事件发展。模型一致表现为"冻结"未观测场景，摄像机回归时恢复到最后一次看到的状态，而非展示正确的物理演变。这一失败在四种控制范式、所有模型系列和所有参数规模下均稳定存在。图像质量更好的大模型在这一维度上没有任何实质性提升。该基准揭示出，在视角干预下的鲁棒世界状态演化上，业界当前接近普遍性的0%成功率——这是一个此前从未被测量的领域空白。 Across all 23 tested models and 9,600 videos, every single system failed the core off-screen continuity test — none were able to reliably advance events while the camera was away. Models consistently "froze" the unobserved scene and restored it to its last seen state when the camera returned, rather than showing correct physical progression. This failure held across all four control paradigms, all model families, and all scales of model size. Larger models with better image quality showed no meaningful improvement on this dimension. The benchmark revealed a near-universal 0% success rate on robust world-state evolution under viewpoint intervention, establishing a clear and previously unmeasured gap in the field.
🏁 结论 本文证明，所有现有AI世界模型——无论规模多大、画面多逼真——从根本上都无法追踪摄像机转开后世界发生了什么，因为它们模拟的是"下一帧看起来怎样"而非"世界真正如何演变"。WRBench为领域提供了首个测量并修复这一盲点的工具，并主张持久的世界状态演化必须成为真正智能世界模型的核心设计目标。 This paper proves that all current AI world models — no matter how big or visually impressive — fundamentally cannot track what happens in the world when the camera looks away, because they simulate "what the next frame looks like" rather than "how the world actually evolves." WRBench gives the field its first tool to measure and fix this blind spot, arguing that persistent world-state evolution must become a core design goal for truly intelligent world models.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

研究团队构建了WRBench评测基准，采用三步评估链。第一步：检查AI是否正确执行了摄像机运动（比如向左平移）。第二步：检查镜头拍摄期间场景是否连续且可识别。第三步（关键测试）：在某个事件进行中（比如球在滚动、蜡烛在燃烧）将摄像机转开，然后转回，问：事件在镜头外有没有继续进行？还是冻结了、重置了？由人类评判员校准"正确续接"应该是什么样子，并对四种不同摄像机控制范式下的AI输出打分。 The researchers built WRBench, a diagnostic benchmark with a three-step evaluation chain. First, they check if the AI correctly executes the camera movement (e.g., pan left). Second, they check if the scene looks continuous and recognizable while in view. Third — the critical test — they pan the camera away during an ongoing event (like a ball rolling or a candle burning), then return the camera and ask: did the event keep progressing off-screen, or did it freeze and reset? Human judges calibrate what "correct" continuation should look like, and the AI's output is scored against this expectation across four different types of camera control systems.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

如果AI世界模型未来要驱动真正智能的机器人、自动驾驶汽车或通用AI助手，它们就必须理解"世界在没人看的时候也在运转"——就像真实大脑一样。这篇论文为整个领域敲响了警钟：现有系统建立在一个有缺陷的基础上，并提供了一个具体的诊断和修复工具。 If AI world models are ever going to power truly intelligent robots, autonomous cars, or general-purpose AI assistants, they need to understand that the world keeps moving even when they aren't looking — just like a real brain does. This paper sounds an alarm that the entire field is building on a flawed foundation, and gives researchers a concrete tool to diagnose and fix it.

🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个新来的侦探进入案发现场，他不是靠直觉猜凶手，而是拿出放大镜、卷尺、相机，一步步收集物证、画出案发地图，最终推理出真相。S-Agent就是给AI配备了这套"侦探工具箱"，让它主动勘察三维场景，而不是闭眼猜答案。Imagine a detective who doesn't just glance at a crime scene photo and guess — instead, they walk around with a tape measure, camera, and notebook, collecting clues from every angle before solving the case. S-Agent gives AI that same detective toolkit for understanding 3D spaces, instead of making wild guesses from a single frozen snapshot.

❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的AI视觉模型（VLM）只会"看"单张图片，像一个人闭上眼睛后只记得最后一帧画面。它们无法理解连续变化的三维空间，比如"这个房间里有几把椅子""这两个物体之间有多远""哪个物体在另一个的左边"。这让AI在真实世界的空间理解任务上表现很差——毕竟现实世界是三维的，而不是一张张孤立的照片。 Existing AI vision models can only look at one picture at a time — like someone who can only remember the last photo they saw and can't build a mental map of a room. They struggle to understand 3D space across multiple views or video frames, failing at tasks like counting objects, measuring distances, or judging directions. The real world is 3D and constantly moving, but these models treat it as a series of disconnected snapshots.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 首创"空间工具调用"范式：AI不再单纯靠视觉猜答案，而是主动调用专业工具（如2D定位、3D几何重建）来收集证据再推理。 • 双记忆机制：设计了"场景记忆"（记录三维场景状态）和"智能体记忆"（记录推理过程），让AI能跨帧、跨步骤积累信息。 • 无需重新训练即可提升现有模型：S-Agent可以直接套在任何开源或闭源VLM上，无需微调就能提升空间推理能力。 • 生成了30万条空间推理轨迹数据S-300K，用于训练紧凑型8B模型，媲美GPT-4级别的大模型。 • First "spatial tool-use" paradigm: Instead of guessing from pixels, the AI actively calls specialized tools (2D grounding, 3D geometry) to gather hard evidence before answering. • Dual memory system: "Scene Memory" tracks the evolving 3D scene; "Agent Memory" keeps a running log of reasoning steps — both working across video frames. • Training-free plug-in: Works on top of any existing AI model (open or closed source) with zero retraining needed. • Generated S-300K, a 300K spatial reasoning trajectory dataset, enabling a compact 8B model to rival frontier closed-source giants.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个多视角和视频空间推理基准上进行，包括SQA3D、ScanQA、EQA、VSI-Bench等权威数据集。基线模型覆盖开源（如Qwen3-VL-8B、LLaVA系列）和闭源（如GPT-4o、Gemini 2.5 Pro）的多种VLM，评估指标包括准确率、计数误差、距离误差等空间推理专项指标。同时测试了"无训练直接增强"和"用S-300K微调后"两种使用方式。 Experiments were run on multiple well-known spatial reasoning benchmarks covering multi-view images and videos, including SQA3D, ScanQA, EQA, and VSI-Bench. Baselines included both open-source models (e.g., Qwen3-VL-8B, LLaVA variants) and powerful closed-source models (e.g., GPT-4o, Gemini 2.5 Pro). The team tested S-Agent both as a plug-in with no training and as a fine-tuned 8B model trained on the generated S-300K dataset, using metrics like accuracy, counting error, and distance error.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

S-Agent把大型视觉语言模型变成一个"指挥官"，它负责规划需要哪些证据，然后派遣专业工具去执行。具体流程：首先，模型看到多视角图像或视频后，决定要问什么问题（比如"这个椅子在哪儿"）；然后，工具层把物体从2D图像中定位出来，再用几何算法"抬升"到三维空间；最后，所有帧的三维证据被汇聚到场景记忆中，形成高层次的空间知识（数量、距离、朝向、相对位置）来回答问题。整个过程就像一个侦探不断收集线索，最终拼出完整图景。 S-Agent turns the big AI vision model into a "commander" that decides what clues are needed, then sends specialist tools to fetch them. Step by step: the model reads multi-view images or video and plans what spatial evidence is required; specialized tools then find objects in 2D frames and "lift" them into 3D space using geometry; all 3D evidence is stored in a Scene Memory that grows across frames; finally, the accumulated knowledge is used to answer high-level spatial questions like counts, distances, and directions. Think of it as a detective that keeps a case board, pinning new clues as they arrive before drawing conclusions.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

空间智能是机器人、自动驾驶、AR/VR等真实场景落地的核心能力，现有模型的空间推理瓶颈正是这些应用的最大障碍。S-Agent提供了一个即插即用的解决方案，有望大幅推进具身智能和空间感知类AI产品的实用化进程。 Spatial intelligence is the backbone of robots, self-driving cars, and AR/VR systems — and the poor 3D reasoning of current AI is a major bottleneck for all of these. S-Agent offers a plug-and-play upgrade that could unlock a new generation of AI products that truly understand the physical world around them.
