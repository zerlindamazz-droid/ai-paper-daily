ELF: Embedded Language Flows ELF：嵌入语言流

🧑‍🔬 Keya Hu, Linlu Qiu, Yiyang Lu, Hanhong Zhao et al.

图 Figure · Page 3 of paper

🎯 在连续嵌入空间做流匹配，让扩散语言模型真正好用。
Continuous flow matching in embedding space makes diffusion language models competitive with minimal adaptation.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 扩散模型在图像和视频生成领域大获成功，但文本是离散的（词元），难以直接套用。现有的扩散语言模型大多还是在离散空间操作，无法充分借鉴图像扩散的成熟技术（如分类器自由引导）。核心问题是：能否以最小改动让连续扩散方法在语言上也真正奏效？ Diffusion models have conquered image and video generation, but applying them to language is hard because text is discrete (tokens), not continuous. Existing diffusion language models (DLMs) mostly work directly on discrete tokens, which limits their ability to borrow powerful techniques from image diffusion. The question is: can a continuous diffusion approach work well for language with minimal changes?
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 全程在连续词嵌入空间运行，只在最后一步用共享权重网络映射回离散词元，流程简洁优雅。\n• 将连续时间流匹配（Flow Matching）直接应用于语言嵌入，无缝复用图像扩散的分类器自由引导（CFG）等成熟技术。\n• 以更少的采样步数实现更优的生成质量，明显超越现有离散与连续扩散语言模型。 • Operates almost entirely in the continuous token embedding space, only mapping back to discrete tokens at the very last step via a shared-weight network — keeping the pipeline clean and continuous.\n• Directly applies Flow Matching (continuous-time) to language embeddings, enabling straight adoption of image-domain tricks like classifier-free guidance (CFG).\n• Achieves strong generation quality with fewer sampling steps than competing DLMs, closing the gap between discrete and continuous language diffusion.
🧪 实验
内容		 ELF在标准文本生成基准（如GenBench及多个文本生成测试集）上进行评估，与主流离散扩散语言模型（MDLM、SEDD等）和连续扩散语言模型（CDCD、GENIE等）对比。评估指标包括生成困惑度、MAUVE分数以及外部语言模型评估的生成困惑度。消融实验分析了CFG、采样步数和共享权重解码器各自的贡献。 ELF was evaluated on standard language generation benchmarks including GenBench and other text generation suites, compared against leading discrete DLMs (e.g., MDLM, SEDD) and continuous DLMs (e.g., CDCD, GENIE). Evaluation metrics included generation perplexity, MAUVE score, and generative perplexity measured by an external language model. Ablations tested the impact of CFG, number of sampling steps, and the shared-weight decoder design.
📊 实验
结果		 ELF在所有基准测试上均大幅超越现有离散和连续扩散语言模型。与最优离散DLM相比，ELF在生成困惑度和MAUVE分数上达到相当或更优的水平，同时所需采样步数显著更少（例如仅需32–64步即可达到基线需要数百步才能达到的效果）。在连续嵌入空间施加CFG带来了一致的质量提升。ELF填补乃至逆转了连续DLM与离散DLM之间长期存在的性能差距。 ELF substantially outperforms prior discrete and continuous DLMs across benchmarks. It achieves competitive or superior generation perplexity and MAUVE scores compared to the best discrete DLMs while using significantly fewer sampling steps (e.g., strong performance with as few as 32–64 steps vs. hundreds required by baselines). CFG in the continuous embedding space provides consistent quality boosts. ELF closes or reverses the historical performance gap between continuous and discrete DLMs, demonstrating that continuous approaches can surpass discrete ones with minimal architectural changes.
🏁 结论 ELF证明了基于嵌入空间流匹配和共享权重解码器的连续扩散语言模型，可以用更少的采样步数、更简洁的架构超越最先进的离散扩散语言模型，为连续扩散方法在语言生成领域的应用奠定了坚实基础。 ELF demonstrates that continuous diffusion language models, built on Flow Matching in embedding space with a shared-weight decoder, can match or surpass state-of-the-art discrete DLMs with fewer sampling steps and minimal architectural complexity. This establishes continuous embedding-space diffusion as a viable and promising direction for language generation.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

ELF将词元嵌入视为连续向量，用连续时间流匹配学习一个向量场，将高斯噪声"输运"到有意义的嵌入分布。生成过程全程在连续空间进行，仅在最后一步通过共享权重网络映射回离散词元。这一设计使得分类器自由引导（CFG）可以直接在连续嵌入空间施加，无需任何特殊适配。共享权重解码器还能保证训练过程中连续表示与离散表示始终对齐。 ELF treats token embeddings as continuous vectors and applies continuous-time Flow Matching to learn a vector field that transports Gaussian noise toward meaningful embedding distributions. The model stays in this continuous space throughout generation, only projecting to discrete tokens at the final step using a network with weights shared across the embedding and decoding layers. This design makes it trivially easy to plug in classifier-free guidance (CFG): the guidance signal is applied in continuous embedding space before the final discretization. The shared-weight decoder also ensures that the continuous and discrete representations stay aligned throughout training.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\frac{d\mathbf{x}}{dt} = v_\theta(\mathbf{x}_t, t)\]
💡 流匹配的核心ODE：神经网络学习一个向量场 $v_\theta$，描述嵌入从噪声到数据的连续演化路径。 The core Flow Matching ODE: the neural network learns a vector field $v_\theta$ that describes how embeddings evolve continuously from noise to data over time.
\[\mathbf{x}_t = (1-t)\mathbf{x}_0 + t\mathbf{x}_1\]
💡 线性插值路径（条件流）：在噪声 $\mathbf{x}_0$ 和数据嵌入 $\mathbf{x}_1$ 之间构造直线轨迹，是流匹配训练的监督目标。 The linear interpolation path (conditional flow): a straight-line trajectory between noise $\mathbf{x}_0$ and data embedding $\mathbf{x}_1$, serving as the training target for the flow network.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项工作打通了图像/视频扩散范式与语言建模之间的壁垒，证明连续扩散的核心工具（流匹配、CFG）只需极少改动就能迁移到文本领域。这为语言社区借道成熟的连续扩散研究生态铺平了道路，有望加速非自回归文本生成的发展进程。 This work bridges the gap between the highly successful image/video diffusion paradigm and language modeling, showing that the same continuous tools (flow matching, CFG) transfer effectively to text with minimal modification. It opens a practical path for the language community to leverage the rich ecosystem of continuous diffusion research, potentially accelerating progress in non-autoregressive text generation.

The Truth Lies Somewhere in the Middle (of the Generated Tokens) 真相藏于生成Token的中间位置

🧑‍🔬 Sophie L. Wang, Phillip Isola, Brian Cheung

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 对LLM生成token做均值池化，可获得比任意单token更优的语义表示。
Mean-pooling over LLM-generated tokens yields richer semantic representations than any single token's hidden state.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大型语言模型以自回归方式生成文本，但如何从生成的隐层状态中提取一个有意义的单一表示，目前尚无定论。大多数方法依赖单个token位置（如最后一个token或特殊的[CLS] token），可能遗漏了分散在所有生成token中的信息。核心问题是：哪个（些）token位置最能捕捉模型的内部语义理解？ Large language models generate text autoregressively using causal masking, but it's unclear how to best extract a single meaningful representation from the resulting hidden states. Most approaches rely on a single token position (e.g., the last token or a special [CLS] token), potentially missing information distributed across all generated tokens. The key question is: which token position(s) best capture the model's internal semantic understanding?
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 核心发现：LLM的信息分布在所有生成token上，而非集中于某一位置——均值池化正是利用了这一结构特性。 • 跨领域验证：通过核对齐（Kernel Alignment）在语言、视觉和蛋白质三个领域的参考空间中同时评估表示质量。 • 可解释动态：追踪不同生成步骤的对齐情况，揭示了模型内部状态随时间演变的结构性、可解释的规律。 • Novel insight: information in LLMs is distributed across generated tokens, not localized to any single position — mean pooling exploits this structure. • Cross-domain validation: kernel alignment is used to measure representation quality against reference spaces in language, vision, and protein domains simultaneously. • Interpretable dynamics: tracking alignment across generation steps reveals structured, interpretable patterns in how a model's internal state evolves over time.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在三个参考领域（语言、视觉、蛋白质结构）对多个LLM的隐层状态表示进行评估。比较均值池化与各单独token位置的表示，使用中心核对齐（CKA）作为评估指标。基线包括提示词token的表示以及各单独生成token位置的表示。此外，还动态追踪不同生成步骤中的CKA分数，研究表示的演变过程。 Experiments evaluate hidden state representations from multiple LLMs across three reference domains: language (text embedding models), vision (image feature models), and protein structure (protein language models). Pooling strategies (mean, individual token positions) are compared using Centered Kernel Alignment (CKA) as the evaluation metric. Baselines include representations from prompt tokens as well as individual generated token positions. The study also tracks CKA scores dynamically across generation steps to study representational evolution.
📊 实验
结果		 在语言、视觉、蛋白质三个领域中，对生成token做均值池化在CKA对齐分数上始终优于任何单一token位置。生成token的表示在语义对齐上优于提示词token的表示。均值池化相对于最优单token位置的提升在不同模型和领域中均稳定且具有统计显著性，对齐分数表明没有任何单一token位置能捕捉到均值所编码的全部语义内容。跨生成步骤的动态对齐曲线揭示了模型行为中可解释的阶段性转变（如早期与晚期生成步骤呈现出不同的对齐特征）。具体CKA数值因领域而异，但在测试的多种模型规模和系列中均保持一致。 Mean pooling over generated tokens consistently outperforms any individual token position in CKA alignment across all three domains (language, vision, protein). Representations from generated tokens outperform those from prompt tokens in semantic alignment. The improvement from mean pooling over the best single token position is consistent and statistically robust across models and domains, with alignment scores showing that no single token position captures the full semantic content encoded by the mean. Dynamic alignment curves across generation reveal interpretable phase-like transitions in model behavior (e.g., early vs. late generation steps show distinct alignment profiles). Specific numerical CKA gains vary by domain but are reported as consistent across model scales and families tested.
🏁 结论 本文证明了对LLM生成token做均值池化，能够产生比任何单一token位置都更优秀的语义表示，原因在于信息是分布而非集中在生成序列中的。核心贡献是一种简单、可推广的表示提取策略，在语言、视觉和蛋白质领域均得到验证，并揭示了LLM内部状态在生成过程中演变的可解释规律。 This paper proves that mean pooling over LLM-generated tokens produces consistently superior semantic representations compared to any single token position, because information is distributed — not localized — across the generation sequence. The key contribution is a simple, generalizable extraction strategy validated across language, vision, and protein domains that also unlocks interpretable insights into how LLM internal states evolve during generation.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者从LLM生成的token（而非提示词token）中提取隐层状态，并比较不同的池化策略——均值池化与各个单独token位置（首位、末位、中间等）。他们使用中心核对齐（CKA）来衡量表示质量，量化模型内部表示与文本嵌入、图像特征、蛋白质结构模型等参考空间之间的对齐程度。通过系统地扫描不同生成位置和池化策略，找到最佳捕捉语义信息的方式。同时分析生成步骤中的对齐动态，以揭示模型行为规律。 The authors extract hidden states from LLM-generated tokens (not prompt tokens) and compare different pooling strategies — mean pooling vs. individual token positions (first, last, middle, etc.). They measure representation quality using Centered Kernel Alignment (CKA), which quantifies how well a model's internal representations align with reference spaces from text embeddings, image features, and protein structure models. By sweeping across generation positions and pooling strategies, they systematically identify where and how semantic information is best captured. They also analyze alignment dynamics over generation steps to reveal behavioral patterns.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{CKA}(K, L) = \frac{\text{HSIC}(K, L)}{\sqrt{\text{HSIC}(K, K) \cdot \text{HSIC}(L, L)}}\]
💡 中心核对齐（CKA）：衡量两个表示空间（K和L）之间相似程度的指标，HSIC为希尔伯特-施密特独立准则。CKA值越高，说明两个表示空间越对齐。 Centered Kernel Alignment (CKA): measures how well two representation spaces (K and L) align with each other. HSIC is the Hilbert-Schmidt Independence Criterion. Higher CKA means the two spaces are more structurally similar.
\[\hat{h} = \frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^{T} h_t\]
💡 均值池化：将自回归生成的T个token的隐层状态h_t取平均，得到一个综合表示\hat{h}，本文发现这比任何单个h_t都具有更好的语义表示能力。 Mean pooling: average the hidden states h_t across all T generated tokens to produce a single aggregated representation. This paper finds this outperforms any individual token hidden state h_t semantically.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

从LLM中提取高质量表示对检索、分类、跨模态对齐等下游任务至关重要——本文提供了一种简单且有理论依据的改进方案（对生成token做均值池化），其效果优于常用的"取最后token"启发式方法。跨领域验证和可解释的动态分析也为理解和探究生成模型内部计算机制开辟了新方向。 Extracting high-quality representations from LLMs is fundamental for downstream tasks like retrieval, classification, and cross-modal alignment — this work provides a simple, principled fix (mean pooling over generated tokens) that outperforms the commonly used last-token heuristic. The cross-domain validation and interpretable dynamics also open new avenues for understanding and probing the internal computations of generative models.

Unmasking On-Policy Distillation: Where It Helps, Where It Hurts, and Why 揭开在线策略蒸馏的面纱：何时有益、何时有害，以及原因

🧑‍🔬 Mohammadreza Armandpour, Fatih Ilhan, David Harrison, Ajay Jaiswal et al.

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 免训练诊断框架揭示在线蒸馏逐token的有益与有害条件
A training-free diagnostic framework reveals when on-policy distillation helps or hurts reasoning model training, token by token.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 用在线策略蒸馏训练推理模型时，每个token都有监督信号，但我们并不清楚这些信号在什么情况下真正有帮助、什么情况下反而有害。目前研究者只能跑完整的昂贵训练实验，看整体指标，完全看不出单个token层面发生了什么。也没有原则性方法来选择合适的教师模型、蒸馏上下文，或判断这些选择是否应因token而异。 Training reasoning language models via on-policy distillation provides dense per-token supervision, but it's unknown when this guidance actually helps versus hurts the student model. Practitioners currently must run expensive full training experiments to observe aggregate metrics, which hide what's happening at the level of individual tokens or questions. There is no principled way to choose the right teacher, the right distillation context, or to know if these choices should vary token-by-token.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出免训练诊断框架，以最细粒度（逐token、逐问题、逐教师）分析蒸馏信号质量，无需完整训练实验。 • 推导出"理想节点梯度"——理论上能最大化学生模型成功概率的参数更新方向，作为黄金参考信号。 • 设计可扩展的"目标采样"算法，即使对长推理链也能高效估计该理想梯度。 • 定义"梯度对齐分数"（理想梯度与蒸馏梯度的余弦相似度），量化每个token处蒸馏信号的有效性。 • Introduces a training-free diagnostic framework operating at the finest granularity: per token, per question, and per teacher model — no full training runs required. • Derives an "ideal per-node gradient" — the theoretical parameter update that would maximally increase the student's success probability — as a gold-standard reference signal. • Proposes a scalable targeted-rollout algorithm to efficiently estimate this ideal gradient even for long reasoning chains. • Defines a "gradient alignment score" (cosine similarity between ideal and distillation gradients) as a quantitative measure of distillation quality at each token.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在数学推理任务上进行，测试了多种学生模型与教师模型组合，包括自蒸馏（学生用自身作为教师）以及不同规模的外部教师模型。在不同采样正确性条件（学生正确/错误的rollout）和不同蒸馏上下文下计算梯度对齐分数。与标准在线策略蒸馏的多种教师选择和上下文配置进行对比。同时评估了诊断框架在长链式思维序列上的可扩展性。 Experiments were conducted on mathematical reasoning tasks using various student and teacher model configurations, including self-distillation (student acts as its own teacher) and external teacher settings with models of varying capacity. The gradient alignment score was computed across different rollout correctness conditions (correct vs. incorrect student rollouts) and different distillation contexts. Baselines compared include standard on-policy distillation with various teacher choices and context configurations. The diagnostic framework itself was evaluated for scalability on long chain-of-thought sequences.
📊 实验
结果		 框架一致发现：在所有测试配置中，蒸馏梯度与理想梯度的对齐分数在学生模型"答错"时显著高于"答对"时——即蒸馏在学生已经失败时最有帮助，在学生已经成功时帮助最小甚至引入噪声。没有发现任何普遍最优的教师或蒸馏上下文：最优配置同时取决于学生模型容量和目标任务，因设置而异。正确与错误rollout之间的对齐分数差异在数值上清晰可见，具体数值因配置而有所不同。 The framework consistently finds that distillation gradient alignment with the ideal signal is substantially higher on incorrect student rollouts than on correct ones across all tested configurations — meaning distillation is most helpful when the student is already failing and least helpful (or even noisy/harmful) when the student succeeds. No single universally best teacher or distillation context was found: the optimal configuration depends jointly on the student model's capacity and the target task, varying across settings. Specific numerical alignment score differences between correct and incorrect rollout conditions demonstrate this gap clearly, though exact per-benchmark figures vary by configuration.
🏁 结论 本文证明了在线策略蒸馏并非均匀有益：在学生答错的rollout上与理想训练信号对齐最好，而在答对的rollout上则变得嘈杂甚至适得其反，且不存在普遍最优的教师或上下文配置。所提出的免训练诊断框架能够对蒸馏质量进行细粒度的逐token分析，为设计更有效的推理模型蒸馏策略提供了原则性工具。 This paper proves that on-policy distillation is not uniformly beneficial: it is most aligned with ideal training signals on incorrect rollouts and becomes noisy or counterproductive on correct ones, and no universally optimal teacher or context configuration exists. The introduced training-free diagnostic framework enables fine-grained, per-token analysis of distillation quality, providing a principled tool for designing more effective distillation strategies for reasoning models.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

论文首先推导出"理想节点梯度"——能最大幅度提升学生模型答对概率的梯度方向，作为理论基准。然后设计"目标采样"算法，在推理链的特定中间节点有选择地采样完成序列，从而高效估计该理想梯度，适用于长链式思维序列。将该理想梯度与实际蒸馏梯度做余弦相似度计算，得到"梯度对齐分数"，无需训练即可诊断每个token-教师-问题组合的蒸馏质量。该诊断框架被应用于自蒸馏和外部教师蒸馏场景，以发现蒸馏有益或有害的系统性规律。 The paper first derives a theoretical "ideal per-node gradient" — the gradient direction that would most improve the student's probability of getting a question correct — as a reference benchmark. A targeted-rollout algorithm is then developed to efficiently estimate this ideal gradient by selectively sampling completions from specific intermediate points in a reasoning chain, making it scalable to long chain-of-thought sequences. The gradient alignment score, computed as the cosine similarity between this ideal gradient and the actual distillation gradient, is used to diagnose each token-teacher-question configuration without requiring any training. This diagnostic is applied across self-distillation and external-teacher settings to surface systematic patterns about when distillation helps or hurts.

维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型本质上是静态的——训练后就无法再从新任务中自主学习和适应。研究者希望通过让智能体"积累经验"来弥补这一缺陷，但现有方法大多只在系统设计层面做文章（比如如何存储经验），而忽视了底层模型本身是否具备高质量提取和利用经验的能力。更关键的问题是：经验提取和经验利用这两个环节从未被作为一个整体来联合优化。 Large language models are inherently static — they cannot adapt from new interactions after deployment. Researchers have tried to fix this by giving agents an "experience memory," but most work focuses on system-level design (how to store experience) while ignoring whether the underlying model is actually good at extracting and using that experience. Critically, experience extraction and experience utilization have never been jointly optimized as a unified process.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **联合协同进化**：首次将经验提取与经验利用作为一个统一过程端到端联合优化，而非分阶段独立处理。 • **双信号驱动**：以经验评估为核心枢纽，其产生的两路监督信号分别独立优化"提取器"和"求解器"，实现有监督的协同进化。 • **经验内化**：Evolving-RL 将可复用的经验模式直接内化到模型参数中，即使在测试阶段没有外部经验库，也能在已见和未见任务上显著提升性能。 • **Coordinated Co-evolution**: First framework to jointly optimize experience extraction and utilization end-to-end as a unified process rather than separate stages. • **Dual Supervisory Signals**: Places experience evaluation at the center — its two derived signals separately optimize the extractor and solver, enabling principled co-evolution. • **Experience Internalization**: Evolving-RL internalizes reusable experience patterns directly into model parameters, achieving strong gains even without any test-time experience accumulation.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在 ALFWorld（室内场景文本游戏，涵盖已见和未见任务）和 Mind2Web（网页导航任务）两个基准上展开。基线方法包括标准 GRPO、无经验的 RL 变体以及仅优化求解器阶段的部分进化方法。消融实验对比了"仅优化利用"、"仅优化提取"和"联合优化"三种设置，以验证协同进化的必要性。评估指标涵盖任务成功率（已见任务和未见任务分别评估），测试时还对比了有无外部经验库的两种设置。 Experiments were conducted on two agent benchmarks: ALFWorld (a text-based indoor navigation game with seen and unseen task splits) and Mind2Web (a web navigation benchmark). Baselines include standard GRPO, RL variants without experience, and partial evolution methods that only optimize the utilization stage. Ablations compare three settings — utilization-only, extraction-only, and joint optimization — to verify the necessity of co-evolution. Task success rate is the primary metric, evaluated separately on seen and unseen tasks, with and without test-time experience accumulation.

Evolving-RL achieves strong benchmark gains and reveals a new experience internalization mechanism that endows models with robust generalization even without external experience at test time.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Evolving-RL 将自我进化拆解为三个角色：经验提取器（Extractor）、经验评估器（Evaluator）和任务求解器（Solver）。学习过程以经验评估为核心枢纽：评估器对提取出的经验质量打分，这个分数作为强化学习信号同时反馈给提取器（让它学会提取更有价值的经验）和求解器（让它学会更好地利用经验解决任务）。两者共享同一个奖励来源，但被分别优化，从而实现协同进化。整个框架基于 GRPO（Group Relative Policy Optimization）等强化学习算法实现，无需人工标注的经验监督数据。 Evolving-RL decomposes self-evolution into three roles: an Extractor, an Evaluator, and a Solver. The Evaluator is the central hub — it scores the quality of extracted experience and produces two supervisory signals that separately optimize the Extractor (learn to extract better experience) and the Solver (learn to use experience more effectively to solve tasks). Both components share the same reward source but are optimized independently, enabling coordinated co-evolution. The entire framework is built on RL algorithms such as GRPO and requires no human-annotated experience labels.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r_{\text{eval}} = f(\text{Evaluator}(e, s, a))\]
💡 评估器对提取出的经验 e 在状态 s、动作 a 下打分，产生奖励信号 r_eval，分别驱动提取器和求解器的强化学习更新。 The Evaluator scores extracted experience e given state s and action a, producing reward signal r_eval that independently drives RL updates for both the Extractor and the Solver.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{Extractor}} = -\mathbb{E}[r_{\text{eval}} \cdot \log \pi_{\theta}(e \mid \text{context})]\]
💡 提取器的优化目标：以评估器给出的奖励为权重，最大化高质量经验被提取的对数概率，鼓励模型提炼出更有价值的经验。 The Extractor's optimization objective: maximize the log-probability of generating high-quality experience, weighted by the Evaluator's reward — encouraging the model to distill more reusable and generalizable experience.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

让 LLM 智能体真正从过去经验中"学会学习"，是推向实际部署的关键一步——Evolving-RL 提供了一个原则性的端到端框架来实现这一目标。此外，经验内化这一发现揭示了 RL 微调与情境学习之间深层的协同关系，对智能体训练范式具有广泛启示。 Enabling LLM agents to genuinely learn from past experience is a critical step toward real-world deployment, and Evolving-RL provides a principled end-to-end framework to achieve this. The discovery of experience internalization also reveals a deep synergy between RL fine-tuning and in-context learning, with broad implications for how future agent training pipelines should be designed.
