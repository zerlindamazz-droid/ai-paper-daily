Qwen-VLA: Unifying Vision-Language-Action Modeling across Tasks, Environments, and Robot Embodiments Qwen-VLA：统一跨任务、跨环境、跨机器人形态的视觉-语言-动作建模

🧑‍🔬 Qiuyue Wang, Mingsheng Li, Jian Guan, Jinhui Ye et al.

图 Figure · Page 1 of paper

🎯 一个模型，搞定所有机器人任务
One unified AI model controls any robot across manipulation, navigation, and beyond.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像以前出行要带地图、指南针、翻译手册三件套，而现在一部智能手机全搞定——Qwen-VLA就是机器人世界里的那部"超级智能手机"，不管你是双臂机械手还是导航小车，它都能一脑通吃。Think of it like going from needing a separate GPS, a language translator, and a driving instructor for every new car you drive — to one universal co-pilot app that works perfectly no matter what vehicle you're in. Qwen-VLA is that universal co-pilot, but for robots.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的机器人AI模型通常只能做一件事——比如只会抓东西，或只会导航，换个任务或换个机器人就完全不行了。就像你家里的洗碗机、扫地机器人、微波炉各自为政，没有一个"万能管家"能把所有活都干了。研究者们希望找到一个统一的AI大脑，能让不同形态的机器人完成各种各样的任务，而不是为每个任务单独训练一个模型。 Most robot AI models are narrow specialists — one model for grabbing objects, another for navigating rooms, and they completely fall apart on new tasks or different robot bodies. It's like hiring a separate worker for every single chore in your house, and none of them can cover for each other. Researchers want a single "universal robot brain" that can handle any task, in any environment, on any robot body.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 统一框架：将机械臂操作、室内导航、轨迹预测三类任务统一到同一个模型中，不再各自为政。 • 具身感知提示：通过文字描述告诉模型"你现在是哪种机器人、用什么方式控制"，让同一个AI大脑驱动不同形态的机器人。 • DiT动作解码器：在强大的语言理解基础上，加入一个专门生成连续动作和运动轨迹的模块，让AI不只会"看懂"，还会"动起来"。 • 超大规模联合预训练：融合机器人操作数据、人类第一视角演示、合成仿真数据、导航数据等多种来源，大幅提升泛化能力。 • Unified task framework: Manipulation, navigation, and trajectory prediction are all handled by one single model — no more siloed specialists. • Embodiment-aware prompting: The model reads plain-text descriptions of which robot it's controlling, allowing the same AI brain to drive totally different robot bodies. • DiT-based action decoder: A special module is added on top of a powerful vision-language model to generate smooth, continuous physical movements — bridging understanding and action. • Large-scale joint pretraining: Trained on a rich mix of robot data, human egocentric videos, simulation data, and navigation datasets for broad generalization.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究者在多个权威机器人基准上测试了Qwen-VLA，涵盖操作类（LIBERO、Simpler-WidowX、RoboTwin、真实ALOHA机器人）、导航类（R2R、RxR视觉-语言导航）以及轨迹预测类（DOMINO动态操作）。他们与各领域专用模型进行比较，并特别设计了场景布局、背景、光照、物体配置、机器人形态等方面的分布外（OOD）泛化测试，以评估模型面对新情境时的适应能力。 The team tested Qwen-VLA on a wide range of standard robot benchmarks: manipulation tasks (LIBERO, Simpler-WidowX, RoboTwin, real-world ALOHA robot), vision-and-language navigation (R2R and RxR), and trajectory-centric prediction (DOMINO dynamic manipulation). They compared against task-specific specialist models and ran dedicated out-of-distribution (OOD) tests involving new scene layouts, lighting changes, background shifts, object configuration changes, and different robot bodies to stress-test generalization.
📊 实验
结果		 • LIBERO操作基准：97.9% 成功率 • Simpler-WidowX操作：73.7% 成功率 • RoboTwin-Easy / Hard：86.1% / 87.2% 成功率 • R2R视觉导航：69.0% OSR（路径成功率） • RxR多语言导航：59.6% SR（成功率） • 真实ALOHA机器人OOD实验：平均76.9% 成功率 • DOMINO动态操作零样本测试：26.6% 成功率（零样本，难度极高） • LIBERO manipulation benchmark: 97.9% success rate • Simpler-WidowX manipulation: 73.7% success rate • RoboTwin-Easy / Hard: 86.1% / 87.2% success rate • R2R vision-language navigation: 69.0% OSR • RxR multilingual navigation: 59.6% SR • Real-world ALOHA robot OOD experiments: 76.9% average success rate • DOMINO dynamic manipulation zero-shot: 26.6% success rate (zero-shot, extremely challenging)
🏁 结论 Qwen-VLA证明了一个统一的视觉-语言-动作基础模型可以同时出色地完成机器人操作、导航和轨迹预测等多类任务，并在面对新环境、新机器人形态时展现出强大的泛化能力，打破了"一任务一模型"的传统局限。 Qwen-VLA proves that a single unified AI model can master manipulation, navigation, and trajectory prediction all at once, generalizing robustly to unseen environments and robot bodies — breaking the long-standing belief that each robot task needs its own specialized model.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Qwen-VLA以阿里巴巴的Qwen视觉-语言大模型为基础"大脑"，负责看懂图像、理解指令、进行推理。在这个大脑之上，研究者加入了一个基于DiT（扩散变换器）的动作解码器，就像给大脑接上了一双"手"，能将理解转化为连续的物理动作和运动轨迹。为了让同一个模型驾驭不同的机器人，他们设计了"具身感知提示"机制——用一段文字告诉模型"你现在是双臂机械手，用关节角度控制"，模型就能自动适应。训练时，将机器人操作、人类示范、仿真数据、导航数据等多种数据源混合在一起进行大规模联合预训练，再经过指令微调，使模型在各类任务上都表现出色。 Qwen-VLA starts with Qwen's powerful vision-language model as its "brain" — it can see, read instructions, and reason about the world. On top of that, researchers attach a DiT-based action decoder, like giving the brain a pair of hands that can translate thoughts into smooth, real-world physical movements. To handle different robot bodies, they use "embodiment-aware prompting" — a short text description tells the model what kind of robot it's running on and how it's controlled, so the same AI adapts automatically. The whole system is trained on a massive, diverse mixture of robot trajectories, human first-person videos, simulated data, and navigation datasets, then fine-tuned with instructions to polish performance across all task types.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项研究向"通用机器人大脑"迈出了重要一步——未来家用机器人、工厂机器人、服务机器人或许只需一套AI系统，就能胜任各种任务，大幅降低研发成本。对于整个AI和机器人行业来说，这种统一框架的思路有望成为下一代具身智能的标准范式。 This work is a major step toward a true "general-purpose robot brain" — in the future, one AI system could power home robots, factory arms, and service robots all at once, slashing development costs and complexity. For the broader AI field, this unified approach could become the blueprint for next-generation embodied intelligence.

CUA-Gym: Scaling Verifiable Training Environments and Tasks for Computer-Use Agents CUA-Gym：为计算机操作智能体扩展可验证训练环境与任务

🧑‍🔬 Bowen Wang, Dunjie Lu, Junli Wang, Tianyi Bai et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 自动生成海量可验证训练数据，让AI学会像人一样操作电脑
A scalable pipeline that auto-generates verified training data to teach AI agents how to use computers like humans.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一个机器人通过做无数道练习题来学开车，而CUA-Gym就是那台自动出题机器——它不仅自己设计赛道、搭建考场，还能自动打分，完全不需要人一道题一道题地手工编写。Think of it like a robot learning to drive by taking millions of driving tests — except CUA-Gym is the machine that automatically invents new test routes, sets up the course, and grades the robot's performance, all without any human having to design each lesson by hand.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 想让AI学会像人一样操作电脑——点击按钮、填写表单、在网页上完成任务——非常困难，因为几乎没有足够的高质量训练数据。现有的数据集要么是人工制作的（数量极少），要么是自动生成的但无法可靠地判断AI有没有完成任务。没有足够的"对错分明"的练习题，AI就无法在真实电脑操作任务上进步。 Teaching AI to use computers — clicking, typing, navigating websites — is incredibly hard because there's almost no good training data for it. Existing datasets are either hand-crafted (very few examples) or auto-generated but unreliable (the AI can't tell if it actually succeeded). Without enough quality practice problems with clear right-or-wrong answers, AI agents can't improve at real computer tasks.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 全自动三智能体流水线（生成器、判别器、协调器）：自动出题、搭建环境、编写评分函数，无需人工干预。 • CUA-Gym-Hub：一套贴近真实软件使用场景的高仿真模拟网站和应用库。 • 两阶段质量过滤（AI多数投票 + 智能体实际试跑）：在训练前淘汰劣质样本。 • 最终数据集包含32,112条已验证训练样本，覆盖110个不同环境，规模远超以往工作。 • A fully automated three-agent pipeline (Generator, Discriminator, Orchestrator) that creates tasks, sets up environments, and writes its own grading functions — no humans needed. • CUA-Gym-Hub: a library of realistic mock websites and apps that mirrors how real software is actually used in the world. • A two-stage quality filter (AI majority voting + agent trial runs) that weeds out bad training examples before they corrupt learning. • The resulting dataset of 32,112 verified training examples covers 110 different environments — orders of magnitude larger than prior work.
🧪 实验
内容		 研究人员使用生成的数据集和GSPO训练算法，训练了两个规模的模型：30亿参数（CUA-Gym-A3B）和170亿参数（CUA-Gym-A17B）。在两个基准测试上评估效果：OSWorld-Verified（标准计算机操作任务测试）和WebArena（训练中未见过的网页导航基准）。与相近规模的其他开源计算机操作智能体进行比较，验证该方法是否带来更好的性能。 The researchers trained two model sizes — a 3-billion-parameter model (CUA-Gym-A3B) and a 17-billion-parameter model (CUA-Gym-A17B) — using their generated dataset with a training algorithm called GSPO. They evaluated performance on two benchmarks: OSWorld-Verified (a standard test of computer-use tasks) and WebArena (a held-out web navigation benchmark not seen during training). They compared against other open-source computer-use agents of similar size to see if their approach leads to better performance.
📊 实验
结果		 CUA-Gym-A3B 在 OSWorld-Verified 上得分 62.1%，CUA-Gym-A17B 得分 72.6%，均超过同等规模的其他开源计算机操作智能体。随着训练数据量和环境多样性的增加，性能平稳提升。两个模型在未见过的 WebArena 基准上也有提升，说明学到的技能可以迁移到新环境。 CUA-Gym-A3B scored 62.1% on OSWorld-Verified, and CUA-Gym-A17B scored 72.6%, both outperforming prior open-source computer-use agents at comparable model sizes. Performance scaled smoothly as more training data and more diverse environments were added. Both models also improved on the held-out WebArena benchmark, showing the skills transfer beyond the training environments.
🏁 结论 CUA-Gym 证明了全自动流水线能够为计算机操作AI生成大规模、高质量的训练数据，训练出的模型超越现有开源同类产品，且能泛化到新任务。开源的流水线、数据集和环境将大大加速"能替人操作电脑的AI"领域的研究进展。 CUA-Gym proves that a fully automated pipeline can generate large-scale, high-quality training data for computer-use AI agents, producing models that beat existing open-source alternatives and generalize to new tasks. The open-sourced pipeline, dataset, and environments could significantly accelerate progress in building AI that reliably operates computers on behalf of users.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

CUA-Gym 让三个AI智能体协作，自动批量生产训练题目。首先，"生成器"智能体为每个任务创建初始电脑状态和目标"黄金"状态；然后，"判别器"智能体编写一段评分脚本，用来判断AI学员有没有正确完成任务；"协调器"智能体负责在两者之间来回调度，纠正错误，直到一切一致且可运行。最后，所有生成的样本还要经过双重质量审核：多个AI评委投票判断样本是否有效，同时实际运行智能体来测试评分函数是否有效。只有通过全部审核的样本才会进入训练集。 CUA-Gym uses three cooperating AI agents to automatically build training problems. First, a "Generator" agent creates a starting computer state and a target "golden" state for each task. Then a "Discriminator" agent writes a reward function — basically a grading script — that can check whether the AI student actually completed the task correctly. An "Orchestrator" agent referees back-and-forth rounds between the two, fixing errors until everything is consistent and executable. Finally, all generated examples go through a double quality check: multiple AI judges vote on whether each example is valid, and actual agent rollouts test whether the reward function works in practice. This ensures only clean, reliable training data gets used.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

能真正操作电脑的AI助手——帮你订机票、填表格、管理文件——是AI最实用的近未来应用之一。CUA-Gym通过自动化数据生成，扫除了这一领域最大的障碍（训练数据匮乏），让构建可靠、实用的计算机操作AI变得更容易、更低成本。 AI assistants that can actually operate your computer — booking flights, filling out forms, managing files — are one of the most practical near-future applications of AI. CUA-Gym removes the biggest roadblock (lack of training data) by automating data creation, making it far easier and cheaper to build capable computer-use agents that work reliably in the real world.

Gemini Embedding 2: A Native Multimodal Embedding Model from Gemini Gemini Embedding 2：来自 Gemini 的原生多模态嵌入模型

🧑‍🔬 Madhuri Shanbhogue, Zhe Li, Shanfeng Zhang, Gustavo Hernández Ábrego et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 一个统一理解视频、音频、图像和文字的嵌入模型，全面超越专项模型
A single embedding model that understands video, audio, image, and text together, outperforming specialized models across benchmarks.
🗣️ 一句话比喻 · Plain-English Analogy就像一把万能钥匙——以前你需要为家里的门、车、储物柜、自行车锁各备一把钥匙，现在一把智能钥匙通通搞定，甚至遇到从没见过的新锁也能自己想办法开。Think of it like a universal remote control: instead of needing four separate remotes for your TV, soundbar, streaming box, and lights, one smart remote learns to control all of them perfectly — and even figures out how to handle gadgets it has never seen before.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有AI搜索和推荐系统通常需要为文字、图片、音频、视频分别训练专门的模型，就像出门旅行要带四本不同语言的词典一样麻烦。当现实内容混合了多种格式时，把这些独立模型的结果拼凑在一起既繁琐又容易出错。 AI search and recommendation systems typically need separate specialized models for each type of data — one for text, another for images, another for audio, and yet another for video. 的亮点		 • 一模型包揽全场：单个模型同时处理视频、音频、图像、文字，甚至支持任意交叉混合输入。 • 植根于Gemini大脑：直接继承谷歌Gemini基础模型的多模态理解能力，而非简单拼接适配器。 • 零样本泛化强：无需额外训练，即可在天文、生物科学、烹饪艺术等冷门领域开箱即用。 • 全面超越专项模型：在各类标准评测中击败专为单一模态设计的专用模型。 • One model for all: A single model handles video, audio, image, and text — even mixed together in any order, no extra glue code needed. • Built on Gemini's brain: Inherits the deep multimodal understanding of Google's Gemini foundation model, not just bolted-on adapters. • Strong zero-shot skills: Works out of the box on niche domains (astronomy, bioscience, culinary arts) it was never explicitly trained on. • Beats specialists: Outperforms models built specifically for each individual modality on standard benchmarks.

Key datasets include MSCOCO (image-text retrieval), Vatex (video-text retrieval), MTEB Multilingual (text retrieval across many languages), and MTEB Code (code search). This establishes a new standard for unified representation learning.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

模型以谷歌强大的多模态基础模型Gemini为出发点，Gemini本身已经能"读懂"文字、图片、音频和视频。为了将这种理解转化为嵌入向量（捕捉语义的紧凑数字指纹），团队采用大规模对比学习——训练时让相似内容的指纹互相靠近，不相关内容的指纹互相远离。训练分多阶段进行，同时覆盖多种任务，使模型学到更通用的理解能力而不局限于某一具体任务。最终，四种模态共享同一套"语义语言"。 The model starts with Gemini, Google's powerful multimodal foundation model that already "understands" text, images, audio, and video together. To turn that understanding into embeddings (compact numerical fingerprints that capture meaning), the team applies large-scale contrastive learning — a training trick where the model learns to give similar content similar fingerprints and push unrelated content apart. Training happens in multiple stages and across multiple tasks at once, so the model gets better at general understanding rather than overfitting to one narrow job. The result is a single shared "language of meaning" across all four modalities.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{R@1} = \frac{\text{Number of queries where correct item is ranked 1st}}{\text{Total queries}}\]
💡 R@1（第一名召回率）：衡量检索模型能否把最相关的结果排在第一位，分数越高越好。 R@1 (Recall at 1): measures how often the correct answer is ranked #1 by the model — higher is better.
\[\text{NDCG@}k = \frac{1}{\text{IDCG}@k} \sum_{i=1}^{k} \frac{2^{rel_i} - 1}{\log_2(i+1)}\]
💡 NDCG@k（归一化折损累积增益）：评估检索结果的排序质量，越靠前的相关结果得分越高，满分为1。 NDCG@k: a ranking quality metric that rewards putting the most relevant results near the top; perfect ranking scores 1.0.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有的AI世界模型（能够模拟游戏或虚拟环境的AI系统）基本只能处理"单人"场景——也就是说，一次只能控制一个角色。但真实世界里，游戏、机器人协作、自动驾驶等场景都涉及多个角色同时行动。如何让多个玩家或机器人同时独立操控、互不干扰，而且AI还能保持视频画面的一致性和流畅性，是一个尚未解决的难题。 Existing AI world models — systems that simulate game environments or virtual worlds — mostly only handle one player at a time. But real-world applications like multiplayer games, robot teams, and self-driving cars need multiple agents acting at the same time. Scaling these models to support several independently controlled players, without the video becoming inconsistent or slow, has been a major unsolved challenge.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 提出"单纯形旋转智能体编码"（Simplex RoPE）：让每个智能体都有独一无二的"身份标签"，但所有智能体地位平等、可互换，不需要人为规定谁是第一个、谁是第二个。 • 设计"稀疏枢纽注意力"（Sparse Hub Attention）：用少量"中转枢纽"代替智能体之间的全量通信，把计算量从平方级降低到线性级，大幅提升效率。 • 实现实时推理：通过将"全局扩散教师模型"蒸馏为"因果学生模型"，以24帧/秒的速度实时生成响应玩家操作的视频，且无需额外训练即可从2位玩家泛化到4位玩家。 • **Simplex Rotary Agent Encoding**: Each agent gets a unique "identity tag" using math (vertices of a regular simplex), so agents are distinguishable but treated equally — no fixed ordering needed. • **Sparse Hub Attention**: Instead of every agent talking to every other agent (expensive!), a small set of "hub" tokens acts as a relay station, cutting communication cost from quadratic to linear. • **Real-time distillation**: A fast student model learns from a slower, high-quality teacher model, achieving 24 FPS interactive generation that generalizes to more players without retraining.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多人虚拟环境中进行，测试场景包含2至4名同时操控的玩家。对比的基线方法包括：基于"槽位"的多智能体编码方案（固定身份槽位）和密集全连接注意力方案。评估指标涵盖视频质量（FID、FVD）、动作可控性（AC）以及智能体间一致性（SSIM、PSNR）。此外还测试了模型在未见过的玩家数量（4人）上的零样本泛化能力。 Experiments were conducted in multiplayer virtual game environments with 2 to 4 simultaneous players. The model was compared against slot-based agent encoding baselines and dense all-to-all attention baselines. Evaluation metrics included video quality (FID, FVD), action controllability (AC), and cross-agent visual consistency (SSIM, PSNR). The team also tested zero-shot generalization — whether the model trained on 2 players could scale to 4 players without any additional training.

结果		 Gamma-World 在多项指标上超越所有基线：视频质量 FVD 显著降低（更低更好），动作可控性 AC 提升，智能体间一致性（SSIM/PSNR）更高。模型在仅用2名玩家训练的情况下，零样本泛化到4名玩家时仍保持性能优势。稀疏枢纽注意力使跨智能体注意力计算复杂度从 O(N²) 降至 O(N)，实时生成速度达到 24 FPS，相比密集注意力基线在速度和质量上实现双重提升。 Gamma-World outperformed all baselines on video quality (lower FVD), action controllability (higher AC scores), and inter-agent consistency (higher SSIM and PSNR). When trained only on 2-player scenarios and tested on 4-player scenarios zero-shot, it maintained its performance advantage. The Sparse Hub Attention reduced cross-agent attention complexity from O(N²) to O(N), and the distilled model achieved real-time generation at 24 FPS — improving both speed and quality over dense-attention and slot-based baselines simultaneously.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Gamma-World 用三个关键设计解决多智能体世界建模问题。首先，它给每个智能体分配一个独特的"旋转角度相位"——就像给每位参赛者贴上颜色不同但地位相同的号码牌，这样模型既能区分谁是谁，又不会偏向某个固定的位置顺序。其次，为了避免所有智能体两两之间都进行高成本的信息交换，它引入少量可学习的"枢纽令牌"充当中转站，大幅减少计算量。最后，为了实现实时响应，它先训练一个高质量但较慢的"教师模型"，再把知识蒸馏进一个更快的"学生模型"，后者可以像流水线一样逐块生成视频帧，并借助KV缓存机制保持上下文一致性。 Gamma-World tackles multi-agent world modeling with three clever tricks. First, it assigns each agent a unique "rotational phase" in the model's encoding space — like giving each player a differently colored badge that marks them as unique but equal in rank. Second, to avoid the expensive cost of every agent communicating with every other agent, it introduces a small set of learnable "hub tokens" that act like a shared bulletin board, passing messages between agents at far lower cost. Third, for real-time play, it trains a high-quality but slow "teacher" model first, then distills its knowledge into a faster "student" model that generates video frame-by-frame using a memory cache, reaching 24 FPS interactive output.

💡 单纯形旋转智能体编码的核心公式：将第 n 个智能体的旋转角度设置为均匀分布在圆上的顶点相位，使每个智能体获得独特且对称的位置编码。 The core formula for Simplex Rotary Agent Encoding: each agent is assigned a rotational phase evenly spaced around a circle (like placing agents at the corners of a regular polygon), giving every agent a unique but symmetric identity.
\[\text{Attention Cost} : O(N^2 T^2) \rightarrow O(N T^2 + N T H)\]
💡 稀疏枢纽注意力将跨智能体的计算复杂度从智能体数量的平方级降低到线性级（H 为枢纽令牌数量），大幅节省计算资源。 Sparse Hub Attention reduces cross-agent attention cost from quadratic (N²) to linear (N) in the number of agents N, where H is the small number of hub tokens — making the system scale efficiently to more players.
