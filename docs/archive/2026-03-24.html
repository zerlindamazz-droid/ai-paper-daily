Nemotron-Cascade 2: Post-Training LLMs with Cascade RL and Multi-Domain On-Policy Distillation Nemotron-Cascade 2：基于级联强化学习与多领域在线蒸馏的大模型后训练

🧑‍🔬 Zhuolin Yang, Zihan Liu, Yang Chen, Wenliang Dai et al.

🎯 30B MoE模型用级联RL与在线蒸馏实现IMO/IOI金牌级推理能力
A 30B MoE model achieves IMO/IOI Gold Medal performance with 20x fewer parameters via Cascade RL and on-policy distillation.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 训练能够达到顶尖水平推理能力的大语言模型，通常需要数千亿参数，部署成本极高。小模型很难在数学、编程、智能体等多个推理领域同时保持强劲性能。此外，后训练阶段普遍存在"此消彼长"的基准退化问题，即提升某一领域时，其他领域往往会出现性能下滑。 Training large language models to reason at frontier-level performance typically requires hundreds of billions of parameters, making deployment expensive and inefficient. Smaller models struggle to maintain broad, consistent performance across diverse reasoning and agentic domains simultaneously. There is also a known challenge of benchmark regression — improving one domain while degrading another — during post-training.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 大幅扩展了 Cascade RL 的覆盖范围，涵盖更广泛的推理与智能体任务领域，提供更丰富的多领域训练信号。 • 创新性地引入多领域在线蒸馏（on-policy distillation）：在 Cascade RL 训练过程中，针对每个领域动态使用最强的中间教师模型进行实时指导，有效遏制基准退化。 • 以极高的"智能密度"著称——仅用3B激活参数即达到IMO、IOI、ICPC金牌级别，参数量仅为同类最优开源模型的1/20。 • Cascade RL is substantially expanded to cover a much broader spectrum of reasoning and agentic domains beyond the original framework, enabling richer multi-domain training signals. • Multi-domain on-policy distillation is introduced: the strongest intermediate teacher models for each specific domain are used to guide the student model in real-time during the Cascade RL process, efficiently recovering benchmark regressions. • Achieves remarkable "intelligence density" — Gold Medal-level performance on IMO, IOI, and ICPC with only 3B activated parameters, using 20x fewer parameters than the only other comparable open model.
🧪 实验
内容		 模型在全面的数学与编程竞赛基准上进行评估，包括2025年国际数学奥林匹克（IMO）、国际信息学奥林匹克（IOI）和ICPC世界总决赛等顶级竞赛题目。此外，还覆盖通用推理、智能体任务和指令遵循等基准。主要对比基线包括 DeepSeek-R1、QwQ，以及 DeepSeekV3.2-Special-671B-A37B（目前唯一另一个达到竞赛级性能的开源模型）。消融实验分析了多领域在线蒸馏相对于普通 Cascade RL 的具体贡献。 The model was evaluated on a comprehensive suite of math and coding competition benchmarks including the 2025 IMO, IOI, and ICPC World Finals to test elite reasoning. Additional benchmarks covered general reasoning, agentic tasks, and instruction following. The primary baseline for comparison was frontier open-weight models including DeepSeek-R1, QwQ, and notably DeepSeekV3.2-Special-671B-A37B — the only other open model to achieve comparable competition-level performance. Ablation studies examined the contribution of multi-domain on-policy distillation versus vanilla Cascade RL.
📊 实验
结果		 Nemotron-Cascade 2（30B MoE，激活3B参数）在2025年IMO、IOI和ICPC世界总决赛上全部达到金牌级别表现。它是继 DeepSeekV3.2-Special-671B-A37B（671B总参数，37B激活参数）之后，全球第二个实现这一里程碑的开源模型。Nemotron-Cascade 2 仅用约1/20的激活参数量就达到了同等竞赛水准，体现出极高的"智能密度"。同时，该模型在多领域推理和智能体基准上也展现出接近顶尖开源模型的强劲性能。 Nemotron-Cascade 2 (30B MoE, 3B activated) achieves Gold Medal-level performance on all three elite competitions: the 2025 IMO, IOI, and ICPC World Finals. It is the second open-weight model ever to achieve this milestone, after DeepSeekV3.2-Special-671B-A37B which has 671B total parameters (37B activated). Nemotron-Cascade 2 accomplishes this with 20x fewer activated parameters, demonstrating exceptionally high intelligence density. Its mathematical and coding reasoning performance approaches that of frontier open models despite its compact size, while also delivering strong agentic capabilities across multi-domain benchmarks.
🏁 结论 Nemotron-Cascade 2 证明了一个紧凑的30B MoE模型（激活3B参数）通过 Cascade RL 结合多领域在线蒸馏，可以达到顶尖的推理水平，并在IMO、IOI、ICPC等精英竞赛中取得金牌级成绩。本文确立了一个重要结论：精心设计的后训练策略能够极大地缩小小模型与大模型之间的能力差距，从根本上重新定义了参数高效模型所能达到的上限。 Nemotron-Cascade 2 demonstrates that a compact 30B MoE model (3B activated parameters) can reach frontier-level reasoning performance through Cascade RL combined with multi-domain on-policy distillation, achieving Gold Medal-level results on elite math and coding olympiads. This work establishes that intelligent post-training strategies can dramatically close the gap between small and large models, redefining what is achievable under tight parameter budgets.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

训练流程分为两大阶段：首先在精心筛选的高质量数据集上进行监督微调（SFT），为模型打下坚实基础。随后应用 Cascade RL——这是一种分阶段强化学习方法，逐步扩展到推理和智能体等更多任务领域，使用可验证的奖励信号来引导学习。关键创新在于，在 Cascade RL 的每个阶段，针对每个领域使用当前最强的中间检查点作为领域教师模型，实时生成在线示范数据，对学生模型进行蒸馏，及时修复性能退化。这一组合机制使30B MoE模型（每个token仅激活3B参数）能够在所有领域持续积累性能增益，而不会出现明显的此消彼长现象。 The training pipeline starts with Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) on a carefully curated high-quality dataset to establish a strong foundation. Then, Cascade RL is applied — a staged reinforcement learning approach that progressively expands coverage across reasoning and agentic domains, using verifiable reward signals. Critically, at each stage of Cascade RL, domain-specific teacher models (the strongest intermediate checkpoints for that domain) are used to generate on-policy demonstrations, distilling knowledge directly into the student model to fix performance regressions as they appear. This combination allows the small 30B MoE model (only 3B parameters activated per token) to steadily accumulate gains across all domains without sacrificing any single area.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Cascade RL} + \text{On-Policy Distillation} \rightarrow \mathcal{L}_{\text{total}} = \mathcal{L}_{\text{RL}} + \lambda \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{KD}}\]
💡 总训练损失由两部分组成：强化学习损失（来自可验证奖励信号）与知识蒸馏损失（来自领域教师模型的在线示范）的加权组合，λ 控制蒸馏强度。 The overall training loss combines a reinforcement learning loss (from verifiable reward signals) with a knowledge distillation loss (from domain-specific teacher model demonstrations generated on-policy), where λ balances the two objectives.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本文打破了"顶尖推理能力必须依赖超大模型"的固有假设，证明通过巧妙的训练策略（Cascade RL + 在线蒸馏），参数量减少20倍的小模型同样能达到精英级智能。对于整个机器学习和机器人研究社区而言，这意味着强大的推理能力有望在资源受限的硬件上部署，大幅降低前沿AI的使用门槛。 This paper challenges the prevailing assumption that frontier reasoning requires massive models, showing that smart training recipes (Cascade RL + on-policy distillation) can unlock elite-level intelligence in a model 20x smaller than competitors. For the ML and robotics community, this means powerful reasoning capabilities could become deployable on resource-constrained hardware, democratizing access to frontier-level AI.

Novel View Synthesis · 新视角合成3D Vision · 三维视觉Neural Rendering · 神经渲染Generative Models · 生成模型
LagerNVS: Latent Geometry for Fully Neural Real-time Novel View Synthesis LagerNVS：用于全神经实时新视角合成的潜在几何方法

🧑‍🔬 Stanislaw Szymanowicz, Minghao Chen, Jianyuan Wang, Christian Rupprecht et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用3D预训练编码器赋予神经网络几何先验，实现实时最优新视角合成。
3D-pretrained encoder gives feed-forward NVS networks strong geometry priors for real-time, state-of-the-art rendering.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 新视角合成（NVS）是指仅凭少量输入图像，生成场景在任意新视角下的图像。传统方法依赖显式三维重建（如NeRF、点云），速度慢；而最新的前馈神经网络虽然快，却放弃了显式三维建模，可能损失几何理解能力。如何在保持实时速度的同时，让网络真正"理解"三维结构，是本文要解决的核心问题。 Novel View Synthesis (NVS) — generating images of a scene from new camera angles given only a few input views — is traditionally done by building an explicit 3D model (like NeRF or point clouds). Recent feed-forward neural networks skip explicit 3D reconstruction entirely, but this risks losing important geometric understanding. The question is: can we get the best of both worlds — fast, fully neural inference with strong 3D awareness?
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **三维感知潜在特征**：编码器由显式三维监督预训练初始化，其内部特征天然携带几何语义，而非仅编码外观信息。 • **轻量解码器+端到端训练**：轻量化解码器与3D编码器联合微调，配合光度损失，在保证质量的同时实现实时推理速度。 • **高度通用**：支持已知或未知相机姿态，能泛化到真实世界数据，并可接入扩散模型解码器实现超出可见区域的生成式外推。 • **3D-Aware Latent Features**: The encoder is initialized from a network pre-trained with explicit 3D supervision, so its internal representations carry genuine geometric meaning — not just appearance statistics. • **Lightweight Decoder + End-to-End Training**: A slim decoder is paired with the 3D encoder and fine-tuned jointly with photometric losses, keeping inference fast enough for real-time rendering. • **Versatility**: Works with or without known camera poses, generalizes to in-the-wild images, and can plug in a diffusion decoder for generative extrapolation beyond visible regions.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在标准NVS基准数据集RealEstate10k（Re10k）及其他真实世界数据集上评估了LagerNVS。对比对象为当前最先进的确定性前馈NVS方法，分别测试了已知相机姿态和未知相机姿态两种设定。评估指标涵盖PSNR、SSIM和LPIPS。此外，还测试了模型在分布外真实场景的泛化能力，并通过替换扩散模型解码器展示了生成式外推变体。 The authors evaluated LagerNVS on standard NVS benchmarks including RealEstate10k (Re10k) and other in-the-wild datasets. They compared against state-of-the-art deterministic feed-forward NVS methods, testing both known-camera and unknown-camera settings. Metrics used include PSNR, SSIM, and LPIPS. They also tested generalization to out-of-distribution real-world scenes and demonstrated a generative variant by swapping in a diffusion-based decoder.
📊 实验
结果		 LagerNVS在RealEstate10k（Re10k）基准上取得**31.4 dB PSNR**，刷新了确定性前馈NVS方法的最优记录。无论在已知还是未知相机姿态设定下均保持领先性能。模型实现实时渲染（单次前向传播），在SSIM和LPIPS指标上同样优于先前方法。泛化实验表明，无需针对特定数据集微调，模型即可在真实世界数据上表现优异。 LagerNVS achieves **31.4 dB PSNR on RealEstate10k (Re10k)**, setting a new state-of-the-art among deterministic feed-forward NVS methods. It maintains this top performance in both known-camera and unknown-camera settings. The model renders in real time (single forward pass), and consistently outperforms prior methods on SSIM and LPIPS metrics as well. Generalization experiments confirm strong performance on in-the-wild data without dataset-specific fine-tuning.
🏁 结论 LagerNVS证明了在前馈NVS网络中注入三维归纳偏置（通过3D预训练编码器）是一种极为有效的设计原则，能在实时速度下实现最优图像质量（Re10k上31.4 dB PSNR）。本文说明，即便最终系统是完全隐式的端到端神经渲染，预训练阶段的显式三维监督依然具有重要价值。 LagerNVS proves that injecting 3D inductive biases into feed-forward NVS networks — via a 3D-pretrained encoder — is a highly effective design principle, yielding state-of-the-art image quality (31.4 PSNR on Re10k) at real-time speed. The work demonstrates that explicit 3D supervision at pre-training time remains valuable even when the final system performs fully implicit, end-to-end neural rendering.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

LagerNVS采用编码器-解码器架构。编码器从一个经过显式三维监督（如深度图或点云损失）预训练的3D重建网络初始化，使其潜在特征天然包含几何结构信息。轻量级解码器直接利用这些潜在特征渲染新视角，无需在推理时构建任何显式三维模型。整个系统以光度损失（像素级重建误差）进行端到端微调，将几何感知的编码器特征进一步优化为高质量图像合成能力。测试时只需一次前向传播，即可达到实时渲染速度。 LagerNVS is an encoder-decoder network where the encoder is bootstrapped from a 3D reconstruction model pre-trained with explicit 3D supervision (e.g., depth or point-cloud losses). This gives the encoder "3D-aware" latent features — internal representations that encode geometric structure, not just RGB textures. A lightweight, efficient decoder then takes these latent features and renders novel views directly, without constructing any explicit 3D representation at inference time. The whole system is fine-tuned end-to-end with photometric (pixel-level reconstruction) losses, allowing the geometry-rich encoder features to be polished for high-quality image synthesis. At test time, the network runs in a single forward pass, achieving real-time rendering speeds.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{photometric}} = \| I_{\text{pred}} - I_{\text{gt}} \|\]
💡 光度损失：衡量网络预测图像与真实图像之间的像素级差异，用于端到端微调整个编码器-解码器网络。 Photometric loss: measures pixel-level difference between the predicted novel view and the ground-truth image, used to fine-tune the full encoder-decoder end-to-end.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

本文挑战了神经渲染领域"完全抛弃三维结构"的趋势，证明几何感知预训练是提升质量和泛化能力的简单而强效的手段。对于机器人、增强现实/虚拟现实和场景理解等应用领域，无需显式三维重建的实时、可泛化NVS能力具有重要的实际价值。 This work challenges the trend of discarding 3D structure entirely in neural rendering pipelines, showing that geometry-aware pretraining is a simple yet powerful lever for boosting both quality and generalization. For the broader robotics and vision community, real-time, generalizable NVS with no explicit 3D reconstruction is a key capability for embodied agents, AR/VR, and scene understanding.

The $\mathbf{Y}$-Combinator for LLMs: Solving Long-Context Rot with $λ$-Calculus LLM的Y组合子：用λ演算解决长上下文衰退问题

🧑‍🔬 Amartya Roy, Rasul Tutunov, Xiaotong Ji, Matthieu Zimmer et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用λ演算框架替代自由递归代码，让LLM长文本推理更可靠高效
A λ-calculus runtime replaces free-form recursive code in LLMs for verifiable, efficient long-context reasoning.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 大语言模型（LLM）处理长文本时受制于固定的上下文窗口。现有的递归语言模型（RLM）虽然尝试通过让模型生成任意控制代码来递归分解问题，但这种"自由发挥"的代码生成难以验证、预测和分析。这类系统既无法保证一定会终止运行，也缺乏对准确率的理论保障。 LLMs struggle with long inputs because their context window is fixed in size. Existing Recursive Language Models (RLMs) try to solve this by having the model generate arbitrary control code to break problems into subproblems, but this free-form code generation is hard to verify, predict, or formally analyze. There are no guarantees about whether such systems will terminate or how accurate they will be.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 用基于λ演算的类型化函数式运行时替代自由递归代码生成，使用一套预先验证的组合子库。 • 提供标准RLM所不具备的形式化保证：终止性、封闭形式的代价界和随递归深度可控的精度缩放。 • 在简单代价模型下推导出最优分区规则，使分解策略具有理论依据。 • 神经推理仅用于有界的叶子子问题，让LLM始终处于安全可预测的角色中。 • Replaces open-ended recursive code generation with a typed functional runtime grounded in λ-calculus, using a compact library of pre-verified combinators. • Provides formal guarantees missing from standard RLMs: termination, closed-form cost bounds, and controlled accuracy scaling with recursion depth. • Derives an optimal partition rule under a simple cost model, making the decomposition strategy theoretically principled. • Neural inference is restricted only to bounded leaf subproblems, keeping the LLM in a safe, predictable role.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者在四个长上下文推理任务上评估了λ-RLM，涵盖文档问答、多跳推理、摘要生成和结构化信息抽取。实验使用了九个不同规模和能力层级的基础LLM。主要基线是标准的开放式RLM（基于REPL），并与直接提示等方法进行了额外比较。评估指标包括任务准确率和推理延迟。 The authors evaluated λ-RLM on four long-context reasoning tasks spanning document QA, multi-hop reasoning, summarization, and structured extraction. Nine base LLMs of varying sizes and capability tiers were tested. The primary baseline was the standard open-ended RLM (REPL-based), with additional comparisons to direct prompting approaches. Metrics included task accuracy and inference latency.
📊 实验
结果		 λ-RLM在36组模型-任务对比中的29组（9个模型×4个任务）中优于标准RLM。各模型层级的平均准确率最高提升+21.9个百分点。推理延迟最高降低4.1倍。这些收益在不同模型规模和能力层级上均保持一致。 λ-RLM outperforms standard RLM in 29 out of 36 model-task comparisons (across 9 models × 4 tasks). Average accuracy improves by up to +21.9 percentage points across model tiers. Inference latency is reduced by up to 4.1×. These gains hold consistently across different model sizes and capability levels.
🏁 结论 λ-RLM证明，用类型化λ演算运行时替代自由递归代码生成，既能获得形式化保证（终止性、代价界、精度缩放），又能在准确率和延迟上取得显著的实证提升。类型化符号控制比开放式递归代码生成更可靠、更高效，是长上下文LLM推理的坚实基础。 λ-RLM demonstrates that replacing free-form recursive code generation with a typed λ-calculus runtime yields both formal guarantees (termination, cost bounds, accuracy scaling) and strong empirical gains in accuracy and latency. Typed symbolic control is a more reliable and efficient foundation for long-context LLM reasoning than open-ended recursive code generation.
🔗 论文
链接		 arXiv: 2603.20105v1  ·  📥 PDF

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

λ-RLM将LLM推理嵌入一个结构化的λ演算运行时中。框架不再让模型自行编写任意递归程序，而是提供一套经过预先验证的高阶组合子（如map、fold和用于递归的Y组合子）来负责所有控制流。LLM只被调用来解决小而有界的叶子子问题，而组合子库负责分解、递归和聚合。这种符号控制与神经推理的分离，使系统能够继承λ演算的数学性质，如终止性保证和可组合的代价分析。 λ-RLM wraps LLM inference inside a structured λ-calculus runtime. Instead of asking the model to write arbitrary recursive programs, the framework provides a small set of pre-verified higher-order combinators (like map, fold, and the Y-combinator for recursion) that handle all control flow. The LLM is only called to solve small, bounded leaf subproblems, while the combinator library manages decomposition, recursion, and aggregation. This separation of symbolic control and neural inference allows the system to inherit the mathematical properties of λ-calculus, such as guaranteed termination and composable cost analysis.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[f = Y(\lambda f . \lambda x . \text{if } \text{base}(x) \text{ then } \text{solve}(x) \text{ else } \text{combine}(f, \text{split}(x)))\]
💡 Y组合子将递归函数定义为不动点：若输入x是基本情况则直接求解，否则分解x后递归求解再合并结果。这是λ-RLM控制递归分解的核心机制。 The Y-combinator expresses a recursive function as a fixed point: if the input x is a base case, solve it directly; otherwise split x, recurse, and combine results. This is the core mechanism λ-RLM uses to control recursive decomposition.
\[\text{Cost}(n) = c_{\text{leaf}} \cdot k + c_{\text{combine}} \cdot (k-1), \quad n = k \cdot m\]
💡 总代价等于k个叶子子问题的求解代价加上(k-1)次合并操作的代价，其中n为总输入长度，m为每个子问题的长度。这给出了封闭形式的代价界。 Total cost equals the cost of solving k leaf subproblems plus the cost of (k-1) combine operations, where n is total input length and m is subproblem length. This yields the closed-form cost bound central to the paper's theoretical guarantees.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

作者提出Hyperagent框架：将任务智能体（负责解决目标问题）与元智能体（负责修改自身和任务智能体）整合进同一个可编辑程序。关键创新在于，元级修改流程本身也是可编辑的，从而允许对"改进机制"进行改进。具体实现为DGM-H：在每轮迭代中，元智能体提出并评估自我修改的变体，保留表现最好的版本。由于元智能体代码本身可被修改，系统能有机地发现更好的搜索策略、记忆结构或评估启发式方法，无需工程师预先注入特定领域假设。 The authors introduce the Hyperagent framework, where a single editable program contains both a task agent (solves the target problem) and a meta agent (modifies the task agent and itself). Crucially, the meta-level modification procedure is itself part of the editable program, so improvements to the improvement process are possible. They instantiate this as DGM-Hyperagents (DGM-H) by extending the Darwin Gödel Machine: at each iteration, the meta agent proposes and evaluates self-modified variants, and the best-performing variants are carried forward. Because the meta-agent code is editable, the system can discover better search strategies, memory structures, or evaluation heuristics organically, without domain-specific assumptions baked in by engineers.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{HyperAgent} = \langle \underbrace{f_{\text{task}}(\cdot)}_\text{task agent} ,\; \underbrace{f_{\text{meta}}(f_{\text{task}}, f_{\text{meta}})}_\text{meta agent (self-referential)} \rangle\]
💡 超级智能体由任务智能体与元智能体组成，关键在于元智能体的输入包含其自身——即f_meta可以修改自己，实现自指性。 A Hyperagent is a pair of a task agent and a meta agent, where the meta agent takes both agents as input, making it self-referential and able to rewrite its own modification procedure.
\[f_{\text{meta}}^{(t+1)} \leftarrow f_{\text{meta}}^{(t)}\bigl(f_{\text{task}}^{(t)},\; f_{\text{meta}}^{(t)}\bigr)\]
💡 在每轮迭代t中，元智能体通过修改自身和任务智能体来生成下一代元智能体，形成自我改进的递归循环。 At each iteration t, the meta agent produces the next generation of itself by taking both the current task agent and itself as inputs — formalizing the recursive self-improvement loop.

本文将AI自我改进的边界从固定的元学习循环推进到真正开放式的元认知系统，是自主AI自举能力方式的根本性转变。若该框架在更大规模下得到验证，Hyperagents有望大幅降低AI开发中的人工工程负担，使系统能在任意领域自主发现并精炼自身的训练与问题求解策略。 This paper pushes the boundary of AI self-improvement from fixed meta-learning loops to truly open-ended metacognitive systems, a fundamental shift in how autonomous AI can bootstrap its own capabilities.
