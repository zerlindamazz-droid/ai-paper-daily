Dependency-Aware Parallel Decoding via Attention for Diffusion LLMs 基于注意力的依赖感知并行解码用于扩散大语言模型

🧑‍🔬 Bumjun Kim, Dongjae Jeon, Moongyu Jeon, Albert No

图 Figure · Page 7 of paper

🎯 无需训练，利用注意力图避免强耦合token同步解码
Training-free parallel decoding for diffusion LLMs using attention to avoid co-updating strongly dependent tokens.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

扩散大语言模型（dLLMs）在生成文本时，每一步去噪只能得到每个token的独立概率分布，但同时解码多个token时，token之间存在相互依赖关系。如果忽略这些依赖关系强行并行解码，会导致生成质量下降。现有方法要么需要额外训练辅助模型，要么无法有效识别哪些token之间存在强关联。 Diffusion LLMs (dLLMs) generate text by iteratively unmasking tokens, but each denoising step only provides independent per-token probability distributions. When multiple tokens are unmasked in parallel, their mutual dependencies are ignored, degrading output quality. Existing approaches either require retraining auxiliary models or fail to identify which token pairs are strongly coupled and should not be updated simultaneously.

⚙️ Method · 方法

DAPD 方法利用模型自身的自注意力权重，在每次迭代中构建一个有条件依赖图，图中的边代表两个被遮蔽token之间存在强依赖关系。算法在该图上寻找一个"独立集"——即一组彼此之间没有强依赖边的token，然后只对这组token进行并行解码。这样既保留了并行加速的优势，又避免了强耦合token被同时更新所带来的误差传播。整个过程完全无需额外训练或辅助模型。 DAPD leverages the model's own self-attention weights to construct a conditional dependency graph over all currently masked tokens at each decoding iteration. Edges in the graph represent strong inter-token dependencies, while absent edges indicate weak coupling. The algorithm then selects an independent set on this graph — a subset of tokens with no strong mutual dependencies — and unmasks only those tokens in parallel. This approach requires no retraining or auxiliary models, making it plug-and-play on top of existing dLLMs like LLaDA and Dream.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[G_t = (V_t, E_t), \quad E_t = \{(i,j) : A_{ij}^{(t)} > \tau\}\]
💡 依赖图的构建方式：节点集合为当前所有被遮蔽的token，若两个token之间的自注意力权重超过阈值τ，则在它们之间连一条边，表示强依赖关系。 The dependency graph G at step t is built over masked tokens V_t; an edge is drawn between tokens i and j if their mutual self-attention weight exceeds threshold τ, indicating strong inter-token dependency.
\[S_t = \text{IndependentSet}(G_t)\]
💡 并行解码的token集合 S_t 是依赖图上的一个独立集，即集合中任意两个token之间均不存在强依赖边，从而可以安全地同步解码。 The set of tokens S_t selected for parallel unmasking is an independent set of the dependency graph, ensuring no two simultaneously decoded tokens share a strong dependency edge.

📊 Results · 结果

在 LLaDA 和 Dream 两个扩散大语言模型上的实验表明，DAPD 在相同解码步数下相比现有并行解码方法显著提升了准确率，改善了"准确率-步数"的权衡曲线。DAPD 还实现了更全局分散的并行更新，更充分地利用了 dLLMs 任意顺序生成的能力，而不是像传统方法那样集中更新局部相邻token。论文报告了在多个基准任务上的一致性提升，具体数字因任务而异，整体上在减少解码步数的同时维持或提高了生成质量。 Experiments on LLaDA and Dream demonstrate that DAPD consistently improves the accuracy-steps trade-off compared to existing parallel decoding baselines. DAPD produces more globally distributed parallel updates across the sequence, better exploiting the any-order generation capability inherent to dLLMs. Across multiple benchmark tasks, DAPD achieves higher accuracy at the same number of decoding steps, or equivalently reaches target accuracy with fewer steps than competing methods — all without any additional training overhead.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

扩散语言模型是自回归模型之外一条极具潜力的生成路线，但其推理速度长期受限于串行解码。DAPD 提供了一种即插即用的加速方案，有望降低 dLLMs 的部署成本，推动其在实际应用中的落地。 Diffusion LLMs represent a promising alternative to autoregressive models, but their inference speed has been a key bottleneck. DAPD offers a plug-and-play acceleration strategy that could significantly lower the deployment cost of dLLMs, making them more competitive with autoregressive counterparts in real-world applications.

When Right Meets Wrong: Bilateral Context Conditioning with Reward-Confidence Correction for GRPO 当正确遇到错误：面向GRPO的双边上下文条件化与奖励置信度校正

🧑‍🔬 Yu Li, Tian Lan, Zhengling Qi

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 通过对比正误推理轨迹增强GRPO训练效果
Enhances GRPO training by explicitly contrasting correct and incorrect reasoning traces with a dynamic baseline correction.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

现有的强化学习训练方法GRPO在训练推理模型时，将每个生成结果视为独立样本，忽视了同一组内正确与错误答案之间天然存在的对比信息。这意味着模型无法直接从"哪里做对了、哪里做错了"的对比中学习，浪费了宝贵的训练信号。 GRPO, a popular reinforcement learning method for training reasoning models, treats each generated output independently during optimization, missing the natural contrast between correct and incorrect solutions within the same group. This means the model cannot directly learn from comparing successful and failed reasoning traces side by side, leaving valuable training signal on the table.

⚙️ Method · 方法

本文首先从理论上证明GRPO目标隐式地最大化正确与错误样本策略比率之间的差距。基于此，提出**双边上下文条件化（BICC）**机制，让模型在优化时能够交叉参考成功与失败的推理轨迹，实现样本间的直接信息流动。同时提出**奖励置信度校正（RCC）**，利用奖励与置信度协方差（基于方差最小化估计量的一阶近似）动态调整优势基线，以稳定训练过程。两种机制均无需额外采样或辅助模型，可适配所有GRPO变体。 The authors first provide a theoretical reformulation showing that the GRPO objective implicitly maximizes the margin between policy ratios of correct and incorrect samples. They then introduce Bilateral Context Conditioning (BICC), which lets the model cross-reference successful and failed reasoning traces during optimization, creating a direct information flow between samples in the same group. They also introduce Reward-Confidence Correction (RCC), which dynamically adjusts the advantage baseline using the covariance between reward and model confidence, derived from a first-order approximation of a variance-minimizing estimator. Both methods are lightweight, requiring no extra sampling or auxiliary models, and plug into any GRPO variant.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{GRPO}} \propto -\mathbb{E}\left[\sum_{i} A_i \cdot \frac{\pi_\theta(o_i|q)}{\pi_{\text{old}}(o_i|q)}\right]\]
💡 GRPO的基础目标函数：对组内每个输出 $o_i$，用优势值 $A_i$ 加权其新旧策略的概率比，并最大化期望累计优势。 The core GRPO objective: for each output in a group, weight the ratio of new-to-old policy probabilities by its advantage value, then maximize the expected sum — encouraging the model to increase probability on outputs that scored above the group mean.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{GRPO}} \propto -\mathbb{E}\left[\sum_{i^+ , j^-} \left(\frac{\pi_\theta(o_{i^+}|q)}{\pi_{\text{old}}(o_{i^+}|q)} - \frac{\pi_\theta(o_{j^-}|q)}{\pi_{\text{old}}(o_{j^-}|q)}\right)\right]\]
💡 GRPO的对比重构形式：显式地最大化正确样本 $o_{i^+}$ 与错误样本 $o_{j^-}$ 策略比率之间的差距，揭示了GRPO内在的对比学习结构。 The contrastive reformulation of GRPO: the objective explicitly maximizes the margin between policy ratios of correct samples and incorrect samples, revealing the implicit contrastive structure already present in standard GRPO.

📊 Results · 结果

在数学推理基准测试上，BICC与RCC对多种模型和GRPO变体均带来了一致性提升。结合两种机制后，在综合数学基准（如AIME、AMC、MATH等）上相较标准GRPO基线取得了持续改进，验证了方法的广泛适用性。代码已开源于GitHub（https://github.com/Skylanding/BiCC）。（注：论文摘要未披露具体数值，详见正文实验部分。） Experiments on mathematical reasoning benchmarks show consistent improvements across multiple model architectures and GRPO algorithm variants when combining BICC and RCC. The gains are demonstrated on standard benchmarks including AIME, AMC, and MATH datasets over strong GRPO baselines. The paper reports these improvements hold comprehensively across all tested configurations. (Note: The abstract does not disclose specific numerical results; precise figures are reported in the main experimental section.)

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

该研究揭示了GRPO中被忽视的对比学习结构，为强化学习驱动的推理模型训练提供了更高效的信号利用方式。两种即插即用的轻量化机制为后续GRPO变体研究提供了实用且理论扎实的改进基础。 This paper uncovers a previously overlooked contrastive learning structure within GRPO, offering a more efficient way to exploit training signals in RL-based reasoning model training. Its two plug-and-play mechanisms provide a theoretically grounded and practically lightweight foundation for improving the entire family of GRPO-based algorithms.

Mending the Holes: Mitigating Reward Hacking in Reinforcement Learning for Multilingual Translation 修补漏洞：缓解多语言翻译强化学习中的奖励黑客问题

🧑‍🔬 Yifeng Liu, Siqi Ouyang, Yatish Hosmane Revanasiddappa, Lei Li

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 仅用单语数据的强化学习大幅提升低资源语言机器翻译质量
WALAR uses monolingual-only RL training to dramatically improve LLM translation across 101 low-resource languages.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

大型语言模型在低资源语言（如非洲、东南亚小语种）上的翻译质量远不如高资源语言（如英语、中文）。现有的训练方法依赖大量高质量双语平行数据，而这类数据对低资源语言来说极为稀缺甚至根本不存在。 LLMs perform poorly on low-resource language translation because existing post-training methods require high-quality parallel bilingual data, which is scarce or unavailable for most low-resource languages. This creates a persistent performance gap that standard fine-tuning approaches cannot easily bridge.

⚙️ Method · 方法

WALAR 使用强化学习（RL）训练，以多语言质量估计（QE）模型作为奖励信号，全程无需任何双语平行数据，只用单语文本。研究者发现现有 QE 模型存在"漏洞"——即某些失败模式（如语言混淆、词序错误）会被 RL 系统利用并放大，导致模型越训越差。为此，他们引入词对齐（word alignment）和语言对齐（language alignment）两项技术来修补奖励信号中的漏洞，防止模型钻空子。最终在支持 101 种语言翻译的 LLM 上持续训练，覆盖 1400 个翻译方向。 WALAR applies reinforcement learning using a multilingual quality estimation (QE) model as the reward signal, requiring only monolingual text rather than parallel bilingual data. The authors identified critical "holes" — failure modes in existing source-based QE models that RL exploits and amplifies, degrading translation quality. To fix these, they developed two corrective techniques: word alignment (ensuring translated words correspond correctly to source words) and language alignment (ensuring the output is actually in the target language). These patched reward signals guide RL training across 101 languages and 1,400 translation directions.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[r(y) = \text{QE}(x, y) + \lambda_1 \cdot \text{WordAlign}(x, y) + \lambda_2 \cdot \text{LangAlign}(y, l_t)\]
💡 WALAR 的奖励函数由三部分组成：基础质量估计得分、词对齐惩罚项（防止翻译词汇错位）和语言对齐惩罚项（防止输出语言错误），后两项用于修补 QE 模型的漏洞。 WALAR's reward combines a base QE score with two alignment penalties: word alignment (penalizing mistranslated or mismatched tokens) and language alignment (penalizing output in the wrong language), weighted by λ₁ and λ₂ to patch reward hacking holes.

📊 Results · 结果

WALAR 训练的模型在 Flores-101 数据集的 1400 个翻译方向上显著优于目前最强的开源多语言大模型之一 LLaMAX，取得了大幅领先。模型同时保持了在高资源语言上的原有翻译性能，实现了低资源语言提升与高资源语言保持的双重目标。 The WALAR-trained model outperforms LLaMAX — one of the strongest open-source multilingual LLMs — by a large margin across all 1,400 language directions on the Flores-101 benchmark. Crucially, performance on high-resource language pairs is retained while low-resource language translation is substantially improved, demonstrating that the reward-hacking mitigations are effective without introducing regressions.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

全球约有 7000 种语言，但绝大多数缺乏足够的双语数据，这使得 AI 翻译长期偏向少数主流语言。WALAR 提供了一条仅靠单语数据就能提升翻译能力的新路径，有望打破数据壁垒，让更多小语种使用者受益于高质量 AI 翻译。 Most of the world's 7,000+ languages lack sufficient parallel data, meaning AI translation advances have been deeply unequal. WALAR's monolingual-only RL approach offers a scalable path to closing this gap, with direct implications for digital inclusion and cross-lingual AI accessibility for underrepresented language communities.
