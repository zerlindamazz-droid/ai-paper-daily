Not All Features Are Created Equal: A Mechanistic Study of Vision-Language-Action Models 并非所有特征生而平等：视觉-语言-动作模型的机制研究

🧑‍🔬 Bryce Grant, Xijia Zhao, Peng Wang

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 揭示VLA模型中视觉、语言与动作的内部机制分工
A mechanistic study revealing how vision, language, and action pathways specialize inside VLA robot models.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型能让机器人同时理解图像、语言指令并执行动作，但我们对其内部如何将多模态输入转化为具体动作知之甚少。就像一台精密机器，我们只知道它能运转，却不清楚每个零件各自在做什么。这种"黑箱"状态阻碍了我们改进、调试和安全部署机器人系统。 VLA models unify perception, language understanding, and motor control in a single neural network, but the internal mechanisms driving their decisions are opaque. It is unclear whether these models truly understand task instructions or rely heavily on visual shortcuts, and how different internal pathways contribute to action generation. This lack of interpretability makes it hard to debug failures, improve robustness, or deploy such systems safely.

⚙️ Method · 方法

研究团队使用三种"机制探针"工具对六个VLA模型（参数量从8000万到70亿不等）进行了大规模解剖实验，共运行了超过39.4万次机器人轨迹。**激活注入**：将一个场景的内部神经激活值强行植入另一个场景，观察机器人行为如何变化，从而判断哪条通路控制动作。**稀疏自编码器（SAE）**：将模型的高维激活分解为可解释的概念特征，最终识别出82个以上的操作概念。**线性探针**：训练简单分类器来检测激活空间中是否编码了特定信息（如任务类型、目标位置等），以此验证各通路的功能分工。 The researchers applied three interpretability tools to six VLA models ranging from 80M to 7B parameters, evaluated over 394,000+ rollout episodes across four benchmarks. First, activation injection transplants internal neural activations from one context into another to reveal which pathways drive behavior. Second, sparse autoencoders (SAEs) decompose high-dimensional activations into interpretable concept features, recovering 82+ manipulation concepts via contrastive identification. Third, linear probes train lightweight classifiers on activations to test what information is encoded where, confirming functional specialization across expert and VLM pathways in multi-pathway architectures.

📊 Results · 结果

**视觉通路主导动作生成**：向空提示（无语言指令）的回合中注入视觉激活，几乎可以完全恢复原始行为；跨任务注入时，99.8%的X-VLA回合轨迹与源任务对齐，说明动作程序与场景坐标紧密绑定。**语言的作用取决于任务结构**：当视觉上下文已能唯一确定任务时，语言指令被模型忽略；而当多个目标共享同一场景时，语言变得至关重要——X-VLA在`libero_goal`任务中，错误提示使成功率从94%骤降至10%；但在`libero_object`任务中，无论提示是否正确，成功率维持在60–100%。**专家通路 vs. VLM通路**：在三个多通路架构（π½、SmolVLA、GR00T）中，专家通路编码运动程序，VLM通路编码目标语义；注入专家通路激活产生的行为位移是注入VLM通路的**2倍**。**表征广度与因果效应无关**：因果消融实验显示，零效应率（即修改某特征对行为无影响的比例）跨模型差异为28–92%，与模型表征宽度无关。 Visual pathways dominate action generation: injecting baseline visual activations into null-prompt episodes recovers near-identical behavior, and cross-task injection causes 99.8% of X-VLA episodes to align with the source trajectory. Language sensitivity is task-dependent: wrong prompts drop X-VLA success from 94% to 10% on libero_goal (multi-goal scene), but have no effect on libero_object (60–100% success regardless of prompt). In all three multi-pathway architectures (π½, SmolVLA, GR00T), expert pathways encode motor programs while VLM pathways encode goal semantics, with expert injection producing 2× greater behavioral displacement. Contrastive SAE analysis recovers 82+ manipulation concepts. Causal ablation reveals zero-effect rates of 28–92% across models, independent of representation width. Per-token SAE processing is essential for action fidelity on most architectures, though mean-pooling improves fidelity specifically on X-VLA.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

这项研究首次系统地揭示了VLA模型内部的功能分工，打破了"语言指令驱动机器人动作"的直觉假设，证明视觉场景才是主要驱动力。研究还发布了交互式工具平台Action Atlas，为机器人领域的可解释性研究提供了重要基础设施，有助于未来更安全、更可靠地设计和调试机器人系统。 This work challenges the intuitive assumption that language instructions primarily drive robot behavior in VLA models, revealing instead that visual scene representations are the dominant control signal—a critical safety and reliability insight. By releasing the Action Atlas platform for interactive exploration of VLA internals across six models, it also provides the community with essential infrastructure for interpretability research in embodied AI.

Optimal Splitting of Language Models from Mixtures to Specialized Domains 语言模型的最优分割：从混合数据到专业领域

🧑‍🔬 Skyler Seto, Pierre Ablin, Anastasiia Filippova, Jiayuan Ye et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用缩放定律优化通用预训练与专业微调的计算资源分配
Scaling laws optimally split compute between general pretraining and domain specialization for language models.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

大型语言模型通常先在海量通用数据上预训练，再在特定领域数据上继续训练以实现专业化。然而，如何在这两个阶段之间合理分配有限的计算资源，目前缺乏系统性的理论指导。在多领域场景下，为每个领域单独训练模型（"分割模型训练"）的计算成本极高，且最优的预训练与专业化训练的比例难以确定。 Large language models are typically pretrained on massive general corpora and then specialized on domain-specific data, but there is no principled way to decide how much compute to allocate to each stage. In multi-domain settings, training separate specialized models is computationally expensive, and the optimal balance between general pretraining and continued specialization training is unknown. Without this guidance, practitioners either over-invest in pretraining or under-invest in specialization, leaving performance on the table.

⚙️ Method · 方法

本文提出一种基于缩放定律的方法，对模型规模为 N、通用预训练使用 D 个 token、专业化继续预训练使用 D' 个 token 的场景，精确预测最终损失。研究者首先独立地在通用语料上预训练多个模型，然后利用拟合的缩放定律预测不同计算分配方案下的专业化损失，从而找到最优的 D 与 D' 分配比例。该方法还能外推到更大的模型规模和更多的训练 token 数，无需为每种配置单独做大规模实验。 The authors develop a scaling law that accurately predicts the loss of a model of size N trained on D general pretraining tokens followed by D' specialization tokens. Multiple models are first pretrained independently on a general corpus, and the fitted scaling law is then used to predict loss across different compute-allocation strategies without running every experiment. This allows the method to identify the optimal split between pretraining and continued pretraining budgets analytically, and it extrapolates reliably to larger model sizes and token counts not seen during fitting.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\mathcal{L}(N, D, D') = f(N, D, D')\]
💡 核心缩放定律：预测规模为 N 的模型在使用 D 个通用预训练 token 和 D' 个专业化 token 后的损失值，是本文方法的核心公式。 The core scaling law: predicts the loss of a model of size N after training on D general pretraining tokens followed by D' specialization tokens. This formula is the foundation for optimizing the compute split.
\[D^*, D'^* = \arg\min_{D + D' = C} \mathcal{L}(N, D, D')\]
💡 最优分配问题：在总计算预算 C 固定的前提下，寻找使最终损失最小的通用预训练 token 数 D 与专业化 token 数 D' 的最优组合。 The optimal allocation problem: given a fixed total compute budget C, find the best split between general pretraining tokens D and specialization tokens D' that minimizes the predicted loss.

📊 Results · 结果

该方法在常识知识与推理基准测试中，跨不同模型规模和计算预算均取得一致性提升。所提出的缩放定律能够准确预测不同模型大小与训练 token 数下的损失，并成功外推到未见过的更大规模配置。与标准两阶段训练范式相比，在相同计算预算下，采用最优计算分配策略的分割模型训练在多个下游基准上表现更优，验证了该方法在实际多领域语言模型训练中的有效性。 The proposed method consistently improves performance across common sense knowledge and reasoning benchmarks for multiple model sizes and compute budgets. The scaling law accurately predicts loss as a function of model size N, pretraining tokens D, and specialization tokens D', and successfully extrapolates to larger model sizes and token counts beyond the fitting regime. Compared to standard two-stage training without optimized compute allocation, the approach achieves better downstream benchmark results under the same total compute budget, demonstrating practical gains in multi-domain split model training scenarios.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着语言模型被广泛部署于医疗、法律、科学等专业领域，如何高效地进行领域专业化训练变得至关重要。本文提供了一套可量化、可外推的理论框架，帮助从业者在不浪费算力的前提下，为每个专业领域找到最优的训练资源分配方案。 As language models are increasingly deployed in specialized domains like medicine, law, and science, efficiently allocating compute for domain specialization is a critical practical challenge. This paper provides a principled, extrapolatable framework that enables practitioners to determine the optimal pretraining-to-specialization compute ratio without exhaustive experimentation, potentially saving significant resources in large-scale model development.

FASTER: Rethinking Real-Time Flow VLAs FASTER：重新思考实时流式视觉-语言-动作模型

🧑‍🔬 Yuxiang Lu, Zhe Liu, Xianzhe Fan, Zhenya Yang et al.

图 Figure · Page 5 of paper

🎯 单步压缩即时反应采样，大幅降低VLA机器人反应延迟
FASTER cuts VLA robot reaction latency tenfold by compressing immediate-action flow sampling into a single step.

❓ Problem · 解决什么问题

将视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型部署到真实机器人时，系统必须快速响应环境变化，但现有方法主要优化轨迹平滑性，忽视了反应延迟这一关键瓶颈。基于流（flow-based）的VLA模型在开始执行任何动作之前，必须完成所有采样步骤，导致反应时间过长，在动态任务中尤为致命。 Deploying VLA models on real robots requires fast responses to dynamic environments, but existing asynchronous inference methods focus on trajectory smoothness while ignoring reaction latency. Flow-based VLAs use a constant sampling schedule that forces the system to complete all denoising steps before any movement can begin, creating a critical bottleneck in real-time responsiveness.

⚙️ Method · 方法

论文首先对动作分块策略中的反应时间进行系统分析，证明反应时间服从由"首次动作时间（TTFA）"和执行时域共同决定的均匀分布。在此基础上，提出FASTER方法，核心是"时域感知调度（Horizon-Aware Schedule）"：在流式采样过程中，自适应地优先对近期动作去噪，将即时反应动作的去噪压缩至仅需一步，同时保留长时域轨迹的生成质量。配合流式客户端-服务器推理流水线，进一步降低端到端延迟，尤其在消费级GPU上效果显著。 The paper first provides a systematic theoretical analysis showing that reaction time follows a uniform distribution jointly governed by Time to First Action (TTFA) and the execution horizon. FASTER then introduces a Horizon-Aware Schedule that adaptively prioritizes near-term actions during flow sampling, compressing the denoising of the immediate reaction action into a single step while maintaining long-horizon trajectory quality. This is paired with a streaming client-server pipeline to further reduce end-to-end latency, particularly on consumer-grade GPUs.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[t_{\text{reaction}} \sim \mathcal{U}(\text{TTFA},\ \text{TTFA} + H_{\text{exec}})\]
💡 反应时间服从均匀分布，其下界为首次动作时间（TTFA），上界为TTFA加上执行时域长度，揭示了降低反应延迟需同时缩短TTFA和执行时域。 Reaction time follows a uniform distribution bounded by the Time to First Action (TTFA) and TTFA plus the execution horizon length, showing that reducing reaction latency requires minimizing both TTFA and the execution horizon.
\[\bar{t}_{\text{reaction}} = \text{TTFA} + \frac{H_{\text{exec}}}{2}\]
💡 平均反应时间等于TTFA加上执行时域的一半，直接量化了两个因素对系统平均响应速度的贡献。 The mean reaction time equals TTFA plus half the execution horizon, directly quantifying how both factors contribute to the system's average response speed.

📊 Results · 结果

FASTER将即时反应动作的去噪步骤压缩了十倍（如在π₀.₅和X-VLA模型中，从多步压缩至单步）。在包含高度动态乒乓球任务的真实机器人实验中，FASTER显著降低了有效反应延迟，在消费级GPU上部署时效果尤为突出，并实现了准确、平滑轨迹的快速生成，解锁了通用策略前所未有的实时响应能力。 FASTER compresses the denoising of immediate-reaction actions by tenfold in flow-based VLAs such as π₀.₅ and X-VLA, reducing it from multiple steps to a single step. Real-world robot experiments, including a highly dynamic table tennis task, demonstrate substantially reduced effective reaction latency on consumer-grade GPUs, while still generating accurate and smooth long-horizon trajectories — unlocking unprecedented real-time responsiveness for generalist robot policies.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

实时响应能力是机器人在动态真实环境中可靠工作的基础，FASTER为通用VLA模型提供了一条无需昂贵硬件即可实现低延迟部署的实用路径。该工作填补了VLA研究中对反应延迟系统性分析的空白，为未来流式生成机器人策略的设计提供了重要理论和工程参考。 Real-time reactivity is fundamental for deploying robots in dynamic, unstructured environments, and FASTER provides a practical path to low-latency VLA deployment without requiring high-end hardware. This work fills a critical gap by offering a systematic theoretical framework for reaction latency in action-chunking policies, guiding future design of responsive generalist robot systems.
