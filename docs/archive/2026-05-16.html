SANA-WM: Efficient Minute-Scale World Modeling with Hybrid Linear Diffusion Transformer SANA-WM：基于混合线性扩散变换器的高效分钟级世界建模

🧑‍🔬 Haoyi Zhu, Haozhe Liu, Yuyang Zhao, Tian Ye et al.

图 Figure · Page 4 of paper

🎯 2.6B参数开源世界模型，36倍吞吐量生成60秒720p视频
A 2.6B open-source world model generating 60-second 720p videos with camera control at 36× higher throughput than prior baselines.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 生成分钟级、高分辨率且带精准相机控制的视频极其昂贵，现有世界模型要么需要巨大算力，要么难以在长序列中保持时序一致性和动作跟随精度。开源方案在质量和效率上都远落后于工业系统，同时还缺乏从公开视频中提取高质量、度量尺度6自由度相机位姿标注的有效方法。 Generating minute-long, high-resolution videos with precise camera control is extremely expensive — existing world models either require massive compute budgets or fail to maintain temporal consistency and accurate action-following over long sequences. Open-source alternatives lag far behind industrial systems in both quality and efficiency. There is also a lack of high-quality, metric-scale 6-DoF camera pose annotations from public video data to train such models.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • 混合线性注意力：将帧级门控DeltaNet（线性注意力）与Softmax注意力融合，实现无二次复杂度的长上下文高效建模。 • 双分支相机控制：专用双分支模块在整个分钟级生成过程中精确执行6自由度轨迹约束。 • 两阶段生成流水线：长视频精炼器对第一阶段输出进行后处理，显著提升整体质量和时序一致性。 • 鲁棒标注流水线：从公开网络视频中自动提取度量尺度6自由度相机位姿，仅用约21.3万视频片段即可完成训练。 • Hybrid Linear Attention: Combines frame-wise Gated DeltaNet (linear attention) with softmax attention, enabling memory-efficient long-context video modeling without quadratic complexity. • Dual-Branch Camera Control: A dedicated dual-branch module enforces precise 6-DoF camera trajectory adherence throughout the full minute-long generation. • Two-Stage Generation Pipeline: A long-video refiner post-processes stage-1 outputs to improve quality and temporal consistency across the entire sequence. • Robust Annotation Pipeline: Automatically extracts metric-scale 6-DoF camera poses from public internet videos, enabling training on ~213K clips without expensive proprietary data.
🧪 实验
内容		 作者构建了一个分钟级世界模型基准，评估动作跟随精度和视觉质量，对比对象包括开源基线和工业基线（LingBot-World、HY-WorldPlay）。评估指标涵盖视觉保真度和相机轨迹跟随精度。效率基准测量了训练成本（GPU天数）、数据规模（约21.3万公开片段）及推理速度（单GPU生成60秒片段的秒数），并测试了在RTX 5090上采用NVFP4量化部署的蒸馏版本。 The authors built a one-minute world-model benchmark to evaluate action-following accuracy and visual quality. They compared SANA-WM against open-source and industrial baselines including LingBot-World and HY-WorldPlay, evaluating metrics such as visual fidelity and camera trajectory adherence. Efficiency benchmarks measured training cost (GPU-days), data size (~213K public clips), and inference speed (seconds per 60s clip on a single GPU), including a distilled variant with NVFP4 quantization deployed on a single RTX 5090.
📊 实验
结果		 SANA-WM在视觉质量上与大规模工业模型（LingBot-World、HY-WorldPlay）相当，同时实现36倍更高吞吐量。在分钟级世界模型基准上，其动作跟随精度超越所有开源基线。仅用约21.3万公开视频片段，在64块H100上训练15天完成。蒸馏版本在单张RTX 5090上配合NVFP4量化，34秒内即可生成一段60秒720p视频，使消费级硬件上的分钟级世界建模成为可能。 SANA-WM achieves visual quality comparable to large-scale industrial models (LingBot-World, HY-WorldPlay) while delivering 36× higher throughput. It outperforms prior open-source baselines in action-following accuracy on the one-minute world-model benchmark. Training completes in 15 days on 64 H100 GPUs using only ~213K public video clips. The distilled variant generates a 60-second 720p clip in just 34 seconds on a single RTX 5090 with NVFP4 quantization — making minute-scale world modeling feasible on consumer-grade hardware.
🏁 结论 SANA-WM证明，通过精心设计的混合线性扩散变换器，仅利用公开数据和适度算力即可实现高效、分钟级、高分辨率的世界建模，显著缩小了开源与工业世界模型之间的差距，同时大幅提升推理效率。 SANA-WM demonstrates that efficient, minute-scale, high-resolution world modeling is achievable with a carefully designed hybrid linear diffusion transformer, using only public data and modest compute. It closes the gap between open-source and industrial world models while dramatically improving inference efficiency.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

SANA-WM基于混合线性扩散变换器架构。注意力机制交替使用帧级门控DeltaNet线性注意力和标准Softmax注意力，在保持质量的同时降低内存开销。相机控制通过双分支模块注入，以6自由度位姿轨迹为条件引导生成。生成分为两个阶段：基础模型先生成粗糙的长视频，精炼器再提升其质量和一致性。为在无专有数据的情况下规模化训练，团队构建了一套自动标注流水线，通过几何重建技术从公开视频中估计度量尺度相机位姿。 SANA-WM is built on a hybrid linear diffusion transformer architecture. Its attention mechanism alternates between Gated DeltaNet linear attention (applied per frame for efficient long-range context) and standard softmax attention, reducing memory costs while retaining quality. Camera control is injected via a dual-branch module that conditions generation on 6-DoF pose trajectories. Generation is split into two stages: a base model produces a rough long video, then a refiner improves its quality and consistency. To train at scale without proprietary data, the team built an automated annotation pipeline that estimates metric-scale camera poses from publicly available videos using geometric reconstruction techniques.

\[\text{Attention}(Q, K, V) = \text{GDN}(Q, K, V) \oplus \text{Softmax}\left(\frac{QK^\top}{\sqrt{d}}\right)V\]
💡 混合线性注意力：将帧级门控DeltaNet（线性复杂度）与标准Softmax注意力结合，兼顾效率与建模能力 Hybrid linear attention combining frame-wise Gated DeltaNet (linear complexity) with standard softmax attention, balancing efficiency and modeling capacity

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

世界模型是训练具身智能和机器人系统的核心基础设施，但其高昂的算力成本长期将其局限于顶级实验室。SANA-WM的开源发布与消费级GPU推理能力，将极大降低这一技术门槛，有望加速基于仿真的机器人学习和自动驾驶研究。 World models are a key building block for training embodied AI agents and robotics systems, but their prohibitive compute cost has limited access to well-resourced labs. SANA-WM's open-source release with consumer-GPU inference democratizes this capability, potentially accelerating research in simulation-based robot learning and autonomous driving.

WarmPrior: Straightening Flow-Matching Policies with Temporal Priors WarmPrior：用时序先验矫直流匹配策略

🧑‍🔬 Sinjae Kang, Chanyoung Kim, Kaixin Wang, Li Zhao et al.

图 Figure · Page 2 of paper

🎯 用历史动作构建时序先验替换高斯噪声，提升机器人操作成功率
Replace Gaussian noise with action-history priors in flow matching to improve robot manipulation policies.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 当前基于扩散模型和流匹配的机器人控制策略，在生成动作时都从标准高斯分布采样噪声作为起点。这个"冷启动"的噪声与实际任务动作之间距离遥远、路径弯曲，导致推理效率低、策略性能受限。如何为生成策略提供一个更合适的出发点，是一个被忽视的设计维度。 Current diffusion and flow-matching robot policies start generation from a standard Gaussian noise distribution, which is far from the actual action space. This "cold start" creates long, curved probability paths that hurt inference efficiency and policy performance. The choice of source distribution has been largely overlooked as a design dimension in generative robot control.
💡 解决
的亮点		 • **核心创新**：提出 WarmPrior——直接用机器人最近的动作历史构建一个"温暖"的先验分布，替换标准高斯噪声，几乎零额外计算成本。 • **理论洞见**：WarmPrior 使流匹配的概率路径显著变直，与最优传输耦合（Rectified Flow）的效果相似，从数学上解释了性能增益的来源。 • **双场景增益**：不仅在标准行为克隆（BC）中提升成功率，还在先验空间强化学习中改善探索分布，同时提升样本效率和最终性能。 • **Core innovation**: WarmPrior constructs a "warm" source distribution from the robot's recent action history, replacing standard Gaussian noise at virtually zero extra cost. • **Theoretical insight**: WarmPrior straightens probability paths in flow matching, analogous to optimal-transport couplings in Rectified Flow, providing a principled explanation for the performance gain. • **Dual-scenario benefit**: Improves both standard behavior cloning and prior-space RL, boosting sample efficiency and final task performance across the board.
🧪 实验
内容		 实验在多个机器人操作基准上进行，涵盖标准行为克隆和先验空间强化学习两种场景。基线包括使用标准高斯先验的扩散策略（Diffusion Policy）和流匹配策略（Flow Matching Policy），以及 Rectified Flow 等方法。评估指标为任务成功率，同时分析概率路径直线度（轨迹曲率）以验证理论解释。 Experiments were conducted on multiple robotic manipulation benchmarks under two settings: standard behavior cloning (BC) and prior-space reinforcement learning. Baselines include standard Gaussian-prior Diffusion Policy, Flow Matching Policy, and Rectified Flow. The primary evaluation metric is task success rate, with additional analysis of probability path straightness (trajectory curvature) to validate the theoretical explanation.
📊 实验
结果		 WarmPrior 在行为克隆任务中相比标准高斯先验的流匹配策略，成功率持续提升；在多个操作任务上超越 Rectified Flow 基线。在先验空间强化学习中，WarmPrior 同时提升了样本效率（更少交互步数达到相同性能）和最终成功率。概率路径曲率分析显示 WarmPrior 的轨迹显著更直，与理论预期一致。（注：论文摘要未披露具体数字，以上为论文所报告的定性与定量趋势。） WarmPrior consistently improves success rates over standard Gaussian-prior flow matching policies across robotic manipulation benchmarks, and outperforms Rectified Flow baselines on multiple tasks. In prior-space RL, it achieves higher final success rates with improved sample efficiency (fewer environment interactions to reach equivalent performance). Path curvature analysis confirms significantly straighter probability trajectories under WarmPrior, validating the theoretical claims. (Note: The abstract does not disclose specific numeric figures; results reflect the paper's reported trends.)
🏁 结论 本文证明了生成式机器人策略中源分布的选择是一个被长期忽视但至关重要的设计维度：用基于最近动作历史构建的 WarmPrior 替换标准高斯噪声，即可在行为克隆和强化学习两种范式下持续提升操作任务成功率，且几乎不增加计算成本。 This paper demonstrates that the source distribution in generative robot policies is a critically underexplored design axis: replacing standard Gaussian noise with WarmPrior—a temporally grounded prior built from recent action history—consistently improves success rates across both behavior cloning and RL paradigms at negligible computational cost.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

WarmPrior 的核心思路很简单：机器人在执行任务时，刚刚做过的动作序列是对"接下来该做什么"的一个天然预测。将这段历史动作（加上少量噪声扰动）作为流匹配的起始分布，而非从零均值高斯分布出发。这样源分布与目标动作分布在空间上更接近，概率流路径更短更直，推理步骤更少即可达到高质量输出。在强化学习场景中，WarmPrior 还天然地约束了探索范围，使智能体在有意义的动作邻域内探索，而不是在整个高斯噪声空间中随机游走。 WarmPrior's key idea is that a robot's recent action history is a natural prediction of its next actions. Instead of sampling from a zero-mean Gaussian, the source distribution is constructed by adding small noise perturbations to the most recent action chunk from the robot's execution buffer. This brings the source distribution much closer to the target action distribution, resulting in straighter and shorter probability flow paths that require fewer inference steps for high-quality outputs. In the RL setting, this warm prior also naturally constrains exploration to a meaningful neighborhood around recent actions, improving sample efficiency.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[p_0 = \mathcal{N}(a_{\text{hist}}, \sigma^2 I)\]
💡 WarmPrior 的源分布：以最近历史动作为均值、加小方差高斯噪声构建，替代标准零均值高斯分布 N(0,I) WarmPrior source distribution: a Gaussian centered at recent action history with small variance, replacing the standard N(0,I) starting distribution in flow matching.
\[\mathcal{L}_{\text{FM}} = \mathbb{E}_{t, x_0 \sim p_0, x_1 \sim p_1} \left\| v_\theta(x_t, t) - (x_1 - x_0) \right\|^2\]
💡 流匹配训练目标：学习速度场 v_θ 拟合从源分布 p_0 到目标动作分布 p_1 的直线路径；WarmPrior 通过缩小 x_0 与 x_1 的距离使路径更直。 Flow matching training objective: learn velocity field v_θ to approximate straight paths from source p_0 to target action distribution p_1. WarmPrior shrinks the gap between x_0 and x_1, making paths straighter and training easier.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

该工作为扩散/流匹配策略提供了一个即插即用的改进方案，无需改变模型结构，仅通过更换先验分布即可提升性能，对机器人学习社区具有直接的实用价值。更重要的是，它开启了"先验分布设计"这一新的研究方向，启发未来工作进一步探索任务感知、状态感知的先验构建方法。 WarmPrior offers a plug-and-play improvement for diffusion and flow-matching policies—no architecture changes needed, just a better starting distribution—making it immediately practical for the robot learning community. More broadly, it establishes source distribution design as a new research axis, opening the door to task-aware and state-conditioned priors that could further advance generative robot control.

🎯 用真实新闻回放模拟未来，系统评测AI智能体的长期适应能力
A benchmark that replays real-world news chronologically to evaluate how well AI agents adapt beyond their knowledge cutoff.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 现有AI智能体越来越多地被部署在动态、开放的真实环境中，但我们缺乏有效的工具来衡量它们能否随着新信息的到来持续自我更新和适应。现有评测基准大多是静态的，无法模拟时间流逝中信息逐渐涌入的真实场景，也无法考察智能体在知识截止日期之后的预测与推理能力。 AI agents are increasingly deployed in open-ended, ever-changing environments, yet we lack rigorous benchmarks to measure their ability to adapt as new information arrives over time. 的亮点		 • 创新性地构建了"时间序回放"仿真环境：将真实新闻文章按时间顺序逐条提供给智能体，模拟真实世界信息流入的过程。 • 在智能体"原生"运行框架下进行评测，无需修改模型结构，测试其对2026年1月至3月世界事件的预测能力。 • 通过消融实验，系统研究了记忆、搜索、长时视野测试时适应（test-time adaptation）和不确定性推理等新兴研究方向的效果。 • Novel "chronological replay" simulation: real news articles are fed to agents in the order they occurred, faithfully mimicking real-world information arrival. • Agents are evaluated in their native harness without architectural changes, testing forecasting on real world events from Jan–Mar 2026. • Systematic ablations illuminate the impact of memory, search, long-horizon test-time adaptation, and uncertainty reasoning as emerging research directions.

⚙️ Method · 方法详解

FutureSim通过将真实新闻文章按时间顺序"回放"给智能体，构建一个基于现实事件的模拟世界。智能体需要在其知识截止日期之后，依靠不断到来的新闻信息对未来的世界事件进行预测（以解答问题的形式）。评测在智能体各自的原生环境中进行，使用Brier评分和准确率等指标衡量预测质量。研究者还对记忆利用、搜索策略、长时程适应等模块进行了消融分析，以识别哪些能力对性能贡献最大。 FutureSim builds a grounded simulation by replaying real news articles to agents in strict chronological order, asking them to forecast world events (formulated as questions) that occur after their training knowledge cutoff. Agents are tested in their native inference harness — no architectural modification required — and performance is measured via accuracy and Brier skill score. Careful ablations then isolate the contribution of components like memory, search, test-time adaptation, and uncertainty reasoning to identify which capabilities most drive adaptive performance.

📐 Key Formulas · 关键公式

\[\text{Brier Skill Score} = 1 - \frac{\text{BS}}{\text{BS}_{\text{ref}}}\]
💡 Brier技能分衡量预测模型相对于参考基线（如无预测基线）的性能提升，分数低于0意味着比不预测还差。 The Brier Skill Score measures how much better a forecasting model is compared to a reference baseline; a score below 0 means the agent performs worse than making no prediction at all.

🌟 Why It Matters · 为什么重要

随着AI智能体被广泛部署于真实动态世界，如何客观评估其持续适应能力成为核心挑战；FutureSim提供了一个可扩展、基于现实的评测框架，将推动记忆、搜索、测试时学习等方向的系统性研究。该基准揭示了当前模型的严重不足，有望引导整个领域朝更具鲁棒性的开放世界智能体发展。 As AI agents are deployed in increasingly dynamic real-world settings, having a principled and realistic benchmark for measuring continuous adaptation is essential for guiding research progress. FutureSim directly exposes the gap between current frontier models and truly adaptive agents, making it a valuable tool to drive advances in memory, search, and test-time learning for open-ended AI systems.

🎯 高压缩比VAE，兼顾重建质量与扩散模型兼容性
A high-compression VAE suite achieving top reconstruction fidelity and fast diffusion model convergence.
维度 中文 English
❓ 待解决
的问题		 变分自编码器（VAE）是图像生成流程的核心组件，但在追求高压缩比时面临两大难题：重建质量下降（尤其是文档等富文本图像），以及高维潜在空间难以被下游扩散模型高效学习。现有VAE难以同时兼顾压缩率、重建保真度和"扩散友好性"。 Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) are critical backbone components for image generation pipelines, but pushing them to high compression ratios causes two major problems: reconstruction quality degrades (especially for text-rich images like documents), and the resulting high-dimensional latent spaces are hard for downstream diffusion models to learn from efficiently. ⚙️ Method · 方法详解

Qwen-Image-VAE-2.0在标准VAE架构上引入全局跳跃连接（GSC），将编码器的低层细节直接传递给解码器，绕过压缩瓶颈；同时扩展潜在通道数以承载更丰富的表示。训练规模扩展至数十亿张图像，并引入合成渲染引擎生成大量富文本图像（文档、图表等），使模型能精准重建细粒度文字。为提升潜在空间对扩散训练的兼容性，采用增强的语义对齐策略使潜在分布更结构化、更易学习。此外，编码器采用非对称无注意力设计，推理阶段编码速度更快，同时不牺牲解码质量。 Qwen-Image-VAE-2.0 upgrades the standard VAE architecture with Global Skip Connections that route low-level detail from encoder to decoder, bypassing the information bottleneck of the compressed latent code, and uses more latent channels to carry richer representations. Training is scaled to billions of images, and a synthetic rendering engine is used to generate diverse text-rich training samples (documents, charts, etc.) so the model learns to reconstruct fine-grained text accurately. To ensure the latent space is compatible with diffusion training, an enhanced semantic alignment strategy aligns latent distributions to be more structured and learnable. Finally, the encoder is made asymmetric and attention-free (lighter than the decoder), so that inference-time encoding is fast without sacrificing decode quality.
